Bunnawat Gresh Captures FJC Award
Monday, March 30th, 1896
17-year-old Bunnawat Gresh made a few Davenport headlines during this past week as the rookie third baseman proved he could compete at the plate with his high-school idols. He captured the attention of fans, journalists and Francophone Junior Circuit officials last week and today took home Player of the Week honors for his effort.

He cracked 8 hits in 15 at-bats to put up a .533 average. Gresh collected 2 home runs, 5 RBIs and scored 3 runs.

In the 1896 season Gresh is hitting .297 with 5 home runs.
