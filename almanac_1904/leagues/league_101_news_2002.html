5-for-5 Day for Jaja
Thursday, July 15th, 1897
A jubilant Hadji Jaja joined the postgame press conference at Ballpark of Quebec today to offer some unusual words of encouragement -- for the Chicagou Frontenacs pitching staff he had just vanquished.

"It may not have looked like it," said the Quebec Nordiques third baseman, "but I saw some awful good pitches today. If luck hadn't been on my side it might have turned out a lot different."

That would not have been the conclusion of anyone who saw his dominant 5-for-5 hitting performance, which led the Nordiques to a 7-2 win.

"He made it look easy," said Nathaniel Forestier, manager of the Frontenacs. "We never figured out what to do with him. Obviously."

Hadji Jaja hit an RBI triple in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, singled in the 7th and hit a two-run single in the 8th.

This season Jaja is batting .332 with 1 home run. He has scored 49 runs and driven in 54.
