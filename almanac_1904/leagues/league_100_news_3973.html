Sterling Cops #1 Pitcher Award in June
Monday, July 1st, 1901
It was a monstrous June for the Canadian League Pitcher of the Month award winner, Alessandro Sterling of the Boston Redcoats. He put up some mighty scary numbers.

Sterling recorded 5 wins and 1 loss in 6 starts. Throwing a total of 55 innings, he yielded 32 hits, mowed down 16 on strikes, gave up 25 walks and put up a solid ERA of 1.64 in a stellar month on the mound.

In 13 starts this season Sterling has notched a 9-4 record and a 2.39 ERA.
