Fawley Gets CL Batter of the Month Trophy
Thursday, September 1st, 1904
When Muangkan Fawley stepped up to the plate in August he did it with confidence, and today he was honored with the Canadian League Batter of the Month award.

The young New York left fielder posted a .341 batting average with 1 home run, 13 RBIs and scored 15 runs. Fawley also worked pitchers for 5 walks to post a .375 on-base percentage.

Fawley has a .347 average and a .404 on-base percentage for the year. He has picked up 127 hits, 4 home runs and driven in 54 runs.
