Roux Wins CL Player of the Week Prize
Monday, July 29th, 1895
Kiatjarern Roux had a week for the ages as he romped to the Canadian League Player of the Week Award. The happy-go-lucky 37-year-old still plays ball like a kid, mashing and slashing as he goes.

The Philadelphia Liberty third baseman hit .538 last week as he chalked up 14 hits in 26 at-bats, no home runs, 8 RBIs and 5 runs scored.

This year Roux has hit at a .324 clip with 99 hits, no home runs, 46 RBIs and 53 runs scored.
