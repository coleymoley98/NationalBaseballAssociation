Spencer Hutton Will Miss 4 Weeks
Sunday, July 12th, 1896
Quebec fans will be disappointed to learn that Spencer Hutton suffered a sprained ankle while running the bases in today's game. "You can't make excuses in baseball, because there's always a game tomorrow," said the team's skipper.
