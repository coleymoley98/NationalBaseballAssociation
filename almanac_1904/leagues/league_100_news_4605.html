Philadelphia Loses Ratipong Allard, Will Miss Season
Saturday, August 9th, 1902
The Philadelphia Liberty and their fans were rocked today by the news that star second baseman Ratipong Allard would miss the rest of the season due to injury. Allard was hurt in a recent game against the Boston Redcoats while colliding with a player at a base. He was immediately removed from the game and taken for examination. Tests today revealed the injury was a pinched nerve. His doctors think that Allard will miss about 4 weeks.

Currently Allard is batting .294 with 65 hits and 1 home run. He has knocked in 30 runs and scored 32 times.
