Anglo Junior Circuit Names Love Best Player
Sunday, August 5th, 1900
Abraham Love of the Halifax Juniors had a fine season this year and today he accepted the Most Valuable Player Award for 1900.

The 17-year-old right fielder put up a .433 average and a .465 on-base percentage, piling up 87 hits, 17 doubles, 5 triples, 8 home runs, 55 RBIs and 43 runs scored.

He received 12 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Sarawut St-Onge of the Pawtucket Juniors finished second in voting, while Jason Maheux of the Fort Wayne Juniors finished third.
