1895 CL Batting Crown Goes to Jones
Sunday, August 25th, 1895
If Tanasak Jones continues to produce like he did in 1895, fans of the Philadelphia Liberty will be very happy. The third baseman posted a .355 batting average this year, which was good enough to capture the Canadian League batting crown.

"There are a lot of talented hitters in the league, to be at the top of that list is an extremely humbling goal," said the 28-year-old.

On the season, Jones put up 127 hits, 22 doubles, 30 triples and 2 home runs. He also added 66 RBIs and scored 86 runs.
