Hutton Wins NL Honor
Monday, June 29th, 1896
At the age of 31, Spencer Hutton might have some potential he hasn't realized yet -- if his success at the plate last week is any indication. The Quebec left fielder put up some exceptional numbers the past seven days with a .441 batting average. For his efforts Hutton collected the National League Player of the Week Award.

He racked up 15 hits in 34 at-bats, 1 home run and 9 RBIs last week in 7 games.

Thus far this year Hutton has hit .323 with 2 home runs and 43 RBIs.
