Jacobs Crushes 3 Homers Against Quebec City
Sunday, March 17th, 1901
Timothy Jacobs went on an explosive hitting spree against the Quebec City Juniors at Juniors Ballpark today, launching a trio of blasts to lead the Sherbrooke Juniors to the 14-6 win.

The second baseman finished 3-for-5 with 3 runs scored and 6 RBI.

"It was just one of those days," Jacobs said in a postgame interview. "I don't know how to explain it. I'm just putting a good swing on the ball."

Quebec City skipper Leomar Durán said Jacobs was "locked-in."

"We made some pretty good pitches," he said. "Those first two homers were exactly where the pitcher wanted to put them. He's just hot right now -- you've just got to tip your hat to him."

Timothy Jacobs was hit by a pitch in the 1st, flied out in the 2nd, grounded out in the 5th, hit a solo-shot off Jahlil Gailes in the 7th, hit a three-run home run off Jahlil Gailes in the 8th and hit a two-run home run off Torian Weatherspoon in the 9th.

This season Jacobs has delivered 21 hits in 56 at-bats for a .375 average with 5 home runs. He is getting on base at a .500 clip and his totals also include 16 RBIs and 21 runs scored. This year Jacobs has played in 16 games.
