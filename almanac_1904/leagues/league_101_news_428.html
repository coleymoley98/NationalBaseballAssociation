Meesuk Leaves Team to Care for Ailing Mother
Friday, August 5th, 1904
Kwanchai Meesuk of the Nordiques is expected to miss at least the next game. Meesuk has taken a leave of absence to be with his ailing mother. "I told him to take all the time he needs," said manager Frédéric Lévesque. "I know what he's been going through, and the most important thing is that he's there for both her and the family."
