Rowdy Players Suspended by League
Wednesday, April 13th, 1904
If there wasn't a history between Harvey McDonald of Sainte-Paul and Brad Birdal of Gauthier, then there is one now. The incident between the two sparked a bench-clearing scrum with those two being the center of attention. Now they will miss 4 and 2 games respectively as a result of their actions. The league office announced their suspensions today.

"We're both really competitive," McDonald said later, "and sometimes things go a little too far because of it."

Presently McDonald is hitting .287 with 11 home runs and 26 RBIs. He has played in 38 games and scored 38 times.

Birdal has taken the mound for 10 relief appearances this season, nailing down no saves while notching a 2-0 record with a 10.02 ERA. The Gauthier reliever has limited opposing batters to a .410 batting average.
