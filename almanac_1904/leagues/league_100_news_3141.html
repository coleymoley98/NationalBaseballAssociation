August's CL Batter of Month Award Announced
Friday, September 1st, 1899
Lee Foreman made his presence known as one of the best young stars in the Canadian League this past month.

The Boston Redcoats center fielder shined through the month of August and has won the Batter of the Month award.

The 23-year-old hit .386 with 2 home runs, 15 RBIs and 18 runs scored.

For the season Foreman is currently batting .277 with 6 home runs and 45 RBIs. In 98 games he has reached base at a .329 rate and scored 70 runs to date.
