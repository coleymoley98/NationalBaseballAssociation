Catteau Beats Lions 8-4 for 100th Win
Saturday, May 5th, 1900
Val Catteau entered the record books today as he collected win number 100 at Ballpark of Quebec.

"Wow! This is a select group of pitchers. I am honored and thrilled to be in it," Catteau said after the game. "I never dreamed of winning this many games."

In his historic outing he went 9 innings and gave up 4 runs on 9 hits. Catteau also had 2 strikeouts and no walks as his Quebec Nordiques topped the Detroit Lions 8-4.

Since joining the league, Catteau has compiled an ERA of 3.33 with a career record of 100-69. He has 458 strikeouts in 1483.2 innings and has held opponents to a .286 batting average.
