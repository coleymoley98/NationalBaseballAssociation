Eagan Wins NL Player of Week Award
Monday, July 7th, 1902
Pat Eagan seems to put youthful exuberance into every pitch he throws and last week he threw up numbers that even the oldest of veterans would look at with envy. That's why the 23-year-old hurler was chosen the National League Player of the Week Award.

Eagan notched 2 wins for the Montreal Royales. He finished the week with 14 strikeouts and an ERA of 1.00 while holding the opposition to a .123 average.

This year Eagan has gone 16-1 with an ERA of 1.60 and has 54 strikeouts in 146 innings.
