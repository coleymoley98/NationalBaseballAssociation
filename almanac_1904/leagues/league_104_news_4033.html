Juniors Take It on Chin, Lose, 12-0, to New Britain
Saturday, July 20th, 1901
Today was Justin Fecteau's turn in the rotation for the New Britain Juniors and he made the most of it, shutting out the Pawtucket Juniors on 2 hits.

The 17-year-old right-hander led the Juniors to an easy 12-0 victory, allowing 1 walk and tallying 2 strikeouts.

"I hope we miss him next time," Pawtucket manager Jetrin Charron said. "Fecteau certainly pitched a strong game against us today. He was in a groove, throwing everything for strikes. Throwing hard. Changing speeds."

This season Fecteau is batting .243 with 7 RBIs, 17 runs scored and 1 home run.
