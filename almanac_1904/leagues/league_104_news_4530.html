MacQueen Hurls Buffalo to Shutout Win
Sunday, July 13th, 1902
Harrison MacQueen of the Buffalo Juniors whitewashed the Hartford Juniors 7-0 in a complete-game win, limiting them to 4 hits while chalking up 8 strikeouts and giving up 1 walk.

"He's got the makeup. He's not afraid to challenge guys," Buffalo manager Amaret Therrien said to BNN reporters after the game. "Harrison is poised out there. He knows how to pitch and how to use his stuff."

This year MacQueen has recorded a 2-3 mark. In 11 starts he has fanned 91 batters in 81.2 innings with a 2.20 ERA.
