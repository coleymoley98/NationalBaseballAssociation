Monday, July 1st, 1895
Despite the number of years he's been in the league, Ryan Verville still gets excited when he puts together a quality stretch like he had in June. The 38-year-old starter for Quebec was named National League Pitcher of the Month earlier today.

He collected 7 wins and 2 losses with 23 strikeouts in his 9 starts, putting up a fine 2.62 ERA in 75.2 innings of work.

In 19 starts this season Verville has registered a 11-7 record and a 3.16 ERA.
