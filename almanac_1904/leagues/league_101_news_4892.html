Royales, Foreman Stonewall St. Louis, 4-0
Monday, May 18th, 1903
Garrett Foreman of the Montreal Royales toyed with the St. Louis Cardinaux today, blanking them 4-0 at Montreal Grounds. He was almost untouchable, baffling them on a 4-hit shutout with 6 strikeouts and 1 walk.

"I'm sweating now because it's hot in here," Foreman said in the clubhouse after the game. "But it didn't even feel like I was sweating out there."

On the season Foreman has compiled a 2-3 mark with a 1.74 ERA in 5 starts. In pitching 46.2 innings he has 30 strikeouts and 6 walks while limiting the opposition to a .201 batting average.
