Toronto Trades O'Coogan for Boston's Pham
Tuesday, July 16th, 1895
BNN has learned the Toronto Blue Sox have reportedly worked out a deal with the Boston Redcoats. Sources close to the Blue Sox indicated that a trade has been reached in principle where 38-year-old RHP Jason Pham and 20-year-old C Christian Laflamme will be swapped to Toronto with 26-year-old 1B Corky O'Coogan heading to Boston. Neither organization would comment on the report, saying it was club policy not to speak about a trade until it was finalized.

Boston will retain a portion of Pham's remaining contract.

To date, O'Coogan has collected 22 hits for a .328 average. He has posted 1 home run, scored 9 runs and driven in 7 runs.

Presently Pham has a 8-11 mark in 23 starts with a 4.86 ERA. He has given up 203 hits in 181.1 innings, struck out 28 and issued 67 walks. Opposing clubs have compiled a .281 batting average against him.
