March's Starter of the Month Goes to Harris
Saturday, April 1st, 1899
Daniel Harris took a much-deserved turn in the spotlight earlier today as he accepted the Francophone Junior Circuit Pitcher of the Month award for March.

The 17-year-old Juniors starter is a relative newcomer to the league, but nonetheless racked up an impressive 6-1 won-lost record in 9 starts. This month he fanned 50 batters in 66.2 innings and compiled a 2.29 ERA.
