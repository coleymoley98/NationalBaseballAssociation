Boston Redcoats Ship Verreault to Toronto
Thursday, July 27th, 1899

This season Verreault has hit at a .167 clip and produced no RBI, no home runs and 1 run.

This season Petchdee is swinging the bat at a .278 pace. He has hit no home runs and scored 25 times, while driving in 36. His on-base percentage is .342 and he has played in 68 games.
