Juniors Suffer 10-0 Drubbing from Juniors
Wednesday, April 22nd, 1896
J.F. Pinsonneault of the Minneapolis Juniors spun his magic on the St. Louis Juniors today. He blanked them 10-0 on 6 hits, registering 8 strikeouts and 1 walk.

"The bottom line was I just kept the ball down," Pinsonneault said. "My breaking stuff was down. Everything was down."

Talking to reporters in the postgame interview, Minneapolis skipper Brien Gilliam said the team "got solid pitching, good defense and took great team at-bats in a lot of different situations."

"J.F. pitched really well but this was a solid performance by everyone," he said.

This season-to-date, Pinsonneault has fashioned a 11-5 mark and 3.76 ERA in 129.1 innings. Opposing clubs are batting .239 against him and he has logged 105 strikeouts.
