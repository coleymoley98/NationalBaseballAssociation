Juniors Chill Gent's Hot Streak
Monday, June 27th, 1904
Lady Luck was not on the side of T.J. Gent or his teammates today at Juniors Ballpark.

Everything went the way of the Morgantown Juniors, who not only came away with the 4-0 victory over Pawtucket, but also held Gent hitless in 3 at-bats to put an end to his 23-game hitting streak.

"It was a good run," Gent said afterward. "It would be much easier to take right now if we had won."

Gent is presently hitting at a .383 pace with no home runs and 11 RBIs. He has played in 25 games and scored 11 times.
