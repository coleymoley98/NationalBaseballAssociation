Juniors Ace Allard Cops Pitcher of the Year Award
Wednesday, August 4th, 1897
Providence ace Komkrit Allard put up some sensational stats for the 1897 season and has been voted the Anglo Junior Circuit Pitcher of the Year Award winner.

The 21-year-old Juniors star made 17 starts and racked up a 15-1 record. He pitched 124.1 innings, collected 148 strikeouts, gave up 45 walks and 76 hits in holding the opposition to a .172 batting average.

He received 11 first place votes out of a possible 12. Joseph Coon of the Morgantown Juniors finished second in voting with 1 first place vote, while Edgar Arleth of the Providence Juniors finished third.
