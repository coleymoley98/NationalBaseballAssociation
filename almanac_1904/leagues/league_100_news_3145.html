Fontaine Voted August's Top CL Rookie
Friday, September 1st, 1899
It was an awesome August for Jocelyn Fontaine. The young Boston starter surprised opponents all month and today was announced as the Canadian League Rookie of the Month.

In 7 starts he registered a 3-3 record and racked up 22 strikeouts and a 2.64 ERA in 61.1 innings.

To date Fontaine is 9-10 in 24 starts with a 3.78 ERA and has 69 strikeouts in 209.2 innings.
