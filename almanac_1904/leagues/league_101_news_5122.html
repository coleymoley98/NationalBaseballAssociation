St. Louis-Chicagou Trade: Sidebottom for Pinsonneault
Saturday, August 1st, 1903
It came down to the last hour of a self-imposed trade deadline, but the St. Louis Cardinaux and Chicagou Frontenacs finally came to an agreement on a trade that had been rumored for weeks. The trade sends St. Louis's 37-year-old LHP Toxteth Sidebottom and 21-year-old C Dudley Cross to Chicagou in a swap for 23-year-old LHP J.F. Pinsonneault. Both GMs seemed relieved that the deal had been completed with one stating, "We had to set a deadline to get it done." The players are expected to report to their new teams by tomorrow.


Sidebottom has taken the mound for 17 relief appearances this season, nailing down 1 save while notching a 1-1 record with a 2.52 ERA. The Chicagou reliever has limited opposing batters to a .324 batting average.

Pinsonneault has appeared in 25 games and captured no saves. Over his career he has no wins and 1 loss and a 3.54 ERA.
