Brasseurs No Match for Jones, Lions in 15-0 Thumping
Thursday, August 20th, 1903
Kittipong Jones of Detroit easily handled the Juneau Brasseurs, stopping them on 3 hits and chucking a complete-game 15-0 shutout.

"He really did a job on us," said Juneau manager Owen Turcotte. "He had good command of his pitches. He changed speeds well and confused us. We never could zero in on him, but he certainly zeroed in on us."

He finished the game with 7 strikeouts and 1 walk in a solid outing.

In 26 starts this year Jones has fashioned a 14-8 record and a 2.66 ERA.
