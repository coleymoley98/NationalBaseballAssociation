Fawley Terrific, Takes FJC Most Valuable Player Award
Wednesday, May 6th, 1896
He may have less experience than most of his Francophone Junior Circuit peers, but that didn't slow Muangkan Fawley down at all in 1896.

At the age of 17, the Juneau left fielder has been named the winner of the Francophone Junior Circuit Most Valuable Player Award.

He had a .401 batting average and a .486 on-base percentage this year. In 48 games he piled up 73 hits, 9 doubles, 4 triples, 12 home runs, 43 RBIs and 52 runs scored.

"I'm blown away by the season I was able to put together," he said. "I'm looking forward to trying to do it all again next year."

He received 12 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Mikael Béguin of the Sainte-Paul Juniors finished second in voting, while Gabriel McCaskell of the Sherbrooke Juniors finished third.
