Philadelphia Trades Laplante for Quebec's Wiegert
Thursday, July 26th, 1900
Earlier today, officials from the Philadelphia Liberty and the Quebec Nordiques announced that they had finalized negotiations on a trade between the two teams. The deal will send 34-year-old LHP Toxteth Sidebottom, 19-year-old LHP Yai Fortenberry and 22-year-old RHP Arran Laplante to Quebec in exchange for 25-year-old C Sly Wiegert.


In his career-to-date, the 22-year-old Laplante has fashioned a 14-15 mark, 4.36 ERA and thrown 264 innings. Opposing clubs are batting .295 lifetime against him and he has logged 83 total strikeouts.
