5-Hit Barrage by Bulot
Sunday, June 5th, 1898
Quincy second baseman Theerawekin Bulot had a day to remember today, 5-for-6 against the Toronto Juniors at Juniors Ballpark as the Juniors picked up a 17-15 victory.

"He was patient up there today," Quincy skipper Ethan Bax told the press after the game. "I like the way he waited for his pitch and wasn't overly aggressive. Bulot has a tendency to chase bad balls sometimes."

Theerawekin Bulot singled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, hit an RBI single in the 5th, singled in the 6th, doubled in the 7th and struck out in the 8th.
