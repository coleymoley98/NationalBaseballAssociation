NL News: Expansion Draft
Thursday, March 19th, 1903
The St. Louis Cardinaux and Juneau Brasseurs, the new kids on the block, are trying to find a jewel in the rough and the established teams are trying to give up nothing of importance in today's National League Expansion Draft. The NL will conduct its expansion draft today for the new clubs that will begin play this season. Each current franchise will, in effect, freeze most of its player roster and protect them from the expansion draft. Recently each club had to submit a list to the league office, which would be used to stock the new teams' rosters.
