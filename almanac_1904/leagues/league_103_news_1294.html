Juniors Plugged by Malone, Juniors, 3-0
Wednesday, April 8th, 1896
Peyton Malone of the Juneau Juniors was cool and collected on the mound, leading his club to a shutout victory over the Minneapolis Juniors, 3-0.

"He was mixing his pitches real well and keeping his composure on the mound," Juneau manager Cyrus Helmick said later. "When Peyton does that, good things happen."

The right-hander had 13 strikeouts, no walks and allowed 5 hits in the win.

This year Malone has a 4-6 record with a 4.33 ERA.
