Gosnell Razor-Sharp, Shuts Out Halifax
Wednesday, June 3rd, 1896
In an excellent mound performance, Hunter Gosnell of the Providence Juniors whitewashed the Halifax Juniors 8-0 on a 5-hitter.

"I had my best stuff and did a good job of mixing up my pitches," he said after the game.

In all, the Providence hurler rang up 8 strikeouts and 2 walks in the shutout win.

"We just didn't swing the bats well today," said Juniors manager Jimmy Rodrigues. "Gosnell did a good job keeping us guessing."
