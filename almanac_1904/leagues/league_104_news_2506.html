The Morgantown Juniors stopped the Canton Juniors by a score of 24-7 at Juniors Ballpark today, with the lion's share of the credit going to third baseman Samuel Vermette. The 16-year-old had a career day, collecting 5 hits.

"Every batter wants to have a quality at-bat every time he steps up to the plate," Vermette said later. "He wants to hit the ball hard."

Vermette did just that in his 7 at-bats, much to the chagrin of the Juniors.
