Pépin Extends Hit Streak to 20 Games
Friday, May 17th, 1895
The Detroit Lions stopped the Quebec Nordiques today 9-8 and Blaise Pépin collected a base hit yet again. The Detroit right fielder finished with 3 hits to run his consecutive-game hitting streak to 20.

"I want to get a hit every time up," a visibly excited Pépin told reporters after the contest. "I want to get a hit every game I play. Right now, I feel like I could take this streak to 100 games."
Blaise Pépin singled in the 1st, doubled in the 3rd, flied out in the 4th, grounded out in the 7th and hit a two-run triple in the 9th.

Pépin is currently hitting at a .414 pace with no home runs and 13 RBIs.
