Nordiques End Bergeron's Streak
Thursday, May 28th, 1896
Chicagou Frontenacs second baseman Charles Bergeron and his hot bat finally met their match today at Ballpark of Quebec.

The Quebec Nordiques snapped his 20-game hitting string, holding Bergeron hitless in 3 at-bats and also whipping Chicagou for good measure.

"We're real pleased with this one," Nordiques manager Frédéric Lévesque said. "Nobody wants to be on the wrong end of a streak like that, and clobbering Chicagou makes it even sweeter."

This year Bergeron is hitting .393 with 1 home run.
