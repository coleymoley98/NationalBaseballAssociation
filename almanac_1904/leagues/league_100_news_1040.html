CL News: Draft Pool Revealed
Wednesday, August 7th, 1895
The biggest anxiety for 1895 new young players is getting drafted. For the young man with something to prove, simple entrance into the draft does not guarantee a big league contract. Some will suffer the excruciating torment of having to stand by as their competitors get picked before them, hoping that they get a slim chance to prove their skills anywhere. At any rate, teams now have 90 days to scout the draft pool before the draft is held.
