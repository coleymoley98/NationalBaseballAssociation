Hit Parade: Heemkhao Pops 5 Hits
Saturday, August 6th, 1898
Royales third baseman Prasarn Heemkhao had his way with Detroit pitching today at Montreal Grounds and mauled them for 5 hits in 5 at-bats. His efforts were all in vain, though, as the Lions ruined his great day by toppling the Royales, 8-7.

"I had a great day, but you've got to give Detroit credit, they did what they had to do to win," he told reporters after the game. "They weren't very impressed with my 5 hits."

"Oh, we noticed what Heemkhao did today," Lions skipper Enzo Kelly said. "He had quite a game. I can tell you this, we will pitch him a little differently the next time we face him."

Prasarn Heemkhao hit a solo-shot off Kittiphan Mai in the 2nd, doubled in the 4th, doubled in the 6th, hit an RBI single in the 7th and singled in the 9th.

Heemkhao is batting .316 this year with 3 home runs, 43 RBIs and 48 runs scored. He has 107 hits in 339 at-bats.
