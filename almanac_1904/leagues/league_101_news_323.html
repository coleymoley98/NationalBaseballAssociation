Chicagou's Lader Named NL's Top Player
Monday, July 4th, 1904
Watiwat Lader seems to put youthful exuberance into every pitch he throws and last week he threw up numbers that even the oldest of veterans would look at with envy. That's why the 25-year-old hurler was chosen the National League Player of the Week Award.

Lader notched 2 wins for the Chicagou Frontenacs. He finished the week with 2 strikeouts and an ERA of 0.64 while holding the opposition to a .260 average.

On the season Lader has compiled a 4-2 mark with a 1.29 ERA in 8 starts. In pitching 55.2 innings he has 18 strikeouts and 5 walks while limiting the opposition to a .246 batting average.
