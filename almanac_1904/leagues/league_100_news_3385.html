Big Day with Bat: Tong 5-for-6
Saturday, May 12th, 1900
Danny Tong had an answer for everything the New York Rebels threw at him today at Canadian League Park I. The Toronto second baseman went 5-for-6 in the game to put his name in the record book and put the Blue Sox on top, 12-4.

Summing up his batting philosophy to BNN writers after the game, Tong said, "I just see the ball and I hit it."

Danny Tong singled in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 2nd, flied out in the 4th, singled in the 6th, singled in the 7th and doubled in the 8th.

Thus far in 1900 Tong is hitting .391 with no home runs, 1 RBIs, 8 runs scored and has compiled a .481 OBP.
