Hitting Streak Still Alive for Raun
Saturday, July 2nd, 1904
Tulimak Raun of the New Britain Juniors extended his hitting streak to 20 straight games with 2 hits in today's 4-0 triumph over the Fort Wayne Juniors at Juniors Ballpark.

"Tulimak has been making it easy for me to pencil his name in the lineup every day," Juniors manager Jason Lafrenière joked after the game.
Tulimak Raun flied out in the 2nd, struck out in the 4th, singled in the 7th and hit a two-run home run off Jet Traivut in the 8th.

So far this season, Raun has recorded a .465 batting average with 47 hits, 9 home runs, 26 RBIs, 34 runs scored and a .534 OBP.
