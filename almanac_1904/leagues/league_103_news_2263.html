Juniors End Béguin's Streak
Thursday, March 24th, 1898
The Sainte-Paul Juniors lost to the Drummondville Juniors 5-1 today at Field of the Juniors, but perhaps the bigger news was the end of Mikael Béguin's 22-game hitting streak. The first baseman went 0-for-4 on the day.

"We felt if we could slow down Béguin, we would win the game," Juniors manager Jake Trudel explained. "They really seemed to feed off his streak, so we tried to put more emphasis on shutting him down."

This year Béguin has collected a .402 batting average to date with 41 hits in 102 at-bats, 24 RBIs, 7 home runs and scored 23 times. He has played in 23 games.
