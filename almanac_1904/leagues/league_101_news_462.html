Brintle Skunks Nordiques
Thursday, August 18th, 1904
The Quebec Nordiques were almost helpless today in their 2-0 loss to the Juneau Brasseurs and hurler Lance Brintle. Brintle kept the Nordiques confused all game, spinning a 8-hit shutout.

"Tip your hat to Brintle," Quebec manager Frédéric Lévesque said in his postgame interview. "He kept us off balance all night."

The 24-year-old left-hander had no walks and 3 strikeouts in a strong performance.

"It just happened to be one of those games where even when I made mistakes, they either fouled them off or someone would make a fine defensive play," Brintle said. "It was just one of those games that things went my way."

This year Brintle has compiled a 10-15 mark with a 2.47 ERA in 27 starts.
