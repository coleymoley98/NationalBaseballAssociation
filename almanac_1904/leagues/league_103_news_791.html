Pitcher of the Year Award Goes to Summers
Sunday, May 5th, 1895
Prayad Summers finished off a fine season by taking home the Francophone Junior Circuit Pitcher of the Year Award today.

The Davenport hurler went 13-3 on the year, posting a 2.14 ERA. Summers also tallied 155 strikeouts in 168.1 innings and allowed opposing batters to hit only .241 against him.

He received 12 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Reuban Seals of the Juneau Juniors finished second in voting, while Ian Nastu of the Coude Juniors finished third.
