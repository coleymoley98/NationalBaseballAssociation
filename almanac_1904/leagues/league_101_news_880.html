Albertson, Nordiques Shut Out Frontenacs, 5-0
Wednesday, June 12th, 1895
James Albertson hurled a fine game for the Quebec Nordiques at Ballpark of Quebec today, holding the Chicagou Frontenacs to 5 hits in a shutout win.

"When he started out, his command wasn't that good, but he's a tough guy," Quebec manager Frédéric Lévesque said. "The more pitches he throws, the better his command is."

The right-hander had 2 strikeouts and 1 walk in the 5-0 victory.

"An outstanding job," Chicagou manager Nathaniel Forestier said. "We couldn't get anything going. Every time we got a hit, he got out of the jam. He pitched a terrific game."

This year Albertson has recorded a 10-2 mark. In 14 starts he has fanned 33 batters in 125 innings with a 3.10 ERA.
