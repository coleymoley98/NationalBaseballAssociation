AJC Best Mound Performer Named
Saturday, August 3rd, 1895
Edgar Arleth joined an elite group of pitchers today by picking up the Anglo Junior Circuit Pitcher of the Year Award for 1895.

The 17-year-old hurler finished the year 14-3 for the Providence Juniors, piling up 148.1 innings, registering 126 strikeouts and walking 31 on his way to a solid 1.82 ERA.

He received 12 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Zachary Royer of the Toronto Juniors finished second in voting, while Ryder Couture of the Pawtucket Juniors finished third.
