Catteau Shuts Out Frontenacs, 4-0
Wednesday, May 14th, 1902
Val Catteau was throwing strikes early and getting ahead in the count, his manager said after the Quebec Nordiques hurler tossed a 4-hit, complete game shutout against the Chicagou Frontenacs.

"I went right after each hitter, trying to get ahead in the count," said Catteau. "I wanted them to swing the bats and keep my pitch count down. It worked. I felt strong the whole game, even in the last two innings."

Catteau walked 2 and struck out 2 in the 4-0 victory.
