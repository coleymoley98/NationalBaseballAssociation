Juniors, Grondin Stonewall Sainte-Paul, 8-0
Tuesday, April 7th, 1903
Mitchell Grondin of the Drummondville Juniors spun his magic on the Sainte-Paul Juniors today. He blanked them 8-0 on 4 hits, registering 3 strikeouts and 2 walks.

"The bottom line was I just kept the ball down," Grondin said. "My breaking stuff was down. Everything was down."

Talking to reporters in the postgame interview, Drummondville skipper Jake Trudel said the team "got solid pitching, good defense and took great team at-bats in a lot of different situations."

"Mitchell pitched really well but this was a solid performance by everyone," he said.

To date this season, Grondin has a record of 7 wins and 1 loss and a 1.61 ERA. He has appeared in 10 games and struck out 53 in 72.2 innings.
