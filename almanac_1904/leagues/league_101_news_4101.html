Star of the Week Selected in NL
Monday, August 12th, 1901
It's always a thrill for fans of the team and students of the game alike when a superstar in the making shows what he's got. Mark Potter, the Montreal Royales 24-year-old center fielder, put himself in the spotlight last week making him the clear-cut choice for National League Player of the Week honors.

He ripped 13 hits in 25 at-bats, compiling a .520 average with no home runs and 1 RBIs.

Potter is batting .341 this year with 4 home runs, 39 RBIs and 69 runs scored. He has 132 hits in 387 at-bats.
