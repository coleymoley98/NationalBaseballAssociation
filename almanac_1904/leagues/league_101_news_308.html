Lavallée Magnificent in 6-0 Montreal Triumph
Thursday, June 30th, 1904
Joaquim Lavallée of the Montreal Royales spun his magic on the Chicagou Frontenacs today. He blanked them 6-0 on 5 hits, registering 2 strikeouts and 3 walks.

"The bottom line was I just kept the ball down," Lavallée said. "My breaking stuff was down. Everything was down."

Talking to reporters in the postgame interview, Montreal skipper Kamnan Rowe said the team "got solid pitching, good defense and took great team at-bats in a lot of different situations."

"Joaquim pitched really well but this was a solid performance by everyone," he said.

This season Lavallée has a 9-5 record with a 2.01 ERA in 14 starts.
