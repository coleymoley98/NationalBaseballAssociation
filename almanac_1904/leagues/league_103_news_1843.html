Coude's Starter Receives FJC Pitching Award
Saturday, May 1st, 1897
There's something about a young rookie winning the Francophone Junior Circuit Pitcher of the Month trophy that sends shock waves through the league. Such is the case with Worawat Nhunart, a 16-year-old starter for the Coude Juniors.

In April, he topped off the month with a 8-0 mark and 1.99 ERA in 8 starts, tossed 58.2 innings, fanned 61 and held opposing teams to a .191 average.

During his career Nhunart has recorded 14 wins and 2 losses with a 2.74 ERA and limited other teams to a .218 average.
