Thatcharin Satawong Wins MVP Trophy
Saturday, March 17th, 1900
Thatcharin Satawong of the Team Michigan had a fine season this year and today he accepted the Most Valuable Player Award for 1900.

The 24-year-old center fielder put up a .519 average and a .567 on-base percentage, piling up 14 hits, 5 doubles, 3 triples, no home runs, 3 RBIs and 7 runs scored.

He received 4 first place votes out of a possible 6. Dominique Binette of the Team Ontario finished second in voting with 2 first place votes, while Kevin Coutu of the Team Michigan finished third.
