White Wins FJC Player of the Week Honors
Monday, March 28th, 1904
It can be difficult for a young player to step out of the shadows and assert himself in the Francophone Junior Circuit, but last week Justin White of the Coude Juniors reminded everyone just why he's here as he threw up numbers that made him an easy selection for Player of the Week. White rocked opposing pitching for a .545 average (12-for-22), no home runs and 9 RBIs to come away with the award.

A look at his season totals shows White with no home runs, .376 BA, .413 OBP, 27 RBIs and 17 runs scored.
