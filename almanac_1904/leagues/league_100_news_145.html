Rebels Get One to Win
Friday, May 6th, 1904
Jozie Rivière of the New York Rebels put the clamps on the opposition and held the Ottawa Indians scoreless in a 4-hit whitewash.

"He's got some good stuff, but we should have done better than that," said Ottawa skipper Nattachai Kantong after the 1-0 loss.

Rivière was on target most of the game and finished with 5 strikeouts and no walks.
