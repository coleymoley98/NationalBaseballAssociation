St-Onge AJC Outstanding Batter of the Month
Friday, August 1st, 1902
Sarawut St-Onge is not used to being associated with Batter of the Month-caliber players, but today the Pawtucket right fielder joined that select company when the Anglo Junior Circuit announced the results of July's voting.

St-Onge totaled 29 hits, 10 walks, 5 home runs, 24 RBIs and 16 runs scored last month to put together a .387 batting average and .495 on-base percentage.

Lifetime St-Onge has connected for 37 home runs, hit .341 and driven in 162 runs.
