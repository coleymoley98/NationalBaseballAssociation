Juniors Boyle, Pitcher of the Year Award Winner
Sunday, August 3rd, 1902
The Anglo Junior Circuit made the official announcement today. The 1902 Anglo Junior Circuit Pitcher of the Year Award has gone to Petcharat Boyle of Fort Wayne.

He had a superlative season, chalking up a 11-2 won-lost mark with an impressive 1.47 ERA.

Boyle logged 14 starts, threw 110.1 innings, yielded 67 hits, struck out 110 and held the opposition to a .171 batting average.

He received 7 first place votes out of a possible 12. Jason Tibbetts of the Morgantown Juniors finished second in voting with 5 first place votes, while Rob Sparkman of the Buffalo Juniors finished third.
