Lybrand Rips 3 Homers Against Juniors
Wednesday, March 11th, 1903
Fans seated in the bleacher section at Field of the Juniors were taking cover all day long as Natipong Lybrand zeroed in on them, rattling three long blasts off the seats in a display of Herculean power.

The big game from the Coude right fielder helped the Juniors take the victory, 12-8, over the Sherbrooke Juniors.

"I told myself not to press on the last one," he told reporters in the postgame press conference. "I knew I couldn't force it -- it has to be a nice easy swing."

For the game, he collected 3 hits in 4 at-bats, drove in 5 and scored 3 runs.

Natipong Lybrand walked in the 2nd, hit a three-run home run off Zachary Jones in the 4th, grounded out in the 6th, hit a solo-shot off Tristan Goudreau in the 8th and hit a solo-shot off Kritsada MacRae in the 9th.

Lybrand is hitting .372 this season with 4 home runs, 14 RBIs and 12 runs scored. He has 16 hits in 43 at-bats.
