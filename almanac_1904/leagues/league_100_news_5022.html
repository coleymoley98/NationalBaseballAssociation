CL Announces Best Batter Award
Wednesday, July 1st, 1903
At only 24 years of age, Roger McLarty could very well be having many more months like the last this in his career.

That would be great news for fans of the New York Rebels, as June was one to remember for the shortstop and he has captured the Canadian League Batter of the Month award.

On the month he hit .345 with no home runs, 4 RBIs and 21 runs scored.

McLarty has collected 76 hits for the Rebels this year, 0 of them home runs. He has also netted 12 RBIs and scored 38 runs while batting .339.
