Boston Twirler Wins CL Top Player Honor
Monday, July 26th, 1897
The Canadian League Player of the Week has been selected. This week the prize went to Phutchapong Sanford of the Boston Redcoats for a very impressive performance. He delivered 2 wins, piled up 18 innings in 2 starts, gave up 12 hits, walked 4, fanned 6 and compiled a 0.50 ERA.

Sanford has posted a 12-11 record for the year with a 4.04 ERA. Teams are hitting .287 against him and he has rung up 52 strikeouts in 216 innings.
