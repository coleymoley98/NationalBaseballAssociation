Albertson Shuts Out Chicagou, 1-0
Tuesday, July 11th, 1899
In an excellent mound performance, James Albertson of the Quebec Nordiques whitewashed the Chicagou Frontenacs 1-0 on a 4-hitter.

"I had my best stuff and did a good job of mixing up my pitches," he said after the game.

In all, the Quebec hurler rang up 4 strikeouts and no walks in the shutout win.

"We just didn't swing the bats well today," said Frontenacs manager Mustafa Campeau. "Albertson did a good job keeping us guessing."

This season Albertson has worked 199.1 innings with 33 strikeouts en route to a 12-8 record and a 2.57 ERA.
