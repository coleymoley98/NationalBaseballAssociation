Roberts Breaks Home Run Record
Wednesday, July 22nd, 1903
With home run number 37, Josh Roberts of the Detroit Lions has set a new all-time record. The previous record was held by Sirisak Winter.

The record-setting home run puts an exclamation point on an exciting pursuit that has captivated fans everywhere. None were more interested in the chase than those in Detroit, and the hometown crowd was rewarded for their devotion by getting to host the historic blast.

"What a wild ride it's been," said Roberts after the game. "I hope now we can get away from the will-he/won't-he stuff and get back to playing baseball."

Josh Roberts walked in the 2nd, grounded out in the 3rd, flied out in the 6th and homered off Val Catteau in the 8th.

Roberts is hitting .304 this year with 4 home runs, 38 RBIs and 29 runs scored. He has 72 hits in 237 at-bats, 24 walks and a .373 on-base percentage. Roberts has played in 67 games.
