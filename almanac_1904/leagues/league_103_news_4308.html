5-Hit Barrage by Brouillette
Monday, March 31st, 1902
Minneapolis left fielder Pierre-Olivier Brouillette had a day to remember today, 5-for-5 against the Gauthier Juniors at Gauthier Grounds as the Juniors picked up a 12-11 victory.

"He was patient up there today," Minneapolis skipper Brien Gilliam told the press after the game. "I like the way he waited for his pitch and wasn't overly aggressive. Brouillette has a tendency to chase bad balls sometimes."

Pierre-Olivier Brouillette singled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, singled in the 7th and hit an RBI double in the 8th.

So far this year, Brouillette is batting .327 with no home runs and 11 RBIs.
