Beadtaku Earns AJC Player of the Week
Monday, June 30th, 1902
It can be difficult for a young player to step out of the shadows and assert himself in the Anglo Junior Circuit, but last week Kiatisak Beadtaku of the Fort Wayne Juniors reminded everyone just why he's here as he threw up numbers that made him an easy selection for Player of the Week. Beadtaku rocked opposing pitching for a .526 average (10-for-19), 1 home run and 2 RBIs to come away with the award.

This year Beadtaku has collected a .407 batting average to date with 11 hits in 27 at-bats, 2 RBIs, 1 home run and scored 7 times. He has played in 12 games.
