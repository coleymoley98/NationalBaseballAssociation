Top Pitcher Award Announced by AJC
Wednesday, August 3rd, 1898
16-year-old Chatchawal Srithong of the Morgantown Juniors put together a good year in 1898 for such a young player. Srithong was named today as the winner of the Anglo Junior Circuit Pitcher of the Year Award.

Srithong crafted an impressive 10-6 record and a 1.98 ERA. In 159.1 innings he allowed 146 hits, struck out 152, and walked 31.

He received 12 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Romie Huynh of the Buffalo Juniors finished second in voting, while Edgar Arleth of the Providence Juniors finished third.
