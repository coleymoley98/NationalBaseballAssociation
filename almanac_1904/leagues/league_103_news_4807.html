Juniors Not Competitive in 8-0 Loss to Gauthier
Sunday, March 22nd, 1903
Wuttikrai Kundrat of the Gauthier Juniors humiliated the Coude Juniors at Gauthier Grounds today, spinning a complete game shutout and holding the visitors to 6 hits.

"He stayed aggressive and his ball was really moving," said Coude manager Cole Siller, after the game.

Kundrat whiffed 5 and walked 1 in the 8-0 win.

"If I make good pitches, I'm going to get some outs," Kundrat said. "I was making good pitches the entire game."

So far this season Kundrat has a 3-4 record with a 4.19 ERA.
