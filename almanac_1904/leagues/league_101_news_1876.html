Catteau Hurls 8-0 Shutout
Tuesday, May 18th, 1897
The Chicagou Frontenacs had no answer today for Quebec Nordiques pitcher Val Catteau. They failed to score a single run against him, losing 8-0. Chicagou managed only 6 hits off Catteau, who had 6 strikeouts.

The extraordinarily popular Catteau got a standing ovation from the hometown crowd as he left the field, the applause continuing until he returned from the dugout to tip his cap to the crowd.

"Nordiques fans are the best fans, in my opinion," he said. "It's a great feeling to throw a shutout under any circumstances but to do it with the crowd behind you like that is just incredible."

Catteau has made 6 starts, tossed 51.2 innings, struck out 25 batters and walked 12. His record is 4-2 with a 2.09 ERA and he has limited other teams to a .226 batting average.
