Méthot Earns CL's Best Pitcher Honor
Tuesday, July 1st, 1902
Nelson Méthot took a much-deserved turn in the spotlight earlier today as he accepted the Canadian League Pitcher of the Month award for June.

The 26-year-old Blue Sox starter is a relative newcomer to the league, but nonetheless racked up an impressive 5-2 won-lost record in 7 starts. This month he fanned 23 batters in 62 innings and compiled a 2.03 ERA.

In 14 starts this season Méthot has rung up a 12-2 record and a 2.04 ERA.
