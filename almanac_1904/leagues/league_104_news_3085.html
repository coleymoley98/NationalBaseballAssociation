AJC Awards Most Valuable Player Award, Boonpen Gets Prize
Friday, August 4th, 1899
Jin Boonpen of the Hartford Juniors had a fine season this year and today he accepted the Most Valuable Player Award for 1899.

The 21-year-old left fielder put up a .363 average and a .430 on-base percentage, piling up 69 hits, 13 doubles, 6 triples, 7 home runs, 46 RBIs and 51 runs scored.

He received 10 first place votes out of a possible 12. Pong Sawangpian of the Springfield Juniors finished second in voting with 2 first place votes, while Mitsuhisa Yamamoto of the Hartford Juniors finished third.
