Champagne Slams 5 Hits for Chicagou
Wednesday, June 26th, 1895
It's not something you read about in the sports pages every day -- a 5-for-6 day. For many, it might come around only once in a career -- and for Theoson Champagne, it came around today.

The 29-year-old center fielder scattered hits all over Montreal Grounds to pace the Chicagou Frontenacs to a 14-5 victory over the Montreal Royales.

Montreal skipper Charles Read told reporters in the press room that "Champagne deserves a hat-tip."

"He hit some good pitches against us," he said. "In fact, according to our scouting reports, Champagne hit several that he is not supposed to be able to hit."

Theoson Champagne hit an RBI single in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, hit a two-run home run off Phinihan Sanphan in the 4th, struck out in the 5th, tripled in the 7th and singled in the 8th.

Presently Champagne is batting .335 and has collected 3 home runs, 48 RBIs, .375 OBP and scored 34 runs.
