Oh Gets August's Top Pitcher Trophy
Monday, September 1st, 1902
Maurice Oh took a much-deserved turn in the spotlight earlier today as he accepted the Canadian League Pitcher of the Month award for August.

The 21-year-old Rebels starter is a relative newcomer to the league, but nonetheless racked up an impressive 5-1 won-lost record in 6 starts. This month he fanned 26 batters in 58.2 innings and compiled a 1.53 ERA.

For the year Oh has a 14-15 record with a 2.66 ERA in 29 starts.
