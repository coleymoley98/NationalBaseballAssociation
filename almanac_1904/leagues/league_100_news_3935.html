Injury Worsens, Return Delayed For Redcoats Gabbidon
Saturday, June 15th, 1901
The Boston Redcoats announced today that Defonte Gabbidon would be missing for longer than expected as the 26-year-old catcher recovers from a torn quad. "Defonte had been working out with the team recently, but felt some discomfort yesterday," the Redcoats GM explained to the press. "After re-examining the injury, the team's medical staff decided that Gabbidon would need extra time to heal. Their estimate is one week more recovery time."

This season Gabbidon is batting .293 with 12 hits, no home runs, 6 RBIs, .370 OBP and has scored 5 runs.
