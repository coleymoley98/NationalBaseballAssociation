New York Twirler Wins CL Best Player Trophy
Monday, August 15th, 1904
One of the most talented pitchers in the Canadian League, Jozie Rivière of the New York Rebels had it all going his way last week and earned the latest Player of the Week honors.

He posted 2 wins and no losses, 8 strikeouts, 18 innings and came away with an excellent 0.50 ERA.

In the current season in 25 starts, Jozie Rivière has a record of 18-5 with 118 strikeouts in 230.2 innings and a 1.91 ERA.
