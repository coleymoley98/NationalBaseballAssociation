NL Announces Rookie of the Month
Wednesday, August 1st, 1900
Donald Licht is beginning to make a name for himself and he did it by posting a 4-4 record and a 3.39 ERA in July. For his spectacular mound performance last month he has been named BNN's Rookie of the Month in the National League.

The 24-year-old newcomer isn't at all intimidated by his lack of experience, at least not outwardly. In fact, by the way he has pitched beyond his years, you could easily mistake him for a veteran.

In 8 starts for the Lions Licht has struck out 13 batters in 63.2 innings, holding opposing hitters to a batting average of .336 during his hot streak.

On his stats sheet for the year Licht shows a 10-6 record, 18 starts, 143.1 innings, 36 strikeouts and a 3.39 ERA.
