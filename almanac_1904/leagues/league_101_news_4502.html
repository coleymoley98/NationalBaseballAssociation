NL's Best Batter Announced
Tuesday, July 1st, 1902
At only 25 years of age, Kevin Joly could very well be having many more months like the last this in his career.

That would be great news for fans of the Detroit Lions, as June was one to remember for the shortstop and he has captured the National League Batter of the Month award.

On the month he hit .400 with no home runs, 13 RBIs and 20 runs scored.

This year Joly has collected a .327 batting average to date with 66 hits in 202 at-bats, 27 RBIs, 1 home run and scored 29 times. He has played in 50 games.
