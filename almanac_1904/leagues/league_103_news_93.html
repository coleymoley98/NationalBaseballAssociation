Tuesday, April 5th, 1904
The Coude Juniors may have proven to be mere mortals today as they lost 5-4 to the Davenport Juniors, but Bowie Moore continued to hit like a god.

The second baseman collected 3 hits in 5 at-bats in the loss and extended his hitting streak to 20 games.

"Moore is playing great and is on fire right now," Juniors manager Cole Siller said. "Hopefully his play can spark the rest of our team and help us pick up some wins."

Moore was apparently not happy with the game and when pressed by reporters in the locker room only said "nothing else matters but the loss."
