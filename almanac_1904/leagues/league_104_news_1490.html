Juniors Pitching Ravaged by Desjardins
Wednesday, July 8th, 1896
Halifax shortstop A.J. Desjardins had a day to remember today, 5-for-6 against the Springfield Juniors at Juniors Ballpark as the Juniors picked up a 12-2 victory.

"He was patient up there today," Halifax skipper Jimmy Rodrigues told the press after the game. "I like the way he waited for his pitch and wasn't overly aggressive. Desjardins has a tendency to chase bad balls sometimes."

A.J. Desjardins doubled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, hit a solo-shot off Jordi Lauzon in the 7th and struck out in the 8th.

Thus far this year Desjardins is hitting .236 with 4 home runs, 18 RBIs and 20 runs scored.
