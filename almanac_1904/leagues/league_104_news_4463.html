Juniors Torpedoed by Pouliot in 2-0 Loss to Fort Wayne
Thursday, June 19th, 1902
Greg Pouliot was in a zone of his own most of the day, shutting out the New Britain Juniors on 4 hits.

"He was too darn good for us today," grumbled New Britain manager Jason Lafrenière after the 2-0 loss to the Fort Wayne Juniors.

Pouliot finished with 10 strikeouts and 2 walks in a strong outing.

In the postgame press conference, Fort Wayne manager Ace Dugandzic said Pouliot "had good velocity, good movement and had those guys off balance. He just pitched his butt off."

To date this year in 6 starts, Pouliot has crafted a 5-0 mark with a 2.41 ERA.
