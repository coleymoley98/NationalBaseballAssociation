Injury Derails Season for O'Sullivan
Thursday, March 5th, 1903
In a news release, the Coude Juniors announced that center fielder Troy O'Sullivan would miss the rest of the season due to a torn posterior cruciate ligament. O'Sullivan sustained the injury on 03/01/1903. He "will be sorely missed," said his manager.
