Juneau Befuddled by Sakowicz in 1-0 Whitewash
Tuesday, August 16th, 1904
The Juneau Brasseurs struggled at the plate today against Nordiques hurler Eugene Sakowicz, who shackled them on 5 hits while hurling a complete game 1-0 shutout.

"What can you say? We didn't hit well," said Brasseurs skipper Owen Turcotte in his postgame comments. "Hopefully we shake this off and bounce back tomorrow."

Sakowicz finished the game with 6 strikeouts and 1 walk in an excellent mound performance.

"He pitched real well," Quebec manager Frédéric Lévesque said. "Eugene made pitches when he had to. He hit his spots and did a great job of mixing in his changeup and using his fastball effectively."

This year Sakowicz has a 4-5 mark in 10 starts with a 2.95 ERA. He has given up 76 hits in 76.1 innings, struck out 44 and issued 13 walks. Opposing clubs have compiled a .260 batting average against him.
