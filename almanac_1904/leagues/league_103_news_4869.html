Carry Heads FJC Best Player List
Wednesday, May 6th, 1903
At the age of 17, Yuthapichai Carry of the Davenport Juniors could very well have his best years ahead of him.

It's a startling thought when you consider the year he had in 1903 was good enough to win the center fielder the Francophone Junior Circuit Most Valuable Player Award.

"See the ball, hit the ball," he said. "That's my basic approach."

The top hitter put up a .350 batting average this year, as well as a .429 on-base percentage. He played 49 games and racked up 64 hits, 14 doubles, 1 triple, 14 home runs, 53 RBIs and 33 runs scored.

He received 6 first place votes out of a possible 12. Bobby Hatakeda of the Juneau Juniors finished second in voting with 5 first place votes, while Weston Woods of the Juneau Juniors finished third with 1 first place vote.
