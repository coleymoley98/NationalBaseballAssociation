Juniors End Pichette's Streak
Monday, April 3rd, 1899
It was a doubly miserable day today for Édouard Pichette of the Quebec City Juniors, as he saw his 28-game hitting streak end and his team lose 8-6 to the Sherbrooke Juniors at Juniors Ballpark. The 17-year-old first baseman was held hitless in 4 at-bats by the Juniors.

"Anyone on a 28-game hitting streak is going to be a tough out," Sherbrooke manager Lance Vandergriff said later. "But we had a plan and we were careful about it, and got it done."

Pichette is presently batting .385 with 4 home runs, 28 runs scored and 21 RBIs. His OBP is .438.
