Oh Wins CL Player of Week Award
Monday, August 22nd, 1904
Maurice Oh drew on his youthful energy and pitching skill to win Canadian League Player of the Week honors last week.

The New York Rebels pitcher registered 2 wins and no losses, 0.00 ERA and 9 strikeouts in 20 innings to sew up the award. The 23-year-old also held opposing hitters to 10 hits and a .149 batting average.

So far in 27 appearances this season, Oh has recorded 24 wins and sustained 3 losses. He has pitched 248 innings, yielded 177 hits, fanned 136 and has a 0.94 ERA.
