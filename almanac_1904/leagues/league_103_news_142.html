Signoret of Sainte-Paul Named Best Pitcher
Thursday, May 5th, 1904
The 1904 Francophone Junior Circuit Pitcher of the Year Award winner is Stephan Signoret of the Sainte-Paul Juniors.

He started 14 games and compiled a 7-3 record with an ERA of 1.23 in 109.1 innings with 133 strikeouts.

He received 11 first place votes out of a possible 12. Jimmy Michell of the Juneau Juniors finished second in voting, while Cedric Audet of the Davenport Juniors finished third with 1 first place vote.
