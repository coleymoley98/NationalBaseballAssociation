100 Victories for Quebec's James Albertson
Friday, May 20th, 1898
Nordiques ace James Albertson reeled in his 100th career victory by beating the Chicagou Frontenacs 4-1 at Ballpark of Quebec. In his historic win, he notched 5 strikeouts in 9 innings, yielded 1 run and 7 hits, while surrendering no walks.

"I tried to focus on each batter," said Albertson after the game, "but as the game goes by, I couldn't help but think about the possibility. 100 is a big number. It's an exclusive club and I'm honored to be a member."

Albertson has a won-lost record of 100 wins and 38 losses with 387 strikeouts and a lifetime 2.81 ERA.
