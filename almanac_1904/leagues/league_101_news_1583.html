Nordiques Take It on Chin, Lose, 18-0, to Detroit
Sunday, August 9th, 1896
Jaafson Gillet of the Detroit Lions was masterful today, pitching a 2-hit shutout, 18-0, against the Quebec Nordiques.

By going the distance, he was at the same time able to give his bullpen a rest, a fact appreciated by Detroit manager Enzo Kelly.

"It's nice to have someone who can do that from time to time," he said. "He had command of every ball and every pitch. Great location, too."

Gillet had 1 strikeout and 1 walk in the outing.

In 32 starts this year Gillet has recorded a 17-12 record and a 3.24 ERA.
