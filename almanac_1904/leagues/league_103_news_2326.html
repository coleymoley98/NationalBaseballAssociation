FJC's Premier Player for 1898 Named
Friday, May 6th, 1898
Thomas Desrosiers took Francophone Junior Circuit by storm this year, and he did it all at the age of 16.

For his spectacular campaign, the Drummondville Juniors star has been named the 1898 Francophone Junior Circuit Most Valuable Player Award recipient.

"To be considered amongst the best in the league is overwhelming," said the left fielder.

On the season he compiled a .356 batting average to go with a .451 on-base percentage. The native of Chicoutimi, The United Provinces also added 14 doubles, 1 triple, 11 home runs, 41 RBIs and 34 runs scored.

He received 12 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Mikael Béguin of the Sainte-Paul Juniors finished second in voting, while Trakoolchat Phomee of the Coude Juniors finished third.
