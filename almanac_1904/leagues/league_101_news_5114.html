NL Honors July's #1 Pitcher
Saturday, August 1st, 1903
Veteran Komkrit Allard put together quite a successful stretch in July for the Detroit Lions, earning himself the National League Pitcher of the Month award.

Allard racked up a 5-2 record in 7 starts, posting a 1.92 ERA over 65.2 innings and striking out 30.

So far this season Allard has a record of 9-10 with 73 strikeouts and 44 walks in 180 innings and a 2.40 ERA.
