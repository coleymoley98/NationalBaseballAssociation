NL News: Draft Pool Revealed
Saturday, August 7th, 1897
National League officials have today announced the list of players eligible for the 1897 first-year player draft. Teams now have a chance to scout the draft pool for 90 days before the draft is held.

Also, the Transactions page gets a new section dedicated to the pool.

The early favorites to be high picks are the following:
1. William Davison, 21 year-old SS out of college.
2. Seth DiClemente, 18 year-old RF out of college.
3. A.J. Desjardins, 21 year-old SS out of college.
4. Pete Cannatelli, 21 year-old RHP out of college.
5. Muangkan Fawley, 18 year-old LF out of college.
6. Jonathan Johnston, 20 year-old RHP out of college.
7. Kevin Joly, 20 year-old SS out of college.
8. Kittipong Jones, 21 year-old LHP out of college.
9. Prayad Windsor, 18 year-old C out of college.
10. Fabrice Hardy, 21 year-old CF out of college.

The official OSA Mock Draft will be released on the day of the draft, or you can review the draft pool on the draft page.
