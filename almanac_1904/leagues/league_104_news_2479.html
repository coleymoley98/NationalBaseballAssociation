Bossicar Hits in 25 Consecutive Games
Wednesday, July 6th, 1898
The streak is alive and well -- at least for today.

Suriyakarn Bossicar did it again, bashing 2 hits in the contest at Ballpark of Buffalo today. For the last 25 games in a row, the 20-year-old center fielder has collected at least one hit. His efforts helped the Buffalo Juniors beat the Pawtucket Juniors 15-11 today.

"I'm happy I kept the streak going," Bossicar told a standing-room-only crowd of reporters gathered in the Buffalo clubhouse. "Honestly though, I'm looking forward to going home and not thinking about this for the rest of the day."
Suriyakarn Bossicar grounded out in the 1st, flied out in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, walked in the 7th and hit an RBI single in the 7th.

This season Bossicar is hitting .413 with 62 hits, 10 home runs, 43 RBIs, scored 44 runs and played in 33 games.
