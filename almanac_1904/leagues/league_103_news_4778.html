Going, Going, Gone! Miron Hits 3 Homers
Wednesday, March 4th, 1903
It was a good day for the Davenport Juniors and an exceptional one for Chanin Miron. The 17-year-old first baseman sent three bombs out of the park to help the Juniors beat the Sherbrooke Juniors 10-8 at Field of the Juniors.

"When you're in the zone, the game really slows down," Miron said when asked about his performance later. "I couldn't even tell you what pitches were thrown. Today feels great, but it is a long season and tomorrow is another day."

For the day, he scored 3 times, drove in 5 runs and smacked 3 hits in 5 at-bats.

Chanin Miron flied out in the 3rd, hit a two-run home run off Zachary Jones in the 5th, struck out in the 6th, hit a solo-shot off Tristan Goudreau in the 8th and hit a two-run home run off Jake Desjardins in the 9th.
