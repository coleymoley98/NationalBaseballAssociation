White 5-for-5 for Juniors
Wednesday, April 8th, 1903
It doesn't happen very often, but it did for Justin White today -- a 5-hit effort. He went on a rampage at Coude Grounds and paced the Juniors to a 9-3 win over the Drummondville Juniors.

After his dynamite batting performance, the Coude left fielder told reporters that he "just tried to get in a good count and have a good at-bat. That's all I can control."

His philosophy paid dividends today. He slammed 5 hits in 5 tries and put his name in the record book. Justin White hit an RBI single in the 1st, doubled in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, hit an RBI single in the 5th and doubled in the 8th.

This season White is batting .295 with 15 RBIs, 24 runs scored and 2 home runs.
