Juneau's Seals Gets Pitcher of the Year Award
Tuesday, May 5th, 1896
This year there was no one better on the mound than Reuban Seals. Posting 14 wins and a 1.71 ERA was only part of the story for the Juneau superstar. For his outstanding performance in 1896 he was chosen as the Francophone Junior Circuit Pitcher of the Year Award winner.

From start to finish Seals never let up. In 17 starts he recorded 14 wins and 1 loss, 126.2 innings, 153 strikeouts, 21 walks, yielded 78 hits, and permitted opposing batters to hit .170 against him.

He received 12 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Watcharapon Chevrier of the Coude Juniors finished second in voting, while Jatupron Margery of the Montreal Juniors finished third.
