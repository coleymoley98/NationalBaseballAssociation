CL Selects Rookie of the Month
Thursday, September 1st, 1904
Few players his age have the consistency to put a solid month together, but Wolfeburg's Lincoln Kellar carved up the opposition all August long and hauled off the Canadian League Rookie of the Month award today as a result.

He sizzled last month with a 4-2 mark, fanning 20 in 51 innings with an ERA of 1.59 in 6 starts.

So far this season Kellar has a 13-10 record, 23 starts, 205.1 innings, 82 strikeouts and a 4.21 ERA.
