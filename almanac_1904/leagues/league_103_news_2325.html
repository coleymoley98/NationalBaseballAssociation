Rosa Chosen FJC's Best Pitcher
Thursday, May 5th, 1898
He's in the early phase of his career, but this past season Cy Rosa performed like a veteran on the hill for the Drummondville Juniors and walked away with the Francophone Junior Circuit Pitcher of the Year Award in the process.

The 16-year-old Juniors star fashioned a 13-6 won-loss record in 1898 with a 1.48 ERA. In 164 innings of work and 23 starts, Rosa struck out 185 while giving up 117 hits and 27 walks. He held opposing batters to a .196 average.

He received 12 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Watcharapon Chevrier of the Coude Juniors finished second in voting, while Joshua Ouellet of the Gallant Juniors finished third.
