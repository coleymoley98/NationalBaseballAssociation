Westbrook Batters Philadelphia Big Time
Tuesday, July 28th, 1903
Boston right fielder Charong Westbrook could do no wrong in today's 12-10 win over the Liberty as he collected 5 hits.

"Things were in his favor in every one of his at-bats," Liberty skipper Tyrone Martin said to the press corps after the game. "He got hitters' counts."

Westbrook agreed, saying that getting ahead early in the counts was "the key" to his game.

"Once you get ahead, it makes it a lot easier because you get a better pitch to hit," he said. "You know the pitcher has to come in with it. And you're ready."

Charong Westbrook singled in the 1st, flied out in the 3rd, hit an RBI double in the 5th, doubled in the 6th, singled in the 8th and singled in the 10th.

In his 1903 season totals, Westbrook has no home runs, .363 BA, .402 OBP, 17 RBIs and 16 runs scored.
