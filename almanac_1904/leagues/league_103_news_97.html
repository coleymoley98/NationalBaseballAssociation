Higgins Sticks it to FJC Pitchers
Monday, April 11th, 1904
It can be difficult for a young player to step out of the shadows and assert himself in the Francophone Junior Circuit, but last week Trayvon Higgins of the Gauthier Juniors reminded everyone just why he's here as he threw up numbers that made him an easy selection for Player of the Week. Higgins rocked opposing pitching for a .714 average (15-for-21), 3 home runs and 10 RBIs to come away with the award.

This season Higgins is hitting at a .371 pace with 6 home runs, 18 RBIs and has scored 18 times.
