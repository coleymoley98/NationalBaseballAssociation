Ho Goes Hitless, Streak Stopped at 22
Saturday, June 1st, 1895
After 22 games, Fort Wayne shortstop Prateep Ho saw his hitting streak end when he went hitless in 2 at-bats. However, the Juniors still managed to take the win, topping the Buffalo Juniors 10-0 at Ballpark of Buffalo.

"It was a good run," Ho said after the game. "It was bound to end at some point. I'm just glad we won the day it ended."
