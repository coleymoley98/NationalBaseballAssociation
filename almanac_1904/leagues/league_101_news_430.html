NL News: Draft Pool Revealed
Sunday, August 7th, 1904
National League today released the final list of all players eligible for next month's rookie draft. Now that the list has been made official, close scrutiny of these players can be expected as scouts begin to review this year's up and coming talent.

BNN has added to its Transactions page a section dedicated to the draft pool, which will be available 30 days prior to draft day.

The early favorites to be high picks are the following:
1. Rungarun Inglis, 20 year-old 3B out of college.
2. Robin Waggoner, 18 year-old RHP out of college.
3. Jimmy Michell, 19 year-old RHP out of college.
4. William Thériault, 18 year-old RHP out of college.
5. Panupan McFadden, 18 year-old RHP out of college.
6. Saksit Diana, 20 year-old LHP out of college.
7. Abraham Love, 21 year-old RF out of college.
8. Alair Givens, 18 year-old RHP out of college.
9. Nicolas Messier, 20 year-old 3B out of college.
10. Arnon Wood, 18 year-old LHP out of college.

The official OSA Mock Draft will be released on the day of the draft, or you can review the draft pool on the draft page.
