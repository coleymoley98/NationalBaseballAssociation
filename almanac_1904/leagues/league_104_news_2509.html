Rivera Blisters Providence
Sunday, July 17th, 1898
There are moments in a player's career that they will not soon forget.

Von Rivera of the Canton Juniors had one such moment at Juniors Ballpark today, when he went 5 for 5 at the plate. His hitting heroics weren't enough to pull out the victory, however, and Canton fell to a 8-7 defeat to the Providence Juniors.

"This was quite a day for me individually, but a little disheartening at the same time," the 20-year-old center fielder told reporters in the postgame interview. "It would have been very special if we had won the game."

Von Rivera singled in the 2nd, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 6th, singled in the 7th and hit a three-run home run off Koty Carswell in the 9th.

So far this season Rivera is hitting .376 with 12 home runs, 35 RBIs and 42 runs scored. He has 68 hits in 181 at-bats and a .415 OBP.
