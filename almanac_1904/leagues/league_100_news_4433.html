Fawley Wins CL Player of the Week Trophy
Monday, June 9th, 1902
One of the league's most exciting young players, Muangkan Fawley of the New York Rebels earned himself Canadian League Player of the Week honors with a standout performance in last week's games.

This season in 35 games Fawley is batting .372 with 1 home run, 11 RBIs and has scored 22 times.
