Anglo Junior Circuit Announces July's Best Batter
Tuesday, August 1st, 1899
Even though he is a newcomer to Anglo Junior Circuit, Matteo Talbot put up some good numbers in July and earned the AJC Batter of the Month award.

He was one of Hartford's offensive leaders with a .333 average in 22 games. He also racked up 6 home runs, 20 RBIs, and 26 runs scored.

In 240 career games Talbot has hit .288 with 262 hits, 21 home runs, 165 RBIs and scored 163 runs.
