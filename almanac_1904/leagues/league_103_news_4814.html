FJC Star of the Week Announced

Pierre-Olivier Brouillette is respected by most Minneapolis Juniors fans for his no-frills style of play. The 18-year-old right fielder went about his business with vigor last week to grab the Francophone Junior Circuit Player of the Week honors.

He compiled a .667 average (12-for-18) with no home runs, 4 RBIs and 2 runs scored.
