Francophone Junior Circuit Pitcher of the Year Named
Friday, May 5th, 1899
Thiang Bourque had a terrific 1899 season in the Francophone Junior Circuit. The Gauthier pitching stalwart received the most votes and has been proclaimed this year's Pitcher of the Year Award winner.

The Juniors ace starred with an impressive 9-2 record. In 17 starts, he gave up 76 base hits and struck out 123 in 110 innings with a 0.98 ERA. Opposing teams hit .185 against him.

He received 11 first place votes out of a possible 12. Jesse Langlois of the Drummondville Juniors finished second in voting, while Hafit Che-moh of the Juneau Juniors finished third with 1 first place vote.
