Wales Goes on Rampage Against Rebels
Monday, July 23rd, 1900
It doesn't happen very often, but it did for Tex Wales today -- a 5-hit effort. He went on a rampage at Polo Grounds and paced the Redcoats to a 7-5 win over the New York Rebels.

After his dynamite batting performance, the Boston shortstop told reporters that he "just tried to get in a good count and have a good at-bat. That's all I can control."

His philosophy paid dividends today. He slammed 5 hits in 5 tries and put his name in the record book. Tex Wales singled in the 2nd, hit a two-run double in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, singled in the 7th and singled in the 9th.

To date Wales has chalked up a .240 average with 1 home run, driven in 32 runs and scored 32 times.
