Arleth Dominates, Whitewashes Liberty
Thursday, July 25th, 1901
Edgar Arleth had the Philadelphia Liberty dancing to his tune today with a sparkling 2-0 shutout performance for the Boston Redcoats. He gave up 5 hits, issued 1 walk and had 4 strikeouts.

Philadelphia hitters were bewildered throughout the game by his delivery.

To date Arleth is 14-7 in 21 starts with a 2.10 ERA and has 87 strikeouts in 180.1 innings.
