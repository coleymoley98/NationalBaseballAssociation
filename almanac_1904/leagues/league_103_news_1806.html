Oh Tames Juniors 5-1, 17 Ks
Thursday, April 1st, 1897
Sainte-Paul Juniors starter Maurice Oh brought his best stuff to Field of the Juniors today against the Juneau Juniors, striking out 17 batters in his club's 5-1 victory.

In a strong performance, the 16-year-old Oh gave up 4 hits in his 8.2 innings of work and issued 2 walks.

"I felt good out there," he said after the game. "My fastball had a lot of life and my forkball was sharp. It was giving hitters fits and working well for me."

This year Oh has a 6-3 mark in 10 starts with a 2.21 ERA. He has given up 68 hits in 77.1 innings, struck out 80 and issued 18 walks. Opposing clubs have compiled a .224 batting average against him.
