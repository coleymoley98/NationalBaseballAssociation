5-Hit Blitz by Gagné
Wednesday, June 29th, 1904
5 hits in a game is generally considered a noteworthy achievement, but for Paitoon Gagné, it felt rather hollow.

The 16-year-old went 5-for-5 at the plate at Juniors Ballpark today, but his Canton Juniors fell to the Springfield Juniors, 5-4.

"It's hard to get too excited," the shortstop told BNN following the game. "After a game like this, everyone's quiet and they just want to go home."

Paitoon Gagné singled in the 2nd, hit an RBI single in the 4th, singled in the 6th, singled in the 8th and singled in the 10th.

Gagné is currently batting .267 with no home runs, 5 RBIs and 7 runs scored. He has 24 hits in 90 at-bats.
