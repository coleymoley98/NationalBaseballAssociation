Sunday, July 7th, 1901
Springfield shortstop Justin Toppin came into today's game against the Hartford Juniors on a hot streak. He left on a cold one.

The Juniors did their jobs well, beating the Springfield Juniors 3-1 and holding Toppin hitless to snap his 23-game hitting streak. The 20-year-old shortstop had a few words to say after finishing 0-for-4, telling reporters only that he "came up short."
