CL Best Rookie Award Announced
Wednesday, June 1st, 1898
For an old man, in baseball terms, 31-year-old Jaafson Gillet has been mighty fortunate to get to the big leagues and have the opportunity to show what he can do. In May the battle-weary newcomer finally made a splash as he won the Canadian League Rookie of the Month Award today.

He posted a 4-6 record with 20 strikeouts and an ERA of 1.95 in 87.2 innings.

Baseball writers for whom he was an afterthought earlier are now scrambling to put out stories about Toronto's Ye Olde Rookie.
