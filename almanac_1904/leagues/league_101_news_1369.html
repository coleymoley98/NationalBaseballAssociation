Wednesday, May 27th, 1896
Chicagou lost to the Quebec Nordiques 4-2 at Ballpark of Quebec today, but Charles Bergeron kept his consecutive-game hitting streak alive with 1 hit. The Frontenacs second baseman has now delivered hits in 20 straight games.
