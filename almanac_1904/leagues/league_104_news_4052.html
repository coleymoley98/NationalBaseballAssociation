It's Official -- Buffalo Fires Jones as Manager
Saturday, July 27th, 1901
The Buffalo Juniors have ended their relationship with skipper Ratipong Jones, firing him this afternoon.

The Juniors indicated they will soon be interviewing candidates for the position.

"This time we hope to get someone with a proven track record. We want an experienced manager and will take our time in getting one," general manager Joe Unknown told reporters.

"No hard feelings," Jones said. "This is just part of the game. Buffalo gave me an opportunity and I appreciate it. They treated me well and fairly. I just wish we could have done better."
