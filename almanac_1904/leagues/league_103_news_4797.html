Monday, March 16th, 1903
It wasn't easy but the Gauthier Juniors made it look that way.

Starter Wuttikrai Kundrat shut out the Minneapolis Juniors despite having what he later called "sub-par" stuff, and the Gauthier Juniors cruised to victory, 1-0. Kundrat allowed only 6 hits and rang up 10 strikeouts but insisted later that he did not deserve primary credit for the win.
