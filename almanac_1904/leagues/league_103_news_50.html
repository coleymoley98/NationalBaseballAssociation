Fréchette Bashes 5 Base Hits
Wednesday, March 9th, 1904
Logan Fréchette was at the head of a Sherbrooke hit parade today at Gauthier Grounds. The first baseman went to the plate 6 times and came away with 5 hits, sparking the Juniors to a 12-8 victory over the Gauthier Juniors.

"I told the guys in the dugout, just be thinking about hitting the ball hard somewhere," the sweet-swinging Fréchette told reporters after the game. "Seems like it worked out well for us today."

Juniors skipper Ti Holley said later that the club gave up "too much plate."

"Not the way we planned it," he said. "Every hitter in the league will rip you when you do that."

Logan Fréchette singled in the 1st, hit a two-run single in the 2nd, grounded out in the 5th, hit a solo-shot off Kraison Ruder in the 7th, singled in the 8th, hit a two-run home run off Adam Jones in the 10th, walked in the 12th and walked in the 14th.

Fréchette is currently batting .250 with 2 home runs, 6 RBIs and 4 runs scored. He has 10 hits in 40 at-bats.
