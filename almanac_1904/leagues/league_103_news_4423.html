Roche shines in winning 1902 Francophone Junior Circuit HR Challenge
Monday, June 2nd, 1902
Juniors Ballpark was the site of the 1902 Home Run Challenge. This year, Randolph Roche beat a group of elite sluggers to win the event. In the final round, the Gallant Juniors catcher went mano a mano against Brahim Da Fonseca, defeating him by a score of 12-11.

"I expected Brahim to give me his best shot," said Roche. "I had just enough good swings to beat him."
