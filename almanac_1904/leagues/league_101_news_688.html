Montreal-Toronto Trade: Brackett for White
Tuesday, February 26th, 1895
It likely won't turn either team into an instant contender or provide a huge franchise shakeup, but the Montreal Royales and Toronto Blue Sox finalized a trade today. Montreal will send 27-year-old 1B Evan Brackett and 22-year-old LF Caleb Potvin to the Blue Sox, while the Royales will receive 40-year-old C Dylan White.

"We think this is a deal that will help out both teams and we're glad to get it done," Royales general manager Ralph Schrimp said.

The trade is expected to be officially completed later today with the players joining their new ballclubs by the end of the day.

Toronto will retain a portion of White's remaining contract.
