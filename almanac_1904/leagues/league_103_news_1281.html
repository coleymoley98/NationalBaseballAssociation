Red-Hot March Lands Béguin Batting Prize in FJC
Wednesday, April 1st, 1896
It's perhaps an award he'll win many more times through his career, as at age 17 Mikael Béguin has a lot of baseball ahead of him.

But for now the young Sainte-Paul Juniors star can enjoy the numbers he put up in March, which have garnered him the nod as the Francophone Junior Circuit Batter of the Month.

His breakout month featured a .434 average with 5 home runs, 29 RBIs and 28 runs scored.
