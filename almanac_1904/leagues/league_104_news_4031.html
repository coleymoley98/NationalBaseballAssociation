Injury Brings Season to End for Dostie
Saturday, July 20th, 1901
The news was not unexpected, but that didn't make it easier to accept for the Canton Juniors: starting pitcher Nick Dostie is out for the year. Dostie suffered a damaged elbow ligament on 07/19/1901. The team knew that the chances of seeing him return were slim to none. But today it was made official.

This season Dostie has compiled a 2-8 mark, 2.48 ERA and made 12 starts.
