34 Games in a Row for Tung
Thursday, March 7th, 1895
Maxwell Tung of the Minneapolis Juniors has now hit safely in 34 straight games. He socked 3 hits in today's 12-0 triumph over Quebec City at Juniors Ballpark.

"34 games is a big chunk of the year, isn't it?" Tung said to reporters. "It's hard to wrap your head around."
