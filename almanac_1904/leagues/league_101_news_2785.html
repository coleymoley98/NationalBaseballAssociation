Detroit Lions Head Scout Wilson Mise Hoping for New Contract
Thursday, March 9th, 1899
Saying he thinks the table is set for the Detroit Lions to have a long run of success, longtime head scout Wilson Mise is hoping to stay on board with the club.

"While we haven't come to a new contract agreement for the coming year, I am really hoping that we can," Mise said. "This organization is really well run and I believe with the prospects coming up in the system we are on the cusp of a lot of success."
