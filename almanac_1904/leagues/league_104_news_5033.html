Injury Brings Season to End for Rowlett
Saturday, July 4th, 1903
After several days of waiting for test results, bad news was delivered to Lloyd Rowlett today. The Providence left fielder will be out the rest of the year due to a ruptured medial collateral ligament he suffered on 06/30/1903.

This year Rowlett is hitting .359 with 2 home runs and 17 RBIs in 27 games played. He also boasts an OBP of .432 with 11 walks and 16 runs scored.
