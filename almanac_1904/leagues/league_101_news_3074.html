NL Presents Batter of Month Trophy to Boehm
Tuesday, August 1st, 1899
For a rookie just breaking in, Rocky Boehm certainly put together a successful month. It was good enough to win the NL Batter of the Month award for July.

The 28-year-old second baseman for the Quebec Nordiques hit .351 with 2 home runs, 15 RBIs and 29 runs scored.

This season Boehm is hitting .346 with 75 hits, 5 home runs, 39 RBIs, scored 51 runs and played in 60 games.
