CL News: Draft Pool Revealed
Monday, August 7th, 1899
Canadian League officials have today announced the list of players eligible for the 1899 first-year player draft. Teams now have a chance to scout the draft pool for 90 days before the draft is held.

Also, the Transactions page gets a new section dedicated to the pool.

The early favorites to be high picks are the following:
1. Benjamin Boucher, 21 year-old SS out of college.
2. Pong Sawangpian, 21 year-old SS out of college.
3. Maurice Oh, 18 year-old RHP out of college.
4. Winfred Winters, 21 year-old SS out of college.
5. Cy Rosa, 17 year-old LHP out of college.
6. Edgar Arleth, 21 year-old RHP out of college.
7. Thomas Robinson, 17 year-old 3B out of college.
8. Ferdinand Dupont, 18 year-old C out of college.
9. Thomas Desrosiers, 17 year-old LF out of college.
10. Arran Weber, 17 year-old RHP out of college.

The official OSA Mock Draft will be released on the day of the draft, or you can review the draft pool on the draft page.
