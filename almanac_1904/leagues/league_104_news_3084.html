Dostie Grabs AJC Pitcher of the Year Award
Thursday, August 3rd, 1899
18-year-old Nick Dostie of the Canton Juniors put together a good year in 1899 for such a young player. Dostie was named today as the winner of the Anglo Junior Circuit Pitcher of the Year Award.

Dostie crafted an impressive 6-5 record and a 1.22 ERA. In 103 innings he allowed 83 hits, struck out 107, and walked 27.

He received 8 first place votes out of a possible 12. Edgar Arleth of the Providence Juniors finished second in voting with 4 first place votes, while Milton Strong of the Toronto Juniors finished third.
