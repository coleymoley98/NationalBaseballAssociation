August's Best Rookie Honored by NL
Tuesday, September 1st, 1903
Yes, Lance Brintle is just a rookie. But he is a special rookie. Today the 24-year-old Juneau starter was awarded the National League Rookie of the Month trophy for his standout performance in August.

He dominated the rookie class, registering 4 wins and 2 losses in 6 starts with 18 strikeouts in 51 innings and a 2.65 ERA.

Not bad for a first-year man.

In season stats Brintle shows a 13-12 record in 27 games, 230.2 innings, 75 strikeouts and a 3.47 ERA.
