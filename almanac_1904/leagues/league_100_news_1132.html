CL News: Winter Meetings Begin
Thursday, October 10th, 1895
An annual rite of passage in Canadian League, the winter meetings signify membership in an exclusive fraternity of men. Some have led clubs to glory, others aspire to. All are here to discuss the latest baseball happenings, to define the offseason with that big signing or trade that could bring victory home. For three days you will watch, learn and help mold the upcoming season. Be ready, but don't be hasty.

This is also a reminder for teams to set their ticket prices for next season. Prices cannot change after season tickets go on sale at the end of the Winter Meetings.
