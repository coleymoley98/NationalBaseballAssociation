Montreal's Shepperd Chosen NL Batter of the Month
Wednesday, June 1st, 1904
A.J. Shepperd of the Montreal Royales spoke softly and carried a big stick in May and was justifiably proclaimed the National League Batter of the Month.

The Royales first baseman rapped out a .368 batting average with 35 hits in 95 at-bats, drove in 11, scored 12 runs and chalked up a .396 on-base percentage.
