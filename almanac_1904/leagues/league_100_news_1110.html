Today is the first day of the free agency period.

With the arrival of free agency comes the opportunity for each club across Canadian League to propel themselves into the playoffs simply by making a few shrewd, well-calculated signings. General managers have to choose wisely where to spend their dollars. An expensive signing could be the key to a playoff spot or it could handcuff a franchise for years to come.
