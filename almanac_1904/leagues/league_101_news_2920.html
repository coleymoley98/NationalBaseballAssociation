Monday, June 5th, 1899
Jayden Queen drew on his youthful energy and pitching skill to win National League Player of the Week honors last week.

The Montreal Royales pitcher registered 3 wins and no losses, 1.33 ERA and 8 strikeouts in 27 innings to sew up the award. The 23-year-old also held opposing hitters to 26 hits and a .250 batting average.

Checking out his stats for the year, Queen has racked up 5 wins and no losses in 4 starts, 2.56 ERA and struck out 15 batters in 45.2 innings.
