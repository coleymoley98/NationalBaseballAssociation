Toronto Trades Connor for New York's Long
Saturday, July 15th, 1899
BNN has learned the Toronto Blue Sox have reportedly worked out a deal with the New York Rebels. Sources close to the Blue Sox indicated that a trade has been reached in principle where 33-year-old LF Teodor Long will be swapped to Toronto with 34-year-old 2B Siwakorn Connor, 23-year-old 3B Jayse Atkinson and 20-year-old SS Bunnawat Gresh heading to New York. Neither organization would comment on the report, saying it was club policy not to speak about a trade until it was finalized.

New York will retain a portion of Long's remaining contract.

In his 181-game career Connor has tagged 190 hits with 23 doubles, 4 triples and 5 home runs. His lifetime batting average is .321 with 96 RBIs and 61 runs scored.

So far this season Long is hitting .318 with 1 home run, 11 RBIs and 17 runs scored. He has 28 hits in 88 at-bats and a .423 OBP.
