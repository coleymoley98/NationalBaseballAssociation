Roger McLarty Named Best CL Batter in July
Thursday, August 1st, 1901
At only 23 years of age, Roger McLarty could very well be having many more months like the last this in his career.

That would be great news for fans of the New York Rebels, as July was one to remember for the shortstop and he has captured the Canadian League Batter of the Month award.

On the month he hit .365 with no home runs, 21 RBIs and 14 runs scored.

In the 1901 season McLarty is hitting .314 with no home runs.
