Juniors-Juniors Fight Nets 4-Game Suspensions
Thursday, March 18th, 1897
At a recent Juniors-Juniors contest at Field of the Juniors, Newin Prariwanta of Sherbrooke took exception to what Pierre-André Pelletier of Drummondville said to him and tackled him. This led to an old-fashioned bench-clearing free-for-all between the two clubs. After the umpires restored order, they ejected both players.

Today the league commissioner's office took action and suspended each player for 4 games, citing them for "inappropriate and aggressive conduct."

This season Prariwanta has hit 1 home run and posted a .232 batting average.

Pelletier has taken the mound for 6 relief appearances this season, nailing down no saves while notching a 0-0 record with a 6.52 ERA. The Drummondville reliever has limited opposing batters to a .268 batting average.
