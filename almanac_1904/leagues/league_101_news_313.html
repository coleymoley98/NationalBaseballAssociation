Detroit Starter Named NL's Premier Pitcher
Friday, July 1st, 1904
Kittipong Jones of Detroit excelled on the mound in June with a 6-1 record and was named the Pitcher of the Month in the National League.

Jones restricted opposing batters to 48 base hits and a .209 batting average. He rang up 33 strikeouts in 62 innings of work, surrendered 12 walks, and fashioned a 1.16 ERA.

So far this year Jones has compiled a 10-2 record with a 1.08 ERA.
