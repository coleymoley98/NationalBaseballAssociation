Jones Gives Quebec Goose Eggs
Tuesday, July 21st, 1903
It was a dismal game for the Quebec Nordiques with Kittipong Jones on the hill. The Detroit Lions starter was in top form and made it a long day for Quebec with a 1-0 shutout. He surrendered a total of 8 hits and piled up 2 strikeouts to pick up the win in a rout.

"No offense to the Nordiques but that was a cakewalk today," Jones said to BNN reporters following the win.

This season-to-date, Jones has fashioned a 11-7 mark and 2.42 ERA in 167.1 innings. Opposing clubs are batting .258 against him and he has logged 62 strikeouts.
