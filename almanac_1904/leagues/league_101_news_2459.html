Montreal's Heemkhao Collects June Batting Honor
Friday, July 1st, 1898
Prasarn Heemkhao earned June's Batter of the Month honors in the National League by reeling off a .380 average, going 41 for 108 with 2 home runs, 17 RBIs and 22 runs scored.

The soon-to-be free agent was hitting with great urgency in an effort to bolster his asking price in the Montreal clubhouse and around the rest of the league.

Thus far this season Heemkhao is hitting .304 with 2 home runs, 28 RBIs and 34 runs scored. He has 65 hits in 214 at-bats and has played in 56 games.
