Boston Trades Wymore for Detroit's Strother
Friday, February 15th, 1901
BNN is reporting a rumored trade between the Boston Redcoats and Detroit Lions has been finalized. The deal, which is expected to be announced sometime during the next few hours, will reportedly send 32-year-old C Alex Wymore to Detroit in return for 25-year-old SS Paul Strother. The clubs were noncommittal on the trade, but did not deny it.


Wymore has put up a career batting average of .309 with 549 hits, 16 homers and 282 RBIs.
