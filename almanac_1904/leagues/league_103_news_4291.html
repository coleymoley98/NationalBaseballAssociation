Injury Brings Season to End for Giesel
Wednesday, March 19th, 1902
"Not what I wanted to hear," said Sainte-Paul's Nattawut Giesel to reporters this afternoon, as he revealed that the injury he sustained on 03/16/1902 would force him to miss the rest of the season. "The doctor made it clear," Giesel said. "There's no chance for me -- none at all -- to come back this year. That's the last thing I wanted to hear." Giesel has been out of action since he suffered a fractured ankle.

Giesel on the season is hitting .176 with no home runs. His stats include 4 RBIs and 6 runs scored.
