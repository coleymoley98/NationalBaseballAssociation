Catteau Whitewashes St. Louis
Tuesday, May 31st, 1904
The Quebec Nordiques rolled to an easy 5-0 win over the St. Louis Cardinaux at Ballpark of Quebec today. Leading the way to victory was starter Val Catteau, who shackled the St. Louis Cardinaux and held them scoreless on a 3-hitter.

"This is what every pitcher wants to do when they go out there," Catteau said in his postgame press conference.

Up to now Catteau has compiled an ERA of 2.60 with a 4-4 mark over 8 starts. He has 28 strikeouts in 72.2 innings and has held opponents to a .237 batting average.
