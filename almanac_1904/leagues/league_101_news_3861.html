#150 for Quebec Pitcher James Albertson
Friday, May 10th, 1901
Quebec's James Albertson took the hill at Ballpark of Quebec today in search of his 150th career win. The 31-year-old ace found it against the Detroit Lions.

After the historic victory, his manager told reporters, "I saw a tremendous sigh of relief when this thing was over. He was just looking to hug everyone in sight."

He worked 9 innings with no strikeouts and no walks in the 8-5 victory, checking Detroit on 9 hits.

"It a big milestone in my career," admitted Albertson. "When you get close to it, you worry about it a lot. It's done now... and I can relax and enjoy it."

Albertson has a lifetime ERA of 2.89 with a record of 150 wins and 86 losses.
