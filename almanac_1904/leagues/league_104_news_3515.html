Fans seated in the bleacher section at Hartford Grounds were taking cover all day long as Dylan Cutwright zeroed in on them, rattling three long blasts off the seats in a display of Herculean power.

The big game from the Buffalo right fielder helped the Juniors take the victory, 11-8, over the Hartford Juniors.

"I told myself not to press on the last one," he told reporters in the postgame press conference. "I knew I couldn't force it -- it has to be a nice easy swing."
