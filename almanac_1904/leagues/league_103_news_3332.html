Watts and Thongman Suspended for Free-for-All
Sunday, April 15th, 1900
If there wasn't a history between Marvin Watts of Quebec City and Chang Thongman of Drummondville, then there is one now. The incident between the two sparked a bench-clearing scrum with those two being the center of attention. Now they will miss 3 and 6 games respectively as a result of their actions. The league office announced their suspensions today.

"We're both really competitive," Watts said later, "and sometimes things go a little too far because of it."

To date Watts has chalked up a .301 average with 5 home runs, driven in 23 runs and scored 27 times.

Taking a look at his season stats for 1900, Thongman has made 28 relief appearances, recorded 2 saves and compiled a 4-4 won-lost mark with a 8.16 ERA. In pitching 57.1 innings, he has totaled 83 strikeouts and 23 walks.
