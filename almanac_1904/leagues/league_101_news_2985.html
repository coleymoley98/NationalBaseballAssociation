National League Announces Top Starter
Saturday, July 1st, 1899
James Albertson's been around the league long enough to appreciate the honor he received today. After going 5-3 in June, the 29-year-old Quebec hurler has been named National League Pitcher of the Month.

He fanned 12 in 77 innings, logged an ERA of 2.57 and allowed opponents to hit .253 against him.

This year Albertson has fanned 27 in 164.1 innings and collected a 10-7 record with a 2.57 ERA.
