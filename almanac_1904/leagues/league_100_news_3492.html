Sunday, July 1st, 1900
There's something about a young rookie winning the Canadian League Pitcher of the Month trophy that sends shock waves through the league. Such is the case with Edgar Arleth, a 22-year-old starter for the Boston Redcoats.

In June, he topped off the month with a 8-2 mark and 2.25 ERA in 10 starts, tossed 88 innings, fanned 29 and held opposing teams to a .226 average.

This year Arleth has posted a record of 14 wins and 2 losses with a 1.91 ERA. He has appeared in 17 games and struck out 44 in 141 innings.
