Veteran LF Roberts Stays in Detroit
Tuesday, June 14th, 1904
Detroit fans celebrated as word of a signing became reality. Josh Roberts won't be pulling up stakes anytime soon, as he has signed a 5-year extension at a pay rate of $2,860 per annum.

Since he is widely seen as an integral part of the club, Detroit officials had to keep Roberts from jumping ship.
