Slip on Wet Floor to Sideline Brown
Saturday, March 6th, 1897
Rangsan Brown will be out of action for the next few weeks after slipping on a wet floor at his home. The Blue Sox second baseman suffered a gluteus strain when his feet went out from underneath him as he unwittingly walked across a recently-mopped kitchen floor. "It's a little embarrassing," said Brown of his aching posterior. "I can't even sit down comfortably at the moment." Although teammates have been cracking jokes at his expense, Brown is taking the injury in stride. "I'm sure the idea of me falling on my backside amuses them quite a bit, but I'm not laughing about having to be out for 2 weeks. I want to get back on the field and help these guys win some ballgames."
