Grenier's 20-Game Hitting Streak Ends
Saturday, July 1st, 1899
Maybe it really is taboo to talk about a hitting streak before it's over. Hours after speaking to the press about his 20-game hitting streak, Pawtucket third baseman Bernard Grenier went hitless in 4 at-bats today at Juniors Ballpark.

The sudden hitting drought came as the Juniors took the 4-3 win over the Toronto Juniors.

"Nope, it's not magic," Grenier said in his postgame press conference. "Nobody jinxed me. Streaks don't last forever. I've heard about it every day for weeks now. My roomie told me to get a hit before every game and wished me luck. I just ran out of hits."

Grenier is presently hitting at a .327 pace with 1 home run and 13 RBIs. He has played in 28 games and scored 15 times.
