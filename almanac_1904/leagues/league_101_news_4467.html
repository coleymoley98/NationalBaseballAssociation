Montreal Pitcher Named NL's #1 Player
Monday, June 23rd, 1902
Pat Eagan is capable of a great game every time he takes the hill and last week the Montreal Royales hurler proved that point with gusto to become the newest National League Player of the Week.

He picked up 3 wins and no losses and rang up 4 strikeouts in 19 innings to finish with a splendid 0.95 ERA.

For the year Eagan has compiled a 12-1 record with a 1.55 ERA.
