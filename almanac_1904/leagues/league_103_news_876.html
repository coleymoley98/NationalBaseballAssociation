Fowlkes not to be denied in HR Challenge
Monday, June 10th, 1895
Juniors Ballpark was host to the 1895 Home Run Challenge, the annual celebration of the longball. Cedric Fowlkes finished first in the field of eight Francophone Junior Circuit sluggers, with Quebec City Juniors first baseman Cedric Fowlkes topping Jamuni Gandy in the final round by a score of 7-6.

"There's no better feeling in the world than hitting a home run, so imagine how I'm feeling after my performance tonight," said Fowlkes.
