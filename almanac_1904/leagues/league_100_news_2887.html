Rivière Pitches Rebels to 8-0 Win
Saturday, May 20th, 1899
In an impressive outing, Jozie Rivière whitewashed the Philadelphia Liberty 8-0.

"I'm getting a lot more downward movement instead of side to side. That side-to-side stuff can get hit a long way," said Rivière, who is throwing with more of an overhand motion.

"It's not rocket science. It was just a little adjustment, but that's all it took."

The New York hurler held Philadelphia scoreless, allowed 7 hits, chalked up 2 strikeouts and yielded 2 walks.

This year Rivière has fanned 10 in 57 innings and collected a 3-1 record with a 0.95 ERA.
