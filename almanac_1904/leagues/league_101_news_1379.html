National League Names Hutton Best Batter
Monday, June 1st, 1896
At the age of 31, Spencer Hutton might have some potential he hasn't realized yet -- if his success at the plate last week is any indication. The Quebec left fielder put up some exceptional numbers the past seven days with a .440 batting average. For his efforts Hutton collected the National League Player of the Week Award.

He racked up 11 hits in 25 at-bats, no home runs and 9 RBIs last week in 6 games.

This season Hutton has delivered 39 hits in 114 at-bats for a .342 average with 1 home run. He is getting on base at a .389 clip and his totals also include 26 RBIs and 16 runs scored. This year Hutton has played in 28 games.
