Toronto Trades Such for Detroit's Ouellet
Monday, March 31st, 1902
Toronto and Detroit announced some roster changes today as the Blue Sox acquired 26-year-old C Woranat Ouellet from the Lions in exchange for 26-year-old RHP Sumethee Such and 20-year-old SS Dusty Funkhouser. Representatives from both teams say they are pleased with the deal.


So far in his career, Such has registered 7 saves and fashioned a 16-11 won-lost mark. In 97 games he has fanned 89 batters in 181 innings and logged a 3.53 ERA.
