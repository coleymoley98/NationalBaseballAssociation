Bang-up Game: Robillard Slams 3 Homers
Wednesday, April 22nd, 1896
Anyone wearing a glove and sitting in the bleachers for today's Juniors-Juniors game had a good chance at snagging a home run ball.

Gallant right fielder Gordon Robillard thrice sent eager fans scurrying to catch a souvenir, helping lead the Gallant to a 8-7 win over Drummondville.

"Hey, the three homers were great, but I'm just trying to help my ballclub," Robillard said later. "I'm not going out there thinking I need another home run."

Robillard was 4-for-5 on the day with 6 RBI.
