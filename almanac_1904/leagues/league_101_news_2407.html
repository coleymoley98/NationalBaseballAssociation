Nordiques P.J. Meitner Swings Hot Bat, NL Winner
Monday, June 13th, 1898
One of the league's most exciting young players, P.J. Meitner of the Quebec Nordiques earned himself National League Player of the Week honors with a standout performance in last week's games.

For the week the 21-year-old right fielder had a lofty .429 average (9-for-21), collecting no home runs, 7 RBIs and 7 runs scored.

This season Meitner is hitting .309 with 1 home run.
