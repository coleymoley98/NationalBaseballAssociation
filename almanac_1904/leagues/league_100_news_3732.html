Boston-Toronto Trade: Little for Rivera
Tuesday, December 18th, 1900
It's all done except the paperwork. The Boston Redcoats and the Toronto Blue Sox have agreed to a trade that will send 25-year-old RF Jim Little and 22-year-old SS Sarut Badaruddin to Toronto, while 30-year-old LHP Von Taylor and 23-year-old CF Von Rivera will be shipped to Boston. A top front office executive with the Redcoats described the trade talks as "quick and easy." "We're glad to get this deal done. We think it's something that helps our ball club and it didn't take too long at all to sort this one out," he said.

Toronto will retain a portion of Taylor's remaining contract.
