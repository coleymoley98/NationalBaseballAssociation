Juneau Stops Roberts's Hit Streak
Saturday, June 25th, 1904
The hitting streak that Detroit left fielder Josh Roberts had kept going for 20 games was ended today by the Juneau Brasseurs. Roberts was stymied over 3 at-bats today, but the rest of his team picked him up, taking the 6-5 victory at Bennett Park.

"Hitting streaks come and go," Roberts said. "It did put a little pressure on me. It's hard to be natural at the plate with something like that always in the back of your mind. If it had to end, I'm glad it was in a win."

Roberts has collected 54 hits for the Lions this year, 3 of them home runs. He has also netted 13 RBIs and scored 21 runs while batting .283.
