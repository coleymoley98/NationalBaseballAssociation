Taylor on Fire, Named Player of the Week
Monday, July 29th, 1895
At the age of 28, Grady Taylor might have some potential he hasn't realized yet -- if his success at the plate last week is any indication. The Detroit second baseman put up some exceptional numbers the past seven days with a .483 batting average. For his efforts Taylor collected the National League Player of the Week Award.

He racked up 14 hits in 29 at-bats, no home runs and 7 RBIs last week in 7 games.

This season Taylor has hit at a .309 clip and produced 62 runs batted in, 8 home runs and 57 runs.
