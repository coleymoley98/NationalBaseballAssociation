Spaulding on Fire: Burns Springfield with 3 HR
Saturday, June 18th, 1904
Every now and then, everything just comes together.

Seamus Spaulding discovered that today at Juniors Ballpark, as he smashed three home runs during the 13-3 Morgantown Juniors win over the Springfield Juniors.

"I got three balls to hit and nailed all three of them," the Morgantown shortstop said in his postgame interview. "The first was a hanging curve, the second was right down the middle and the last one was a good pitch, but I guessed right and put the wood to it."

Spaulding finished the day with 3 hits in 5 at-bats and 8 RBIs.

Seamus Spaulding hit a three-run home run off Atit Goyette in the 1st, flied out in the 3rd, flied out in the 5th, hit a GRAND SLAM off Rama Comtois in the 6th and hit a solo-shot off Rama Comtois in the 9th.

This year Spaulding is hitting .230 with 6 home runs.
