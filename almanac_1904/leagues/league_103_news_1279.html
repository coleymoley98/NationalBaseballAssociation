Juniors Torpedoed by Graf in 2-0 Loss to Sainte-Paul
Monday, March 30th, 1896
Pranthep Graf was dynamite at Field of the Juniors today, blowing away the Gauthier Juniors in a shutout victory. The Sainte-Paul Juniors starter had everything working as he held the Juniors to 1 hit in the 2-0 win.

"He was pure nasty out there today," said Gauthier skipper Ti Holley. "I don't think any team in the league would've had a chance against what he was bringing."

Graf finished the game with 10 strikeouts and 2 walks.

This year Graf has made 10 starts, thrown 82.1 innings, fanned 92 batters and walked 22. His won-lost record is 5-1 with a 2.95 ERA.
