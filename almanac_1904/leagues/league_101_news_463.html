Montreal Blanks Frontenacs, Foreman at His Best
Thursday, August 18th, 1904
In a remarkable performance, Montreal Royales starter Garrett Foreman beat the Chicagou Frontenacs 4-0 in an impressive 6-hit shutout.

"My arm felt good, mechanics were good and my stuff was working," he said later. "It doesn't get any better than this."

The Royales hurler finished with 6 strikeouts and 3 walks. He showed pinpoint control and a sharp fastball.

"It was a rough one for my guys," Chicagou manager Mustafa Campeau said following the game. "We weren't at our best and their guy was."

This year Foreman has recorded a 13-9 mark. In 26 starts he has fanned 155 batters in 228 innings with a 1.34 ERA.
