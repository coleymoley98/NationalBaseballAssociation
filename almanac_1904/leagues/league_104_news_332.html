Juniors Chill Raun's Hot Streak
Monday, July 4th, 1904
The Quincy Juniors got more than the 13-9 victory at Juniors Ballpark today -- they also put an end to the 21-game hitting streak of New Britain Juniors left fielder Tulimak Raun by holding him without a hit in 3 at-bats.

After the game, Raun seemed more concerned with his team taking the loss than with the demise of his streak.

"I had some great looks at some pitches that I should have hit," he said. "I missed some that I shouldn't have swung at. All in all, I wasn't much help with the bat today."

This season Raun is batting .453 with 26 RBIs, 34 runs scored and 9 home runs.
