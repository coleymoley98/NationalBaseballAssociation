Wrice Named CL Rookie of the Month
Monday, June 1st, 1903
One of the bright young starters in the Canadian League, Amporn Wrice is living up to the potential that Redcoats scouts thought might be there.

As a starter for Boston, Wrice paced his team with a 2-1 record in May to earn himself Rookie of the Month accolades.

He made 4 starts, tossed 51 innings, struck out 23 batters and allowed the opposition to hit .245 against him.
