Philadelphia Trades Papillon for Boston's Pelletier
Tuesday, April 25th, 1899
Newspapers in Philadelphia and Boston reported that the Liberty have reached a deal sending 33-year-old RHP Danny Papillon to the Redcoats for 26-year-old C Fabrice Pelletier.

Philadelphia will retain a portion of Papillon's remaining contract.

Papillon has gone 17-20 with an ERA of 3.44 in his career, piling up 59 strikeouts in 314 innings pitched.
