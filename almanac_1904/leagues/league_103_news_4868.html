Michell Picked Best FJC Pitcher
Tuesday, May 5th, 1903
This past season was outstanding for Jimmy Michell. The Juneau mound star sizzled with a 7-3 record and captured the prestigious 1903 FJC Pitcher of the Year Award.

The Juniors hurler posted a 1.57 ERA in 13 starts with 151 strikeouts and 19 walks in 97.2 innings. Michell limited opposing teams to a .140 batting average.

He received 12 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. John Nichols of the Quebec City Juniors finished second in voting, while Mitchell Grondin of the Drummondville Juniors finished third.
