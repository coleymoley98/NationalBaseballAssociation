Redcoats Get One to Win
Saturday, May 23rd, 1903
It wasn't easy but Watcharapon Chevrier made it look that way.

Chevrier silenced the New York Rebels, 1-0, in a Boston Redcoats triumph at Huntington Avenue Grounds today. Despite the appearance of dominance, Chevrier said later that he felt like he was struggling all game. He gave up 4 walks and 3 hits.

"It didn't feel quite right today, but I made the decision to bear down and work through it," he said. "I made a few mistakes but it goes to show what you can do with grit and not much else."

Checking out his stats for the season, Chevrier has racked up 3 wins and 3 losses in 6 starts, 3.51 ERA and struck out 27 batters in 48.2 innings.
