Detroit Trades Eastabrooks for Toronto's Hill
Saturday, July 5th, 1902
Sometimes a change of scenery can be a beneficial to a baseball player. That is the hope in Detroit and Toronto as the teams came to an agreement today which sends 27-year-old 2B Brandon Eastabrooks to the Blue Sox and 29-year-old RHP Malik Hill and 19-year-old 2B Newin Vézina to the Lions. "Only time will tell if this is the right move for everyone," stated the Toronto GM.


Currently Eastabrooks is batting .319 with 22 hits and no home runs. He has knocked in 7 runs and scored 10 times.
