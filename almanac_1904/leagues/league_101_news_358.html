Day-to-Day Injury Shelves Winters
Tuesday, July 12th, 1904
The Quebec Nordiques received some welcome news this morning. They will not need to place shortstop Winfred Winters on the injured list. During yesterday's game against the Chicagou Frontenacs, he was injured running the bases. The injury, however, is not serious. The Quebec star performer is listed as day-to-day with a stiff back.

Winters is hitting .355 for the season with no home runs, 17 RBIs and 31 runs scored. He has 72 hits in 203 at-bats and played in 53 games.
