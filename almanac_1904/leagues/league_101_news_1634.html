NL Top Batter Trophy Won by Roberts
Tuesday, September 1st, 1896
At only 25 years of age, Josh Roberts could very well be having many more months like the last this in his career.

That would be great news for fans of the Detroit Lions, as August was one to remember for the left fielder and he has captured the National League Batter of the Month award.

On the month he hit .395 with 2 home runs, 15 RBIs and 22 runs scored.

Roberts is hitting .383 so far this season with 61 RBIs and 78 runs scored. He has 153 hits including 10 home runs, 27 walks and .427 OBP.
