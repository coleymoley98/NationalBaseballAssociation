Carry puts on power show
Monday, June 1st, 1903
Yuthapichai Carry and Jermaine Barksdale put on quite the show at Ballpark of Davenport during the 1903 Home Run Challenge. Davenport Juniors center fielder Carry ended up victorious after a final round score of 9-8.

"I was pretty nervous tonight, but hearing the crowd's reaction to my first homer calmed me down and I got in a nice groove," said Carry.
