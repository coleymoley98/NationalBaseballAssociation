With Win Oh Sets Record
Saturday, August 20th, 1904
"Alessandro Sterling is a darn good pitcher. I never thought of myself in his league. After today, I guess I might be fairly good, too ... winning 24 games in a season is really something." That's what Maurice Oh of the New York Rebels said after he broke the league record for most victories in a season. Oh chalked up his 24th win today at Exposition Park, passing Alessandro Sterling, the previous record holder.

In 27 starts this season Oh has compiled a 24-3 record and a 0.94 ERA.
