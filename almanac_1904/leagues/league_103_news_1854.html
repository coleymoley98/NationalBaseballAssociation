Sainte-Paul's Oh Takes Best Pitcher Prize
Wednesday, May 5th, 1897
Maurice Oh of the Sainte-Paul Juniors will be adding a new centerpiece to his mantel after being tabbed for the Francophone Junior Circuit Pitcher of the Year Award today.

The 16-year-old hurler started 17 games this season, tallying a 12-4 record. In 133 innings, Oh gave up 109 hits, struck out 143, walked 32 and held opponents to a .212 average as he posted a fine 2.03 ERA.

He received 8 first place votes out of a possible 12. Dean Potter of the Quebec City Juniors finished second in voting with 4 first place votes, while Garland Givan of the Sherbrooke Juniors finished third.
