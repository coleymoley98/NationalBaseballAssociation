Hill Breezes to Shutout Victory
Tuesday, July 12th, 1904
Malik Hill had the Juneau Brasseurs dancing to his tune today with a sparkling 3-0 shutout performance for the Detroit Lions. He gave up 2 hits, issued no walks and had 3 strikeouts.

Juneau hitters were bewildered throughout the game by his delivery.

"When you face a guy who hides the ball like he does, it takes a couple of at-bats to try to find his release point," opposing manager Owen Turcotte said. "We just never figured him out."

So far this season, Hill has logged a 6-4 record with a 2.14 ERA. He has started 9 games, hurled 80 innings and registered 29 strikeouts and 21 walks.
