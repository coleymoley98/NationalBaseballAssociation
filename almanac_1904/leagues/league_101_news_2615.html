Montreal Blanks Quebec, 3-0
Sunday, August 21st, 1898
Jayden Queen of the Montreal Royales was on top of his game today.

The 22-year-old tantalized the Quebec Nordiques with crisp curves and changeups, mixed in with just enough fastballs to keep the batters honest and came away with a 3-0 shutout.

"He had the changeup working," Quebec manager Frédéric Lévesque said in postgame comments. "You knew it was coming and you still grounded out -- and then he threw it even slower."

The Montreal hurler had 1 strikeout, gave up no walks and surrendered 4 hits in his complete-game effort.

Queen told reporters after the game that "keeping the ball down is the key to being successful in this league."

"If you don't do that, they'll make you pay," he said.

On the season Queen has a 19-11 record with a 2.33 ERA.
