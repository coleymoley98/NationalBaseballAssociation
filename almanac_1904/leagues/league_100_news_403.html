Meitner Wins CL's Best Bat Award
Monday, August 1st, 1904
P.J. Meitner said the baseball "looked as big as a beach ball" in July.

It's no surprise then that the Boston right fielder captured the Canadian League Batter of the Month Award.

On the month, he had a .351 batting average, with no home runs, 13 RBIs, 22 runs scored and a .407 on-base percentage.

Meitner is currently batting .307 with no home runs, 42 RBIs and 44 runs scored. He has 88 hits in 287 at-bats.
