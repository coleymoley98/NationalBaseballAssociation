Detroit-Montreal Trade: Burns for Jones
Sunday, November 3rd, 1901
The Lions and Royales announced a deal that was completed today. Detroit sent 26-year-old RF Sarunyu Burns and 20-year-old SS Stuart Konishi to Montreal for 25-year-old LHP Kittipong Jones and 22-year-old C Felix Fournier. Both teams appear happy with the outcome of the trade. The general managers told the Baseball News Network they felt the swap was beneficial for all parties involved.


Career-to-date Burns is hitting .301 with a total of 8 home runs.

Jones owns a 11-5 won-lost record with a 3.41 ERA for his career. In 27 games he has recorded no saves and the 25-year-old has 31 strikeouts in 177 innings of work.
