Nordiques End Pépin's Streak
Sunday, May 19th, 1895
Detroit Lions right fielder Blaise Pépin and his hot bat finally met their match today at Ballpark of Quebec.

The Quebec Nordiques snapped his 21-game hitting string, holding Pépin hitless in 1 at-bat and also whipping Detroit for good measure.

"We're real pleased with this one," Nordiques manager Frédéric Lévesque said. "Nobody wants to be on the wrong end of a streak like that, and clobbering Detroit makes it even sweeter."

Pépin is hitting at a .406 mark this season. His stats show no home runs, 16 RBIs and 15 runs scored. He has played 15 games for the Lions.
