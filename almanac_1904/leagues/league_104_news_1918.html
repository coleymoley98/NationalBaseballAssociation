Great Day for Liles -- 5 Hits
Saturday, June 12th, 1897
Daquein Liles was at the head of a Canton hit parade today at Juniors Ballpark. The right fielder went to the plate 5 times and came away with 5 hits, sparking the Juniors to a 15-7 victory over the Toronto Juniors.

"I told the guys in the dugout, just be thinking about hitting the ball hard somewhere," the sweet-swinging Liles told reporters after the game. "Seems like it worked out well for us today."

Juniors skipper Samuel Sénécal said later that the club gave up "too much plate."

"Not the way we planned it," he said. "Every hitter in the league will rip you when you do that."

Daquein Liles singled in the 1st, hit an RBI triple in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, doubled in the 7th and hit a solo-shot off Micah Farnborough in the 8th.

This year Liles has hit at a .400 pace and produced 11 RBIs, 1 home run and scored 14 runs.
