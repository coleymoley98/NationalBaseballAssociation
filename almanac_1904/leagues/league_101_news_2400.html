Solis Slips on Grass, Suffers Mild Injury
Monday, June 6th, 1898
Blake Solis of the Nordiques may miss a game or two after slipping on the wet grass during an afternoon warm-up at the team's practice facility. Solis and his teammates were just returning to the field after a brief rain delay when the catcher took an awkward spill on the infield. "My feet just went out from under me," Solis explained shortly after the incident. "All I know right now is that I'm pretty darn sore." Even though there was no immediate evidence of any serious injury, manager Frédéric Lévesque told reporters after practice that he would be keeping Solis out of the Nordiques next game "as a precaution".
