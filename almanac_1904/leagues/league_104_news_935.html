Juniors Get Zip in 4-0 Loss to Siriwong
Sunday, June 30th, 1895
Tiloka Siriwong of the Quincy Juniors was on top of his game today.

The 17-year-old tantalized the New Britain Juniors with crisp curves and changeups, mixed in with just enough fastballs to keep the batters honest and came away with a 4-0 shutout.

"He had the changeup working," New Britain manager Conrad Thompson said in postgame comments. "You knew it was coming and you still grounded out -- and then he threw it even slower."

The Quincy hurler had 6 strikeouts, gave up 3 walks and surrendered 2 hits in his complete-game effort.

Siriwong told reporters after the game that "keeping the ball down is the key to being successful in this league."

"If you don't do that, they'll make you pay," he said.

On the season Siriwong has 6 wins and 3 losses and a 2.77 ERA.
