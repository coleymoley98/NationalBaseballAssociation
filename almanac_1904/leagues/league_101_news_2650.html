August's Top NL Rookie Named
Thursday, September 1st, 1898
One of the bright young starters in the National League, Peyton Malone is living up to the potential that Lions scouts thought might be there.

As a starter for Detroit, Malone paced his team with a 5-3 record in August to earn himself Rookie of the Month accolades.

He made 8 starts, tossed 72.2 innings, struck out 16 batters and allowed the opposition to hit .210 against him.

In 10 starts this season Malone has rung up a 5-5 record and a 2.91 ERA.
