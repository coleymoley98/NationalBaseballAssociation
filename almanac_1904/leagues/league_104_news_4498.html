League Suspends Malone and Boyle for Fisticuffs
Monday, June 30th, 1902
Baseball officials have suspended two players for "inappropriate and aggressive conduct" in yesterday's Pawtucket-Fort Wayne contest at Juniors Ballpark. The Juniors Wes Malone and the Juniors Petcharat Boyle will have to sit out 4 and 9 games respectively for their parts in the brawl.

So far this season Malone has gotten 31 hits in 105 at-bats for a .295 batting average with 1 home run. His stats include 14 RBIs and 18 runs scored. He has played in 27 games.

Boyle has made 10 starts this season, posting 7 wins and 1 loss. Opposing clubs are batting .158 against him and he has 83 strikeouts in 77.1 innings with a 1.40 ERA.
