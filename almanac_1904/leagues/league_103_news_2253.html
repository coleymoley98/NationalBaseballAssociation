Big Day for Stearns: Swats 6 Hits
Friday, March 18th, 1898
Chaiyawat Stearns was sizzling at the plate today. In a game played at Juniors Ballpark, the Gauthier left fielder blistered the Minneapolis Juniors for 6 hits in 6 at-bats to pace his club to a 10-1 victory.

"Stearns was too tough to get out today," Juniors skipper Brien Gilliam said in his postgame interview. "No matter what we threw him, he hit it. And he hit some good pitches, too."

Chaiyawat Stearns singled in the 1st, hit an RBI double in the 3rd, singled in the 4th, hit a two-run home run off Jirat Huff in the 6th, doubled in the 8th and singled in the 9th.

To date Stearns has chalked up a .333 average with 2 home runs, driven in 9 runs and scored 10 times.
