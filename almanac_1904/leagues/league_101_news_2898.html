Cannatelli Too Tough, Blanks Quebec
Saturday, May 27th, 1899
Montreal hurler Pete Cannatelli put on quite a show today as he blitzed the Quebec Nordiques with a 5-0 shutout. He baffled them with fastballs and changeups, allowing 4 hits with 1 strikeout and no walks to pick up the win.

"I think every time we were looking for a fastball, he threw a changeup and every time we were looking for a changeup, he threw a fastball," Quebec manager Frédéric Lévesque told reporters after the game.

This year Cannatelli has fashioned a 5-4 mark, 2.88 ERA and pitched 84.1 innings. Opposing clubs are batting .287 against him and he has logged 25 strikeouts.
