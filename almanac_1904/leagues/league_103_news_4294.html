Hill Torches Juniors for 5 Hits
Friday, March 21st, 1902
Keith Hill was the King of Swing today at Field of the Juniors today in the 9-2 Gallant Juniors win over the Sherbrooke Juniors. The Gallant second baseman stepped into the batter's box 6 times against the Sherbrooke and delivered 5 hits. It was a sensational game for him.

"Unbelievable feeling," he told the press. "It felt good. 5-for-6 isn't bad for your batting average either."
