Boston Starter Named CL's Top Pitcher
Thursday, August 1st, 1895
Phutchapong Sanford took a much-deserved turn in the spotlight earlier today as he accepted the Canadian League Pitcher of the Month award for July.

The 27-year-old Redcoats starter is a relative newcomer to the league, but nonetheless racked up an impressive 6-3 won-lost record in 10 starts. This month he fanned 33 batters in 85 innings and compiled a 3.07 ERA.

So far this season, Sanford has logged a 18-9 record with a 2.98 ERA. He has started 29 games, hurled 244.1 innings and registered 92 strikeouts and 73 walks.
