Red-Hot Meitner Scorches Lions
Wednesday, May 18th, 1898
It was a big day for P.J. Meitner and he brought the Quebec Nordiques along for the ride.

Meitner led his club to a 18-1 victory over the Detroit Lions today at Ballpark of Quebec, smoking Detroit pitching for 5 hits in 5 at-bats. The 21-year-old right fielder later called the victory "sweet."

P.J. Meitner hit an RBI single in the 1st, hit a sac fly in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, singled in the 5th, singled in the 6th and hit a two-run double in the 7th.

Meitner is hitting .329 this year with no home runs and 11 RBIs. He also has an OBP of .410, 11 walks and scored 13 times.
