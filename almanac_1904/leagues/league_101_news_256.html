Lions Not Competitive in 7-0 Loss to Montreal
Thursday, June 16th, 1904
Pete Cannatelli of the Montreal Royales spun his magic on the Detroit Lions today. He blanked them 7-0 on 5 hits, registering 5 strikeouts and no walks.

"The bottom line was I just kept the ball down," Cannatelli said. "My breaking stuff was down. Everything was down."

Talking to reporters in the postgame interview, Montreal skipper Kamnan Rowe said the team "got solid pitching, good defense and took great team at-bats in a lot of different situations."

"Pete pitched really well but this was a solid performance by everyone," he said.

This season Cannatelli has a 3-1 record with a 2.02 ERA in 5 starts.
