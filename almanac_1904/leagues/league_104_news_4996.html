Ridin' High: Rousselle Hits for Cycle
Thursday, June 25th, 1903
Apichart Rousselle was at the front of a Juniors hit parade today at Juniors Ballpark. The first baseman finished 4-for-5 and hit for the cycle as Fort Wayne triumphed 10-7 over the Pawtucket Juniors.

"Apichart was putting on a real clinic out there," Fort Wayne manager Ace Dugandzic said following the game.

Rousselle finished the game with 2 RBI and had 1 single, 1 double, 1 triple and 1 home run.

Apichart Rousselle grounded out in the 1st, doubled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, hit an RBI triple in the 7th and hit a solo-shot off J.P. Sauvageau in the 8th.

So far in 1903 Rousselle is batting .327 with 3 home runs, 17 RBIs and 23 runs scored. He has 34 hits in 104 at-bats and a .369 OBP.
