Super Swinger -- Branch Slams 5 Hits
Tuesday, May 12th, 1896
Montreal right fielder Thanawat Branch had a day to remember today, 5-for-6 against the Quebec Nordiques at Ballpark of Quebec as the Royales picked up a 15-0 victory.

"He was patient up there today," Montreal skipper Charles Read told the press after the game. "I like the way he waited for his pitch and wasn't overly aggressive. Branch has a tendency to chase bad balls sometimes."

Thanawat Branch singled in the 1st, hit a two-run single in the 2nd, flied out in the 4th, singled in the 6th, singled in the 9th and hit a three-run home run off Kerry Pope in the 9th.

Currently Branch has 2 home runs and a .341 batting average.
