Yoshioka Bangs Out 5 Base Hits
Saturday, June 27th, 1903
It was high-fives all around for Alex Yoshioka at Polo Grounds today.

He got them from his teammates and he got them at the plate during an outstanding hitting performance against the Philadelphia Liberty. Yoshioka smashed 5 hits in 5 at-bats on his monster day, leading his club to a 15-6 win.

"Alex was hitting everything," said Rebels manager Adisak Cigan. "It really was a special game for him."

In the opposing dugout, Liberty manager Tyrone Martin told BNN beat writers that Yoshioka "ambushed" the club.

"Obviously we didn't have much success against him, but we'll have another chance," he said. "Hopefully, Yoshioka oversleeps next time."

Alex Yoshioka singled in the 1st, hit a three-run triple in the 3rd, hit an RBI single in the 4th, singled in the 6th and hit an RBI double in the 7th.

Yoshioka is currently batting .288 with 1 home run and 17 RBIs.
