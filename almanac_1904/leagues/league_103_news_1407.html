1896 HR Challenge won by McCaskell
Monday, June 8th, 1896
Chicks, they say, dig the long ball. But not just women. Everyone at Field of the Juniors was thrilled to witness the 1896 Home Run Challenge. When it was over, Gabriel McCaskell took home the trophy. The Sherbrooke Juniors right fielder prevailed in the final round over Muangkan Fawley by a score of 8-7.

"It was a dream come true, winning the Home Run Challenge," said McCaskell. "I couldn't be happier."

During his career McCaskell has hit for a .325 average and totaled 178 hits, 20 home runs and 114 RBIs.
