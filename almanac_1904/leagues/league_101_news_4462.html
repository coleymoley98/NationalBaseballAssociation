Frontenacs Flame Out Against Foreman, Royales
Thursday, June 19th, 1902
Garrett Foreman dominated the Chicagou Frontenacs with a brilliant 4-hit shutout in a Montreal Royales 5-0 triumph at Montreal Grounds. The Montreal hurler fanned 6 and walked 2 in the game and was singled out for praise afterward by the opposing manager.

"He really kept us in check today," Frontenacs skipper Mustafa Campeau said in his postgame comments. "Everything looked to be moving. Seemed like the pitch was right down the middle and it went over down and away. We took some pathetic swings."

In pitching stats this season Foreman has rung up 7 wins and 5 losses in 13 games. He has an ERA of 3.18 and has fanned 48 batters in 110.1 innings.
