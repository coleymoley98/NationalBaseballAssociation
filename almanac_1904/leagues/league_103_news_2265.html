Lynch Puts Spell on Minneapolis
Friday, March 25th, 1898
Magic was in the air and on the mound at Ballpark of Montreal today as Baldwin Lynch held the Minneapolis Juniors spellbound in his 8-0 shutout victory. The Montreal Juniors pitcher went the distance, rang up 10 strikeouts, walked 0 and held the Juniors to 4 hits to garner the win.

"He just had us in his thrall," Minneapolis manager Brien Gilliam said later. "We couldn't put anything together against him."

This year Lynch has compiled a 4-0 record with a 3.34 ERA in 8 starts.
