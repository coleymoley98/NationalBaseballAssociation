Chicagou-New York Trade: Callas for Hancocks
Thursday, March 19th, 1903
It likely won't turn either team into an instant contender or provide a huge franchise shakeup, but the Chicagou Frontenacs and New York Rebels finalized a trade today. Chicagou will send 25-year-old LF Ray Callas to the Rebels, while the Frontenacs will receive 26-year-old RHP Victor Hancocks and 23-year-old LHP Prayad Summers.

"We think this is a deal that will help out both teams and we're glad to get it done," Frontenacs general manager Aurélien Gendron said.

The trade is expected to be officially completed later today with the players joining their new ballclubs by the end of the day.


During his 167-game career Callas has batted .279 and collected 178 hits, 3 home runs and 77 RBIs.
