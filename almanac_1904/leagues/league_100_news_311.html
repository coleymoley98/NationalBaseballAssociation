Canadian League Names Rookie of Month
Friday, July 1st, 1904
Seksit Kittipornpracha is beginning to make a name for himself and he did it by posting a 7-0 record and a 1.86 ERA in June. For his spectacular mound performance last month he has been named BNN's Rookie of the Month in the Canadian League.

The 23-year-old newcomer isn't at all intimidated by his lack of experience, at least not outwardly. In fact, by the way he has pitched beyond his years, you could easily mistake him for a veteran.

In 7 starts for the Rebels Kittipornpracha has struck out 27 batters in 63 innings, holding opposing hitters to a batting average of .217 during his hot streak.

In 9 starts this season, Kittipornpracha has picked up 7 wins while suffering 2 losses. He has pitched 79.1 innings, allowed 73 hits, struck out 32 and posted a 2.27 ERA.
