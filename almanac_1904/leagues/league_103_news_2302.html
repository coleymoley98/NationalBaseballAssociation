FJC Names Juniors Généreux Winner
Monday, April 25th, 1898
So far in his young career, Jean Généreux has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Francophone Junior Circuit in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 19-year-old Minneapolis Juniors right fielder hit at a torrid .417 clip as he collected 10 hits in 24 at-bats, 3 home runs, 7 RBIs and 7 runs scored.

A look at his season totals shows Généreux with 6 home runs, .178 BA, .251 OBP, 20 RBIs and 22 runs scored.
