Quincy Star Woods Cops Pitcher of the Year Award
Saturday, August 4th, 1900
Pongrit Woods of the Quincy Juniors had a fine season and has been rewarded with the Anglo Junior Circuit Pitcher of the Year Award for 1900.

The Juniors ace fashioned an ERA of 1.33 as he won 9 games, dropped 5, fanned 148 and gave up 26 walks in 115 innings of work.

He received 12 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. James Belton of the Hartford Juniors finished second in voting, while Ronald Rice of the Pawtucket Juniors finished third.
