NL Picks Rookie of the Month
Monday, July 1st, 1895
One of the bright young starters in the National League, Val Catteau is living up to the potential that Nordiques scouts thought might be there.

As a starter for Quebec, Catteau paced his team with a 5-4 record in June to earn himself Rookie of the Month accolades.

He made 10 starts, tossed 73.2 innings, struck out 20 batters and allowed the opposition to hit .298 against him.

Catteau has made 19 starts this season, posting 9 wins and 9 losses. Opposing clubs are batting .296 against him and he has 44 strikeouts in 146.1 innings with a 3.63 ERA.
