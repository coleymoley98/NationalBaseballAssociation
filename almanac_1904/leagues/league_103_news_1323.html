Juniors Torpedoed by Graf in 7-0 Loss to Sainte-Paul
Saturday, April 25th, 1896
Pranthep Graf of the Sainte-Paul Juniors put on a dazzling performance at Juniors Ballpark today. He shut out the Juneau Juniors 7-0 on a well-pitched 1-hitter, ringing up 12 strikeouts and issuing 2 walks.

"I felt strong out there all day," Graf said later.

Over the season Graf has thrown 177.1 innings and dished out 184 strikeouts with a 12-4 mark and a 3.50 ERA.
