Lions End Price's Streak
Saturday, August 6th, 1898
It was the best of both worlds for the Detroit Lions today at Montreal Grounds, as they defeated the Montreal Royales 8-7 and also snapped Zachary Price's 20-game hitting streak. The 26-year-old Montreal left fielder failed to get a hit in 3 at-bats.

"Nothing personal against Price, we just wanted to win the game," said Lions skipper Enzo Kelly. "We did a great job keeping him off-balance and guessing."

When asked by reporters about his streak coming to an end, Price frowned and said, "I won't lie and say that I'm not disappointed about it, but I'd feel much better if we had won."

This year Price is batting .340 with 118 hits and 2 home runs. He has scored 60 times and has batted in 55 runs.
