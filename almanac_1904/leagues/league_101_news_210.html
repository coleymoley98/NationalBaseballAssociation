Frontenacs, Summerford Buzz Lions, 1-0
Thursday, June 2nd, 1904
It wasn't easy but the Chicagou Frontenacs made it look that way.

Starter Jamie Summerford shut out the Detroit Lions despite having what he later called "sub-par" stuff, and the Chicagou Frontenacs cruised to victory, 1-0. Summerford allowed only 6 hits and rang up 3 strikeouts but insisted later that he did not deserve primary credit for the win.

"It didn't feel quite right today, but I got a lot of help to get through," he said. "I trusted my catcher and the guys behind me bailed me out more than once when I screwed up."

This year Summerford has made 9 starts, thrown 78 innings, fanned 35 batters and walked 11. His won-lost record is 7-2 with a 2.54 ERA.
