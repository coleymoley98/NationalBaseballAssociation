Frey: Big Hit in the National League
Monday, June 8th, 1903
It can be difficult for a young player to step out of the shadows and assert himself in the National League, but last week Ara Frey of the Detroit Lions reminded everyone just why he's here as he threw up numbers that made him an easy selection for Player of the Week. Frey rocked opposing pitching for a .394 average (13-for-33), 1 home run and 5 RBIs to come away with the award.

This year Frey is batting .318 with 49 hits, 3 home runs, 22 RBIs, 1 walk, .335 OBP and has scored 21 runs.
