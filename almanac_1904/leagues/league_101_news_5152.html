Frontenacs Suffer 9-0 Drubbing from Royales
Friday, August 14th, 1903
The Chicagou Frontenacs manager was philosophical in the clubhouse following a shutout loss to the Montreal Royales. The Frontenacs were overpowered by Montreal starter Fabien Girard, who hurled his club to victory, 9-0.

"It was just one of those games -- shake it off and do better next time," Mustafa Campeau said.

Over 9 innings, Girard blanked Chicagou on 6 hits with no walks and 5 strikeouts.

"I kept them off balance. I could tell a lot of them were late on fastballs because they were fouling them off. Once you see that, it sets them up for other pitches," Girard said.

"I set them up, where if they chased one breaking ball, they were going to chase another one."

On his stats sheet this season Girard shows a 11-4 record, 17 starts, 145 innings, 97 strikeouts and a 2.11 ERA.
