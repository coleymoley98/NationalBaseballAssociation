CL BREAKING NEWS: Expansion Teams Announced
Friday, March 18th, 1904
The Canadian League has announced the teams that have won the bid for the current expansion process! The new teams will be the Ottawa Indians and the Wolfeburg Celtics.

Existing teams have to submit the protection lists for the upcoming expansion draft until March 18th, 1904.The draft itself is held one day later.

League officials are excited to see these new cities compete!
