CL Honors Malone, #1 Batter in June
Friday, July 1st, 1904
For a rookie just breaking in, Wes Malone certainly put together a successful month. It was good enough to win the CL Batter of the Month award for June.

The 23-year-old left fielder for the Boston Redcoats hit .381 with no home runs, 9 RBIs and 15 runs scored.

In 1904 Malone has hit at a .338 clip with 45 hits, no home runs, 14 RBIs and has scored 20 times.
