Jones of Philadelphia Earns CL Batting Crown
Monday, August 26th, 1901
Many talented Canadian League players can go their entire careers and never even get a sniff of a batting title.

Hunter Jones has already captured one at age 25.

The Philadelphia left fielder batted .330 to capture the 1901 Canadian League batting championship.

He collected 109 hits, 11 doubles, 3 triples and 1 home run for the year. In 83 games Jones totaled 49 RBIs and scored 38 runs.
