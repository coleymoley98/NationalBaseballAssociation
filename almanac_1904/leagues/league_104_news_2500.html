Bossicar's Hit Streak Reaches 30 Games
Tuesday, July 12th, 1898
Suriyakarn Bossicar of the Buffalo Juniors extended his hitting streak to 30 straight games with 2 hits in today's 2-1 triumph over the Morgantown Juniors at Ballpark of Buffalo.

"Suriyakarn has been making it easy for me to pencil his name in the lineup every day," Juniors manager Ratipong Jones joked after the game.
Suriyakarn Bossicar singled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, flied out in the 5th, grounded out in the 8th and walked in the 10th.

Thus far this year Bossicar is batting .424 with 11 home runs, 48 RBIs and 48 runs scored.
