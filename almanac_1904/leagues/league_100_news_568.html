CL News: First-Year Player Draft
Saturday, November 5th, 1904
The 1904 Canadian League amateur draft will be conducted today. The cream of the crop in amateur baseball circles will be plucked by the baseball hierarchy. Diamond journalists and experts say these ten names are at the top of the first-year player shopping list:


1. Robin Waggoner, 18 year-old RHP out of college.
2. Rungarun Inglis, 21 year-old 3B out of college.
3. Jimmy Michell, 19 year-old RHP out of college.
4. William Thériault, 18 year-old RHP out of college.
5. Alair Givens, 18 year-old RHP out of college.
6. Saksit Diana, 21 year-old LHP out of college.
7. Panupan McFadden, 18 year-old RHP out of college.
8. Arnon Wood, 18 year-old LHP out of college.
9. Nicolas Messier, 21 year-old 3B out of college.
10. Paul Dyer, 21 year-old C out of college.

Ottawa, Wolfeburg, Detroit, St. Louis and Juneau have the top five picks and hope to cash in this year.
