Nordiques P.J. Meitner to Miss Year
Saturday, August 20th, 1898
P.J. Meitner will soon find out from his doctors if his season will continue. The first diagnosis was not too positive and indicated the Quebec right fielder could miss the remainder of the year. Meitner was injured while throwing the ball in the Nordiques-Royales clash at Montreal Grounds recently. Doctors said he has a strained shoulder and would be out a minimum of 6 weeks.

This season Meitner is batting .320 with 1 home run. His totals include 60 RBIs and 68 runs scored.
