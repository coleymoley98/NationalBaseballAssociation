Frey of Detroit NL Batter of the Month
Thursday, September 1st, 1904
Detroit Lions fans believe they've got a talented young star in Ara Frey.

He did nothing to dissuade that notion in August, as he hit .378 with 1 home run, 23 RBIs and 14 runs scored.

The end result was the first baseman being named the National League Batter of the Month.

This year Frey is batting .311 with 4 home runs.
