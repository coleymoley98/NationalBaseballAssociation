Mill Selected AJC's #1 Player
Monday, August 5th, 1901
Attapon Mill of the Morgantown Juniors had a fine season this year and today he accepted the Most Valuable Player Award for 1901.

The 21-year-old left fielder put up a .388 average and a .459 on-base percentage, piling up 76 hits, 12 doubles, 4 triples, 13 home runs, 42 RBIs and 48 runs scored.

He received 12 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Abraham Love of the Halifax Juniors finished second in voting, while Natarip Green of the Morgantown Juniors finished third.
