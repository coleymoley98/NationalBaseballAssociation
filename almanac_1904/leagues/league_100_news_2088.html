So far in his young career, Jason Totten has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Canadian League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 22-year-old New York Rebels center fielder hit at a torrid .435 clip as he collected 10 hits in 23 at-bats, 2 home runs, 12 RBIs and 5 runs scored.

This year Totten has socked 50 hits in 202 at-bats for a .248 average with 3 home runs. He is getting on base at a .330 pace and his totals also include 32 RBIs and 34 runs scored.
