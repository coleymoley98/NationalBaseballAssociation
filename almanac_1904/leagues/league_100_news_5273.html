Downing for Nguyen in Major Trade
Thursday, November 5th, 1903
A major trade has been executed between the Philadelphia Liberty and Boston Redcoats, one that has stunned the baseball world. The Liberty traded 26-year-old 3B Jake Downing and 22-year-old 3B Pathomchai Chuwet to the Redcoats for 28-year-old 2B Colin Nguyen.

"Putting this deal together was a lot of hard work, but the payoff will be tremendous," Philadelphia GM Ralph Schrimp said when contacted by BNN. "Liberty fans ought to be excited about it."

BNN analysts are still debating who got the better end of the deal, but Redcoats manager Herb Lamaison said he already knows all he needs: "This trade will win us more ballgames," he said.


Downing has a lifetime .279 average and .350 on-base percentage in his career with 541 hits, 72 doubles, 13 triples, 14 home runs, 247 RBIs and 242 runs scored.

Nguyen currently has a career .306 batting average with 475 hits, good for 1 home run, 162 RBIs and 234 runs.
