Jean-François Corbeil Strokes 3 Home Runs
Wednesday, April 15th, 1896
Jean-François Corbeil brutalized the Davenport Juniors pitching staff, launching three home runs in today's 15-0 victory at Juniors Ballpark. The Juneau Juniors shortstop was red-hot, the ball just exploding off his bat all day.

"I don't try to hit homers," he said later. "I try to go out there and put a good swing on the ball, and sometimes it is going to go out of the park. I didn't try to hit the first one or the second one or the third one."

For the game, he went 3-for-6 with 5 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

"Too much Jean-François Corbeil today," Juniors manager Dechanan Richardson said. "That's my assessment of the game. That's what our problems boiled down to."

Jean-François Corbeil grounded out in the 1st, struck out in the 2nd, hit a two-run home run off Jet Blais in the 4th, hit a two-run home run off Curtis Cove in the 5th, hit a solo-shot off Curtis Cove in the 7th and struck out in the 8th.
