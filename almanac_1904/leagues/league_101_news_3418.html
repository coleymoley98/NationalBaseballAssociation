NL Names Rookie of the Month
Friday, June 1st, 1900
Pranthep Graf is beginning to make a name for himself and he did it by posting a 4-3 record and a 3.48 ERA in May. For his spectacular mound performance last month he has been named BNN's Rookie of the Month in the National League.

The 22-year-old newcomer isn't at all intimidated by his lack of experience, at least not outwardly. In fact, by the way he has pitched beyond his years, you could easily mistake him for a veteran.

In 7 starts for the Nordiques Graf has struck out 18 batters in 54.1 innings, holding opposing hitters to a batting average of .299 during his hot streak.
