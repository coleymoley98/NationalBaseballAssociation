DiClemente Socks it to Juniors, Cracks 5 Hits
Monday, March 2nd, 1896
Today at Coude Grounds, the Drummondville Juniors fell to the Coude Juniors despite a splendid performance by right fielder Seth DiClemente.

DiClemente had an exceptional day at the plate, tagging Juniors pitching for 5 base hits, but it was not enough for Drummondville to come away with the win. Coude whipped Drummondville by a score of 10-9.

Seth DiClemente singled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, hit an RBI single in the 5th, struck out in the 6th, singled in the 9th and doubled in the 11th.
