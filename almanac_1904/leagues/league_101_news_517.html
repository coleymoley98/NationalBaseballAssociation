Josh Roberts Takes MVP Trophy
Sunday, September 11th, 1904
Detroit's Josh Roberts put the wraps on a fine campaign today by winning the 1904 National League Most Valuable Player Award.

The 33-year-old veteran scorched the opposition with a .312 average, picking up 125 hits, 19 doubles, 3 triples, 5 home runs and 47 RBIs, while scoring 52 runs.

He received 4 first place votes out of a possible 6. Ara Frey of the Detroit Lions finished second in voting, while Kittipong Jones of the Detroit Lions finished third with 2 first place votes.
