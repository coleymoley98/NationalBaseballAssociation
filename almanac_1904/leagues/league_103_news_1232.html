Sherbrooke-St. Louis Fight Results in Suspensions
Thursday, March 5th, 1896
If there wasn't a history between Khae Griffiths of Sherbrooke and Gavin Hopkins of St. Louis, then there is one now. The incident between the two sparked a bench-clearing scrum with those two being the center of attention. Now they will miss 4 and 9 games respectively as a result of their actions. The league office announced their suspensions today.

"We're both really competitive," Griffiths said later, "and sometimes things go a little too far because of it."

For the year Griffiths is batting .381 with 2 home runs.
