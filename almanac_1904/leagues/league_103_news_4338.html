Juniors Lead with Chin, Get KO'd, 9-0
Thursday, April 24th, 1902
Today was Ron Vigil's turn in the rotation for the Sainte-Paul Juniors and he made the most of it, shutting out the Davenport Juniors on 4 hits.

The 16-year-old right-hander led the Juniors to an easy 9-0 victory, allowing 3 walks and tallying 6 strikeouts.

"I hope we miss him next time," Davenport manager Dechanan Richardson said. "Vigil certainly pitched a strong game against us today. He was in a groove, throwing everything for strikes. Throwing hard. Changing speeds."

In 13 starts this season Vigil has rung up a 5-4 record and a 1.94 ERA.
