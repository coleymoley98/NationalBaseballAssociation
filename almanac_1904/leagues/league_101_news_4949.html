St. Louis No Match for Jones
Tuesday, June 9th, 1903
The St. Louis Cardinaux manager was philosophical in the clubhouse following a shutout loss to the Detroit Lions. The Cardinaux were overpowered by Detroit starter Kittipong Jones, who hurled his club to victory, 5-0.

"It was just one of those games -- shake it off and do better next time," Andy Bernatchez said.

Over 9 innings, Jones blanked St. Louis on 6 hits with 2 walks and 2 strikeouts.

"I kept them off balance. I could tell a lot of them were late on fastballs because they were fouling them off. Once you see that, it sets them up for other pitches," Jones said.

"I set them up, where if they chased one breaking ball, they were going to chase another one."

This year in 10 starts, Jones has recorded 5 wins and absorbed 5 losses. He has pitched 88.1 innings, allowed 84 hits, struck out 30 and has a 2.65 ERA.
