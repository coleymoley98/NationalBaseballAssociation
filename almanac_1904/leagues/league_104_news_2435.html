Wednesday, June 22nd, 1898
Some moments become linked with a particular player's career forever. Willis Vallois of the Quincy Juniors had one of those moments when he went 5-for-5 at the plate today in the 14-3 win over the Morgantown Juniors at Juniors Ballpark.

"I won't forget this anytime soon, and I don't expect it to happen too often," the 21-year-old third baseman told reporters in the postgame interview. "I'll be happy to just get a hit or two every day. Enough to hang around the league a while longer. Like 10, maybe 15 more years."

Willis Vallois singled in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 3rd, hit an RBI double in the 5th, hit a three-run home run off Robert Emerson in the 6th and singled in the 7th.
