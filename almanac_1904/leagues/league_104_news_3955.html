Barrage of Hits Gets Cumming AJC Award
Monday, June 24th, 1901
So far in his young career, Kyle Cumming has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Anglo Junior Circuit in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 18-year-old Fort Wayne Juniors left fielder hit at a torrid .467 clip as he collected 14 hits in 30 at-bats, 3 home runs, 11 RBIs and 7 runs scored.

Currently Cumming has 4 home runs and a .351 batting average.
