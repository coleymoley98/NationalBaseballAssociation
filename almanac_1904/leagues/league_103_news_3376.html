Villain Heads FJC Best Player List
Sunday, May 6th, 1900
Sarod Villain of the Juneau Juniors had a fine season this year and today he accepted the Most Valuable Player Award for 1900.

The 17-year-old shortstop put up a .316 average and a .427 on-base percentage, piling up 59 hits, 6 doubles, 1 triple, 11 home runs, 36 RBIs and 47 runs scored.

He received 6 first place votes out of a possible 12. Newin Vézina of the Sainte-Paul Juniors finished second in voting with 5 first place votes, while Dan McNeill of the Sainte-Paul Juniors finished third with 1 first place vote.
