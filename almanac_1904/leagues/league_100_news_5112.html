Suthep Aiton might not have fully digested the impact of winning July's Canadian League Rookie of the Month award when he got the news earlier today, but the 20-year-old quickly learned that he was no longer your typical rookie.

Posting a mark of 3-4 won-lost record will do that. In 7 starts he tossed 60 innings with 19 strikeouts and a 2.55 ERA. Opposing teams hit .244 against him during the month.

Not bad for a first-year man.
