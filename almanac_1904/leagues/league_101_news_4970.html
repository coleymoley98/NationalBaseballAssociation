Juneau Southpaw Tosses 7-0 Shutout
Tuesday, June 16th, 1903
Nigel Henderson of the Juneau Brasseurs spun his magic on the Detroit Lions today. He blanked them 7-0 on 5 hits, registering 1 strikeout and 3 walks.

"The bottom line was I just kept the ball down," Henderson said. "My breaking stuff was down. Everything was down."

Talking to reporters in the postgame interview, Juneau skipper Owen Turcotte said the team "got solid pitching, good defense and took great team at-bats in a lot of different situations."

"Nigel pitched really well but this was a solid performance by everyone," he said.

This year Henderson has a 4-6 record and a 4.98 ERA.
