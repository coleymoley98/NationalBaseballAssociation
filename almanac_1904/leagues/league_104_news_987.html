Injury Derails Season for Siriwong
Wednesday, July 17th, 1895
The Quincy Juniors have lost the services of Tiloka Siriwong for the rest of this season. He suffered a damaged elbow ligament in a recent ballgame and the follow-up report from the medical staff is not good. Siriwong is out for the year.
