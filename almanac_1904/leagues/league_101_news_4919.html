National League Announces May's Top Pitcher
Monday, June 1st, 1903
Despite the number of years he's been in the league, Garrett Foreman still gets excited when he puts together a quality stretch like he had in May. The 26-year-old starter for Montreal was named National League Pitcher of the Month earlier today.

He collected 5 wins and 3 losses with 46 strikeouts in his 8 starts, putting up a fine 1.57 ERA in 74.2 innings of work.
