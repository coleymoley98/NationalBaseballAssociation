Francophone Junior Circuit Best Pitcher Chosen
Sunday, May 5th, 1901
Dom Charpentier was the most outstanding pitcher in the Francophone Junior Circuit this year and today was acclaimed the Pitcher of the Year Award winner for his efforts.

Charpentier had a marvelous season, going 10-3 with an impressive 1.38 ERA in 15 starts. He struck out 139 in 104.1 innings and limited opposing batters to a .186 batting average.

He received 12 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Curtis Jacobs of the Quebec City Juniors finished second in voting, while Chang Thongman of the Drummondville Juniors finished third.
