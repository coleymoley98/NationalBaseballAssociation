Bouchard's 21-Game Hitting Streak Ends
Monday, May 19th, 1902
It just wasn't Stéphan Bouchard's day.

The Montreal Royales took the 7-3 win today over the Quebec Nordiques, but the Nordiques mound staff did what nobody else could -- they stopped the 21-game hitting streak of Royales left fielder Stéphan Bouchard.

"We might have focused a bit too much on stopping Bouchard," Quebec manager Frédéric Lévesque commented afterward. "He had a nice hitting streak going, but the Royales have other hitters and they did their job today."

So far this season Bouchard has no home runs and a .423 batting average.
