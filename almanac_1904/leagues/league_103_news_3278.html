Sago Smacks 5 Hits for Juniors
Sunday, March 4th, 1900
The Juneau Juniors stopped the Gauthier Juniors by a score of 15-14 at Gauthier Grounds today, with the lion's share of the credit going to right fielder Kim Sago. The 18-year-old had a career day, collecting 5 hits.

"Every batter wants to have a quality at-bat every time he steps up to the plate," Sago said later. "He wants to hit the ball hard."

Sago did just that in his 6 at-bats, much to the chagrin of the Juniors.

"Not much that I can say," offered Gauthier manager Ti Holley. "He just hammered the heck out of us."

Kim Sago singled in the 1st, hit a two-run single in the 2nd, singled in the 5th, singled in the 6th, singled in the 8th and grounded out in the 9th.
