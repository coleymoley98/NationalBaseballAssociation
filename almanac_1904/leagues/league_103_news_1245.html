Big Day for Kessler: Swats 5 Hits
Wednesday, March 11th, 1896
5 times is the charm -- at least for Nitz Kessler.

The Sherbrooke Juniors second baseman tore up the Sainte-Paul Juniors to the tune of 5 hits in 7 at-bats at Field of the Juniors today. The offensive onslaught from Kessler powered the Juniors to a 16-13 win.

"Kessler was a tough out today," Sainte-Paul manager J.J. Aubut said to the press in the postgame interview. "He hit some very good pitches with that quick bat of his. Not an easy out, that's for sure."

Nitz Kessler singled in the 1st, hit a two-run double in the 2nd, grounded out in the 4th, hit a two-run single in the 5th, hit an RBI triple in the 6th, singled in the 8th and grounded out in the 9th.

Kessler is hitting .316 for the season with no home runs, 12 RBIs and 6 runs scored. He has 12 hits in 38 at-bats and played in 9 games.
