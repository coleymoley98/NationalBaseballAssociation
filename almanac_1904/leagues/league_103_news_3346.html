Big 3-HR Day for Jansen
Saturday, April 21st, 1900
Gabriel Jansen went yard three times in a Davenport Juniors victory over the Coude Juniors at Coude Grounds today.

"I was in a groove," he said. "Every pitch they tossed up there looked like a grapefruit."

For the day, the Davenport third baseman went 4-for-5 against Coude with 5 RBIs and 4 runs scored in a brilliant display of power hitting that led the Juniors to the 11-3 win.

"I tell you what. I'm shocked because I didn't think that last ball had enough to get out," he told BNN reporters in the clubhouse following the game. "I thought it was just going to be a long out."

Gabriel Jansen grounded out in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, hit a solo-shot off Olivier Lemieux in the 5th, walked in the 7th, hit a two-run home run off Taksin Gagnon in the 8th and hit a two-run home run off Taksin Gagnon in the 9th.

Currently Jansen is batting .275 with 5 home runs and 18 RBIs.
