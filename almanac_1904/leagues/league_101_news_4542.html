Boyle's Treatment Not Going Well, Suffers Setback
Tuesday, July 15th, 1902
The Quebec Nordiques announced today that Adrian Boyle would be missing for longer than expected as the 27-year-old second baseman recovers from a strained rib cage muscle. "Adrian had been working out with the team recently, but felt some discomfort yesterday," the Nordiques GM explained to the press. "After re-examining the injury, the team's medical staff decided that Boyle would need extra time to heal. Their estimate is 2 weeks more recovery time."

This season Boyle is hitting .294 with 37 hits, 2 home runs, 8 RBIs, scored 15 runs and played in 31 games.
