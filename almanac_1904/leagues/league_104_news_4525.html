Providence-Hartford Brawl, Moreau and Simard Suspended
Tuesday, July 8th, 1902
Yesterday at Juniors Ballpark Luka Moreau and James Simard got into each other's space and then into each other's face. They began to grapple and fell to the ground. That's when it boiled over into a full-fledged bench-clearing tussle between the Providence Juniors and Hartford Juniors. Reason finally prevailed as the umpires restored order. After much discussion, only the two major combatants were ejected from the game.

Today the league office notified the ballclubs that Moreau will have to sit out 5 while Simard would be given a 3-game suspension for their roles in the brawl.

So far this year, Moreau is batting .224 with 4 home runs and 28 RBIs.

Simard is currently batting .143 with no home runs and 4 RBIs.
