Juniors Show No Mercy in 12-0 Thrashing of Juniors
Sunday, April 17th, 1898
Drummondville Juniors pitcher Jesse Langlois really got it done at Juniors Ballpark today in his 12-0 shutout victory over the Quebec City Juniors, shutting them down on 5 hits.

"It was just one of those games where everything went right. I threw a lot of strikes," Langlois said. "And the pitches they did get to hit, they didn't do anything with them."

The 16-year-old issued no walks and had 6 strikeouts in the Juniors win.

On the season Langlois has a 11-2 record with a 2.33 ERA.
