Rieth Sensational, 5-for-5
Sunday, May 14th, 1899
Chicagou Frontenacs center fielder John Rieth had a day to remember at Ballpark of Quebec today, ripping apart the Quebec Nordiques and sparking the Frontenacs to a 13-7 victory.

Rieth wrote his name in the record books with a 5-hit barrage, finishing his special day 5-for-5.

"I'm usually a first ball hitter," Rieth told BNN reporters in the postgame press conference. "I think pitchers think batters are going to let the first one go by. I never do. I've always found that it's better to pull the trigger early in the count. Pitchers get real nasty when they get ahead of you, so I try to avoid it."

John Rieth doubled in the 1st, walked in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, singled in the 6th, hit an RBI double in the 8th and hit an RBI single in the 9th.

So far this year Rieth is batting .351 with no home runs.
