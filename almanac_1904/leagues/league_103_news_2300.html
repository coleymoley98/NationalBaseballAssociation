Juniors Shut Out Juniors, Che-moh Winner
Wednesday, April 20th, 1898
The Juneau Juniors did little to support starter Hafit Che-moh against the Davenport Juniors, but it turned out he didn't need the help.

The right-hander spun a complete game shutout against the Juniors, striking out 12 and surrendering 3 hits. The performance from Che-moh allowed Juneau to escape with the 2-0 win despite an anemic offense.

"Nobody came to me and said 'Hafit, we're gonna need you to win this one for us today,' but I could feel it," Che-moh told BNN reporters following the win. "I'm the guy that will step up for you when you need it."

This year Che-moh has compiled a 7-5 mark with a 1.76 ERA.
