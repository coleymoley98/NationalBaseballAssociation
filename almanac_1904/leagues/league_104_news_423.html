Colbourn Chosen AJC's Best Pitcher
Wednesday, August 3rd, 1904
New Britain's Brendan Colbourn took home the 1904 Anglo Junior Circuit Pitcher of the Year Award earlier today after a remarkable season on the mound.

With a record of 8-3, Colbourn was certainly a worthy winner. He made 13 starts and posted 105.2 innings, 121 strikeouts and a 1.36 ERA.

He received 12 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Marc Fournier of the Fort Wayne Juniors finished second in voting, while Naithim Boonsom of the Buffalo Juniors finished third.
