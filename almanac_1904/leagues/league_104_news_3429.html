Belton Guns Down Juniors
Monday, June 4th, 1900
The Hartford Juniors did little to support starter James Belton against the Halifax Juniors, but it turned out he didn't need the help.

The right-hander spun a complete game shutout against the Juniors, striking out 9 and surrendering 2 hits. The performance from Belton allowed Hartford to escape with the 3-0 win despite an anemic offense.

"Nobody came to me and said 'James, we're gonna need you to win this one for us today,' but I could feel it," Belton told BNN reporters following the win. "I'm the guy that will step up for you when you need it."
