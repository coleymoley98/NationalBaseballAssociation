1901 AJC Pitcher of the Year Award Named
Sunday, August 4th, 1901
Chatchawal Srithong finished off a fine season by taking home the Anglo Junior Circuit Pitcher of the Year Award today.

The Morgantown hurler went 11-2 on the year, posting a 1.46 ERA. Srithong also tallied 116 strikeouts in 105 innings and allowed opposing batters to hit only .197 against him.

He received 11 first place votes out of a possible 12. Senquez Hopkins of the Buffalo Juniors finished second in voting with 1 first place vote, while Christopher Berglin of the Fort Wayne Juniors finished third.
