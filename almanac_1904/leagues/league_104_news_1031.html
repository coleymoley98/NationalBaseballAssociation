Jake Dieckmann Chosen AJC's Best Player
Sunday, August 4th, 1895
Jake Dieckmann of the Springfield Juniors proved that you can step out of the shadows and onto the stage if you've got the talent and the drive to hone your skills. Hard work, long hours and a tireless resolve paid off for him today in the form of the Anglo Junior Circuit Most Valuable Player Award.

Dieckmann hit for a .344 average while collecting 67 hits, 9 doubles, 2 triples, 4 home runs, 31 RBIs and scoring 39 times.

He received 4 first place votes out of a possible 12. Harrison Murch of the Halifax Juniors finished second in voting with 3 first place votes, while Stan Adams of the Toronto Juniors finished third with 2 first place votes.
