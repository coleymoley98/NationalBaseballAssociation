AJC's Top Player, Weangsong of Fort Wayne
Monday, July 18th, 1898
So far in his young career, Tossakorn Weangsong has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Anglo Junior Circuit in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 16-year-old Fort Wayne Juniors third baseman hit at a torrid .559 clip as he collected 19 hits in 34 at-bats, 3 home runs, 10 RBIs and 8 runs scored.

Presently Weangsong is batting .358 with 64 hits and 6 home runs. He has knocked in 34 runs and scored 27 times.
