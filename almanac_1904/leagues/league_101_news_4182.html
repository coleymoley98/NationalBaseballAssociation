Detroit-Quebec Trade: Adams for Mentink
Wednesday, October 9th, 1901
Sutat Durham, general manager of the Detroit Lions, has signed off on a trade for Quebec's 25-year-old CF Cal Mentink. To complete the deal, the Lions will send 26-year-old LF Stan Adams and 23-year-old RHP Curtis Cove to the Nordiques.


Mentink has a career .278 batting average with a .329 OBP. He has hit 7 homers, driven in 89 runs and scored 161 runs since he came into the league.
