Bourbonnais Nails 4 Homers Against Springfield
Sunday, June 28th, 1903
In a game for the ages, Morgantown Juniors right fielder Wade Bourbonnais launched 4 home runs at Juniors Ballpark against the Springfield Juniors.

Time after time, Bourbonnais found his stroke against the shell-shocked Juniors pitching staff, finishing with 6 RBIs and scoring 4 times as the Juniors took the 10-0 victory.

"They pitch 'em and I hit 'em. I just did it a little better than them today," Bourbonnais said to reporters after his historic game. "I won big today, but they might get me out 4 times tomorrow."

Wade Bourbonnais struck out in the 1st, hit a solo-shot off J.R. Blais in the 4th, hit a two-run home run off J.R. Blais in the 6th, hit a solo-shot off Ronny Rochon in the 8th and hit a two-run home run off Roland Greer in the 9th.

Bourbonnais on the season is batting .323 with 7 home runs. His totals include 20 RBIs and 17 runs scored. He has played in 25 games.
