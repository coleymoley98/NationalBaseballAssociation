Montreal Rookie Hurls His Way to NL Honor
Sunday, August 1st, 1897
Few players his age have the consistency to put a solid month together, but Montreal's Reuban Seals carved up the opposition all July long and hauled off the National League Rookie of the Month award today as a result.

He sizzled last month with a 3-2 mark, fanning 18 in 52.2 innings with an ERA of 3.08 in 7 starts.

Checking out his stats for the season, Seals has racked up 6 wins and 7 losses in 16 starts, 3.49 ERA and struck out 41 batters in 116 innings.
