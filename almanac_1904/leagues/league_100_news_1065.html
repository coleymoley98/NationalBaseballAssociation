Konchok Breaks New Single-Season Record with Win #22
Wednesday, August 21st, 1895
The Boston Redcoats posted a win today at Huntington Avenue Grounds. Piyawat Konchok, the winning pitcher, set a single-season record with the victory. He now has 22 wins this year. Phutchapong Sanford held the old mark. "I'm having the time of my life," Konchok told reporters after the game. "What's not to like?"

Season-to-date Konchok has rung up 22 wins and 13 losses in 36 appearances. He has an ERA of 3.71 and has fanned 114 batters in 296.1 innings.
