Gosnell Tabbed NL's Top Rookie
Friday, September 1st, 1899
One of the bright young starters in the National League, Hunter Gosnell is living up to the potential that Frontenacs scouts thought might be there.

As a starter for Chicagou, Gosnell paced his team with a 5-2 record in August to earn himself Rookie of the Month accolades.

He made 8 starts, tossed 68 innings, struck out 11 batters and allowed the opposition to hit .263 against him.

Gosnell has posted a 12-12 record for the year with a 4.40 ERA. Teams are hitting .308 against him and he has rung up 40 strikeouts in 204.2 innings.
