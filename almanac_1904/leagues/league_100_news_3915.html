New York-Quebec Trade: Guay for Lynch
Tuesday, June 4th, 1901
Sometimes a change of scenery can be a beneficial to a baseball player. That is the hope in New York and Quebec as the teams came to an agreement today which sends 25-year-old RF Tommy Guay and 24-year-old LF Édouard Ratté to the Nordiques and 26-year-old LF Joseph Lynch to the Rebels. "Only time will tell if this is the right move for everyone," stated the Quebec GM.


Currently Guay is batting .355 with no home runs and 7 RBIs.
