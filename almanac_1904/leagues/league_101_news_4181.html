Montreal Royales Ship Hannah to New York
Tuesday, October 8th, 1901
According to BNN there were mixed emotions by the players involved in the trade between the Montreal Royales and the New York Rebels. As part of the deal, the Royales swapped 25-year-old 2B Frank Hannah to the Rebels getting 25-year-old RHP Khaosai Lahso and 23-year-old LF Amnach Chawna in return. The players indicated it was good to get a fresh new start in another city, but it was difficult to leave your friends on the old team.


In 316 career games Hannah has batted .289 with 344 hits with 27 doubles, 39 triples and 6 home runs. He has also collected 132 RBIs and scored 198 runs.
