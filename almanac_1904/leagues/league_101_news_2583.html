Foreman Gets 8-0 Shutout
Sunday, August 7th, 1898
It wasn't easy but Garrett Foreman made it look that way.

Foreman silenced the Detroit Lions, 8-0, in a Montreal Royales triumph at Montreal Grounds today. Despite the appearance of dominance, Foreman said later that he felt like he was struggling all game. He gave up 1 walk and 5 hits.

"It didn't feel quite right today, but I made the decision to bear down and work through it," he said. "I made a few mistakes but it goes to show what you can do with grit and not much else."

To date Foreman is 17-10 in 33 starts with a 2.15 ERA and has 137 strikeouts in 281 innings.
