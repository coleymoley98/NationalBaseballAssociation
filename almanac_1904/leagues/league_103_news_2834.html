Juniors Pitching Ravaged by Yuan
Wednesday, April 19th, 1899
Montreal second baseman Anirut Yuan had a day to remember today, 5-for-5 against the St. Louis Juniors at Ballpark of St. Louis as the Juniors picked up a 11-7 victory.

"He was patient up there today," Montreal skipper Jules Couture told the press after the game. "I like the way he waited for his pitch and wasn't overly aggressive. Yuan has a tendency to chase bad balls sometimes."

Anirut Yuan singled in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 2nd, walked in the 4th, singled in the 6th, singled in the 7th and doubled in the 8th.

Yuan is hitting .220 so far this season with 12 RBIs and 29 runs scored. He has 39 hits including 1 home run, 16 walks and .310 OBP.
