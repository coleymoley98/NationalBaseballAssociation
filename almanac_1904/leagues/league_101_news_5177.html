Montreal Hurler Captures 100th Victory
Sunday, August 23rd, 1903
Montreal's Garrett Foreman took the hill at Montreal Grounds today in search of his 100th career win. The 26-year-old ace found it against the St. Louis Cardinaux.

After the historic victory, his manager told reporters, "I saw a tremendous sigh of relief when this thing was over. He was just looking to hug everyone in sight."

He worked 1 innings with no strikeouts and no walks in the 7-6 victory, checking St. Louis on no hits.

"It a big milestone in my career," admitted Foreman. "When you get close to it, you worry about it a lot. It's done now... and I can relax and enjoy it."

In career stats, the 26-year-old Foreman has won 100 and lost 78, recording 791 strikeouts and 419 walks in 1671.1 innings with a 2.83 ERA. Opponents have hit .251 against him.
