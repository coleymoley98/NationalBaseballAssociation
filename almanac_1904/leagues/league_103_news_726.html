Simoyan and Fazzini Brawl, League Suspends Them
Sunday, March 24th, 1895
After an intense confrontation, both the Juniors Kasemchai Simoyan and the Juniors Gregory Fazzini were handed their punishments today. The league commissioner's office issued suspensions for an on-the-field conflict that occurred at a recent Davenport-Gallant game. Kasemchai Simoyan will miss 4 games, while Gregory Fazzini was suspended for 9 games.

"Sometimes we go too far and have to pay the consequences," Kasemchai Simoyan commented later. "Hopefully cooler heads will prevail the next time we play."

For the year Simoyan is batting .352 with 31 hits and 1 home run. He has scored 17 times and driven in 10.

Fazzini has taken the mound for 6 relief appearances this season, nailing down no saves while notching a 1-0 record with a 3.07 ERA. The Gallant reliever has limited opposing batters to a .237 batting average.
