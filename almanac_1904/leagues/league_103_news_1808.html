Youngblood Electric in 7-0 Win
Sunday, April 4th, 1897
In an excellent mound performance, Clap Youngblood of the Gauthier Juniors whitewashed the St. Louis Juniors 7-0 on a 2-hitter.

"I had my best stuff and did a good job of mixing up my pitches," he said after the game.

In all, the Gauthier hurler rang up 10 strikeouts and 1 walk in the shutout win.

"We just didn't swing the bats well today," said Juniors manager Sherwin Diggs. "Youngblood did a good job keeping us guessing."

This year Youngblood has socked 12 hits in 38 at-bats for a .316 average with no home runs. He is getting on base at a .366 pace and his totals also include 7 RBIs and 5 runs scored.
