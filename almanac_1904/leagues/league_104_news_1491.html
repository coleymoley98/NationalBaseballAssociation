5-Hit Blitz by Savard
Wednesday, July 8th, 1896
It's not something you read about in the sports pages every day -- a 5-for-5 day. For many, it might come around only once in a career -- and for Benny Savard, it came around today.

The 19-year-old first baseman scattered hits all over Juniors Ballpark to pace the Canton Juniors to a 8-7 victory over the Providence Juniors.

Providence skipper Luke Cairns told reporters in the press room that "Savard deserves a hat-tip."

"He hit some good pitches against us," he said. "In fact, according to our scouting reports, Savard hit several that he is not supposed to be able to hit."

Benny Savard walked in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, singled in the 7th, singled in the 9th and hit a solo-shot off Bridger Errico in the 10th.

This season Savard is batting at a .306 clip with 8 home runs and 23 RBIs.
