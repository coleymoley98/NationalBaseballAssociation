Sakowicz Stops Montreal
Tuesday, August 23rd, 1904
Eugene Sakowicz subdued the Montreal Royales, 2-0, pitching a 10-hit gem for the Quebec Nordiques at Montreal Grounds.

"He was kind of teasing them with his off-speed stuff, and his fastball was moving everywhere," Quebec skipper Frédéric Lévesque said afterward.

Sakowicz registered 5 strikeouts and issued 2 walks in his superb performance.

"We just didn't swing the bats very well. Or else he just pitched good, whichever way you want to look at it," said Montreal manager Kamnan Rowe after the defeat.

On the season Sakowicz has compiled a 6-5 mark with a 2.67 ERA in 12 starts. In pitching 94.1 innings he has 57 strikeouts and 17 walks while limiting the opposition to a .265 batting average.
