It's Official: 2B Taylor Signs with Royales
Thursday, October 10th, 1895
Royales officials presented Grady Taylor to members of the local print media today as their new second baseman.

The deal, worth $10,860, guarantees the 28-year-old a home in Montreal for 6 years. It should improve the ballclub, assuming he plays to his potential.

Taylor has a career .330 average with 267 hits, including 37 doubles, 19 triples and 16 homers, collected 150 RBIs, scored 188 runs and compiled a .399 OBP.
