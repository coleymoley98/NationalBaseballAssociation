Redcoats Sanford Chosen August's Best in CL
Wednesday, September 1st, 1897
Phutchapong Sanford's been around the league long enough to appreciate the honor he received today. After going 5-2 in August, the 29-year-old Boston hurler has been named Canadian League Pitcher of the Month.

He fanned 18 in 70.2 innings, logged an ERA of 2.29 and allowed opponents to hit .239 against him.

This year Sanford has a 18-13 record with a 3.54 ERA.
