Hit Parade: Boucher Pops 5 Hits
Sunday, June 5th, 1898
Today at Juniors Ballpark, the Quincy Juniors topped the Toronto Juniors in spite of a standout batting performance by shortstop Benjamin Boucher.

Boucher had a very impressive day at the plate, spanking Quincy pitching for 5 base hits, but couldn't do enough for a win. Quincy whipped Toronto by the score of 17-15.

Benjamin Boucher hit an RBI double in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, hit an RBI double in the 5th, hit a sac fly in the 6th, hit a solo-shot off Bernard Muller in the 7th and singled in the 9th.

Boucher on the season is hitting .542 with 1 home run. His stats include 9 RBIs and 9 runs scored.
