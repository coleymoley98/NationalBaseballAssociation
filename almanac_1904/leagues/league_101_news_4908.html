Catteau Defeats Cardinaux For #150
Wednesday, May 27th, 1903
History was made today at St. Louis Grounds -- Val Catteau won game #150 to seal his name in the league record books. The Quebec ace downed the St. Louis Cardinaux 9-1 to gain entrance into the elite 150 Club.

"I was wound up at first. I think I threw too hard," remarked Catteau, "I had to calm down and go out there and pitch... not just throw. I think I got better as the game wore on. I started to think less about 150 wins and more about doing what I had to do."

During his career Catteau's won-lost record is 150 wins and 102 losses with a 3.16 ERA. He has limited other teams to a .281 average.
