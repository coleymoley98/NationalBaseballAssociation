Bob Walks Away from the Game
Friday, July 22nd, 1898
Jetsada Bob said he will miss "leading the boys into battle" after this season. At 38 years old, the team leader of the Boston Redcoats has announced he will retire at season's end.

"I've always tried to be a guy that leads by example and I'd like to think I've been a good leader in my career," he said. "There's a lot of things I'm going to miss about the sport, but I think most of all is the camaraderie with the guys."

During his career Bob has recorded 28 wins and 26 losses with a 4.17 ERA and limited other teams to a .295 average.
