NL Star of the Week Honored
Monday, July 18th, 1904
It's always a thrill for fans of the team and students of the game alike when a superstar in the making shows what he's got. James Millner, the Quebec Nordiques 25-year-old first baseman, put himself in the spotlight last week making him the clear-cut choice for National League Player of the Week honors.

He ripped 9 hits in 14 at-bats, compiling a .643 average with no home runs and 2 RBIs.

This year Millner is hitting .279 with 63 hits, 1 home run, 21 RBIs and has scored 33 runs.
