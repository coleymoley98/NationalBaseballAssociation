Nordiques Albertson Stops Frontenacs
Thursday, May 23rd, 1895
James Albertson of the Quebec Nordiques worked his mound magic on the Chicagou Frontenacs. He blanked them 5-0 on 6 hits, registering 3 strikeouts and 1 walk.

"Making good pitches is the name of the game and I made some good ones in this one," said Albertson after the game.

This year Albertson has a 5-2 mark in 8 starts with a 3.39 ERA. He has given up 85 hits in 71.2 innings, struck out 18 and issued 8 walks. Opposing clubs have compiled a .282 batting average against him.
