Chicagou Frontenacs Ship Desjardins to Philadelphia
Saturday, February 13th, 1904
Everybody is overjoyed with a trade consummated today by the Chicagou Frontenacs and the Philadelphia Liberty. In the deal Chicagou got 25-year-old LHP Watiwat Lader and 21-year-old CF Logan Descôteaux from Philadelphia and dealt 28-year-old SS A.J. Desjardins to the Liberty. The general managers that made the swap are happy and the players are happy, too. One of the GMs commented, "This was a good swap for all concerned."


Lifetime Desjardins has compiled a .246 batting average and collected 79 hits, 1 home run and 17 RBIs.
