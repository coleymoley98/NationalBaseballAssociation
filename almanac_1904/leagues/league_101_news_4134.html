NL Best Newcomer Trophy Awarded
Sunday, September 1st, 1901
Joël Poulin might not have fully digested the impact of winning August's National League Rookie of the Month award when he got the news earlier today, but the 24-year-old quickly learned that he was no longer your typical rookie.

Posting a mark of 3-3 won-lost record will do that. In 6 starts he tossed 52.1 innings with 19 strikeouts and a 2.92 ERA. Opposing teams hit .251 against him during the month.

Not bad for a first-year man.

So far this season Poulin has a record of 7-14 with 63 strikeouts and 72 walks in 189.2 innings and a 4.56 ERA.
