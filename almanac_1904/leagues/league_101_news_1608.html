Queen Breaks Record with Strikeout
Thursday, August 20th, 1896
In today's game at Montreal Grounds, Jayden Queen, the Montreal pitcher, set a new single-season record for strikeouts in a season. Queen toppled the old record when he recorded strikeout number 100. Ryan Verville held the previous high mark.

So far this year Queen has a 11-16 record with a 4.68 ERA.
