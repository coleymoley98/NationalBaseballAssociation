Royer Hurls 4-0 Shutout
Saturday, July 14th, 1900
The Chicagou Frontenacs shut out the Detroit Lions 4-0 today behind the strong pitching of Zachary Royer. Royer surrendered only 5 hits in recording the complete-game victory, and struck out 3.

Following the last out, Royer appeared to celebrate a little too boisterously for Detroit manager Adrian Miramontes, who could be seen scolding the famously outspoken right-hander as the clubs left the field. Royer's own manager then intervened with some words for his counterpart.

The Lions skipper would not comment on the exchange, but at the Frontenacs press conference, Royer confirmed that it was regarding the celebration.

"He basically told me I was being a lousy winner," Royer said.

"And I told him he was being a lousy loser," Chicagou manager Mustafa Campeau added.

In season stats Royer shows a 13-9 record in 24 games, 211.2 innings, 70 strikeouts and a 1.91 ERA.
