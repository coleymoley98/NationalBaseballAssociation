Hutchison Shows Off, Hits for Cycle
Friday, July 24th, 1896
Some days everything just falls in place.

It all came together for A.J. Hutchison and the New Britain Juniors today at Juniors Ballpark. The Juniors went home winners and the second baseman had a 4-for-6 day that included the cycle, a baseball rarity.

"He sure was a wrecking ball today," said New Britain manager Conrad Thompson following his club's 10-6 taming of the Canton Juniors.

A.J. Hutchison doubled in the 1st, grounded out in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, tripled in the 7th, hit a solo-shot off Butch Gourlay in the 9th and grounded out in the 11th.

This season Hutchison is batting .206 with 2 home runs. His totals include 7 RBIs and 14 runs scored.
