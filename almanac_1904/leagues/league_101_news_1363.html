Quebec's Tanguay Snaps Up Player of the Week
Monday, May 25th, 1896
A battle-tested Thawatchai Tanguay declared war on opposing pitchers last week, attacking the ball like the well-schooled veteran he is. Today the 37-year-old Quebec first baseman collected the National League Player of the Week Award.

He wrapped up the week with a .474 average, 9 hits in 19 at-bats, 1 home run, 6 RBIs and 4 runs scored.

For the year Tanguay has hit at a .346 clip with 15 RBIs, 1 home run and 9 runs scored.
