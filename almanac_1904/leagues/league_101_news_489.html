Winters Tops NL with .341 Average
Thursday, August 25th, 1904
Winfred Winters was born to be a ballplayer.

"Pops always said I learned to swing a bat before I learned to walk."

Winters, the 26-year-old Quebec shortstop, really displayed his hitting ability this past season.

His .341 batting average led all National League hitters. He collected 109 hits, socked no home runs, scored 46 runs and drove in 23 runs. The NL's top batter also played in 82 games, had 4 doubles, 9 triples and walked 14 times.
