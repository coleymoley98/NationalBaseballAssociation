AJC Best Mound Performer Named
Monday, August 3rd, 1896
This past season Zachary Royer put on quite a show in the Anglo Junior Circuit. In 17 starts he posted a 9-4 record for Toronto to capture the 1896 Pitcher of the Year Award.

In 17 starts and 139 innings, Royer yielded 87 hits, holding the opposition to a .175 average. He chalked up 186 strikeouts and posted a 1.04 ERA.

He received 12 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Romie Huynh of the Buffalo Juniors finished second in voting, while Kittipong Jones of the Quincy Juniors finished third.
