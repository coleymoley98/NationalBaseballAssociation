Lagacé Earns April's Top Batter Prize in FJC
Wednesday, May 1st, 1901
Michael Lagacé put together a good April to earn the Francophone Junior Circuit Batter of the Month prize. A virtual unknown, he made the most of his chance to prove to Minneapolis officials that he belongs on the big league roster.

The Juniors right fielder posted a .432 batting average with 5 home runs, 19 RBIs and 21 runs scored.

His career numbers show Lagacé has batted .302 and collected 171 hits, 22 home runs and 85 RBIs.
