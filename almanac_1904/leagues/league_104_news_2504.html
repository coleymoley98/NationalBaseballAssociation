Malone Swings for the Cycle
Thursday, July 14th, 1898
Finishing with 4 hits is a great day for any player, but Wes Malone made it even more special by hitting for the cycle at Juniors Ballpark today.

Malone had 1 single, 1 double, 1 triple and 1 home run for the Pawtucket Juniors and led them to a 17-1 decision over the Springfield Juniors. The left fielder also finished with 5 runs scored and 1 RBI.

"Awesome," Malone said when asked how the accomplishment felt.

Wes Malone tripled in the 1st, doubled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, walked in the 6th, hit a solo-shot off J.R. Betts in the 7th and flied out in the 9th.

Malone is presently hitting at a .382 pace with 6 home runs, 22 RBIs and has scored 45 times.
