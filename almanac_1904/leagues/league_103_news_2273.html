Rosa Whitewashes Montreal, 7-0
Thursday, March 31st, 1898
Drummondville Juniors pitcher Cy Rosa really got it done at Ballpark of Montreal today in his 7-0 shutout victory over the Montreal Juniors, shutting them down on 5 hits.

"It was just one of those games where everything went right. I threw a lot of strikes," Rosa said. "And the pitches they did get to hit, they didn't do anything with them."

The 16-year-old issued 1 walk and had 12 strikeouts in the Juniors win.

In 12 starts this year Rosa has crafted a 6-4 record and a 1.61 ERA.
