FJC's #1 Batter Announced
Monday, April 1st, 1901
When Josh Birdsong stepped up to the plate in March he did it with confidence, and today he was honored with the Francophone Junior Circuit Batter of the Month award.

The young Sherbrooke left fielder posted a .359 batting average with 13 home runs, 40 RBIs and scored 23 runs. Birdsong also worked pitchers for 11 walks to post a .426 on-base percentage.
