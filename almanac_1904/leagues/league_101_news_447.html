Summerford Stops Quebec Cold, 4-0
Sunday, August 14th, 1904
It wasn't easy but the Chicagou Frontenacs made it look that way.

Starter Jamie Summerford shut out the Quebec Nordiques despite having what he later called "sub-par" stuff, and the Chicagou Frontenacs cruised to victory, 4-0. Summerford allowed only 3 hits and rang up 1 strikeout but insisted later that he did not deserve primary credit for the win.

"It didn't feel quite right today, but I got a lot of help to get through," he said. "I trusted my catcher and the guys behind me bailed me out more than once when I screwed up."

Summerford has fashioned a 13-11 mark for the season, 2.18 ERA and thrown 235.2 innings in 26 starts. Opposing clubs are batting .233 against him.
