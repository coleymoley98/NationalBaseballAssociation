FJC Player of the Week Announced
Monday, April 23rd, 1900
Youth was served last week as 16-year-old Jukkapant Simard put together a remarkable stretch on the mound, outpacing all other Francophone Junior Circuit pitchers and hitters to win Player of the Week honors.

The Coude Juniors hurler rang up 29 strikeouts in 23 innings, compiling an ERA of 0.39 and posting a fine 3-0 mark.

For the season Simard has logged 10 wins and 2 losses and struck out 129 batters in 113.2 innings while compiling a 1.27 ERA.
