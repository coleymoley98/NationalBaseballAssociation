Boston Trades Sansook for New York's Becker
Tuesday, December 17th, 1895
In a hastily called press conference, Boston announced today a trade with New York that will send 26-year-old 1B Saharat Sansook to the Rebels in exchange for 31-year-old RHP Moussa Becker and 22-year-old RHP Alessandro Sterling. All players are expected to be in their respective new cities in time for today's games.


In his 165-game career Sansook is hitting .307 with 208 hits, 33 doubles, 6 triples and 9 home runs. He has scored 117 runs and driven in 121.
