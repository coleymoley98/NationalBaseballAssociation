One Run Enough for Gallant
Tuesday, March 29th, 1898
Joshua Ouellet of the Gallant Juniors whitewashed the Sherbrooke Juniors 1-0 in a complete-game win, limiting them to 3 hits while chalking up 7 strikeouts and giving up 3 walks.

"He's got the makeup. He's not afraid to challenge guys," Gallant manager Logan Dubé said to BNN reporters after the game. "Joshua is poised out there. He knows how to pitch and how to use his stuff."

This year Ouellet has made 9 starts, thrown 61.1 innings, fanned 44 batters and walked 7. His won-lost record is 5-2 with a 1.32 ERA.
