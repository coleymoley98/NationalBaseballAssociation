East Division Defeats West Division 17-16 in Tight All-Star Game
Tuesday, June 11th, 1895
Juniors Ballpark was the place to be for baseball fans tonight, as the best FJC players gathered for the annual all-star game.

On the field, the East Division downed the West Division by a score of 17-16. Off the field, game MVP Julius Hoskins said he thinks everyone had a good night.

"You looked into the stands and it seemed like everyone was having a fantastic time," said the 17-year-old, who went 3-for-5 in the game. "A game like this is good for the sport."
