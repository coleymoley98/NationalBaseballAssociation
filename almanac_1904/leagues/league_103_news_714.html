5-for-5 Day for Piyaporn
Monday, March 11th, 1895
It was a big day for Sompob Piyaporn and he brought the Davenport Juniors along for the ride.

Piyaporn led his club to a 16-7 victory over the Coude Juniors today at Ballpark of Davenport, smoking Coude pitching for 5 hits in 5 at-bats. The 19-year-old catcher later called the victory "sweet."

Sompob Piyaporn hit an RBI single in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, hit a two-run single in the 4th, singled in the 6th and doubled in the 8th.

In his season totals Piyaporn has no home runs and a .325 batting average.
