McDaniels Sets Home Run Record
Friday, August 14th, 1896
Bruce McDaniels has had a spectacular year this season. Today at Montreal Grounds the Montreal great cracked his 11th roundtripper of the season and broke the league record held by the legendary Grady Taylor. The fans went wild and gave him repeated curtain calls for his historic blast. Speaking with reporters at the postgame press conference McDaniels told them how he was going to celebrate his new record. "I'll celebrate a little with my family tonight, but not too much. Gotta to go back to work tomorrow, you know."

Bruce McDaniels grounded out in the 1st and homered off Danny Papillon in the 3rd.

So far this season McDaniels has gotten 122 hits in 377 at-bats for a .324 batting average with 11 home runs. His stats include 69 RBIs and 64 runs scored. He has played in 95 games.
