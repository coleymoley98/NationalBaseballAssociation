Injury Setback for Frontenacs Sala
Tuesday, August 23rd, 1898
The Chicagou Frontenacs plan to take it slow with left fielder D.J. Sala, knowing that his recovery from knee tendinitis would likely include a few bumps in the road. A team spokesman told BNN, "D.J. has up days and down days. It's still a process. He was feeling really good and then he had a step back. The doctors say it will be about 6 days before he can be activated."

This season Sala has hit at a .422 clip and produced 6 runs batted in, no home runs and 12 runs.
