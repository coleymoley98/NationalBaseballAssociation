Quincy Skunked by Fournier, 4-0
Monday, June 6th, 1904
Marc Fournier was in a zone of his own most of the day, shutting out the Quincy Juniors on 3 hits.

"He was too darn good for us today," grumbled Quincy manager Ethan Bax after the 4-0 loss to the Fort Wayne Juniors.

Fournier finished with 7 strikeouts and 2 walks in a strong outing.

In the postgame press conference, Fort Wayne manager Ace Dugandzic said Fournier "had good velocity, good movement and had those guys off balance. He just pitched his butt off."
