Monday, September 3rd, 1900
The Boston Redcoats ignited a huge celebration as they beat the Toronto Blue Sox 5-1 to win the Canadian Series.

Boston won the series 4-2, capturing the title for the 5th time.

"We stepped it up at the right time, and now we're the champs," said Boston manager Herb Lamaison. "It's a great feeling."

The champs finished the regular season 70-38 and claimed first place in the .

"I am so happy for our city right now," he said.
