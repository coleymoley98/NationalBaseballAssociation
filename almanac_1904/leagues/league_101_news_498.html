Sakowicz Named August's #1 Newcomer
Thursday, September 1st, 1904
He's only in his debut season, but Eugene Sakowicz performed more like a veteran on the hill for the Quebec Nordiques and walked away with August's National League Rookie of the Month Award in the process.

Last month this young hurler recorded 4 wins and 1 loss in 5 starts. He threw 44 innings, giving up 7 walks, fanning 29 and compiling a 1.64 ERA.

On the year Sakowicz has compiled a 6-5 mark with a 2.67 ERA.
