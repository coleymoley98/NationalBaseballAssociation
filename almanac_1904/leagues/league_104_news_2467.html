Pelletier Slaps 5 Hits
Sunday, July 3rd, 1898
It's not something you read about in the sports pages every day -- a 5-for-7 day. For many, it might come around only once in a career -- and for Alexandre Pelletier, it came around today.

The 19-year-old catcher scattered hits all over Juniors Ballpark to pace the Buffalo Juniors to a 30-6 victory over the Toronto Juniors.

Toronto skipper Samuel Sénécal told reporters in the press room that "Pelletier deserves a hat-tip."

"He hit some good pitches against us," he said. "In fact, according to our scouting reports, Pelletier hit several that he is not supposed to be able to hit."

Alexandre Pelletier singled in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, hit a solo-shot off Siripong More in the 2nd, hit an RBI single in the 3rd, walked in a run in the 4th, struck out in the 4th, struck out in the 6th and singled in the 8th.

This year Pelletier is batting .270 with 31 hits and 1 home run. He has scored 19 times and has batted in 18 runs.
