Hit Parade: Marquis Gets Cycle
Tuesday, March 17th, 1896
Davenport left fielder Carson Marquis had a field day at Juniors Ballpark, battering Quebec City pitchers and hitting for the cycle.

Marquis sparked the Juniors to a 8-5 triumph over the Juniors by going 5-for-5 with 6 RBI and scoring 2 runs.

"I was seeing the ball great today," Marquis said. "I'm glad it translated into results on the field."

Carson Marquis hit an RBI double in the 1st, hit a two-run home run off Cromwell Kidwell in the 3rd, singled in the 6th, hit an RBI single in the 7th and hit a two-run triple in the 9th.

For the season Marquis has hit .356 with 2 home runs and 17 RBIs. He has played in 16 games and scored 10 times.
