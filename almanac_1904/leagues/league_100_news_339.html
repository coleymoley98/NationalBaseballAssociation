Wolfeburg Trades Wales for Toronto's Medlin
Friday, July 8th, 1904
BNN has learned the Wolfeburg Celtics have reportedly worked out a deal with the Toronto Blue Sox. Sources close to the Celtics indicated that a trade has been reached in principle where 39-year-old 3B Prasarn Heemkhao and 23-year-old RHP Witchaya Medlin will be swapped to Wolfeburg with 29-year-old SS Tex Wales and 22-year-old CF Prasarn Chanapa heading to Toronto. Neither organization would comment on the report, saying it was club policy not to speak about a trade until it was finalized.

Toronto will retain a portion of Heemkhao's remaining contract.

So far this year Wales has hit .260 with no home runs, 28 RBIs and 22 runs scored.

In 9 starts this season Medlin has crafted a 4-5 record with a 3.46 ERA.
