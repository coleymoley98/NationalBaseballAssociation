Nathan Giguère Wins MVP in East Division Victory over West Division
Tuesday, June 8th, 1897
Although his team came out on the losing end, nobody could deny Nathan Giguère's performance in this year's all-star game.

The West Division All-Stars fell to the East Division All-Stars by a score of 13-12, but Giguère was still named the game's MVP.

"You would have liked to have won the game of course, but being named the MVP is a great honor," he said from his team's dressing room at Ballpark of Montreal.

In the contest, Giguère went 2 for 4.
