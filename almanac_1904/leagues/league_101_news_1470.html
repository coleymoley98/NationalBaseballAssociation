NL Announces June's Best Starter
Wednesday, July 1st, 1896
James Albertson took a much-deserved turn in the spotlight earlier today as he accepted the National League Pitcher of the Month award for June.

The 26-year-old Nordiques starter is a relative newcomer to the league, but nonetheless racked up an impressive 8-1 won-lost record in 9 starts. This month he fanned 24 batters in 81.2 innings and compiled a 2.09 ERA.

This season Albertson has 16 wins and 3 losses and a 2.07 ERA.
