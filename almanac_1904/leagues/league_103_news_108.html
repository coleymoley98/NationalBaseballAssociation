FJC's Top Player, Thompson of St. Louis
Monday, April 18th, 1904
J.R. Thompson burst onto the Francophone Junior Circuit stage in a big way last week. This promising rookie catcher for St. Louis garnered everyone's respect by putting up some remarkable numbers and picking up the Player of the Week trophy.

The 16-year-old haunted opposing pitchers hitting .481 (13-for-27) with 1 home run and 8 RBIs last week.

For the year Thompson is hitting .360 with 2 home runs. His stats include 18 runs batted in and 21 runs scored.
