Phasunan Takes Personal Leave
Tuesday, July 30th, 1895
Montreal right fielder Thayathorn Phasunan will miss an indefinite period of time to tend to "personal issues". Phasunan refused to elaborate on what exactly the issues were, but added that they were "more important than baseball, that's for sure." Royales manager Charles Read commented that they will "expect to be without Phasunan for quite some time and that we'll have to plan long-term to be without him."
