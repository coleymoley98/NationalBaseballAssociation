St. Louis Starter Named FJC's Top Pitcher
Wednesday, May 1st, 1901
Vic Marleau took a much-deserved turn in the spotlight earlier today as he accepted the Francophone Junior Circuit Pitcher of the Month award for April.

The 16-year-old Juniors starter is a relative newcomer to the league, but nonetheless racked up an impressive 5-3 won-lost record in 11 starts. This month he fanned 69 batters in 62 innings and compiled a 2.76 ERA.

So far in Marleau's career, the 16-year-old starter has won 7 and lost 7, collecting 139 strikeouts with a 3.18 ERA. Opponents have hit .231 against him.
