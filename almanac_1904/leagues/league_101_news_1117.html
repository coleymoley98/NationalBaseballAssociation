Chicagou's Ty Johnson Suffers Setback
Thursday, September 26th, 1895
Things have gotten worse for Ty Johnson of the Chicagou Frontenacs. The 35-year-old left fielder will not be back as soon as originally expected. Johnson is undergoing treatment for a torn abdominal muscle, but his doctors have expanded his recovery time to at least 4 months. An unidentified team official told BNN, "Ty tried to rush his rehab and suffered a setback."

Ty Johnson is expected to undergo surgery in Chicagou(Illinois) tomorrow.

Career-to-date Johnson has rung up a .364 average, played in 138 games and collected 20 doubles, 27 triples and 9 home runs.
