Kittiphan Mai's Home Robbed
Wednesday, May 5th, 1897
Kittiphan Mai's home in Detroit has been robbed, the reliever has told media.

"They took pretty much everything, completely cased the place," said the 29-year-old grimly. "I'm not sure if it was someone who knew I was a pro ballplayer or just a random thing, but it feel terrible either way."

Thankfully, Mai said, his family wasn't home during the incident.

"We can replace possessions, but no one was hurt, that's the main thing."

Danny Hinck said morale around the clubhouse is low, but they are trying to band together to help Mai through the situation.

"We're all bummed out, but happy he's OK and hopefully they can catch the guy who did this," he said.
