FJC Player of the Week Named
Monday, April 18th, 1898
So far in his young career, Trakoolchat Phomee has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Francophone Junior Circuit in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 16-year-old Coude Juniors right fielder hit at a torrid .563 clip as he collected 18 hits in 32 at-bats, 3 home runs, 8 RBIs and 9 runs scored.

In 43 games this season Phomee has compiled a .357 batting average with 60 hits and 7 home runs. He has also scored 33 times and knocked in 32 runs.
