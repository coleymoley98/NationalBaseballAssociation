August's Top Hurler is Albertson
Wednesday, September 1st, 1897
In August when the Quebec manager wrote the name "Albertson" on the lineup card, he knew he had a good shot at winning. Start after start in the month, James Albertson took the hill, took charge and usually got the win.

He dominated with a 6-1 mark and 1.99 ERA in 7 starts. The gifted Nordiques star pitched 63.1 innings, fanned 22 and held opposing teams to a .261 average.

For his outstanding mound performance Albertson was named the NL Pitcher of the Month by the baseball writers.

This season Albertson has 25 wins and 8 losses and a 2.61 ERA.
