Rebels Get One to Win
Sunday, May 31st, 1903
It was a dismal game for the Toronto Blue Sox with Jozie Rivière on the hill. The New York Rebels starter was in top form and made it a long day for Toronto with a 1-0 shutout. He surrendered a total of 5 hits and piled up 6 strikeouts to pick up the win in a rout.

"No offense to the Blue Sox but that was a cakewalk today," Rivière said to BNN reporters following the win.

In 8 starts this season Rivière has fashioned a 3-5 record and a 2.97 ERA.
