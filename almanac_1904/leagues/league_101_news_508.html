Manager Bernatchez Gone in St. Louis
Saturday, September 3rd, 1904
Today the Cardinaux made an announcement: The era of Andy Bernatchez is over in St. Louis.

After a disappointing season, the club officials sat down with Bernatchez to discuss their decision to make a change.

"It wasn't a surprise to me," Bernatchez said speaking with reporters. "As a team we did not perform up to people's expectations, nor to my expectations. When that happens, someone has to take the blame. This year, that person was me."

In an official statement, Cardinaux general manager Remus Borel expressed gratitude for Bernatchez's efforts, but confirmed the need to look for a different voice in the clubhouse.

"We thank Andy for providing class, professionalism, experience and leadership during his tenure with the club, but believe we need to make this important change to our team's management," he said.
