Suttles Electric in 3-0 Win
Thursday, April 18th, 1895
The Drummondville Juniors did little to support starter Kasen Suttles against the Coude Juniors, but it turned out he didn't need the help.

The left-hander spun a complete game shutout against the Juniors, striking out 9 and surrendering 2 hits. The performance from Suttles allowed Drummondville to escape with the 3-0 win despite an anemic offense.

"Nobody came to me and said 'Kasen, we're gonna need you to win this one for us today,' but I could feel it," Suttles told BNN reporters following the win. "I'm the guy that will step up for you when you need it."

In 15 starts this season Suttles has crafted a 8-5 record with a 2.67 ERA.
