Coude Hurler Tops in Pitcher of the Year Award Voting
Saturday, May 5th, 1900
Jukkapant Simard was overpowering this season for the Coude Juniors and earned the 1900 Francophone Junior Circuit Pitcher of the Year Award for his efforts.

In a standout year he went 10-2 in 16 starts with 129 strikeouts in 114 innings and a 1.26 ERA.

He received 10 first place votes out of a possible 12. Suthep Aiton of the Juneau Juniors finished second in voting with 2 first place votes, while Robbie Ryu of the Gallant Juniors finished third.
