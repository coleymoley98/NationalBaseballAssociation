Record "W" for Watson
Monday, July 13th, 1903
It's time to celebrate in Toronto.

Adam Watson, the sturdy Blue Sox right-hander, has set a new career record for regular season wins, with 113. He breaks the record held by Phutchapong Sanford.

Blue Sox owner Anan Morency was on-hand for the game at Canadian League Park I and credited Watson with having a "drive to excel."

"Adam has always been a real credit to the game of baseball," he said.

Up to now Watson has compiled an ERA of 2.81 with a 7-9 mark over 17 starts. He has 60 strikeouts in 150.1 innings and has held opponents to a .270 batting average.
