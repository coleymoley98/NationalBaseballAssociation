Harris Slams 5 Hits for Detroit
Tuesday, August 8th, 1899

Speaking with reporters after his standout performance, the 24-year-old second baseman said, "Get 5 hits, win a ballgame, it's nice. I could get used to this."

Leon Harris singled in the 1st, hit an RBI double in the 4th, singled in the 5th, doubled in the 8th and hit an RBI single in the 9th.

Harris is hitting .289 for the season with 3 home runs, 21 RBIs and 34 runs scored. He has 73 hits in 253 at-bats and played in 61 games.
