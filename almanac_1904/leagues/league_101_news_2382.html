Neal Voted May's Top NL Rookie
Wednesday, June 1st, 1898
Piroj Neal is an old man by baseball standards. In fact, after so many years of being overlooked, the 33-year-old rookie for Quebec is a bit surprised himself that he's living the good life right now. In May Neal reeled off a 5-3 record in 9 starts as he claimed the National League Rookie of the Month Award.

He posted an ERA of 3.14 with 18 strikeouts in 77.1 innings and held opponents to a .246 average.
