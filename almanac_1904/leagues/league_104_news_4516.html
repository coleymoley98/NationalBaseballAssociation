Anglo Junior Circuit Pitcher Honored
Monday, July 7th, 1902
Ronald Rice drew on his youthful energy and pitching skill to win Anglo Junior Circuit Player of the Week honors last week.

The Pawtucket Juniors pitcher registered 3 wins and no losses, 0.42 ERA and 26 strikeouts in 21.2 innings to sew up the award. The 20-year-old also held opposing hitters to 7 hits and a .097 batting average.

This year Rice has a 7-3 mark in 13 starts with a 2.72 ERA. He has given up 62 hits in 82.2 innings, struck out 91 and issued 17 walks. Opposing clubs have compiled a .201 batting average against him.
