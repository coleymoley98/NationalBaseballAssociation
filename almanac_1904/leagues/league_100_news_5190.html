Roger McLarty Wins CL Batting Title
Tuesday, August 25th, 1903
Many talented Canadian League players can go their entire careers and never even get a sniff of a batting title.

Roger McLarty has already captured one at age 25.

The New York shortstop batted .351 to capture the 1903 Canadian League batting championship.

He collected 149 hits, 18 doubles, 10 triples and 2 home runs for the year. In 103 games McLarty totaled 36 RBIs and scored 74 runs.
