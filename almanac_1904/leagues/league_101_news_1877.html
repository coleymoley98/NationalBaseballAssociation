Kittiphan Mai's Home Robber Arrested
Friday, May 21st, 1897
Kittiphan Mai was all smiles today as he told local reporters that the man who robbed his home recently has been arrested by local authorities.

"They caught him," he said. "I think I'm even going to be able to get some of my stuff back."

Mai said his Detroit Lionsteammates provided great support for him while he was struggling with this issue and he believes the team has come together and bonded as a result.

"Thankfully it's a happy ending and this low-life will get what he deserves," he said.

Danny Hinck said many of the guys on the team gave Mai a big hug when they heard the news.

"We're happy for him, it's been stressing him out, so it's nice to see it come to a conclusion," he said.
