Great Day for Ongaro -- 5 Hits
Wednesday, May 12th, 1897
Raffy Ongaro had an answer for everything the Montreal Royales threw at him today at Montreal Grounds. The Quebec second baseman went 5-for-6 in the game to put his name in the record book and put the Nordiques on top, 12-8.

Summing up his batting philosophy to BNN writers after the game, Ongaro said, "I just see the ball and I hit it."

Raffy Ongaro singled in the 2nd, singled in the 3rd, grounded out in the 4th, singled in the 6th, hit an RBI single in the 7th and singled in the 9th.

To date this year Ongaro is batting .357 with 1 home run.
