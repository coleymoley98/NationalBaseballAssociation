Sokam wins 1901 HR Challenge
Monday, June 3rd, 1901
Ballpark of Drummondville was host to the 1901 Home Run Challenge, the annual celebration of the longball. Kan Sokam finished first in the field of eight Francophone Junior Circuit sluggers, with Drummondville Juniors left fielder Kan Sokam topping Perry Gélinas in the final round by a score of 9-8.

"There's no better feeling in the world than hitting a home run, so imagine how I'm feeling after my performance tonight," said Sokam.
