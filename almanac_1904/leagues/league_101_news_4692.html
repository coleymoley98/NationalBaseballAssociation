Star 3B Signs Pact with Royales
Monday, October 13th, 1902
Happy times in Montreal as the Royales and star third baseman Enzo Paquette put the finishing touches on a $6,000 contract extension that will keep the 27-year-old in town 3 years longer.

The signing is great news for Montreal because players of this caliber are hard to come by.

In his career Paquette has played in 521 games with a .273 batting average. Among his 425 hits are 64 doubles, 6 triples and 18 home runs. He also has scored 227 runs and driven in 221 runs.
