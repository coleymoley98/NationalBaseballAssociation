Tanner Batters Canton Big Time
Tuesday, June 28th, 1898
Perry Tanner put on a hitting clinic today as the Fort Wayne Juniors dumped the Canton Juniors 10-7 at Juniors Ballpark.

The Fort Wayne right fielder made Canton defenders earn their pay, lashing Canton pitching for 5 hits in 5 at-bats in the game. He told BNN reporters after the game that his strategy was "just hit it where they pitched it."

"Too often I try too hard to pull the ball," Tanner said in his postgame press conference. "Today, I just went with the pitch."

Perry Tanner singled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 4th, hit an RBI double in the 6th and hit an RBI double in the 7th.

Tanner is batting .450 with 3 home runs and 22 RBIs this season.
