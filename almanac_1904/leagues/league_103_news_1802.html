Givan Tosses 4-0 Shutout for Sherbrooke
Monday, March 29th, 1897
The Montreal Juniors struggled at the plate today against Juniors hurler Garland Givan, who shackled them on 6 hits while hurling a complete game 4-0 shutout.

"What can you say? We didn't hit well," said Juniors skipper Jules Couture in his postgame comments. "Hopefully we shake this off and bounce back tomorrow."

Givan finished the game with 5 strikeouts and 1 walk in an excellent mound performance.

"He pitched real well," Sherbrooke manager Lance Vandergriff said. "Garland made pitches when he had to. He hit his spots and did a great job of mixing in his changeup and using his fastball effectively."

In 11 starts this year Givan has fashioned a 5-3 record and a 2.51 ERA.
