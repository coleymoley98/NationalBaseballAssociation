FJC Announces Player of the Week
Monday, March 24th, 1902
Rookie jitters had no place in Jeff Gladisch's game last week as the 16-year-old Sainte-Paul pitcher propelled himself to the Francophone Junior Circuit Player of the Week award.

He posted 3 wins, 15 strikeouts and a 0.48 ERA last week.

Checking out his stats for the season, Gladisch has racked up 4 wins and 2 losses in 6 starts, 2.54 ERA and struck out 48 batters in 49.2 innings.
