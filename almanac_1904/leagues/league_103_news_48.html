Archambault and Luckhurst Suspended 5 Games for Brawl
Monday, March 7th, 1904
The fans at Ballpark of St. Louis saw it all yesterday in the Juniors-Juniors game -- pushes, shoves, scuffles, a few tackles, a few swings, a few feeble punches -- and a baseball game, too. When the ruckus was over, only two players were sent to the showers by the umpire crew. St. Louis's Keith Archambault and Sainte-Paul's Theodore Luckhurst were ejected from the game.

Today the two players were suspended 5 games by the league commissioner.
