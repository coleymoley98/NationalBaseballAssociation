Arleth Takes CL Top Starter Trophy
Thursday, August 1st, 1901
Edgar Arleth took a much-deserved turn in the spotlight earlier today as he accepted the Canadian League Pitcher of the Month award for July.

The 23-year-old Redcoats starter is a relative newcomer to the league, but nonetheless racked up an impressive 7-0 won-lost record in 7 starts. This month he fanned 33 batters in 63 innings and compiled a 1.57 ERA.

Thus far this season, Arleth has tossed 189.1 innings in 22 starts, fanned 91 batters, walked 27 and put up a 2.09 ERA with 15 wins and 7 losses.
