Detroit's Frey Bags NL Batting Award
Tuesday, September 1st, 1903
It's perhaps an award he'll win many more times through his career, as at age 23 Ara Frey has a lot of baseball ahead of him.

But for now the young Detroit Lions star can enjoy the numbers he put up in August, which have garnered him the nod as the National League Batter of the Month.

His breakout month featured a .309 average with 5 home runs, 23 RBIs and 27 runs scored.

Frey is hitting at a .338 mark this season. His stats show 11 home runs, 75 RBIs and 83 runs scored. He has played 106 games for the Lions.
