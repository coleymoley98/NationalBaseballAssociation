Mayer Fires 2-0 Shutout in Royales Win
Tuesday, August 13th, 1895
Teerati Mayer shut 'em down and shut 'em out, hurling the Montreal Royales past the Chicagou Frontenacs 2-0 in a complete game victory.

"This is a game of adjustments and I made some good ones in this game," he told BNN sportswriters in the postgame interview.

Mayer held the Chicagou Frontenacs without a run, tossing a 4-hitter while walking 2 and fanning 1.

His record this season is 6-24 in 34 starts and Mayer has a 5.68 ERA.
