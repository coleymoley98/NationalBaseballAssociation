Krutyai Blasts 3 into Bleachers
Thursday, July 7th, 1898
Sarawut Krutyai went on an explosive hitting spree against the Toronto Juniors at Juniors Ballpark today, launching a trio of blasts to lead the Morgantown Juniors to the 17-2 win.

The second baseman finished 5-for-5 with 5 runs scored and 8 RBI.

"It was just one of those days," Krutyai said in a postgame interview. "I don't know how to explain it. I'm just putting a good swing on the ball."

Toronto skipper Samuel Sénécal said Krutyai was "locked-in."

"We made some pretty good pitches," he said. "Those first two homers were exactly where the pitcher wanted to put them. He's just hot right now -- you've just got to tip your hat to him."

Sarawut Krutyai singled in the 2nd, hit a three-run home run off Siripong More in the 3rd, doubled in the 5th, was hit by a pitch in the 6th, hit a two-run home run off Satja Gleeson in the 7th and hit a three-run home run off Satja Gleeson in the 9th.

Thus far this season Krutyai is hitting .550 with 4 home runs, 14 RBIs and 10 runs scored. He has 11 hits in 20 at-bats and has played in 7 games.
