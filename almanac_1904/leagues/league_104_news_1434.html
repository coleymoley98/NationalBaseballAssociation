Keats Spectacular in 4-0 Win
Friday, June 19th, 1896
Sebastian Keats subdued the Springfield Juniors, 4-0, pitching a 2-hit gem for the Quincy Juniors at Juniors Ballpark.

"He was kind of teasing them with his off-speed stuff, and his fastball was moving everywhere," Quincy skipper Ethan Bax said afterward.

Keats registered 8 strikeouts and issued 2 walks in his superb performance.

"We just didn't swing the bats very well. Or else he just pitched good, whichever way you want to look at it," said Springfield manager Asante Jones after the defeat.

This season Keats has worked 65.1 innings with 63 strikeouts en route to a 4-3 record and a 3.44 ERA.
