Juniors Ouellet Smacks 5 Hits
Sunday, June 23rd, 1895
Noah Ouellet was unstoppable, but the Buffalo Juniors were not.

Despite his hitting heroics at Juniors Ballpark today, the Juniors lost 10-9 to the New Britain Juniors. Ouellet battered New Britain for 5 hits in 6 at-bats. His only consolation will be a boost in his batting average and a brief mention in the record book.

Ouellet just shook his head after the game, telling BNN reporters, "It's the team with the most runs that wins, not the most hits."

Noah Ouellet singled in the 1st, hit a two-run single in the 2nd, hit an RBI double in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, grounded out in the 7th and singled in the 9th.

Ouellet is hitting .395 for the season with 1 home run, 16 RBIs and 18 runs scored. He has 34 hits in 86 at-bats and played in 20 games.
