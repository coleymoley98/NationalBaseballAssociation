Player of the Week: Quincy's Thériault
Monday, July 20th, 1903
Scott Thériault is respected by most Quincy Juniors fans for his no-frills style of play. The 16-year-old first baseman went about his business with vigor last week to grab the Anglo Junior Circuit Player of the Week honors.

He compiled a .483 average (14-for-29) with 4 home runs, 11 RBIs and 10 runs scored.

So far this season Thériault has gotten 64 hits in 168 at-bats for a .381 batting average with 9 home runs. His stats include 37 RBIs and 31 runs scored. He has played in 45 games.
