Star SS Signs Extension with Quebec
Friday, July 16th, 1897
Happy times in Quebec as the Nordiques and star shortstop Nathan Shaw put the finishing touches on a $7,200 contract extension that will keep the 31-year-old in town 4 years longer.

The signing is great news for Quebec because players of this caliber are hard to come by.

In 67 games this year Shaw has posted a .354 average and put up these numbers: 5 home runs, 46 RBIs and 50 runs scored.
