AJC Player of the Week Chosen
Monday, July 6th, 1896
21-year-old Pat Sharpe made a few Morgantown headlines during this past week as the rookie first baseman proved he could compete at the plate with his high-school idols. He captured the attention of fans, journalists and Anglo Junior Circuit officials last week and today took home Player of the Week honors for his effort.

He cracked 13 hits in 22 at-bats to put up a .591 average. Sharpe collected no home runs, 4 RBIs and scored 4 runs.

This season Sharpe is batting .366 with 2 home runs. He has scored 14 runs and driven in 20.
