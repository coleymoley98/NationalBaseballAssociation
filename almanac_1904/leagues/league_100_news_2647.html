Philadelphia Pitcher Shows His Best Stuff in August
Thursday, September 1st, 1898
Few players his age have the consistency to put a solid month together, but Philadelphia's Jonathan Johnston carved up the opposition all August long and hauled off the Canadian League Rookie of the Month award today as a result.

He sizzled last month with a 4-3 mark, fanning 19 in 72.1 innings with an ERA of 2.99 in 8 starts.

Checking out his stats for the season, Johnston has racked up 17 wins and 12 losses in 29 starts, 2.53 ERA and struck out 84 batters in 252.1 innings.
