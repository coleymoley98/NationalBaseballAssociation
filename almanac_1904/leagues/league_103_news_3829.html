Hits Blitz for Juniors Vanier
Monday, April 22nd, 1901
Jack Vanier of the Gallant Juniors helped his club in a big way today, hitting for the cycle in their 12-10 win against the Drummondville Juniors. The 4 hits, 3 runs batted in and 4 runs scored for Vanier led the way for the Juniors.

"It was just a perfect day at the ballpark," Vanier said later.

Jack Vanier grounded out in the 1st, walked in the 2nd, tripled in the 5th, singled in the 7th, hit a solo-shot off Zackery Newsome in the 8th, hit an RBI double in the 9th and walked in a run in the 10th.

For the season Vanier is currently batting .407 with 1 home run and 4 RBIs. In 15 games he has reached base at a .484 rate and scored 7 runs to date.
