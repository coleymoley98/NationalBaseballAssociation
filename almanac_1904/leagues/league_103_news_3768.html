Juniors, Jacobs Stonewall Minneapolis, 7-0
Friday, March 1st, 1901
Curtis Jacobs of the Quebec City Juniors humiliated the Minneapolis Juniors at Juniors Ballpark today, spinning a complete game shutout and holding the visitors to 2 hits.

"He stayed aggressive and his ball was really moving," said Minneapolis manager Brien Gilliam, after the game.

Jacobs whiffed 11 and walked 0 in the 7-0 win.

"If I make good pitches, I'm going to get some outs," Jacobs said. "I was making good pitches the entire game."
