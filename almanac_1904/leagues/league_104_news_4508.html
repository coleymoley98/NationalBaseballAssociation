Juniors Get One to Win
Tuesday, July 1st, 1902
The Morgantown Juniors did little to support starter Jason Tibbetts against the Quincy Juniors, but it turned out he didn't need the help.

The left-hander spun a complete game shutout against the Juniors, striking out 13 and surrendering 2 hits. The performance from Tibbetts allowed Morgantown to escape with the 1-0 win despite an anemic offense.

"Nobody came to me and said 'Jason, we're gonna need you to win this one for us today,' but I could feel it," Tibbetts told BNN reporters following the win. "I'm the guy that will step up for you when you need it."

For the year Tibbetts has compiled a 5-0 record with a 1.28 ERA.
