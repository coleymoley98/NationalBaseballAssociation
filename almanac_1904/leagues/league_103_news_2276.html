One Run Enough for Davenport
Saturday, April 2nd, 1898
Arthur Joly handcuffed the Drummondville Juniors at Ballpark of Drummondville today, coming away with a 4-hit, complete game shutout.

"I had everything working for me out there," the Davenport Juniors left-hander said later. "I used every one of my pitches, hit both sides of the plate really well and felt like I could do whatever I wanted."

With his stuff working, Joly had little trouble with the Juniors and led the Juniors to victory, 1-0. He recorded 12 strikeouts and no walks in an impressive performance.

To this point in the season, Joly shows a 4-5 record, 9 starts, 57.1 innings, 59 strikeouts and a 1.73 ERA.
