Guay Wins NL Player of the Week
Monday, July 6th, 1903
Tommy Guay is respected by most Quebec Nordiques fans for his no-frills style of play. The 27-year-old right fielder went about his business with vigor last week to grab the National League Player of the Week honors.

He compiled a .450 average (9-for-20) with no home runs, 4 RBIs and 6 runs scored.

Guay is batting .320 this year with 1 home run, 30 RBIs and 33 runs scored. He has 65 hits in 203 at-bats.
