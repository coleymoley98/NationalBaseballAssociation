Juniors Plugged by Graf, Juniors, 3-0
Monday, March 2nd, 1896
Pranthep Graf really had his stuff working at Field of the Juniors today. The Sainte-Paul Juniors hurler mystified the Davenport Juniors and shut them out 3-0. Graf allowed a total of 8 hits in the ballgame and struck out 11.

"The shutout is nice but I'm just happy we won," Graf said with a smile after the game. "Coach can be a real bear when we lose."
