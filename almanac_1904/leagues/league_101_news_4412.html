St-Jacques Takes Charge, Blanks Quebec, 6-0
Sunday, June 1st, 1902
Samuel St-Jacques of the Detroit Lions spun his magic on the Quebec Nordiques today. He blanked them 6-0 on 5 hits, registering 3 strikeouts and 1 walk.

"The bottom line was I just kept the ball down," St-Jacques said. "My breaking stuff was down. Everything was down."

Talking to reporters in the postgame interview, Detroit skipper Adrian Miramontes said the team "got solid pitching, good defense and took great team at-bats in a lot of different situations."

"Samuel pitched really well but this was a solid performance by everyone," he said.

Thus far this season, St-Jacques has worked 41.1 innings with 14 strikeouts en route to a 2-3 record and a 2.83 ERA.
