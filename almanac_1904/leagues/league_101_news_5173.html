Royer Shines, Shuts Out St. Louis, 6-0
Friday, August 21st, 1903
Chicagou Frontenacs fans were pleased to see so many goose eggs up on the scoreboard as Zachary Royer blanked the St. Louis Cardinaux, 6-0 at St. Louis Grounds today. Royer stopped them on 3 hits while walking 2 and striking out 2.

"Once he gets in a groove, he can be dynamite," Frontenacs skipper Mustafa Campeau said. "He was on top of his game in this one."

Thus far this season, Royer has worked 234.1 innings with 97 strikeouts en route to a 12-12 record and a 2.65 ERA.
