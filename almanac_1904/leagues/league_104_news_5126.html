Anglo Junior Circuit Best Pitcher Chosen
Monday, August 3rd, 1903
When the name of Saksit Diana appeared on the Halifax lineup card in 1903, it was a virtual guarantee of a victory or at least a well-pitched game. For his brilliant performance this season he was named the Anglo Junior Circuit Pitcher of the Year Award winner today.

He started 14 games, running up a 9-1 record and a 1.20 ERA. Diana held opponents to a .160 average while striking out 113, walking 24 and allowing 59 hits in 104.2 innings of work.

He received 12 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Eddie Tresch of the Morgantown Juniors finished second in voting, while Rob Sparkman of the Buffalo Juniors finished third.
