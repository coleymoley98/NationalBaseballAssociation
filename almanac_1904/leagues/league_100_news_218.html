Week's Premier Player: Boston's Malone
Monday, June 6th, 1904
So far in his young career, Wes Malone has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Canadian League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 23-year-old Boston Redcoats left fielder hit at a torrid .522 clip as he collected 12 hits in 23 at-bats, no home runs, 5 RBIs and 5 runs scored.

This year Malone is batting .385 with no home runs and 10 RBIs.
