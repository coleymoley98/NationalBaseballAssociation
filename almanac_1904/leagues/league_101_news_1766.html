Chicagou Frontenacs Ship Heasley to Detroit
Wednesday, February 17th, 1897
In an effort to add some depth to its roster, the Chicagou Frontenacs have swapped 30-year-old RHP Max Heasley and 21-year-old LF Harrison Murch to the Detroit Lions for 27-year-old 2B Sompong Voss. The Frontenacs GM told reporters, "We saw a chance to upgrade our ball club and took it."

Chicagou will retain a portion of Heasley's remaining contract.

During his career Heasley has 8 wins and 4 losses with 5 saves. His ERA stands at 5.40, and he has 24 strikeouts in 51.2 innings.
