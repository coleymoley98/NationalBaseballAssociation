Barrage of Hits Gets DiPaolo FJC Award
Monday, April 6th, 1903
Gerard DiPaolo burst onto the Francophone Junior Circuit stage in a big way last week. This promising rookie left fielder for Minneapolis garnered everyone's respect by putting up some remarkable numbers and picking up the Player of the Week trophy.

The 17-year-old haunted opposing pitchers hitting .424 (14-for-33) with 3 home runs and 7 RBIs last week.

This year DiPaolo has collected a .344 batting average to date with 43 hits in 125 at-bats, 17 RBIs, 5 home runs and scored 36 times. He has played in 32 games.
