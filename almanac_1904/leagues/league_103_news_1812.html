Ruckus Reaps 4-Game Suspensions
Friday, April 9th, 1897
Quebec City's Jack McDaniel and Drummondville's Pierre-André Pelletier have been handed 4-game suspensions for their parts in yesterday's upheaval at Juniors Ballpark. The teams were notified today that the suspensions would go into effect immediately. According to the league commissioner McDaniel and Pelletier provoked and incited the brawl and bore the blame for what transpired.

McDaniel is hitting .333 this year with 4 home runs and 25 RBIs. He also has an OBP of .387, 7 walks and scored 27 times.

Pelletier has taken the mound for 12 relief appearances this season, nailing down no saves while notching a 0-0 record with a 9.35 ERA. The Drummondville reliever has limited opposing batters to a .284 batting average.
