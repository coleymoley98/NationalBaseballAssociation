Juniors Get One to Win
Wednesday, June 15th, 1904
Before the game, Geoffrey Ladouceur said he liked his chances. His instincts proved to be right. In a superb outing at Juniors Ballpark, the Juniors pitcher whitewashed the Pawtucket Juniors 1-0.

"I just had a feeling it was going to be my day," Ladouceur said in the clubhouse afterward. "My control was awesome. I could throw my curveball even when I was behind in the count."

The Canton starter held Pawtucket scoreless, giving up 6 hits while registering 7 strikeouts and issuing no walks.

"We didn't have very many good at-bats," Juniors manager Jetrin Charron said in his postgame interview. "When that happens, you usually lose."

In 4 starts this season Ladouceur has compiled a 2-3 record and a 5.74 ERA.
