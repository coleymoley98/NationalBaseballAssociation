Veteran Re-Signs with Redcoats
Sunday, September 16th, 1900
The Boston Redcoats opened their pocketbook and persuaded Boyce Lu to continue his career in front of the fans who have grown to appreciate his talent and contributions.

The club reached a 2-year, $3,360 agreement on a contract extension that will retain the talented Lu.

"Boyce just wants to get paid," the third baseman said at a press conference today to discuss the negotiations. "If you don't wanna pay Boyce, Boyce don't wanna play for you."

His career numbers show Lu has batted .317 and collected 809 hits, 22 home runs and 368 RBIs.
