Girard Voted NL Player of the Week
Monday, May 23rd, 1904
Youth was served last week as 22-year-old Fabien Girard put together a remarkable stretch on the mound, outpacing all other National League pitchers and hitters to win Player of the Week honors.

The Montreal Royales hurler rang up 11 strikeouts in 18 innings, compiling an ERA of 0.50 and posting a fine 2-0 mark.

This year Girard has posted a 5-1 mark in 6 starts and has a 1.19 ERA.
