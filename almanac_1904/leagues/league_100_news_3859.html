5-Hit Barrage by Higgins
Wednesday, May 8th, 1901
No matter what the Philadelphia Liberty threw at him today, James Higgins hit it. He easily handled Liberty pitching, going 5 for 5 and helping the Redcoats to a 10-3 triumph at Huntington Avenue Grounds.

"The way I was swinging today, I think I could have closed my eyes and hit the ball," he told BNN reporters in the postgame interview.

Told of Higgins's quip, Liberty skipper Anisong Ilic said he "would take him up on that next time."

James Higgins singled in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, singled in the 6th and doubled in the 8th.
