Cannatelli Hurls Montreal to Shutout Victory
Saturday, August 15th, 1903
Pete Cannatelli subdued the Chicagou Frontenacs, 2-0, pitching a 6-hit gem for the Montreal Royales at West Side Grounds.

"He was kind of teasing them with his off-speed stuff, and his fastball was moving everywhere," Montreal skipper Charles Read said afterward.

Cannatelli registered 3 strikeouts and issued no walks in his superb performance.

"We just didn't swing the bats very well. Or else he just pitched good, whichever way you want to look at it," said Chicagou manager Mustafa Campeau after the defeat.

To date this season, Cannatelli has a record of 13 wins and 10 losses and a 2.66 ERA. He has appeared in 24 games and struck out 84 in 203.1 innings.
