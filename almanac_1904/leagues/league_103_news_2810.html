Sheikh Smacks 5 Hits for Juniors
Thursday, March 30th, 1899
Pak Sheikh had an answer for everything the Montreal Juniors threw at him today at Field of the Juniors. The Sainte-Paul third baseman went 5-for-6 in the game to put his name in the record book and put the Juniors on top, 30-5.

Summing up his batting philosophy to BNN writers after the game, Sheikh said, "I just see the ball and I hit it."

Pak Sheikh singled in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 2nd, walked in the 4th, flied out in the 5th, singled in the 6th, hit an RBI double in the 6th and hit an RBI double in the 8th.

Looking at his stats this year, Sheikh has compiled a .467 batting average, while hitting no home runs and driving in 6 runs. He has played in 14 games and scored 9 runs.
