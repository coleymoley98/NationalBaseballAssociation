Coude's White Snaps Up Player of the Week
Monday, April 25th, 1904
So far in his young career, Justin White has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Francophone Junior Circuit in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 17-year-old Coude Juniors center fielder hit at a torrid .500 clip as he collected 12 hits in 24 at-bats, no home runs, 6 RBIs and 4 runs scored.

Thus far this season White is hitting .341 with no home runs, 45 RBIs and 35 runs scored. He has 71 hits in 208 at-bats and has played in 49 games.
