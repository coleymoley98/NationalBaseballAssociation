Best FJC Hurler Chosen for 1902
Monday, May 5th, 1902
Wuttikrai Kundrat was really special this season. You could feel the electricity in the air every time he took the mound for the Gauthier Juniors. Today he was honored by his selection as the 1902 Francophone Junior Circuit Pitcher of the Year Award recipient.

He started 16 games, logging 125 innings, 11 wins, 1 loss, 176 strikeouts and a 1.73 ERA. Kundrat gave up 64 hits, while walking 35 and holding opponents to a .148 average.

He received 8 first place votes out of a possible 12. Alair Givens of the Juneau Juniors finished second in voting with 2 first place votes, while Adrian Laflamme of the Gallant Juniors finished third with 2 first place votes.
