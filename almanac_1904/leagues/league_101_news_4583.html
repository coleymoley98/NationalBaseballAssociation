Boehm Wins Best Bat Trophy in July
Friday, August 1st, 1902
Rocky Boehm was a lot to handle for National League pitchers in the month of July. The Quebec second baseman posted a .378 batting average, which helped lead him to being named the National League Batter of the Month. Boehm also added 1 home run, 19 RBIs, 17 runs scored and a .453 on-base percentage.

This year Boehm has batted at a .338 pace with 54 hits, 1 home run, 23 RBIs and scored 26 times.
