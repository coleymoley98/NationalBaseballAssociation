Catteau Sets New Mark with Win
Monday, June 13th, 1904
Val Catteau has set the baseball world aglow with victory number 171 of his storied career.

The Quebec Nordiques superstar recorded the record-breaking win at Ballpark of Quebec today to snap the all-time mark previously held by the legendary James Albertson. Speaking at a press conference after the historic game, Catteau said "number 171 was in a class by itself."

"I want to thank all of my fleet-footed outfielders for making this day possible," he said with a smile. "Without them I wouldn't be here today."

Checking out his stats for the year, Catteau has racked up 6 wins and 5 losses in 11 starts, 2.77 ERA and struck out 40 batters in 100.2 innings.
