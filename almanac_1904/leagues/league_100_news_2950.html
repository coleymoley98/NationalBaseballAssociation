Red-Hot LaPorte Scorches Rebels
Thursday, June 15th, 1899
No matter what the New York Rebels threw at him today, Chongrak LaPorte hit it. He easily handled Rebels pitching, going 5 for 6 and helping the Redcoats to a 14-6 triumph at Polo Grounds.

"The way I was swinging today, I think I could have closed my eyes and hit the ball," he told BNN reporters in the postgame interview.

Told of LaPorte's quip, Rebels skipper Tyrone Martin said he "would take him up on that next time."

Chongrak LaPorte singled in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, grounded out in the 6th, hit a two-run single in the 7th and doubled in the 9th.

LaPorte has collected 17 hits for the Redcoats this year, 0 of them home runs. He has also netted 5 RBIs and scored 3 runs while batting .378.
