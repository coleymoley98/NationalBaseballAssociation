St. Louis Pitcher Gets June's Rookie Trophy
Wednesday, July 1st, 1903
In his first year in the league, Kane Tildon is beginning to pay huge dividends on his club's financial investment. He captured the National League Rookie of the Month Award for a spectacular month on the mound.

During June this St. Louis rookie started 7 games, logged 58 innings and chalked up 4 wins and 3 losses. He struck out 18, walked 15 and rang up an eye-catching 3.10 ERA. Opposing batters hit only .234 against him.

This season in 14 starts Kane Tildon has a record of 6-7 with 31 strikeouts in 114.1 innings and a 3.38 ERA.
