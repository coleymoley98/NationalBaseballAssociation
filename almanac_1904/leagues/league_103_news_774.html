Lader Takes Charge, Blanks Quebec City, 4-0
Thursday, April 25th, 1895
Watiwat Lader was in a zone of his own most of the day, shutting out the Quebec City Juniors on 7 hits.

"He was too darn good for us today," grumbled Quebec City manager Leomar Durán after the 4-0 loss to the Gauthier Juniors.

Lader finished with 6 strikeouts and 1 walk in a strong outing.

In the postgame press conference, Gauthier manager Ti Holley said Lader "had good velocity, good movement and had those guys off balance. He just pitched his butt off."

This season Lader has gone 9-4 with an ERA of 4.50, posting 97 strikeouts in 126 innings.
