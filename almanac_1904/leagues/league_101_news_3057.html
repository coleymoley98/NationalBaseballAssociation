Detroit Trades Julius for Chicagou's Bowles
Friday, July 28th, 1899
You can focus on the negatives after being traded to a new team -- finding a new place to stay, leaving teammates and friends, having to start anew -- but one player hopes to focus on the positives. "It means I'm wanted somewhere. At least one team sees potential in me." Those were the comments heard after the Detroit Lions came to an agreement to deal 33-year-old C Aaron Julius and 23-year-old RHP Abe Brown to the Chicagou Frontenacs for 23-year-old 2B Warren Bowles and 24-year-old 3B Seymour Buchanan earlier today. The deal is expected to be formally announced later this evening.


This season in 26 games, Julius has hit at a .318 clip with 27 hits, no home runs, 15 RBIs and 9 runs scored.
