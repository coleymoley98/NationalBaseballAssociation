Moore Stretches Hit String to 25
Monday, April 11th, 1904
And the streak goes on.

Bowie Moore collected yet another hit in the 9-1 Coude Juniors win over the Juniors at Juniors Ballpark today. The Coude second baseman finished 3-for-4 in the game. During an extended batting practice session before the game, the famously hard-working Moore had fielded questions from reporters about his hitting streak, which now sits at 25 games.

"I'm not doing too much special," he said. "I'm well-prepared and I'm just trying to get the bat on the ball and put it in play."
Bowie Moore hit an RBI triple in the 1st, hit a two-run double in the 2nd, hit a sac fly in the 4th, struck out in the 5th and singled in the 8th.

So far this year, Moore is batting .396 with no home runs and 30 RBIs.
