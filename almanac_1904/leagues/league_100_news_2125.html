Rocky Boehm Named Premier CL Player
Monday, September 13th, 1897
Rocky Boehm has been around Canadian League enough to know a good hot streak when he sees one -- and he got to see one firsthand all year. For his standout run through the 1897 season, he's taking home the Most Valuable Player Award in the Canadian League.

The Toronto second baseman put up a batting average of .331 with 18 doubles, 20 triples, 6 home runs and 63 RBIs.

He received 3 first place votes out of a possible 4. Jabari Perrault of the New York Rebels finished second in voting with 1 first place vote, while Boyce Lu of the Boston Redcoats finished third.
