Sakowicz Tabbed CL's Top Rookie
Sunday, September 1st, 1901
Few players his age have the consistency to put a solid month together, but Toronto's Eugene Sakowicz carved up the opposition all August long and hauled off the Canadian League Rookie of the Month award today as a result.

He sizzled last month with a 6-0 mark, fanning 30 in 56.1 innings with an ERA of 0.96 in 6 starts.

Thus far this season, Sakowicz has tossed 202 innings in 23 starts, fanned 91 batters, walked 37 and put up a 2.45 ERA with 18 wins and 5 losses.
