Best NL Rookie Named, Nod to Laflamme
Saturday, August 1st, 1903
Adrian Laflamme is beginning to make a name for himself and he did it by posting a 4-2 record and a 2.25 ERA in July. For his spectacular mound performance last month he has been named BNN's Rookie of the Month in the National League.

The 19-year-old newcomer isn't at all intimidated by his lack of experience, at least not outwardly. In fact, by the way he has pitched beyond his years, you could easily mistake him for a veteran.

In 6 starts for the Lions Laflamme has struck out 34 batters in 60 innings, holding opposing hitters to a batting average of .207 during his hot streak.

So far this year Laflamme has delivered no saves. In 4 relief appearances he has 66 strikeouts in 136.1 innings with a 3.10 ERA and 8 wins and 5 losses.
