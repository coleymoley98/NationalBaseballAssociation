Injury Shuts Down Wilby for Season
Thursday, June 30th, 1898
The news was not unexpected, but that didn't make it easier to accept for the Canton Juniors: starting pitcher Noel Wilby is out for the year. Wilby suffered a bone spur in his elbow on 06/29/1898. The team knew that the chances of seeing him return were slim to none. But today it was made official.

Over the season Wilby has thrown 45 innings and dished out 35 strikeouts with a 1-6 mark and a 9.00 ERA.
