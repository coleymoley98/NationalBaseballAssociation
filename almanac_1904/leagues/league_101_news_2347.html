Mai Leads Detroit to 2-0 Win Over Quebec
Thursday, May 19th, 1898
The Quebec Nordiques met their match today in Detroit hurler Kittiphan Mai. He kept them guessing the whole game and spun a 5-hit shutout.

"He just worked us over," Quebec skipper Frédéric Lévesque said. "He probably didn't even need a shower after the game -- he barely worked up a sweat."

In the 2-0 victory, Mai whiffed 2 and gave up 6 walks.

This year Mai has fashioned a 2-2 mark, 2.72 ERA and pitched 39.2 innings. Opposing clubs are batting .252 against him and he has logged 5 strikeouts.
