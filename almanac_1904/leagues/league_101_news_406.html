NL Awards Player of the Week Honors
Monday, August 1st, 1904
Val Catteau has honed his skills over the years, learning to pitch with more finesse than he once displayed. This past week that knowledge paid off in a performance that earned him National League Player of the Week honors.

The 28-year-old Quebec Nordiques pitcher recorded 2 wins and no losses while working 22 innings, striking out 9, and posting a 0.41 ERA.

This year Catteau has worked 197.2 innings with 78 strikeouts en route to a 12-10 record and a 2.55 ERA.
