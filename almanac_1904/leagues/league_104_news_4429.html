Juniors Pitching Ravaged by Tanner
Friday, June 6th, 1902
Perry Tanner exploded for a barrage of base hits today at Juniors Ballpark.

The Fort Wayne right fielder had a field day in the 9-8 triumph over the Pawtucket Juniors, finishing 6-for-6.

"My hitting philosophy is simple," he said. "I focus on just seeing the ball and I try to get a good pitch."

Tanner must have focused at least 6 times and apparently got some good pitches, too, leading to one of his best days ever with the bat. Perry Tanner singled in the 1st, hit a two-run single in the 2nd, doubled in the 5th, hit a two-run single in the 6th, singled in the 8th and hit an RBI single in the 9th.

So far on the season Tanner is hitting .520 with no home runs, 7 RBIs and 3 runs scored. He has 13 hits and played in 6 games.
