AJC Top Pitcher Award for July to Huynh
Saturday, August 1st, 1896
Romie Huynh hasn't been around long enough to know that youngsters shouldn't be winning Anglo Junior Circuit Pitcher of the Month. But when you put up numbers like his, you're going to get some notice.

In July the Buffalo starter earned a 5-2 mark in 10 starts. He threw 72 innings, struck out 75 and compiled a solid 1.63 ERA.

Huynh has a lifetime record of 20 wins and 16 losses and a 4.12 ERA.
