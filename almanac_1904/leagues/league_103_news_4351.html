Gauthier Starter Named FJC's Premier Pitcher
Thursday, May 1st, 1902
Wuttikrai Kundrat took a much-deserved turn in the spotlight earlier today as he accepted the Francophone Junior Circuit Pitcher of the Month award for April.

The 15-year-old Juniors starter is a relative newcomer to the league, but nonetheless racked up an impressive 6-0 won-lost record in 8 starts. This month he fanned 87 batters in 63.2 innings and compiled a 1.55 ERA.

Kundrat has gone 11-1 with an ERA of 1.73 in his career, piling up 176 strikeouts in 125 innings pitched.
