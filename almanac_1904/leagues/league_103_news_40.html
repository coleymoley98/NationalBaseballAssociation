Juniors Lead with Chin, Get KO'd, 14-0
Friday, March 4th, 1904
The Gauthier Juniors shut out the Montreal Juniors 14-0 today behind the strong pitching of Jerome Zimny. Zimny surrendered only 4 hits in recording the complete-game victory, and struck out 10.

Following the last out, Zimny appeared to celebrate a little too boisterously for Montreal manager Jules Couture, who could be seen scolding the famously outspoken right-hander as the clubs left the field. Zimny's own manager then intervened with some words for his counterpart.

The Juniors skipper would not comment on the exchange, but at the Juniors press conference, Zimny confirmed that it was regarding the celebration.

"He basically told me I was being a lousy winner," Zimny said.

"And I told him he was being a lousy loser," Gauthier manager Ti Holley added.
