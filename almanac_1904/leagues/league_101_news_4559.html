Injury Ends Career for St-Jacques
Thursday, July 24th, 1902
The expression on his face said it all when starting pitcher Samuel St-Jacques was talking to reporters at a special press conference today. He was carefully examined by specialists the past couple of days, and after several tests the diagnosis was a ruptured ulnar collateral ligament. In this case, it means his career is likely over. The Detroit Lions issued the following statement: "It is with great sadness that we see Samuel ending his career due to injury. He was a great competitor and teammate and will be missed by the Lions organization." St-Jacques is 20 years old.

For the year St-Jacques has a 7-6 record with a 2.85 ERA in 16 starts.
