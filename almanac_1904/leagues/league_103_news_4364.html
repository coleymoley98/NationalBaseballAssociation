FJC's Premier Player: Rump of Coude
Tuesday, May 6th, 1902
Azibo Rump of the Coude Juniors had a fine season this year and today he accepted the Most Valuable Player Award for 1902.

The 19-year-old first baseman put up a .396 average and a .468 on-base percentage, piling up 74 hits, 15 doubles, no triples, 22 home runs, 63 RBIs and 48 runs scored.

He received 12 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Troy O'Sullivan of the Coude Juniors finished second in voting, while Randolph Roche of the Gallant Juniors finished third.
