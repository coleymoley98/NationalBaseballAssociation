Tremblay Shackles Chicagou
Sunday, July 16th, 1899
Marc-Joseph Tremblay was in a mood to talk following the Detroit Lions contest against the Chicagou Frontenacs, expounding to BNN reporters on his pitching philosophy. He had good reason to be excitable, having tossed a complete game shutout of the Frontenacs, 3-0.

"There are three important elements in pitching success: location, location, and location," Tremblay said. "I was hitting my spots time and time again."

The right-hander blanked Chicagou on a 4-hitter, allowing 4 walks and racking up 1 strikeout.

To date this season Tremblay has compiled a 5-8 record with a 5.22 ERA in 16 starts.
