Carbonaro Sensational, 5-for-5
Saturday, March 19th, 1904
Jameson Carbonaro was a tough out and went 5-for-5 in a fine hitting display today, but the Coude Juniors managed to overcome his performance and top the Quebec City Juniors, 16-13.

"I got my pitches and I connected," Carbonaro said. "They all found the right holes on the diamond. Some days you hit shots and they're right at somebody, and some days they fall in. And some days you lose, no matter how well you play."

On this occasion, Carbonaro played well enough to get his name in the record books. Jameson Carbonaro hit an RBI double in the 2nd, doubled in the 4th, doubled in the 6th, hit a two-run double in the 7th and doubled in the 9th.

This year Carbonaro is hitting .220 with 1 home run and 7 RBIs in 18 games played. He also boasts an OBP of .343 with 11 walks and 8 runs scored.
