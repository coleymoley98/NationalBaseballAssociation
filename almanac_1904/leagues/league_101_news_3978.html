Great Month for NL Rookie
Monday, July 1st, 1901
Last month Wayne Tang tantalized and tamed National League teams and ran up 3 victories.
In addition the Chicagou starter also was awarded the league's Rookie of the Month trophy.
He was honored for his outstanding performance during the month of June.

Tang coasted through the NL with 3 wins and 3 losses and a 3.41 ERA as he struck out 24 in 58 innings and held opposing hitters to a .274 batting average.

This season-to-date, Tang has fashioned a 5-3 mark and 3.66 ERA in 83.2 innings. Opposing clubs are batting .281 against him and he has logged 34 strikeouts.
