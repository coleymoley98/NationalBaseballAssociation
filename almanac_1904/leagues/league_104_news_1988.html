Hartford's Talbot Goes Ballistic with 5 Hits
Wednesday, July 7th, 1897
It was a parade of hits for Matteo Talbot of the Hartford Juniors at Juniors Ballpark today.

Connecting with regularity, Talbot finished the day with a total of 5 hits in 6 at-bats, but the Juniors rose to the occasion and rained on his parade with a 12-11 win.

"Talbot was really good, we were just a little better," said Juniors manager Samuel Sénécal in the postgame interview. "It's all right with me if he gets 5 hits every game against us, as long as we win."

Matteo Talbot singled in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, hit a two-run single in the 6th, singled in the 7th and grounded out in the 9th.

So far this season Talbot has gotten 36 hits in 138 at-bats for a .261 batting average with 4 home runs. His stats include 29 RBIs and 25 runs scored. He has played in 34 games.
