Liberty Listless in 7-0 Loss to Redcoats
Wednesday, May 18th, 1904
Amporn Wrice of the Boston Redcoats spun his magic on the Philadelphia Liberty today. He blanked them 7-0 on 5 hits, registering 7 strikeouts and 2 walks.

"The bottom line was I just kept the ball down," Wrice said. "My breaking stuff was down. Everything was down."

Talking to reporters in the postgame interview, Boston skipper Herb Lamaison said the team "got solid pitching, good defense and took great team at-bats in a lot of different situations."

"Amporn pitched really well but this was a solid performance by everyone," he said.

So far this season Wrice has a 3-2 record, 5 starts, 43 innings, 26 strikeouts and a 1.88 ERA.
