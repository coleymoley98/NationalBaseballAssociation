Philadelphia Left Fielder CL's Best Batter in June
Friday, July 1st, 1898
It's perhaps an award he'll win many more times through his career, as at age 22 Hunter Jones has a lot of baseball ahead of him.

But for now the young Philadelphia Liberty star can enjoy the numbers he put up in June, which have garnered him the nod as the Canadian League Batter of the Month.

His breakout month featured a .423 average with 1 home run, 10 RBIs and 19 runs scored.

In 53 games this year Jones has posted a .403 average and put up these numbers: 1 home run, 20 RBIs and 34 runs scored.
