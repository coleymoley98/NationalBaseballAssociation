Bad News for Ranger -- Career Likely Over
Sunday, May 1st, 1904
The Montreal Royales have announced the retirement of reliever Juste Ranger. The news, while disappointing, does not come as a complete surprise. Ranger has been out of action with a partially torn ulnar collateral ligament. He recently said that it had put his baseball future "seriously in doubt." All doubt is now removed: he has chosen to retire from the sport. The 21-year-old Ranger will have more to say about his decision when he meets with the media later this afternoon.
