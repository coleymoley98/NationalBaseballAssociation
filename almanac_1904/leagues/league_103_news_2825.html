Juniors Boily and Juniors Holloman Suspended for Fight
Saturday, April 15th, 1899
After an intense confrontation, both the Juniors Noor Boily and the Juniors Tyler Holloman were handed their punishments today. The league commissioner's office issued suspensions for an on-the-field conflict that occurred at a recent Montreal-Minneapolis game. Noor Boily will miss 3 games, while Tyler Holloman was suspended for 7 games.

"Sometimes we go too far and have to pay the consequences," Noor Boily commented later. "Hopefully cooler heads will prevail the next time we play."

In this current campaign Boily is hitting at a .275 clip with no home runs and 19 RBIs.

For the season Holloman has logged 4 wins and 6 losses and struck out 100 batters in 102.2 innings while compiling a 5.00 ERA.
