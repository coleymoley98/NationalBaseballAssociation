Rebels Chill Lu's Hot Streak
Monday, June 24th, 1895
It was the best of both worlds for the New York Rebels today at Huntington Avenue Grounds, as they defeated the Boston Redcoats 3-1 and also snapped Boyce Lu's 28-game hitting streak. The 31-year-old Boston third baseman failed to get a hit in 4 at-bats.

"Nothing personal against Lu, we just wanted to win the game," said Rebels skipper Antoine Morissette. "We did a great job keeping him off-balance and guessing."

When asked by reporters about his streak coming to an end, Lu frowned and said, "I won't lie and say that I'm not disappointed about it, but I'd feel much better if we had won."

This season Lu is batting .363 with no home runs. His totals include 28 RBIs and 39 runs scored.
