The New York Rebels shut out the Philadelphia Liberty 13-0 today behind the strong pitching of Seksit Kittipornpracha. Kittipornpracha surrendered only 5 hits in recording the complete-game victory, and struck out 1.

Kittipornpracha's own manager then intervened with some words for his counterpart.
