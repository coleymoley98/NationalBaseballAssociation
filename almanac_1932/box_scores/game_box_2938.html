Frontenacs Top Indians
It's party time in Chicagou. The confetti is flying and residents are smiling.

The Chicagou Frontenacs have reached the mountaintop, capturing the 1920 Riel Cup today. They defeated the Ottawa Indians 10-9 at Lincoln Park to officially wrap up the series 4-3.

Asked how it feels to be champion, third baseman MacKenzie Budge was to the point.

"It feels great, like a million bucks. I'm so happy right now, not just for me but for all the guys," he said. "We put a lot of work into this and deserve to be champions."

The team is planning a victory parade and fan appreciation day later in the week. Chicagou finished the regular season 69-43 and claimed first place in the National League.
