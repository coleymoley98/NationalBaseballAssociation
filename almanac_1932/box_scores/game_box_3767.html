Green on Fire: 5-for-6 for Team
Team second baseman Hubert Green added his name to the record books with a 5-hit spree at Ecatepec Memorial Grounds today, but found his special moment spoiled when the Team Oregon took a shootout win over the Quebec.

"I would have much preferred getting only a couple of hits and winning the game," he confessed to reporters afterward. "It's hard to go back into the clubhouse and celebrate a 5-hit game when you also lost."

Quebec skipper Zack Santora called Green's game "a once-in-a-lifetime moment."

"It's a shame the guy can't really enjoy it," he said. "It's also a shame what happened with our pitching today, but at least that we can try to fix."

Hubert Green doubled in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, hit an RBI single in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, doubled in the 7th and grounded out in the 8th.
