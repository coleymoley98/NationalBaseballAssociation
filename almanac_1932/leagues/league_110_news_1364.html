Davenport Shuts Out Saskatoon Behind Leclerc, 3-0
Tuesday, June 2nd, 1931
Oliver Leclerc kept the Saskatoon Jr. Coyotes hitters off balance all game long as the Davenport Rivermen pitcher blanked the Jr. Coyotes, 3-0.

Leclerc gave up 4 hits, struck out 9 and walked 2. In the postgame press conference, the right-hander was reluctant to talk about the outing at length.

"It was a nice win in a long season," he said. "I don't want to dwell too much on it."
