Cincinnati Reds Ship Patry to Wolfeburg
Tuesday, January 10th, 1928
The Cincinnati Reds announced a deal with the Celtics today, sending 33-year-old RHP Ignace Patry and 23-year-old minor league RF Ryder Watkins to Wolfeburg for 26-year-old minor league LF Jaidon Fronckowiak. It had been talked about for the past few weeks and everything came together late last night. All players are expected to be joining their respective clubs shortly.

Cincinnati will retain a portion of Patry's remaining contract.

In his career Patry has collected 41 victories and 41 defeats along with 2 saves. He has a lifetime 3.04 ERA and has struck out 287 in 757.1 innings.
