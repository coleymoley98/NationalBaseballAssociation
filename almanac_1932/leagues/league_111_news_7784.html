#1 EBL Hurler Named
Thursday, August 19th, 1926
Frankie Richir was really special this season. You could feel the electricity in the air every time he took the mound for the Fort Wayne Pioneers. Today he was honored by his selection as the 1926 Eastern Baseball League EBL Pitcher of the Year recipient.

He started 14 games, logging 98.1 innings, 9 wins, 3 losses, 122 strikeouts and a 2.20 ERA. Richir gave up 75 hits, while walking 22 and holding opponents to a .209 average.

He received 7 first place votes out of a possible 10. Alfie Lafond of the Hamilton Jr. Steelers finished second in voting with 3 first place votes, while Ricky Greene of the Toronto Jr. Blue Sox finished third.
