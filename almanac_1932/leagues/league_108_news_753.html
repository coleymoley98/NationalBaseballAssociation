Players Suspended in Rainiers-Thunder Donnybrook
Saturday, July 19th, 1930
After an intense confrontation, both the Rainiers Calvin Chambers and the Thunder Niphon Ruangklan were handed their punishments today. The league commissioner's office issued suspensions for an on-the-field conflict that occurred at a recent Seattle-Winnipeg game. Calvin Chambers will miss 5 games, while Niphon Ruangklan was suspended for 6 games.

"Sometimes we go too far and have to pay the consequences," Calvin Chambers commented later. "Hopefully cooler heads will prevail the next time we play."

Chambers is hitting .350 this season with 4 home runs, 13 RBIs and 10 runs scored. He has 14 hits in 40 at-bats.

So far this year Ruangklan has a 3-3 record with a 4.29 ERA.
