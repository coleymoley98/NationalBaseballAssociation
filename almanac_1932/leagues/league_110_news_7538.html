O'Leary Extends Hit Streak to 25 Games
Friday, July 16th, 1926
Adams right fielder Bones O'Leary keeps on racking up hits every time he plays.

His hitting streak now stands at 25 games after collecting 2 hits at Ballpark of Adams today. The Mounties took the 4-1 victory over the Saskatoon Jr. Coyotes.

"I have been seeing the ball really good the last few weeks, but I am a bit surprised how things are working out," O'Leary said. "I've gotten a couple flares to drop in, so I've also been a little lucky."
Bones O'Leary singled in the 1st, flied out in the 3rd, singled in the 6th and grounded out in the 8th.

This year O'Leary is batting .407 with 6 home runs. He has scored 40 runs and driven in 41.
