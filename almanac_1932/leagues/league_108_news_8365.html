Hits Galore: Watkins Drills 6 Hits
Wednesday, June 1st, 1927
It was a big day for Ryder Watkins and he brought the Calgary Wranglers along for the ride.

Watkins led his club to a 14-9 victory over the Winnipeg Thunder today at Thunder Stadium, smoking Winnipeg pitching for 6 hits in 6 at-bats. The 23-year-old first baseman later called the victory "sweet."

Ryder Watkins hit an RBI double in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, hit an RBI double in the 4th, singled in the 6th, hit a solo-shot off Tida Tremblay in the 8th and singled in the 9th.
