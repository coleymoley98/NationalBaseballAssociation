Richir Sharp in 1-0 Shutout
Sunday, June 20th, 1926
In an outstanding mound performance, Fort Wayne Pioneers starter Frankie Richir fired a complete-game shutout against the Buffalo Rapids at Ballpark of Buffalo today.

Richir was the key to the Pioneers's 1-0 win. The 18-year-old right-hander was in high spirits on the mount for much of the game, his mood largely undented by the 2 hits and 4 walks he allowed.

"2 hits?" he said with a laugh following the game. "Listen, I'd be happy with a 30-hitter as long as it was a shutout."

To date this season, Richir has a record of 3 wins and no losses and a 0.45 ERA. He has appeared in 5 games and struck out 42 in 39.2 innings.
