Injury Worsens, Return Delayed For Cardinaux Silawarn
Tuesday, September 7th, 1926
St. Louis center fielder Phakhawat Silawarn has suffered a setback in his recovery from an intercostal strain. According to Cardinaux officials, he will be out of action for 2 weeks. "Doctors are concerned with the rate of healing and have decided that Silawarn will need additional time in rehab before he will be cleared to play," one team spokesman explained to reporters.

This season Silawarn is batting .201 with 26 RBIs, 40 runs scored and 4 home runs.
