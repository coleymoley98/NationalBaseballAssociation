Coulter Grabs EL Reliever of the Year Award
Saturday, September 21st, 1929
His manager and teammates call Isaac Coulter a true bullpen ace.

The Eastern League calls him the winner of the Reliever of the Year Award for 1929.

The London Indians relief specialist out of Chicagou, The United Provinces, won the award with 9 saves in 24 relief appearances, 55 strikeouts in 52.2 innings and 21 walks.

He compiled a record of 7 wins and no losses and had a 2.39 ERA.

He received 8 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Garland Brewer of the Hamilton Blue Sox finished second in voting, while Hervé Brunet of the Sherbrooke Nordiques finished third.
