Kunyarat Named Top Reliever for CBL
Saturday, August 11th, 1928
At the young age of only 18, Komkrit Kunyarat has been named the Central Baseball League Reliever of the Year Award winner for 1928.

The future appears bright for the Winnipeg Jr. Thunder relief ace, who captured the award with 5 saves in 23 relief appearances, 48 strikeouts in 32 innings and a record of 4-0 to go with his 1.41 ERA.

He received 8 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Leo Sutton of the Davenport Rivermen finished second in voting, while Todd Brafford of the Adams Mounties finished third.
