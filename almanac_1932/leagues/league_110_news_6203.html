Le Meur 5-for-6 Against Jr. Coyotes
Thursday, June 18th, 1925
Tanat Le Meur was at the head of a Omaha Jr. Falcons hit parade today at Omaha Grounds. The 17-year-old went to the plate 6 times and came away with 5 hits, but it was the Saskatoon Jr. Coyotes that marched off with the 13-10 win.

"We didn't have it today," griped Jr. Falcons skipper Jesse O'Byrne following the game. "Tanat did fine, but as a team, we didn't do it with the bats and we didn't do it pitching."

Le Meur did more than fine, dominating the Jr. Coyotes at the plate during his record-making 5-for-6 performance.

"Each time I went to the plate, I was thinking about hitting the ball hard somewhere," the sweet-swinging second baseman told reporters. "When I focus on that, I usually have quality at-bats."

Tanat Le Meur singled in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, singled in the 5th, struck out in the 5th, singled in the 8th and singled in the 9th.

To date this year Le Meur is batting .476 with 1 home run.
