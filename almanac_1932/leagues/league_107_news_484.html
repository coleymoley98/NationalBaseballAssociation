Gango Belts Bunch of Base Hits
Thursday, June 19th, 1930
The Ranier Seahawks stopped the Abbotsford Alpines by a score of 7-1 at Alpines Ballpark today, with the lion's share of the credit going to first baseman Jake Gango. The 18-year-old had a career day, collecting 5 hits.

"Every batter wants to have a quality at-bat every time he steps up to the plate," Gango said later. "He wants to hit the ball hard."

Gango did just that in his 5 at-bats, much to the chagrin of the Alpines.

"Not much that I can say," offered Abbotsford manager Erick Sethna. "He just hammered the heck out of us."

Jake Gango singled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, singled in the 7th and doubled in the 9th.

Gango is hitting .343 this season with 3 home runs, 10 RBIs and 10 runs scored. He has 23 hits in 67 at-bats.
