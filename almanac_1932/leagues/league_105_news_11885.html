Sexauer Signs for 3 years
Tuesday, November 4th, 1930
Fans hailed the signing of Seamus Sexauer today as the Boston Redcoats left fielder came to terms with the club.

Set to make $38,000 over the upcoming 3 years, Sexauer signed primarily out of loyalty to the organization, thrilling the most diehard supporters who think there isn't much loyalty in a market driven by free agency.

In his 520-game career Sexauer is batting .333 with 135 doubles, 9 triples, 48 home runs and 340 RBIs. He has stolen 5 bases and scored 354 times.
