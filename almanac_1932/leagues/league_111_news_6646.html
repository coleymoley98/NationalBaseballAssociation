Eastern Baseball League Crowns Canton's Adeleye
Thursday, August 20th, 1925
Canton ace Prayad Adeleye put up some sensational stats for the 1925 season and has been voted the Eastern Baseball League EBL Pitcher of the Year winner.

The 18-year-old Legends star made 12 starts and racked up a 6-1 record. He pitched 85.1 innings, collected 96 strikeouts, gave up 18 walks and 56 hits in holding the opposition to a .183 batting average.

He received 10 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Clyde Grill of the North Bay Battalion finished second in voting, while Colt Munro of the Fort Wayne Pioneers finished third.
