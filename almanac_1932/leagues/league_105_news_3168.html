Palmisano Batters Detroit Big Time
Friday, July 29th, 1932
It was a parade of hits for Jerold Palmisano of the Chicagou Frontenacs at Wrigley Field 1928 today.

Connecting almost every time he went to the plate, Palmisano finished the day with a total of 5 hits in 5 at-bats. His strong individual effort propelled the Frontenacs to a 7-0 victory.

"Palmisano was really good and we were really bad," groaned Lions manager Jake Downing. "Next time he's that hot, we'll just walk him."

Jerold Palmisano doubled in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, hit a two-run triple in the 4th, hit an RBI single in the 6th and hit a solo-shot off Hafit Duangsawang in the 8th.

This season Palmisano is batting .359 with 28 hits and 1 home run. He has scored 16 times and has 14 runs batted in.
