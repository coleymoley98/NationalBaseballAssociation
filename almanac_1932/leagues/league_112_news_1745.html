Boyer Holds Canadiens Scoreless
Monday, July 13th, 1931
In an outstanding mound performance, Drummondville Voltigeurs starter Eli Boyer fired a complete-game shutout against the Sherbrooke Canadiens at Field of the Juniors today.

Boyer was the key to the Voltigeurs's 4-0 win. The 16-year-old right-hander was in high spirits on the mount for much of the game, his mood largely undented by the 7 hits and no walks he allowed.

"7 hits?" he said with a laugh following the game. "Listen, I'd be happy with a 30-hitter as long as it was a shutout."

In 13 starts this season Boyer has chalked up a 6-1 record and a 1.61 ERA.
