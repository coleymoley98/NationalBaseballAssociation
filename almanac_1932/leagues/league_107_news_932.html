Vancouver Hurler Tops in WBL Pitcher of the Year Voting
Monday, August 11th, 1930
The WBL Pitcher of the Year for 1930 has been announced and the Western Baseball League winner is Jeremy Labrie of the Vancouver Giants.

With a record of 7-1, Labrie was certainly a worthy winner. He made 14 starts and rang up 99.1 innings, 108 strikeouts and a 1.27 ERA.

He received 8 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Chase Mullins of the Victoria Admirals finished second in voting, while Gustav Lafrenière of the Abbotsford Alpines finished third.
