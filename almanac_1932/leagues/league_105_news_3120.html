White Sox Chill Hadzima's Hot Streak
Sunday, July 24th, 1932
It was a doubly miserable day today for Winfield Hadzima of the Philadelphia Liberty, as he saw his 25-game hitting streak end and his team lose 5-2 to the Chicagou White Sox at Comiskey Park 1910. The 26-year-old left fielder was held hitless in 4 at-bats by the White Sox.

"Anyone on a 25-game hitting streak is going to be a tough out," Chicagou manager Tommy Dupuis said later. "But we had a plan and we were careful about it, and got it done."

This year Hadzima has collected a .294 batting average to date with 84 hits in 286 at-bats, 32 RBIs, 5 home runs and scored 45 times. He has played in 67 games.
