Boston Redcoats Ship Bélanger to Cincinnati
Wednesday, November 10th, 1926
Trade winds have been blowing through Boston. This time, a deal has materialized between the Redcoats and Reds. According to sources inside the organization, Boston will ship 27-year-old SS Nicolas Bélanger to Cincinnati for 32-year-old LHP Jude Ng and 21-year-old minor league RHP Kittichai Kejmanee.


Over his career Bélanger has batted .340 and collected 557 hits, 21 home runs and 270 RBIs.

Lifetime Ng has notched 106 wins and 109 losses and compiled a 3.74 ERA.
