Coureurs Take It on Chin, Lose, 13-0, to Drummondville
Tuesday, July 13th, 1926
Olivier Tardif of the Drummondville Voltigeurs spun his magic on the Champlain Coureurs today. He blanked them 13-0 on 4 hits, registering 4 strikeouts and 3 walks.

"The bottom line was I just kept the ball down," Tardif said. "My breaking stuff was down. Everything was down."

Talking to reporters in the postgame interview, Drummondville skipper Tai Grigsby said the team "got solid pitching, good defense and took great team at-bats in a lot of different situations."

"Olivier pitched really well but this was a solid performance by everyone," he said.

Tardif has made 11 starts, tossed 72.1 innings, struck out 50 batters and walked 18. His record is 5-3 with a 4.48 ERA and he has limited other teams to a .308 batting average.
