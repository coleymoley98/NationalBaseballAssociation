Lebel Selected Top Performer in WL
Monday, July 11th, 1932
So far in his young career, Marcel Lebel has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Western League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 24-year-old Saskatoon Coyotes first baseman hit at a torrid .429 clip as he collected 9 hits in 21 at-bats, 2 home runs, 6 RBIs and 5 runs scored.

So far this season Lebel is batting .333 with 3 home runs, 32 RBIs and 15 runs scored. He has 43 hits in 129 at-bats, 8 walks and a .382 OBP. Lebel has played in 32 games.
