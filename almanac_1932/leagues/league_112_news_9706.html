Canadiens, Rainey Stonewall Hartford, 4-0
Saturday, June 23rd, 1928
Sherbrooke Canadiens pitcher Maxwell Rainey really got it done at Hartford Grounds today in his 4-0 shutout victory over the Hartford Whalers, shutting them down on 5 hits.

"It was just one of those games where everything went right. I threw a lot of strikes," Rainey said. "And the pitches they did get to hit, they didn't do anything with them."

The 15-year-old issued no walks and had 7 strikeouts in the Canadiens win.
