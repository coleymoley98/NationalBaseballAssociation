Skipper Aubrey Nagle Dismissed in Toronto
Saturday, October 10th, 1931
Aubrey Nagle has managed his last game with the Toronto Braves.

Nagle met with team officials yesterday and his termination was officially announced today in a prepared statement from club general manager Chuachen Rattanakool.

"Aubrey Nagle has done an admirable job here, but I think it's time to go in another direction," the statement read. "At the end of the day, we just aren't getting the results we want with Aubrey and at this point we're willing to try to turn things around with a new manager."

A replacement has yet to be named, but a decision is expected to come forward shortly.

Team owner William Walden told BNN that the team had only been contemplating a managerial shift in the past week. Nagle left the clubhouse quietly upon learning the news and refused to comment when pressed by reporters.
