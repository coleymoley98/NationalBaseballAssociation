Juneau-Hamilton Trade: Markham for Wong
Saturday, July 18th, 1931
Sometimes a change of scenery can be a beneficial to a baseball player. That is the hope in Juneau and Hamilton as the teams came to an agreement today which sends 29-year-old RHP Shawn Markham to the Steelers and 27-year-old minor league 1B Nathan Wong to the Brasseurs. "Only time will tell if this is the right move for everyone," stated the Hamilton GM.


So far this season Markham is 1-and-0 with no saves and a 0.69 ERA. He has given up 15 hits in 13 innings with 2 strikeouts.

This year Wong is batting .267 with 3 home runs.
