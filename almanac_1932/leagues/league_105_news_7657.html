Norrell Smacks 5 Hits for Rebels
Friday, July 30th, 1926
No matter what the Chicagou White Sox threw at him today, Trent Norrell hit it. He easily handled White Sox pitching, going 5 for 5 and helping the Rebels to a 19-10 triumph at Yankee Stadium 1923.

"The way I was swinging today, I think I could have closed my eyes and hit the ball," he told BNN reporters in the postgame interview.

Told of Norrell's quip, White Sox skipper Daniel Verreault said he "would take him up on that next time."

Trent Norrell singled in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 2nd, walked in the 4th, singled in the 6th, singled in the 7th and hit a two-run single in the 8th.

So far this year Norrell has 3 home runs and a .338 batting average.
