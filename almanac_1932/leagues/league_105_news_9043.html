Woodmore Voted Best NL Pitcher in August
Thursday, September 1st, 1927
Despite the number of years he's been in the league, Eric Woodmore still gets excited when he puts together a quality stretch like he had in August. The 28-year-old starter for Chicagou was named National League Pitcher of the Month earlier today.

He collected 5 wins and 1 loss with 24 strikeouts in his 6 starts, putting up a fine 2.33 ERA in 54 innings of work.

This year Woodmore has worked 189.1 innings with 75 strikeouts en route to a 17-5 record and 2.57 ERA.
