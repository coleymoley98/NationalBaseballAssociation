Saskatoon's Westbrooks Elbows His Way to WL Award
Tuesday, September 24th, 1929
Somkiat Westbrooks of the Saskatoon Coyotes has been selected the premier pitcher in the Western League and will receive the Pitcher of the Year Award for 1929.

The top vote-getter completed the year with a 13-4 record. He started 17 times, threw 147.1 innings, chalked up 70 strikeouts and 55 walks, limited hitters to a .230 batting average and posted an impressive 2.50 ERA.

He received 5 first place votes out of a possible 8. Ansley LaVallee of the Seattle Rainiers finished second in voting with 2 first place votes, while Sid Moth of the Edmonton Eskimos finished third with 1 first place vote.
