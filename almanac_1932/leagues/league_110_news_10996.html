Central Baseball League Picks Top Player
Monday, July 1st, 1929
Central Baseball League pitchers will attest that it was tough to get Apichet Legault of the Lincoln Pioneers out last week. His all-around performance at the plate made him the league's most recent Player of the Week.

The 18-year-old first baseman boasted a .440 average with 1 home run, 7 RBIs and 5 runs scored. Legault went 11-for-25 for the week.

This season Legault has delivered 30 hits in 104 at-bats for a .288 average with 2 home runs. He is getting on base at a .308 clip and his totals also include 15 RBIs and 10 runs scored. This year Legault has played in 26 games.
