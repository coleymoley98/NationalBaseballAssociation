Roca Spectacular in 7-0 Win
Monday, June 18th, 1928
Steve Roca of the Detroit Lions humiliated the St. Louis Cardinaux at Bennett Park today, spinning a complete game shutout and holding the visitors to 3 hits.

"He stayed aggressive and his ball was really moving," said St. Louis manager Miguel Roque, after the game.

Roca whiffed 9 and walked 3 in the 7-0 win.

"If I make good pitches, I'm going to get some outs," Roca said. "I was making good pitches the entire game."

Presently Roca has a 6-2 mark in 10 starts with a 3.11 ERA. He has given up 74 hits in 81 innings, struck out 41 and issued 21 walks. Opposing clubs have compiled a .251 batting average against him.
