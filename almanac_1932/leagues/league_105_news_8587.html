Brasseurs Hennessy Named NL's Best Pitcher
Friday, July 1st, 1927
No doubt about it!
Juneau starter Steely Hennessy earned his National League Pitcher of the Month award.
He made that very clear as he mowed his way through league hitters in June.
During the month Hennessy chalked up a 5-1 record with a 3.06 ERA.

The Brasseurs star made 6 starts and surrendered 57 hits in 53 innings. He struck out 18 and walked 11.

His record for the year is 7-2 in 10 starts and Hennessy has a 2.83 ERA.
