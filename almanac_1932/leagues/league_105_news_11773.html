Frontenacs Dominate, Sweep Division Series
Wednesday, September 10th, 1930
The Chicagou Frontenacs rode the strong arm of Harry Larkings to earn a sweep of the Quebec Nordiques in the Division Series.

Chicagou won the final game at Ballpark of Quebec by a 3-0 count and the series 3-0. Larkings was instrumental in the series win and was named MVP.

"Hopefully we can do it all again in the League Chamionship Series," he said.

For the series, Larkings had a 0.00 ERA over 9 innings pitched, with a 1-0 record.

The Chicagou Frontenacs don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Detroit Lions - Montreal Royales series. Detroit leads the series 2 games to 1. The League Chamionship Series schedule will be announced after the Division Series finishes.
