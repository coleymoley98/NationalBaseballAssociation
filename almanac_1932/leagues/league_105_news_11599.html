NBA News: Protections lists for Expansion Draft must be submitted
Wednesday, November 6th, 1929
The time has come for National Baseball Association general managers to submit their lists of protected players for the expansion draft. The deadline for filing is tomorrow. Each team can protect up to 25 players in its organization. The new franchises will use these lists to stock their rosters.

The protection lists can be compiled and submitted in the expansion draft screen, which is now available in the league menu.
