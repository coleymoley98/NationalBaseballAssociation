Hinckley Runs Hit String to 20 Straight Games
Sunday, July 14th, 1929
The streak is alive and well -- at least for today.

Songsak Hinckley did it again, bashing 1 hit in the contest at Bennett Park today. For the last 20 games in a row, the 23-year-old third baseman has collected at least one hit. His efforts helped the St. Louis Cardinaux beat the Detroit Lions 4-3 today.

"I'm happy I kept the streak going," Hinckley told a standing-room-only crowd of reporters gathered in the St. Louis clubhouse. "Honestly though, I'm looking forward to going home and not thinking about this for the rest of the day."
Songsak Hinckley hit an RBI double in the 2nd, flied out in the 4th, grounded out in the 6th and flied out in the 8th.

This year Hinckley is batting .329 with 68 hits and 2 home runs. He has scored 26 times and has batted in 29 runs.
