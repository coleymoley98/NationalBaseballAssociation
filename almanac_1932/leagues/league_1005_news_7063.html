O'Geary, on Top of His Game, Beats Team, 5-0
Wednesday, March 24th, 1926
John O'Geary subdued the Team Iowa, 5-0, pitching a 6-hit gem for the Team Pallister at Toronto Memorial Fields.

"He was kind of teasing them with his off-speed stuff, and his fastball was moving everywhere," Team skipper Russell Staley said afterward.

O'Geary registered 2 strikeouts and issued 3 walks in his superb performance.

"We just didn't swing the bats very well. Or else he just pitched good, whichever way you want to look at it," said Team manager Jason Chalifoux after the defeat.

This year O'Geary has gone 1-2 with an ERA of 7.09 and has 10 strikeouts in 26.2 innings.
