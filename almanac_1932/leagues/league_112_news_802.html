The Math Says - Whalers Out of Playoffs
Thursday, July 24th, 1930
The Hartford Whalers were mathematically eliminated from postseason contention today. Florian Goyette of the Whalers said it's a tough way to finish out the season.

"To be eliminated from playoff contention in the last few games, it's not easy," he said.

Hartford last appeared in the postseason in 1927. This is their 3rd consecutive year missing the playoffs, and in total they have not advanced past the regular season 34 times in their 36-year history. In the last 10 seasons, the Whalers have missed the playoffs 8 times.
