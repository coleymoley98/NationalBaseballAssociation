Top Pitcher Award Announced by EBL
Wednesday, August 19th, 1931
Daley Ireland of the Fort Wayne Pioneers fired on all cylinders in 1931 and dominated Eastern Baseball League batters. For his superb season he has been selected the EBL Pitcher of the Year winner.

During the year Ireland compiled a 7-2 record, a 0.59 ERA and 91 strikeouts while working 76.1 innings.

He received 10 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Val Bonnet of the Toronto Jr. Blue Sox finished second in voting, while Donne Grigg of the Toronto Jr. Blue Sox finished third.
