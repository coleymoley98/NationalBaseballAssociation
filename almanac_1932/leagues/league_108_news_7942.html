Saskatoon's Sulley Elbows His Way to WL Award
Monday, September 20th, 1926
The Western League Pitcher of the Year Award winner has been chosen and the trophy goes to Saskatoon hurler Kyle Sulley for his outstanding season in 1926.

Sulley was quite impressive with a 11-5 record and a 3.42 ERA. In 16 starts and 134.1 innings, he struck out 94 and gave up 118 hits and 52 walks. Teams hit .232 against him.

He received 5 first place votes out of a possible 8. Weerasak Morillon of the Vancouver Giants finished second in voting with 3 first place votes, while Chaiya Junjira of the Calgary Wranglers finished third.
