Dostie Cracks 3 HRs for Minneapolis
Thursday, July 2nd, 1931
Minneapolis Jr. Saints bombardier Rattanapon Dostie set his sights on the stands and blasted three balls over the fence as his club took a 9-2 win over the Adams Mounties today.

"I guess you could say I was a good guesser today, especially on the last one," Dostie told writers in the postgame press room. "I was ahead in the count, so I gambled that I'd get a certain pitch, and got all of it."

For the game, the Minneapolis center fielder had 4 hits in 5 at-bats, drove in 5, and scored 3 runs.

Rattanapon Dostie struck out in the 1st, singled in the 4th, hit a solo-shot off François Fortin in the 6th, hit a two-run home run off Thanakarn Buangam in the 7th and hit a two-run home run off Bryon Tonge in the 9th.

This season Dostie has delivered 54 hits in 119 at-bats for a .454 average with 5 home runs. He is getting on base at a .504 clip and his totals also include 21 RBIs and 20 runs scored. This year Dostie has played in 30 games.
