Blast Puts McInnis Atop Home Run List
Tuesday, March 8th, 1932
Phuwanart McInnis has long been known as a big bopper. Now he owns the all-time home run record, having blasted number 9 at Ecatepec Memorial Grounds today.

The Team star broke the record held by William Byrd. McInnis reportedly received a congratulatory phone call after the game from top Canadian Senior Baseball Tournament officials.

"Congratulations messages are coming in from everywhere," McInnis said. "It's incredible."

The Dakota will hold a special ceremony for McInnis at a later date.

Phuwanart McInnis flied out in the 1st, doubled in the 4th and homered off Logan L'Heureux in the 6th.
