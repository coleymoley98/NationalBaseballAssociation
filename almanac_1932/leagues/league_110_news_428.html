Players Suspended in Jr. Saints-Jr. Falcons Donnybrook
Thursday, June 12th, 1930
After an intense confrontation, both the Jr. Saints Virgile Martel and the Jr. Falcons Chayapol Tin were handed their punishments today. The league commissioner's office issued suspensions for an on-the-field conflict that occurred at a recent Minneapolis-Omaha game. Virgile Martel will miss 4 games, while Chayapol Tin was suspended for 6 games.

"Sometimes we go too far and have to pay the consequences," Virgile Martel commented later. "Hopefully cooler heads will prevail the next time we play."

Thus far this year Martel is hitting .375 with no home runs, 2 RBIs and 8 runs scored.

Thus far this season, Tin has worked 18.1 innings with 22 strikeouts en route to a 2-1 record and a 4.91 ERA.
