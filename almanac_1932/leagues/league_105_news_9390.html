Toronto Trades Aurandt for Baltimore's Gist
Sunday, March 18th, 1928
It came down to the last hour of a self-imposed trade deadline, but the Toronto Blue Sox and Baltimore Robins finally came to an agreement on a trade that had been rumored for weeks. The trade sends Toronto's 33-year-old RHP Nick Aurandt and 21-year-old minor league 3B Blake Beaudry to Baltimore in a swap for 33-year-old C L.J. Gist. Both GMs seemed relieved that the deal had been completed with one stating, "We had to set a deadline to get it done." The players are expected to report to their new teams by tomorrow.

Baltimore will retain a portion of Gist's remaining contract.

During his career the 33-year-old Aurandt has racked up 30 wins, 43 losses and 5 saves. His lifetime ERA is 4.95 in 154 games and 752 innings.

Lifetime Gist is batting .278 with 181 doubles, 17 triples, 26 home runs and 450 RBIs in 938 games.
