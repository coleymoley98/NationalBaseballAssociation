Hamilton Celebrates Wild Card Win
Wednesday, July 22nd, 1931
Cheers, laughter, and even a few tears filled the Hamilton Jr. Steelers clubhouse as the team celebrated a Eastern Baseball League wild card berth.

"We didn't win the division like we wanted to, but I'm still smiling," said first baseman Earnest Ettrick. "In the end, we've still got a chance to win the Adams Cup and that's the important thing."

The club has a 28-20 record and will be seeking the 2nd Eastern Baseball League championship in team history.
