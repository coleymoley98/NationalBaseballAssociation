Wednesday, December 2nd, 1931
The Braves today announced that they have come to terms with Sang Sunanda, a 25-year-old third baseman they've been scouting for a while.

Sunanda will earn $18,120 a year over 5 years and is expected to play a key role with the club.

Sunanda is batting .274 lifetime with 654 hits, 98 doubles, 57 triples, 56 home runs, 343 RBIs to date. He has also scored 353 runs.
