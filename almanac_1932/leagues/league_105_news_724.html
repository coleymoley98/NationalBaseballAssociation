Baltimore Downs Wolfeburg as Baluch Tosses 3-0 Shutout
Monday, July 14th, 1930
Liam Baluch kept the opposition off the scoreboard and the Baltimore Robins were all smiles after winning 3-0 against the Wolfeburg Celtics at League Park today. The right-handed Baluch gave up no runs in his start, permitted 4 hits and had 4 strikeouts.

"If there was a stat for 'team shutout,' this would be it," Baluch said later. "My catcher called a great game, guys delivered at the plate and when I messed something up the guys behind me were where they needed to be."

This season-to-date, Baluch has fashioned a 3-4 mark and 5.17 ERA in 71.1 innings. Opposing clubs are batting .282 against him and he has logged 24 strikeouts.
