CBL News: Opening Day
Monday, June 1st, 1931
How sweet it is! Central Baseball League starts play today. It is a new season and a new beginning for all the boys of baseball. Opening Day is here! It is a day of great excitement for everyone in the world of baseball -- players, fans, sportswriters, broadcasters, managers, coaches, scouts, groundkeepers, batboys, ballpark vendors, agents and the owners. Everybody is eagerly awaiting the first pitch of the game and the first home run. Old records will be broken and new marks set. It all gets under way today with this lineup of games:

Omaha Jr. Falcons @ Minneapolis Jr. Saints
St. Louis Jr. Cardinaux @ Davenport Rivermen
Adams Mounties @ Saskatoon Jr. Coyotes
Winnipeg Jr. Thunder @ Lincoln Pioneers
