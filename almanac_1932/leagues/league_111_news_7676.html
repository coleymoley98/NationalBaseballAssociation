Gilliland Takes EBL's Best Bat Trophy
Sunday, August 1st, 1926
Hamilton Jr. Steelers fans may just have witnessed a star player blossoming before their eyes in July.

Ray Gilliland put up a remarkable month at the age of 18 and has been named the Eastern Baseball League Batter of the Month.

For the month, Gilliland hit .393 with 7 home runs, 23 RBIs and 20 runs scored.

This season Gilliland has delivered 60 hits in 187 at-bats for a .321 average with 9 home runs. He is getting on base at a .386 clip and his totals also include 36 RBIs and 30 runs scored. This year Gilliland has played in 50 games.
