Moore Earns June's Top Batter Prize in ABL
Friday, July 1st, 1927
When Leonidas Moore stepped up to the plate in June he did it with confidence, and today he was honored with the Atlantic Baseball League Batter of the Month award.

The young Pawtucket right fielder posted a .333 batting average with 8 home runs, 27 RBIs and scored 24 runs. Moore also worked pitchers for 13 walks to post a .408 on-base percentage.
