Saenz Top CBL Pitcher in July
Wednesday, August 1st, 1928
Ray Saenz hasn't been around long enough to know that youngsters shouldn't be winning Central Baseball League Pitcher of the Month. But when you put up numbers like his, you're going to get some notice.

In July the Davenport starter earned a 5-1 mark in 6 starts. He threw 48 innings, struck out 44 and compiled a solid 1.50 ERA.

This season Saenz has worked 116.2 innings with 92 strikeouts en route to a 10-3 record and a 2.08 ERA.
