Shin Jacks 3 into the Stands
Sunday, July 25th, 1926
The old superstition that "bad news comes in threes" certainly came true for the New Britain Yankees today.

Hartford left fielder Ryan Shin tripled up on the home runs to propel the Whalers to the 19-5 win at Hartford Grounds.

"We had our chances to win the game, but we just didn't come through," New Britain manager Ara Frey said. "Shin hit the heck out of the ball."

In addition to the homers, Shin collected 3 hits in 5 at-bats, 5 RBIs and scored 3 runs.

"Easy swings get you home runs," he said after the game. "I kept telling myself not to press. They have to be nice and easy swings."

Ryan Shin flied out in the 1st, hit a solo-shot off Owen Hellman in the 2nd, hit a solo-shot off B.J. Siteti in the 3rd, hit a three-run home run off B.J. Siteti in the 4th and flied out in the 6th.

Shin is hitting at a .351 mark this season. His stats show 9 home runs, 34 RBIs and 51 runs scored. He has played 49 games for the Whalers.
