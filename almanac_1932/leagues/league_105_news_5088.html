CL Star of the Week Announced
Monday, June 23rd, 1924
There's nothing like being in the prime of your baseball career -- and playing like it. Just ask Stephan Pelissier, the Ottawa first baseman, who swung a sweet bat all week to not only aid his team, but also pick up the Canadian League Player of the Week honors.
