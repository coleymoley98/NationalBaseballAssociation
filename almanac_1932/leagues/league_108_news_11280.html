Season-Ending Injury for Rampillon
Monday, August 5th, 1929
Benny Rampillon had hoped for the best, but expected the worst. Today, he learned from the Saskatoon medical staff that he would miss the rest of the season. The Saskatoon starting pitcher suffered a damaged elbow ligament in an earlier ballgame. He admitted to being discouraged, but said he plans to come back strong next year.

Thus far this season, Rampillon has worked 69.1 innings with 39 strikeouts en route to a 4-3 record and a 4.80 ERA.
