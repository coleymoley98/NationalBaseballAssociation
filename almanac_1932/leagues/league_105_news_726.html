Dubourg Runs Hit String to 20 Straight Games
Monday, July 14th, 1930
Dustin Dubourg of the Minnesota Saints extended his hitting streak to 20 straight games with 3 hits in today's 14-5 triumph over the St. Louis Cardinaux at St. Louis Grounds.

"Dustin has been making it easy for me to pencil his name in the lineup every day," Saints manager A.J. Provost joked after the game.
Dustin Dubourg grounded out in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 2nd, grounded out in the 4th, singled in the 6th, walked in the 7th and singled in the 9th.

On the year Dubourg has hit .366 with 3 home runs, 42 RBIs and scored 48 runs.
