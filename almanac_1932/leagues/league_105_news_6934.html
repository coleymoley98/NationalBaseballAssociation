Boston Redcoats Ship Taricco to Minnesota
Tuesday, December 15th, 1925
The Baseball News Network is reporting that Boston and Minnesota have agreed in principle to a swap that will send 25-year-old 2B Ongard Taricco to the Saints with 32-year-old RHP Aindriú Hoban and 24-year-old minor league 2B Chayaphon McGee joining the Redcoats. Boston general manager Nick Varin would not confirm the trade, but a team representative close to the negotiations said the deal is official and is now only awaiting approval from the league.


His lifetime stats show Taricco has batted .274 and collected 204 hits, 2 home runs and 121 RBIs.

In his career Hoban has amassed 96 wins and 124 losses with an ERA of 3.89 and 521 strikeouts.
