Tuttle Tosses 7-0 Shutout for Omaha
Wednesday, July 20th, 1932
Wyatt Tuttle was in a zone of his own most of the day, shutting out the Edmonton Eskimos on 3 hits.

"He was too darn good for us today," grumbled Edmonton manager Nattaporn Lamontagne after the 7-0 loss to the Omaha Falcons.

Tuttle finished with no strikeouts and 1 walk in a strong outing.

In the postgame press conference, Omaha manager Aubrey Nagle said Tuttle "had good velocity, good movement and had those guys off balance. He just pitched his butt off."

This season Tuttle has worked 63.2 innings with 14 strikeouts en route to a 5-2 record and a 3.96 ERA.
