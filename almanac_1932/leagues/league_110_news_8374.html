Gildea Goes 5-for-6
Friday, June 3rd, 1927
5 times is the charm -- at least for Jayden Gildea.

The Davenport Rivermen third baseman tore up the Winnipeg Jr. Thunder to the tune of 5 hits in 6 at-bats at Ballpark of Davenport today. The offensive onslaught from Gildea powered the Rivermen to a 12-9 win.

"Gildea was a tough out today," Winnipeg manager Rafaël Bérubé said to the press in the postgame interview. "He hit some very good pitches with that quick bat of his. Not an easy out, that's for sure."

Jayden Gildea singled in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, tripled in the 5th, flied out in the 7th and singled in the 8th.
