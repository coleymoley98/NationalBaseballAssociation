White Sox Prevail, as Division Series Goes to the Wire
Wednesday, September 21st, 1932
In an exciting Division Series that had its fair share of drama and intrigue, perhaps it is National Baseball Association fans who have come out the winner.

But on the field, the winners were the Chicagou White Sox, who edged the Toronto Blue Sox 3-2 to advance to the League Chamionship Series.

Chicagou won the final game by a 7-0 count to wrap up the series. Narakorn Wasiak earned series MVP honors, by hitting .333 with a .409 on-base percentage. He had 1 home runs, drove in 2 RBI and scored 2 runs.

The Chicagou White Sox will play against the Ottawa Indians in the next round. Ottawa won their series against the Wolfeburg Celtics 3 games to 2. The League Chamionship Series schedule will be announced after the Division Series finishes.
