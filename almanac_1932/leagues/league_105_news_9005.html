5 Hits for Royales Welter
Friday, August 26th, 1927
It wasn't just another day at the ballpark for Cole Welter of the Montreal Royales. Welter went on a tear at Montreal Grounds and cracked hit after hit off Minnesota pitching. When the dust settled on the diamond, the 23-year-old third baseman had a career 5-for-5 day and something he will long remember.

"Yeah, it'll be something nice to tell the grandkids, but I don't think I'll tell them we lost," he joked in the BNN postgame interview.

Despite Welter's personal accolades, the Minnesota Saints walked off the field with the 17-12 win.

Cole Welter hit an RBI single in the 1st, walked in the 3rd, doubled in the 5th, hit an RBI single in the 5th, singled in the 7th and doubled in the 9th.
