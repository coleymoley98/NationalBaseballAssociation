Lei Socks it to Nordiques, Cracks 5 Hits
Sunday, June 2nd, 1929
Kitti Lei had an answer for everything the Quebec Nordiques threw at him today at Ballpark of Quebec. The Montreal left fielder went 5-for-6 in the game to put his name in the record book and put the Royales on top, 13-6.

Summing up his batting philosophy to BNN writers after the game, Lei said, "I just see the ball and I hit it."

Kitti Lei doubled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 4th, singled in the 6th, singled in the 8th and grounded out in the 9th.

So far on the season Lei is hitting .330 with 2 home runs, 14 RBIs and 16 runs scored. He has 35 hits and played in 26 games.
