North Bay Captures East Crown
Tuesday, July 12th, 1927
The North Bay Battalion are champions of the East, but don't plan the parade quite yet.

"It's a nice accomplishment, but we haven't done anything until we win the Adams Cup," said first baseman Roy Broehl. "We're going to celebrate this for sure, but the real celebration is still to come for fans in North Bay."

The club's record currently sits at 30-8. It is the team's 2nd division title and they will be seeking the franchise's 4th Eastern Baseball League championship.

Team manager Arran Laplante said he'll take some time to celebrate the division title, but then it's "back to work" for members of the team and coaching staff.
