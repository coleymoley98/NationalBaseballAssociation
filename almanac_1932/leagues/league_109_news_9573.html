5-Hit Blitz by Hamilton's Boonkriang
Sunday, June 3rd, 1928
Apirak Boonkriang was unstoppable, but the Hamilton Blue Sox were not.

Despite his hitting heroics at Ballpark of Hamilton today, the Blue Sox lost 7-3 to the London Indians. Boonkriang battered London for 5 hits in 5 at-bats. His only consolation will be a boost in his batting average and a brief mention in the record book.

Boonkriang just shook his head after the game, telling BNN reporters, "It's the team with the most runs that wins, not the most hits."

Apirak Boonkriang hit a two-run home run off Norihiro Uchiyama in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, singled in the 8th, singled in the 10th and walked in the 12th.
