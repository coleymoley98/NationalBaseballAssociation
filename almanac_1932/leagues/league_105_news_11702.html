Detroit Loses Kosho Toda, Will Miss Season
Thursday, August 28th, 1930
Kosho Toda has to call it a season. The Detroit Lions right fielder sustained a season-ending injury against the St. Louis Cardinaux and will not play again this year according to club doctors. Toda was injured while throwing the ball. His doctors diagnosed it as a strained hamstring and stated that he would not be able to play for 8 weeks.
