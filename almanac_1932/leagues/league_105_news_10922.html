Liberty End Wintringham's Streak
Sunday, June 23rd, 1929
It was a doubly miserable day today for David Wintringham of the Ottawa Indians, as he saw his 26-game hitting streak end and his team lose 4-3 to the Philadelphia Liberty at Lincoln Park. The 23-year-old left fielder was held hitless in 3 at-bats by the Liberty.

"Anyone on a 26-game hitting streak is going to be a tough out," Philadelphia manager Kendrick Markowski said later. "But we had a plan and we were careful about it, and got it done."

So far this season Wintringham has 13 home runs and a .376 batting average.
