Leven of Providence ABL Batter of the Month
Tuesday, July 1st, 1930
It's perhaps an award he'll win many more times through his career, as at age 15 Eli Leven has a lot of baseball ahead of him.

But for now the young Providence Bruins star can enjoy the numbers he put up in June, which have garnered him the nod as the Atlantic Baseball League Batter of the Month.

His breakout month featured a .466 average with 6 home runs, 25 RBIs and 31 runs scored.
