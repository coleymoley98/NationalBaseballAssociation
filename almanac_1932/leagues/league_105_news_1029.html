Pittsburgh Quakers Ship Legendre to Juneau
Monday, January 5th, 1931
Reports have surfaced that Pittsburgh has agreed to send 30-year-old RHP J.R. Legendre to the Juneau Brasseurs in exchange for 20-year-old minor league RHP Alfons Veal and 18-year-old minor league 2B Luc Cyr. "It's a done deal," said one executive speaking on the condition of anonymity. "I'm surprised word got around so quickly. We just signed off on this an hour ago." The deal apparently is awaiting physicals before an official announcement is made by either team.


In his career the 30-year-old Legendre has accumulated a 15-22 won-lost mark and opposing clubs have hit .299 against him.
