Great Week for Pittsburgh's Ryan Shin
Monday, July 15th, 1929
It can be difficult for a young player to step out of the shadows and assert himself in the Eastern League, but last week Ryan Shin of the Pittsburgh Liberty reminded everyone just why he's here as he threw up numbers that made him an easy selection for Player of the Week. Shin rocked opposing pitching for a .550 average (11-for-20), 2 home runs and 5 RBIs to come away with the award.

This season Shin is batting .345 with 48 hits and 4 home runs. He has scored 19 times and has 22 runs batted in.
