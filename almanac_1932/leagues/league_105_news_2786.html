Steelers Suffer 9-0 Drubbing from Liberty
Wednesday, June 15th, 1932
Jayden Mays of the Philadelphia Liberty worked his mound magic on the Hamilton Steelers. He blanked them 9-0 on 4 hits, registering 5 strikeouts and 3 walks.

"Making good pitches is the name of the game and I made some good ones in this one," said Mays after the game.

This year Mays has worked 62 innings with 21 strikeouts en route to a 6-2 record and 3.77 ERA.
