Stevens Scores EBL Best Hurler Honors
Sunday, August 17th, 1930
Brodie Stevens of the Springfield Falcons will be adding a new centerpiece to his mantel after being tabbed for the Eastern Baseball League EBL Pitcher of the Year today.

The 16-year-old hurler started 13 games this season, tallying a 8-1 record. In 101.1 innings, Stevens gave up 59 hits, struck out 135, walked 26 and held opponents to a .169 average as he posted a fine 1.33 ERA.

He received 10 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Donne Grigg of the Toronto Jr. Blue Sox finished second in voting, while Randolph More of the Morgantown Mountaineers finished third.
