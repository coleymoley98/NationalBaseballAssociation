All game long, Edmonton's Jim Blackton was rattling the walls at Oracle Park and banging out base hits against the Vancouver Giants. When the dust finally settled on the diamond, he had a 5-hit day and the Eskimos had a 9-7 victory.

Speaking with reporters after his standout performance, the 23-year-old shortstop said, "Get 5 hits, win a ballgame, it's nice. I could get used to this."

Jim Blackton flied out in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, doubled in the 5th, singled in the 7th, hit a two-run single in the 8th and singled in the 9th.

In this current campaign Blackton is hitting at a .333 clip with 3 home runs and 18 RBIs.
