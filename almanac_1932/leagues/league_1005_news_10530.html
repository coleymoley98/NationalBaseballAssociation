Sérafin Goes on Rampage Against Iowa
Thursday, March 7th, 1929
Liam Sérafin exploded for a barrage of base hits today at Blue Sox Ballpark.

The Team left fielder had a field day in the 15-11 triumph over the Team Iowa, finishing 5-for-5.

"My hitting philosophy is simple," he said. "I focus on just seeing the ball and I try to get a good pitch."

Sérafin must have focused at least 5 times and apparently got some good pitches, too, leading to one of his best days ever with the bat. Liam Sérafin hit an RBI single in the 1st, hit a two-run double in the 2nd, walked in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, singled in the 7th and hit a two-run double in the 8th.
