Voltigeurs Come up Short of Playoffs
Wednesday, July 25th, 1928
The Drummondville Voltigeurs were mathematically eliminated from postseason contention today. Gordon Bélanger of the Voltigeurs said it's a tough way to finish out the season.

"To be eliminated from playoff contention in the last few games, it's not easy," he said.

This is the club's 4th playoff miss in a row. They last appeared in the postseason in 1924. In the last 10 seasons, the Voltigeurs have missed the playoffs 8 times. In total, the Voltigeurs have missed the playoffs 32 times in their 34-year history.
