Battalion Dominate, Sweep Division Finals
Saturday, July 30th, 1927
The North Bay Battalion made it look easy and are heading to the Adams Cup.

They have ousted the Buffalo Rapids in a sweep, the final win coming by a 3-1 score, clinching the 3-0 series win.

"Everything's clicking for us right now," said Loyd McGee, who was named MVP for the series.

McGee hit .500 with a .500 on-base percentage. He added 2 home runs, drove in 7 RBI and scored 2 runs.

The North Bay Battalion will play against the Gallant Black Sox in the next round. Gallant won their series against the Fort Wayne Pioneers 3 games to 0. The Adams Cup schedule will be announced after the Division Finals finishes.
