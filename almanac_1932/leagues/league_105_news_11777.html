Royales Prevail, as Division Series Goes to the Wire
Saturday, September 13th, 1930
With a narrow 3-2 series win, the Montreal Royales have eliminated the Detroit Lions and are Division Series winners.

"We tried not to look ahead to the League Chamionship Series, but we thought we'd be there for sure," said third baseman Clifton Blacksher, who was named series MVP.

Montreal won the final game by a score of 15-6, wrapping it up at Montreal Grounds.

Blacksher's big series saw him hit .609 with a .609 on-base percentage. He had 2 home runs, drove in 12 RBI and scored 7 runs.

The Montreal Royales will play against the Chicagou Frontenacs in the next round. Chicagou won their series against the Quebec Nordiques 3 games to 0. The League Chamionship Series schedule will be announced after the Division Series finishes.
