Chicagou Trades Freund for Quebec's Burch
Monday, July 14th, 1930
28-year-old RHP Logan Freund will now be playing for the Quebec Nordiques and 27-year-old 3B Stuart Burch will be suiting up for the Chicagou White Sox. The trade was announced today at a press conference by club officials.


Up to now Freund has recorded a record of 7-5. In 12 starts he has fanned 12 batters in 111.2 innings with a 4.11 ERA.

Burch on the season is hitting .304 with no home runs. His stats include 6 RBIs and 4 runs scored.
