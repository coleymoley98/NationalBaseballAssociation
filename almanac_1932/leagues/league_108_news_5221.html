Coyotes Blank Winnipeg, Auman Winner
Monday, July 7th, 1924
It was not a good day at the plate for the Winnipeg Thunder. They came up empty when it mattered against Saskatoon Coyotes right-hander Jeremiah Auman, who scattered 3 hits across 9 innings and held the Thunder scoreless.

"We had 3 hits so I know he wasn't completely untouchable, but he was close," Winnipeg manager Jayden Queen said later.

Auman chalked up 7 strikeouts and 1 walk in the 3-0 Coyotes win.
