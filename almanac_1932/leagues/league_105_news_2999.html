Hits Blitz for Braves Petrovsky
Friday, July 8th, 1932
Some days, a great game just isn't enough.

Gideon Petrovsky of the Toronto Braves found that out the hard way today. Petrovsky had a great day at the plate, pounding out 4 hits in 5 at-bats and hitting for the cycle, but watched the Philadelphia Mets take a 8-7 victory.

"It is a frustrating feeling to know you did so much and still lost," the 21-year-old third baseman said later. "But it is a feeling you have to get used to if you want to play baseball for very long. It just happens."

Petrovsky had 1 single, 1 double, 1 triple and 1 home run, as well as 2 runs batted in and 3 runs scored in the contest.

Gideon Petrovsky hit an RBI triple in the 2nd, doubled in the 5th, singled in the 7th, hit a solo-shot off John O'Geary in the 9th and flied out in the 10th.
