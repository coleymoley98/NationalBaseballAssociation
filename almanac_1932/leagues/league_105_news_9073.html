Celtics Come up Short of Playoffs
Thursday, September 8th, 1927
The Wolfeburg Celtics will play out the rest of the schedule, but they will do so without any chance of making the playoffs. The club was mathematically eliminated from playoff contention today.

The Celtics have missed the playoffs 19 times in their 23 years as a National Baseball Association franchise. This playoff miss is the club's 10th in a row, with their last postseason appearance coming in 1916.
