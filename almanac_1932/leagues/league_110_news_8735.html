CBL Awards Best Batter Prize to Doyle
Monday, July 18th, 1927
Lincoln Pioneers right fielder Jeremy Doyle had a great time at the plate last week, running up some sensational numbers that no one else could match. Few people were surprised today when the Central Baseball League named him Player of the Week.

Doyle banged out 13 hits in 27 at-bats on the way to a .481 batting average. He also racked up 6 home runs, 10 RBIs and 9 runs scored.

Presently Doyle is batting .384 with 9 home runs and 32 RBIs. He has played in 43 games and scored 38 times.
