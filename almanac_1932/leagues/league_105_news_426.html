Home Run a Record-Setter for Moore
Thursday, June 12th, 1930
Methanon Moore has just become the greatest home run hitter in history. In the culmination of a pursuit that has been watched around the sport for years, the Quebec superstar broke the old mark set by league legend Noah Auger. The new record stands at 88.

"I knew I hit it," Moore said. "I knew I got it. I was like, 'Whew, finally.' The closer you get to a record, the harder it gets."

Methanon Moore singled in the 1st, singled in the 4th, singled in the 6th and homered off Warwick Van Allen in the 7th.

Thus far this year Moore has hit .345 with 3 home runs and 28 RBIs.
