Toronto's Cooper Belli Suffers Setback
Wednesday, July 23rd, 1924
"It's worse than we thought." That was the news from the Blue Sox clubhouse today as it was learned that left fielder Cooper Belli will be out for an added 5 days as a result of a recent injury. His treatment for an oblique strain will have to be extended. Initially it didn't seem the injury was a major concern, but the Toronto left fielder told team trainers of some discomfort yesterday morning, and upon examination it was determined to be a more serious injury. "We'd rather take this thing slowly and make sure Cooper gets back onto the field healthy," said the team's head trainer.

Looking at his stats this year, Belli has compiled a .291 batting average, while hitting no home runs and driving in 4 runs. He has played in 15 games and scored 6 runs.
