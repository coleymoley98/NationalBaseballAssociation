White Sox Lose Nontawat Campbell
Friday, October 7th, 1932
Baseball can be a contact sport at times. It can exact a harsh toll on a player's body. In a recent game against Chicagou Frontenacs, White Sox center fielder Nontawat Campbell was injured throwing the ball and is expected to miss 6 weeks with a strained hamstring. The team did not announce any roster moves today related to this injury. The Chicagou skipper told BNN that personnel decisions would be announced as early as tomorrow.

This season in 101 games, Campbell has hit at a .262 clip with 95 hits, 2 home runs, 48 RBIs and 48 runs scored.
