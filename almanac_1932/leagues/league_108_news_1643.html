Eston Stops Winnipeg
Friday, July 3rd, 1931
Saskatoon Coyotes hurler Greg Eston was at his best as he whitewashed the Winnipeg Thunder 8-0 today.

"He just kept the ball down, did a good job, made his pitches," said Winnipeg manager Kendrick Markowski. "We didn't do a good job making adjustments, so we helped him out a little bit."

Eston fired a 4-hitter and totaled 4 strikeouts and no walks in the shutout performance.
