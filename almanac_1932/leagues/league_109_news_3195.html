EL Honors Beshel, #1 Batter in July
Monday, August 1st, 1932
Even though he is a newcomer to Eastern League, Luca Beshel put up some good numbers in July and earned the EL Batter of the Month award.

He was one of Kingston's offensive leaders with a .462 average in 27 games. He also racked up 1 home run, 14 RBIs, and 29 runs scored.

Beshel's current batting average stands at .446 and he has chalked up 2 home runs and 19 RBIs.
