Worns Tosses 4-0 Shutout Against Canton
Tuesday, July 3rd, 1923
Miles Worns of the Springfield Falcons was lights-out in a 4-hit whitewash of the Canton Legends.

"I had my good stuff and it went where I wanted it to go," he told BNN reporters after the contest.

Worns registered 6 strikeouts and 1 walk in shutting out Canton, 4-0.

This season Worns has 2 wins and 2 losses and a 2.98 ERA.
