Garnier Touches All the Bases, Hits for Cycle
Tuesday, July 17th, 1928
Jordane Garnier put on a show at Edmonton Grounds today, hitting for the cycle and driving the Edmonton Eskimos to victory, 16-3.

"To be honest, I was just going out there to have some fun," the third baseman said after his team's win over the Saskatoon Coyotes. "I wasn't trying for trying for the cycle or anything, it simply happened."

Garnier went 4-for-4 in the game with 5 RBI and 3 runs scored.

Jordane Garnier hit an RBI triple in the 1st, hit an RBI double in the 3rd, hit a sac fly in the 4th, hit an RBI single in the 6th and hit a solo-shot off Somkiat Westbrooks in the 7th.

In the 1928 season Garnier is hitting .276 with 3 home runs.
