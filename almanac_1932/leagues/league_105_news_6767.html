NBA News: Playoffs begin
Monday, September 14th, 1925
The National Baseball Association playoffs begin today with the League Championship Series. For one lucky team, destiny is not far away. Soon one of the playoff teams will win the Riel Cup and be honored as the best of the best. In due time, they can have their parade, hoist up that championship banner, and celebrate. It will be the crowning glory of the 1925 season. Here are the clubs that will be in action today:
Philadelphia Liberty versus Boston Redcoats and Juneau Brasseurs versus Minnesota Saints.
