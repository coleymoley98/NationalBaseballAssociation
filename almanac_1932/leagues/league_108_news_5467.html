Chen Goes 5-for-5
Friday, August 8th, 1924
There was no storybook ending. Loik Chen of the Saskatoon Coyotes socked it to the visiting Portland Thorns with a terrific 5-hit game today, but his team didn't get the win. The Coyotes fell, 7-6, at Coyotes Ballpark.

"I don't want to sound ungrateful, but something is missing here," Chen complained following the loss. "When you have a great game you should win the game, too."

He definitely had an unforgettable day in the batter's box, ripping out 5 hits in 5 at-bats. Saskatoon manager James Sangapong sympathized with his catcher, telling BNN reporters after the game that "Loik played a whale of a game, but sometimes individual brilliance is not enough."

Loik Chen singled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, hit an RBI single in the 6th and singled in the 8th.

In 55 games this season Chen is batting .356 with 72 hits and 2 home runs. He has scored 32 runs and driven in 41.
