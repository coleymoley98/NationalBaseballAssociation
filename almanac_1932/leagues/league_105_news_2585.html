Joseph Esteve Tames Boston 8-3 for #100
Friday, May 13th, 1932
Joseph Esteve entered the record books today as he collected win number 100 at Fenway Park 1912.

"Wow! This is a select group of pitchers. I am honored and thrilled to be in it," Esteve said after the game. "I never dreamed of winning this many games."

In his historic outing he went 9 innings and gave up 3 runs on 8 hits. Esteve also had 2 strikeouts and 1 walk as his Philadelphia Liberty topped the Boston Redcoats 8-3.

Esteve has collected 100 wins and 92 losses over his career with a 4.01 ERA. The 37-year-old hurler also has struck out 517 batters in 1773.2 innings and allowed 2011 hits.
