Red-Hot Guilford Scorches Thorns
Friday, June 24th, 1927
It's not something you read about in the sports pages every day -- a 5-for-5 day. For many, it might come around only once in a career -- and for Patrick Guilford, it came around today.

The 27-year-old center fielder scattered hits all over Thorns Ballpark to pace the Omaha Falcons to a 11-6 victory over the Portland Thorns.

Portland skipper Romie Huynh told reporters in the press room that "Guilford deserves a hat-tip."

"He hit some good pitches against us," he said. "In fact, according to our scouting reports, Guilford hit several that he is not supposed to be able to hit."

Patrick Guilford singled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, hit an RBI single in the 6th and hit an RBI double in the 8th.

Guilford is batting .354 with 0 home runs and 10 RBIs this season.
