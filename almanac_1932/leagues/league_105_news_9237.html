St. Louis-Cincinnati Trade: Siegel for Alvarez
Friday, November 25th, 1927
It's all done except the paperwork. The St. Louis Cardinaux and the Cincinnati Reds have agreed to a trade that will send 30-year-old RHP Lindsey Siegel to Cincinnati, while 28-year-old SS Teeratat Alvarez will be shipped to St. Louis. A top front office executive with the Cardinaux described the trade talks as "quick and easy." "We're glad to get this deal done. We think it's something that helps our ball club and it didn't take too long at all to sort this one out," he said.


Siegel has a lifetime record of 77 wins and 71 losses and a 3.73 ERA.

In his 415-game career, Alvarez has hit at a .330 clip and collected 523 hits with 81 doubles, 27 triples, 5 homers, and 136 RBIs.
