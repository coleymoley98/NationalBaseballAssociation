Title Captured - Won by St. Louis
Thursday, July 14th, 1932
The St. Louis Jr. Cardinaux may not have ever won a Dumont Cup, but you wouldn't have guessed it from looking inside their clubhouse today.

The club celebrated its championship like they've been hosting such festivities for a long time.

"Our team may not have ever won a championship, but we know how to party," said starting pitcher Naret Perron. "This is nothing compared to what you'll see if we can win the Dumont Cup."

The club's 31-9 record led to the franchise's 2nd division crown.
