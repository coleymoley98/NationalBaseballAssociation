Ridin' High: Gross Hits for Cycle
Saturday, July 12th, 1924
Thomas Gross truly did it all at West Side Grounds today.

The Quebec Nordiques shortstop hit for the cycle -- collecting 1 single, 1 double, 1 triple and 1 home run -- as he paced his club to victory, 10-0 over the Chicagou Frontenacs.

"You know that sweet sound you hear when you hit the ball just right? I heard that every time today," Gross said later. "It was a heck of a feeling."

It was a game good enough for him to make a little history. He bombed Chicagou pitching for 4 hits in 5 at-bats and his name will now go into the record books. Gross also racked up 4 RBI and 2 runs scored in the game.

Thomas Gross hit an RBI triple in the 2nd, hit a two-run home run off Joseph Theriot in the 4th, hit an RBI single in the 5th, flied out in the 7th and doubled in the 9th.

Currently Gross is batting .254 with 47 hits and 2 home runs. He has knocked in 27 runs and scored 22 times.
