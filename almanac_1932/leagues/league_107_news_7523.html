Apple Sox Not Competitive in 7-0 Loss to Spokane
Tuesday, July 13th, 1926
It wasn't easy but Ambroise Thériault made it look that way.

Thériault silenced the Kelowna Apple Sox, 7-0, in a Spokane Eagles triumph at Eskimos Ballpark today. Despite the appearance of dominance, Thériault said later that he felt like he was struggling all game. He gave up 1 walk and 4 hits.

"It didn't feel quite right today, but I made the decision to bear down and work through it," he said. "I made a few mistakes but it goes to show what you can do with grit and not much else."

This year Thériault has a 3-4 record with a 5.69 ERA in 9 starts.
