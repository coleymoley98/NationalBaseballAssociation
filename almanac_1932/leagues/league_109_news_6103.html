Paquette Ups Hit Streak to 20 Consecutive Games
Monday, June 1st, 1925
The London Indians may have proven to be mere mortals today as they lost 12-3 to the Hamilton Blue Sox, but Sripatana Paquette continued to hit like a god.

The second baseman collected 2 hits in 3 at-bats in the loss and extended his hitting streak to 20 games.

"Paquette is playing great and is on fire right now," Indians manager Bunnawat Gresh said. "Hopefully his play can spark the rest of our team and help us pick up some wins."

Paquette was apparently not happy with the game and when pressed by reporters in the locker room only said "nothing else matters but the loss."
