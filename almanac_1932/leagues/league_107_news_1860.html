Townes Tosses 5-0 Shutout
Saturday, July 25th, 1931
Goose eggs were the order of the day for the Kelowna Apple Sox as starter Manitho Townes shut out the Kelowna Apple Sox 5-0 at Broncos Ballpark. The Boise pitcher struck out 9 and walked 2 while allowing 5 hits.

"Townes was good but he wasn't flawless," Kelowna manager Kittithat Frye said later. "We had a few opportunities to make something happen and we didn't do it. Not to take anything away from Boise, but this is on us."

This season Townes has compiled a 10-2 mark with a 3.10 ERA in 14 starts.
