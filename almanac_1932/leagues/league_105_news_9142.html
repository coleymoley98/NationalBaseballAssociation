Woodmore Breezes to Shutout Victory
Saturday, October 1st, 1927
Eric Woodmore of the Chicagou Frontenacs was on fire against the Chicagou White Sox at Comiskey Park 1910 today. He shut them out 5-0 on 5 hits, collecting no strikeouts and allowing no walks.

"The first two pitches I'm looking for an out, a ground ball or a fly ball," Woodmore said later. "I'm trying to get quick outs. I've never been a big believer in trying to strike a guy out from the very first pitch. It's too easy to end up behind in the count doing that."

In 24 starts this season Woodmore has gathered 17 wins while suffering 7 losses. He has pitched 211.2 innings, allowed 194 hits, struck out 79 and has a 2.85 ERA.
