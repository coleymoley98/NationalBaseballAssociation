Juneau Celebrates Wild Card Win
Tuesday, September 15th, 1931
The Juneau Brasseurs are heading to the playoffs.

While they couldn't win the National League West, they will nonetheless get the opportunity to play for the franchise's 3rd Riel Cup.

"Don't overlook us as a wild card team, that's all I'll say," said starting pitcher Arthur Jenkins.

That confidence and swagger has been characteristic of the Brasseurs this season, with many feeling they are better than their 59-53 record indicates.
