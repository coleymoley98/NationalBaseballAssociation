Chicagou Trades Partin for Boston's Doheny
Thursday, November 1st, 1923
Today at a press conference the Chicagou White Sox announced a trade has been completed with the Boston Redcoats. The White Sox have shipped 25-year-old RF Ali Partin and 20-year-old minor league CF Casim Abrams to the Redcoats for 26-year-old SS Andy Doheny.


In 172 career games, Partin has put up a .331 average, collected 22 doubles, 5 triples, and 4 home runs, driven in 66 runs, and scored 80 times.

Lifetime Doheny is batting .296 with 50 doubles, 26 triples, 5 home runs and 106 RBIs in 295 games.
