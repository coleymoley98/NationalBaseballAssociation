Jenkins Pitches New England to Shutout Victory
Sunday, March 27th, 1932
Arthur Jenkins bent a little, but he didn't break against the Team Dakota. The Team New England pitcher hurled a 10-hit 4-0 shutout in a fine performance, surrendering 1 walk and tallying 6 strikeouts.

"Arthur is good at getting out of jams," New England manager Sheehan Thompson told reporters after the game. "He's almost more effective when he gets guys on base or gets behind in the count, because he uses your aggressiveness against you."

This year Jenkins has a 2-2 record and a 5.16 ERA.
