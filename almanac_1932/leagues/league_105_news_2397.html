Veteran SS Comtois Stays in Detroit
Thursday, February 18th, 1932
Fans hailed the signing of Joshua Comtois today as the Detroit Lions shortstop came to terms with the club.

Set to make $86,600 over the upcoming 6 years, Comtois signed primarily out of loyalty to the organization, thrilling the most diehard supporters who think there isn't much loyalty in a market driven by free agency.

In his career Comtois has hit .291 with 1014 hits, 42 home runs, 485 RBIs and scored 469 runs.
