Jones at His Best, Blanks Edmonton
Monday, August 3rd, 1931
Winnipeg Thunder starter Samuel Jones really did a number on the Edmonton Eskimos today, shutting them out 8-0 on 9 hits in a complete game victory. He issued 2 walks and had 3 strikeouts.

"We didn't have good at-bats," Edmonton manager Nattaporn Lamontagne. "I don't make excuses, but when we were playing the game, our energy didn't show. That comes from either extremely good pitching or extremely lousy hitting and seeing as our team has a bunch of good hitters, I have to tip my cap to Jones."

This season Jones has fanned 32 in 98 innings and collected a 5-6 record with a 4.41 ERA.
