Federico Sánchez Selected August's Best Batter
Saturday, September 1st, 1923
It's perhaps an award he'll win many more times through his career, as at age 22 Federico Sánchez has a lot of baseball ahead of him.

But for now the young Quebec Nordiques star can enjoy the numbers he put up in August, which have garnered him the nod as the National League Batter of the Month.

His breakout month featured a .372 average with 2 home runs, 12 RBIs and 24 runs scored.

Looking at his season stats, Sánchez has compiled a .331 batting average and a .364 on-base percentage, while hitting 4 home runs and driving in 45 runs.
