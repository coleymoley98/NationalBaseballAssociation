Andy O'Neill Reaches 100 Wins
Monday, May 7th, 1928
Philadelphia ace Andy O'Neill solidified his place in league history with his 100th career victory today at Lincoln Park. O'Neill and the Liberty bested Ottawa 3-1. He checked the Indians on 9 hits in 9 innings and finished the day with 4 strikeouts and 1 walk.

"I've worked hard for this day," O'Neill said. "Sometimes I didn't think it would happen. Most of the time it seemed so far off. Now it's here. It's like a dream!"

In his career O'Neill has fanned 527 batters and walked 395 in 1728.1 innings of work. His lifetime won-lost record is 100 victories and 91 defeats with a 4.12 ERA and opposing hitters have compiled a .295 against him.
