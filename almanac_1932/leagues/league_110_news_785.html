Jr. Saints Celebrate Wild Card Win
Tuesday, July 22nd, 1930
As the clock struck midnight in the Minneapolis Jr. Saints clubhouse, the celebration showed no signs of slowing down. The Jr. Saints were certainly ready to celebrate their Central Baseball League wild card berth.

"It's time to let off a little steam after a grind of a season," said starting pitcher Wittaya Brookover. "We may act a bit like teenagers tonight celebrating, but we'll be back to being big leaguers tomorrow."

With a 31-17 record, Minneapolis will be seeking its 5th Central Baseball League championship.
