Smashing Success: O'Dell Wallops 3 HR
Tuesday, July 7th, 1925
Whatever Bobby O'Dell ate for breakfast before his game against the Spokane Eagles today, it gave him some impressive power.

The Victoria left fielder smacked three balls up and out of the park at Admirals Ballpark. His home run barrage led the Admirals to take the 7-5 victory over the Eagles.

"When they throw it in your wheelhouse, you've got a shot at a homer," he told BNN reporters after the game. "I got three shots today and nailed them all."

On his 3-homer day, he went 3-for-5, scored 3 times, drove in 6 and put on a display that history will remember.

Bobby O'Dell hit a solo-shot off Ben Hébert in the 1st, grounded out in the 3rd, struck out in the 5th, hit a three-run home run off Mario Spivey in the 7th and hit a two-run home run off Ted McGuyveur in the 9th.

Currently O'Dell has 6 home runs and a .317 batting average.
