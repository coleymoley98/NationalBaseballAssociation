Young Star Helps Propel Canadian League to Win in Midseason Classic
Friday, July 17th, 1931
The best players the NBA has to offer took to SunTrust Park tonight for an all-star showcase.

When the dust settled, the Canadian League bested the National League by a score of 5-1.

Helping the Canadian League to victory was Harry Wilder, who said it's a memory he'll cherish.

"You can tell your kids and your grandkids some day that you were the MVP of the all-star game, that's a cool moment," he said.

Wilder has played in 410 games and posted a lifetime .320 average. He has 505 hits, 100 doubles, 31 triples and 42 homers. In addition he has driven in 282 runs and scored 326 times.
