Voltigeurs Win Tight Saint Lawrence Race
Sunday, July 22nd, 1923
The pennant has been won, the Drummondville Voltigeurs are champions of the Saint Lawrence.

Starting Pitcher Donte Green said his team is a "gritty and determined" club that were poised to win the division from day one.

"We knew the day the season opened that we'd be champions," he said. "It was just a matter of going out there and doing it."

At the helm, team manager Tai Grigsby said his club is certainly not short on confidence.

"There's a lot of belief in the dugout and in the clubhouse," he said.

Drummondville's record currently sits at 30-18 and it's the 3rd division win for the franchise.
