Cardinaux Suffer 9-0 Drubbing from Saints
Saturday, August 11th, 1928
Pierce Ramirez of the Minnesota Saints was cool and collected on the mound, leading his club to a shutout victory over the St. Louis Cardinaux, 9-0.

"He was mixing his pitches real well and keeping his composure on the mound," Minnesota manager A.J. Provost said later. "When Pierce does that, good things happen."

The right-hander had 4 strikeouts, 1 walk and allowed 2 hits in the win.

In 16 starts this year Ramirez has fashioned a 7-8 record and a 4.00 ERA.
