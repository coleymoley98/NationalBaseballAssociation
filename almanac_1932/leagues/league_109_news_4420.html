Duran on Fire: Burns Hamilton with 3 HR
Sunday, August 26th, 1923
Providence Redcoats right fielder Ollie Duran had a magnificent game against the Hamilton Blue Sox today, rocketing three balls out of Providence Grounds to help the Redcoats take the 18-2 victory.

"I got some good pitches to hit and I creamed them," he said after the game. "The first was a big, fat fastball and the last two were hanging curves. All three were mistakes."

For the game, Duran went 4-for-5 with 8 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

"There's a fine line between hitting home runs and line drives," Providence manager Grayson St-Louis said later. "Seems like Ollie found his home run stroke today."
