Redcoats Advance to Riel Cup
Wednesday, September 23rd, 1925
The Boston Redcoats are one step closer to winning a Riel Cup, and the season came to a close for the Philadelphia Liberty.

Boston won the League Championship Series 4-2, with a 9-8 victory in the deciding game at Fenway Park 1912 today.

Boston shortstop Carmen Morales was named the MVP of the series, after hitting .517 with a .500 on-base percentage. He had 0 homers, drove in 4 RBI and scored 6 runs.

"I just hope I can keep it up in the Riel Cup," he said.

The Boston Redcoats will play against the Minnesota Saints in the next round. Minnesota won their series against the Juneau Brasseurs 4 games to 2. The Riel Cup schedule will be announced after the League Championship Series finishes.
