League Chamionship Series Goes the Distance, Wolfeburg Prevails
Monday, September 22nd, 1930
The Wolfeburg Celtics are off to the Riel Cup after an exciting League Chamionship Series triumph.

Wolfeburg downed the Boston Redcoats, but needed the entire series to do so, with a 6-4 win tonight at Fenway Park 1912 wrapping up the series 4-3.

"It's every guy in this room that made this happen. Without us all pulling in the same direction, we don't win this series," said Élie Lafon, who earned MVP honors for a standout offensive series.

Lafon put up a .514 with a .528 on-base percentage in the series. He also had 1 home runs, drove in 7 runs and scored 3 runs himself.

The Wolfeburg Celtics don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Montreal Royales - Chicagou Frontenacs series. That series is tied at 3 games apiece. The Riel Cup schedule will be announced after the League Chamionship Series finishes.
