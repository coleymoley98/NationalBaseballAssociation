Pioneers, Jones Buzz Jr. Falcons, 1-0
Tuesday, June 19th, 1923
The Lincoln Pioneers shut out the Omaha Jr. Falcons 1-0 today behind the strong pitching of Ernard Jones. Jones surrendered only 3 hits in recording the complete-game victory, and struck out 4.

Following the last out, Jones appeared to celebrate a little too boisterously for Omaha manager Jesse O'Byrne, who could be seen scolding the famously outspoken right-hander as the clubs left the field. Jones's own manager then intervened with some words for his counterpart.

The Jr. Falcons skipper would not comment on the exchange, but at the Pioneers press conference, Jones confirmed that it was regarding the celebration.

"He basically told me I was being a lousy winner," Jones said.

"And I told him he was being a lousy loser," Lincoln manager Louie Rodham added.
