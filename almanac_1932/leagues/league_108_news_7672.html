Sulley Earns WL's Best Pitcher Honor
Sunday, August 1st, 1926
The Western League scribes have selected July's Pitcher of the Month and have awarded the honor to Kyle Sulley of Saskatoon. He was outstanding on the hill last month and has the numbers to prove it -- a 5-1 record.

The Coyotes star performer made 6 starts with a 2.94 ERA and surrendered 36 hits in 52 innings. He struck out 36 and walked 27.

In 11 starts this season Sulley has notched a 9-2 record and a 3.49 ERA.
