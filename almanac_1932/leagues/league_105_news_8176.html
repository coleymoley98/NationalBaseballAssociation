St. Louis Trades Silawarn for Minnesota's Blakey
Wednesday, March 2nd, 1927
BNN is reporting a rumored trade between the St. Louis Cardinaux and Minnesota Saints has been finalized. The deal, which is expected to be announced sometime during the next few hours, will reportedly send 25-year-old CF Phakhawat Silawarn, 18-year-old minor league LF Jun Horn and 23-year-old minor league LF Jean-Christophe Labelle to Minnesota in return for 26-year-old LHP Pairote Blakey. The clubs were noncommittal on the trade, but did not deny it.


Lifetime Silawarn is batting .294 with 72 doubles, 39 triples, 20 home runs and 187 RBIs in 472 games.

So far in his career Blakey has 24 wins and 21 losses and a 3.45 ERA.
