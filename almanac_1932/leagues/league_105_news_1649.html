Philadelphia Trades Le Roy for Chicagou's Downing
Saturday, July 4th, 1931
It likely won't turn either team into an instant contender or provide a huge franchise shakeup, but the Philadelphia Mets and Chicagou Frontenacs finalized a trade today. Philadelphia will send 28-year-old 2B Marc Le Roy to the Frontenacs, while the Mets will receive 20-year-old minor league 3B Foster Downing, 20-year-old minor league LHP Chanathip McGreal, 22-year-old minor league RHP Colt Munro and 23-year-old minor league RHP John O'Geary.

"We think this is a deal that will help out both teams and we're glad to get it done," Mets general manager Ferdinand Dupont said.

The trade is expected to be officially completed later today with the players joining their new ballclubs by the end of the day.


For the year Le Roy is batting .377 with 1 home run.
