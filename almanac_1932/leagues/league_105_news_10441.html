1929 National Baseball Association Hall of Fame Inductees to be announced next week
Wednesday, December 26th, 1928
With the 1929 National Baseball Association Hall of Fame announcement next week, fans are eagerly looking forward to seeing how many electors will vote for Naithim Boonsom.

With an impressive 14-year career, Montreal Royales fans are busy making the trek to hear his name announced as a first-ballot Hall of Famer.

While not the only name on the ballot, Boonsom is widely expected to be the top name when results are announced.

Eligible voters can cast their ballots until the results are announced on January 2nd, 1929.

In his career Boonsom has crafted a 164-119 record with a 2.44 ERA in 2780.2 innings. Opposing clubs are batting .246 against him and he has rung up 1231 strikeouts.
