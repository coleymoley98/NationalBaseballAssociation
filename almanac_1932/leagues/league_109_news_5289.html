Girard Hits for Cycle Against Albany
Thursday, July 17th, 1924
London Indians right fielder Remi Girard had one of the best days of his career today when he hit for the cycle and paved the way for the Indians 10-1 win over the Albany Rebels at Progressive Field.

"Everything was just clicking today," he said.

Girard shelled the Rebels for 4 hits in 5 at-bats, collected 4 RBI and scored 2 runs.

Remi Girard hit a two-run home run off Wit Ormond in the 1st, hit an RBI triple in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, hit an RBI double in the 7th and struck out in the 8th.

So far this season Girard has gotten 35 hits in 149 at-bats for a .235 batting average with 6 home runs. His stats include 24 RBIs and 20 runs scored. He has played in 41 games.
