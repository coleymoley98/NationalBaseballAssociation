O'Gorman Gets July's Top Pitcher Trophy
Wednesday, August 1st, 1928
Veteran Ray O'Gorman put together quite a successful stretch in July for the Montreal Royales, earning himself the National League Pitcher of the Month award.

O'Gorman racked up a 5-0 record in 5 starts, posting a 2.96 ERA over 45.2 innings and striking out 19.

This year O'Gorman has posted a record of 10 wins and 4 losses with a 2.87 ERA. He has appeared in 16 games and struck out 53 in 138 innings.
