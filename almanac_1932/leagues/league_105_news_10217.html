David Wintringham is August's #1 Hitter in CL
Saturday, September 1st, 1928
It's perhaps an award he'll win many more times through his career, as at age 22 David Wintringham has a lot of baseball ahead of him.

But for now the young Ottawa Indians star can enjoy the numbers he put up in August, which have garnered him the nod as the Canadian League Batter of the Month.

His breakout month featured a .360 average with 9 home runs, 26 RBIs and 23 runs scored.

So far this season Wintringham has hit .326 with 18 home runs, 72 RBIs and scored 65 times.
