Toronto Crowned Kings of the Canadian League West
Thursday, September 10th, 1931
Toronto Blue Sox starting pitcher Kyle Gandy said celebrating a division championship never gets old.

"If this is what the outcome of every regular season was, I'd be a happy guy," he said. "It takes a lot of talent and determination to win a division like the Canadian League West."

The club's 62-45 record has allowed it to officially seal up the division title, for the 6th time in franchise history.

Manager Jabari Perrault said it would be a "pure joy and pleasure" to be able to bring the 4th Riel Cup title to Toronto.
