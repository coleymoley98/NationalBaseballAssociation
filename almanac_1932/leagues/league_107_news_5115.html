Eichhorn Stands Out in Kelowna Shutout
Tuesday, June 24th, 1924
Fans at Ballpark of Kelowna witnessed the Kelowna Apple Sox blank the Boise Broncos, 5-0 behind a superb effort by starter Kane Eichhorn. The Kelowna left-hander allowed 4 hits in recording the shutout.

"Kane was good today but I don't want to focus too much on a single game," Kelowna manager Supravee Dube said later. "If you're capable of playing at this level, you're capable of having a good game. The real question is if you can learn from that game and repeat it."

Eichhorn has a 2-1 season record so far. In 5 starts, he has fanned 43 batters in 39 innings with a 1.15 ERA.
