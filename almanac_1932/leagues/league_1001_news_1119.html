Hit Parade: Lavallée Pops 5 Hits
Thursday, March 12th, 1931
It was high-fives all around for Danusorn Lavallée at Ciudad Mexico Stadium today.

He got them from his teammates and he got them at the plate during an outstanding hitting performance against the Team Dakota. Lavallée smashed 5 hits in 5 at-bats on his monster day, leading his club to a 6-5 win.

"Danusorn was hitting everything," said Ontario manager Anusorn Bostock. "It really was a special game for him."

In the opposing dugout, Dakota manager Tex Wales told BNN beat writers that Lavallée "ambushed" the club.

"Obviously we didn't have much success against him, but we'll have another chance," he said. "Hopefully, Lavallée oversleeps next time."

Danusorn Lavallée doubled in the 2nd, hit an RBI single in the 4th, singled in the 6th, doubled in the 8th and singled in the 9th.
