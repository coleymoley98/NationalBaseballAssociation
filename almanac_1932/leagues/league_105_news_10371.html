St. Louis-Philadelphia Trade: Jobin for Haro
Saturday, November 3rd, 1928
Trade winds have been blowing through St. Louis. This time, a deal has materialized between the Cardinaux and Liberty. According to sources inside the organization, St. Louis will ship 29-year-old RHP Éric Jobin and 21-year-old minor league SS Pairat Charoenpura to Philadelphia for 25-year-old C Shane Haro and 23-year-old RHP Jacob McGowan.


Thus far in his career, Jobin has worked 1303.1 innings with 648 strikeouts en route to a 71-66 record. The 29-year-old also has a 3.46 ERA.
