Lunn Records Shutout Against Brasseurs
Wednesday, May 16th, 1928
The Montreal Royales did little to support starter Raj Lunn against the Juneau Brasseurs, but it turned out he didn't need the help.

The right-hander spun a complete game shutout against the Brasseurs, striking out 1 and surrendering 4 hits. The performance from Lunn allowed Montreal to escape with the 3-0 win despite an anemic offense.

"Nobody came to me and said 'Raj, we're gonna need you to win this one for us today,' but I could feel it," Lunn told BNN reporters following the win. "I'm the guy that will step up for you when you need it."
