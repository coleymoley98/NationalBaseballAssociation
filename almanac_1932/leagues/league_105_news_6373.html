CL Star of the Week Chosen
Monday, July 13th, 1925
So far in his young career, Wayne Womack has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Canadian League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 22-year-old Baltimore Robins third baseman hit at a torrid .500 clip as he collected 10 hits in 20 at-bats, 3 home runs, 7 RBIs and 4 runs scored.

This season Womack is batting .311 with 38 RBIs, 30 runs scored and 5 home runs.
