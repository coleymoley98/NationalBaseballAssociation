Hits Galore: McGee Drills 5 Hits
Saturday, July 23rd, 1927
Getting 5 hits in a game has always been memorable feat in baseball. In the North Bay's 6-2 win over Toronto today, left fielder Loyd McGee was red-hot, scorching them for hit after hit at Juniors Ballpark.

"To hit the ball consistently well, you have to relax," McGee said afterward. "I was relaxed for the first four hits, but I have to admit, I got a little tight when I realized I had a chance for five. On the last at-bat, I was ready to swing at anything close."

Loyd McGee doubled in the 1st, doubled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, hit a two-run home run off Will Simard in the 7th and singled in the 9th.

McGee is currently hitting at a .275 pace with 5 home runs and 27 RBIs.
