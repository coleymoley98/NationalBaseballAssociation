Michigan Ace Richardson Cops Best Pitcher
Wednesday, April 9th, 1930
With speculation about his retirement gaining steam last offseason, Michigan ace Kitsada Richardson had something to prove. He proved it in award-winning style as he accepted the Canadian Senior Baseball Tournament Best Pitcher today.

The veteran Team hurler posted a record of 4 wins and 3 losses this year with a 1.80 ERA. In 7 starts he struck out 16 and walked 5 in 60 innings.

He received 10 first place votes out of a possible 18. Louka Delâge of the Team Columbia finished second in voting with 6 first place votes, while Elijah Van Buren of the Team Michigan finished third with 1 first place vote.
