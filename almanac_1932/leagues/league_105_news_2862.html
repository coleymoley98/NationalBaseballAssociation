Morikawa Goes on Rampage Against Rebels
Friday, June 24th, 1932
Players get multi-hit games all the time, but 6-hit games are something else entirely. Takeaki Morikawa walloped 6 hits in 6 at-bats for the Boston Redcoats in a career day at Fenway Park 1912 today, but it was the New York Rebels who won the ballgame, 10-9.

"My swing was really, really there today," Morikawa said afterward. "I made some good, solid contact and when that happens, good things happen. And hey, 6-for-6 and losing is better than 0-for-6 and losing, right?"

Takeaki Morikawa singled in the 1st, hit an RBI triple in the 2nd, doubled in the 4th, singled in the 5th, hit a two-run single in the 7th and singled in the 9th.

Thus far in 1932 Morikawa is hitting .264 with no home runs, 15 RBIs, 14 runs scored and has compiled a .313 OBP.
