Broncos Not Competitive in 7-0 Loss to Ranier
Wednesday, July 11th, 1923
It was a dismal game for the Boise Broncos with Kittichai Kejmanee on the hill. The Ranier Seahawks starter was in top form and made it a long day for Boise with a 7-0 shutout. He surrendered a total of 4 hits and piled up 12 strikeouts to pick up the win in a rout.

"No offense to the Broncos but that was a cakewalk today," Kejmanee said to BNN reporters following the win.

So far in 12 appearances this season, Kejmanee has recorded 7 wins and sustained 4 losses. He has pitched 90 innings, yielded 64 hits, fanned 107 and has a 2.70 ERA.
