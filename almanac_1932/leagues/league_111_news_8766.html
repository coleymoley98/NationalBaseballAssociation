Hamilton Loses Thereson for Season
Wednesday, July 20th, 1927
In a news release, the Hamilton Jr. Steelers announced that reliever Khairul Thereson would miss the rest of the season due to a torn flexor tendon in his elbow. Thereson sustained the injury on 07/17/1927. He "will be sorely missed," said his manager.

To date in the season, Thereson is 1-1 with 15 relief appearances and 2 saves. He has held opposing batters to a .271 average, pitched 24.2 innings, struck out 31 and walked 15 while recording a 6.93 ERA.
