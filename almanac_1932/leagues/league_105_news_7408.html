Quebec Starter Named NL's Top Pitcher
Thursday, July 1st, 1926
An outstanding 5-0 record in June earned Daniel Ibarra the Pitcher of the Month Award in the National League. When his name was penciled in to start on the Quebec lineup card, it meant trouble for opposing teams.

In 49 innings of work, Ibarra limited opposing batters to 47 base hits and a .251 batting average. He notched 17 strikeouts, issued 11 walks and had a 2.76 ERA.

This year Ibarra has fashioned a 8-1 mark, 3.21 ERA and pitched 92.2 innings. Opposing clubs are batting .257 against him and he has logged 40 strikeouts.
