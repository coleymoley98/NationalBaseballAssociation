WBL WBL Pitcher of the Year Announced
Monday, August 15th, 1932
Eagles ace Suksayam Hardin is the latest Western Baseball League WBL Pitcher of the Year trophy winner. The Spokane star shined on the diamond time and again this year and finished with an outstanding 7-2 record.

In 14 starts he tossed 95 innings, posted 119 strikeouts and sported a 1.42 ERA. Opposing batters hit .218 when Hardin was pitching.

He received 8 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Krit Kupalang of the Everett Silvertips finished second in voting, while Gustav Lafrenière of the Abbotsford Alpines finished third.
