Betts Belts 5 Hits
Thursday, June 23rd, 1927
A jubilant Basil Betts joined the postgame press conference at Oracle Park today to offer some unusual words of encouragement -- for the Omaha Falcons pitching staff he had just vanquished.

"It may not have looked like it," said the Vancouver Giants right fielder, "but I saw some awful good pitches today. If luck hadn't been on my side it might have turned out a lot different."

That would not have been the conclusion of anyone who saw his dominant 5-for-6 hitting performance, which led the Giants to a 14-11 win.

"He made it look easy," said Kiatisuk Sanders, manager of the Falcons. "We never figured out what to do with him. Obviously."

Basil Betts singled in the 1st, grounded out in the 3rd, singled in the 4th, hit a solo-shot off A.J. Robertson in the 6th, doubled in the 8th and singled in the 8th.
