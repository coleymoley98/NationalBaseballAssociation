Gibbs Takes Best Bat Trophy in July
Sunday, August 1st, 1926
Clarence Gibbs may be much younger than other hitters in the Eastern League, but he performed like a seasoned veteran in July to win the Batter of the Month award.

The 20-year-old Trois-Rivieres second baseman hit .274 with 9 home runs, 20 RBIs and 31 runs scored.

Gibbs is hitting .216 this season with 12 home runs, 30 RBIs and 48 runs scored. He has 41 hits in 190 at-bats.
