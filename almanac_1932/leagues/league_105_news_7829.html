Masson Fashions Shutout Against Detroit
Saturday, August 28th, 1926
In an outstanding mound performance, Montreal Royales starter Steven Masson fired a complete-game shutout against the Detroit Lions at Bennett Park today.

Masson was the key to the Royales's 2-0 win. The 22-year-old right-hander was in high spirits on the mount for much of the game, his mood largely undented by the 3 hits and 1 walk he allowed.

"3 hits?" he said with a laugh following the game. "Listen, I'd be happy with a 30-hitter as long as it was a shutout."
