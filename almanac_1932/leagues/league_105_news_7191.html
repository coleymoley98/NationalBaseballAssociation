Frontenacs, Drouin Stonewall Quebec, 4-0
Thursday, June 3rd, 1926
Dominic Drouin of the Chicagou Frontenacs humiliated the Quebec Nordiques at West Side Grounds today, spinning a complete game shutout and holding the visitors to 11 hits.

"He stayed aggressive and his ball was really moving," said Quebec manager Eric Scism, after the game.

Drouin whiffed 3 and walked 3 in the 4-0 win.

"If I make good pitches, I'm going to get some outs," Drouin said. "I was making good pitches the entire game."

Drouin has started 5 times this year, pitched 40.1 innings, fanned 13 batters and walked 12. His current record is 2 wins and 1 loss with a 3.79 ERA.
