Recovery Slow For Reds Keith Saibara
Saturday, October 22nd, 1927
The Cincinnati Reds plan to take it slow with starting pitcher Keith Saibara, knowing that his recovery from a partially torn ulnar collateral ligament would likely include a few bumps in the road. A team spokesman told BNN, "Keith has up days and down days. It's still a process. He was feeling really good and then he had a step back. The doctors say it will be about 4-5 months before he can be activated."

During his career Saibara has a 11-10 won-lost record with a 3.82 ERA.
