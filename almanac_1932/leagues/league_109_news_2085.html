Robins Captures Wild Card
Monday, August 24th, 1931
The "ultimate goal" remains in reach for the Mario Spivey and the Harrisburg Robins.

The squad today captured a wild card berth and Spivey said the team's plan is simple.

"Get to the playoffs, win the World Series," he said. "If that's not your goal as a Eastern League team, I don't know what's wrong with you."

If the Robins were to win the World Series, it would be the 3rd championship trophy in franchise history.
