Another Setback For Trois-Rivieres's Alexis Paré
Thursday, August 16th, 1928
Yesterday Trois-Rivieres shortstop Alexis Paré sustained a setback in his recovery from a strained hip flexor. The Royales medical staff told him it would be at least one week before he will be able to return to action. Paré told reporters, "The healing process has gone extremely well. This is just a little hiccup and a little setback." Apparently Paré worked a little too hard in a recent workout and caused more damage. His doctors have advised him to do only light exercise and give the injury time to heal.

Paré on the season is hitting .373 with 2 home runs. His stats include 21 RBIs and 29 runs scored.
