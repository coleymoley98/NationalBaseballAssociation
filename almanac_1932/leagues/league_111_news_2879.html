EBL Player of the Week Goes to Staats of Hamilton
Monday, June 27th, 1932
16-year-old Jimmy Staats made a few Hamilton headlines during this past week as the rookie right fielder proved he could compete at the plate with his high-school idols. He captured the attention of fans, journalists and Eastern Baseball League officials last week and today took home Player of the Week honors for his effort.

He cracked 11 hits in 17 at-bats to put up a .647 average. Staats collected 3 home runs, 10 RBIs and scored 8 runs.

This year Staats has hit at a .354 pace and produced 22 RBIs, 5 home runs and scored 19 runs.
