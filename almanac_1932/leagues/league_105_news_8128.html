Ottawa Indians Ship Boutin to Philadelphia
Wednesday, January 19th, 1927
It took some time to iron out the details, but the Indians and Liberty have agreed to a swap. Philadelphia will get 30-year-old LF Akarin Boutin with Ottawa receiving 20-year-old minor league LF Arthit Kurata and 19-year-old minor league 3B Sorrasak Beaudoin in return. A source close to the trade talks said the two clubs were initially looking to make a bigger deal, but discussions broke off late last night. The two sides reconvened early today and eventually settled on this deal.
