Beaulieu Beguiles Winnipeg
Saturday, July 23rd, 1927
Guillaume Beaulieu handcuffed the Winnipeg Thunder at Thunder Stadium today, coming away with a 5-hit, complete game shutout.

"I had everything working for me out there," the Omaha Falcons right-hander said later. "I used every one of my pitches, hit both sides of the plate really well and felt like I could do whatever I wanted."

With his stuff working, Beaulieu had little trouble with the Thunder and led the Falcons to victory, 5-0. He recorded 9 strikeouts and 3 walks in an impressive performance.

This year Beaulieu has worked 92 innings with 49 strikeouts en route to a 6-5 record and 3.33 ERA.
