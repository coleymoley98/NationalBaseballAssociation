That's All She Wrote: Career Over for Street
Thursday, August 9th, 1923
The expression on his face said it all when starting pitcher Andy Street was talking to reporters at a special press conference today. He was carefully examined by specialists the past couple of days, and after several tests the diagnosis was a torn rotator cuff. In this case, it means his career is likely over. The Seattle Rainiers issued the following statement: "It is with great sadness that we see Andy ending his career due to injury. He was a great competitor and teammate and will be missed by the Rainiers organization." Street is 25 years old.
