Eastern Baseball League Selects #1 Starter in July
Wednesday, August 1st, 1928
Over the past month Rod Gibb has turned quite a few heads. He's young, talented and able to pitch with the established arms in the Eastern Baseball League. He proved all that and now he's been named July's Pitcher of the Month.

The rookie for the Gallant Black Sox posted 6-1 mark and a 2.68 ERA. In 6 starts he tossed 43.2 innings, permitted 36 hits and fanned 57 batters.

Gibb has posted a 7-3 record for the year with a 3.17 ERA. Teams are hitting .244 against him and he has rung up 99 strikeouts in 82.1 innings.
