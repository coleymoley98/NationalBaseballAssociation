Jr. Falcons Blank St. Louis, Chénard Winner
Friday, July 15th, 1932
St. Louis dropped a 1-0 shutout to Enzo Chénard and the Omaha Jr. Falcons at Omaha Grounds today. The Jr. Cardinaux offered little resistance to Chénard, who stopped them on 2 hits, fanned 3 and walked 1.

"It just felt right today," the right-handed Chénard told reporters after the win. "Even with the rain, it didn't throw me off my rhythm."

In 13 starts this year Chénard has recorded a 2-5 record and a 3.33 ERA.
