Best CL Pitcher Announced
Friday, October 17th, 1924
Louis Mongeau of the New York Rebels is in the spotlight today. He was voted the top pitcher in the Canadian League and will be presented the 1924 Pitcher of the Year Award.

He started 25 times and compiled a 16-8 record with an ERA of 2.97 in 218.1 innings with 66 strikeouts.

He received 16 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Rick Mathews of the New York Rebels finished second in voting, while Ray O'Gorman of the Baltimore Robins finished third.
