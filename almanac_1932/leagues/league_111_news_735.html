Kongthep Pitches Jr. Steelers to Shutout Victory
Tuesday, July 15th, 1930
The North Bay Battalion met their match today in Hamilton hurler Wittaya Kongthep. He kept them guessing the whole game and spun a 6-hit shutout.

"He just worked us over," North Bay skipper Arran Laplante said. "He probably didn't even need a shower after the game -- he barely worked up a sweat."

In the 2-0 victory, Kongthep whiffed 7 and gave up no walks.

In 8 starts this season Kongthep has notched a 5-2 record and a 3.33 ERA.
