Sulley Spins No-Hitter Against Vancouver
Sunday, June 21st, 1925
It was a terrific day for Kyle Sulley of the Saskatoon Coyotes at Oracle Park today. The left-hander stood out with a fantastic 10-0 no-hitter against the Vancouver Giants.

"I knew I had a no-no going in about the sixth inning when I noticed no one was talking to me anymore," he said, referring to the time-honored superstition of avoiding the pitcher so as not to jinx the no-hitter. "Don't let people fool you. Everybody on the field and in the ballpark knows what's happening. They just pretend they don't."

For the game, Sulley totaled 5 walks and 9 strikeouts in a very strong outing.

"His mechanics were perfect, and I don't think he threw one bad pitch in the whole game," Saskatoon manager Maurice Wigglesworth said. "He was that good."
