Lanser Chosen NL's Best Newcomer
Tuesday, September 1st, 1931
Kent Lanser is beginning to make a name for himself and he did it by posting a 3-2 record and a 2.74 ERA in August. For his spectacular mound performance last month he has been named BNN's Rookie of the Month in the National League.

The 25-year-old newcomer isn't at all intimidated by his lack of experience, at least not outwardly. In fact, by the way he has pitched beyond his years, you could easily mistake him for a veteran.

In 6 starts for the Mets Lanser has struck out 12 batters in 46 innings, holding opposing hitters to a batting average of .253 during his hot streak.

This season Lanser has crafted a 7-9 mark with a 4.24 ERA in 153 innings. Opposing clubs are batting .269 against him and he has 47 strikeouts.
