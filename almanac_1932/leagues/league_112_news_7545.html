Highlanders Out of Playoff Race
Sunday, July 18th, 1926
Those hoping to see the Halifax Highlanders in the playoffs this year will be disappointed. The club was officially eliminated from postseason contention today.

The Highlanders have missed the playoffs 31 times in their 32 years as a Atlantic Baseball League franchise. This playoff miss is the club's 4th in a row, with their last postseason appearance coming in 1921. In the last 10 seasons, the Highlanders have missed the playoffs 9 times. Halifax has won the Laurier Cup 4 times in their history.
