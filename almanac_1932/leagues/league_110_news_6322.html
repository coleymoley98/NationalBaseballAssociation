Sunday, July 5th, 1925
Totchtawan Zwick of the St. Louis Jr. Cardinaux spun his magic on the Minneapolis Jr. Saints today. He blanked them 11-0 on 3 hits, registering 9 strikeouts and 1 walk.

"The bottom line was I just kept the ball down," Zwick said. "My breaking stuff was down. Everything was down."

"Totchtawan pitched really well but this was a solid performance by everyone," he said.
