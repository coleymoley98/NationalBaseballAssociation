North Bay Takes Division Finals in Deciding Game
Thursday, August 2nd, 1923
It took a while, but the North Bay Battalion finally disposed of the Springfield Falcons 6-5 in the series clincher at Juniors Ballpark.

Winning the finale gave the Battalion the series, 3-2. They will now advance to the Adams Cup.

The Most Valuable Player Award went to North Bay center fielder Gautam Mijangos. He batted .429 in the series, collecting 8 RBIs and scoring 4 runs.

The North Bay Battalion will play against the Buffalo Rapids in the next round. Buffalo won their series against the Morgantown Mountaineers 3 games to 1. The Adams Cup schedule will be announced after the Division Finals finishes.
