Boulay Has 20-Game Hitting Streak
Friday, May 31st, 1929
Minnesota lost to the Detroit Lions 7-1 at Field of the Saints today, but Taradin Boulay kept his consecutive-game hitting streak alive with 1 hit. The Saints first baseman has now delivered hits in 20 straight games.

"Taradin has a nice streak going, but the scoreboard tells the important story today," Minnesota manager A.J. Provost told reporters after the game. "We lost, no matter how many hits anyone had."
Taradin Boulay grounded out in the 1st, grounded out in the 4th, doubled in the 6th and struck out in the 9th.

For the season Boulay is batting .413 with 38 hits and 1 home run. He has scored 17 times and batted in 16.
