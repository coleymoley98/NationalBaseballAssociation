Guérin Hurls Frontenacs to 5-0 Shutout Win
Sunday, June 19th, 1927
Thomas Guérin kept the Detroit Lions hitters off balance all game long as the Chicagou Frontenacs pitcher blanked the Lions, 5-0.

Guérin gave up 6 hits, struck out 4 and walked 3. In the postgame press conference, the right-hander was reluctant to talk about the outing at length.

"It was a nice win in a long season," he said. "I don't want to dwell too much on it."

This year Guérin has worked 64.1 innings with 33 strikeouts en route to a 3-3 record and 3.36 ERA.
