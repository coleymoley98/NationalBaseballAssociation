Sánchez Pounds NL Pitchers This Week
Monday, August 27th, 1923
It can be difficult for a young player to step out of the shadows and assert himself in the National League, but last week Federico Sánchez of the Quebec Nordiques reminded everyone just why he's here as he threw up numbers that made him an easy selection for Player of the Week. Sánchez rocked opposing pitching for a .519 average (14-for-27), no home runs and 2 RBIs to come away with the award.

So far in 1923 Sánchez is batting .329 with 4 home runs, 42 RBIs and 85 runs scored. He has 137 hits in 416 at-bats and a .362 OBP.
