Player of the Week Trophy to Cordier
Monday, July 28th, 1924
Noël Cordier is respected by most Saskatoon Coyotes fans for his no-frills style of play. The 20-year-old shortstop went about his business with vigor last week to grab the Western League Player of the Week honors.

He compiled a .458 average (11-for-24) with 2 home runs, 10 RBIs and 8 runs scored.

Hitting .426 to date, Cordier has 3 home runs and 13 RBIs. He has played in 14 games.
