Nedd Grabs WL Reliever of the Year Award
Monday, September 19th, 1932
Kyle Nedd said he thinks hitters often underestimate him, as he's only 20 years old.

But he'll certainly be getting a lot more respect now, as the Victoria Admirals rising star has won the Western League Reliever of the Year Award for 1932.

He captured the award on the strength of his 3.86 ERA over 30.1 innings. He also had 3 saves in 22 relief appearances, 22 strikeouts and only 13 walks.

He received 10 first place votes, as a unanimous winner.
