Boston Starter Named CL's Top Pitcher
Thursday, September 1st, 1927
In August, D.J. Rhoden tore through the Canadian League like a seasoned veteran -- quite impressive for a player only 24 years old.

The young Boston starter posted a 6-0 mark and an ERA of 2.30 to earn the Pitcher of the Month award. In 47 innings he collected 13 strikeouts while limiting the opposition to a .237 average.

To date this season Rhoden is 12-3 with 17 starts and an ERA of 3.07 with 29 strikeouts in 140.2 innings.
