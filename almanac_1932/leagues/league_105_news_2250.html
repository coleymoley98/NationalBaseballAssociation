Royales Win League Chamionship Series
Thursday, October 1st, 1931
Arlo Cunningham sparked his Montreal Royales past the pesky Detroit Lions in a closely-contested League Chamionship Series.

Montreal topped Detroit 4-3 at Montreal Grounds to decide the series, 4-2

For his efforts, Cunningham was rewarded with the series MVP trophy for his .440 batting average, 3 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

"You never want to leave anything on the field," Cunningham said. "You never want to miss a play of defense you could have made, or give up an extra base you could have gotten, because in a series this tight you never know what you'll need to win."

The Montreal Royales don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Boston Redcoats - Toronto Blue Sox series. Boston leads the series 3 games to 2. The Riel Cup schedule will be announced after the League Chamionship Series finishes.
