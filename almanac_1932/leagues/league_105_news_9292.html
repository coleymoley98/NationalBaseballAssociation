Baltimore Trades Kraisil for New York's LeNoir
Wednesday, January 4th, 1928
A trade between Baltimore and New York was made official this morning. The Robins have sent 30-year-old 1B Phanupong Kraisil to the Rebels in exchange for 20-year-old minor league 3B Victor Roberts, 18-year-old minor league LHP Rocky Goodine and 20-year-old minor league 2B Peter LeNoir.


In his 936-game career Kraisil has hit .311 with 1107 hits, 49 home runs, and 549 RBIs and has scored 520 runs.
