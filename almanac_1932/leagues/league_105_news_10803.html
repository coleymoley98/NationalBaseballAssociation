Boulay Has 25-Game Hitting Streak
Thursday, June 6th, 1929
The streak is alive and well -- at least for today.

Taradin Boulay did it again, bashing 2 hits in the contest at Field of the Saints today. For the last 25 games in a row, the 23-year-old first baseman has collected at least one hit. His efforts helped the Minnesota Saints beat the St. Louis Cardinaux 5-2 today.

"I'm happy I kept the streak going," Boulay told a standing-room-only crowd of reporters gathered in the Minnesota clubhouse. "Honestly though, I'm looking forward to going home and not thinking about this for the rest of the day."
Taradin Boulay walked in the 1st, hit an RBI double in the 3rd, singled in the 5th and grounded out in the 7th.

Thus far this season in 28 games Boulay is batting .432 with 3 home runs, 24 RBIs and 22 runs scored. He has 48 hits in 111 at-bats.
