CBL Reliever of the Year Award Winner Named
Wednesday, August 15th, 1923
B.J. Diggs said he thinks hitters often underestimate him, as he's only 16 years old.

But he'll certainly be getting a lot more respect now, as the Saskatoon Jr. Coyotes rising star has won the Central Baseball League Reliever of the Year Award for 1923.

He captured the award on the strength of his 1.29 ERA over 48.2 innings. He also had 2 saves in 14 relief appearances, 52 strikeouts and only 12 walks.

He received 8 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Nikon Beattie of the Adams Mounties finished second in voting, while Anuwat Mazza of the Winnipeg Jr. Thunder finished third.
