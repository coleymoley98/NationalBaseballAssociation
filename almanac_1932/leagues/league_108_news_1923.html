WL's Top Hitter Announced
Saturday, August 1st, 1931
When Oliver Legault stepped up to the plate in July he did it with confidence, and today he was honored with the Western League Batter of the Month award.

The young Calgary first baseman posted a .355 batting average with 2 home runs, 36 RBIs and scored 24 runs. Legault also worked pitchers for 24 walks to post a .485 on-base percentage.

For the season Legault is currently batting .344 with 4 home runs and 63 RBIs. In 57 games he has reached base at a .459 rate and scored 49 runs to date.
