Friday, July 1st, 1927
Luke Reppenhagen is beginning to make a name for himself and he did it by posting a 2-1 record and a 3.60 ERA in June. For his spectacular mound performance last month he has been named BNN's Rookie of the Month in the National League.

The 23-year-old newcomer isn't at all intimidated by his lack of experience, at least not outwardly. In fact, by the way he has pitched beyond his years, you could easily mistake him for a veteran.

In 0 starts for the Reds Reppenhagen has struck out 8 batters in 15 innings, holding opposing hitters to a batting average of .241 during his hot streak.
