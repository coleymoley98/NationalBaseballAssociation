King Earns August's Best Pitcher Honor
Thursday, September 1st, 1932
Charles-Olivier King is the latest National League Pitcher of the Month trophy winner. The Chicagou starter sparkled on the diamond time and again last month and finished August with an outstanding 4-2 record.

In 6 starts he tossed 47.2 innings, posted 40 strikeouts, and sported a 2.27 ERA.

King has made 22 starts this season, posting 13 wins and 5 losses. Opposing clubs are batting .241 against him and he has 130 strikeouts in 175.2 innings with a 2.61 ERA.
