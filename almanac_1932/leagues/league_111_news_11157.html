Monday, July 22nd, 1929
Earnest Ettrick burst onto the Eastern Baseball League stage in a big way last week. This promising rookie first baseman for Hamilton garnered everyone's respect by putting up some remarkable numbers and picking up the Player of the Week trophy.

The 16-year-old haunted opposing pitchers hitting .375 (9-for-24) with 4 home runs and 9 RBIs last week.
