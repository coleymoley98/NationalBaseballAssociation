Cardinaux Flame Out Against Aubry, Brasseurs
Tuesday, August 20th, 1929
Wuttinun Aubry of the Juneau Brasseurs shut out the St. Louis Cardinaux 7-0 at St. Louis Grounds today, holding them to 5 hits with 1 walk and 3 strikeouts.

Pressed for comments on his club's defeat, St. Louis skipper Virgil McLain called the outing from Aubry "a jewel."

"The way Aubry was pitching, it would have been tough for anyone to beat him," he said.

This season to date Aubry has a record of 8 wins and 9 losses with a 3.51 ERA, has appeared in 21 games and struck out 60 in 143.2 innings.
