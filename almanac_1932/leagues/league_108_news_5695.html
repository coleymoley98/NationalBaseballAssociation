WL Awards Outstanding Pitcher Trophy
Sunday, September 21st, 1924
The 1924 Western League Pitcher of the Year Award has gone to Omaha Falcons ace Elliot Provost after a stellar 10-4 season.

In 15 starts he piled up 129.1 innings, 74 strikeouts, 39 walks and a 2.23 ERA. Provost held opposing hitters to a .239 batting average.

He received 7 first place votes out of a possible 8. Tommy Audette of the Saskatoon Coyotes finished second in voting with 1 first place vote, while Martino Gutierrez of the Omaha Falcons finished third.
