Jr. Royales Torpedoed by Lowenstein in 2-0 Loss to Quebec City
Thursday, July 2nd, 1931
Steve Lowenstein was impressive at Juniors Ballpark today in the Quebec City Jr. Nordiques' 2-0 shutout victory over the Montreal Jr. Royales. The Quebec City pitcher mystified the Jr. Royales in a strong performance, giving up 4 hits and totaling 2 walks and 14 strikeouts.

"Steve had them chasing a lot of pitches and talking to themselves," Quebec City manager Supravee Dube. "It was something special to watch."

In 7 starts this season Lowenstein has notched a 5-1 record and a 0.87 ERA.
