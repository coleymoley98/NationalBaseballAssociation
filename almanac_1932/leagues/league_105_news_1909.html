Blue Sox End Paquette's Streak
Friday, July 31st, 1931
Lady Luck was not on the side of Sripatana Paquette or his teammates today at Lincoln Park.

Everything went the way of the Toronto Blue Sox, who not only came away with the 19-2 victory over Ottawa, but also held Paquette hitless in 3 at-bats to put an end to his 24-game hitting streak.

"It was a good run," Paquette said afterward. "It would be much easier to take right now if we had won."

For the year Paquette has hit .420 with 2 home runs, 20 RBIs and scored 16 runs.
