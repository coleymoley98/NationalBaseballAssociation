Coronado Blisters Ranier
Wednesday, July 22nd, 1931
It was a magical day at the plate for Kelowna Apple Sox shortstop Narong Coronado. The 15-year-old Coronado helped lift Kelowna to a 17-14 win over the Ranier Seahawks today at Ballpark of Kelowna. Coronado smacked 5 hits in 5 at-bats to spark his club to victory.

Narong Coronado hit a two-run home run off Benjamin Brann in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, hit a three-run home run off Cade Harlock in the 5th, hit a three-run home run off Raymond Tétreault in the 6th and walked in the 8th.

This season Coronado is batting .357 with 7 home runs. He has scored 34 runs and driven in 47.
