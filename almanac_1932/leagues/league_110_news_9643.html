Gallegos Is King of Swing: Goes Deep 3 Times
Tuesday, June 12th, 1928
Winnipeg Jr. Thunder right fielder Devin Gallegos had a magnificent game against the Davenport Rivermen today, rocketing three balls out of Ballpark of Davenport to help the Jr. Thunder take the 11-2 victory.

"I got some good pitches to hit and I creamed them," he said after the game. "The first was a big, fat fastball and the last two were hanging curves. All three were mistakes."

For the game, Gallegos went 3-for-4 with 4 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

"There's a fine line between hitting home runs and line drives," Winnipeg manager Rusty Dang said later. "Seems like Devin found his home run stroke today."

Devin Gallegos flied out in the 1st, hit a solo-shot off Raphael Hétu in the 2nd, hit a solo-shot off Raphael Hétu in the 4th and hit a two-run home run off André Lamontagne in the 7th.

In the current season, Gallegos has put up a .409 batting average with 18 hits, 5 home runs, 14 runs batted in and scored 16 runs.
