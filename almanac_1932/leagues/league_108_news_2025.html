Rainiers Lead with Chin, Get KO'd, 9-0
Thursday, August 13th, 1931
Wade Atkinson of the Victoria Admirals was lights-out in a 8-hit whitewash of the Seattle Rainiers.

"I had my good stuff and it went where I wanted it to go," he told BNN reporters after the contest.

Atkinson registered 10 strikeouts and no walks in shutting out Seattle, 9-0.

This year Atkinson has gone 6-1 with an ERA of 2.03 and has 38 strikeouts in 62 innings.
