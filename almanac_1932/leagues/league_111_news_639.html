Morgantown's Madgewick Gets EBL Player of the Week
Monday, July 7th, 1930
So far in his young career, Mark Madgewick has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Eastern Baseball League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 16-year-old Morgantown Mountaineers left fielder hit at a torrid .536 clip as he collected 15 hits in 28 at-bats, 1 home run, 9 RBIs and 6 runs scored.

This season Madgewick is swinging the bat at a .407 rate with 55 hits in 135 at-bats. To date he has hit 3 home runs, scored 31 runs and driven in 28.
