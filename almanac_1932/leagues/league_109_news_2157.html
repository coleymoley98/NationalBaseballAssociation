Techonan Patcharapa of the Harrisburg Robins was on fire against the Trois-Rivieres Royales at Trois-Rivieres Grounds today. He shut them out 4-0 on 5 hits, collecting 6 strikeouts and allowing no walks.

"The first two pitches I'm looking for an out, a ground ball or a fly ball," Patcharapa said later. "I'm trying to get quick outs. I've never been a big believer in trying to strike a guy out from the very first pitch. It's too easy to end up behind in the count doing that."

This year Patcharapa has fashioned a 6-2 mark, 4.22 ERA and pitched 79 innings. Opposing clubs are batting .239 against him and he has logged 98 strikeouts.
