Mongeau Tops Career List with Win #165
Friday, June 13th, 1924
In an epic game at Polo Grounds, Louis Mongeau claimed his history-making victory number 165, which made the New York Rebels ace the winningest pitcher ever. The tally exceeded the all-time wins record held by the legendary Naithim Boonsom and put Mongeau's name at the top of the league record books.

"It's hard to see your name equated with the game's immortals," the new record holder told the press after the game. "Most of us don't see ourselves as legends. Still the accolades are quite nice."

Checking out his stats for the season, Mongeau has notched 6 wins and 1 loss in 7 starts, 2.73 ERA and struck out 21 batters in 62.2 innings.
