Hughs Is King of Swing: Goes Deep 3 Times
Friday, July 16th, 1926
At T-Mobile Park today, Troy Hughs hit homer after homer after homer. But they didn't add up to enough to beat the Coude Voyageurs as the St. John's Mariners went down to defeat, 16-13.

"It was a good game for me, not so good for our team," the St. John's right fielder said. "I got lucky. I made some good swings on some pitches. We just didn't put up one in the win column."

On his monumental day, he had 5 hits in 5 at-bats, drove in 4 runs, and scored 3 times.

"The third one was totally unexpected," Hughs told reporters after his extraordinary game. "I just wanted to make good contact, never thinking I would get another home run and bang, I got number three."

Troy Hughs hit a solo-shot off A.R. Lo in the 2nd, singled in the 2nd, hit a two-run home run off Choem Yathawee in the 4th, walked in the 5th, hit a solo-shot off Fabio Cloutier in the 8th and doubled in the 9th.

So far this year, Hughs is batting .323 with 11 home runs and 34 RBIs.
