Postseason Bid Ends for Sherbrooke
Wednesday, July 16th, 1924
A late-season playoff push is not in the cards for the Sherbrooke Canadiens. The club today was mathematically knocked out of playoff contention.

"There are a lot of long faces around right now," said Canadiens manager C.J. Chambers. "You know in your head that every year, most teams in baseball have to go through this, but it still hurts when you realize you've lost your shot at a title."

Sherbrooke last appeared in the postseason in 1922. This is their 2nd consecutive year missing the playoffs, and in total they have not advanced past the regular season 29 times in their 30-year history. In the last 10 seasons, the Canadiens have missed the playoffs 9 times. Sherbrooke has won the Laurier Cup 2 times in their history.
