White Sox End Rufolo's Streak
Monday, June 29th, 1931
The Baltimore Robins lost to the Chicagou White Sox 9-7 today at Comiskey Park 1910, but perhaps the bigger news was the end of Edge Rufolo's 24-game hitting streak. The center fielder went 0-for-3 on the day.

"We felt if we could slow down Rufolo, we would win the game," White Sox manager Tommy Dupuis explained. "They really seemed to feed off his streak, so we tried to put more emphasis on shutting him down."

This year Rufolo is batting .363 with 66 hits, 3 home runs, 35 RBIs, 24 walks, .435 OBP and has scored 43 runs.
