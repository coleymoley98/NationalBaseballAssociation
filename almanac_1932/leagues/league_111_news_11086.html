Mountaineers Spank Jr. Steelers, 10-0
Friday, July 12th, 1929
Morgantown Mountaineers pitcher Jimmy Strasburg really got it done at Juniors Ballpark today in his 10-0 shutout victory over the Hamilton Jr. Steelers, shutting them down on 4 hits.

"It was just one of those games where everything went right. I threw a lot of strikes," Strasburg said. "And the pitches they did get to hit, they didn't do anything with them."

The 19-year-old issued no walks and had 11 strikeouts in the Mountaineers win.

To date Strasburg is 10-1 in 12 starts with a 2.49 ERA and has 95 strikeouts in 101.1 innings.
