Hadzima Snares EL Top Batter Award in July
Saturday, August 1st, 1925
Winfield Hadzima made his presence known as one of the best young stars in the Eastern League this past month.

The Pittsburgh Liberty left fielder shined through the month of July and has won the Batter of the Month award.

The 19-year-old hit .337 with 9 home runs, 42 RBIs and 20 runs scored.

Hadzima is hitting .274 for the season with 13 home runs, 60 RBIs and 35 runs scored. He has 57 hits in 208 at-bats and played in 53 games.
