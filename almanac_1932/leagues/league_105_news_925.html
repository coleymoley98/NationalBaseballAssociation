Roca Passes Mongeau For Most Wins Ever
Saturday, August 9th, 1930
As expected, another all-time career mark has fallen. Steve Roca of the Detroit Lions passed the great Louis Mongeau by racking up win number 205 at Bennett Park today.

"It's amazing that this old arm hasn't fallen off after all these years," Roca told the reporters after the historic event. "I just hope there are a few more wins left in it."

To date this year Roca has compiled a 11-6 mark with a 3.86 ERA in 17 starts.
