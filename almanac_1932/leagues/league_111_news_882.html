Springfield Takes Division Finals in Deciding Game
Friday, August 1st, 1930
In an exciting Division Finals that had its fair share of drama and intrigue, perhaps it is Eastern Baseball League fans who have come out the winner.

But on the field, the winners were the Springfield Falcons, who edged the Toronto Jr. Blue Sox 3-2 to advance to the Adams Cup.

Springfield won the final game by a 4-3 count to wrap up the series. Lek Sams earned series MVP honors, by hitting .421 with a .478 on-base percentage. He had 2 home runs, drove in 8 RBI and scored 5 runs.

The Springfield Falcons will play against the Hamilton Jr. Steelers in the next round. Hamilton won their series against the Morgantown Mountaineers 3 games to 0. The Adams Cup schedule will be announced after the Division Finals finishes.
