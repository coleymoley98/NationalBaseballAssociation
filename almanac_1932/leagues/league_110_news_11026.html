Hit Parade: Gearlds Pops 5 Hits
Thursday, July 4th, 1929
Rivermen right fielder Nicky Gearlds had his way with Saskatoon pitching today at Jr. Coyotes Ballpark and mauled them for 5 hits in 5 at-bats. His efforts were all in vain, though, as the Jr. Coyotes ruined his great day by toppling the Rivermen, 4-3.

"I had a great day, but you've got to give Saskatoon credit, they did what they had to do to win," he told reporters after the game. "They weren't very impressed with my 5 hits."

"Oh, we noticed what Gearlds did today," Jr. Coyotes skipper Daquein Liles said. "He had quite a game. I can tell you this, we will pitch him a little differently the next time we face him."

Nicky Gearlds singled in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 3rd, singled in the 6th, singled in the 8th and hit a two-run double in the 10th.
