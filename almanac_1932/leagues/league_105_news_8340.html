Teran Selected Player of the Week
Monday, May 30th, 1927
Orber Teran of the Quebec Nordiques has just enjoyed one of the better weeks of his career. That fact was not lost on National League officials, who named him Player of the Week.

Teran logged a .536 batting average for the Quebec Nordiques, racking up 7 RBIs and 1 home run.

Thus far in 1927 Teran is hitting .383 with 3 home runs, 11 RBIs, 16 runs scored and has compiled a .440 OBP.
