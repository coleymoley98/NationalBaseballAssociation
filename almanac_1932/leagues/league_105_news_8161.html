Ottawa Trades Castleberry for Quebec's Lepage
Thursday, February 17th, 1927
Sometimes a change of scenery can be a beneficial to a baseball player. That is the hope in Ottawa and Quebec as the teams came to an agreement today which sends 34-year-old LHP Jeremy Castleberry to the Nordiques and 27-year-old minor league LF Xavier Lepage and 19-year-old minor league 3B Zach Gaumer to the Indians. "Only time will tell if this is the right move for everyone," stated the Quebec GM.


Lifetime Castleberry has chalked up 136 wins and 135 losses and compiled a 3.43 ERA.

His career numbers show Lepage is hitting .337 with a total of 2 home runs.
