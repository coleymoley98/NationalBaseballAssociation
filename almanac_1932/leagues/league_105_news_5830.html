Cincinnati-Detroit Trade: di Matteo for Nomura
Saturday, December 13th, 1924
Earlier today, officials from the Cincinnati Reds and the Detroit Lions announced that they had finalized negotiations on a trade between the two teams. The deal will send 30-year-old LHP Franck di Matteo to Detroit in exchange for 20-year-old minor league SS Andreo Rodríguez and 20-year-old minor league RHP Samornchai Nomura.


Since he joined the league, di Matteo has collected 49 victories and 59 defeats with a 3.82 ERA.
