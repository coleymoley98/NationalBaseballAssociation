Juneau Devastated by Playoff Elimination
Saturday, July 25th, 1925
Looking at the bigger picture, Montree Sommee of the Juneau Jr. Brasseurs said he's excited for what's to come in the years ahead for his squad. But in the present, he said, the club is quite disappointed with the realization today that they are now mathematically out of playoff contention for the 1925 season.

The Jr. Brasseurs have missed the playoffs 30 times in their 31 years as a Eastern Baseball League franchise. This playoff miss is the club's 4th in a row, with their last postseason appearance coming in 1920. In the last 10 seasons, the Jr. Brasseurs have missed the playoffs 9 times. Juneau has won the Adams Cup 3 times in their history.
