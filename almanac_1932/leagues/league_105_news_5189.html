Myers Extends Hit Streak to 20 Games
Friday, July 4th, 1924
Chicagou lost to the St. Louis Cardinaux 5-2 at St. Louis Grounds today, but Patton Myers kept his consecutive-game hitting streak alive with 2 hits. The Frontenacs catcher has now delivered hits in 20 straight games.

"Patton has a nice streak going, but the scoreboard tells the important story today," Chicagou manager Jetsadaporn Sheerin told reporters after the game. "We lost, no matter how many hits anyone had."
Patton Myers singled in the 2nd, flied out in the 4th and hit an RBI single in the 6th.

This year Myers is batting .423 with 1 home run and 20 RBIs.
