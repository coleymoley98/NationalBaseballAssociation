Ibarra Shines, Shuts Out Juneau, 8-0
Tuesday, August 14th, 1923
Daniel Ibarra subdued the Juneau Brasseurs, 8-0, pitching a 2-hit gem for the Quebec Nordiques at Brasseurs Ballpark.

"He was kind of teasing them with his off-speed stuff, and his fastball was moving everywhere," Quebec skipper Eric Scism said afterward.

Ibarra registered 6 strikeouts and issued 1 walk in his superb performance.

"We just didn't swing the bats very well. Or else he just pitched good, whichever way you want to look at it," said Juneau manager Jake Downing after the defeat.

In the current season in 21 starts, Daniel Ibarra has a record of 10-11 with 94 strikeouts in 175.1 innings and a 3.29 ERA.
