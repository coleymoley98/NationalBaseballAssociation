Baltimore Robins Ship Serre to Cincinnati
Monday, November 30th, 1931
After a week of negotiations, the Robins and Reds have come to terms on a deal that will send 27-year-old CF Emmanuel Serre to Cincinnati for 25-year-old LF Chatri Phinizy and 23-year-old minor league RHP Timo Cobarrubias.


Over his career Serre is batting .305 with 46 home runs and 253 RBIs. In 489 games he has delivered 542 hits, scored 369 runs, stolen 72 bases and gotten on base at a .391 clip.

In his career Phinizy has accumulated a .292 batting average with 148 hits. His totals include 29 doubles, 1 triple, 18 home runs, 32 strikeouts, 79 runs scored, 86 RBIs and a lifetime .354 OBP.
