Chicagou-Baltimore Trade: Roca for Fogarty
Monday, July 13th, 1925
The Chicagou White Sox and Baltimore Robins have put the finishing touches on a deal that was weeks in the making. Chicagou will part with 30-year-old RHP Steve Roca, while the Robins have dealt 25-year-old 2B Drayton Fogarty and 19-year-old minor league 1B A.J. Lepage.

"This one took a while to figure out," said Chicagou general manager Lance Brintle. "We must have gone back to the drawing board five or six times over the past few weeks. I'm glad we finally got the deal done. I think it is a good one for both clubs."


So far this season Roca has compiled a 4-7 record with a 5.26 ERA.

This year Fogarty has collected a .379 batting average to date with 11 hits in 29 at-bats, 3 RBIs, no home runs and scored 2 times. He has played in 11 games.
