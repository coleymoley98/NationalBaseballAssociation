King of the Hill Announced by Canadian League
Sunday, October 7th, 1928
This year's winner of the coveted Canadian League Pitcher of the Year Award is Jude Ng of the Boston Redcoats.

During the 1928 campaign, Ng excelled with a 20-3 won-lost mark in 23 starts. In 206.1 innings he yielded 179 hits and 43 walks, while striking out 61 and compiling an impressive 2.53 ERA.

He received 13 first place votes out of a possible 16. Andy O'Neill of the Philadelphia Liberty finished second in voting with 3 first place votes, while Dylan Dawkins of the New York Rebels finished third.
