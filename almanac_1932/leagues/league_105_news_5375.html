Dupuis Slaps 5 Hits
Saturday, July 26th, 1924
It was high-fives all around for Eli Dupuis at Lincoln Park today.

He got them from his teammates and he got them at the plate during an outstanding hitting performance against the Ottawa Indians. Dupuis smashed 5 hits in 5 at-bats on his monster day, leading his club to a 11-4 win.

"Eli was hitting everything," said Celtics manager Lyle Diggle. "It really was a special game for him."

In the opposing dugout, Indians manager Owen Turcotte told BNN beat writers that Dupuis "ambushed" the club.

"Obviously we didn't have much success against him, but we'll have another chance," he said. "Hopefully, Dupuis oversleeps next time."

Eli Dupuis hit an RBI single in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, hit an RBI single in the 4th, singled in the 6th and hit an RBI triple in the 9th.

So far this year in 54 games Dupuis is hitting .330 with 1 home run, 30 RBIs and 26 runs scored.
