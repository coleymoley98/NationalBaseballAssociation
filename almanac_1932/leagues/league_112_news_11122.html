Page Gives Halifax Goose Eggs
Monday, July 15th, 1929
Finley Page held the Halifax Highlanders to 2 hits and fashioned a fine 5-0 shutout at Juniors Ballpark today.

Speaking after the game, Quincy Gunners manager Graham Cross said "Page had everything going for him today."

He changed speeds effectively, stayed down, had a lot of movement," he said. "He was masterful."

Page rung up 7 strikeouts and surrendered no walks in the game.

"We couldn't get anything going. He certainly had our number," Halifax manager Kip Lofton told reporters.

In 11 starts this season Page has gathered 8 wins while suffering 3 losses. He has pitched 89.1 innings, allowed 73 hits, struck out 84 and has a 1.91 ERA.
