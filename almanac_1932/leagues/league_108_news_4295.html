Big Day with Bat: Campbell 5-for-5
Saturday, August 4th, 1923
It was high-fives all around for Archie Campbell at Edmonton Grounds today.

He got them from his teammates and he got them at the plate during an outstanding hitting performance against the Edmonton Eskimos. Campbell smashed 5 hits in 5 at-bats on his monster day, leading his club to a 18-13 win.

"Archie was hitting everything," said Giants manager Donovan Held. "It really was a special game for him."

In the opposing dugout, Eskimos manager Girvan Greene told BNN beat writers that Campbell "ambushed" the club.

"Obviously we didn't have much success against him, but we'll have another chance," he said. "Hopefully, Campbell oversleeps next time."

Archie Campbell singled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, hit a GRAND SLAM off Chris Sucharski in the 5th, walked in the 7th and hit a two-run home run off Aimery McKinney in the 8th.

Presently Campbell is batting .354 with 75 hits and 10 home runs. He has knocked in 53 runs and scored 42 times.
