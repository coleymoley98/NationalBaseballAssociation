Blue Sox End Nadeau's Streak
Thursday, September 1st, 1927
The Toronto Blue Sox got more than the 9-8 victory at Griffith Stadium 1911 today -- they also put an end to the 22-game hitting streak of Chicagou White Sox shortstop Noël Nadeau by holding him without a hit in 4 at-bats.

After the game, Nadeau seemed more concerned with his team taking the loss than with the demise of his streak.

"I had some great looks at some pitches that I should have hit," he said. "I missed some that I shouldn't have swung at. All in all, I wasn't much help with the bat today."

Nadeau is batting .313 this year with no home runs, 52 RBIs and 56 runs scored. He has 103 hits in 329 at-bats.
