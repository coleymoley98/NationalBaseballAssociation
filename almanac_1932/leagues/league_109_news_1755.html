Hot Bat Cooled, Nalley Hitless Against Rebels
Tuesday, July 14th, 1931
The Rebels stopped Gunner Nalley's 23-game hitting streak today at SunTrust Park, but they couldn't stop the Tecumseh Braves, falling by the score of 9-4.

Nalley didn't get a hit for the first time in weeks, finishing 0-for-3.

"It's just as well," Braves manager Calvert DeShon said. "We couldn't spend the whole season going out of our way not to jinx him."

To date Nalley has chalked up a .331 average with 6 home runs, driven in 26 runs and scored 22 times.
