Nuregesan Electric in 7-0 North Bay Win
Thursday, June 21st, 1923
Vihaan Nuregesan was in a zone of his own most of the day, shutting out the Morgantown Mountaineers on 3 hits.

"He was too darn good for us today," grumbled Morgantown manager Annan Wills after the 7-0 loss to the North Bay Battalion.

Nuregesan finished with 10 strikeouts and 1 walk in a strong outing.

In the postgame press conference, North Bay manager Louie Farris said Nuregesan "had good velocity, good movement and had those guys off balance. He just pitched his butt off."

So far in 5 appearances this season, Nuregesan has recorded 2 wins and sustained 1 loss. He has pitched 35.1 innings, yielded 33 hits, fanned 32 and has a 3.31 ERA.
