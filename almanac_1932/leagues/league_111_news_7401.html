Rapids Chill Wohlers's Hot Streak
Wednesday, June 30th, 1926
Fort Wayne Pioneers third baseman Santipab Wohlers and his hot bat finally met their match today at Juniors Ballpark.

The Buffalo Rapids snapped his 21-game hitting string, holding Wohlers hitless in 4 at-bats and also whipping Fort Wayne for good measure.

"We're real pleased with this one," Rapids manager Thawatchai Doors said. "Nobody wants to be on the wrong end of a streak like that, and clobbering Fort Wayne makes it even sweeter."

A look at his current stats shows Wohlers with a .372 average, 4 home runs, 20 RBIs and 24 runs scored. He has played in 27 games.
