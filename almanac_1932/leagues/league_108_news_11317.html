WL Player of the Week Selected
Monday, August 12th, 1929
His hard work and hustle paid off this week. The hard-working and hard-playing Adrian McNeely was rewarded with the Western League Player of the Week honors.

The scrappy Calgary Wranglers right fielder hit .520 while collecting 13 hits in 25 at-bats, 3 home runs and 7 RBIs.

To date this year McNeely is batting .354 with 5 home runs.
