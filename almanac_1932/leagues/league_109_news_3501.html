Best EL Reliever Named
Thursday, September 15th, 1932
His manager and teammates call Oliver Settle a true bullpen ace.

The Eastern League calls him the winner of the Reliever of the Year Award for 1932.

The Brampton Blue Sox relief specialist out of Joliet, The United Provinces, won the award with 4 saves in 32 relief appearances, 34 strikeouts in 74.2 innings and 19 walks.

He compiled a record of 7 wins and 2 losses and had a 3.62 ERA.

He received 9 first place votes out of a possible 10. Monty Walden of the Columbus Mets finished second in voting with 1 first place vote, while Wutthisak Pohji of the Columbus Mets finished third.
