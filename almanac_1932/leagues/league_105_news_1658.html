Philadelphia Trades Cork for Baltimore's Martel
Sunday, July 5th, 1931
The Baseball News Network is reporting that the Liberty and Robins have consummated a trade. Baltimore will receive 25-year-old RHP Cyril Cork and 19-year-old minor league CF Gabe Papineau in a swap for 19-year-old minor league 3B Virgile Martel. A league analyst called the trade a "fairly even transaction."


So far this year Cork has 3 wins and 6 losses and a 6.75 ERA.
