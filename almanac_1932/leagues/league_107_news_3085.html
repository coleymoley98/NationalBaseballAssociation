One Run Enough for Abbotsford
Monday, July 18th, 1932
It was a dismal game for the Ranier Seahawks with Gustav Lafrenière on the hill. The Abbotsford Alpines starter was in top form and made it a long day for Ranier with a 1-0 shutout. He surrendered a total of 3 hits and piled up 4 strikeouts to pick up the win in a rout.

"No offense to the Seahawks but that was a cakewalk today," Lafrenière said to BNN reporters following the win.

Lafrenière has gone 4-5 this season with an ERA of 1.75 and 84 strikeouts in 87.2 innings pitched. Other clubs are hitting .194 against him.
