Lei Wins NL Batter of the Month
Saturday, September 1st, 1928
Montreal Royales fans may just have witnessed a star player blossoming before their eyes in August.

Kitti Lei put up a remarkable month at the age of 24 and has been named the National League Batter of the Month.

For the month, Lei hit .422 with 1 home run, 19 RBIs and 22 runs scored.

So far in 1928 Lei is batting .336 with 9 home runs, 67 RBIs and 78 runs scored. He has 131 hits in 390 at-bats and a .407 OBP.
