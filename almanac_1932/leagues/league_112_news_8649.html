Great Day for Praveekorn -- 5 Hits
Thursday, July 7th, 1927
Gumchai Praveekorn got the glory and the headlines today, but the St. John's Mariners left with the win. The Coude center fielder easily handled the Mariners pitching, going 5-for-6, but his Coude Voyageurs came out on the short end of the 15-13 score.

Praveekorn told BNN reporters in the postgame interview he would happily trade "three or four" of his hits for a win.

"Heck, maybe even all of them," he said with a laugh. "Right now, it just seems like one loss in a long season but who knows what it could mean at the end."

Gumchai Praveekorn singled in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, grounded out in the 5th, singled in the 8th and singled in the 9th.

On the year Praveekorn has hit .270 with no home runs, 9 RBIs and scored 23 runs.
