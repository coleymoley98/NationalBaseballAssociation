Wolfeburg Loses Sylvia for 4-5 Weeks
Monday, August 1st, 1927
Elvis Sylvia verified reports that he is probably heading to the injured list. The injury that has kept the Wolfeburg center fielder out of action since 07/28/1927 -- shoulder tendinitis -- is significant enough, he said, that he will have to "shut it down for a while." How long? "Probably 4-5 weeks," he said.

Presently Sylvia is batting .376 with 3 home runs and 19 RBIs. He has played in 43 games and scored 35 times.
