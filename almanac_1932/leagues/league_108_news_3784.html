Gagnon Rocks Coyotes for 3 HRs
Sunday, June 3rd, 1923
Omaha catcher Sidney Gagnon led the Falcons to a 19-8 triumph over the Saskatoon Coyotes, crushing three home runs.

"He's scary good when he's swinging the bat like that," said Omaha skipper Emeril Martineau.

Indeed, Gagnon put up some "scary good" numbers: three homers, 4 hits in 5 at-bats with 4 runs and 5 RBIs.

"I'm just thinkin' about winning ballgames," Gagnon said. "When you think about home runs, you very seldom get 'em."

Sidney Gagnon singled in the 1st, was hit by a pitch in the 2nd, grounded out in the 4th, hit a two-run home run off Jir Thiffault in the 5th, hit a solo-shot off Jir Thiffault in the 7th and hit a two-run home run off Elias McNeal in the 8th.
