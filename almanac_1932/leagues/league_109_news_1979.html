Gonzalez Tosses 4-0 Shutout Against Columbus
Friday, August 7th, 1931
Sam Gonzalez of the Brampton Blue Sox was right on target as he blanked the Columbus Mets 4-0, yielding 3 hits and totaling 9 strikeouts and 3 walks.
