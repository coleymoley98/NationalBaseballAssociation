St. Louis Players Still Upset Over Poker Ban
Thursday, June 28th, 1923
Members of the St. Louis Cardinaux say they are still upset with the manager's insistence that games of poker not be played in the clubhouse.

"It's a stupid rule and it hasn't really improved our focus or preparation or anything," said Michael Hopkins. "All it has done is make the guys in the clubhouse mad."

But team manager Surabordee Yodyingyong said he didn't plan to change his mind on the issue, as he believes the team can now better focus on baseball.

"We shouldn't even be talking about poker," he said.
