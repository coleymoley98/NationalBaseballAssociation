Red-Hot Dawkins Cools Off Boston
Sunday, July 10th, 1927
Dylan Dawkins shut out the Boston Redcoats 1-0 on a 6-hit masterpiece for the New York Rebels at Fenway Park 1912 today.

"He was kind of teasing them with his off-speed stuff, and his fastball was moving everywhere," New York skipper Alexis Blouin said afterward.

Dawkins registered no strikeouts and issued no walks in pitching a gem.

"We just didn't swing the bats very well. Or else he just pitched good, whichever way you want to look at it," said Boston manager Brian Ahn after the defeat.

This season Dawkins has compiled a 7-6 mark with a 4.51 ERA in 15 starts.
