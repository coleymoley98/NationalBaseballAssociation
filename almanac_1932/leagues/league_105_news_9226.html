Quebec Trades di Matteo for Philadelphia's Reasoner
Monday, November 14th, 1927
The Quebec Nordiques and Philadelphia Liberty have put the finishing touches on a deal that was weeks in the making. Quebec will part with 33-year-old LHP Franck di Matteo, while the Liberty have dealt 27-year-old C Yin Reasoner.

"This one took a while to figure out," said Quebec general manager Al Trainor. "We must have gone back to the drawing board five or six times over the past few weeks. I'm glad we finally got the deal done. I think it is a good one for both clubs."


In his career the 33-year-old di Matteo has accumulated a 68-82 won-lost mark and opposing clubs have hit .278 against him.

Lifetime in 114 games Reasoner is batting .229 with 2 home runs and 35 RBIs.
