Minnesota Trades Lacroixille for Ottawa's Mizuno
Saturday, November 9th, 1929
It has been in the rumor mill for several weeks, but today team officials for Minnesota and Ottawa confirmed an agreement. The Saints dealt 29-year-old RHP Jiramet Lacroixille to the Indians for 26-year-old CF Hironori Mizuno.


Lacroixille has made 210 games with 12 saves in his career. His record includes 58 wins and 79 losses with a 4.86 ERA and 333 strikeouts in 1161 innings.
