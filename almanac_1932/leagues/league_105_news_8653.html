Beaupré Takes Charge, Blanks Quebec, 3-0
Friday, July 8th, 1927
In an excellent mound performance, Gautier Beaupré of the Juneau Brasseurs whitewashed the Quebec Nordiques 3-0 on a 4-hitter.

"I had my best stuff and did a good job of mixing up my pitches," he said after the game.

In all, the Juneau hurler rang up 5 strikeouts and 1 walk in the shutout win.

"We just didn't swing the bats well today," said Nordiques manager Eric Scism. "Beaupré did a good job keeping us guessing."

In 8 starts this season Beaupré has gathered 3 wins while suffering 4 losses. He has pitched 58 innings, allowed 56 hits, struck out 20 and has a 2.33 ERA.
