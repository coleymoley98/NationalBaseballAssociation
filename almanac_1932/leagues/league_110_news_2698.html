Naret Perron bent a little, but he didn't break against the Lincoln Pioneers. The St. Louis Jr. Cardinaux pitcher hurled a 6-hit 5-0 shutout in a fine performance, surrendering no walks and tallying 10 strikeouts.

"Naret is good at getting out of jams," Jr. Cardinaux manager Jasper Widow told reporters after the game. "He's almost more effective when he gets guys on base or gets behind in the count, because he uses your aggressiveness against you."
