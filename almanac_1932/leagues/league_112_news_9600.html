Huettner Pitches Highlanders to Shutout Victory
Thursday, June 7th, 1928
Zachary Huettner led the Halifax Highlanders to a 2-0 shutout victory over the Providence Bruins at Juniors Ballpark today.

"My pitching coach told me before the game just to bear down and focus," Huettner recalled later. "He said no one was going to hit me if I'm throwing a good breaking ball with good location."

Huettner yielded 4 hits and had 9 strikeouts and 1 walk in the standout performance.

"I thought he put the ball where he wanted to," Providence manager Marshall Kirkpatrick said. "And that's just about the whole secret to pitching."
