Wild Card Clinched by Nordiques
Tuesday, September 13th, 1932
For the Quebec Nordiques, it's not how you start, but how you finish. That's the mantra the team is following after today qualifying for the playoffs.

"We may be going into the playoffs as a wild card team, but we plan to finish the playoffs as champions," said center fielder Doug Tison. "There's no shortage of confidence from guys in our clubhouse."

With a record of 57-55, there will of course be teams with better track records in the postseason. But team manager Kiatisuk Sanders said his club has "infinite optimism" that they can win the franchise's 12th National Baseball Association championship.
