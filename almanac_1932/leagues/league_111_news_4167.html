Legends Come up Short of Playoffs
Sunday, July 22nd, 1923
Canton Legends manager Sean Wilkinson said his players are "devastated" to have missed the playoffs. Today it became official -- the club has run out of games and was mathematically eliminated from qualifying for a spot in the postseason.

Before this season, the Legends last missed the playoffs in 1921. In the 29 years they have been a Eastern Baseball League franchise, they have missed the playoffs 28 times. In the last 10 years, they have missed the playoffs 9 times.
