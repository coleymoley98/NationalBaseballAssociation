Thorns, Renard Stonewall Omaha, 5-0
Wednesday, September 5th, 1923
Chet Renard of the Portland Thorns humiliated the Omaha Falcons at Thorns Ballpark today, spinning a complete game shutout and holding the visitors to 4 hits.

"He stayed aggressive and his ball was really moving," said Omaha manager Emeril Martineau, after the game.

Renard whiffed 6 and walked 4 in the 5-0 win.

"If I make good pitches, I'm going to get some outs," Renard said. "I was making good pitches the entire game."

In season stats Renard shows a 5-2 record in 13 games, 86.2 innings, 56 strikeouts and a 3.63 ERA.
