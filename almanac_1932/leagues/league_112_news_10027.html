ABL Announces Outstanding Batter Award
Wednesday, August 1st, 1928
Drummondville Voltigeurs fans may just have witnessed a star player blossoming before their eyes in July.

Windsor Correa put up a remarkable month at the age of 17 and has been named the Atlantic Baseball League Batter of the Month.

For the month, Correa hit .397 with 8 home runs, 19 RBIs and 14 runs scored.

This season Correa is batting .348 with 64 hits, 14 home runs, 36 RBIs, 24 walks, .419 OBP and has scored 34 runs. He has played in 50 games.
