National League Names Best Rookie
Thursday, October 16th, 1924
League officials announced today that Carter Farmer of the Minnesota Saints has been selected the 1924 Rookie of the Year Award in the National League.

The talented first baseman finished his first year batting .351 with 10 home runs, 75 RBIs and 60 runs scored.

He received 16 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Bryan Coker of the Juneau Brasseurs finished second in voting, while D.J. Pham of the Montreal Royales finished third.
