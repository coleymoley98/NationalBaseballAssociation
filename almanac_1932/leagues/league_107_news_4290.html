Skipper Jesse Daniels out in Victoria
Saturday, August 4th, 1923
All is not well in Victoria.

Jesse Daniels's tenure as the Admirals skipper might end up being remembered more for a memorable exit than for anything he accomplished on the baseball diamond.

Daniels was fired as the manager of the Victoria Admirals today and didn't take the news particularly well. Upon hearing of his termination, Daniels immediately went to the owner's office, where he laid into the Admirals primary decision-maker with an expletive-filled tirade behind closed doors.

"He called me most every name in the book. It wasn't pretty," said Victoria owner The owner of the Victoria Admirals. "I'd say Jesse was simply reacting in the heat of the moment. Overall though, I would have preferred a classier exit."

After his verbal ambush of the team owner, Daniels stormed out of the stadium and was not immediately available for comment.

Club officials have scheduled a meeting tomorrow to appoint an interim manager and begin the search for Daniels's replacement.
