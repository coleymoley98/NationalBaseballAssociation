Mercier Has Season End
Friday, July 31st, 1931
Owen Mercier had hoped for the best, but expected the worst. Today, he learned from the Albany medical staff that he would miss the rest of the season. The Albany reliever suffered a sore shoulder in an earlier ballgame. He admitted to being discouraged, but said he plans to come back strong next year.

Mercier has pitched 45 innings and molded a 5.40 ERA with 6 saves. He has surrendered 41 hits while registering 30 strikeouts on his way to a 0-4 record for the Rebels this year.
