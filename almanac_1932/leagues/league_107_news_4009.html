Leboeuf Wins WBL Best Player Award
Monday, July 2nd, 1923
Barry Leboeuf burst onto the Western Baseball League stage in a big way last week. This promising rookie first baseman for Spokane garnered everyone's respect by putting up some remarkable numbers and picking up the Player of the Week trophy.

The 14-year-old haunted opposing pitchers hitting .533 (8-for-15) with 2 home runs and 7 RBIs last week.

Leboeuf is hitting .393 this year with 5 home runs, 22 RBIs and 17 runs scored. He has 35 hits in 89 at-bats, 13 walks and a .476 on-base percentage. Leboeuf has played in 25 games.
