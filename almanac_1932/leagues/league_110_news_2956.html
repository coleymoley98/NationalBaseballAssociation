CBL Names Top Player of the Week
Monday, July 4th, 1932
So far in his young career, Mark Delatte has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Central Baseball League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 18-year-old St. Louis Jr. Cardinaux third baseman hit at a torrid .583 clip as he collected 14 hits in 24 at-bats, 2 home runs, 8 RBIs and 6 runs scored.

Delatte is currently hitting at a .421 pace with 6 home runs and 25 RBIs.
