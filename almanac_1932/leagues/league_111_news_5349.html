Battalion Get One to Win
Wednesday, July 23rd, 1924
Springfield dropped a 1-0 shutout to Carleton Buchanan and the North Bay Battalion at Juniors Ballpark today. The Falcons offered little resistance to Buchanan, who stopped them on 3 hits, fanned 14 and walked 2.

"It just felt right today," the left-handed Buchanan told reporters after the win. "Even with the rain, it didn't throw me off my rhythm."

Buchanan has posted a 5-1 record for the year with a 1.82 ERA. Teams are hitting .148 against him and he has rung up 83 strikeouts in 59.1 innings.
