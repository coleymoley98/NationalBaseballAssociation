Portland Thorns Hurler WL's Premier Pitcher
Thursday, September 22nd, 1932
Chanchai Bourgault was the most outstanding pitcher in the Western League this year and today was acclaimed the Pitcher of the Year Award winner for his efforts.

Bourgault had a marvelous season, going 8-2 with an impressive 2.59 ERA in 11 starts. He struck out 30 in 90.1 innings and limited opposing batters to a .238 batting average.

He received 8 first place votes out of a possible 10. Tadamasa Moriguchi of the Vancouver Giants finished second in voting with 1 first place vote, while Martino Gutierrez of the Omaha Falcons finished third with 1 first place vote.
