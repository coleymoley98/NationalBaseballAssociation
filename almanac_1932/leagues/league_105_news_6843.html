Cheng Collects CL Rookie of the Year Award
Tuesday, October 13th, 1925
This year's best newcomer has been chosen and the 1925 Canadian League Rookie of the Year Award trophy went to Toronto right fielder Dustin Cheng.

It was a season to remember for the 21-year-old Blue Sox rookie. He wrapped up his first year with a .346 batting average, 15 doubles, 8 triples and 11 home runs. Cheng also had 53 RBIs and scored 61 times.

He received 9 first place votes out of a possible 16. Jakkapan Holland of the Wolfeburg Celtics finished second in voting with 4 first place votes, while Phuwanart McInnis of the Boston Redcoats finished third with 2 first place votes.
