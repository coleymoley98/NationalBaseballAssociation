Another Setback For Toronto's Xavier Lepage
Friday, July 6th, 1928
The Toronto Blue Sox announced today that Xavier Lepage would be missing for longer than expected as the 28-year-old left fielder recovers from plantar fascitis. "Xavier had been working out with the team recently, but felt some discomfort yesterday," the Blue Sox GM explained to the press. "After re-examining the injury, the team's medical staff decided that Lepage would need extra time to heal. Their estimate is 2 weeks more recovery time."

This year Lepage has hit at a .288 clip with 21 hits, 1 home run, 5 RBIs and 13 runs scored.
