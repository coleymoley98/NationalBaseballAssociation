Falcons Chill Knapp's Hot Streak
Saturday, July 28th, 1928
Lorne Knapp, third baseman for the Portland Thorns, gave it a good try, but his time chasing history came to an end when he went hitless in 3 tries against the Omaha Falcons today. Not only did the Falcons break his 21-game hitting streak, they also beat Portland by the score of 18-4.

"Now that the streak has ended, I can admit I'll breathe a bit easier the next time I step up to bat," Knapp said, speaking with the media after the game. "It was tough because I put a little pressure on myself. Now that it's over, I will definitely be a little bit looser at the plate."

Knapp is hitting .357 for the season with 1 home run, 24 RBIs and 22 runs scored. He has 61 hits in 171 at-bats and played in 49 games.
