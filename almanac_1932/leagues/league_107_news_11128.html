Eason Electric in 8-0 Vancouver Win
Wednesday, July 17th, 1929
"Good pitching beats good hitting" is an old baseball adage.

Well, good pitching also beats bad hitting.

Azman Eason of the Vancouver Giants proved the point at Ballpark of Kelowna today, holding the Kelowna Apple Sox to 4 hits in a complete game shutout victory.

"Eason made us look real bad today," Apple Sox manager Jesse Daniels said in his postgame interview. "He was outstanding. He was down in the zone with all his pitches. Everything was sinking."

Eason had 12 strikeouts and gave up 2 walks as the Giants took the 8-0 win.

In 15 starts this season Eason has crafted a 7-5 record with a 1.79 ERA.
