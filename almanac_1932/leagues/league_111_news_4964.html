Lepage Cracks 5 Hits
Monday, June 2nd, 1924
A.J. Lepage really sizzled at the plate in today's 11-9 win over Mountaineers. The Buffalo first baseman blistered Morgantown for 5 hits in 5 at-bats.

"Lepage was too tough to get out today," the Mountaineers skipper said in the postgame interview. "No matter what we threw him, he hit it. And he hit some good pitches, too."

A.J. Lepage tripled in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 2nd, hit an RBI single in the 4th, doubled in the 6th and hit a solo-shot off Nattaporn Piller in the 8th.
