Champs Crowned: Winnipeg Jr. Thunder
Monday, July 16th, 1928
Members of the Winnipeg Jr. Thunder say winning the title is special, but ultimately they will only be remembered with a championship season.

"We want to put ourselves in the history books and win the Dumont Cup," said closer Komkrit Kunyarat. "We all believe it can happen, it's just a matter of putting in the hard work and getting the breaks."

It's the 4th division win for the club and they will be vying for their 3rd Dumont Cup win.

"To see the team get here, it makes you believe in yourself and all the systems you've put in place," said manager Rusty Dang.
