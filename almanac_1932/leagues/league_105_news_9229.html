Chicagou White Sox Ship Boudreau to Quebec
Sunday, November 20th, 1927
Done deal! The Chicagou White Sox have agreed to send 26-year-old C Kritsana Boudreau and 23-year-old minor league C Todd Ribar to the Quebec Nordiques for 32-year-old RHP Sommai Ratté. The trade, rumored for days, was completed in the wee hours this morning.


During his career Boudreau has hit .315 with 457 hits, 16 home runs, 216 RBIs and scored 204 runs.

In his career, Ratté has crafted a 112-97 mark with a 3.71 ERA and tossed 1867 innings. Opposing clubs are batting .287 against him and he has 507 strikeouts.
