ABL Honors Garnier, Best Hitter in July
Friday, August 1st, 1924
Even though he is a newcomer to Atlantic Baseball League, Jordane Garnier put up some good numbers in July and earned the ABL Batter of the Month award.

He was one of Sherbrooke's offensive leaders with a .398 average in 21 games. He also racked up 6 home runs, 14 RBIs, and 19 runs scored.

Garnier is currently hitting at a .317 pace with 12 home runs, 27 RBIs and has scored 36 times.
