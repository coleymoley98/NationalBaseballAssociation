Monday, June 16th, 1924
Crawford Jones of the Montreal Jr. Royales spun his magic on the Pointe-Sainte-Anne Acadiens today. He blanked them 8-0 on 3 hits, registering 9 strikeouts and no walks.

"Crawford pitched really well but this was a solid performance by everyone," he said.
