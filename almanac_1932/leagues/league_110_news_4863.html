Injury Brings Season to End for Watt
Tuesday, May 6th, 1924
Withaya Watt verified reports that he is probably heading to the injured list. The injury that has kept the Saskatoon reliever out of action since 05/03/1924 -- a partially torn ulnar collateral ligament -- is significant enough, he said, that he will have to "shut it down for a while." How long? "Probably 8-9 months," he said.
