Minnesota Lose, 6-0, to Nordiques, Potter
Thursday, August 16th, 1928
Ira Potter kept the opposition off the scoreboard and the Quebec Nordiques were all smiles after winning 6-0 against the Minnesota Saints at Field of the Saints today. The right-handed Potter gave up no runs in his start, permitted 6 hits and had 3 strikeouts.

"If there was a stat for 'team shutout,' this would be it," Potter said later. "My catcher called a great game, guys delivered at the plate and when I messed something up the guys behind me were where they needed to be."

In 21 starts this season Potter has recorded a 8-12 record and a 3.21 ERA.
