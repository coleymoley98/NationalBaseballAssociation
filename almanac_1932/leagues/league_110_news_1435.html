Pioneers Plugged by Parish, Mounties, 4-0
Thursday, June 11th, 1931
Pitakpong Parish called it a "mixture of smarts and luck," but whatever the combination, it added up to a complete game shutout of the Lincoln Pioneers.

The Adams Mounties right-hander blanked the Pioneers 4-0 at Field of the Juniors today, allowing 5 hits and collecting 13 strikeouts. He walked 2.
