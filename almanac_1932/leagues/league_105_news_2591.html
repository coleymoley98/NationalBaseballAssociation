Aucoin on a Tear, Hits for Cycle
Sunday, May 15th, 1932
Finishing with 4 hits is a great day for any player, but Jean-Michel Aucoin made it even more special by hitting for the cycle at Ballpark of Quebec today.

Aucoin had 1 single, 1 double, 1 triple and 1 home run for the Quebec Nordiques and led them to a 6-5 decision over the Chicagou Frontenacs. The right fielder also finished with 3 runs scored and 2 runs batted in.

"Awesome," Aucoin said when asked how the accomplishment felt.

Jean-Michel Aucoin flied out in the 1st, tripled in the 4th, hit a two-run home run off Eric Woodmore in the 6th, doubled in the 8th, walked in the 10th and singled in the 13th.

Aucoin is batting at a .500 pace this season, picking up 8 hits in 16 at-bats with 2 home runs, 3 RBIs and 4 runs scored.
