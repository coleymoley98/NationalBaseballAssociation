5-for-6 Game for Panyarachun
Tuesday, June 19th, 1923
Springfield right fielder Xuwicha Panyarachun had a day to remember today, 5-for-6 against the Hamilton Jr. Steelers at Jr. Steelers Ballpark as the Falcons picked up a 17-2 victory.

"He was patient up there today," Springfield skipper Cal Mentink told the press after the game. "I like the way he waited for his pitch and wasn't overly aggressive. Panyarachun has a tendency to chase bad balls sometimes."

Xuwicha Panyarachun singled in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, grounded out in the 5th, hit an RBI single in the 6th, hit a two-run home run off Joseph Jolivet in the 8th and hit a two-run double in the 9th.

Panyarachun is currently batting .324 with 8 home runs and 15 RBIs.
