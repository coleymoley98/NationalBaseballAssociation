Saturday, August 31st, 1929
"Not what I wanted to hear," said Wolfeburg's Mathéo Jobin to reporters this afternoon, as he revealed that the injury he sustained on 08/28/1929 would force him to miss the rest of the season. "The doctor made it clear," Jobin said. "There's no chance for me -- none at all -- to come back this year. That's the last thing I wanted to hear." Jobin has been out of action since he suffered a torn flexor tendon in his elbow.

This season Jobin has worked 149.1 innings with 45 strikeouts en route to a 7-13 record and a 4.76 ERA.
