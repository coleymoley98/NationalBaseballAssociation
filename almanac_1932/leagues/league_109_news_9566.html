Harrisburg No Match for Daves
Friday, June 1st, 1928
Martin Daves of the Sherbrooke Nordiques humiliated the Harrisburg Robins at Nordiques Ballpark today, spinning a complete game shutout and holding the visitors to 5 hits.

"He stayed aggressive and his ball was really moving," said Harrisburg manager Cedric Chéron, after the game.

Daves whiffed 8 and walked 2 in the 4-0 win.

"If I make good pitches, I'm going to get some outs," Daves said. "I was making good pitches the entire game."
