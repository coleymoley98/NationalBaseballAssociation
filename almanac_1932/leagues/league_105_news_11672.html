Hall of Fame Selection announcement coming next week
Wednesday, December 25th, 1929
The 1930 National Baseball Association Hall of Fame class will soon be welcomed into baseball history.

The leading candidate for enshrinement is former first round draft pick Von Moore, who put up an impressive 13-year career.

Moore retired in 1925 at the age of 34, and said if elected he plans to go in as a member of the Toronto Blue Sox.

Voters are reminded that ballots may be cast up until the votes are announced on January 1st, 1930.

In his career Moore has compiled a .329 batting average and collected 1336 hits, 14 home runs and 491 RBIs.
