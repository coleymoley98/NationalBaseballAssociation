Derricotte Rockets 3 HR Against Quincy
Monday, July 18th, 1932
Coude third baseman Ari Derricotte blasted three homers today at Coude Grounds in the 5-4 Voyageurs victory over the Quincy Gunners.

For the game, Ari Derricotte scored 3 times and knocked in 4 runs. When he hit his last home run, his teammates, hearing the crack of the bat, leaped off the bench and rushed on the field to greet the jubilant Derricotte when he reached home plate.

"It was just one game, but it sure was a lot of fun -- 4-for-5, three roundtrippers and a win," a gleeful Derricotte told BNN reporters in a postgame interview. "What a day."

Ari Derricotte hit a solo-shot off Matthias Newstrom in the 1st, grounded out in the 3rd, singled in the 6th, hit a two-run home run off Jordie Godbout in the 8th and hit a solo-shot off Liam Fillion in the 11th.

Presently Derricotte is batting .331 with 54 hits and 9 home runs. He has knocked in 35 runs and scored 29 times.
