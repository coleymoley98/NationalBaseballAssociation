Calgary Loses Woods for Season
Sunday, August 16th, 1925
"Not what I wanted to hear," said Calgary's Teerapong Woods to reporters this afternoon, as he revealed that the injury he sustained on 08/15/1925 would force him to miss the rest of the season. "The doctor made it clear," Woods said. "There's no chance for me -- none at all -- to come back this year. That's the last thing I wanted to hear." Woods has been out of action since he suffered a broken bone in the elbow.

To date in the season, Woods is 0-0 with 9 relief appearances and no saves. He has held opposing batters to a .516 average, pitched 10.2 innings, struck out 8 and walked 15 while recording a 22.78 ERA.
