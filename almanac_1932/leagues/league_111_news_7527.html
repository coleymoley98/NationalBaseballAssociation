Legault Ups Hit Streak to 20 Consecutive Games
Wednesday, July 14th, 1926
The streak is alive and well -- at least for today.

Oliver Legault did it again, bashing 1 hit in the contest at Juniors Ballpark today. For the last 20 games in a row, the 18-year-old first baseman has collected at least one hit. His efforts helped the Morgantown Mountaineers beat the Toronto Jr. Blue Sox 5-3 today.
