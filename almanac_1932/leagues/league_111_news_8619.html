EBL Votes Jr. Steelers Usher Top Player
Monday, July 4th, 1927
So far in his young career, Colton Usher has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Eastern Baseball League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 18-year-old Hamilton Jr. Steelers second baseman hit at a torrid .536 clip as he collected 15 hits in 28 at-bats, no home runs, 6 RBIs and 7 runs scored.

Usher has a .383 average and a .442 on-base percentage for the year. He has picked up 44 hits, 1 home run and driven in 13 runs.
