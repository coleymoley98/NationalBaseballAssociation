EL Announces Player of the Week
Monday, August 23rd, 1926
His hard work and hustle paid off this week. The hard-working and hard-playing Adrian McNeely was rewarded with the Eastern League Player of the Week honors.

The scrappy Trois-Rivieres Royales right fielder hit .357 while collecting 10 hits in 28 at-bats, 4 home runs and 10 RBIs.

For the season McNeely has produced 74 hits in 245 at-bats for a .302 average with 13 home runs and gotten on base at a .383 clip.
