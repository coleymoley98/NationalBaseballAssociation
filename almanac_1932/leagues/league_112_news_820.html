Jr. Royales Captures Wild Card
Saturday, July 26th, 1930
The Montreal Jr. Royales fell short of a Saint Lawrence crown, but they have still clinched a spot in the playoffs.

Manager Calvin Pitts said regardless of how his team gets into the postseason he thinks they have a good shot at bringing home the Laurier Cup championship.

"We just have to concentrate on playing good baseball at the right time," he said.
