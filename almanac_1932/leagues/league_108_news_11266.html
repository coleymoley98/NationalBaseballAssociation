Eskimos Flame Out Against Lafond, Giants
Friday, August 2nd, 1929
Alfie Lafond kept the Edmonton Eskimos hitters off balance all game long as the Vancouver Giants pitcher blanked the Eskimos, 2-0.

Lafond gave up 6 hits, struck out 9 and walked 2. In the postgame press conference, the right-hander was reluctant to talk about the outing at length.

"It was a nice win in a long season," he said. "I don't want to dwell too much on it."

This season in 13 starts Alfie Lafond has a record of 2-6 with 94 strikeouts in 94.2 innings and a 4.18 ERA.
