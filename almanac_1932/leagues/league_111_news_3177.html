Buffalo Win Division Finals
Saturday, July 30th, 1932
The Buffalo Rapids will continue the trek towards a Adams Cup title, while the Morgantown Mountaineers are headed home for the season.

Buffalo won the Division Finals with a clinching game 4-1 victory at Ballpark of Buffalo, concluding the series at 3-1.

"We didn't want this thing to go any further, that's for sure," Pete Kearns said after officially being named series MVP.

He turned in a dominant offensive performance to win the award, with a .389 batting average, .389 on-base percentage, 1 home runs, 2 RBI and 3 runs scored.

The Buffalo Rapids will play against the North Bay Battalion in the next round. North Bay won their series against the Springfield Falcons 3 games to 0. The Adams Cup schedule will be announced after the Division Finals finishes.
