Ottawa Indians Ship O'Neill to Montreal
Wednesday, December 17th, 1924
The Ottawa Indians announced a deal with the Royales today, sending 28-year-old RHP Andy O'Neill to Montreal for 19-year-old minor league 1B Aldo MacNally, 22-year-old minor league SS Terapat Ramsey, 18-year-old minor league RHP Christian Carpenter and 22-year-old RHP Andrew Patterson. It had been talked about for the past few weeks and everything came together late last night. All players are expected to be joining their respective clubs shortly.


O'Neill has rolled up a 63-58 career record and he boasts a lifetime ERA of 4.23.
