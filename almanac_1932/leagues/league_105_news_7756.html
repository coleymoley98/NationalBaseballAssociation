Detroit Blanks Frontenacs, Sumonrat at His Best
Saturday, August 14th, 1926
Magic was in the air and on the mound at Bennett Park today as Panuwat Sumonrat held the Chicagou Frontenacs spellbound in his 2-0 shutout victory. The Detroit Lions pitcher went the distance, rang up 4 strikeouts, walked 2 and held the Frontenacs to 6 hits to garner the win.

"He just had us in his thrall," Chicagou manager Jetsadaporn Sheerin said later. "We couldn't put anything together against him."

In pitching stats this season Sumonrat has rung up 10 wins and 8 losses in 20 games. He has an ERA of 2.51 and has fanned 66 batters in 165 innings.
