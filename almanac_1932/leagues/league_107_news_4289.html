Skipper Aiton Shown the Door in Abbotsford
Saturday, August 4th, 1923
Weeks of speculation were officially ended today when it was announced that Suthep Aiton has been dismissed as the manager of the Abbotsford Alpines.

"I can't really say I'm surprised. I saw this coming for a while, but I guess there was nothing much I could really do to prevent it," Suthep Aiton told BNN. "It's not easy to manage when every decision you make is closely scrutinized and criticized, but that's what I dealt with."

A team official said the firing was the end result of "major and irreconcilable differences of opinion" between Aiton and the front office. When pressed for more information, Abbotsford general manager Joe Unknown skirted the issue.

"We're not going to get into that right now. It's not really important why he was fired -- we're just going in a different direction," he said.

BNN originally reported Aiton's tenure with the club was in jeopardy three weeks ago, which team officials at the time dismissed as "just a rumor."
