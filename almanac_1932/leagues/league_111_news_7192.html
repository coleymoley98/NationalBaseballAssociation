5-Hit Blitz by Springfield's Beshel
Thursday, June 3rd, 1926
Some moments become linked with a particular player's career forever. Luca Beshel of the Springfield Falcons had one of those moments when he went 5-for-6 at the plate today in the 10-9 win over the Buffalo Rapids at Ballpark of Buffalo.

"I won't forget this anytime soon, and I don't expect it to happen too often," the 16-year-old left fielder told reporters in the postgame interview. "I'll be happy to just get a hit or two every day. Enough to hang around the league a while longer. Like 10, maybe 15 more years."

Luca Beshel hit an RBI single in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, doubled in the 5th, singled in the 7th, singled in the 8th and flied out in the 9th.
