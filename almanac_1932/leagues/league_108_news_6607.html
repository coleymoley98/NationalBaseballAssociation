Millette Pitches Thunder to Shutout Victory
Thursday, August 13th, 1925
Winnipeg Thunder pitcher Songpole Millette really got it done at Thunder Stadium today in his 3-0 shutout victory over the Edmonton Eskimos, shutting them down on 6 hits.

"It was just one of those games where everything went right. I threw a lot of strikes," Millette said. "And the pitches they did get to hit, they didn't do anything with them."

The 19-year-old issued 3 walks and had 2 strikeouts in the Thunder win.

In season stats Millette shows a 5-6 record in 14 games, 90.2 innings, 35 strikeouts and a 5.66 ERA.
