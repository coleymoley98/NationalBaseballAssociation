Robins End Rodríguez's Streak
Friday, August 26th, 1932
Albany left fielder Elias Rodríguez came into today's game against the Harrisburg Robins on a hot streak. He left on a cold one.

The Robins did their jobs well, beating the Albany Rebels 7-3 and holding Rodríguez hitless to snap his 24-game hitting streak. The 23-year-old left fielder had a few words to say after finishing 0-for-1, telling reporters only that he "came up short."
