Mojica Grabs WL Reliever of the Year Award
Friday, September 17th, 1926
The manager of the Calgary Wranglers said he always had confidence handing the ball to Bruno Mojica out of the bullpen.

The 28-year-old was a dominant performer all year for Calgary and his efforts have been rewarded with the Western League Reliever of the Year Award for 1926.

The bullpen ace had an impressive 5.54 ERA this season, with no saves in 21 relief appearances. He fanned 23 batters in 39 innings and sported a record of 1 win and 1 loss.

He ended up tied with Wutthisak Pohji with 32 total votes and 32 first place votes.
