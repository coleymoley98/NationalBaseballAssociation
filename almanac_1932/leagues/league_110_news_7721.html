Jr. Saints Give Manager Daniels the Brush-Off
Saturday, August 7th, 1926
Jesse Daniels let everybody know how he felt after being released by the Minneapolis Jr. Saints. He didn't hold his tongue.

"It's a bush town with a bush press and a bush management team," said the enraged Daniels. "Politics is the blame for this decision. I still had the support of the players."

The sacking leaves Minneapolis looking for a new manager, with many names being linked to the opening.
