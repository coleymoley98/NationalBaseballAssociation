Montreal Jr. Royales Capture Laurier Cup
Sunday, August 14th, 1927
The Montreal Jr. Royales were good enough to get past the Quincy Gunners at Juniors Ballpark today and secure the 5th championship in franchise history.

"When you wear Jr. Royales on your shirt, you're good at something," Montreal skipper Calvin Pitts told the press.

Jr. Royales first baseman Jakkapan McNeely was named MVP of the series after he went .370 with 4 home runs and 14 RBI.

"It feels totally incredible," McNeely said. "I could live to 1,000 years old and I'd remember this moment."

The new champions capped off a great season with a 3-0 victory over the Gunners to win the Laurier Cup 4-3.
