Dang Forced out as Manager in Winnipeg
Friday, August 5th, 1932
There was not a dry eye in the Winnipeg Jr. Thunder clubhouse today as word spread of Rusty Dang's dismissal as the team's manager.

Despite the team's recent woes, Dang was widely respected.

"He was a real player's manager, a real leader and mentor to so many of the guys on this club," said one player.

Another player had the same sentiment. "Man, this ain't right. I know baseball is a business, but Rusty deserved better."
