CBL Star of the Week Honored
Monday, July 9th, 1928
The premier performer in the Central Baseball League this week was Gunner Nalley of the Davenport Rivermen.

The hot-hitting 18-year-old first baseman put up a .387 average and .444 on-base percentage, piling up 12 hits in 31 at-bats, 3 home runs, 11 RBIs and 7 runs scored.

That kind of hitting got him the CBL Player of the Week honors.

Nalley is hitting at a .301 mark this season. His stats show 7 home runs, 34 RBIs and 20 runs scored. He has played 36 games for the Rivermen.
