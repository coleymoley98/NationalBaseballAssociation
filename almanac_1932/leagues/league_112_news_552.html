Big Day with Bat: Winther 6-for-7
Sunday, June 29th, 1930
Cayden Winther got the glory and the headlines today, but the Montreal Jr. Royales left with the win. The Quincy second baseman easily handled the Jr. Royales pitching, going 6-for-7, but his Quincy Gunners came out on the short end of the 21-20 score.

Winther told BNN reporters in the postgame interview he would happily trade "three or four" of his hits for a win.

"Heck, maybe even all of them," he said with a laugh. "Right now, it just seems like one loss in a long season but who knows what it could mean at the end."

Cayden Winther singled in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, hit an RBI single in the 5th, struck out in the 6th, hit a three-run double in the 7th, hit a solo-shot off Thimotée Marie in the 9th and singled in the 10th.

So far on the season Winther is hitting .250 with 3 home runs, 15 RBIs and 14 runs scored. He has 25 hits and played in 26 games.
