Montreal Secures Top Spot in National League East
Saturday, September 5th, 1931
The Montreal Royales have silenced their critics, backed up those who believed in them and won the National League East.

"You always think you've got a chance to win the division, but until you get it done you never really know how things are going to play out," said starting pitcher Bunnawat Phomee. "I think we're all grateful to get the chance to vie for the Riel Cup."

It is the 13th division crown for the franchise and manager Hervé Morel said in a sporting landscape where you are judged by what you've most recently accomplished, "it feels incredibly good to be at the top of the pile."
