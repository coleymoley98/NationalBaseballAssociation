Red Sox Blank St. John's, Hastie Winner
Sunday, July 8th, 1923
In an outstanding mound performance, Pawtucket Red Sox starter Gray Hastie fired a complete-game shutout against the St. John's Mariners at Juniors Ballpark today.

Hastie was the key to the Red Sox's 3-0 win. The 16-year-old right-hander was in high spirits on the mount for much of the game, his mood largely undented by the 4 hits and 1 walk he allowed.

"4 hits?" he said with a laugh following the game. "Listen, I'd be happy with a 30-hitter as long as it was a shutout."

This year Hastie has posted a record of 6 wins and 3 losses with a 2.84 ERA. He has appeared in 9 games and struck out 48 in 63.1 innings.
