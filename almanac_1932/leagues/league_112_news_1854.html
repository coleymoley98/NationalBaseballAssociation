Voltigeurs Win Tight Saint Lawrence Race
Saturday, July 25th, 1931
Eli Boyer said it takes "a lot of dedication and sacrifice" to win a division title. That's why he and his Drummondville Voltigeurs teammates were so excited to celebrate their Saint Lawrence championship today.

"It's been a team effort all year long," he said. "You don't get to this point without the guys in the room believing in what we're doing."

That belief has paced the club to a 32-18 record on the season. It is the club's 5th Saint Lawrence championship and puts them in position for their 2nd Atlantic Baseball League championship.

"It's a talented bunch in that room," manager Tai Grigsby said pointing to his celebrating players. "I wouldn't bet against us."
