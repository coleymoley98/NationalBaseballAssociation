Toronto Braves Ship Lacroixille to Minnesota
Sunday, July 12th, 1931
Both sides appear content with the trade finalized today that will send 30-year-old RHP Jiramet Lacroixille to the Minnesota Saints, while the Toronto Braves will receive 25-year-old RF Zat Jones.

"This is a move that filled a few gaps in our roster," said Chuachen Rattanakool, Braves general manager.

For his part, the Saints GM said the trade was made "in the best interest of our organization's future." BNN analysts reported that on paper the trade seemed to be a plus for both ball clubs. The players involved are expected to join their new teams for the next scheduled game.


This year Lacroixille has a 7-2 record with a 4.44 ERA in 13 starts.

So far this season Jones has gotten 25 hits in 84 at-bats for a .298 batting average with no home runs. His stats include 11 RBIs and 3 runs scored. He has played in 27 games.
