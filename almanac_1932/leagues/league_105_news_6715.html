Altsman Earns August's Top Batter Prize in NL
Tuesday, September 1st, 1925
Maverick Altsman may be much younger than other hitters in the National League, but he performed like a seasoned veteran in August to win the Batter of the Month award.

The 21-year-old Detroit second baseman hit .341 with 6 home runs, 20 RBIs and 19 runs scored.

This year Altsman has hit at a .294 clip with 96 hits, 10 home runs, 52 RBIs and 54 runs scored.
