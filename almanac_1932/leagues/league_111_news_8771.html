Pioneers Celebrate Wild Card Win
Thursday, July 21st, 1927
It's time to start thinking about the playoffs for the Fort Wayne Pioneers.

The West club today secured a wild card berth, which starting pitcher Frankie Richir said is something the club is "extremely proud of," even though they had hoped to win the division.

With a record of 26-21, the Pioneers won't be going into the playoffs as favorites, but Richir said he believes the Pioneers have a good chance to bring home the franchise's 9th Adams Cup.
