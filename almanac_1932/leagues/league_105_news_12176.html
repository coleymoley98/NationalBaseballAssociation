Will Louis Mongeau be elected to the National Baseball Association Hall of Fame?
Wednesday, December 28th, 1932
With the 1933 National Baseball Association Hall of Fame announcement next week, fans are eagerly looking forward to seeing how many electors will vote for Louis Mongeau.

With an impressive 17-year career, Cincinnati Reds fans are busy making the trek to hear his name announced as a first-ballot Hall of Famer.

While not the only name on the ballot, Mongeau is widely expected to be the top name when results are announced.

Eligible voters can cast their ballots until the results are announced on January 4th, 1933.

In his career Mongeau has chalked up 204 wins and 219 losses and compiled a 3.45 ERA.
