Adams Shut Out, 3-0, By Jr. Falcons, Horner
Monday, June 16th, 1930
In an outstanding mound performance, Omaha Jr. Falcons starter Bilal Horner fired a complete-game shutout against the Adams Mounties at Ballpark of Adams today.

Horner was the key to the Jr. Falcons's 3-0 win. The 17-year-old right-hander was in high spirits on the mount for much of the game, his mood largely undented by the 7 hits and 1 walk he allowed.

"7 hits?" he said with a laugh following the game. "Listen, I'd be happy with a 30-hitter as long as it was a shutout."

Horner has taken the mound for 5 relief appearances this season, nailing down 1 save while notching a 2-0 record with a 4.38 ERA. The Omaha reliever has limited opposing batters to a .269 batting average.
