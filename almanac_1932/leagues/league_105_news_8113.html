Thursday, January 6th, 1927
Royales officials presented Lashawn Montano to members of the local print media today as their new reliever.

The deal, worth $5,600, guarantees the 32-year-old a home in Montreal for 1 year. It should improve the ballclub, assuming he plays to his potential.
