Tienphosuwan Sets Season Mark with Hit #40
Tuesday, March 25th, 1924
The sensational season continued for Supphakorn Tienphosuwan at Guadalajara Memorial Coliseum. In today's game, Tienphosuwan broke the single-season hit record established by Brody Behrendt. It was hit number 40 for the Pittsburgh Liberty's second baseman. Tienphosuwan called it "a moment I'll remember the rest of my life."

Supphakorn Tienphosuwan doubled in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 3rd and singled in the 4th.

This season Tienphosuwan is swinging the bat at a .519 rate with 40 hits in 77 at-bats. To date he has hit 3 home runs, scored 24 runs and driven in 30.
