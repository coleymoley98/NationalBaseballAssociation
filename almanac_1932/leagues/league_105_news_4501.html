Redcoats Arnon Roberge to Miss Year
Sunday, September 9th, 1923
Doctors in Boston said Arnon Roberge might not play anymore this year. The Redcoats center fielder suffered a separated shoulder during a game against the New York Rebels and will be out for at least 3 weeks. Club officials told BNN they do not expect him to return to the team this season.

Roberge is currently hitting at a .312 pace with 2 home runs, 55 RBIs and has scored 74 times.
