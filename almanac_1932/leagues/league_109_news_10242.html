Elkins 5-for-5 Against Nordiques
Wednesday, September 5th, 1928
It was high-fives all around for Richie Elkins at Trois-Rivieres Grounds today.

He got them from his teammates and he got them at the plate during an outstanding hitting performance against the Sherbrooke Nordiques. Elkins smashed 5 hits in 5 at-bats on his monster day, leading his club to a 7-4 win.

"Richie was hitting everything," said Royales manager Jeremy Anglin. "It really was a special game for him."

In the opposing dugout, Nordiques manager Malik Hill told BNN beat writers that Elkins "ambushed" the club.

"Obviously we didn't have much success against him, but we'll have another chance," he said. "Hopefully, Elkins oversleeps next time."

Richie Elkins singled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, hit an RBI single in the 5th, hit a solo-shot off Martin Daves in the 7th and singled in the 8th.

Elkins is presently hitting at a .254 pace with 9 home runs, 36 RBIs and has scored 37 times.
