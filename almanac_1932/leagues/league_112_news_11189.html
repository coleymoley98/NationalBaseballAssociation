Voltigeurs Win Tight Saint Lawrence Race
Wednesday, July 24th, 1929
It was far from a foregone conclusion, but the Drummondville Voltigeurs are now champions of the Saint Lawrence.

"We battled, day in and day out," said closer Milan Flamand. "This is one to savor, as it was close right down to the end and that puts a lot of pressure on a team."

With a record of 28-21, the Voltigeurs have officially won their 4th division crown.

Manager Tai Grigsby said there's "a lot of pride and excitement" involved in coming out on top of the Saint Lawrence.

The club will now be looking to win the 2nd Atlantic Baseball League championship in franchise history.
