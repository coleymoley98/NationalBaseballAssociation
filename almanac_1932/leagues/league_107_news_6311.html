St-Onge Goes 5-for-5
Thursday, July 2nd, 1925
Even as a kid, Sivakorn St-Onge said his coaches told him the key to hitting was making contact, and letting everything else flow from there. At Ballpark of Kelowna today, St-Onge showed how well he learned that lesson.

The 17-year-old second baseman had 5 hits in 5 at-bats for the Kelowna Apple Sox, helping his team knock off the Ranier Seahawks by a score of 7-5.

"They always said, just keep your eye on the ball, don't overswing, make contact and you never know what will happen," he said. "That's all I did today."

Sivakorn St-Onge singled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, singled in the 7th and singled in the 8th.
