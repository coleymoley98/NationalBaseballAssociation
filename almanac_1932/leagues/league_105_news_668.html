Morikawa's Hit Streak Reaches 20 Games
Tuesday, July 8th, 1930
Takeaki Morikawa continued his streak at League Park today, even as the ballgame did not go his way. The Boston second baseman was 2-for-4 in today's contest and now has hit safely in his last 20 games. The Redcoats were not as fortunate, losing 10-7 to the Wolfeburg Celtics.

"Morikawa was hitting everything we threw at him," said Wolfeburg manager Chaichan Phonsa. "He is a tough out. Fortunately, things went our way in the ballgame today and we came away with a win."
Takeaki Morikawa singled in the 1st, struck out in the 2nd, doubled in the 5th and flied out in the 7th.

Thus far this year Morikawa is hitting .367 with 2 home runs, 39 RBIs and 48 runs scored.
