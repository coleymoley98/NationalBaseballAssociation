Fredericks's Condition Worse, Suffers Another Setback
Tuesday, July 5th, 1932
The Philadelphia Mets plan to take it slow with center fielder Wilfred Fredericks, knowing that his recovery from a strained abdominal muscle would likely include a few bumps in the road. A team spokesman told BNN, "Wilfred has up days and down days. It's still a process. He was feeling really good and then he had a step back. The doctors say it will be about one week before he can be activated."

Thus far this year Fredericks has hit .283 with no home runs and 3 RBIs.
