Jarikanon Goes Yard 3 Times
Wednesday, May 7th, 1930
In a powerful performance that left the fans at SunTrust Park buzzing, Toronto Braves shortstop Sanun Jarikanon blasted a trio of home runs en route to a 13-11 win over the Juneau Brasseurs.

"Everything was clicking," Jarikanon told the press. "My timing was good and I got pitches I could handle and I didn't miss them."

Jarikanon shined brilliantly in the win, with 4 hits in 6 at-bats, 7 RBIs and 4 runs scored.

"It's not just one guy, it's everybody," Braves manager Peerapong Owens said. "Sanun played great but it takes more than one guy to win a game."

Sanun Jarikanon singled in the 1st, hit a three-run home run off Dirk Edwards in the 2nd, hit a two-run home run off Dirk Edwards in the 4th, struck out in the 6th, grounded out in the 9th and hit a two-run home run off Stephen Côté in the 10th.
