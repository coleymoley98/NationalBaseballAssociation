Leelapayachon Breaks All-Time Hits Record!
Thursday, March 19th, 1931
It's the talk of the baseball world: Peerapong Leelapayachon of the Team Saskatchewan has set a new record for all-time hits with 82. The record was previously held by Valéry Jetté. He recorded the record-breaker in a contest against the Hamilton Steelers at Ciudad Mexico Stadium today.

"It all seems unreal," Leelapayachon said, seemingly rendered nearly speechless by the moment. "The tributes, the fanfare. It's unreal."

Peerapong Leelapayachon singled in the 1st.

This season in 10 games Leelapayachon is batting .405 with no home runs, 3 RBIs and has scored 8 times.
