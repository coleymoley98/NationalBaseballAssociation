Padungchok Beats Rebels 8-3 for 100th Win
Monday, September 3rd, 1923
It was a great day for Suttipong Padungchok today at Polo Grounds -- he picked up victory number 100 to join the great pitchers of the past in the exclusive 100 Club. The Ottawa ace stopped the New York Rebels 8-3, yielding 7 hits and recording 3 strikeouts and 2 walks in his milestone game.

"100 wins puts you in some good company," Padungchok told reporters. "I'm honored to be there."

Over his career Padungchok has 100 victories and 84 defeats with a 3.43 ERA. The 32-year-old hurler also has struck out 731 batters in 1744.2 innings and surrendered 1826 hits.
