Bad News for Robins, Vasquez Injured
Thursday, July 5th, 1923
The Baltimore Robins will have to make do without Suriya Vasquez for 7 weeks. He suffered a fractured cheekbone while being hit by a pitch in the game today against the Ottawa Indians. His teammates, understandably, were upset about losing the second baseman. "That's a tough break for him," one of them said. "But this team is resilient. We will just fill in until he gets back."

This year Vasquez has socked 59 hits in 194 at-bats for a .304 average with 1 home run. He is getting on base at a .358 pace and his totals also include 22 RBIs and 27 runs scored.
