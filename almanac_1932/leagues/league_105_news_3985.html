Canadian League Names Rookie of Month
Sunday, July 1st, 1923
Ambler Stahl is still a bit shy, but then, who could blame the newest Canadian League Rookie of the Month honoree? He hasn't been around the league long enough to fully know the ropes yet, despite the fact that he's putting up veteran-like numbers.

In June the Ottawa hitter tagged opposing pitching for a .371 average with 33 hits, 3 home runs and 13 RBIs.

This year Stahl is batting .364 with 4 home runs. He has scored 30 runs and driven in 27.
