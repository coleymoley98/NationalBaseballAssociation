Sometimes a change of scenery can be a beneficial to a baseball player. That is the hope in Philadelphia and Toronto as the teams came to an agreement today which sends 25-year-old C Mario Troutman to the Blue Sox and 27-year-old LHP Cooper Yuschak to the Liberty. "Only time will tell if this is the right move for everyone," stated the Toronto GM.


Looking at his career stats, Troutman is batting .307 and has totaled 9 home runs.

His career record is 51 victories and 45 defeats. Yuschak also has struck out 277 batters and compiled a 3.53 ERA.
