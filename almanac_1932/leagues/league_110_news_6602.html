CBL Hands Out Reliever of the Year Award
Wednesday, August 12th, 1925
At the young age of only 18, Watanyu Gonsombut has been named the Central Baseball League Reliever of the Year Award winner for 1925.

The future appears bright for the Saskatoon Jr. Coyotes relief ace, who captured the award with 7 saves in 22 relief appearances, 66 strikeouts in 40 innings and a record of 3-1 to go with his 2.25 ERA.

He received 8 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Aktaporn Pathomattakul of the Adams Mounties finished second in voting, while Leroy Mobley of the Lincoln Pioneers finished third.
