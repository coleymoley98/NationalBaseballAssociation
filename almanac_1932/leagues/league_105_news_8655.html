Saints Get No Resistance from Lions in 11-0 Beat-Down
Saturday, July 9th, 1927
Nathan Voisin kept the opposition off the scoreboard and the Minnesota Saints were all smiles after winning 11-0 against the Detroit Lions at Bennett Park today. The left-handed Voisin gave up no runs in his start, permitted 8 hits and had 6 strikeouts.

"If there was a stat for 'team shutout,' this would be it," Voisin said later. "My catcher called a great game, guys delivered at the plate and when I messed something up the guys behind me were where they needed to be."

For this year Voisin has chalked up 3 wins and 3 losses in 6 starts with 22 strikeouts in 49.1 innings and a 4.01 ERA.
