Uribe Goes Cycling
Saturday, August 18th, 1923
Joey Uribe had a banner day at the plate today, stringing together all four legs of the cycle as the Calgary Wranglers beat the Vancouver Giants, 33-15.

Uribe drilled 4 hits in 7 at-bats, collecting 7 runs batted in and scoring 3 runs to capture the win for Calgary.

"I was fortunate to see some good pitches and I was fortunate to be able to make the most of them," he said later. "It was a good day for us."

Joey Uribe singled in the 1st, hit a three-run triple in the 2nd, walked in the 3rd, grounded out in the 4th, grounded out in the 5th, doubled in the 7th, hit a three-run home run off Busta Randle in the 7th and struck out in the 8th.

This season Uribe is batting .327 with 18 hits, 1 home run, 11 RBIs, .413 OBP and has scored 13 runs.
