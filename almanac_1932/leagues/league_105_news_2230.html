Toronto Takes Division Series
Monday, September 21st, 1931
All playoffs end with joy on one side and despair on the other. Today, the Toronto Blue Sox are the ones feeling overwhelmed with excitement, and the Ottawa Indians are the ones thinking about what they could have done differently.

The Blue Sox took a 13-5 victory at Lincoln Park today, which gave Toronto a 3-1 series win.

With the win, Toronto will advance to play in the League Chamionship Series.

"We aren't ready to go home yet," said Blue Sox third baseman Santipab Wohlers, who was named series MVP.

The Toronto Blue Sox don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Wolfeburg Celtics - Boston Redcoats series. Boston leads the series 2 game to 1. The League Chamionship Series schedule will be announced after the Division Series finishes.
