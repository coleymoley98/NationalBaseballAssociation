Jr. Blue Sox End Shaw's Streak
Tuesday, July 24th, 1928
Andrew Shaw's 22-game hitting streak has now come and gone. It was snapped today as the Gallant Black Sox suffered a 17-13 setback against the Toronto Jr. Blue Sox.

Shaw went without a hit in 3 trips to the plate. He was visibly upset during a postgame press conference, but it appeared that the streak had little to do with it.

"The hitting streak was one of those things where it was fun while it lasted, but I'm not going to lose any sleep about it tonight," he said. "On the other hand, I am going to lose sleep about dropping this game today."

This year Shaw has socked 69 hits in 183 at-bats for a .377 average with 5 home runs. He is getting on base at a .410 pace and his totals also include 26 RBIs and 30 runs scored.
