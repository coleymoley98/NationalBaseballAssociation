Jr. Nordiques Richards Strikes out 15 Mariners
Sunday, July 17th, 1927
Quebec City Jr. Nordiques starter Logan Richards brought his best stuff to Juniors Ballpark today against the St. John's Mariners, striking out 15 batters in his club's 3-2 victory.

In a strong performance, the 18-year-old Richards gave up 3 hits in his 9 innings of work and issued 4 walks.

"I felt good out there," he said after the game. "My fastball had a lot of life and my sinker was sharp. It was giving hitters fits and working well for me."

This season Richards has gone 3-5 with an ERA of 2.51, posting 115 strikeouts in 97 innings.
