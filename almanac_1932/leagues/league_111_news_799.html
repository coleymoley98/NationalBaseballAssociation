Playoff Bound: Mountaineers Win Wild Card
Thursday, July 24th, 1930
Morgantown Mountaineers manager James Ferro said an East championship was the goal, but they are still pleased to have won a wild card position.

"You'd rather go in there as the top team, but all that matters is we're in the playoffs," he said. "Once the postseason starts, everyone's got a chance."

Morgantown starting pitcher Curt Krogman said the members of the team are all incredibly focused on capturing a Adams Cup, which would be the club's 3rd.

"We all want this badly," he said.
