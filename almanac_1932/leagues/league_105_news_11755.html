Boulay Hits .389 to Earn NL Crown
Friday, September 5th, 1930
To hit .389 in National Baseball Association is an extraordinary feat.

It becomes even more outstanding when you consider it was done by a 24-year-old.

Taradin Boulay of the Minnesota Saints is your National League batting champion.

On the year, Boulay collected 169 hits, 41 doubles, 4 triples and 10 home runs for the year. In 112 games Boulay totaled 75 RBIs and scored 80 runs.
