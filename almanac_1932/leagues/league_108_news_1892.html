Humprey Hot, Eagles Cold
Tuesday, July 28th, 1931
Max Humprey held the Spokane Eagles to 8 hits and fashioned a fine 1-0 shutout at Eagles Ballpark today.

Speaking after the game, Edmonton Eskimos manager Nattaporn Lamontagne said "Humprey had everything going for him today."

He changed speeds effectively, stayed down, had a lot of movement," he said. "He was masterful."

Humprey rung up 1 strikeout and surrendered 1 walk in the game.

"We couldn't get anything going. He certainly had our number," Spokane manager Laurent Fontaine told reporters.

This season Humprey has a 6-4 record with a 3.52 ERA in 10 starts.
