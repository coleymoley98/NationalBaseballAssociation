Collard Mows Down 15 Rivermen
Sunday, July 20th, 1930
Adams Mounties starter Manus Collard brought his best stuff to Ballpark of Adams today against the Davenport Rivermen, striking out 15 batters in his club's 4-1 victory.

In a strong performance, the 18-year-old Collard gave up 3 hits in his 8.2 innings of work and issued no walks.

"I felt good out there," he said after the game. "My fastball had a lot of life and my splitter was sharp. It was giving hitters fits and working well for me."

On the season Collard has compiled a 7-1 mark with a 3.64 ERA in 9 starts. In pitching 66.2 innings he has 79 strikeouts and 16 walks while limiting the opposition to a .224 batting average.
