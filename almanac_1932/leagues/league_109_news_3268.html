Robins Snap Bender's Streak at 26
Monday, August 8th, 1932
For 26 straight games, Drew Bender tried to ignore his hitting streak. It wasn't easy.

Well, the 21-year-old Providence Redcoats catcher can forget about it now -- it's over, courtesy of the Harrisburg Robins. The Robins held him hitless in 4 at-bats at Providence Grounds today and snapped his consecutive-game hitting string at 26.

There was at least one good note to the end of the streak, however, as the Redcoats took the win by the score of 10-2.

"I tried to ignore it for weeks, but you guys in the media wouldn't let me," he joked with reporters after the game. "I'm looking forward to some peace and quiet now."

So far this season Bender has no home runs and a .381 batting average.
