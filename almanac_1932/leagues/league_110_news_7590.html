No Late-Season Magic Coming: Davenport Out of Playoffs
Thursday, July 22nd, 1926
Members of the Davenport Rivermen are left wondering what could have been today, as the team has been eliminated from postseason contention.

"You look back at a handful of games that easily could have gone the other way for us," said starting pitcher Renato Joly. "We really think we were good enough to get to the playoffs this year, so it stings."

The Rivermen have missed the playoffs 31 times in their 32 years as a Central Baseball League franchise. This playoff miss is the club's 5th in a row, with their last postseason appearance coming in 1920. In the last 10 seasons, the Rivermen have missed the playoffs 9 times. Davenport last won the Dumont Cup in 1909.
