Morales Smacks 5 Hits for Redcoats
Thursday, May 22nd, 1930
Even as a kid, Carmen Morales said his coaches told him the key to hitting was making contact, and letting everything else flow from there. At Fenway Park 1912 today, Morales showed how well he learned that lesson.

The 32-year-old shortstop had 5 hits in 6 at-bats for the Boston Redcoats, helping his team knock off the New York Rebels by a score of 10-9.

"They always said, just keep your eye on the ball, don't overswing, make contact and you never know what will happen," he said. "That's all I did today."

Carmen Morales grounded out in the 1st, doubled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, singled in the 7th, singled in the 8th and singled in the 10th.

For the season, Morales is hitting at a .452 clip with no home runs, 11 RBIs and has scored 15 runs. He has 33 hits, 2 walks and a .474 on-base percentage.
