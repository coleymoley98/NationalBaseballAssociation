Rowdy Players Suspended by League
Friday, July 8th, 1932
Will Baribeau of Adams and Kittipoom Iversen of Winnipeg were the main participants in a recent bench-clearing brawl and today the league handed out its punishment. Baribeau will have to miss 3 games for his part in the brawl, while Iversen was given 6 games. League officials announced their suspensions today.

Baribeau is batting .217 this season in 36 games with 3 home runs, 21 RBIs and 17 runs scored. He has 26 hits in 120 at-bats.

For this year Iversen has chalked up 3 wins and 3 losses in 8 starts with 35 strikeouts in 42.2 innings and a 6.12 ERA.
