Paholpat Defeats Robins For #150
Saturday, June 13th, 1931
History was made today at Sportsmans Park 1911 -- Chutipol Paholpat won game #150 to seal his name in the league record books. The Ottawa ace downed the Baltimore Robins 8-4 to gain entrance into the elite 150 Club.

"I was wound up at first. I think I threw too hard," remarked Paholpat, "I had to calm down and go out there and pitch... not just throw. I think I got better as the game wore on. I started to think less about 150 wins and more about doing what I had to do."

His lifetime totals give Paholpat 150 victories and 149 defeats with a 4.13 ERA.
