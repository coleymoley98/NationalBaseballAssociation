Coronado Belts 3 HRs
Wednesday, July 22nd, 1931
Kelowna Apple Sox shortstop Narong Coronado sensed he was going to have a good day at the plate, but he still might have underestimated it a little.

Prior to the start of today's contest with the Ranier Seahawks, Coronado told teammates he was sure he would hit a home run. He did -- three times.

"During batting practice I really had my swing down pat," he recalled. "I felt some thunder in the lumber."

The big day from Coronado pushed the Apple Sox past the Seahawks, 17-14. To go along with the homers, he collected 9 RBIs and scored 3 runs.

"I was seeing good, feeling good and swinging good," Coronado told reporters after the game.

Narong Coronado hit a two-run home run off Benjamin Brann in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, hit a three-run home run off Cade Harlock in the 5th, hit a three-run home run off Raymond Tétreault in the 6th and walked in the 8th.

In his season totals Coronado has 7 home runs and a .357 batting average.
