Hit Record Belongs to Martinka
Tuesday, March 29th, 1932
Baseball has a new Hit King.

"Team Dakota veteran Kelsey Martinka surpassed the old mark in a contest at Villanueva Fields today, logging hit number 118.

"I never expected to have the wonderful career that I have been so blessed with," Martinka told the press after the game in which he set the new league career record. "I just hoped to be around a decade or so and be a good ballplayer. Well, it's been amazing."

With 118 hits, the Team star surpasses the record previously held by the great Lucien Diehl.

Kelsey Martinka doubled in the 1st.
