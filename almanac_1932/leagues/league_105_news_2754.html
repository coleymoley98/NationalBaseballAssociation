NL Player of the Week Goes to Cease of Toronto
Monday, June 13th, 1932
Javon Cease has the tools to stick in this league for many years. The 24-year-old first baseman of the Toronto Braves had a hot bat in the National League over the past seven days and won the Player of the Week honors.

Cease batted .480 with 12 hits in 25 at-bats, 1 home run and 5 RBIs.

This season Cease is batting .351 with 7 home runs. His totals include 21 RBIs and 30 runs scored.
