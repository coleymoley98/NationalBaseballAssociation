Mariners Dominate, Sweep Division Finals
Friday, August 1st, 1930
The St. John's Mariners are on to the Laurier Cup after a convincing series win over the Quebec City Jr. Nordiques.

St. John's wrapped up the series sweep with a 3-2 win in the last game, to take the series 4-0.

"We expected to win and we executed the way we needed to," said Watcharin Sankla, who was named series MVP.

For the series, Sankla hit .500 with a .526 on-base percentage. He had 0 home runs, drove in 5 RBI and scored 3 runs.

The St. John's Mariners don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Pointe-Sainte-Anne Acadiens - Providence Bruins series. Providence leads the series 3 game to 1. The Laurier Cup schedule will be announced after the Division Finals finishes.
