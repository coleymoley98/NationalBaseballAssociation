Bad Behavior Costs Gomez and Igl
Thursday, June 2nd, 1932
Lorenzo Gomez of the Winnipeg Jr. Thunder and Gabriel Igl of the Minneapolis Jr. Saints will be on the sidelines for a few days. They have been suspended for 4 and 6 games respectively by the league office for causing the bench-emptying free-for-all at the recent Jr. Thunder-Jr. Saints game at Jr. Thunder Ballpark.

Neither player had any comment about the suspensions. Both clubs told BNN Gomez and Igl will begin serving them soon.
