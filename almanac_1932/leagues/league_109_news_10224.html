Aurandt Takes EL #1 Pitcher Trophy
Saturday, September 1st, 1928
Nick Aurandt's been around the league long enough to appreciate the honor he received today. After going 4-0 in August, the 33-year-old Harrisburg hurler has been named Eastern League Pitcher of the Month.

He fanned 36 in 50.1 innings, logged an ERA of 2.32 and allowed opponents to hit .238 against him.

This year in 18 starts, Aurandt has recorded 5 wins and absorbed 6 losses. He has pitched 117.1 innings, allowed 124 hits, struck out 97 and has a 4.14 ERA.
