Voyageurs Lu Named ABL's #1 Player
Monday, July 23rd, 1923
Elwood Lu burst onto the Atlantic Baseball League stage in a big way last week. This promising rookie first baseman for Coude garnered everyone's respect by putting up some remarkable numbers and picking up the Player of the Week trophy.

The 15-year-old haunted opposing pitchers hitting .462 (12-for-26) with 3 home runs and 9 RBIs last week.
