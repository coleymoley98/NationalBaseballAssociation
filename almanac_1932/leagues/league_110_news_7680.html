Ruel CBL Outstanding Batter of the Month
Sunday, August 1st, 1926
Laurent Ruel made his presence known as one of the best young stars in the Central Baseball League this past month.

The Winnipeg Jr. Thunder left fielder shined through the month of July and has won the Batter of the Month award.

The 16-year-old hit .411 with 5 home runs, 35 RBIs and 20 runs scored.

Batting .379 to date, Ruel has 6 home runs and 50 RBIs.
