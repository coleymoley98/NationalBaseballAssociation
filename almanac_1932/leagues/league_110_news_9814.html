Lapierre Slams 5 Hits for Lincoln
Saturday, July 7th, 1928
It was nearly a perfect day for Justin Lapierre at Field of the Juniors. Although the Lincoln Pioneers left fielder was sensational at the plate, collecting 5 base hits in 9 at-bats, it just wasn't enough to keep the Pioneers from falling to the Davenport Rivermen. Davenport spoiled his great day at the plate by taking a 20-15 win.

Justin Lapierre flied out in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, doubled in the 4th, hit a two-run home run off André Lamontagne in the 5th, struck out in the 8th, singled in the 10th, struck out in the 12th, grounded out in the 14th and singled in the 15th.

In this current campaign Lapierre is hitting at a .333 clip with 1 home run and 7 RBIs.
