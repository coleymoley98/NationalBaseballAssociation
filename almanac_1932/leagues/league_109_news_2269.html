You're Out! Braves Fire Manager DeShon
Saturday, October 10th, 1931
Calvert DeShon has been released as the Tecumseh Braves manager.

Braves officials said it is time to make a change and get the club heading in a different direction.

The dismissed skipper was philosophical about his fate.

"When things don't go right, the first thing management does is get a new manager," he said. "That seems to be the quickest solution to making things better -- or at least, making them seem better. That's just the way baseball is."
