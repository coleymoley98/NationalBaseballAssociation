Steelers Wins, 8-0, as Halliburton Mystifies Boston
Wednesday, August 26th, 1931
It wasn't easy but the Hamilton Steelers made it look that way.

Starter Ryan Halliburton shut out the Boston Redcoats despite having what he later called "sub-par" stuff, and the Hamilton Steelers cruised to victory, 8-0. Halliburton allowed only 3 hits and rang up 4 strikeouts but insisted later that he did not deserve primary credit for the win.

"It didn't feel quite right today, but I got a lot of help to get through," he said. "I trusted my catcher and the guys behind me bailed me out more than once when I screwed up."

This season in 21 starts Ryan Halliburton has a record of 8-13 with 45 strikeouts in 164.2 innings and a 3.88 ERA.
