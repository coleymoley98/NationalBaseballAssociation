New York Over Wolfeburg 5-4, 150th Win for Mongeau
Saturday, July 7th, 1923
Louis Mongeau of the New York Rebels nailed down his 150th career victory with a 5-4 win over the Wolfeburg Celtics today at Polo Grounds. Mongeau, 31, fired no strikeouts over 0.2 innings of work. He gave up no runs, no walks and allowed 4 hits.

"I feel great," Mongeau said afterward. "I try not to get caught up in the moment. It's hard with so much press coverage these days, but I tune all that out when I go out there and pitch."

Lifetime Mongeau has chalked up 150 wins and 168 losses and compiled a 3.21 ERA.
