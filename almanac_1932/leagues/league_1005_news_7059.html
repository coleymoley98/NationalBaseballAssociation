Morneau Belts 5 Hits
Monday, March 22nd, 1926
No matter what the Team Manitoba threw at him today, Zachary Morneau hit it. He easily handled Manitoba pitching, going 5 for 6 and helping the Erie to a 23-1 triumph at Toronto Memorial Fields.

"The way I was swinging today, I think I could have closed my eyes and hit the ball," he told BNN reporters in the postgame interview.

Told of Morneau's quip, Manitoba skipper Reid Candlemaker said he "would take him up on that next time."

Zachary Morneau walked in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, hit a two-run single in the 4th, hit an RBI double in the 5th, hit a two-run single in the 5th, grounded out in the 6th and singled in the 8th.

This season Morneau is batting .519 with no home runs. He has scored 13 runs and driven in 21.
