EBL's Star Player Named
Monday, July 4th, 1932
It can be difficult for a young player to step out of the shadows and assert himself in the Eastern Baseball League, but last week Pete Kearns of the Buffalo Rapids reminded everyone just why he's here as he threw up numbers that made him an easy selection for Player of the Week. Kearns rocked opposing pitching for a .458 average (11-for-24), 3 home runs and 15 RBIs to come away with the award.

Kearns is hitting .295 for the season with 5 home runs, 23 RBIs and 19 runs scored. He has 31 hits in 105 at-bats and played in 31 games.
