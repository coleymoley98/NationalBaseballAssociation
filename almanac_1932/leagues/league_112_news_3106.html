Acadiens Win Saint Lawrence Crown
Thursday, July 21st, 1932
Pointe-Sainte-Anne Acadiens fans have reason to celebrate today, as their franchise is the class of the Saint Lawrence.

With their record standing at 31-16, the club officially wrapped up the division title today, for the 3rd time. Manager Joseph Jones said everyone on their roster is looking forward to the postseason and trying to capture the club's 4th championship.

"That's what you dream about your whole life," Jones said.

Starting Pitcher Supete Edwards added that it's a great feeling to win the division, but said ultimately, a Laurier Cup victory is what will mark success for the club.
