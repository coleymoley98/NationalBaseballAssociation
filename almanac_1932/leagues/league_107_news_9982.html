While it remains to be seen what the playoffs will bring for the Vancouver Giants, the club took the first step by clinching the title.

"It's exactly where we wanted to be and where we talked about being during spring training," said starting pitcher Azman Eason. "There's not a guy in the room that doesn't believe in this team. We're all pulling in the same direction right now."

It was the team's 3rd division title and they will attempt to capture the franchise's 2nd Western Baseball League championship.

"The end goal hasn't changed - win the Bennett Cup," said manager Danupong Chan-inkaew. "We think we've got the team to do it."
