Gallegos Stretches Hit String to 20
Wednesday, June 22nd, 1927
The Winnipeg Jr. Thunder fell to the Omaha Jr. Falcons in Jr. Thunder Ballpark today, even as 15-year-old right fielder Devin Gallegos upped his consecutive-game hitting streak to 20 games. Gallegos finished with 1 hit in 3 at-bats, but could not stop the Jr. Thunder from dropping the 10-6 decision.

"Yeah, I kept my streak going, but it would have been better to come away with a win," said Gallegos.
Devin Gallegos grounded out in the 1st, grounded out in the 2nd, walked in the 4th and doubled in the 6th.

This season Gallegos has hit at a .500 clip with 27 RBIs, 6 home runs and 23 runs scored.
