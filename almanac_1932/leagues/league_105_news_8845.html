Detroit Starter Selected Best Pitcher
Monday, August 1st, 1927
Despite the number of years he's been in the league, Panuwat Sumonrat still gets excited when he puts together a quality stretch like he had in July. The 25-year-old starter for Detroit was named National League Pitcher of the Month earlier today.

He collected 5 wins and 2 losses with 20 strikeouts in his 7 starts, putting up a fine 2.17 ERA in 62.1 innings of work.

In 17 starts this year Sumonrat has fashioned a 10-7 record and a 3.11 ERA.
