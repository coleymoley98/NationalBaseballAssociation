Team Helpless Against McGowan, Lose 8-0
Tuesday, March 10th, 1931
Jacob McGowan kept the opposition off the scoreboard and the Team Michigan were all smiles after winning 8-0 against the Team Dakota at Ciudad Mexico Stadium today. The right-handed McGowan gave up no runs in his start, permitted 3 hits and had 2 strikeouts.

"If there was a stat for 'team shutout,' this would be it," McGowan said later. "My catcher called a great game, guys delivered at the plate and when I messed something up the guys behind me were where they needed to be."
