Mongeau Collects CL Pitching Award
Wednesday, August 1st, 1923
Despite the number of years he's been in the league, Louis Mongeau still gets excited when he puts together a quality stretch like he had in July. The 31-year-old starter for New York was named Canadian League Pitcher of the Month earlier today.

He collected 6 wins and no losses with 15 strikeouts in his 5 starts, putting up a fine 1.16 ERA in 46.2 innings of work.

This season Mongeau has compiled a 13-4 mark with a 2.74 ERA.
