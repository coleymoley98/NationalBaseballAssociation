100 Wins for Robins Siegel
Friday, July 11th, 1930
Lindsey Siegel of the Baltimore Robins will go down as one of the league's legendary pitchers after his 9-5 win today against the Philadelphia Liberty.

"It felt like it was a postseason game, one out at a time," said his manager. "It was something special to see."

The 32-year-old Siegel picked up his 100th career victory at Sportsmans Park 1911. He hurled 9 innings, gave up 9 hits and registered 3 strikeouts along with 2 walks.

"I've worked a long time for this," Siegel said. "I can't believe it's really here. It feels great!"

To date in his career Siegel is 100-102 with an ERA of 3.88 and 591 strikeouts.
