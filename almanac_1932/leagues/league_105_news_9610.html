Detroit Blanked by Guérin
Sunday, June 10th, 1928
Thomas Guérin of the Chicagou Frontenacs whitewashed the Detroit Lions 3-0 in a complete-game win, limiting them to 7 hits while chalking up 4 strikeouts and giving up no walks.

"He's got the makeup. He's not afraid to challenge guys," Chicagou manager Jetsadaporn Sheerin said to BNN reporters after the game. "Thomas is poised out there. He knows how to pitch and how to use his stuff."

So far this year Guérin has made 7 starts, tossed 60.2 innings, struck out 30 batters and walked 20. His record is 7-0 with a 2.23 ERA and he has limited opposing teams to a .248 batting average.
