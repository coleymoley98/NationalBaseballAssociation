Albertson on Fire, 5-for-5 Against Philadelphia
Sunday, July 17th, 1927
Fans at Comiskey Park 1910 got to see not only a home-team victory today, but also one of the most impressive feats of hitting of the season.

The Chicagou White Sox outplayed the Philadelphia Liberty en route to a 12-4 win, but the standout was third baseman Chanchai Albertson. Albertson netted 5 hits in 5 at-bats and finished with a dynamite game line.

"Hey, we tried everything we could think of to get him out," Liberty manager Hervé Morel said in his postgame comments. "We threw everything but the kitchen sink against him and he hit it. Maybe we should've thrown the sink, too."

Chanchai Albertson hit an RBI single in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 4th, singled in the 7th and hit an RBI single in the 8th.

For the season Albertson has hit .330 with 6 home runs and 36 RBIs. He has played in 60 games and scored 39 times.
