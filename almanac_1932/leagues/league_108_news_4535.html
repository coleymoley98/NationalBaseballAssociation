1923 WL Pitcher of the Year Award Chosen
Thursday, September 20th, 1923
In 1923, the premier pitcher in the Western League was Marcello Thébault. The Omaha starter is this year's recipient of the Pitcher of the Year Award.

He went 13-3 in 18 starts with 106 strikeouts in 159.1 innings and an impressive 3.28 ERA.

He received 7 first place votes out of a possible 8. Mark Detraz of the Omaha Falcons finished second in voting with 1 first place vote, while Corbin Whisler of the Vancouver Giants finished third.
