Ottawa Wins Canadian League East Crown
Sunday, September 4th, 1932
While it remains to be seen what the playoffs will bring for the Ottawa Indians, the club took the first step by clinching the Canadian League East title.

"It's exactly where we wanted to be and where we talked about being during spring training," said left fielder David Wintringham. "There's not a guy in the room that doesn't believe in this team. We're all pulling in the same direction right now."

It was the team's 4th division title and they will attempt to capture the franchise's 1st National Baseball Association championship.

"The end goal hasn't changed - win the Riel Cup," said manager Frank Boon. "We think we've got the team to do it."
