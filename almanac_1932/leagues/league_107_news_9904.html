Peterson and Prokop Brawl, League Suspends Them
Tuesday, July 17th, 1928
After an intense confrontation, both the Admirals Varrien Peterson and the Alpines J.P. Prokop were handed their punishments today. The league commissioner's office issued suspensions for an on-the-field conflict that occurred at a recent Victoria-Abbotsford game. Varrien Peterson will miss 3 games, while J.P. Prokop was suspended for 7 games.

"Sometimes we go too far and have to pay the consequences," Varrien Peterson commented later. "Hopefully cooler heads will prevail the next time we play."
