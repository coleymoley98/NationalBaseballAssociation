One Run Enough for Springfield
Saturday, June 21st, 1924
In an outstanding mound performance, Springfield Falcons starter Chaiwut Blackburn fired a complete-game shutout against the Juneau Jr. Brasseurs at Juniors Ballpark today.

Blackburn was the key to the Falcons's 1-0 win. The 18-year-old right-hander was in high spirits on the mount for much of the game, his mood largely undented by the 3 hits and 3 walks he allowed.

"3 hits?" he said with a laugh following the game. "Listen, I'd be happy with a 30-hitter as long as it was a shutout."

This year Blackburn has a 2-1 record with a 3.68 ERA in 4 starts.
