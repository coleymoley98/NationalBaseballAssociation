Mackinnon Ups Hit Streak to 20 Consecutive Games
Monday, June 27th, 1932
The number just keeps getting higher.

St. Louis first baseman Ed Mackinnon has now extended his hitting string to 20 games. He did it today with 2 hits in 4 at-bats as the St. Louis took the 10-1 win over the Minneapolis Jr. Saints at Juniors Ballpark.

"Who knows how long it will go on?" Minneapolis manager Pongsak Sparks said. "He's certainly tough to pitch to right now."
Ed Mackinnon grounded out in the 1st, flied out in the 3rd, hit an RBI single in the 5th, walked in the 7th and hit an RBI single in the 8th.

This season Mackinnon is hitting .356 with 32 hits, 2 home runs, 20 RBIs, scored 13 runs and played in 25 games.
