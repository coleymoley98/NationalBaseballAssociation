National League Names Rookie of Month
Saturday, September 1st, 1923
Valéry Jetté's eyes seem to light up whenever he sees the baseball. The young Montreal third baseman has been hitting everything in sight for the better part of a month.

In August he was voted the Rookie of the Month Award.

During the month he hit .304 with 3 doubles, 4 triples and 2 home runs. Jetté also scored 21 times and drove in 6 runs.

For the season Jetté has produced 59 hits in 221 at-bats for a .267 average with 3 home runs and gotten on base at a .345 clip.
