Wolfeburg's Trottier Bags CL Batting Award
Wednesday, September 1st, 1926
Charles Trottier battered Canadian League pitchers all August and wound up taking home the Batter of the Month Award.

"I've been around National Baseball Association long enough to know you really have to work hard day in and day out to be able to put up this kind of consistency," the 27-year-old Trottier told reporters. "I put in a lot of work, even on the days I might not take the field, and it's always nice when everything comes together for me like it did this last month."

In 28 games, Trottier hit .423 with 3 home runs, 22 RBIs and scored 25 runs.

Currently Trottier is batting .367 with 8 home runs and 67 RBIs.
