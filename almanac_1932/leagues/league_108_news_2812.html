Banks Silences Saskatoon, 8-0
Sunday, June 19th, 1932
Suwat Banks of Spokane easily handled the Saskatoon Coyotes, stopping them on 5 hits and chucking a complete-game 8-0 shutout.

"He really did a job on us," said Saskatoon manager Peerapong Owens. "He had good command of his pitches. He changed speeds well and confused us. We never could zero in on him, but he certainly zeroed in on us."

He finished the game with no strikeouts and 4 walks in a solid outing.
