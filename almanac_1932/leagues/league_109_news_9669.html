Younger Wins EL Player of the Week Prize
Monday, June 18th, 1928
This week he let his bat do the talking. That's why Josiah Younger of the London Indians was named the Eastern League Player of the Week.

For the week the 20-year-old first baseman batted .455 (10-for-22) with 5 home runs, 8 runs and 11 RBIs.

Thus far this year Younger has hit .345 with 5 home runs and 11 RBIs.
