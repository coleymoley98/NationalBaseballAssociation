Detroit Lions Ship Patterson to Juneau
Thursday, July 7th, 1932
The Detroit Lions have parted ways with 36-year-old RHP Nicola Patterson in a trade with the Juneau Brasseurs for 24-year-old minor league SS Jim Blackton, 23-year-old minor league LHP Rocky Goodine and 19-year-old minor league 2B Kareem Richardson. Lions general manager Jeevan Bahrama confirmed the deal in an announcement this morning.


Patterson has taken the mound for 11 relief appearances this season, nailing down 2 saves while notching a 2-1 record with a 2.22 ERA. The Juneau reliever has limited opposing batters to a .248 batting average.
