Redcoats Crowned Kings of the Canadian League East
Sunday, September 6th, 1931
It's a banner year for the Boston Redcoats, they have won the Canadian League East.

"There's so much pride in our room and on our roster, anything but a division title wouldn't have been acceptable," said center fielder Samuel Gilliard. "In the playoffs we'll just have to take it one day at a time and keep battling."

The Redcoats this year have battled to a 62-41 mark, which is good for the club's 13th Canadian League East title.

"I'm extremely proud of my players and look forward to seeing what we can do in the postseason," said manager Brian Ahn.
