Gunners Heading to Laurier Cup
Wednesday, August 3rd, 1927
The championship dreams are still alive for the Quincy Gunners, while it's back to the drawing board for the Hartford Whalers.

By a score of 9-5, Quincy won the decisive game at Juniors Ballpark today. The Gunners took the series 4-2.

Jacob Firth of the Gunners was named the Division Finals MVP.

"All our guys are really focused winning the Laurier Cup," Firth said. "I'm not too concerned about personal awards."

The Quincy Gunners don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Halifax Highlanders - Montreal Jr. Royales series. Montreal leads the series 3 games to 2. The Laurier Cup schedule will be announced after the Division Finals finishes.
