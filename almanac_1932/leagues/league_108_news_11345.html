Aquino and Guillemin Fight, Get Suspended
Thursday, August 15th, 1929
Nat Aquino of the Vancouver Giants and Lamarcus Guillemin of the Winnipeg Thunder will be on the sidelines for a few days. They have been suspended for 4 and 6 games respectively by the league office for causing the bench-emptying free-for-all at the recent Giants-Thunder game at Oracle Park.

Neither player had any comment about the suspensions. Both clubs told BNN Aquino and Guillemin will begin serving them soon.

Aquino is batting at a .345 pace this season, picking up 48 hits in 139 at-bats with 5 home runs, 27 RBIs and 21 runs scored.

In 14 starts this season Guillemin has registered a 5-7 record and a 5.25 ERA.
