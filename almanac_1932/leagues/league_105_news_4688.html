Hall of Fame Selection announcement coming next week
Wednesday, December 26th, 1923
The last ballots are being sent in for the 1924 National Baseball Association Hall of Fame.

Headlining this year's class is 11-year veteran Lowell Cohen. Best known for his time with the Quebec Nordiques, BNN analysts are busy speculating whether his career is enough to get enshrined.

All eligible electors are reminded to make sure their ballots are received before the results are announced on January 2nd, 1924.

The 41-year-old Cohen has sat down 608 batters on strikes, walked 239 and put up a 2.35 ERA. His career won-lost record is 92-51.
