Dodson Wins CBL Honor
Monday, July 7th, 1930
Chevy Dodson punched, poked and pummeled the baseball last week and today he garnered a majority of the votes for the Central Baseball League Player of the Week award.

The St. Louis Jr. Cardinaux left fielder slashed out a .485 average with 16 hits in 33 at-bats, 3 home runs and 12 RBIs.

So far in 1930 Dodson is batting .435 with 11 home runs, 31 RBIs and 29 runs scored. He has 54 hits in 124 at-bats and a .493 OBP.
