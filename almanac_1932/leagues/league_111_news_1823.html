Injury Sidelines Bethea for Season
Tuesday, July 21st, 1931
In a news release, the Buffalo Rapids announced that reliever Zeshon Bethea would miss the rest of the season due to a partially torn ulnar collateral ligament. Bethea sustained the injury on 07/17/1931. He "will be sorely missed," said his manager.

This season Bethea is 1-and-6 with no saves and a 9.17 ERA. He has given up 53 hits in 36.1 innings with 34 strikeouts.
