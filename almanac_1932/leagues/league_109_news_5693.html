Eastern League Top Pitcher Selected
Saturday, September 20th, 1924
The Pitcher of the Year Award for 1924 has been presented and the Eastern League winner is Bastien Rioux of Pittsburgh.

"It's always nice to receive an award like this," said Rioux. "I just hope I can improve on my performance next year and be in the running again," the Liberty star told reporters.

Rioux excelled with an impressive 12-4 record in 21 starts. In 130.2 innings he yielded 113 hits and 36 walks, while striking out 134 and compiling a fine 2.27 ERA.

He received 7 first place votes out of a possible 8. Watcharin Reske of the Pittsburgh Liberty finished second in voting, while Etienne Phaneuf of the Harrisburg Robins finished third.
