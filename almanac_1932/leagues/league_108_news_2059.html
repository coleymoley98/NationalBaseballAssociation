Monday, August 17th, 1931
Fans at Eagles Ballpark witnessed the Portland Thorns blank the Spokane Eagles, 7-0 behind a superb effort by starter Isaac Dupont. The Portland left-hander allowed 6 hits in recording the shutout.

"Isaac was good today but I don't want to focus too much on a single game," Portland manager Charlie Fleury said later. "If you're capable of playing at this level, you're capable of having a good game. The real question is if you can learn from that game and repeat it."
