League Punishes Pair for Fighting
Thursday, June 17th, 1926
J'Michael Blow of the St. Louis Jr. Cardinaux and Xavier Evremond of the Winnipeg Jr. Thunder will be on the sidelines for a few days. They have been suspended for 3 and 6 games respectively by the league office for causing the bench-emptying free-for-all at the recent Jr. Cardinaux-Jr. Thunder game at Ballpark of St. Louis.

Neither player had any comment about the suspensions. Both clubs told BNN Blow and Evremond will begin serving them soon.

This season Blow is batting .267 with 12 hits, 3 home runs, 17 RBIs, .450 OBP and has scored 11 runs.
