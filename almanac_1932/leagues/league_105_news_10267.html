Montreal Sweeps into the Riel Cup
Sunday, September 16th, 1928
The Chicagou Frontenacs were simply overmatched by the Montreal Royales in the League Championship Series.

The end result was a sweep, as Montreal has cruised to the Riel Cup, with the final win coming at Wrigley Field 1928 by a score of 2-1.

"We're clicking and running on all cylinders at the moment," said Tim Michaels, who was named MVP of the League Championship Series. "We're a dangerous team when we play like this."

For the series, Michaels hit .462 with a .588 on-base percentage. He had 0 home runs, drove in 5 RBI and scored 1 runs.

The Montreal Royales will play against the Boston Redcoats in the next round. Boston won their series against the Toronto Blue Sox 4 games to 1. The Riel Cup schedule will be announced after the League Championship Series finishes.
