Player of the Week: Quebec's Haas
Monday, August 9th, 1926
Erez Haas of the Quebec Nordiques had an award-winning week and today was voted the Player of the Week by the National League.

For the week the 26-year-old catcher batted .550 (11-for-20) with no home runs and 5 RBIs.

So far this season Haas has gotten 66 hits in 218 at-bats for a .303 batting average with 2 home runs. His stats include 38 RBIs and 31 runs scored. He has played in 59 games.
