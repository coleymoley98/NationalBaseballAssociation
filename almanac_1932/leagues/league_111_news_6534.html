North Bay's Campisano Snaps up July Batting Award
Saturday, August 1st, 1925
At only 17 years of age, Walt Campisano could very well be having many more months like the last this in his career.

That would be great news for fans of the North Bay Battalion, as July was one to remember for the first baseman and he has captured the Eastern Baseball League Batter of the Month award.

On the month he hit .380 with 5 home runs, 19 RBIs and 15 runs scored.

Campisano is batting .323 with 10 home runs and 42 RBIs this season.
