Pelletier Earns CL Rookie Honor
Thursday, October 16th, 1924
Last spring Jason Pelletier was happy to just make the Redcoats roster. He was overjoyed to see the name "Pelletier" on the lineup card, too. Now the 20-year-old Boston right fielder has capped off his debut season by winning the coveted Canadian League Rookie of the Year Award.

Pelletier won the award by hitting .317 and collecting 118 hits, 8 home runs, 81 RBIs and 20 walks, along with 59 runs scored and a .347 on-base percentage.

He received 13 first place votes out of a possible 16. Phakhawat Gascon of the New York Rebels finished second in voting with 3 first place votes, while Wayne Womack of the Baltimore Robins finished third.
