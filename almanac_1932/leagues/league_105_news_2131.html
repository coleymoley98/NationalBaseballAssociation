CL Best Rookie Award Announced
Tuesday, September 1st, 1931
21-year-old Ambroise Thériault is deceptively poised for such a young man. In 1 starts he bagged 3 wins in August and took home the Canadian League Rookie of the Month Award.

The Wolfeburg hurler logged a 3-0 record, 17 strikeouts in 27.2 innings, held opponents to a .255 average and compiled an ERA of 0.98.

Thériault's record up to now is 5-and-4 with 1 save and a 5.19 ERA. He has given up 129 hits in 102.1 innings with 42 strikeouts.
