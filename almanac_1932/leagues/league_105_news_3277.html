Cincinnati Third Baseman Out
Wednesday, August 10th, 1932
Dealing with injuries is part of the game, and now Eli Sinclair of the Reds knows this firsthand. He was injured against the Toronto Braves while running the bases. Because of a strained lat, the Cincinnati third baseman is expected to miss 1-2 weeks. Sinclair was a little upset, but understands it comes with the territory. "Baseball can be a contact sport. You try to be careful, but no matter how careful you are, injuries happen."

So far this season Sinclair is hitting .321 with 8 home runs.
