ABL Trophy Goes to Sylvia of St. John's
Monday, July 6th, 1925
18-year-old Elvis Sylvia made a few St. John's headlines during this past week as the rookie center fielder proved he could compete at the plate with his high-school idols. He captured the attention of fans, journalists and Atlantic Baseball League officials last week and today took home Player of the Week honors for his effort.

He cracked 16 hits in 31 at-bats to put up a .516 average. Sylvia collected 1 home run, 10 RBIs and scored 9 runs.

In 32 games this season Sylvia has batted at a .310 pace with 39 hits and 3 home runs.
