Cincinnati-Montreal Trade: Benton for Davies
Monday, September 14th, 1925
Cincinnati and Montreal have agreed in principle to a trade. The Reds will send 26-year-old RF Mauseng Benton to the Royales for 23-year-old minor league C Yin Poonjuang, 19-year-old minor league LF James Cardin, 23-year-old minor league LF Ronnie Davies and 21-year-old minor league RF Supakit Plante. The deal is expected to be finalized shortly and the clubs will be introducing their new players to the press when the paperwork is complete.


Benton on the season is hitting .297 with 9 home runs. His stats include 34 RBIs and 43 runs scored.
