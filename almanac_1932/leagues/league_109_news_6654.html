Blue Sox Suffer 9-0 Drubbing from Royales
Friday, August 21st, 1925
In an outstanding mound performance, Trois-Rivieres Royales starter Steven Masson fired a complete-game shutout against the Hamilton Blue Sox at Trois-Rivieres Grounds today.

Masson was the key to the Royales's 9-0 win. The 21-year-old right-hander was in high spirits on the mount for much of the game, his mood largely undented by the 5 hits and 1 walk he allowed.

"5 hits?" he said with a laugh following the game. "Listen, I'd be happy with a 30-hitter as long as it was a shutout."

To date this year in 13 starts, Masson has crafted a 4-3 mark with a 2.75 ERA.
