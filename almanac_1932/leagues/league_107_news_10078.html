Skipper Daniels Let Go by Victoria
Thursday, August 9th, 1928
"I'm just a scapegoat," said Jesse Daniels to reporters in the stadium parking lot after the Victoria Admirals announced his firing as manager early today. "You think I did a bad job? It's the players who couldn't hit, couldn't pitch, couldn't catch the damn ball. And you can print that," he added, as he stamped out a cigarette and stomped off.

Daniels's replacement wasn't immediately named, but a Victoria official said an announcement could come as soon as tomorrow.
