Calgary Captures Crown
Saturday, August 23rd, 1930
Fans of the Calgary Wranglers have a lot to get excited about, as the club has captured the crown.

It's the 2nd time in franchise history that the club has finished atop the standings and they now have an opportunity to capture the franchise's 3rd Western League championship.

Manager Carter Lapointe said there's "a lot of excitement" in Calgary over the club right now and they hope to live up to expectations.

For his part, left fielder Liam Sérafin said the division championship is "just a start" for his team.

"We want to go all the way, win the World Series," he said. "Winning the division - we could get used to this."
