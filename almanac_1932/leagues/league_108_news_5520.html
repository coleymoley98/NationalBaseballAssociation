Star of the Week Named in WL
Monday, August 18th, 1924
It can be difficult for a young player to step out of the shadows and assert himself in the Western League, but last week William Nicol of the Portland Thorns reminded everyone just why he's here as he threw up numbers that made him an easy selection for Player of the Week. Nicol rocked opposing pitching for a .444 average (8-for-18), 2 home runs and 12 RBIs to come away with the award.

So far this season Nicol is hitting .235 with 7 home runs, 39 RBIs and 33 runs scored. He has 44 hits in 187 at-bats and a .397 OBP.
