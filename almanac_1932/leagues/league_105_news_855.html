Hamilton Steelers Ship Guglielmo to Chicagou
Thursday, July 31st, 1930
A trade was announced today at a press conference in Hamilton. Not satisfied with their current roster, the Hamilton Steelers general manager shook things up by acquiring 24-year-old minor league RHP Hayden Desjardins from the Chicagou White Sox for 34-year-old RHP Zach Guglielmo.

"We're a better team now," Steelers general manager Kalman Thompson said. "We've added several pieces to the puzzle."


To date in the season, Guglielmo is 5-7 with 39 relief appearances and 7 saves. He has held opposing batters to a .293 average, pitched 68 innings, struck out 30 and walked 22 while recording a 5.16 ERA.
