Setback for Cincinnati and Rafael Hernández
Sunday, August 2nd, 1931
Rafael Hernández, shortstop for the Cincinnati Reds, was making great progress in healing back spasms according to a team spokesman, but suffered a setback yesterday. "Rafael was doing great in his rehab until he overdid it in a recent workout. Now doctors say it will be at least 4 weeks before he will be able to rejoin the club."

Hernández is hitting .287 this season with 1 home run, 8 RBIs and 11 runs scored. He has 27 hits in 94 at-bats.
