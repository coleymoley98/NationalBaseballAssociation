Playoffs? No Chance for Boston
Friday, August 27th, 1926
The 1926 National Baseball Association season has a ways to go still, but the final weeks won't have playoff implications for the Boston Redcoats -- they were officially eliminated from postseason contention today.

Before this season, the Redcoats last missed the playoffs in 1923. In the 32 years they have been a National Baseball Association franchise, they have missed the playoffs 11 times. In the last 10 years, they have missed the playoffs 5 times. Boston last won the Riel Cup in 1921.
