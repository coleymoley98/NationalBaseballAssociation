Atlantic Baseball League Selects Best Performer
Monday, June 20th, 1932
17-year-old Harry Gascoyne made a few Halifax headlines during this past week as the rookie second baseman proved he could compete at the plate with his high-school idols. He captured the attention of fans, journalists and Atlantic Baseball League officials last week and today took home Player of the Week honors for his effort.

He cracked 12 hits in 23 at-bats to put up a .522 average. Gascoyne collected 1 home run, 6 RBIs and scored 4 runs.

For the season, Gascoyne is hitting at a .366 clip with 1 home run, 10 RBIs and has scored 8 runs. He has 26 hits, 2 walks and a .387 on-base percentage.
