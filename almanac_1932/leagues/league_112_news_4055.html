Barrage of Hits Gets Smyth ABL Award
Monday, July 9th, 1923
It can be difficult for a young player to step out of the shadows and assert himself in the Atlantic Baseball League, but last week Leroy Smyth of the Pawtucket Red Sox reminded everyone just why he's here as he threw up numbers that made him an easy selection for Player of the Week. Smyth rocked opposing pitching for a .556 average (15-for-27), 3 home runs and 9 RBIs to come away with the award.

On the year Smyth has hit .417 with 13 home runs, 37 RBIs and scored 32 runs.
