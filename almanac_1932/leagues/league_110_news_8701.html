McClendon Gets 4-0 Shutout
Wednesday, July 13th, 1927
A favor to his bullpen. That's what Turner McClendon was doing at Jr. Coyotes Ballpark today as he blanked the Saskatoon Jr. Coyotes in a 9-inning complete game shutout.

"I went out there to try to eat up innings and help out our bullpen," the St. Louis Jr. Cardinaux right-hander said later. "I felt I had my good stuff, and it turned out my command was solid."

McClendon notched 7 strikeouts, surrendered 1 walk, allowed 5 hits, and kept the bullpen phone quiet all day long.

This year McClendon has posted a record of 6 wins and 1 loss with a 3.15 ERA. He has appeared in 9 games and struck out 66 in 60 innings.
