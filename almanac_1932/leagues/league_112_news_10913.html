Maier and Goldstein Fight, Suspended 4 Games
Saturday, June 22nd, 1929
The league commissioner's office had responded quickly to yesterday's major incident at Juniors Ballpark involving the Highlanders Tui Maier and the Voyageurs Isaac Goldstein. Both players have been given 4-game suspensions for violation of the league code of conduct. The suspensions will be effective immediately.

Thus far this year Maier has hit .355 with 3 home runs and 13 RBIs.

Goldstein's record so far this season is 1-and-1 with no saves and a 5.30 ERA. He has given up 15 hits in 18.2 innings with 15 strikeouts.
