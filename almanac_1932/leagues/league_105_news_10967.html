Kimmel Day-to-Day with Injury
Sunday, June 30th, 1929
The topic du jour around Minnesota is whether Grayson Kimmel of the Saints will suit up for today's contest. He was injured yesterday against the Reds while throwing the ball. The standout 23-year-old right fielder has a sore elbow and didn't rule out playing, but did say that if pain persisted, he would not risk further injury.

"Grayson will play if he can," said the team doctor. "He hasn't got a major injury, but at the same time, we need to monitor the situation and protect him as much as possible."

On the year Kimmel has hit .331 with 6 home runs, 35 RBIs and scored 25 runs.
