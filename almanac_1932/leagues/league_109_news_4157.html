Kameron Antonelli had an impressive outing at Harrisburg Grounds today, hurling the Harrisburg Robins to a 1-0 shutout victory over the London Indians.

"He threw strikes and he was always around the plate," London skipper Nattaporn Lamontagne said. "The ball was doing everything he wanted it to do. We hit some balls hard, but they were all right at the fielders."
