Morgantown Sits Atop East
Wednesday, July 22nd, 1925
Morgantown Mountaineers catcher Jaturon Malakul said celebrating a division championship never gets old.

"If this is what the outcome of every regular season was, I'd be a happy guy," he said. "It takes a lot of talent and determination to win a division like the East."

The club's 33-15 record has allowed it to officially seal up the division title, for the 7th time in franchise history.

Manager Logan Hevey said it would be a "pure joy and pleasure" to be able to bring the 3rd Adams Cup title to Morgantown.
