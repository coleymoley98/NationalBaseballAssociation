Star SP Signs Pact with Royales
Sunday, March 20th, 1932
The latest out of Montreal indicates that Glenden Croat has re-signed with the Royales and that the 30-year-old starting pitcher is set to earn $76,000 over a 5-year span.

Team officials tell BNN the deal will be officially announced at a press conference tomorrow.

During his career Croat has compiled a 23-15 record with a 4.26 ERA in 34 starts.
