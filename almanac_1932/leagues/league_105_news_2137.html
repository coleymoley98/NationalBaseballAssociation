Simandl Gets 4-0 Shutout

All went well for Coy Simandl of the Philadelphia Mets as he pitched his team to a 4-0 shutout victory over the Minnesota Saints, limiting them to 5 hits. In the postgame interview, both managers had glowing things to say about Simandl on the day.

In the contest, the Philadelphia left-hander rang up 1 and surrendered 2 walks.

"He didn't give us any second chances," said Saints manager Datsakorn Bergeron. "It's a guessing game with him and today we were always guessing wrong."

This season Simandl has compiled a 6-8 mark, 3.94 ERA and made 20 starts.
