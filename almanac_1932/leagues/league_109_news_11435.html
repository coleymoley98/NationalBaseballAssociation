Campisano Top Hitter in EL in August
Sunday, September 1st, 1929
It's perhaps an award he'll win many more times through his career, as at age 21 Walt Campisano has a lot of baseball ahead of him.

But for now the young Hamilton Blue Sox star can enjoy the numbers he put up in August, which have garnered him the nod as the Eastern League Batter of the Month.

His breakout month featured a .385 average with 5 home runs, 21 RBIs and 18 runs scored.

For the year Campisano has hit .375 with 13 home runs, 45 RBIs and scored 48 runs.
