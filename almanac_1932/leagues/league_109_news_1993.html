Player of the Week: Prioleau of the Nordiques
Monday, August 10th, 1931
Yutta Prioleau demonstrated his potential to be a star in the game last week and today he took home the Eastern League Player of the Week Award.

The Sherbrooke right fielder wreaked havoc on opposing pitchers last week, pounding them for a .517 average (15-for-29) with 1 home run and 10 RBIs on his way to claiming the honor.

For the season Prioleau has produced 81 hits in 239 at-bats for a .339 average with 2 home runs and gotten on base at a .409 clip.
