Blue Sox Come up Short of Playoffs
Wednesday, September 10th, 1924
While they stayed relevant late into the season, there will be no postseason berth for the Toronto Blue Sox. The club today was mathematically eliminated from postseason contention.

Toronto last appeared in the postseason in 1916. This is their 8th consecutive year missing the playoffs, and in total they have not advanced past the regular season 18 times in their 30-year history. In the last 10 seasons, the Blue Sox have missed the playoffs 8 times. Toronto has won the Riel Cup 3 times in their history.
