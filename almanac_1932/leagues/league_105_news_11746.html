Ottawa Captures Wild Card
Wednesday, September 3rd, 1930
Ottawa Indians manager Frank Boon said an Canadian League East championship was the goal, but they are still pleased to have won a wild card position.

"You'd rather go in there as the top team, but all that matters is we're in the playoffs," he said. "Once the postseason starts, everyone's got a chance."

Ottawa left fielder David Wintringham said the members of the team are all incredibly focused on capturing a Riel Cup, which would be the club's 1st.

"We all want this badly," he said.
