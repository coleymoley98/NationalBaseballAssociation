Voltigeurs Advance to Laurier Cup
Friday, August 2nd, 1929
Fans at Juniors Ballpark saw the Division Finals come to a close today, as the Drummondville Voltigeurs defeated the Pawtucket Red Sox by a score of 4-2, and advanced to the Laurier Cup.

Drummondville won the series 4-1 and will now continue their march toward the Laurier Cup.

"It feels good to be moving on, I can't deny it," said first baseman Windsor Correa, who was named series MVP. "Not everyone thought we'd make it this far."

The Drummondville Voltigeurs don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Champlain Coureurs - Halifax Highlanders series. Champlain leads the series 3 games to 2. The Laurier Cup schedule will be announced after the Division Finals finishes.
