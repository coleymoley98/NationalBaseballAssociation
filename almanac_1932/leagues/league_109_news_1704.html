Nalley's Hit Streak Soars to 20 Games
Thursday, July 9th, 1931
The Tecumseh Braves may have proven to be mere mortals today as they lost 2-0 to the Portland Liberty, but Gunner Nalley continued to hit like a god.

The first baseman collected 2 hits in 3 at-bats in the loss and extended his hitting streak to 20 games.

"Nalley is playing great and is on fire right now," Braves manager Calvert DeShon said. "Hopefully his play can spark the rest of our team and help us pick up some wins."

Nalley was apparently not happy with the game and when pressed by reporters in the locker room only said "nothing else matters but the loss."
Gunner Nalley doubled in the 2nd, walked in the 4th, singled in the 7th and grounded out in the 9th.

This season Nalley has hit at a .322 clip with 22 RBIs, 6 home runs and 21 runs scored. He has played in 37 games.
