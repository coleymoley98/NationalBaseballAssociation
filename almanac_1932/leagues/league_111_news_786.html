Toronto Captures Wild Card
Tuesday, July 22nd, 1930
Although they may be heading to the postseason as wild card winners, there's no shortage of expectation on the Toronto Jr. Blue Sox.

"We expect to win the Adams Cup, simple as that," starting pitcher Donne Grigg said bluntly after his team officially wrapped up a Eastern Baseball League wild card spot.

Should the team make good on those expectations, it will be the 5th Adams Cup title in franchise history.
