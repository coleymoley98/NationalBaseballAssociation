Alpines Sweep Bennett Cup
Thursday, August 1st, 1929
The Abbotsford Alpines ignited a huge celebration as they beat the Vancouver Giants 3-2 to win the Bennett Cup.

Abbotsford won the series 3-0, capturing the title for the 1st time.

"We stepped it up at the right time, and now we're the champs," said Abbotsford manager Erick Sethna. "It's a great feeling."

The champs finished the regular season 35-15 and claimed first place in the .

In the jubilant clubhouse after the decisive game, series MVP and third baseman Jongrak Colburn said the Alpines have "great fans and they deserve this."

"I am so happy for our city right now," he said.
