Hat Trick of Home Runs for Dostie
Friday, June 6th, 1930
Whatever Rattanapon Dostie ate for breakfast before his game against the Lincoln Pioneers today, it gave him some impressive power.

The Minneapolis center fielder smacked three balls up and out of the park at Juniors Ballpark. His home run barrage led the Jr. Saints to take the 18-7 victory over the Pioneers.

"When they throw it in your wheelhouse, you've got a shot at a homer," he told BNN reporters after the game. "I got three shots today and nailed them all."

On his 3-homer day, he went 5-for-5, scored 4 times, drove in 8 and put on a display that history will remember.

Rattanapon Dostie hit a solo-shot off Jongrak Thawanya in the 1st, hit a two-run home run off R.J. Doucet in the 3rd, was hit by a pitch in the 4th, hit an RBI single in the 6th, hit a GRAND SLAM off René Arriaga in the 7th and doubled in the 8th.

For the season Dostie is hitting .480, batted in 12 runs with 4 home runs and 7 runs scored.
