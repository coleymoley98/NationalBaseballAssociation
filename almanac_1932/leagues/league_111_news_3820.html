MacDonald Swats 3 HR Against Buffalo
Saturday, June 9th, 1923
Benjamin MacDonald brutalized the Buffalo Rapids pitching staff, launching three home runs in today's 12-8 victory at Juniors Ballpark. The Morgantown Mountaineers shortstop was red-hot, the ball just exploding off his bat all day.

"I don't try to hit homers," he said later. "I try to go out there and put a good swing on the ball, and sometimes it is going to go out of the park. I didn't try to hit the first one or the second one or the third one."

For the game, he went 4-for-5 with 6 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

"Too much Benjamin MacDonald today," Rapids manager Thawatchai Doors said. "That's my assessment of the game. That's what our problems boiled down to."

Benjamin MacDonald singled in the 2nd, hit a solo-shot off Saksit Deesai in the 4th, grounded out in the 6th, hit a solo-shot off Barrfind Culley in the 8th and hit a GRAND SLAM off Sid Cypert in the 9th.

MacDonald is hitting .394 so far this season with 12 RBIs and 7 runs scored. He has 13 hits including 3 home runs, 2 walks and .429 OBP.
