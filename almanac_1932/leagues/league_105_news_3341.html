Superstar C Inks Deal with Royales
Saturday, August 20th, 1932
Happy times in Montreal as the Royales and star catcher William Nicol put the finishing touches on a $20,000 contract extension that will keep the 28-year-old in town 2 years longer.

The signing is great news for Montreal because players of this caliber are hard to come by.

Nicol on the season is batting .325 with 6 home runs. His totals include 37 RBIs and 23 runs scored. He has played in 46 games.
