Western Baseball League Picks July's Best Batter
Sunday, August 1st, 1926
Even though he is a newcomer to Western Baseball League, J.J. Chevallier put up some good numbers in July and earned the WBL Batter of the Month award.

He was one of Boise's offensive leaders with a .403 average in 24 games. He also racked up 4 home runs, 33 RBIs, and 16 runs scored.

This year Chevallier is batting .367 with 5 home runs.
