Cardinaux, Thébault Stonewall Minnesota, 4-0
Sunday, May 23rd, 1926

"He stayed aggressive and his ball was really moving," said Minnesota manager A.J. Provost, after the game.

"If I make good pitches, I'm going to get some outs," Thébault said. "I was making good pitches the entire game."
