Acadiens Halt Lacasse's Hit Streak
Tuesday, June 19th, 1923
Bradley Lacasse went 0-for-4 at Field of the Acadiens today to break his consecutive-game hitting streak at 21 games. The 15-year-old Drummondville third baseman failed to get a hit today in the Voltigeurs 7-3 victory over the Pointe-Sainte-Anne Acadiens at Field of the Acadiens.

Lacasse did not make himself available to reporters after the game.

"I think Bradley is a little upset," Drummondville manager Tai Grigsby said. "He'll get over it."

During the current season Lacasse is batting at a .381 clip with 1 home run and 6 RBIs. He has played in 15 games and scored 8 times.
