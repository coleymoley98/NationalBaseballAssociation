Leboeuf Earns July's Top Batter Prize in WBL
Wednesday, August 1st, 1923
It's perhaps an award he'll win many more times through his career, as at age 14 Barry Leboeuf has a lot of baseball ahead of him.

But for now the young Spokane Eagles star can enjoy the numbers he put up in July, which have garnered him the nod as the Western Baseball League Batter of the Month.

His breakout month featured a .362 average with 9 home runs, 21 RBIs and 22 runs scored.

So far this season Leboeuf is hitting .376 with 13 home runs.
