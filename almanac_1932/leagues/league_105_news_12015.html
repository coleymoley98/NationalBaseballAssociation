Rebels Announce Deal for Veteran RP
Thursday, December 17th, 1931
Team insiders were tight-lipped prior to today's deal that brought 37-year-old reliever Lashawn Montano to New York.

Montano is considered one of the league's A-list players and certainly will enhance any chances the Rebels have to win a playoff spot this year.

On his career ledger Montano has a 98-73 mark, 576 appearances, 102 saves and a 3.19 ERA. He has 371 strikeouts in 991.2 innings pitched.
