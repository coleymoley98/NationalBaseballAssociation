West Title Goes to Jr. Brasseurs
Thursday, July 22nd, 1926
"We're not done yet!"

That was the resounding sentiment in the Juneau Jr. Brasseurs locker room when they were told they had clinched the West title, for the 8th time. With a 34-14 record, Juneau's stars certainly feel like they have a good chance to go all the way.

"We've learned a lot under Von Rivera's tutelage this year. He's taught us to never be over-confident, and to play every game like a championship game. I think with him and the team we have, we have a great chance to bring a 4th championship to Juneau this year," said starting pitcher Vince Berger.
