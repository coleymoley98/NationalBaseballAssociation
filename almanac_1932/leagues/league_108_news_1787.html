Cobarrubias at His Best, Blanks Seattle
Sunday, July 19th, 1931
Fans at Thunder Stadium witnessed the Winnipeg Thunder blank the Seattle Rainiers, 1-0 behind a superb effort by starter Timo Cobarrubias. The Winnipeg right-hander allowed 6 hits in recording the shutout.

"Timo was good today but I don't want to focus too much on a single game," Winnipeg manager Kendrick Markowski said later. "If you're capable of playing at this level, you're capable of having a good game. The real question is if you can learn from that game and repeat it."

Cobarrubias has a 5-3 season record so far. In 9 starts, he has fanned 36 batters in 76.1 innings with a 2.95 ERA.
