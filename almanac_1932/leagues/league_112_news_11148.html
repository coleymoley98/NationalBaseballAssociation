Voyageurs Come up Short of Playoffs
Sunday, July 21st, 1929
The playoff chase by the Coude Voyageurs has run out of steam. The club was officially eliminated from postseason contention today.

"We came up short," manager Tanongsak Petchdee said.

This is the club's 3rd playoff miss in a row. They last appeared in the postseason in 1926. In the last 10 seasons, the Voyageurs have missed the playoffs 8 times. In total, the Voyageurs have missed the playoffs 33 times in their 35-year history. Coude has won the Laurier Cup 5 times in their history.
