Canadian League Selects Best Rookie
Saturday, October 6th, 1928
It was a good season for Wolfeburg's Danny Sellers, capped off today by the announcement that he has been named the Canadian League Rookie of the Year Award winner.

The talented right fielder hit .299 in 93 games this season, racking up 103 hits, 15 home runs, 55 RBIs and 59 runs scored.

He received 16 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Zach Gaumer of the Ottawa Indians finished second in voting, while Cliff Yasuda of the Toronto Blue Sox finished third.
