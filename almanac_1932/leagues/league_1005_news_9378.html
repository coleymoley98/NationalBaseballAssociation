Ashcroft Breaks Old Record with His 7 Home Run
Tuesday, March 13th, 1928
Dean Ashcroft has had the all-time home run record held by Alex Champagne in his sights for quite a while now.

As of today, it belongs to him. At Los Angeles Grounds, he launched home run number 7 to surpass Champagne in the record book and complete his long-watched pursuit.

"It doesn't take away our focus on winning the championship," said the Team star, "but I'll admit I'm excited about this accomplishment."

Dean Ashcroft walked in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, flied out in the 5th and homered off Christopher Thériault in the 6th.
