Nordiques Bonilla Sizzles with Cycle
Friday, June 3rd, 1932
Adam Bonilla truly did it all at Progressive Field today.

The Sherbrooke Nordiques center fielder hit for the cycle -- collecting 1 single, 1 double, 1 triple and 1 home run -- as he paced his club to victory, 12-5 over the Kingston Indians.

"You know that sweet sound you hear when you hit the ball just right? I heard that every time today," Bonilla said later. "It was a heck of a feeling."

It was a game good enough for him to make a little history. He bombed Kingston pitching for 4 hits in 4 at-bats and his name will now go into the record books. Bonilla also racked up 2 RBI and 3 runs scored in the game.

Adam Bonilla singled in the 1st, walked in the 3rd, hit a two-run home run off Jon Bachtiar in the 4th, doubled in the 5th, tripled in the 6th and walked in the 8th.
