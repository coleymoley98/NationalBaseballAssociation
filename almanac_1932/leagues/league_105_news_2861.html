Saints Not Competitive in 9-0 Loss to Chicagou
Friday, June 24th, 1932
Logan L'Heureux held the Minnesota Saints to 3 hits and fashioned a fine 9-0 shutout at Field of the Saints today.

Speaking after the game, Chicagou Frontenacs manager Archie Cooper said "L'Heureux had everything going for him today."

He changed speeds effectively, stayed down, had a lot of movement," he said. "He was masterful."

L'Heureux rung up 5 strikeouts and surrendered no walks in the game.

"We couldn't get anything going. He certainly had our number," Minnesota manager Datsakorn Bergeron told reporters.

Checking out his stats for the season, L'Heureux has racked up 5 wins and 2 losses in 7 starts, 3.10 ERA and struck out 30 batters in 58 innings.
