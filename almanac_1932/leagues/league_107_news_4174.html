Barry Leboeuf Wallops 3 Homers for Eagles
Sunday, July 22nd, 1923
Barry Leboeuf went yard three times in a Spokane Eagles victory over the Victoria Admirals at Eskimos Ballpark today.

"I was in a groove," he said. "Every pitch they tossed up there looked like a grapefruit."

For the day, the Spokane first baseman went 3-for-4 against Victoria with 7 RBIs and 3 runs scored in a brilliant display of power hitting that led the Eagles to the 19-5 win.

"I tell you what. I'm shocked because I didn't think that last ball had enough to get out," he told BNN reporters in the clubhouse following the game. "I thought it was just going to be a long out."

Barry Leboeuf hit a solo-shot off Brock Naughton in the 1st, flied out in the 4th, hit a three-run home run off Brock Naughton in the 4th and hit a three-run home run off Charles Sauvé in the 6th.

Leboeuf on the season is batting .388 with 12 home runs. His totals include 39 RBIs and 36 runs scored. He has played in 45 games.
