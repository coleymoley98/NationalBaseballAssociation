Balls and Strikes Without the Strikes
Thursday, June 19th, 1930
Nick Nance found being on the mound today a tough spot. Said Nance, "I just couldn't get a strike to save my life. After the first few walks, I started trying to aim, which is just a big no-no."

Braves Manager, Peerapong Owens, wasn't ready to write this down as a total loss. "We saw a lot of things on the mound today, most of which we can fix up. Nick is a good kid and is going to be fine. In fact, I would wager if we put in the work and he puts in the effort like we know he can, he'll come out with better control than he has ever had."
