Pittsburgh Manager McLain Named Top Skipper in EL
Thursday, September 22nd, 1927
The Pittsburgh Liberty had an outstanding season, much of it due to their astute and gifted skipper. For his managerial excellence, Virgil McLain reaped the reward of being voted Eastern League Manager of the Year.

"A manager is only as good as the players he's managing," McLain told reporters. "If I didn't have the talent to work with, making all the right moves wouldn't have helped a bit."

Under McLain the Liberty finished first in the with a record of 49-33.
