NBA News: First-Year Player Draft
Monday, November 5th, 1923
Lost in all the hoopla of today's amateur draft are the young men who won't be going first, tenth or even fiftieth. Many of them will probably never make National Baseball Association rosters, but one and all will be spurred on by the possibility that someday they'll be playing on a championship club. Toronto, Juneau, Wolfeburg, Montreal and Philadelphia will have the first five choices. BNN analysts report that these players are likely to be high picks in the 1923 draft:


1. Chris Gagnon, 18 year-old 2B out of college.
2. Simon Hoagland, 18 year-old SS out of college.
3. Ned Silven, 18 year-old SS out of college.
4. Clarence Gibbs, 18 year-old 2B out of college.
5. Remi Girard, 18 year-old RF out of college.
6. Ryan Jacobs, 18 year-old 3B out of college.
7. Aldo MacNally, 18 year-old 1B out of college.
8. Gautam Mijangos, 18 year-old CF out of college.
9. Joel Cottle, 18 year-old LHP out of college.
10. Arsan Lin, 18 year-old LF out of college.
