On a Roll: Polk Hits for Cycle
Sunday, June 3rd, 1923
Requiring speed, power and regular contact, the cycle is often seen as a test of a complete hitter. For Mozes Polk, consider that test passed.

The 15-year-old third baseman hit for the cycle at Ballpark of Ranier today to lead the Kelowna Apple Sox to a 25-6 win over the Ranier Seahawks. All told, Polk delivered 4 hits in 5 at-bats with 4 RBI and 4 runs scored.

"It feels good of course, but I'm not going to kid anyone -- there's a lot of luck in hitting for the cycle," he said after the game. "Several unique things have to come together all in the same game."

Mozes Polk grounded out in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, walked in the 4th, hit a two-run triple in the 5th, hit a two-run home run off Mason Roberts in the 6th, walked in the 7th and doubled in the 9th.
