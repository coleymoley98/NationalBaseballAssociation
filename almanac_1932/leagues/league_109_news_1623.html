EL Top Batter Trophy Won by Moore
Wednesday, July 1st, 1931
At only 21 years of age, Eldrick Moore could very well be having many more months like the last this in his career.

That would be great news for fans of the Harrisburg Robins, as June was one to remember for the center fielder and he has captured the Eastern League Batter of the Month award.

On the month he hit .394 with 6 home runs, 31 RBIs and 22 runs scored.
