No Late-Season Magic Coming: Buffalo Out of Playoffs
Thursday, July 24th, 1930
A close finish won't make it any easier for the Buffalo Rapids to end their season early. The club has now been mathematically eliminated from playoff contention for the 1930 season.

"There were some things broken and some things said that are not repeatable," Rapids manager Thawatchai Doors.

Buffalo last appeared in the postseason in 1928. This is their 2nd consecutive year missing the playoffs, and in total they have not advanced past the regular season 31 times in their 36-year history. In the last 10 seasons, the Rapids have missed the playoffs 5 times. Buffalo last won the Adams Cup in 1926.
