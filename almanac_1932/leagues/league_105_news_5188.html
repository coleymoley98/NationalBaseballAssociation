Blue Sox Show No Mercy in 15-0 Thrashing of Liberty
Friday, July 4th, 1924
Today was Tadanori Kamai's turn in the rotation for the Toronto Blue Sox and he made the most of it, shutting out the Philadelphia Liberty on 4 hits.

The 27-year-old right-hander led the Blue Sox to an easy 15-0 victory, allowing 2 walks and tallying 5 strikeouts.
