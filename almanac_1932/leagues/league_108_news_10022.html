Edmonton's Garnier Collects July Batting Honor
Wednesday, August 1st, 1928
At only 21 years of age, Jordane Garnier could very well be having many more months like the last this in his career.

That would be great news for fans of the Edmonton Eskimos, as July was one to remember for the third baseman and he has captured the Western League Batter of the Month award.

On the month he hit .375 with 5 home runs, 34 RBIs and 25 runs scored.

So far this season Garnier has hit .306 with 7 home runs, 53 RBIs and scored 41 times.
