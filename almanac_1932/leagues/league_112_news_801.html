Jr. Nordiques Win Tight Saint Lawrence Race
Thursday, July 24th, 1930
Quebec City Jr. Nordiques fans are walking a little prouder today after their team officially clinched the Saint Lawrence.

"There's no reason to believe we can't win it all," starting pitcher Steve Lowenstein said bluntly. "Whether or not we're favorites, we can definitely do what it takes to bring the Laurier Cup home to Quebec City."

The division crown is the 5th for the Jr. Nordiques. The club will be looking for its 2nd Laurier Cup victory.

Team manager Supravee Dube said "preparation and focus" will be key to bring home the championship.
