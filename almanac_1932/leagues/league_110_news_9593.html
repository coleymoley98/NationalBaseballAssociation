Jr. Saints Chill Gallegos's Hot Streak
Monday, June 4th, 1928
It was the best of both worlds for the Minneapolis Jr. Saints today at Juniors Ballpark, as they defeated the Winnipeg Jr. Thunder 8-2 and also snapped Devin Gallegos's 23-game hitting streak. The 16-year-old Winnipeg right fielder failed to get a hit in 4 at-bats.

"Nothing personal against Gallegos, we just wanted to win the game," said Jr. Saints skipper Prasarn Heemkhao. "We did a great job keeping him off-balance and guessing."

When asked by reporters about his streak coming to an end, Gallegos frowned and said, "I won't lie and say that I'm not disappointed about it, but I'd feel much better if we had won."
