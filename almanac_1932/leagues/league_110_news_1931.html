CBL's #1 Batter Chosen
Saturday, August 1st, 1931
Minneapolis Jr. Saints fans may just have witnessed a star player blossoming before their eyes in July.

Rattanapon Dostie put up a remarkable month at the age of 17 and has been named the Central Baseball League Batter of the Month.

For the month, Dostie hit .360 with 7 home runs, 25 RBIs and 22 runs scored.

This year Dostie is hitting .403 with 9 home runs and 40 RBIs to his credit. He has gotten on base at a .456 clip and scored 38 runs.
