Nordiques Self Chosen August's Best in EL
Wednesday, September 1st, 1926
Over the past month Jeremy Self has turned quite a few heads. He's young, talented and able to pitch with the established arms in the Eastern League. He proved all that and now he's been named August's Pitcher of the Month.

The rookie for the Sherbrooke Nordiques posted 5-2 mark and a 3.45 ERA. In 7 starts he tossed 57.1 innings, permitted 43 hits and fanned 59 batters.

Self has a 12-8 season record so far. In 19 starts, he has fanned 146 batters in 144.2 innings with a 3.67 ERA.
