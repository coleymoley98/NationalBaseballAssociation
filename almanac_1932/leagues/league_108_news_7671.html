Great July Gets Schneider WL Best Hitter Award
Sunday, August 1st, 1926
For a rookie just breaking in, Russ Schneider certainly put together a successful month. It was good enough to win the WL Batter of the Month award for July.

The 20-year-old first baseman for the Omaha Falcons hit .398 with 4 home runs, 28 RBIs and 24 runs scored.

For the season Schneider is hitting .399, batted in 51 runs with 10 home runs and 37 runs scored.
