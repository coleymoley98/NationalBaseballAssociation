Choi Takes Best Bat Trophy in July
Thursday, August 1st, 1929
Casey Choi may be much younger than other hitters in the Central Baseball League, but he performed like a seasoned veteran in July to win the Batter of the Month award.

The 17-year-old Minneapolis first baseman hit .352 with 5 home runs, 28 RBIs and 20 runs scored.

This year Choi has batted at a .359 pace with 70 hits, 8 home runs, 54 RBIs and scored 31 times.
