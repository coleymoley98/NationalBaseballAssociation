Chicagou Trades Herring for Boston's Davies
Thursday, July 12th, 1923
The White Sox and Redcoats confirmed this afternoon that a trade had been agreed upon. In the deal, Chicagou will get 27-year-old minor league 3B Basil Davies, while Boston will receive 28-year-old LHP Parinya Herring.


Currently Herring has a 5-4 won-lost record in 8 starts with a 3.61 ERA. He also has 12 strikeouts in 72.1 innings.

His lifetime stats show that Davies has hit .265 and connected for 8 home runs.
