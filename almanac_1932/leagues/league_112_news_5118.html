Edwards Cracks 5 Hits
Wednesday, June 25th, 1924
Jeff Edwards put on a hitting clinic today as the Pointe-Sainte-Anne Acadiens dumped the Coude Voyageurs 10-1 at Coude Grounds.

The Pointe-Sainte-Anne catcher made Coude defenders earn their pay, lashing Coude pitching for 5 hits in 5 at-bats in the game. He told BNN reporters after the game that his strategy was "just hit it where they pitched it."

"Too often I try too hard to pull the ball," Edwards said in his postgame press conference. "Today, I just went with the pitch."

Jeff Edwards singled in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, doubled in the 5th, doubled in the 7th and singled in the 9th.

In 21 games this season Edwards has batted at a .316 pace with 24 hits and no home runs.
