Wranglers Win Tight Race
Saturday, August 29th, 1925
Fans of the Calgary Wranglers have a lot to get excited about, as the club has captured the crown.

It's the 1st time in franchise history that the club has finished atop the standings and they now have an opportunity to capture the franchise's 2nd Western League championship.

Manager Eric Fitzsimmons said there's "a lot of excitement" in Calgary over the club right now and they hope to live up to expectations.

For his part, starting pitcher Logan L'Heureux said the division championship is "just a start" for his team.

"We want to go all the way, win the World Series," he said. "Winning the division - we could get used to this."
