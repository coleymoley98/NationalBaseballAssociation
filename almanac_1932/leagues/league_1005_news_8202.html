Thursday, March 17th, 1927
Some days you can't win for losing. Just ask Aldo MacNally -- he was the King of Swing against the Pennsylvania, but his Michigan were beaten 19-12 anyway.

The Team first baseman had 6 chances against the Team and delivered 5 hits in a sensational game.

"Unbelievable feeling," he told the press. "It felt great to be hitting like that, but a downer when you look at the scoreboard. 5-for-6 does wonders for your confidence, but it's a shame we couldn't win, too."

Aldo MacNally singled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 4th, singled in the 6th, doubled in the 8th and flied out in the 9th.

Presently MacNally is hitting .525 with no home runs and 6 RBIs. He has played in 9 games and scored 13 times.
