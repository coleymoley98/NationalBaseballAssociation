5-Hit Barrage by Parnham
Tuesday, July 19th, 1932
Anucha Parnham put on a hitting clinic today as the Trois-Rivieres Royales dumped the Tecumseh Braves 12-0 at SunTrust Park.

The Trois-Rivieres second baseman made Tecumseh defenders earn their pay, lashing Tecumseh pitching for 5 hits in 6 at-bats in the game. He told BNN reporters after the game that his strategy was "just hit it where they pitched it."

"Too often I try too hard to pull the ball," Parnham said in his postgame press conference. "Today, I just went with the pitch."

Anucha Parnham singled in the 1st, hit an RBI triple in the 2nd, grounded out in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, singled in the 7th and hit an RBI single in the 8th.

This season Parnham has hit at a .351 clip with 31 RBIs, 4 home runs and 27 runs scored. He has played in 39 games.
