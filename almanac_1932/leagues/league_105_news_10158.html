Baltimore Punchless in 9-0 Loss to Indians
Monday, August 20th, 1928
"Good pitching beats good hitting" is an old baseball adage.

Well, good pitching also beats bad hitting.

Norihiro Uchiyama of the Ottawa Indians proved the point at Sportsmans Park 1911 today, holding the Baltimore Robins to 7 hits in a complete game shutout victory.

"Uchiyama made us look real bad today," Robins manager Eldin Gabriel said in his postgame interview. "He was outstanding. He was down in the zone with all his pitches. Everything was sinking."

Uchiyama had 8 strikeouts and gave up 3 walks as the Indians took the 9-0 win.

So far this year Uchiyama has a 4-1 record with a 2.38 ERA.
