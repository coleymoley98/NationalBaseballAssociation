Lévesque and Willcox Suspended for Free-for-All
Sunday, June 16th, 1929
Laurent Lévesque of Quincy and Attapong Willcox of Quebec City were the main participants in a recent bench-clearing brawl and today the league handed out its punishment. Lévesque will have to miss 4 games for his part in the brawl, while Willcox was given 6 games. League officials announced their suspensions today.

In the 1929 season Lévesque is hitting .191 with 1 home run.
