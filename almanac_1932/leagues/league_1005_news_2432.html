Arsenault Blisters Team
Wednesday, March 9th, 1932
Kittipoom Arsenault was at the head of a Team hit parade today at Villanueva Fields. The second baseman went to the plate 7 times and came away with 5 hits, sparking the Pallister to a 29-10 victory over the Team Saskatchewan.

"I told the guys in the dugout, just be thinking about hitting the ball hard somewhere," the sweet-swinging Arsenault told reporters after the game. "Seems like it worked out well for us today."

Saskatchewan skipper Morris McGhee said later that the club gave up "too much plate."

"Not the way we planned it," he said. "Every hitter in the league will rip you when you do that."

Kittipoom Arsenault doubled in the 1st, grounded out in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 2nd, walked in the 4th, flied out in the 5th, hit a two-run single in the 6th, singled in the 8th and hit an RBI single in the 8th.
