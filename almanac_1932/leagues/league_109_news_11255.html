Gunning Garners EL Batter of the Month Award
Thursday, August 1st, 1929
Even though he is a newcomer to Eastern League, Russ Gunning put up some good numbers in July and earned the EL Batter of the Month award.

He was one of Albany's offensive leaders with a .404 average in 25 games. He also racked up 5 home runs, 15 RBIs, and 20 runs scored.

Hitting .383 to date, Gunning has 11 home runs and 40 RBIs. He has played in 53 games.
