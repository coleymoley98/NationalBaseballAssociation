Boise Narrowly Wins
Tuesday, July 28th, 1931
Manitho Townes of the Boise Broncos said he'll remember his team's 1931 championship for the rest of his life.

"To pull it out by only one game, it's pretty memorable," the starting pitcher said. "Something to tell the grand kids one day."

The team's record sits at 36-15 and the division title is the 3rd in team history.

Team manager Rudy Seguro said "it would mean so much to Boise" to bring the 3rd Western Baseball League championship home.

"It's Bennett Cup or bust at this point," he said.
