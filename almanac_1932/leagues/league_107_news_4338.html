Vancouver's Rushing Takes Best Pitcher Prize
Sunday, August 12th, 1923
The WBL Pitcher of the Year for 1923 has been presented and the Western Baseball League winner is Myles Rushing of Vancouver.

"It's always nice to receive an award like this," said Rushing. "I just hope I can improve on my performance next year and be in the running again," the Giants star told reporters.

Rushing excelled with an impressive 7-2 record in 12 starts. In 89.1 innings he yielded 55 hits and 19 walks, while striking out 128 and compiling a fine 1.51 ERA.

He received 8 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Verne Hunt of the Ranier Seahawks finished second in voting, while Bentley Lundeen of the Ranier Seahawks finished third.
