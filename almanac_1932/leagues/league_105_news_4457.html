NL Top Pitcher Award for August to Yuschak
Saturday, September 1st, 1923
In August, Cooper Yuschak tore through the National League like a seasoned veteran -- quite impressive for a player only 23 years old.

The young Quebec starter posted a 5-0 mark and an ERA of 2.04 to earn the Pitcher of the Month award. In 53 innings he collected 11 strikeouts while limiting the opposition to a .242 average.

Currently Yuschak has a 11-7 won-lost record in 21 starts with a 3.14 ERA. He also has 65 strikeouts in 169 innings.
