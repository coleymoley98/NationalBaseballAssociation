WL's Top Starter is Goulet

There's something about a young rookie winning the Western League Pitcher of the Month trophy that sends shock waves through the league. Such is the case with François Goulet, a 19-year-old starter for the Edmonton Eskimos.

In July, he topped off the month with a 5-0 mark and 2.91 ERA in 4 starts, tossed 43.1 innings, fanned 26 and held opposing teams to a .189 average.

In 9 starts this year Goulet has posted 8 wins while suffering 2 losses. He has pitched 83.1 innings, allowed 63 hits, struck out 46 and has a 3.89 ERA.
