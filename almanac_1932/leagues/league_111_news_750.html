East Champs Crowned: Hamilton Jr. Steelers
Saturday, July 19th, 1930
It's a banner year for the Hamilton Jr. Steelers, they have won the East.

"There's so much pride in our room and on our roster, anything but a division title wouldn't have been acceptable," said starting pitcher Mason MacCabe. "In the playoffs we'll just have to take it one day at a time and keep battling."

The Jr. Steelers this year have battled to a 31-14 mark, which is good for the club's 3rd East title.

"I'm extremely proud of my players and look forward to seeing what we can do in the postseason," said manager Fabrice Hardy.
