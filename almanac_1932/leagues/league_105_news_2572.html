White Sox, Lunn Stonewall Boston, 4-0
Monday, May 9th, 1932
Raj Lunn of the Chicagou White Sox put the clamps on the opposition and held the Boston Redcoats scoreless in a 3-hit whitewash.

"He's got some good stuff, but we should have done better than that," said Boston skipper Brian Ahn after the 4-0 loss.

Lunn was on target most of the game and finished with 5 strikeouts and 3 walks.
