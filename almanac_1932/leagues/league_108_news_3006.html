Great Day for Klein -- 5 Hits
Saturday, July 9th, 1932
It was high-fives all around for Adam Klein at Edmonton Grounds today.

He got them from his teammates and he got them at the plate during an outstanding hitting performance against the Edmonton Eskimos. Klein smashed 5 hits in 6 at-bats on his monster day, leading his club to a 16-4 win.

"Adam was hitting everything," said Coyotes manager Peerapong Owens. "It really was a special game for him."

In the opposing dugout, Eskimos manager Nattaporn Lamontagne told BNN beat writers that Klein "ambushed" the club.

"Obviously we didn't have much success against him, but we'll have another chance," he said. "Hopefully, Klein oversleeps next time."

Adam Klein singled in the 2nd, hit an RBI single in the 3rd, hit an RBI double in the 5th, doubled in the 6th, grounded out in the 7th and singled in the 9th.

Klein is hitting .290 this year with no home runs, 5 RBIs and 3 runs scored. He has 9 hits in 31 at-bats, 4 walks and a .371 on-base percentage. Klein has played in 21 games.
