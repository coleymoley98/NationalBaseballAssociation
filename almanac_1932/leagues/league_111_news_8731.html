Star of the Week Honored in EBL
Monday, July 18th, 1927
It can be difficult for a young player to step out of the shadows and assert himself in the Eastern Baseball League, but last week Eldrick Moore of the Fort Wayne Pioneers reminded everyone just why he's here as he threw up numbers that made him an easy selection for Player of the Week. Moore rocked opposing pitching for a .476 average (10-for-21), 2 home runs and 9 RBIs to come away with the award.

This season Moore has delivered 47 hits in 143 at-bats for a .329 average with 6 home runs. He is getting on base at a .442 clip and his totals also include 33 RBIs and 31 runs scored. This year Moore has played in 42 games.
