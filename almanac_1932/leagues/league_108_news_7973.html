Vancouver Fires Manager, Lamontagne Will Be Replaced
Tuesday, October 5th, 1926
The Vancouver Giants and Nattaporn Lamontagne have parted ways. It was announced today by club officials that Nattaporn Lamontagne had been dismissed as the team's manager.

Giants GM Joe Unknown told reporters that "certain goals were mutually agreed upon this season and, in our estimation, they have not been reached. Our team has not made the progress we were promised."

Lamontagne told BNN that he is "proud of what has been accomplished in my tenure as the Giants manager. We have a better club now than when I first got here. I'm just sorry it ended this way."
