CBL Best Hitter Trophy Goes to Wilder
Wednesday, August 1st, 1923
At only 17 years of age, Harry Wilder could very well be having many more months like the last this in his career.

That would be great news for fans of the Adams Mounties, as July was one to remember for the right fielder and he has captured the Central Baseball League Batter of the Month award.

On the month he hit .318 with 7 home runs, 22 RBIs and 17 runs scored.

Looking at his season stats, Wilder has compiled a .301 batting average and a .385 on-base percentage, while hitting 14 home runs and driving in 41 runs.
