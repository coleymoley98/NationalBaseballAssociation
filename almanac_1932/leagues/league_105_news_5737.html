CL Reliever of the Year Award Winner Named
Tuesday, October 14th, 1924
Nicola Patterson now has a big bargaining chip as he potentially heads towards free agency - he is the winner of the 1924 Canadian League Reliever of the Year Award.

The Boston Redcoats star turned in what he called the "year of my life," in winning the award.

It included a 3.52 ERA through 79.1 innings and 50 relief appearances. He recorded 15 saves, 24 strikeouts and had only 38 walks. He compiled a record of 11-11, while limiting hitters to a .295 average.

He received 16 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Lashawn Montano of the Baltimore Robins finished second in voting, while Noah Asselin of the Chicagou White Sox finished third.
