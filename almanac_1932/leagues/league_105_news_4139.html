Auger Dominates in 8-3 Win over Canadian League All-Stars
Tuesday, July 17th, 1923
Ballpark of Quebec was the place to be for baseball fans tonight, as the best NBA players gathered for the annual all-star game.

On the field, the National League downed the Canadian League by a score of 8-3. Off the field, game MVP Noah Auger said he thinks everyone had a good night.

"You looked into the stands and it seemed like everyone was having a fantastic time," said the 26-year-old, who went 2-for-2 in the game. "A game like this is good for the sport."

In his career Auger has hit .321 with 695 hits, 44 home runs, 295 RBIs and scored 348 runs.
