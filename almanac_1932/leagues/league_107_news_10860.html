Anton Gagné Strokes 3 Home Runs
Friday, June 14th, 1929
Anton Gagné of the Broncos went on a hitting spree at Broncos Ballpark today and earned membership in the select club of hitters who have hit three home runs in a game. He blasted three into the stands in a 12-11 loss to the Kelowna Apple Sox.

"I'm not happy with the outcome of the game, but it was still a great game for me," Gagné said in the clubhouse following the loss. "I have been making solid contact lately, but not getting any lift. Today I got it up in the air and out of the ballpark. I had my home run swing down pat. Got some good pitches to hit, too."

The Boise center fielder did indeed have quite a game, collecting 3 hits in 4 at-bats, 8 RBIs and scoring 4 runs.

Anton Gagné grounded out in the 2nd, was hit by a pitch in the 3rd, hit a three-run home run off Jonathan d'Artagnan in the 5th, hit a GRAND SLAM off Vernon Sánchez in the 7th and hit a solo-shot off Alphonse Plante in the 8th.
