Basil Betts Drills 3 HR in One Game
Monday, June 18th, 1923
Sherbrooke Nordiques right fielder Basil Betts had a magnificent game against the Providence Redcoats today, rocketing three balls out of Providence Grounds to help the Nordiques take the 10-3 victory.

"I got some good pitches to hit and I creamed them," he said after the game. "The first was a big, fat fastball and the last two were hanging curves. All three were mistakes."

For the game, Betts went 3-for-4 with 5 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

"There's a fine line between hitting home runs and line drives," Sherbrooke manager Chaichan Phonsa said later. "Seems like Basil found his home run stroke today."
