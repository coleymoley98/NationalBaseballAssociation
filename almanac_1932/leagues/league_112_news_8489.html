Labbé of Sherbrooke Player of the Week
Monday, June 20th, 1927
It can be difficult for a young player to step out of the shadows and assert himself in the Atlantic Baseball League, but last week Liam Labbé of the Sherbrooke Canadiens reminded everyone just why he's here as he threw up numbers that made him an easy selection for Player of the Week. Labbé rocked opposing pitching for a .417 average (10-for-24), 2 home runs and 7 RBIs to come away with the award.
