Lawson Pops 3 Out of the Park Against Falcons
Wednesday, June 24th, 1931
Portland Thorns right fielder Michael Lawson sensed he was going to have a good day at the plate, but he still might have underestimated it a little.

Prior to the start of today's contest with the Omaha Falcons, Lawson told teammates he was sure he would hit a home run. He did -- three times.

"During batting practice I really had my swing down pat," he recalled. "I felt some thunder in the lumber."

The big day from Lawson pushed the Thorns past the Falcons, 24-6. To go along with the homers, he collected 7 RBIs and scored 3 runs.

"I was seeing good, feeling good and swinging good," Lawson told reporters after the game.

Michael Lawson hit a two-run home run off Jeremy Wingert in the 1st, hit a three-run home run off Nur Suttirat in the 1st, walked in the 3rd, hit a two-run home run off Thong Kon Byers in the 4th, walked in the 5th and grounded out in the 8th.

This season Lawson is hitting at a .380 pace with 4 home runs, 21 RBIs and has scored 24 times.
