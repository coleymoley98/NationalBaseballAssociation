Rehab Road Tough For Blue Sox Dylan Sharp
Tuesday, September 22nd, 1925
The Toronto Blue Sox announced today that starting pitcher Dylan Sharp will not be returning as quickly as expected from injury. "There was a bit of a setback, but nothing to be concerned about," one team official told reporters. "Dylan will probably miss another 4 days because of this, but everyone expects him to make a full recovery."

Sharp has fashioned a 10-9 mark for the season, 3.86 ERA and thrown 167.2 innings in 22 starts. Opposing clubs are batting .280 against him.
