Red-Hot Mayberry Scorches Robins
Friday, July 31st, 1931
Chicagou second baseman Amnart Mayberry had a day to remember today, 5-for-7 against the Baltimore Robins at Sportsmans Park 1911 as the White Sox picked up a 20-4 victory.

"He was patient up there today," Chicagou skipper Tommy Dupuis told the press after the game. "I like the way he waited for his pitch and wasn't overly aggressive. Mayberry has a tendency to chase bad balls sometimes."

Amnart Mayberry singled in the 1st, doubled in the 3rd, hit a two-run home run off René Caron in the 4th, singled in the 7th, hit an RBI double in the 8th, flied out in the 9th and grounded out in the 9th.

Currently Mayberry is batting .323 with 1 home run and 42 RBIs.
