Toronto Crowned Kings of the East
Tuesday, July 21st, 1931
It's official! The Toronto Jr. Blue Sox have secured the top spot in the East. Toronto's manager Pongsak Strong was quick to give credit to his players.

"They believed in this team all year, and never gave up."

It won't be an easy road for Toronto as they try to compete for their 5th Adams Cup victory. They have to hope that their success will translate to the post-season, which is certainly not always the case. This was the 7th division title for the team.
