Canadian League Selects Best Rookie
Wednesday, October 21st, 1931
The top-ranked rookie performer in the Canadian League was Pittsburgh second baseman Hunter Olson. It was announced today by league officials that he had been named the winner of the 1931 Rookie of the Year Award.

Olson chalked up some good season stats, including a .308 batting average and .355 on-base percentage, while racking up 2 home runs, 28 RBIs and 35 runs scored.

He received 9 first place votes out of a possible 20. Chi Broadus of the Wolfeburg Celtics finished second in voting with 8 first place votes, while Ben Tessier of the Pittsburgh Quakers finished third with 3 first place votes.
