St. Louis Downs Cincinnati as Joly Tosses 2-0 Shutout
Tuesday, September 2nd, 1930
Renato Joly of the St. Louis Cardinaux shut out the Cincinnati Reds 2-0, stopped them on 6 hits and had 5 strikeouts and 1 walk in a strong outing at St. Louis Grounds today.

"He just came out and was on the attack from the first pitch he threw," St. Louis manager Virgil McLain said later. "When Renato stays ahead in the count, he does well. That's the key."

Joly has a 5-5 season record so far. In 12 starts, he has fanned 48 batters in 94.1 innings with a 2.48 ERA.
