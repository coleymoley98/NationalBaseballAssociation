Larkings Shuts Out Quebec, 3-0
Wednesday, September 10th, 1930
Fans at Ballpark of Quebec witnessed the Chicagou Frontenacs blank the Quebec Nordiques, 3-0 behind a superb effort by starter Harry Larkings. The Chicagou right-hander allowed 6 hits in recording the shutout.

"Harry was good today but I don't want to focus too much on a single game," Chicagou manager Archie Cooper said later. "If you're capable of playing at this level, you're capable of having a good game. The real question is if you can learn from that game and repeat it."

To date this season, Larkings has a record of 11 wins and 10 losses and a 4.06 ERA. He has appeared in 25 games and struck out 68 in 190.2 innings.
