5-for-6 Day for Wintringham
Thursday, September 1st, 1927
It's not something you read about in the sports pages every day -- a 5-for-6 day. For many, it might come around only once in a career -- and for David Wintringham, it came around today.

The 21-year-old left fielder scattered hits all over Sportsmans Park 1911 to pace the Ottawa Indians to a 14-10 victory over the Baltimore Robins.

Baltimore skipper Eldin Gabriel told reporters in the press room that "Wintringham deserves a hat-tip."

"He hit some good pitches against us," he said. "In fact, according to our scouting reports, Wintringham hit several that he is not supposed to be able to hit."

David Wintringham singled in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, hit an RBI double in the 5th, struck out in the 6th, hit an RBI single in the 7th and singled in the 9th.

So far this season Wintringham has 5 home runs and a .348 batting average.
