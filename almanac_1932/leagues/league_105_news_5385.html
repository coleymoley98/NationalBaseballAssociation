Player of the Week: Chicagou's Motley
Monday, July 28th, 1924
At the age of 28, Travis Motley might have some potential he hasn't realized yet -- if his success at the plate last week is any indication. Motley is hitting .263 this season with 7 home runs, 40 RBIs and 28 runs scored. He has 60 hits in 228 at-bats.
