CL Selects Rookie Star of the Month
Monday, August 1st, 1927
Ottawa's Beau Lobdell collected 24 hits to snare the Canadian League Rookie of the Month for July.

He batted .471 with 1 home run, 12 RBIs and 15 runs scored.

The 20-year-old is barely old enough to shave, but he could definitely contend for Rookie of the Year if he keeps up this pace.

So far this season Lobdell is batting .416 with 1 home run, 19 RBIs and 20 runs scored. He has 42 hits in 101 at-bats, 6 walks and a .450 OBP. Lobdell has played in 30 games.
