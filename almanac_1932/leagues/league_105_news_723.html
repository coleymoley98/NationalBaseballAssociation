Pittsburgh Quakers Ship Sulley to Cincinnati
Monday, July 14th, 1930
The Quakers and Reds pulled the trigger on a deal that will see 32-year-old LHP Kyle Sulley head to Cincinnati in exchange for 19-year-old minor league RF Quinn Poonra, 23-year-old RHP Lamarcus Guillemin, 20-year-old minor league 1B Terry Dobratz and 19-year-old minor league LHP Ashley Matcham. A source close to Pittsburgh officials said "The teams spent long hours coming to the agreement and in the end, both agreed the deal should help both clubs."


Up to now Sulley has compiled an ERA of 3.92 with a 6-4 mark over 13 starts. He has 59 strikeouts in 114.2 innings and has held opponents to a .299 batting average.
