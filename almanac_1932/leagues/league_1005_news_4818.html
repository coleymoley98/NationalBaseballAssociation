Rachal Tops Canadian Junior Baseball Championship Pitchers
Friday, March 28th, 1924
Dee Dee Rachal of the Team Erie had a tremendous season and was chosen the Best Pitcher winner in the Canadian Junior Baseball Championship.

He crafted a standout 3-0 mark in 3 starts, striking out 9, while walking 2. He worked 21 innings, yielded 11 hits and posted a 0.43 ERA. Opposing clubs had a .155 average against him.

He received 16 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Noah Daigle of the Team Minnesota finished second in voting, while Mark Lancaster of the Team Ontario finished third.
