Hit Parade: Boucher Pops 5 Hits
Sunday, June 24th, 1923
Max Boucher told the press after his 5-hit day, "Every batter wants to have a quality at-bat every time he steps up to the plate. He wants to hit the ball hard."

Well, that's just what Boucher of the Coude Voyageurs did against the Drummondville Voltigeurs. He went to the plate 6 times and 5 times he delivered base hits, but his club still suffered a 13-12 defeat.

"Not much I can say," offered Boucher in the postgame press conference. "I got a bunch of hits, but it would have been much better to get a win. Can I trade 5 hits for the win?"

Max Boucher singled in the 1st, hit a solo-shot off Damon Pott in the 3rd, struck out in the 4th, hit an RBI triple in the 6th, hit an RBI single in the 7th and hit an RBI single in the 9th.

Boucher is presently hitting at a .360 pace with 9 home runs and 23 RBIs. He has played in 22 games and scored 22 times.
