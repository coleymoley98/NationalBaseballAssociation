Sunday, September 2nd, 1928
Fans at Comiskey Park 1910 witnessed the Wolfeburg Celtics blank the Chicagou White Sox, 5-0 behind a superb effort by starter Kritsana Dubois. The Wolfeburg right-hander allowed 3 hits in recording the shutout.

"Kritsana was good today but I don't want to focus too much on a single game," Wolfeburg manager A.J. Desjardins said later. "If you're capable of playing at this level, you're capable of having a good game. The real question is if you can learn from that game and repeat it."

So far this year Dubois has made 8 starts, tossed 63 innings, struck out 14 batters and walked 15. His record is 2-4 with a 4.43 ERA and he has limited opposing teams to a .284 batting average.
