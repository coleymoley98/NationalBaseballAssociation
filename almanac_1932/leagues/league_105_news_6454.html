Another Setback For Ottawa's Ambler Stahl
Tuesday, July 21st, 1925
Just when it looked like Ambler Stahl would soon return to the Ottawa Indians, the first baseman suffered yet another setback in his recovery from a strained rib cage muscle. According to his doctors, treatment was progressing well until yesterday, when Stahl experienced quite a bit of pain during a workout. Further examination revealed he had caused more damage and would now be disabled for at least 5 days.

So far this season Stahl is hitting .324 with 4 home runs.
