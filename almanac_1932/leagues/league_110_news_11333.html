CBL Best Manager is Wilkins of the Mounties
Wednesday, August 14th, 1929
Timothy Wilkins, skipper of the Adams Mounties, was voted 1929 Central Baseball League Manager of the Year and accepted the award earlier today.

He guided Adams to first place in the with a 32-18 record.

"I'm very happy about this," he said at the press conference. "But I'm more happy about what my boys did on the field. They battled their butts off for me all year. They should get the credit."
