Vine Chosen NL's Best Newcomer
Wednesday, August 1st, 1923
Yes, Jackson Vine is just a rookie. But he is a special rookie. Today the 22-year-old Quebec starter was awarded the National League Rookie of the Month trophy for his standout performance in July.

He dominated the rookie class, registering 4 wins and 1 loss in 6 starts with 16 strikeouts in 50.1 innings and a 1.25 ERA.

Not bad for a first-year man.

This season Vine has gone 7-2 with an ERA of 2.38, posting 28 strikeouts in 90.2 innings.
