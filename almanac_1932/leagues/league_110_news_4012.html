CBL Votes Mounties Hopson Top Player
Monday, July 2nd, 1923
The Player of the Week has been chosen by the Central Baseball League. The award went to Apisit Hopson, center fielder of the Adams Mounties.

In a sensational hitting performance, he wrecked league pitchers with a .478 average to win the honor. Hopson went 11-for-23 for the week, totaled 3 home runs and 12 RBIs and scored 9 times.

So far this season Hopson has 7 home runs and a .342 batting average.
