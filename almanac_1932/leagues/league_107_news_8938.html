1927 WBL Best Player, Ranier's Brisebois
Monday, August 15th, 1927
Jean Brisebois took Western Baseball League by storm this year, and he did it all at the age of 15.

For his spectacular campaign, the Ranier Seahawks star has been named the 1927 Western Baseball League WBL Player of the Year recipient.

"To be considered amongst the best in the league is overwhelming," said the right fielder.

On the season he compiled a .404 batting average to go with a .467 on-base percentage. The native of Vancouver, The United Provinces also added 9 doubles, 3 triples, 11 home runs, 38 RBIs and 46 runs scored.

He received 7 first place votes out of a possible 8. Panuwat Delannoy of the Vancouver Giants finished second in voting with 1 first place vote, while Hudson Schilcher of the Vancouver Giants finished third.
