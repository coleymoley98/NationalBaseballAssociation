Morales Cracks 5 Hits
Sunday, September 5th, 1926
There aren't many who could be pro ballplayers and there are even fewer who could put up 5 hits in a day.

Carmen Morales is one of those rare few, as he went 5-for-5 against the Philadelphia Liberty at Fenway Park 1912 today. The monster effort from the 28-year-old shortstop helped the Boston Redcoats take the win, 8-2.

"We made some bad pitches and he took advantage of it," said Liberty manager Hervé Morel. "That's the game, though. Don't make mistakes and you'll win ballgames. Make mistakes and, well, you saw what happened today."

Carmen Morales hit an RBI single in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, hit a two-run single in the 6th and singled in the 8th.

Morales on the season is hitting .350 with 2 home runs. His stats include 70 RBIs and 74 runs scored.
