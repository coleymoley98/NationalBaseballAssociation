Cincinnati Trades Kwangkeaw for Montreal's Flam
Tuesday, December 9th, 1924
In an effort to add some depth to its roster, the Cincinnati Reds have swapped 32-year-old C Angelo Kwangkeaw to the Montreal Royales for 36-year-old RHP Peerawit Flam and 23-year-old minor league RHP Antony Delâge. The Reds GM told reporters, "We saw a chance to upgrade our ball club and took it."


In 843 career games Kwangkeaw has hit .264 with 769 hits, 18 home runs, 345 RBIs and scored 233 runs.

Over his career Flam has a 23-27 won-lost record with a 4.73 ERA. In 80 games he has recorded 3 saves, and the 36-year-old has fanned 142 batters in 445.1 innings.
