East Title Goes to Mountaineers
Friday, July 20th, 1923
The Morgantown Mountaineers have silenced their critics, backed up those who believed in them and won the East.

"You always think you've got a chance to win the division, but until you get it done you never really know how things are going to play out," said closer Nattaporn Piller. "I think we're all grateful to get the chance to vie for the Adams Cup."

It is the 5th division crown for the franchise and manager Annan Wills said in a sporting landscape where you are judged by what you've most recently accomplished, "it feels incredibly good to be at the top of the pile."
