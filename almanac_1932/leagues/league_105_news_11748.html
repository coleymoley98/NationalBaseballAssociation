Toronto Devastated by Playoff Elimination
Wednesday, September 3rd, 1930
Jabari Perrault, manager for the Toronto Blue Sox said it's a "dark day" for his club. The reason? The Blue Sox were officially eliminated from playoff contention earlier today.

Toronto last appeared in the postseason in 1928. This is their 2nd consecutive year missing the playoffs, and in total they have not advanced past the regular season 23 times in their 36-year history. In the last 10 seasons, the Blue Sox have missed the playoffs 9 times. Toronto last won the Riel Cup in 1907.
