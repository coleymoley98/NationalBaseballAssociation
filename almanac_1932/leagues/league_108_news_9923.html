Injury Brings Season to End for Laflamme
Sunday, July 22nd, 1928
The news was not unexpected, but that didn't make it easier to accept for the Vancouver Giants: starting pitcher Supachai Laflamme is out for the year. Laflamme suffered a torn rotator cuff on 07/20/1928. The team knew that the chances of seeing him return were slim to none. But today it was made official.

This season Laflamme has compiled a 4-5 mark with a 4.23 ERA.
