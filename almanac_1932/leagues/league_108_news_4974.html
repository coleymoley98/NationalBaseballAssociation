Roberts Goes 5-for-5 Against Calgary
Thursday, June 5th, 1924
It was a high-five day for Virgil Roberts, at least for a while.

The Portland third baseman got plenty of them from his teammates when he reached the five-hit milestone during today's game against the Calgary Wranglers, but they were in short supply at the end, as Calgary took a 7-6 win. Roberts finished with an impressive 5-for-5 line, but the Thorns couldn't hold off the Wranglers.

"Virgil was hitting everything," said Thorns manager Maurice Wigglesworth. "It's too bad the other guys couldn't rise to the occasion."

Virgil Roberts hit a solo-shot off Sakutaro Kaneko in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, hit an RBI single in the 7th and singled in the 9th.

This year Roberts has hit 1 home run and posted a .444 batting average.
