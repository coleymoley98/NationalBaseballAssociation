Atlantic Baseball League Announces July's Best Batter
Friday, August 1st, 1930
Champlain Coureurs fans may just have witnessed a star player blossoming before their eyes in July.

Chad Pelletier put up a remarkable month at the age of 17 and has been named the Atlantic Baseball League Batter of the Month.

For the month, Pelletier hit .333 with 9 home runs, 23 RBIs and 18 runs scored.

Thus far this year Pelletier is hitting .292 with 12 home runs, 36 RBIs and 36 runs scored.
