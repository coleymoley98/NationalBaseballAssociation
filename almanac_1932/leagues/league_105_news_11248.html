Despite the number of years he's been in the league, Ray O'Gorman still gets excited when he puts together a quality stretch like he had in July. The 34-year-old starter for Montreal was named National League Pitcher of the Month earlier today.

He collected 5 wins and no losses with 27 strikeouts in his 6 starts, putting up a fine 1.15 ERA in 55 innings of work.

O'Gorman has fashioned a 12-4 mark for the season, 3.38 ERA and thrown 138.1 innings in 17 starts. Opposing clubs are batting .253 against him.
