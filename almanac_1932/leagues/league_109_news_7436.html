No Hits for Wilder, Streak Ends at 33
Sunday, July 4th, 1926
Maybe it really is taboo to talk about a hitting streak before it's over. Hours after speaking to the press about his 33-game hitting streak, Albany center fielder Harry Wilder went hitless in 3 at-bats today at Rebels Stadium.

The sudden hitting drought came as the Rebels took the 7-4 win over the Trois-Rivieres Royales.

"Nope, it's not magic," Wilder said in his postgame press conference. "Nobody jinxed me. Streaks don't last forever. I've heard about it every day for weeks now. My roomie told me to get a hit before every game and wished me luck. I just ran out of hits."

So far this year Wilder is hitting .371 with 10 home runs.
