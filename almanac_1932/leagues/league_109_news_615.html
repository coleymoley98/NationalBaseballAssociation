Redcoats End Dubois's Streak
Wednesday, July 2nd, 1930
The Providence Redcoats got more than the 17-0 victory at SunTrust Park today -- they also put an end to the 21-game hitting streak of Tecumseh Braves third baseman Niki Dubois by holding him without a hit in 4 at-bats.

After the game, Dubois seemed more concerned with his team taking the loss than with the demise of his streak.

"I had some great looks at some pitches that I should have hit," he said. "I missed some that I shouldn't have swung at. All in all, I wasn't much help with the bat today."

So far this year, Dubois is batting .377 with 7 home runs and 23 RBIs.
