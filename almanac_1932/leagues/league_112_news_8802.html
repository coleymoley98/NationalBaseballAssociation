Highlanders Clinch Wild Card
Monday, July 25th, 1927
As the clock struck midnight in the Halifax Highlanders clubhouse, the celebration showed no signs of slowing down. The Highlanders were certainly ready to celebrate their Atlantic Baseball League wild card berth.

"It's time to let off a little steam after a grind of a season," said starting pitcher Arthur Jenkins. "We may act a bit like teenagers tonight celebrating, but we'll be back to being big leaguers tomorrow."

With a 29-21 record, Halifax will be seeking its 5th Atlantic Baseball League championship.
