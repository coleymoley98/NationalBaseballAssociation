Shaw Week's Best Player
Monday, June 23rd, 1930
It was an exceptional week for Bertie Shaw of the Saskatoon Jr. Coyotes.

First he beat the devil out of the ball, hitting .536 and as you would expect, he was voted the Player of the Week in the Central Baseball League. Last week Shaw went 15-for-28, notched no home runs and 8 RBIs and scored 4 runs. All in all, a very good week for him.

For the season Shaw has produced 38 hits in 81 at-bats for a .469 average with no home runs and gotten on base at a .533 clip.
