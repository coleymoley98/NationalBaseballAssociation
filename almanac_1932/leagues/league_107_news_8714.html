Broncos Séverine Stops Apple Sox
Saturday, July 16th, 1927
The Boise Broncos did little to support starter Mathis Séverine against the Kelowna Apple Sox, but it turned out he didn't need the help.

The right-hander spun a complete game shutout against the Apple Sox, striking out 9 and surrendering 1 hit. The performance from Séverine allowed Boise to escape with the 1-0 win despite an anemic offense.

"Nobody came to me and said 'Mathis, we're gonna need you to win this one for us today,' but I could feel it," Séverine told BNN reporters following the win. "I'm the guy that will step up for you when you need it."

In 9 starts this year Séverine has crafted a 4-2 record and a 4.68 ERA.
