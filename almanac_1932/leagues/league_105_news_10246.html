Ottawa-Boston Trade: Baum for Childs
Saturday, September 8th, 1928
34-year-old minor league LHP Billy Wall and 21-year-old minor league 1B Wirat Baum will now be playing for the Boston Redcoats and 20-year-old minor league RHP Dallas Sontikul, 23-year-old minor league 2B Oliver Childs and 24-year-old minor league C Calvy Gaddis will be suiting up for the Ottawa Indians. The trade was announced today at a press conference by club officials.
