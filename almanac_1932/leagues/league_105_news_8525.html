28-Game Streak Ends, Stahl Hitless
Thursday, June 23rd, 1927
It just wasn't Ambler Stahl's day.

The Ottawa Indians took the 9-8 win today over the New York Rebels, but the Rebels mound staff did what nobody else could -- they stopped the 28-game hitting streak of Indians first baseman Ambler Stahl.

"We might have focused a bit too much on stopping Stahl," New York manager Alexis Blouin commented afterward. "He had a nice hitting streak going, but the Indians have other hitters and they did their job today."

In his season totals, Stahl has 2 home runs, .417 BA, .444 OBP, 35 RBIs and 26 runs scored. He has played in 35 games.
