Red-Hot Schneider Scorches Apple Sox
Saturday, June 2nd, 1928
It was a big day for Melvyn Schneider and he brought the Spokane Eagles along for the ride.

Schneider led his club to a 13-4 victory over the Kelowna Apple Sox today at Ballpark of Kelowna, smoking Kelowna pitching for 5 hits in 5 at-bats. The 16-year-old right fielder later called the victory "sweet."

Melvyn Schneider singled in the 1st, hit a three-run home run off Carlton Gathers in the 3rd, hit an RBI single in the 5th, singled in the 7th and doubled in the 9th.
