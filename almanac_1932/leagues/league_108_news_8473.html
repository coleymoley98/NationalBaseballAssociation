Injury Brings Season to End for Patenaude
Saturday, June 18th, 1927
In a news release, the Calgary Wranglers announced that second baseman Matteo Patenaude would miss the rest of the season due to a torn posterior cruciate ligament. Patenaude sustained the injury on 06/16/1927. He "will be sorely missed," said his manager.

Presently Patenaude is batting .353 and has collected no home runs, 7 RBIs, .441 OBP and scored 13 runs.
