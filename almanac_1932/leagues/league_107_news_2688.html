Silvertips Kupalang Gets No-Hitter
Saturday, June 4th, 1932
Everett Silvertips starter Krit Kupalang came up big today at Broncos Ballpark with an exceptional 3-0 outing against the Boise Broncos. Not only did he whip them, but Kupalang held them hitless, too.

"My arm was a bit sore in warmups," confessed Kupalang. "Sometimes you go out there and you're not feeling all that hot. You get into the game and all of a sudden in the seventh inning you look up and see the scoreboard and think to yourself, 'Oh, my goodness, I have a no-hitter!' You're so focused on what you're doing, it just happens."

In the course of the no-hitter, Kupalang collected 9 strikeouts and surrendered 2 walks -- a day good enough to get his name in the history books.
