Pittsburgh Trades Bowers for Quebec's Roberts
Friday, July 15th, 1932
Reports have surfaced that Pittsburgh has agreed to send 28-year-old 3B Stan Bowers to the Quebec Nordiques in exchange for 24-year-old minor league LF Teerapol Roberts, 20-year-old minor league RF Alex Deslauriers and 19-year-old minor league RHP Bowie Wilkins. "It's a done deal," said one executive speaking on the condition of anonymity. "I'm surprised word got around so quickly. We just signed off on this an hour ago." The deal apparently is awaiting physicals before an official announcement is made by either team.


This season Bowers is batting at a .250 clip with no home runs and 11 RBIs.

His all-time numbers show Roberts has compiled a .276 batting average and collected 82 hits, 3 home runs and 24 RBIs.
