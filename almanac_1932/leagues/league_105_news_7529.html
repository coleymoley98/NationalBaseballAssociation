Chicagou Frontenacs Ship Lacroixille to Montreal
Thursday, July 15th, 1926
Several Frontenacs and Royales players soon will have to start shopping for a new home. It was announced today that the two teams came to agreement on a trade. Chicagou will get 20-year-old minor league C Ojai Edwards and Montreal will pick up 25-year-old RHP Jiramet Lacroixille in return. "I'm not surprised by the news," one of the players told reporters, "It's part of the business and I understand that. I am looking forward to playing for the Frontenacs."


Up to now Lacroixille has compiled an ERA of 5.28 with a 2-7 mark over 10 starts. He has 27 strikeouts in 75 innings and has held opponents to a .285 batting average.
