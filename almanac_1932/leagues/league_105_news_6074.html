Montreal Shuts Out Quebec Behind Howarth, 3-0
Thursday, May 28th, 1925
In an outstanding mound performance, Montreal Royales starter Dwight Howarth fired a complete-game shutout against the Quebec Nordiques at Montreal Grounds today.

Howarth was the key to the Royales's 3-0 win. The 32-year-old right-hander was in high spirits on the mount for much of the game, his mood largely undented by the 12 hits and 3 walks he allowed.

"12 hits?" he said with a laugh following the game. "Listen, I'd be happy with a 30-hitter as long as it was a shutout."
