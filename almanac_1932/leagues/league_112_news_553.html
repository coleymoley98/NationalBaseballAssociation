Sparks Torches Gunners for 5 Hits
Sunday, June 29th, 1930
Even as a kid, Sir'Anthony Sparks said his coaches told him the key to hitting was making contact, and letting everything else flow from there. At Ballpark of Montreal today, Sparks showed how well he learned that lesson.

The 15-year-old second baseman had 5 hits in 6 at-bats for the Montreal Jr. Royales, helping his team knock off the Quincy Gunners by a score of 21-20.

"They always said, just keep your eye on the ball, don't overswing, make contact and you never know what will happen," he said. "That's all I did today."

Sir'Anthony Sparks walked in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, hit a three-run home run off Dural Loftis in the 5th, struck out in the 8th, hit a two-run single in the 9th and hit an RBI single in the 11th.

In his 1930 season totals, Sparks has 2 home runs, .393 BA, .438 OBP, 20 RBIs and 26 runs scored.
