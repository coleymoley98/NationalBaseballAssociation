Tremblay Sharp, Whitewashes Nordiques, 3-0
Thursday, June 18th, 1931
André Tremblay was dynamite at Ballpark of Quebec today, blowing away the Quebec Nordiques in a shutout victory. The Cincinnati Reds starter had everything working as he held the Nordiques to 4 hits in the 3-0 win.

"He was pure nasty out there today," said Quebec skipper Donatien Auger. "I don't think any team in the league would've had a chance against what he was bringing."

This year Tremblay has worked 73.1 innings with 35 strikeouts en route to a 5-3 record and a 2.09 ERA.
