Dawkins Tosses 5-0 Shutout for Ottawa
Friday, August 1st, 1924
Dylan Dawkins of the Ottawa Indians was lights-out in a 6-hit whitewash of the Chicagou White Sox.

"I had my good stuff and it went where I wanted it to go," he told BNN reporters after the contest.

Dawkins registered no strikeouts and no walks in shutting out Chicagou, 5-0.

This year Dawkins has registered 7 wins and 7 losses in 17 starts with a 3.01 ERA while registering 20 strikeouts and 22 walks in 125.2 innings.
