Ottawa Indians Ship Flam to Chicagou
Thursday, November 19th, 1925
Several Indians and Frontenacs players soon will have to start shopping for a new home. It was announced today that the two teams came to agreement on a trade. Ottawa will get 21-year-old SS Brendan Grenier and Chicagou will pick up 37-year-old RHP Peerawit Flam and 20-year-old minor league 2B Edmund Collingwood in return. "I'm not surprised by the news," one of the players told reporters, "It's part of the business and I understand that. I am looking forward to playing for the Indians."


Lifetime Flam has a 28-34 won-lost record. His career ERA is 4.73.
