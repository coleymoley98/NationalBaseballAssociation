Ottawa Trades Lopez for St. Louis's Stahn
Tuesday, July 14th, 1931
There were plenty of hugs and handshakes all around as 25-year-old 2B Jean-David Lopez and 20-year-old minor league 2B Julian García left the Ottawa Indians today en route to joining the St. Louis Cardinaux organization. 28-year-old RHP Jiradej Stahn and 21-year-old minor league RHP Patrick Riewerts will be traveling to Ottawa Indians to complete the swap.

St. Louis will retain a portion of Stahn's remaining contract.

Currently Lopez is batting .353 with no home runs, 9 RBIs and 9 runs scored.

Stahn has taken the mound for 16 relief appearances this season, nailing down 1 save while notching a 1-4 record with a 3.30 ERA. The Ottawa reliever has limited opposing batters to a .243 batting average.
