Rapids Captures Wild Card
Monday, July 25th, 1932
The Buffalo Rapids celebrated wildly tonight as the team officially captured a wild card position in the Eastern Baseball League.

"In some ways you're more excited than if you would have won the division, because you know you were closer to being left out of the playoffs," said right fielder Jaxon Soucy. "It's not lost on our guys that once the playoffs start we're going to have to step things up and play our best."

The Rapids have a 29-21 record and will be looking to turn their wild card berth into the franchise's 4th Adams Cup win.
