Van Allen Shines in 8-0 Gem
Saturday, June 13th, 1931
Warwick Van Allen of the Juneau Brasseurs was masterful today, pitching a 5-hit shutout, 8-0, against the Minnesota Saints.

By going the distance, he was at the same time able to give his bullpen a rest, a fact appreciated by Juneau manager Joshua Barry.

"It's nice to have someone who can do that from time to time," he said. "He had command of every ball and every pitch. Great location, too."

Van Allen had 5 strikeouts and no walks in the outing.

This year Van Allen has compiled a 7-1 record with a 3.22 ERA.
