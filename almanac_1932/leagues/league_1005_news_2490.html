Ettrick Breaks Old Record with His 11 Home Run
Tuesday, March 29th, 1932
The pursuit is over. Earnest Ettrick of the Team Erie has broken the career home record of Dean Ashcroft by hitting number 11. Even as pressure mounted and he inched closer to the hallowed record, Ettrick tried to remain even-keel.

"I took it one home run at a time," he said. "Records have a way of taking care of themselves."

Earnest Ettrick grounded out in the 1st, singled in the 3rd and homered off Brady Lopez in the 4th.

In 16 games Ettrick is hitting .431 this season with 28 hits, 7 home runs, 30 RBIs, 7 walks, .486 OBP and has scored 21 runs.
