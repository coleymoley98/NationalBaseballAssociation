Quebec Trades Poltham for Montreal's Interiano
Sunday, November 9th, 1930
Done deal! The Quebec Nordiques have agreed to send 25-year-old C Nophand Poltham and 19-year-old minor league RF Nualphan McGill to the Montreal Royales for 26-year-old LHP Will Interiano. The trade, rumored for days, was completed in the wee hours this morning.


In his career Interiano has tossed 818.2 innings, fanned 340 batters and walked 243. His lifetime won-lost record is 55 wins and 41 losses with a 3.19 ERA.
