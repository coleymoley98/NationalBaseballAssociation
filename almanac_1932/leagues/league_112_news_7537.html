Hits Galore: Hughs Drills 5 Hits
Friday, July 16th, 1926
Troy Hughs was unstoppable, but the St. John's Mariners were not.

Despite his hitting heroics at T-Mobile Park today, the Mariners lost 16-13 to the Coude Voyageurs. Hughs battered Coude for 5 hits in 5 at-bats. His only consolation will be a boost in his batting average and a brief mention in the record book.

Hughs just shook his head after the game, telling BNN reporters, "It's the team with the most runs that wins, not the most hits."

Troy Hughs hit a solo-shot off A.R. Lo in the 2nd, singled in the 2nd, hit a two-run home run off Choem Yathawee in the 4th, walked in the 5th, hit a solo-shot off Fabio Cloutier in the 8th and doubled in the 9th.

For the year Hughs has hit .323 with 11 home runs, 34 RBIs and scored 32 runs.
