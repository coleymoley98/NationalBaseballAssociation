Michaels Wins NL's Best Bat Trophy
Wednesday, September 1st, 1926
Seasoned by several years of professional experience, 26-year-old Tim Michaels put together a solid offensive month for Montreal and was crowned August's top batter in the National League.

He batted .453 with 4 home runs, 18 RBIs and 11 runs scored.

So far this season Michaels has gotten 73 hits in 205 at-bats for a .356 batting average with 5 home runs. His stats include 36 RBIs and 32 runs scored. He has played in 63 games.
