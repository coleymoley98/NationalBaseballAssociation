One Run Enough for Baltimore
Saturday, June 2nd, 1928
Liam Baluch kept the opposition off the scoreboard and the Baltimore Robins were all smiles after winning 1-0 against the Chicagou White Sox at Comiskey Park 1910 today. The right-handed Baluch gave up no runs in his start, permitted 1 hit and had 4 strikeouts.

"If there was a stat for 'team shutout,' this would be it," Baluch said later. "My catcher called a great game, guys delivered at the plate and when I messed something up the guys behind me were where they needed to be."

In 7 starts this season, Baluch has picked up 4 wins while suffering 2 losses. He has pitched 61 innings, allowed 51 hits, struck out 23 and posted a 2.66 ERA.
