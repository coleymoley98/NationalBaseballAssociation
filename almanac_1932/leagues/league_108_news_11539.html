Manager Huynh Discharged by Portland
Tuesday, October 1st, 1929
Few players in the Portland Thorns organization seemed upset to learn about Romie Huynh's dismissal as manager, a move announced today in a team press release.

While no player would speak on the record, one disgruntled reliever complained about Huynh's confrontational style, saying "He's the kind of guy who thinks the best leader is the guy who yells the most."

Another added, "Romie wanted to win, but he just didn't have the respect of the clubhouse."

Huynh's replacement has not yet been named, though an official announcement is expected soon.
