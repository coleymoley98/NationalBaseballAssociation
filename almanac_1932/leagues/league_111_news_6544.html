Division Finals Goes the Distance, Canton Prevails
Sunday, August 2nd, 1925
Canton Legends won a nail-biting series against the Morgantown Mountaineers 3-2, wrapping up the Division Finals finale 16-3 at Juniors Ballpark.

Legends first baseman Wyatt Campo was named the series MVP.

He batted .273, drove in 12 runs and scored 2 to lead Canton into the next round of play.

"Wyatt was real solid for us, real solid," said Legends manager Sean Wilkinson. "I normally say I'd like to have nine of him out there, but thankfully we got by with just one this time."

The Canton Legends will play against the Fort Wayne Pioneers in the next round. Fort Wayne won their series against the North Bay Battalion 3 games to 1. The Adams Cup schedule will be announced after the Division Finals finishes..
