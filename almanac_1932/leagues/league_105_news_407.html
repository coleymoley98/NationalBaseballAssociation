200 Wins for Lions Roca
Monday, June 9th, 1930
It was a special day at Bennett Park for Detroit's Steve Roca. The Lions ace logged his 200th career win against the Brasseurs. In the 7-0 decision Roca tossed 9 innings, surrendered 4 hits with 2 walks and 5 strikeouts.

"I'm real fortunate that I had the opportunity to play with such good players during my career," said Roca. "I just couldn't have drawn it up any better. I'm glad I did it in this uniform, too. Detroit is a great organization to play for."

Since his debut, Roca has marked up 200 wins and 135 losses and fashioned an ERA of 3.29. He has fanned 1287 batters while pitching 3071.2 innings.
