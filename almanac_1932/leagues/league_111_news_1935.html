Legends on to Adams Cup
Saturday, August 1st, 1931
The Canton Legends are off to the Adams Cup.

By a final score of 9-3 at Jr. Steelers Ballpark, Canton ended the Division Finals and knocked out the Hamilton Jr. Steelers by a series score of 3-1.

"Two great teams, only one can go on and we're happy that's us," said Murithat Mizell, who was named series MVP. "There's no easy games, no easy series in the Eastern Baseball League playoffs."

For the series, Mizell hit .563 with a .611 on-base percentage. He had 1 home runs, drove in 6 RBI and scored 7 runs.

The Canton Legends will play against the Toronto Jr. Blue Sox in the next round. Toronto won their series against the Fort Wayne Pioneers 3 games to 0. The Adams Cup schedule will be announced after the Division Finals finishes.
