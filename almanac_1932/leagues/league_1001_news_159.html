Guérin Establishes New Record for Ks
Thursday, April 3rd, 1930
It was an historic game at Blue Sox Ballpark. The stage was set for Team legend Thomas Guérin to write his name in the league record book for the most strikeouts in a career -- and he followed through.

Guérin sat down batter number 49 of his career in a blaze of glory to set a new record for strikeouts, breaking the mark held previously by the great Steve Roca.

"When you first come into the league, it never enters your mind about breaking records," Guérin told reporters at a postgame press conference. "Only when you've been around as long as I have do records become a possibility. Now that it's happened, I've got to get back to worrying about winning ballgames and not worrying about records. Don't get me wrong, it's great to have your name in the record book, but it'll be nice to get back to normalcy, too."

In 6 starts this season Guérin has notched a 3-2 record and a 3.38 ERA.
