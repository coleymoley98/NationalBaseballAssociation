SP Ratté Agrees to $77,400, 6-Year Offer from Quebec
Wednesday, December 15th, 1926
The Quebec Nordiques have called a press conference later today to announce the signing of starting pitcher Sommai Ratté to a 6-year, $77,400 contract.

Both Ratté and the Nordiques are said to be very pleased to come to this agreement.

Since coming into the league, Ratté has a 102-86 mark and a 3.72 ERA. The 31-year-old hurler also has struck out 446 batters in 1673.2 innings and permitted 1904 hits.
