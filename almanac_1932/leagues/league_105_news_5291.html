Great Day for Leboeuf, Hits for Cycle
Friday, July 18th, 1924
Dean Leboeuf grabbed a piece of baseball history at Polo Grounds today, when he hit for the cycle and added his name to the record books. The Baltimore Robins took a little gloss off his shine, however, when they grabbed a 7-5 victory over the New York Rebels.

For the day, Leboeuf went 5-for-7 with 2 RBI and 1 run scored.

"I felt comfortable at the plate in a way I haven't felt all season," Leboeuf said after the game. "It was like they were throwing beach balls at me. The ball seemed that big to me. And it just floated in."

Dean Leboeuf hit a two-run home run off Maurice Dionne in the 1st, flied out in the 3rd, singled in the 6th, doubled in the 8th, tripled in the 9th, flied out in the 12th and doubled in the 13th.
