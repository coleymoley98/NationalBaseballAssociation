Great Stroke: Osborne Gets 5 Hits
Wednesday, July 2nd, 1924
No matter what the Buffalo Rapids threw at him today, Oscar Osborne hit it. He easily handled Rapids pitching, going 5 for 5 and helping the Jr. Steelers to a 14-3 triumph at Ballpark of Buffalo.

"The way I was swinging today, I think I could have closed my eyes and hit the ball," he told BNN reporters in the postgame interview.

Told of Osborne's quip, Rapids skipper Thawatchai Doors said he "would take him up on that next time."

Oscar Osborne singled in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, hit an RBI double in the 4th, walked in the 5th, singled in the 7th and singled in the 9th.

Thus far this year Osborne is hitting .337 with 6 home runs, 16 RBIs and 16 runs scored.
