Krogman Fires Shutout in Spokane Victory
Thursday, June 23rd, 1932
Curt Krogman put a hex on the Vancouver Giants in a sparkling 3-0 shutout performance, mesmerizing them on a 5-hit gem.

"I felt good physically," he said after the game. "Everything's comfortable. You should pitch well when that's the case."

The Giants were anything but comfortable against the 20-year-old Spokane Eagles hurler, who fanned 5 and gave up 5 walks.

"Krogman used his changeup very well, very effectively," said opposing manager D.J. Sala. "He threw it in hitter's counts."
