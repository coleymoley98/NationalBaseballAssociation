Falcons Win 1930 Adams Cup
Thursday, August 7th, 1930
It was a hard-fought battle for both teams to make it this far. But as the saying goes, "to the victors go the spoils." For the victorious Springfield Falcons, that meant the Eastern Baseball League Adams Cup trophy.

The Falcons took a 10-4 victory over the Hamilton Jr. Steelers at Juniors Ballpark, winning their 1st championship after taking the series 3-1.

"We've got grit," said Falcons first baseman Hubert Lehoux, who was named MVP of the series. "We know how to win when we have to."

The new titleholders completed the regular season with a first-place finish in the West and a 33-17 record.
