Davenport-Adams Brawl, Gateson and Cork Suspended
Wednesday, June 18th, 1924
Robert Gateson of the Davenport Rivermen and Cyril Cork of the Adams Mounties received notification from the league commissioner's office that they had been suspended for 4 and 6 games respectively for their part in the on-field ruckus in a recent game at Ballpark of Davenport.

This season Gateson is batting .304 with 17 hits and no home runs. He has scored 7 times and has 7 RBI.

Season-to-date Cork has rung up 1 win and 1 loss in 6 appearances. He has an ERA of 4.66 and has fanned 31 batters in 29 innings.
