Mijangos Captures NL Batter of Month Award
Thursday, September 1st, 1932
Seasoned by several years of professional experience, 27-year-old Gautam Mijangos put together a solid offensive month for Chicagou and was crowned August's top batter in the National League.

He batted .400 with 4 home runs, 25 RBIs and 17 runs scored.

For the season Mijangos is batting .306 with 83 hits and 9 home runs. He has scored 52 times and batted in 50.
