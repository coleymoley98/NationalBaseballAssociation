Minnesota Wins SS Salo Sweepstakes
Monday, November 3rd, 1930
Zak Salo and Minnesota put the finishing touches on a $17,850-a-year deal that will keep the 24-year-old in a Minnesota uniform for 8 years.

Team officials reportedly held their breath at the last moment, since Salo had second thoughts mulling over other offers.

During his career Salo has played in 492 games, batted .310 with 19 home runs and 571 base hits.
