Chicagou Secures Top Spot in National League West
Thursday, September 1st, 1932
The Chicagou Frontenacs may have made it look easy, but the club said winning the National League West was no easy feat.

With a record of 63-37, the team has wrapped up the division in plenty of time to prepare for the postseason, where they will vie for the franchise's 5th Riel Cup.

"We may have won the division by a large number of games, but that doesn't mean it wasn't a lot of hard work," said starting pitcher Eric Woodmore. "We put a lot into this and now it's time to focus on the Riel Cup."
