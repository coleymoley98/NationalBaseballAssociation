Toronto Trades McFadden for Cincinnati's Lawson
Friday, June 22nd, 1923
In a move that has left many fans wondering, the Toronto Blue Sox have traded 37-year-old RHP Panupan McFadden and 19-year-old minor league LHP André Tremblay to the Cincinnati Reds. In return, the Blue Sox receive 19-year-old minor league RF Michael Lawson, 19-year-old minor league LF Josiah Cristinzio and 23-year-old minor league RHP Antonio Point.


In 9 starts this season, McFadden has recorded 2 wins and absorbed 5 losses. He has pitched 59.1 innings, allowed 75 hits, struck out 13 and has a 4.70 ERA.
