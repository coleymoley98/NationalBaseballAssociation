Viloria Gets Award for Relief Work
Tuesday, September 16th, 1930
His manager and teammates call Seamus Viloria a true bullpen ace.

The Western League calls him the winner of the Reliever of the Year Award for 1930.

The Calgary Wranglers relief specialist out of Chicagou, The United Provinces, won the award with 6 saves in 21 relief appearances, 27 strikeouts in 45.1 innings and 31 walks.

He compiled a record of 2 wins and 2 losses and had a 4.17 ERA.

He received 10 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Myles Rushing of the Winnipeg Thunder finished second in voting, while Kyle Nedd of the Seattle Rainiers finished third.
