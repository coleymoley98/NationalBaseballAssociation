New York Trades Jones for Baltimore's Kau
Wednesday, December 4th, 1929
The New York Rebels and Baltimore Robins have put the finishing touches on a deal that was weeks in the making. New York will part with 25-year-old RHP Baird Jones, while the Robins have dealt 23-year-old RHP Randy Kau.

"This one took a while to figure out," said New York general manager Grady Taylor. "We must have gone back to the drawing board five or six times over the past few weeks. I'm glad we finally got the deal done. I think it is a good one for both clubs."


Since joining the league, Jones has compiled an ERA of 5.60 with a career record of 8-16. He has 58 strikeouts in 229.2 innings and has held opponents to a .307 batting average.
