Aquino Rips Ottawa for 5 Hits
Friday, September 23rd, 1932
There aren't many who could be pro ballplayers and there are even fewer who could put up 5 hits in a day.

Nat Aquino is one of those rare few, as he went 5-for-6 against the Ottawa Indians at Lincoln Park today. The monster effort from the 23-year-old first baseman helped the Chicagou White Sox take the win, 17-8.

"We made some bad pitches and he took advantage of it," said Indians manager Frank Boon. "That's the game, though. Don't make mistakes and you'll win ballgames. Make mistakes and, well, you saw what happened today."

Nat Aquino hit an RBI single in the 1st, hit a two-run double in the 2nd, flied out in the 4th, hit a two-run triple in the 6th, doubled in the 8th and doubled in the 9th.

On the year Aquino is hitting .290 with 49 hits, 5 home runs, 35 RBIs, .376 OBP and has scored 22 runs.
