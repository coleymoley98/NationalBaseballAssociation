Steelers No Match for Lundeen, Robins in 12-0 Thumping
Thursday, May 8th, 1930
It wasn't easy but Bentley Lundeen made it look that way.

Lundeen silenced the Hamilton Steelers, 12-0, in a Baltimore Robins triumph at Steelers Ballpark today. Despite the appearance of dominance, Lundeen said later that he felt like he was struggling all game. He gave up 2 walks and 5 hits.

"It didn't feel quite right today, but I made the decision to bear down and work through it," he said. "I made a few mistakes but it goes to show what you can do with grit and not much else."
