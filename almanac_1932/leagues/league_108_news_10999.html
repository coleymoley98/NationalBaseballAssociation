Seattle Loses Lebeau for Season
Monday, July 1st, 1929
"Not what I wanted to hear," said Seattle's Bunnawat Lebeau to reporters this afternoon, as he revealed that the injury he sustained on 06/30/1929 would force him to miss the rest of the season. "The doctor made it clear," Lebeau said. "There's no chance for me -- none at all -- to come back this year. That's the last thing I wanted to hear." Lebeau has been out of action since he suffered a damaged elbow ligament.

This year Lebeau has recorded a 1-4 mark. In 7 starts he has fanned 48 batters in 47 innings with a 4.60 ERA.
