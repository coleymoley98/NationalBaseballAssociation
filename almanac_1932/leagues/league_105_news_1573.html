Boisvert Hurls Ottawa to Shutout Victory
Sunday, June 28th, 1931
It wasn't easy but Caleb Boisvert made it look that way.

Boisvert silenced the Philadelphia Liberty, 6-0, in a Ottawa Indians triumph at Lincoln Park today. Despite the appearance of dominance, Boisvert said later that he felt like he was struggling all game. He gave up no walks and 6 hits.

"It didn't feel quite right today, but I made the decision to bear down and work through it," he said. "I made a few mistakes but it goes to show what you can do with grit and not much else."

This season Boisvert has gone 6-2 with an ERA of 3.86, posting 26 strikeouts in 79.1 innings.
