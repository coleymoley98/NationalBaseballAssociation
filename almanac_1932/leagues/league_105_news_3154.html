Big Day with Bat: McNeil 5-for-5
Monday, July 25th, 1932
5 times is the charm -- at least for Rodney McNeil.

The Pittsburgh Quakers third baseman tore up the Wolfeburg Celtics to the tune of 5 hits in 5 at-bats at Ballpark of Pittsburgh today. The offensive onslaught from McNeil powered the Quakers to a 15-4 win.

"McNeil was a tough out today," Wolfeburg manager Chaichan Phonsa said to the press in the postgame interview. "He hit some very good pitches with that quick bat of his. Not an easy out, that's for sure."

Rodney McNeil hit an RBI double in the 2nd, hit a two-run home run off Elliot Provost in the 3rd, singled in the 6th, singled in the 7th and hit an RBI double in the 8th.

On the year McNeil is hitting .282 with 35 hits, 3 home runs, 24 RBIs, .333 OBP and has scored 20 runs.
