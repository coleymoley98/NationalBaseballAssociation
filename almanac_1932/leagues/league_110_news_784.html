Jr. Cardinaux Delatte and Rivermen Petersen Suspended for Fight
Monday, July 21st, 1930
Mark Delatte of the St. Louis Jr. Cardinaux and Rick Petersen of the Davenport Rivermen received notification from the league commissioner's office that they had been suspended for 3 and 7 games respectively for their part in the on-field ruckus in a recent game at Ballpark of St. Louis.

This season Delatte is batting .259 with 17 RBIs, 22 runs scored and 2 home runs.

Over the season, Petersen has made 22 relief appearances with 2 saves. He has K'd 42 batters in 44 innings, yielded 19 walks and has a 6.14 ERA. Petersen's record this year is 1-5.
