Giants Show No Mercy in 15-0 Thrashing of Alpines
Wednesday, June 30th, 1926
There was no spell, no black magic -- just good pitching.

Azman Eason of the Vancouver Giants blanked the Abbotsford Alpines, 15-0, at Alpines Ballpark today, and he did it the old-fashioned way. After finishing the 4-hit, complete-game shutout, he was all smiles.

"I threw a lot of my pitches for strikes in this game," Eason said. "I mixed it up and tried keeping these guys off balance."

Eason walked 2 and struck out 3 in the victory.

"He was throwing his breaking ball for strikes and we never capitalized on any of his mistakes," Alpines manager Erick Sethna said later.

Up to now Eason has recorded a record of 2-4. In 6 starts he has fanned 30 batters in 41 innings with a 2.41 ERA.
