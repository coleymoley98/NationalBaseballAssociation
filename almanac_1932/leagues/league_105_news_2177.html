Mets Snap Teran's Streak at 21
Wednesday, September 9th, 1931
Orber Teran went 0-for-4 at Citi Field today to break his consecutive-game hitting streak at 21 games. The 32-year-old Montreal right fielder failed to get a hit today in the Royales 6-1 victory over the Philadelphia Mets at Citi Field.

Teran did not make himself available to reporters after the game.

"I think Orber is a little upset," Montreal manager Hervé Morel said. "He'll get over it."

Hitting .299 to date, Teran has 3 home runs and 39 RBIs. He has played in 93 games.
