Saint Lawrence Winner is Quebec City Jr. Nordiques
Monday, July 26th, 1926
The owner of the Quebec City Jr. Nordiques called it a "proud day" for his franchise.

"Any time you win a division, it's a special accomplishment," said The owner of the Quebec City Jr. Nordiques. "I couldn't be happier to be associated with this group of players."

The Jr. Nordiques have in fact wrapped up the Saint Lawrence and left fielder Thaiwat Jinta said the team is looking forward to the postseason.

"It's what you play for, the chance to win a Laurier Cup," he said. "Our guys know what's at stake and what this means for our team and city."

The club has a record of 32-19 and have secured the 4th division championship in franchise history. The Jr. Nordiques will be looking for their 2nd Atlantic Baseball League championship.
