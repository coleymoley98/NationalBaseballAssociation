Thursday, June 12th, 1924
The Fort Wayne Pioneers couldn't stop Vernon Haynes today, but they stopped the Juneau Jr. Brasseurs, 12-11. Haynes went 5-for-5 in the game at Juniors Ballpark, but his team still came out on the wrong end of the score.
