Rowdy Players Suspended by League
Tuesday, August 23rd, 1927
Deron Nave of the Sherbrooke Nordiques and Ben Fandel of the Albany Rebels will be on the sidelines for a few days. They have been suspended for 2 and 6 games respectively by the league office for causing the bench-emptying free-for-all at the recent Nordiques-Rebels game at Nordiques Ballpark.

Neither player had any comment about the suspensions. Both clubs told BNN Nave and Fandel will begin serving them soon.

Nave is batting at a .261 pace this season, picking up 65 hits in 249 at-bats with 4 home runs, 32 RBIs and 28 runs scored.

To date this year in 16 starts, Fandel has crafted a 8-4 mark with a 4.54 ERA.
