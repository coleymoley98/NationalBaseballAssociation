Star SP Morillon Signs for 6 years
Tuesday, June 7th, 1932
Weerasak Morillon and Chicagou officials have reached an agreement keeping the 26-year-old starting pitcher around another 6 years.

Though terms were not confirmed, insiders tell BNN that Morillon re-signed for $17,533 per year.

Morillon has posted a 4-2 record for the year with a 3.02 ERA. Teams are hitting .246 against him and he has rung up 16 strikeouts in 50.2 innings.
