Another Setback For Philadelphia's Logan Freund
Friday, September 17th, 1926
The Philadelphia Liberty plan to take it slow with closer Logan Freund, knowing that his recovery from back spasms would likely include a few bumps in the road. A team spokesman told BNN, "Logan has up days and down days. It's still a process. He was feeling really good and then he had a step back. The doctors say it will be about one week before he can be activated."

To date Freund has 3 saves with a 7-5 mark and 4.80 ERA. Opposing teams are hitting .322 against him.
