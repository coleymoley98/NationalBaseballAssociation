One Run Enough for Detroit
Saturday, August 11th, 1928
The Juneau Brasseurs had no answer today for Detroit Lions pitcher Hafit Duangsawang. They failed to score a single run against him, losing 1-0. Juneau managed only 7 hits off Duangsawang, who had 2 strikeouts.

The extraordinarily popular Duangsawang got a standing ovation from the hometown crowd as he left the field, the applause continuing until he returned from the dugout to tip his cap to the crowd.

"Lions fans are the best fans, in my opinion," he said. "It's a great feeling to throw a shutout under any circumstances but to do it with the crowd behind you like that is just incredible."

So far this season Duangsawang has a 4-4 record with a 3.22 ERA.
