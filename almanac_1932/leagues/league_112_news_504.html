ABL Honors Quebec City Right Fielder
Monday, June 23rd, 1930
15-year-old Doug Siragusa made a few Quebec City headlines during this past week as the rookie right fielder proved he could compete at the plate with his high-school idols. He captured the attention of fans, journalists and Atlantic Baseball League officials last week and today took home Player of the Week honors for his effort.

He cracked 13 hits in 28 at-bats to put up a .464 average. Siragusa collected 3 home runs, 12 RBIs and scored 7 runs.

Thus far in 1930 Siragusa is hitting .354 with 6 home runs, 20 RBIs, 17 runs scored and has compiled a .400 OBP.
