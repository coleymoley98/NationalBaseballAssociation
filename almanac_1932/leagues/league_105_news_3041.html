Reds Sinclair Suffers Setback
Wednesday, July 13th, 1932
Things have gotten worse for Eli Sinclair of the Cincinnati Reds. The 23-year-old third baseman will not be back as soon as originally expected. Sinclair is undergoing treatment for a mild abdominal strain, but his doctors have expanded his recovery time to at least one week. An unidentified team official told BNN, "Eli tried to rush his rehab and suffered a setback."

So far this season Sinclair has gotten 56 hits in 181 at-bats for a .309 batting average with 7 home runs. His stats include 28 RBIs and 31 runs scored. He has played in 50 games.
