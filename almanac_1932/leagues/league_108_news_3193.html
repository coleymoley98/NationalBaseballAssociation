Western League Selects #1 Starter in July
Monday, August 1st, 1932
Over the past month Daoyod Sampahungsith has turned quite a few heads. He's young, talented and able to pitch with the established arms in the Western League. He proved all that and now he's been named July's Pitcher of the Month.

The rookie for the Edmonton Eskimos posted 6-1 mark and a 3.49 ERA. In 7 starts he tossed 56.2 innings, permitted 60 hits and fanned 41 batters.

This year Sampahungsith has posted a record of 9 wins and 3 losses with a 3.91 ERA. He has appeared in 12 games and struck out 67 in 94.1 innings.
