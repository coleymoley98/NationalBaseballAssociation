Giants Get One to Win
Tuesday, August 2nd, 1932
The Calgary Wranglers met their match today in Vancouver hurler Tommy Ouellet. He kept them guessing the whole game and spun a 5-hit shutout.

"He just worked us over," Calgary skipper Hunter Jones said. "He probably didn't even need a shower after the game -- he barely worked up a sweat."

In the 1-0 victory, Ouellet whiffed 8 and gave up 2 walks.

Ouellet has made 11 starts, tossed 89 innings, struck out 45 batters and walked 37. His record is 5-5 with a 4.45 ERA and he has limited other teams to a .262 batting average.
