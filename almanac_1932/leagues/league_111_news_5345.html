Tuesday, July 22nd, 1924
They won't go into the Eastern Baseball League playoffs as odds-on favorites, but the Hamilton Jr. Steelers still like their chances after capturing a wild card playoff berth.

"We feel good. We obviously wanted to win the East, but we still feel we have what it takes to make a run here," said starting pitcher Alfie Lafond. "If we can win this thing, no one's going to care that we didn't win the division."

The club will be vying for its 1st Eastern Baseball League championship.
