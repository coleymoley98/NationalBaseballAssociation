Gunners Won't Get Playoff Berth
Tuesday, July 15th, 1924
This won't be the year the Quincy Gunners raise a championship banner. The club was today eliminated from the playoff race.

Before this season, the Gunners last missed the playoffs in 1922. In the 30 years they have been a Atlantic Baseball League franchise, they have missed the playoffs 29 times. In the last 10 years, they have missed the playoffs 9 times. Quincy last won the Laurier Cup in 1923.
