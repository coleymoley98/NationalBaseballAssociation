Ottawa Trades Etling for Montreal's Martínez
Sunday, July 15th, 1928
You can focus on the negatives after being traded to a new team -- finding a new place to stay, leaving teammates and friends, having to start anew -- but one player hopes to focus on the positives. "It means I'm wanted somewhere. At least one team sees potential in me." Those were the comments heard after the Ottawa Indians came to an agreement to deal 24-year-old 1B Nantapol Etling to the Montreal Royales for 20-year-old minor league RHP Elih Rivas, 19-year-old minor league LF Avery Meza and 20-year-old minor league CF Seville Martínez earlier today. The deal is expected to be formally announced later this evening.


This season Etling has hit 3 home runs and posted a .333 batting average.
