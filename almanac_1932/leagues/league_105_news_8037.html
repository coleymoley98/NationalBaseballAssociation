Star SP Ibarra Settles on Montreal
Monday, November 15th, 1926
Royales officials presented Daniel Ibarra to members of the local print media today as their new starting pitcher.

The deal, worth $144,600, guarantees the 30-year-old a home in Montreal for 7 years. It should improve the ballclub, assuming he plays to his potential.

Ibarra has rolled up a 68-51 career record and he boasts a lifetime ERA of 3.30.
