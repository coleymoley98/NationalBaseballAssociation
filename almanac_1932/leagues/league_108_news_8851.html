Moragne Wins WL Top Pitcher Honors
Monday, August 1st, 1927
Veteran Justin Moragne put together quite a successful stretch in July for the Vancouver Giants, earning himself the Western League Pitcher of the Month award.

Moragne racked up a 5-1 record in 6 starts, posting a 1.84 ERA over 53.2 innings and striking out 36.

In 12 starts this season Moragne has chalked up a 8-3 record and a 3.51 ERA.
