Leboeuf Batters Sherbrooke Big Time
Monday, August 20th, 1928
Dean Leboeuf had an answer for everything the Sherbrooke Nordiques threw at him today at Nordiques Ballpark. The Albany left fielder went 5-for-5 in the game to put his name in the record book and put the Rebels on top, 10-7.

Summing up his batting philosophy to BNN writers after the game, Leboeuf said, "I just see the ball and I hit it."

Dean Leboeuf hit an RBI triple in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, doubled in the 6th, hit an RBI single in the 7th and singled in the 9th.

This year Leboeuf is batting .240 with 10 home runs.
