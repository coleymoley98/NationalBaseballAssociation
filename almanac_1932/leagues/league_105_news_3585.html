Cincinnati Trades Olivas for Ottawa's Greene
Tuesday, February 6th, 1923
Both sides appear content with the trade finalized today that will send 25-year-old CF Edison Olivas, 20-year-old minor league 2B Teerawat Miller and 20-year-old minor league 3B Medardo Rosas to the Ottawa Indians, while the Cincinnati Reds will receive 29-year-old LHP Jaxon Greene.

"This is a move that filled a few gaps in our roster," said Techatorn Jones, Reds general manager.

For his part, the Indians GM said the trade was made "in the best interest of our organization's future." BNN analysts reported that on paper the trade seemed to be a plus for both ball clubs.


In his career Greene has fanned 536 batters and walked 450 in 1439.1 innings of work. His lifetime won-lost record is 88 victories and 73 defeats with a 3.75 ERA and opposing hitters have compiled a .282 against him.
