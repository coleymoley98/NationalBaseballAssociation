Redish Slams 5 Hits for Victoria
Tuesday, July 1st, 1930
All game long, Victoria's Rupert Redish was rattling the walls at Alpines Ballpark and banging out base hits against the Abbotsford Alpines. When the dust finally settled on the diamond, he had a 5-hit day and the Admirals had a 23-0 victory.

Speaking with reporters after his standout performance, the 15-year-old second baseman said, "Get 5 hits, win a ballgame, it's nice. I could get used to this."

Rupert Redish hit an RBI single in the 1st, hit an RBI double in the 2nd, hit a solo-shot off Edouard Letendre in the 3rd, doubled in the 4th and hit a solo-shot off Harris Fyfe in the 6th.

Redish is batting .472 this season in 17 games with 2 home runs, 11 RBIs and 9 runs scored. He has 17 hits in 36 at-bats.
