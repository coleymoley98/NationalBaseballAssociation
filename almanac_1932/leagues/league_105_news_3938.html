Trottier Selected CL Player of the Week
Monday, June 25th, 1923
One of the league's most exciting young players, Charles Trottier of the New York Rebels earned himself Canadian League Player of the Week honors with a standout performance in last week's games.

For the week the 23-year-old left fielder had a lofty .522 average (12-for-23), collecting no home runs, 6 RBIs and 5 runs scored.

In his stats this season, Trottier has posted a batting average of .303 with 50 hits in 165 at-bats, 1 home run and 31 RBIs. He has also scored 31 runs, walked 19 times and compiled a .377 OBP.
