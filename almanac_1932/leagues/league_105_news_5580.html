Browning Shuts Out Lions
Thursday, August 28th, 1924
The Detroit Lions were almost helpless today in their 5-0 loss to the Minnesota Saints and hurler Taye Browning. Browning kept the Lions confused all game, spinning a 8-hit shutout.

"Tip your hat to Browning," Detroit manager Brian Ahn said in his postgame interview. "He kept us off balance all night."

The 22-year-old right-hander had 1 walk and 3 strikeouts in a strong performance.

"It just happened to be one of those games where even when I made mistakes, they either fouled them off or someone would make a fine defensive play," Browning said. "It was just one of those games that things went my way."
