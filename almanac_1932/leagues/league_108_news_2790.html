5-for-5 Day for Goodson
Wednesday, June 15th, 1932
Ivory Goodson put on a hitting clinic today as the Portland Thorns dumped the Spokane Eagles 22-12 at Thorns Ballpark.

The Portland first baseman made Spokane defenders earn their pay, lashing Spokane pitching for 5 hits in 5 at-bats in the game. He told BNN reporters after the game that his strategy was "just hit it where they pitched it."

"Too often I try too hard to pull the ball," Goodson said in his postgame press conference. "Today, I just went with the pitch."

Ivory Goodson singled in the 1st, singled in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 2nd, hit a GRAND SLAM off Atiwit Rangsima in the 4th, singled in the 6th and walked in the 7th.

In his season totals, Goodson has 2 home runs, .286 BA, .343 OBP, 15 RBIs and 10 runs scored. He has played in 14 games.
