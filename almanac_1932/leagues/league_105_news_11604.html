Star C Lavallée Settles on Boston
Saturday, November 9th, 1929
The Redcoats today announced that they have come to terms with Danusorn Lavallée, a 27-year-old catcher they've been scouting for a while.

Lavallée will earn $18,300 a year over 8 years and is expected to play a key role with the club.

During his career Lavallée has hit .291 with 344 hits, 23 home runs, 215 RBIs and scored 170 runs.
