Braddock Walks Away from the Game
Friday, August 28th, 1925
Mike Braddock said he's proud of what he's accomplished in professional baseball and he's now comfortable walking away from the sport.

The Providence Redcoats reliever announced today that this season will be his last.

"It's Freedom 38," he joked with reporters. "I'm happy to get out now and am looking forward to enjoying retirement."

Braddock has registered up a career 1-and-1 record with 5 saves and a 2.50 ERA.
