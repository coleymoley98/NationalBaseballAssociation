Mantaux Goes 5-for-6 Against Team
Friday, March 12th, 1926
It seemed that Team center fielder Denis Mantaux could do no wrong as the Montana KO'd the Iowa, 21-1 today. Mantaux rapped out 5 hits in 6 at-bats and seemed to know which pitches Iowa hurlers were throwing.

"I think the Montana were stealing signs," growled one unhappy Team fan after the game.

Denis Mantaux hit an RBI double in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 4th, hit a two-run single in the 5th, walked in the 6th, singled in the 8th and flied out in the 9th.
