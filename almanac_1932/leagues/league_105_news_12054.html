Chicagou Sweeps White Sox
Friday, October 7th, 1932
Today the Frontenacs fans broke out the brooms at Comiskey Park 1910 and it worked -- Chicagou swept the Chicagou White Sox 4-0 and are the 1932 National Baseball Association Riel Cup champions for the 5th time in their history.

The Frontenacs derailed Chicagou 14-9 to rap up the title.

Chicagou finished the season with a record of 70-42 and ranked first in the National League West.
