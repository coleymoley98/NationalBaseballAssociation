Petchaporn 5-for-5 Against Black Sox
Thursday, July 24th, 1924
Obb Petchaporn said "it felt like taking batting practice" today at Juniors Ballpark, and it showed in the box score. The Canton third baseman had a fantastic 5-for-5 day against the Gallant Black Sox, leading his club to victory, 6-1.

"I was seeing the ball very well and it was going just about anywhere I wanted," Petchaporn said later."

Obb Petchaporn singled in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, singled in the 5th, hit a solo-shot off Kraisee Murakami in the 7th and hit an RBI double in the 8th.

This year Petchaporn has hit at a .328 clip with 67 hits, 6 home runs, 42 RBIs and 40 runs scored.
