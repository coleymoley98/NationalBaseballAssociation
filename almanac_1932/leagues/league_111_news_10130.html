Tiatrakul Scores EBL Best Hurler Honors
Saturday, August 18th, 1928
Adisorn Tiatrakul of the Hamilton Jr. Steelers is in the spotlight today. He was voted the top pitcher in the Eastern Baseball League and will be presented the 1928 EBL Pitcher of the Year.

He started 11 times and compiled a 7-0 record with an ERA of 1.35 in 73.1 innings with 90 strikeouts.

He received 10 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Cameron Peal of the North Bay Battalion finished second in voting, while Karter Cherry of the Buffalo Rapids finished third.
