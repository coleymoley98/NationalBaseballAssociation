Hat Trick of Home Runs for Poulin
Wednesday, July 30th, 1924
For Charles Poulin, it was not an average day at the ballpark.

Fans at Ballpark of Drummondville today watched Poulin power the Providence Bruins to a 18-9 win over the Drummondville Voltigeurs, blasting a trifecta of home runs.

"I've always loved hitting in Ballpark of Drummondville," the right fielder told reporters following his big day. "You can see the ball so well."

For the game, he collected 3 hits in 4 at-bats, scored 4 runs and had 6 RBIs.

Charles Poulin flied out in the 1st, hit a solo-shot off Donte Green in the 3rd, hit a three-run home run off J.J. Dostie in the 4th, walked in the 6th and hit a two-run home run off Andy Sarrazin in the 7th.

In 49 games this season Poulin has compiled a .318 batting average with 57 hits and 8 home runs. He has also scored 35 times and knocked in 38 runs.
