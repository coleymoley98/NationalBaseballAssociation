CL Hands Out Reliever of the Year Award
Thursday, October 10th, 1929
The Wolfeburg Celtics will no doubt have to pony up some big cash if they want to keep star reliever Dwight Lynch.

That's because the 31-year-old today was named the Canadian League Reliever of the Year Award winner for 1929.

As it stands now, he's heading for free agency unless his team can put forward an extension offer. As of yet, there's been no word about an impending deal.

Lynch captured the award with a 2.92 ERA this season over 61.2 innings of work. He made 38 relief appearances and had 7 saves, 21 strikeouts, 28 walks and a record of 7 and 4. Opposing hitters managed only a .318 against him.

He ended up tied with Wachirawit Therrien with 64 total votes and 64 first place votes.
