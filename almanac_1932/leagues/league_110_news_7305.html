Davenport Downs Saskatoon as Joly Tosses 1-0 Shutout
Sunday, June 20th, 1926
Davenport Rivermen pitcher Renato Joly really got it done at Jr. Coyotes Ballpark today in his 1-0 shutout victory over the Saskatoon Jr. Coyotes, shutting them down on 5 hits.

"It was just one of those games where everything went right. I threw a lot of strikes," Joly said. "And the pitches they did get to hit, they didn't do anything with them."

The 18-year-old issued 1 walk and had 9 strikeouts in the Rivermen win.

So far this year Joly has 4 wins and no losses and a 0.68 ERA.
