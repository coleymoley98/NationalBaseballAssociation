Quebec City Captures Wild Card
Wednesday, July 25th, 1928
Steve Lowenstein of the Quebec City Jr. Nordiques said fighting for a playoff spot is an "absolute grind" in Atlantic Baseball League.

The grind is now over, as the Jr. Nordiques have captured a wild card berth.

"It's a weight off our shoulders and now we can exhale," said the starting pitcher. "It's hard to describe how much effort and focus goes into this."

Team manager Supravee Dube said he believes his club has something to prove and they are looking forward to the postseason.
