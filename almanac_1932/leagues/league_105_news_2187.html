Indians Captures Wild Card
Sunday, September 13th, 1931
It came down to the wire for the Ottawa Indians, but they have clinched a spot in the National Baseball Association postseason.

"We wanted the Canadian League East division, but once that wasn't possible, it was tunnel vision to try to get the wild card," said left fielder David Wintringham. "We feel we've got as good a chance as any other team now that we're in the playoffs."

The team currently sits with a 58-52 record and will be vying for their 1st Riel Cup championship.
