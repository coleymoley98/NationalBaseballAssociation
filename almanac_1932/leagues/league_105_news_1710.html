More Woes For Frontenacs Watcharapol Tremblay
Saturday, July 11th, 1931
Things have gotten worse for Watcharapol Tremblay of the Chicagou Frontenacs. The 23-year-old left fielder will not be back as soon as originally expected. Tremblay is undergoing treatment for a knee sprain, but his doctors have expanded his recovery time to at least 2 weeks. An unidentified team official told BNN, "Watcharapol tried to rush his rehab and suffered a setback."

This year Tremblay has 6 home runs and a .276 batting average.
