Boston's Ratté Nets CL Pitcher of the Year Award
Wednesday, October 14th, 1925
Sommai Ratté of the Boston Redcoats is in the spotlight today. He was acclaimed the best pitcher in the Canadian League and will be honored with the 1925 Pitcher of the Year Award.

He started 24 games and compiled a 15-7 record with an ERA of 3.60 in 195 innings with 31 strikeouts.

He received 15 first place votes out of a possible 16. Jetniphat Fulton of the Boston Redcoats finished second in voting with 1 first place vote, while Nat Harvest of the Baltimore Robins finished third.
