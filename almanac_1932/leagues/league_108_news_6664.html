Marrou Wins WL Player of the Week Honors
Monday, August 24th, 1925
Boyce Marrou demonstrated his potential to be a star in the game last week and today he took home the Western League Player of the Week Award.

The Seattle first baseman wreaked havoc on opposing pitchers last week, pounding them for a .467 average (14-for-30) with 5 home runs and 15 RBIs on his way to claiming the honor.

This year Marrou is hitting .367, produced 79 RBIs and 24 home runs, scored 72 times and gotten on base at a .471 rate.
