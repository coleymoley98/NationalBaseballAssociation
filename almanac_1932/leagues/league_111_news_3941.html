Henriquez Sticks it to EBL Pitchers
Monday, June 25th, 1923
It can be difficult for a young player to step out of the shadows and assert himself in the Eastern Baseball League, but last week Alec Henriquez of the Morgantown Mountaineers reminded everyone just why he's here as he threw up numbers that made him an easy selection for Player of the Week. Henriquez rocked opposing pitching for a .667 average (6-for-9), 2 home runs and 4 RBIs to come away with the award.

Currently Henriquez is hitting .522 and has collected 3 home runs, 8 RBIs and scored 9 times, while playing in 12 games.
