Wednesday, June 18th, 1930
Roméo Boudreau put a hex on the Victoria Admirals in a sparkling 3-0 shutout performance, mesmerizing them on a 1-hit gem.

"I felt good physically," he said after the game. "Everything's comfortable. You should pitch well when that's the case."

"Boudreau used his changeup very well, very effectively," said opposing manager Michel Arquette. "He threw it in hitter's counts."
