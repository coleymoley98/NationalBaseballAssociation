Setback for Toronto and Kittiphon Seerot
Wednesday, November 7th, 1928
Just when it looked like Kittiphon Seerot would soon return to the Toronto Blue Sox, the first baseman suffered yet another setback in his recovery from a concussion. According to his doctors, treatment was progressing well until yesterday, when Seerot experienced quite a bit of pain during a workout. Further examination revealed he had caused more damage and would now be disabled for at least 8 months.

During his career Seerot has hit .327 with 199 hits, 30 home runs, 122 RBIs and scored 105 runs.
