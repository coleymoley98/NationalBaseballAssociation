Valcourt Bangs Out 5 Base Hits
Wednesday, June 17th, 1931
A.J. Valcourt was sizzling at the plate today. In a game played at Broncos Ballpark, the Boise shortstop blistered the Ranier Seahawks for 5 hits in 6 at-bats to pace his club to a 15-9 victory.

"Valcourt was too tough to get out today," Seahawks skipper Jesse Daniels said in his postgame interview. "No matter what we threw him, he hit it. And he hit some good pitches, too."

A.J. Valcourt singled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, hit a solo-shot off Jaturapat Langevin in the 5th, hit an RBI single in the 6th, hit an RBI double in the 7th and struck out in the 8th.
