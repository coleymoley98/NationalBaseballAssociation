Injury Sidelines Aoki for Season
Tuesday, August 23rd, 1932
The news was not unexpected, but that didn't make it easier to accept for the Sherbrooke Nordiques: starting pitcher Kazuhiro Aoki is out for the year. Aoki suffered a torn flexor tendon in his elbow on 08/21/1932. The team knew that the chances of seeing him return were slim to none. But today it was made official.

This season Aoki has a 4-7 mark in 13 starts with a 6.12 ERA. He has given up 117 hits in 82.1 innings, struck out 55 and issued 27 walks. Opposing clubs have compiled a .328 batting average against him.
