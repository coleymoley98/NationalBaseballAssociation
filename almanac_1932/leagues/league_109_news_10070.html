Royales End Bonilla's Streak
Tuesday, August 7th, 1928
The 22-game hitting streak of Sherbrooke Nordiques center fielder Adam Bonilla came to an end today at Nordiques Ballpark. The Trois-Rivieres Royales held him hitless in 4 at-bats and also sent the Nordiques down to defeat, 8-3.

Later, Bonilla acknowledged to BNN reporters that the hit streak meant something to him.

"It was special," he said, "but I think other people make a bigger deal out of it than I do, or the players do. It was what we're all supposed to do; go out and hit the ball and try to score runs. It was a cool deal, but it's over now. Let's try to start a new one."

For the season Bonilla is batting .303 with 57 hits and 3 home runs. He has scored 36 times and batted in 24.
