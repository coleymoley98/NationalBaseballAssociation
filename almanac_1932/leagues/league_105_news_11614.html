Juneau Trades Aubry for St. Louis's Humprey
Monday, November 11th, 1929
Juneau and St. Louis have agreed to a trade today. 26-year-old RHP Wuttinun Aubry will go to the Cardinaux. In exchange the Brasseurs will receive 20-year-old minor league 2B Ángel Anaya and 20-year-old minor league RHP Max Humprey.


During his career Aubry has 26 victories and 34 defeats with a 3.68 ERA.
