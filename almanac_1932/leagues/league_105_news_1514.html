Indians David Wintringham Wins CL Award
Monday, June 22nd, 1931
It's always a thrill for fans of the team and students of the game alike when a superstar in the making shows what he's got. David Wintringham, the Ottawa Indians 25-year-old left fielder, put himself in the spotlight last week making him the clear-cut choice for Canadian League Player of the Week honors.

He ripped 15 hits in 30 at-bats, compiling a .500 average with 2 home runs and 6 RBIs.

Wintringham has a .406 average and a .444 on-base percentage for the year. He has picked up 76 hits, 11 home runs and driven in 43 runs.
