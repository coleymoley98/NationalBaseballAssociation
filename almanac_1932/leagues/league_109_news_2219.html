Anglin Wins EL Manager of the Year Trophy
Sunday, September 20th, 1931
Jeremy Anglin celebrated a successful season with the Trois-Rivieres Royales by hosting team officials and select media at a Trois-Rivieres area first-class eatery. But when he first came to town, the 1931 Eastern League Manager of the Year was met with a good measure of skepticism.

"Very few people believed in our ballclub when I got here. It just goes to show you that a bunch of talented ballplayers playing together can make any old manager a genius," a modest Anglin joked, not fully recognizing his own stature in the baseball community.
