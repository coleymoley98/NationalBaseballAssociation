League Punishes Pair for Fighting
Tuesday, July 17th, 1923
Jeff Moye of the Springfield Falcons and Pralong Cardinal of the Toronto Jr. Blue Sox will be on the sidelines for a few days. They have been suspended for 4 and 9 games respectively by the league office for causing the bench-emptying free-for-all at the recent Falcons-Jr. Blue Sox game at Juniors Ballpark.

Neither player had any comment about the suspensions. Both clubs told BNN Moye and Cardinal will begin serving them soon.

Looking at his season stats, Moye has compiled a .284 batting average and a .354 on-base percentage, while hitting no home runs and driving in 14 runs.

This year in 17 relief appearances, Cardinal has collected 3 saves while posting an ERA of 7.97 and 20 strikeouts in 40.2 innings. His totals for the year also include 2 wins and 2 losses.
