Rebels Montano Claims 100th Victory
Friday, August 26th, 1932
Lashawn Montano of the New York Rebels nailed down his 100th career victory with a 15-14 win over the Baltimore Robins today at Yankee Stadium 1923. Montano, 38, fired no strikeouts over 3 innings of work. He gave up no runs, no walks and allowed 1 hit.

"I feel great," Montano said afterward. "I try not to get caught up in the moment. It's hard with so much press coverage these days, but I tune all that out when I go out there and pitch."

Montano has appeared in 597 games and captured 102 saves over his career. He has 100 wins and 74 losses and a 3.23 ERA with 385 strikeouts in 1062.2 innings.
