Injury Brings Season to End for Paré
Tuesday, July 17th, 1923
In a news release, the Halifax Highlanders announced that shortstop Alexis Paré would miss the rest of the season due to a fractured elbow. Paré sustained the injury on 07/14/1923. He "will be sorely missed," said his manager.

Batting .215 to date, Paré has no home runs and 7 RBIs.
