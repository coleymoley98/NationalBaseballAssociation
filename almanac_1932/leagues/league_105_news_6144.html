Lei Batters Cincinnati Big Time
Tuesday, June 9th, 1925
Kitti Lei was at the head of a Montreal Royales hit parade today at Great American Ball Park. The 21-year-old went to the plate 5 times and came away with 5 hits, but it was the Cincinnati Reds that marched off with the 19-3 win.

"We didn't have it today," griped Royales skipper Donatien Auger following the game. "Kitti did fine, but as a team, we didn't do it with the bats and we didn't do it pitching."

Lei did more than fine, dominating the Reds at the plate during his record-making 5-for-5 performance.

"Each time I went to the plate, I was thinking about hitting the ball hard somewhere," the sweet-swinging left fielder told reporters. "When I focus on that, I usually have quality at-bats."

Kitti Lei singled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, doubled in the 7th and doubled in the 9th.

Lei is hitting .306 this year with 3 home runs and 12 RBIs. He also has an OBP of .370, 10 walks and scored 22 times.
