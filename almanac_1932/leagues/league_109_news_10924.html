McNair Pitches 5-0 Shutout
Sunday, June 23rd, 1929
In an outstanding mound performance, Harrisburg Robins starter Don McNair fired a complete-game shutout against the Pittsburgh Liberty at Liberty Ballpark today.

McNair was the key to the Robins's 5-0 win. The 22-year-old right-hander was in high spirits on the mount for much of the game, his mood largely undented by the 4 hits and 2 walks he allowed.

"4 hits?" he said with a laugh following the game. "Listen, I'd be happy with a 30-hitter as long as it was a shutout."

To date McNair is 3-1 in 5 starts with a 2.43 ERA and has 41 strikeouts in 40.2 innings.
