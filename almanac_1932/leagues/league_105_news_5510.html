Indians Lead with Chin, Get KO'd, 9-0
Friday, August 15th, 1924
The Ottawa Indians had no answer today for Baltimore Robins pitcher Ray O'Gorman. They failed to score a single run against him, losing 9-0. Ottawa managed only 7 hits off O'Gorman, who had 3 strikeouts.

The extraordinarily popular O'Gorman got a standing ovation from the hometown crowd as he left the field, the applause continuing until he returned from the dugout to tip his cap to the crowd.

"Robins fans are the best fans, in my opinion," he said. "It's a great feeling to throw a shutout under any circumstances but to do it with the crowd behind you like that is just incredible."

In 20 starts this season O'Gorman has fashioned a 12-5 record and a 3.51 ERA.
