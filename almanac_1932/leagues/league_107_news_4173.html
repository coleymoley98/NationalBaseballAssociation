Bédard Keeps Hit Streak Alive
Sunday, July 22nd, 1923
J.F. Bédard's consecutive-game hitting streak has now climbed to 20 games. The Abbotsford first baseman smacked 1 hit against the Boise Broncos in today's game at Alpines Ballpark, but he expressed mixed emotions about the streak after watching the Alpines drop the game, 7-4.

"I feel good about the streak, but don't feel good when we lose," he told reporters. "I'd love to get a hit as well as a win next time so I can celebrate properly."
J.F. Bédard singled in the 1st, grounded out in the 4th, flied out in the 5th and grounded out in the 8th.

Thus far this season Bédard is hitting .258 with no home runs, 14 RBIs and 24 runs scored. He has 49 hits in 190 at-bats and has played in 46 games.
