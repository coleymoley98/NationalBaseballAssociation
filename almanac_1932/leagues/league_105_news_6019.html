Cyr Goes 5-for-5
Thursday, May 14th, 1925
The Wolfeburg Celtics stopped the Baltimore Robins by a score of 9-4 at League Park today, with the lion's share of the credit going to left fielder Félix Cyr. The 21-year-old had a career day, collecting 5 hits.

"Every batter wants to have a quality at-bat every time he steps up to the plate," Cyr said later. "He wants to hit the ball hard."

Cyr did just that in his 5 at-bats, much to the chagrin of the Robins.

"Not much that I can say," offered Baltimore manager Eldin Gabriel. "He just hammered the heck out of us."

Félix Cyr singled in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 2nd, singled in the 3rd, doubled in the 5th and singled in the 7th.
