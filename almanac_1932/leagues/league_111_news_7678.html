Buffalo Starter Wins July's Pitcher Award
Sunday, August 1st, 1926
Sean Raeburn hasn't been around long enough to know that youngsters shouldn't be winning Eastern Baseball League Pitcher of the Month. But when you put up numbers like his, you're going to get some notice.

In July the Buffalo starter earned a 4-0 mark in 6 starts. He threw 35 innings, struck out 36 and compiled a solid 1.29 ERA.

This season-to-date, Raeburn has fashioned a 7-1 mark and 3.14 ERA in 66 innings. Opposing clubs are batting .224 against him and he has logged 67 strikeouts.
