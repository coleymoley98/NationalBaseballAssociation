Farmer Blisters Juneau
Sunday, September 7th, 1924
It wasn't just another day at the ballpark for Carter Farmer of the Minnesota Saints. Farmer went on a tear at Field of the Saints and cracked hit after hit off Juneau pitching. When the dust settled on the diamond, the 21-year-old first baseman had a career 5-for-5 day and something he will long remember.

"Yeah, it'll be something nice to tell the grandkids, but I don't think I'll tell them we lost," he joked in the BNN postgame interview.

Despite Farmer's personal accolades, the Juneau Brasseurs walked off the field with the 12-8 win.

Carter Farmer hit a two-run triple in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, hit a solo-shot off Trevon Turbin in the 6th and singled in the 8th.

This season Farmer has hit at a .343 clip and produced 67 runs batted in, 9 home runs and 56 runs.
