Van Allen Tosses 2-0 Shutout for Juneau
Friday, July 6th, 1928
In a very sharp performance at Great American Ball Park, Warwick Van Allen of the Juneau Brasseurs blanked the Cincinnati Reds, 2-0. He permitted 6 hits and finished with 4 strikeouts and 1 walk in the Brasseurs victory.

"Warwick goes out there and just goes about his business," Brasseurs manager Jake Downing said in his postgame press conference. "I don't think he gets too concerned about what's going on. No matter the pitch, you know he's going to be where he needs to be."

Thus far this season, Van Allen has tossed 106.1 innings in 12 starts, fanned 33 batters, walked 33 and put up a 1.69 ERA with 7 wins and 3 losses.
