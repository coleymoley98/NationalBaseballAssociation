Philadelphia Liberty Ship Bonneau to Chicagou
Thursday, December 1st, 1932
The Philadelphia Liberty addressed a concern today, coming to terms with the Chicagou Frontenacs on a trade that will send 25-year-old SS Henri Bonneau from the Liberty to the Frontenacs in exchange for 29-year-old minor league RHP Marcel Cuevas and 20-year-old minor league 2B Justin Bledsoe.


A check of his lifetime stats shows Bonneau has played in 107 games with a .222 batting average. Among his 75 hits are 1 home run. He also has scored 24 runs and driven in 31 runs.
