Celtics Win Division Series
Wednesday, September 10th, 1930
Fans of the New York Rebels groaned when Danny Sellers stepped up to the plate in the Division Series.

And for good reason too, as the Wolfeburg Celtics player was instrumental in guiding his team to a 3-1 series win over the Rebels.

The series was clinched tonight at Yankee Stadium 1923, with Wolfeburg coming out on top 2-1 in the deciding game.

Sellers's strong series included a .500 batting average, with a .583 on-base percentage. He had 1 home runs, drove in 6 RBI and scored 2 runs.

The Wolfeburg Celtics don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Ottawa Indians - Boston Redcoats series. Ottawa leads the series 2 games to 1. The League Chamionship Series schedule will be announced after the Division Series finishes.
