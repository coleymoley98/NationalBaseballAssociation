Cincinnati Trades Côté for Chicagou's Watkins
Saturday, August 20th, 1927
BNN is reporting a rumored trade between the Cincinnati Reds and Chicagou Frontenacs has been finalized. The deal, which is expected to be announced sometime during the next few hours, will reportedly send 33-year-old 3B Tom Côté to Chicagou in return for 23-year-old minor league RF Ryder Watkins, 20-year-old minor league RHP Lamarcus Guillemin, 19-year-old minor league SS Sornnarai Gagnon, 21-year-old minor league C Ojai Edwards and 21-year-old minor league RHP Watcharapong Hanprab. The clubs were noncommittal on the trade, but did not deny it.


For the season Côté is currently batting .321 with no home runs and 20 RBIs. In 62 games he has reached base at a .401 rate and scored 31 runs to date.
