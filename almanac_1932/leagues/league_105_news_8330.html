Tuesday, May 24th, 1927
Charles Trottier continued his streak at League Park today, even as the ballgame did not go his way. The Wolfeburg left fielder was 2-for-4 in today's contest and now has hit safely in his last 25 games. The Celtics were not as fortunate, losing 8-2 to the New York Rebels.
