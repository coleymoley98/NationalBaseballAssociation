Player of the Week Trophy to Jones
Monday, August 22nd, 1932
So far in his young career, Mohammed Jones has kept a low profile, but after his performance at the plate last week, he attracted the notice of the rest of the Eastern League in a big way as he was named the Player of the Week today for his efforts.

The 21-year-old Brampton Blue Sox first baseman hit at a torrid .577 clip as he collected 15 hits in 26 at-bats, 5 home runs, 14 RBIs and 10 runs scored.

Currently Jones is batting .393 with 17 home runs and 82 RBIs.
