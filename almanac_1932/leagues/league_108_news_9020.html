Martínez Caps Great Week
Monday, August 29th, 1927
At the age of 27, P.J. Martínez might have some potential he hasn't realized yet -- if his success at the plate last week is any indication. The Saskatoon right fielder put up some exceptional numbers the past seven days with a .500 batting average. For his efforts Martínez collected the Western League Player of the Week Award.

He racked up 11 hits in 22 at-bats, 1 home run and 10 RBIs last week in 6 games.

Looking at his present stats, Martínez has compiled a .313 batting average in 59 games with a .443 on-base percentage, while hitting 5 home runs and driving in 42 runs.
