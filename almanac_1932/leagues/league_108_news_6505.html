Kehagias on Fire, 5-for-5 Against Portland
Monday, July 27th, 1925
It wasn't just another day at the ballpark for Kaeo Kehagias of the Seattle Rainiers. Kehagias went on a tear at Thorns Ballpark and cracked hit after hit off Portland pitching. When the dust settled on the diamond, the 22-year-old shortstop had a career 5-for-5 day and something he will long remember.

"Yeah, it'll be something nice to tell the grandkids, but I don't think I'll tell them we lost," he joked in the BNN postgame interview.

Despite Kehagias's personal accolades, the Portland Thorns walked off the field with the 3-2 win.

Kaeo Kehagias singled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, singled in the 7th and doubled in the 9th.

In the current season, Kehagias has put up a .377 batting average with 78 hits, 2 home runs, 26 RBI and scored 49 runs.
