Injury Ends Career for Hanselman
Saturday, May 24th, 1924
The baseball career of Michael Hanselman is over. A spokesperson for the Seattle Rainiers clarified earlier comments about the 20-year-old starting pitcher. Hanselman, he said, will retire effective immediately as the result of a torn ulnar collateral ligament. Though he had spoken optimistically about playing again, Hanselman informed the team that he has decided to retire. It is a sad day for Seattle -- a sad day for those who had hoped that Hanselman would return.
