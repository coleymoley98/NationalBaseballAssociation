Gibbs Skunks Reds
Saturday, May 11th, 1929
In a seemingly effortless performance, Jarel Gibbs of the Detroit Lions shot down the Cincinnati Reds 4-0 on a 3-hit shutout.

"Jarel was just locating so well and using his pitches," Detroit manager Jake Downing said later. "He wasn't leaving anything over the plate."

Gibbs recorded 5 strikeouts and 1 walk in a strong outing.

"When he needed to make pitches, he elevated his game," Cincinnati manager Hoot Sinkus said in his postgame comments to BNN. "We couldn't get a key hit off him."
