Guimond Takes Personal Leave
Wednesday, July 28th, 1926
Quebec reliever Krissadee Guimond will miss an indefinite period of time to tend to "personal issues". Guimond refused to elaborate on what exactly the issues were, but added that they were "more important than baseball, that's for sure." Nordiques manager Eric Scism commented that they will "expect to be without Guimond for quite some time and that we'll have to plan long-term to be without him."
