Gustin Bashes 5 Base Hits
Tuesday, March 19th, 1929
It was high-fives all around for Landon Gustin at Blue Sox Ballpark today.

He got them from his teammates and he got them at the plate during an outstanding hitting performance against the Team Manitoba. Gustin smashed 5 hits in 5 at-bats on his monster day, leading his club to a 10-2 win.

"Landon was hitting everything," said Montana manager Tommy Dupuis. "It really was a special game for him."

In the opposing dugout, Manitoba manager Kris Syas told BNN beat writers that Gustin "ambushed" the club.

"Obviously we didn't have much success against him, but we'll have another chance," he said. "Hopefully, Gustin oversleeps next time."

Landon Gustin hit a two-run home run off Kevin Devlin in the 2nd, doubled in the 5th, singled in the 6th, tripled in the 8th and singled in the 9th.

This season Gustin has hit 2 home runs and posted a .405 batting average.
