EBL Selects Marquis July's #1 Pitcher
Monday, August 1st, 1932
Andrew Marquis took a much-deserved turn in the spotlight earlier today as he accepted the Eastern Baseball League Pitcher of the Month award for July.

The 16-year-old Black Sox starter is a relative newcomer to the league, but nonetheless racked up an impressive 5-0 won-lost record in 6 starts. This month he fanned 36 batters in 37.2 innings and compiled a 0.48 ERA.

On the season Marquis has compiled a 10-1 mark with a 1.88 ERA in 12 starts. In pitching 71.2 innings he has 75 strikeouts and 23 walks while limiting the opposition to a .205 batting average.
