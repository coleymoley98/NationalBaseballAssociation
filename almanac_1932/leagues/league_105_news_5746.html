Marc Silva Nabs Best Player Trophy
Saturday, October 18th, 1924
What a way to finish off the year -- being honored with the 1924 Most Valuable Player Award in the National League. It had already been a very special season for Detroit's Marc Silva, but today's announcement really put the icing on his cake.

All season long Marc Silva gave opposing pitchers fits by hitting .343 as he collected 139 hits, 21 doubles, 2 triples, 12 home runs and 71 RBIs.

He received 15 first place votes out of a possible 16. León Plaza of the Detroit Lions finished second in voting, while Joshua Comtois of the Detroit Lions finished third.
