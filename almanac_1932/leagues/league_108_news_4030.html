Saskatoon Punchless in 12-0 Loss to Eskimos
Tuesday, July 3rd, 1923
Nathan Voisin kept the opposition off the scoreboard and the Edmonton Eskimos were all smiles after winning 12-0 against the Saskatoon Coyotes at Coyotes Ballpark today. The left-handed Voisin gave up no runs in his start, permitted 3 hits and had 8 strikeouts.

"If there was a stat for 'team shutout,' this would be it," Voisin said later. "My catcher called a great game, guys delivered at the plate and when I messed something up the guys behind me were where they needed to be."

This year Voisin has worked 55.1 innings with 40 strikeouts en route to a 5-2 record and 4.23 ERA.
