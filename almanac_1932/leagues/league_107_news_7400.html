Cage Belts Bunch of Base Hits
Wednesday, June 30th, 1926
Even as a kid, Knox Cage said his coaches told him the key to hitting was making contact, and letting everything else flow from there. At Alpines Ballpark today, Cage showed how well he learned that lesson.

The 18-year-old catcher had 5 hits in 6 at-bats for the Vancouver Giants, helping his team knock off the Abbotsford Alpines by a score of 15-0.

"They always said, just keep your eye on the ball, don't overswing, make contact and you never know what will happen," he said. "That's all I did today."

Knox Cage hit an RBI single in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, hit an RBI single in the 6th, doubled in the 7th and grounded out in the 9th.

This year Cage has collected a .301 batting average to date with 25 hits in 83 at-bats, 8 RBIs, no home runs and scored 5 times. He has played in 22 games.
