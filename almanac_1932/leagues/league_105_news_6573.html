NBA News: Draft Pool Revealed
Friday, August 7th, 1925
National Baseball Association officials have today announced the list of players eligible for the 1925 first-year player draft. Teams now have a chance to scout the draft pool for 90 days before the draft is held.

Also, the Transactions page gets a new section dedicated to the pool.

The early favorites to be high picks are the following:
1. Beau Lobdell, 18 year-old 2B out of college.
2. Xavier Durand, 18 year-old SS out of college.
3. Surapong St-Onge, 18 year-old SS out of college.
4. Zach Gaumer, 18 year-old 3B out of college.
5. Elvis Sylvia, 18 year-old CF out of college.
6. Jenson Rew, 18 year-old 3B out of college.
7. Clark McGregor, 18 year-old RF out of college.
8. Randall Hatz, 18 year-old SS out of college.
9. Brian Petdon, 18 year-old LF out of college.
10. Woody Carchidi, 18 year-old 2B out of college.

The official OSA Mock Draft will be released on the day of the draft, or you can review the draft pool on the draft page.
