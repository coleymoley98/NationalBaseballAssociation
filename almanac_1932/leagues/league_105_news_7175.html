Drolet Cops CL Batter of the Month Honors
Tuesday, June 1st, 1926
At only 25 years of age, Therdsak Drolet could very well be having many more months like the last this in his career.

That would be great news for fans of the Baltimore Robins, as May was one to remember for the center fielder and he has captured the Canadian League Batter of the Month award.

On the month he hit .462 with 3 home runs, 16 RBIs and 20 runs scored.
