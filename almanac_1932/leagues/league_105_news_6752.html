Consecutive-Game Hit Streak Continues for Holland
Thursday, September 10th, 1925
Chalk up another one for Wolfeburg Celtics catcher Jakkapan Holland. He now has hit in 25 straight games. Holland went 1-for-4 today's game at Comiskey Park 1910 but saw his club defeated 4-3 by the host Chicagou White Sox.

"Playing on the road always makes it harder to get hits and to get wins," Celtics manager A.J. Desjardins said after the game. "We got one of the two, but unfortunately it was the wrong one."
Jakkapan Holland doubled in the 3rd, flied out in the 5th, flied out in the 7th and grounded out in the 9th.

Presently Holland is batting .364 with 5 home runs and 56 RBIs. He has played in 92 games and scored 46 times.
