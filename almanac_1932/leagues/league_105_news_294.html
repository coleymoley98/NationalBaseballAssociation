Rebels Stop Morikawa at 20
Saturday, May 24th, 1930
Boston second baseman Takeaki Morikawa knew his hitting streak would end sooner or later. He just would have preferred later. Morikawa went 0-for-6 today in the Redcoats 10-1 victory over the New York Rebels, snapping his 20-game hit streak.

"Heck yeah I'm disappointed it's over," he said later. "My financial advisor is disappointed too. When you're riding a streak like that, everybody wants a piece of you. Nobody wants the guy who went 0-for-6."

In 18 games this season Morikawa has batted at a .361 pace with 30 hits and 1 home run.
