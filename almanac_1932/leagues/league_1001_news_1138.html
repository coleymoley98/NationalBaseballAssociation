Cordier Hits in 27 Straight Games
Tuesday, March 17th, 1931
With 1 hit at Ciudad Mexico Stadium today, Noël Cordier added another game to his hitting streak -- the Team shortstop has now hit safely for 27 games in a row. But the hitting feat wasn't enough for the Columbia today, as they fell, 6-5, to the Team Illinois.

"Frankly, I don't care about hits," Team manager Jérémie Favreau said later. "I'll let him get 4 hits in 4 at-bats if it means we get a win."
Noël Cordier hit an RBI single in the 1st, grounded out in the 3rd, grounded out in the 5th, walked in the 7th and struck out in the 9th.

Cordier is hitting .382 this year with no home runs and 7 RBIs. He also has an OBP of .395, 2 walks and scored 6 times.
