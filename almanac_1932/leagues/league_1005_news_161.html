1930 CJBT Best Pitcher Chosen
Friday, April 4th, 1930
The Canadian Junior Baseball Championship made the official announcement today. The 1930 Canadian Junior Baseball Championship Best Pitcher has gone to Jirawat Wagers of Team.

He had a superlative season, chalking up a 2-0 won-lost mark with an impressive 1.96 ERA.

Wagers logged 4 starts, threw 23 innings, yielded 20 hits, struck out 11 and held the opposition to a .220 batting average.

He received 11 first place votes out of a possible 18. Thomas Pépin of the Team Michigan finished second in voting with 4 first place votes, while Sergio Halloway of the Team Pennsylvania finished third with 2 first place votes.
