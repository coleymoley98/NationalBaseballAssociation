Cincinnati-Wolfeburg Trade: Jutras for Maier
Saturday, February 8th, 1930
Everybody is overjoyed with a trade consummated today by the Cincinnati Reds and the Wolfeburg Celtics. In the deal Cincinnati got 19-year-old minor league CF Léandre Robidoux and 19-year-old minor league SS Tui Maier from Wolfeburg and dealt 26-year-old RHP Eliot Jutras to the Celtics. The general managers that made the swap are happy and the players are happy, too. One of the GMs commented, "This was a good swap for all concerned."


Since coming into the league, Jutras has a 31-34 mark and a 4.02 ERA. The 26-year-old hurler also has struck out 217 batters in 553.2 innings and permitted 550 hits.
