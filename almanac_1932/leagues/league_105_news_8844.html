CL Selects Drouin July's #1 Pitcher
Monday, August 1st, 1927
Whether Dominic Drouin stays in Wolfeburg beyond this season remains to be seen, but while he's here he's making the most of it. In July the 31-year-old certainly pulled out all the stops by winning the Canadian League Pitcher of the Month award.

He put up some fancy numbers with a 5-1 record and an ERA of 2.50. In 54 innings he struck out 16, surrendered 51 hits, 12 walks and proved to be a solid performer in 6 starts last month.

This season Drouin has worked 159.2 innings with 40 strikeouts en route to a 10-8 record and a 4.06 ERA.
