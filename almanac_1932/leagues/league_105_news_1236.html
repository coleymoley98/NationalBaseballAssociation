Wednesday, May 6th, 1931
The "hardest feat in sports" debate is a never-ending one with no correct answer, but hitting three home runs is certainly in the conversation. That's exactly what Mozes Polk did today at Yankee Stadium 1923, going deep three times and carrying his team to victory, 22-20.

"It all came together. I was seeing the ball well and I got some good pitches to hit," Polk said. "Good things happen, when that happens."

"It ain't like we weren't trying," New York manager William Davison said. "He was hot and really torched my boys today."
