WL Presents Outstanding Pitcher Award
Thursday, September 24th, 1925
Rainiers ace Pierce Ramirez is the latest Western League Pitcher of the Year Award trophy winner. The Seattle star shined on the diamond time and again this year and finished with an outstanding 12-7 record.

In 19 starts he tossed 161 innings, posted 106 strikeouts and sported a 3.30 ERA. Opposing batters hit .253 when Ramirez was pitching.

He received 7 first place votes out of a possible 8. Kyle Sulley of the Saskatoon Coyotes finished second in voting, while Logan L'Heureux of the Calgary Wranglers finished third with 1 first place vote.
