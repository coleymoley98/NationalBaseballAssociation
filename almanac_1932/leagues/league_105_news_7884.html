Player of the Week Goes to Quebec Hurler
Monday, September 6th, 1926
Giovanni Ratanaveroj drew on his youthful energy and pitching skill to win National League Player of the Week honors last week.

The Quebec Nordiques pitcher registered 2 wins and no losses, 0.53 ERA and 3 strikeouts in 17 innings to sew up the award. The 24-year-old also held opposing hitters to 12 hits and a .211 batting average.

In 17 starts this season Ratanaveroj has gathered 13 wins while suffering 4 losses. He has pitched 147 innings, allowed 130 hits, struck out 34 and has a 2.57 ERA.
