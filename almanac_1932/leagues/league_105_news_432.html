Hit Streak Over, Braves Stop Balderas
Saturday, June 14th, 1930
Dennis Balderas saw his 21-game hitting streak wither and die today during the Quebec Nordiques win over Toronto at Ballpark of Quebec. The 25-year-old right fielder was held hitless in 4 at-bats by the Toronto Braves, but his teammates produced enough offense to take the game, 4-1.

"Am I disappointed about the hit streak? Maybe, just a little bit," a seemingly upbeat Balderas told reporters in the clubhouse. "But really, how could you be unhappy after you hit in 21 straight games? I'm just glad it lasted as long as it did. Life is good."

This season Balderas is hitting at a .360 pace with 1 home run, 22 RBIs and has scored 24 times.
