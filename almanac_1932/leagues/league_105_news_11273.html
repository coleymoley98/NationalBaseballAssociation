Roca Struts Stuff in Win Over Minnesota
Saturday, August 3rd, 1929
Detroit Lions pitcher Steve Roca blanked the Minnesota Saints 5-0 at Field of the Saints today, tossing a 5-hitter while issuing 1 walk and collecting 3 strikeouts.
