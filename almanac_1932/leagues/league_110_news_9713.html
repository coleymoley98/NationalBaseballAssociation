Nylan 5-for-6 for Rivermen
Sunday, June 24th, 1928
There aren't many who could be pro ballplayers and there are even fewer who could put up 5 hits in a day.

Vorawan Nylan is one of those rare few, as he went 5-for-6 against the Adams Mounties at Ballpark of Davenport today. The monster effort from the 18-year-old catcher helped the Davenport Rivermen take the win, 20-6.

"We made some bad pitches and he took advantage of it," said Mounties manager Timothy Wilkins. "That's the game, though. Don't make mistakes and you'll win ballgames. Make mistakes and, well, you saw what happened today."

Vorawan Nylan hit an RBI single in the 2nd, hit a two-run double in the 3rd, grounded out in the 4th, hit an RBI double in the 5th, hit an RBI single in the 6th and singled in the 8th.

This season Nylan has no home runs and a .378 batting average.
