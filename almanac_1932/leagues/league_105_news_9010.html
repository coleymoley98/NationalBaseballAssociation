Baltimore Trades Dubois for Juneau's Gleason
Saturday, August 27th, 1927
BNN is reporting a rumored trade between the Baltimore Robins and Juneau Brasseurs has been finalized. The deal, which is expected to be announced sometime during the next few hours, will reportedly send 36-year-old RHP Kritsana Dubois to Juneau in return for 19-year-old minor league C Brett Marom, 24-year-old C Yuttapong Gleason, 20-year-old minor league LHP Surasak Pona and 19-year-old minor league CF Kyle Vachon. The clubs were noncommittal on the trade, but did not deny it.


To date this year Dubois has fashioned a 6.22 ERA over 85.1 innings with a 3-4 record. He has made 12 relief appearances with 15 strikeouts and no saves.

So far this season Gleason has put up a .200 batting average with 17 hits, no home runs and 6 RBIs.
