Jr. Thunder Win Tight Race
Tuesday, July 24th, 1923
Jeremy Self called it "one of the best parts of being a pro ballplayer."

The Winnipeg Jr. Thunder starting pitcher was referring to winning a division title, as his club has just captured the crown.

"There's not many better feelings than winning a tight pennant race," he said. "We worked hard for it all year, have our record at 34-15 right now and feel we're in good shape for the postseason."

It was the 1st time the Jr. Thunder have won the division. It may be a high for Self and his teammates, but there is certainly one better feeling he's now hoping for.

"Winning the Dumont Cup," he said. "Nothing would top that."
