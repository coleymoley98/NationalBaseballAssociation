Stahl Picked CL Player of the Week
Monday, July 16th, 1923
It's always a thrill for fans of the team and students of the game alike when a superstar in the making shows what he's got. Ambler Stahl, the Ottawa Indians 20-year-old first baseman, put himself in the spotlight last week making him the clear-cut choice for Canadian League Player of the Week honors.

He ripped 14 hits in 25 at-bats, compiling a .560 average with 1 home run and 8 RBIs.

This year Stahl is batting .382 with 79 hits, 7 home runs, 38 RBIs, 23 walks, .440 OBP and has scored 40 runs.
