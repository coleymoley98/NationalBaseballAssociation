Nordiques Castleberry Claims 150th Victory
Saturday, August 4th, 1928
Nordiques ace Jeremy Castleberry reeled in his 150th career victory by beating the St. Louis Cardinaux 4-1 at St. Louis Grounds. In his historic win, he notched 3 strikeouts in 9 innings, yielded 1 run and 9 hits, while surrendering 2 walks.

"I tried to focus on each batter," said Castleberry after the game, "but as the game goes by, I couldn't help but think about the possibility. 150 is a big number. It's an exclusive club and I'm honored to be a member."

Castleberry has fashioned a 150-155 won-lost record in his career with a 3.46 ERA.
