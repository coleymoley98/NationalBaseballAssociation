Jude Ng Notches Number 150
Monday, July 14th, 1930
Jude Ng of the Boston Redcoats will go down as one of the league's legendary pitchers after his 12-4 win today against the Pittsburgh Quakers.

"It felt like it was a postseason game, one out at a time," said his manager. "It was something special to see."

The 35-year-old Ng picked up his 150th career victory at Ballpark of Pittsburgh. He hurled 9 innings, gave up 9 hits and registered 4 strikeouts along with 2 walks.

"I've worked a long time for this," Ng said. "I can't believe it's really here. It feels great!"

His career mark is 150 victories and 135 defeats. Ng also has struck out 765 batters and compiled a 3.77 ERA.
