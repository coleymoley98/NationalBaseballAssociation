Stahl's Hit Streak Soars to 20 Games
Tuesday, June 8th, 1926
Ambler Stahl's consecutive-game hitting streak has now climbed to 20 games. The Ottawa first baseman smacked 3 hits against the Wolfeburg Celtics in today's game at Lincoln Park, but he expressed mixed emotions about the streak after watching the Indians drop the game, 10-5.

"I feel good about the streak, but don't feel good when we lose," he told reporters. "I'd love to get a hit as well as a win next time so I can celebrate properly."
Ambler Stahl singled in the 1st, walked in the 3rd, hit a two-run single in the 5th, doubled in the 7th and walked in the 9th.

In the 1926 season Stahl is hitting .308 with 1 home run.
