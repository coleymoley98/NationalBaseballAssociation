Week's #1 Player: Minneapolis's Stephens
Monday, June 30th, 1924
The Player of the Week has been chosen by the Central Baseball League. The award went to Alec Stephens, first baseman of the Minneapolis Jr. Saints.

In a sensational hitting performance, he wrecked league pitchers with a .476 average to win the honor. Stephens went 10-for-21 for the week, totaled 2 home runs and 6 RBIs and scored 7 times.

This year Stephens has batted at a .347 pace with 33 hits, 6 home runs, 24 RBIs and scored 22 times.
