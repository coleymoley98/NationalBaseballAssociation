Hamilton-Cincinnati Trade: St-Cyr for Carr
Sunday, July 12th, 1931
BNN has learned the Hamilton Steelers have reportedly worked out a deal with the Cincinnati Reds. Sources close to the Steelers indicated that a trade has been reached in principle where 19-year-old minor league SS Kittipoom Arsenault, 23-year-old minor league 1B Max Carr and 24-year-old minor league C Virgil García will be swapped to Hamilton with 30-year-old minor league RHP Narakorn St-Cyr heading to Cincinnati. Neither organization would comment on the report, saying it was club policy not to speak about a trade until it was finalized.
