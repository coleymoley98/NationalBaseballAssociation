Chicagou Narrowly Wins Canadian League
Tuesday, September 11th, 1923
It's official! The Chicagou White Sox have secured the top spot in the Canadian League. Chicagou's manager Phi Fini was quick to give credit to his players.

"They believed in this team all year, and never gave up."

It won't be an easy road for Chicagou as they try to compete for their 2nd Riel Cup victory. They have to hope that their success will translate to the post-season, which is certainly not always the case. This was the 3rd division title for the team.
