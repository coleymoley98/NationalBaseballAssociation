National League Announces Great Glove Award Winners
Monday, October 17th, 1932
Great Glove Awards are the symbol for defensive excellence, a recognition of the best of the best in defense. The National League Great Glove Award trophies for 1932 have been awarded to these outstanding fielders:

Pitcher
Bunnawat Phomee (Montreal Royales)

Catcher
Cameron MacAlister (Juneau Brasseurs)

First Baseman
Surapong St-Onge (Chicagou Frontenacs)

Second Baseman
Woody Carchidi (Detroit Lions)

Third Baseman
Warren Gaines (Quebec Nordiques)

Shortstop
Arlo Cunningham (Philadelphia Mets)

Left Fielder
Pape Morel (Toronto Braves)

Center Fielder
Carter Henson (St. Louis Cardinaux)

Right Fielder
Glenn Hurd (Detroit Lions)
