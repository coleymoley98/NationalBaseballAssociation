Lunn Cops #1 Pitcher Award in July
Friday, August 1st, 1924
Raj Lunn of Quebec had an award-winning July as he took home the National League Pitcher of the Month award.

"He told me once people used to make fun of the way he pitches," Lunn's pitching coach told reporters. "There wasn't anybody laughing in July except for Raj."

Lunn was all business when he took the mound though, collecting a 5-0 record in 5 starts. He struck out 17 batters in 44 innings and kept opponents to a .163 batting average.

In season stats Lunn shows a 8-3 record in 14 games, 78.2 innings, 28 strikeouts and a 2.52 ERA.
