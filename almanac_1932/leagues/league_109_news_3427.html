EL Starter of the Month Goes to Rachal
Thursday, September 1st, 1932
The Pitcher of the Month Award has been announced and the Eastern League winner is Harrisburg hurler, Dee Dee Rachal. For his fine efforts in August he has been named the league's #1 pitcher.

During the month, Rachal excelled with a 2.01 ERA and 5 wins and 1 loss. In 53.2 innings he struck out 18 and gave up 40 hits and 14 walks.

In 17 starts this season Rachal has gathered 7 wins while suffering 7 losses. He has pitched 134 innings, allowed 154 hits, struck out 46 and has a 5.17 ERA.
