Hama Rewarded with 1929 WBL WBL Player of the Year
Monday, August 12th, 1929
Chinnawat Hama took Western Baseball League by storm this year, and he did it all at the age of 18.

For his spectacular campaign, the Abbotsford Alpines star has been named the 1929 Western Baseball League WBL Player of the Year recipient.

"To be considered amongst the best in the league is overwhelming," said the center fielder.

On the season he compiled a .250 batting average to go with a .358 on-base percentage. The native of Portland, The United Provinces also added 6 doubles, 3 triples, 3 home runs, 19 RBIs and 19 runs scored.

He received 5 first place votes out of a possible 8. Glenn Bloom of the Everett Silvertips finished second in voting with 3 first place votes, while Ollie Fortier of the Everett Silvertips finished third.
