Lebeau Toys with Montreal in 8-0 Win
Tuesday, September 6th, 1932
Bunnawat Lebeau allowed 6 hits while giving up no walks and collecting 3 strikeouts as the Minnesota Saints blanked the Montreal Royales, 8-0.

In the postgame interview, the 23-year-old right-hander said he was focused on "trying to keep the ball down and letting guys put it in play."

"It worked for me today," Lebeau said.

So far this year Lebeau has made 23 starts, tossed 167 innings, struck out 76 batters and walked 60. His record is 8-8 with a 3.56 ERA and he has limited opposing teams to a .246 batting average.
