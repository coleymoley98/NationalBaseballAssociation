Eagles Hurler Named July's #1 Starter
Saturday, August 1st, 1931
Suksayam Hardin hasn't been around long enough to know that youngsters shouldn't be winning Western Baseball League Pitcher of the Month. But when you put up numbers like his, you're going to get some notice.

In July the Spokane starter earned a 6-1 mark in 8 starts. He threw 56.1 innings, struck out 72 and compiled a solid 2.56 ERA.

In 17 starts this season Hardin has crafted a 10-3 record with a 2.84 ERA.
