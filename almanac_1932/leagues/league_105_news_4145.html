5 Hits for Redcoats Bélanger
Thursday, July 19th, 1923
It's not something you read about in the sports pages every day -- a 5-for-5 day. For many, it might come around only once in a career -- and for Nicolas Bélanger, it came around today.

The 24-year-old shortstop scattered hits all over Fenway Park 1912 to pace the Boston Redcoats to a 6-4 victory over the New York Rebels.

New York skipper Alexis Blouin told reporters in the press room that "Bélanger deserves a hat-tip."

"He hit some good pitches against us," he said. "In fact, according to our scouting reports, Bélanger hit several that he is not supposed to be able to hit."

Nicolas Bélanger singled in the 1st, doubled in the 3rd, singled in the 6th, singled in the 7th and hit a two-run double in the 8th.

In 1923 Bélanger has hit at a .414 clip with 65 hits, 4 home runs, 33 RBIs and has scored 35 times.
