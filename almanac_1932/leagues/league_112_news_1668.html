Michaud Selected Player of the Week
Monday, July 6th, 1931
Anthony Michaud burst onto the Atlantic Baseball League stage in a big way last week. This promising rookie left fielder for Pointe-Sainte-Anne garnered everyone's respect by putting up some remarkable numbers and picking up the Player of the Week trophy.

The 17-year-old haunted opposing pitchers hitting .533 (16-for-30) with 4 home runs and 10 RBIs last week.

This season Michaud is batting .397 with 28 RBIs, 34 runs scored and 6 home runs.
