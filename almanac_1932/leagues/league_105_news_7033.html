Cincinnati Trades Paquin for Baltimore's Agueda
Sunday, March 14th, 1926
The Baseball News Network is reporting that Cincinnati and Baltimore have agreed in principle to a swap that will send 33-year-old RHP Joe Paquin and 20-year-old minor league 3B Billy Mitvongsa to the Robins with 27-year-old C Bruno Agueda joining the Reds. Cincinnati general manager Techatorn Jones would not confirm the trade, but a team representative close to the negotiations said the deal is official and is now only awaiting approval from the league.


In 112 career games Agueda has hit .289 with 108 hits, 5 home runs, 49 RBIs and scored 30 runs.
