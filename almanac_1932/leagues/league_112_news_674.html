Voyageurs Chill Leven's Hot Streak
Thursday, July 10th, 1930
The 24-game hitting streak of Providence Bruins first baseman Eli Leven came to an end today at Coude Grounds. The Coude Voyageurs held him hitless in 4 at-bats and also sent the Bruins down to defeat, 7-6.

Later, Leven acknowledged to BNN reporters that the hit streak meant something to him.

"It was special," he said, "but I think other people make a bigger deal out of it than I do, or the players do. It was what we're all supposed to do; go out and hit the ball and try to score runs. It was a cool deal, but it's over now. Let's try to start a new one."

Batting .445 to date, Leven has 6 home runs and 31 RBIs.
