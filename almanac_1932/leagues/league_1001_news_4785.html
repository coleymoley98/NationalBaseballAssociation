Great Day for Cowan -- 5 Hits
Wednesday, March 12th, 1924
5 times is the charm -- at least for Simon Cowan.

The Team Columbia second baseman tore up the Team Illinois to the tune of 5 hits in 5 at-bats at San Francisco Memorial Fields today. The offensive onslaught from Cowan powered the Columbia to a 8-4 win.

"Cowan was a tough out today," Team manager Rafaël Dionne said to the press in the postgame interview. "He hit some very good pitches with that quick bat of his. Not an easy out, that's for sure."

Simon Cowan hit an RBI double in the 2nd, doubled in the 3rd, doubled in the 5th, singled in the 7th and doubled in the 9th.
