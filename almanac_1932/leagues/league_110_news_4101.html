Cork Electric in 7-0 Adams Win
Saturday, July 14th, 1923
Cyril Cork hurled a fine game for the Adams Mounties at Omaha Grounds today, holding the Omaha Jr. Falcons to 5 hits in a shutout win.

"When he started out, his command wasn't that good, but he's a tough guy," Adams manager Joseph Jones said. "The more pitches he throws, the better his command is."

The right-hander had 10 strikeouts and 2 walks in the 7-0 victory.

"An outstanding job," Omaha manager Jesse O'Byrne said. "We couldn't get anything going. Every time we got a hit, he got out of the jam. He pitched a terrific game."

To date this season Cork has compiled a 6-4 record with a 2.14 ERA in 11 starts.
