Omaha Loses Larkins for Season
Saturday, June 9th, 1928
In a news release, the Omaha Jr. Falcons announced that third baseman Andy Larkins would miss the rest of the season due to a broken bone in the elbow. Larkins sustained the injury on 06/08/1928. He "will be sorely missed," said his manager.

To date, Larkins has collected 6 hits for a .188 average. He has posted 2 home runs, scored 5 runs and driven in 7 runs.
