Thorns Captures Wild Card
Thursday, August 27th, 1931
While you set out to win a division each year, members of the Portland Thorns seemed plenty satisfied with their place as Western League wild card winners.

"The crown would have been great, but we're still in a better spot than all the teams that are going home after the regular season is over," said starting pitcher Isaac Dupont. "You can't complain."

There was certainly no complaining going on as the Thorns clubhouse was filed with loud music and even louder celebrations following the postseason clinch.
