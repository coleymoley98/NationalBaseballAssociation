Team Shuts Out Team Behind Jutras, 3-0
Sunday, March 23rd, 1930
The Team Quebec struggled at the plate today against Ontario hurler Eliot Jutras, who shackled them on 5 hits while hurling a complete game 3-0 shutout.

"What can you say? We didn't hit well," said Quebec skipper Allan Shaw in his postgame comments. "Hopefully we shake this off and bounce back tomorrow."

Jutras finished the game with 2 strikeouts and 5 walks in an excellent mound performance.

"He pitched real well," Team manager Noppadon Krarayoon said. "Eliot made pitches when he had to. He hit his spots and did a great job of mixing in his changeup and using his fastball effectively."
