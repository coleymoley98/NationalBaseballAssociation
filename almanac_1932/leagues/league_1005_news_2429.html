Mujahideen Sizzles with 1-Game Batting Streak
Wednesday, March 9th, 1932
Muneer Mujahideen collected 4 hits in today's game and extended his hitting streak to 1 games. His club, the Team Quebec, beat the Team Cordillera 18-11.

"Right now, when they throw me the ball, it looks the size of a softball," Mujahideen quipped to a BNN reporter after the game. "I'm excited about how things are clicking."
Muneer Mujahideen singled in the 1st, grounded out in the 2nd, grounded out in the 4th, singled in the 5th, hit a sac fly in the 6th, hit a two-run single in the 7th and singled in the 9th.
