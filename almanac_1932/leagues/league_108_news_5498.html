Coyotes Suffer 12-0 Drubbing from Eskimos
Tuesday, August 12th, 1924
Edmonton Eskimos hurler Cédric Dufour was at his best as he whitewashed the Saskatoon Coyotes 12-0 today.

"He just kept the ball down, did a good job, made his pitches," said Saskatoon manager James Sangapong. "We didn't do a good job making adjustments, so we helped him out a little bit."

Dufour fired a 7-hitter and totaled 8 strikeouts and 1 walk in the shutout performance.
