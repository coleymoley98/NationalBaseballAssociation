Moore Electric in 3-0 Pointe-Sainte-Anne Win
Tuesday, July 12th, 1927
The Pointe-Sainte-Anne Acadiens shut out the Drummondville Voltigeurs 3-0 today behind the strong pitching of J.J. Moore. Moore surrendered only 2 hits in recording the complete-game victory, and struck out 10.

Following the last out, Moore appeared to celebrate a little too boisterously for Drummondville manager Tai Grigsby, who could be seen scolding the famously outspoken left-hander as the clubs left the field. Moore's own manager then intervened with some words for his counterpart.

The Voltigeurs skipper would not comment on the exchange, but at the Acadiens press conference, Moore confirmed that it was regarding the celebration.

"He basically told me I was being a lousy winner," Moore said.

"And I told him he was being a lousy loser," Pointe-Sainte-Anne manager Jesse O'Byrne added.

In 9 starts this season Moore has recorded a 7-2 record and a 2.42 ERA.
