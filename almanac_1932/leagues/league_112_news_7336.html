Big Day with Bat: Mann 5-for-5
Tuesday, June 22nd, 1926
It was a sensational day at the plate for Ongyai Mann as he led the charge to a 6-4 win over the Bruins. The Coude center fielder was pure misery for the Providence pitching staff. He left them shell-shocked with 5 hits in 5 at-bats in an explosive display of hitting.

"Mann put the ball into play and he really hit it hard," said Marshall Kirkpatrick, manager for the Bruins, after the game. "When you do that, you've got a good chance to do some damage. And that's what happened. I feel like we threw some good pitches, but he just put some good swings on the ball."

Ongyai Mann singled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, singled in the 7th, walked in the 9th and hit a two-run home run off Adun Chisholm in the 10th.

Mann on the season is batting .442 with 4 home runs. His totals include 15 RBIs and 12 runs scored. He has played in 16 games.
