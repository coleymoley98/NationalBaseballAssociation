Juneau Blanks Falcons, Powe Wins
Monday, July 1st, 1929
It was a masterful game today for Victor Powe. The Juneau Jr. Brasseurs hurler pitched shutout ball against the Springfield Falcons en route to a 1-0 win at Juniors Ballpark. In the game, Powe rang up 8 strikeouts, walked 1 and allowed 4 hits.

"Victor looked like a real ace out there," Juneau manager Ken Herron said.

This year Powe has worked 53 innings with 50 strikeouts en route to a 4-0 record and 1.87 ERA.
