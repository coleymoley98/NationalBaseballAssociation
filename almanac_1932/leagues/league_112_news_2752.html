Montreal Whitewashes Sherbrooke, Gainsbourg Winner
Sunday, June 12th, 1932
It wasn't easy but the Montreal Jr. Royales made it look that way.

Starter François Gainsbourg shut out the Sherbrooke Canadiens despite having what he later called "sub-par" stuff, and the Montreal Jr. Royales cruised to victory, 5-0. Gainsbourg allowed only 4 hits and rang up 7 strikeouts but insisted later that he did not deserve primary credit for the win.

"It didn't feel quite right today, but I got a lot of help to get through," he said. "I trusted my catcher and the guys behind me bailed me out more than once when I screwed up."
