Ottawa Trades Paquette for Toronto's Hamblin
Thursday, March 24th, 1932
The Indians and the Braves finalized a deal this afternoon. Ottawa has traded for 25-year-old RF Worradet Hamblin and 20-year-old LF Vin Foreman and in return Toronto will receive 26-year-old 2B Sripatana Paquette.


All-time Paquette has played in 449 games, batted .352 with 32 home runs and 590 base hits.

In his 131-game career Hamblin is hitting .266 with 97 hits, 16 doubles, 8 triples and 4 home runs. He has scored 53 runs and driven in 46.
