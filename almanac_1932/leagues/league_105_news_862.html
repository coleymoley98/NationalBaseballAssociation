July's Best NL Hurler is Haas
Friday, August 1st, 1930
Kevin Haas took a much-deserved turn in the spotlight earlier today as he accepted the National League Pitcher of the Month award for July.

The 24-year-old Brasseurs starter is a relative newcomer to the league, but nonetheless racked up an impressive 5-1 won-lost record in 6 starts. This month he fanned 16 batters in 52 innings and compiled a 3.12 ERA.

This season Haas has worked 146 innings with 50 strikeouts en route to a 10-6 record and a 2.96 ERA.
