Hit Parade: Spears Pops 5 Hits
Saturday, July 19th, 1924
A jubilant Armani Spears joined the postgame press conference at Field of the Saints today to offer some unusual words of encouragement -- for the Minnesota Saints pitching staff he had just vanquished.

"It may not have looked like it," said the Detroit Lions first baseman, "but I saw some awful good pitches today. If luck hadn't been on my side it might have turned out a lot different."

That would not have been the conclusion of anyone who saw his dominant 5-for-5 hitting performance, which led the Lions to a 12-4 win.

"He made it look easy," said A.J. Provost, manager of the Saints. "We never figured out what to do with him. Obviously."

Armani Spears doubled in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 2nd, walked in the 4th, singled in the 6th, singled in the 8th and hit an RBI single in the 9th.

Spears on the season is hitting .296 with 8 home runs. His stats include 50 RBIs and 40 runs scored.
