Wild Card Clinched by Sherbrooke
Monday, July 25th, 1932
The Sherbrooke Canadiens say they are ready for the challenge that lies ahead.

The challenge is to capture a Laurier Cup title as a wild card team.

"The Saint Lawrence is a strong division and we still feel good about our chances even though we didn't win it," said starting pitcher Kiatjarern Bank. "It's going to be a tougher road to a title, but it's one we're ready to embark on."

The team has a 29-21 record and is seeking its 3rd Atlantic Baseball League championship.
