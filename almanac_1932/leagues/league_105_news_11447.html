White Sox Come up Short of Playoffs
Monday, September 2nd, 1929
While they stayed relevant until late in the season, a playoff berth wasn't meant to be this season for the Chicagou White Sox. The club was eliminated from playoff contention today.

"Even though we knew where things stood, it's a disappointment," White Sox manager Eric Scism said.

Chicagou last appeared in the postseason in 1927. This is their 2nd consecutive year missing the playoffs, and in total they have not advanced past the regular season 14 times in their 19-year history. In the last 10 seasons, the White Sox have missed the playoffs 5 times.
