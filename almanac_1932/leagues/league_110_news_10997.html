Choe Takes Best Bat Award in June
Monday, July 1st, 1929
Woody Choe put together a good June to earn the Central Baseball League Batter of the Month prize. A virtual unknown, he made the most of his chance to prove to Winnipeg officials that he belongs on the big league roster.

The Jr. Thunder first baseman posted a .365 batting average with 12 home runs, 21 RBIs and 23 runs scored.
