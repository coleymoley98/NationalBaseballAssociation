Royales No Match for Richardson, Saints in 9-0 Thumping
Tuesday, August 26th, 1924
Kitsada Richardson kept the Montreal Royales hitters from doing any damage at Montreal Grounds today.

Montreal could only muster 10 hits and take 2 walks against the left-handed Richardson, who controlled them throughout the game as Minnesota took the contest, 9-0. Richardson added 3 strikeouts in his complete-game effort.

"I don't know if I was on or if they were off today," Richardson told reporters after the shutout win. "But whatever it was, I hope it happens again next time."

So far this season Richardson has a record of 11-6 with 69 strikeouts and 62 walks in 174.1 innings and a 2.27 ERA.
