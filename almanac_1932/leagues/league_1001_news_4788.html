Beauséjour Clips Quebec for Cycle
Friday, March 14th, 1924
Most fans have never seen a player hit for the cycle, and most players have never done it -- but it happened today at San Francisco Memorial Fields. J.R. Beauséjour of the Team Columbia accomplished the feat against Team Quebec.

Beauséjour went 4-for-5 with 1 single, 1 double, 1 triple and 1 home run in the 13-1 Team victory.

"That was one for the grandkids, am I right?" Beauséjour said after the game.

Beauséjour also scored 4 runs and had 6 RBI in a day to remember for both Beauséjour and the crowd at San Francisco Memorial Fields.

J.R. Beauséjour hit a two-run home run off J.R. Legendre in the 1st, hit a two-run single in the 2nd, doubled in the 4th, hit a two-run triple in the 5th and grounded out in the 7th.

This season Beauséjour has hit at a .476 clip with 7 RBIs, 1 home runs and 7 runs scored. He has played in 5 games.
