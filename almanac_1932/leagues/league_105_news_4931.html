Royales Get No Resistance from Brasseurs in 11-0 Beat-Down
Saturday, May 31st, 1924
Today was Jeremy Castleberry's turn in the rotation for the Montreal Royales and he made the most of it, shutting out the Juneau Brasseurs on 4 hits.

The 31-year-old left-hander led the Royales to an easy 11-0 victory, allowing no walks and tallying 5 strikeouts.

Castleberry has made 5 starts so far this year and put up a 2-2 won-lost record with a 2.72 ERA. In 43 innings he has allowed 43 hits, walked 13 and struck out 24.
