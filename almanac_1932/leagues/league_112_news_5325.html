Atlantic Won: Bruins on Top
Monday, July 21st, 1924
Providence Bruins fans have reason to celebrate, their club has won the Atlantic.

"It feels good to clinch it for our fans," said starting pitcher Christian Tauzin. "I know there's a lot of expectations on this team and we hope to live up to them."

The division title is the team's 7th. They will now be searching for the franchise's 6th Laurier Cup victory.

"We all hope the best is yet to come," said manager Marshall Kirkpatrick.
