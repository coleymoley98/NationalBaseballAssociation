Maverick Altsman Hurt, Out for Year
Thursday, September 4th, 1924
Maverick Altsman is doubtful to make the playoffs this year, according to sources within the Detroit organization. Although the injury was initially thought to be not serious, tests have revealed the player will be out up to 6 weeks with a fractured hand. The 21-year-old second baseman suffered the injury running the bases in a recent game against the St. Louis Cardinaux.

For the season, Altsman is hitting at a .323 clip with 6 home runs, 58 RBIs and has scored 46 runs. He has 85 hits, 46 walks and a .416 on-base percentage.
