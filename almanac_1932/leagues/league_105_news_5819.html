Veteran Horn Signs 1-Year Deal
Tuesday, December 2nd, 1924
The Chicagou Frontenacs presented 38-year-old veteran Pummared Horn to the media today as they finalized a $6,800-a-year deal that brings the reliever to town for 1 year.

Horn has a reputation as one of the smartest guys in the clubhouse and Chicagou officials hope they can get as much out of Horn in the dugout as they can on the field.

Horn has made 397 appearances in his career, putting up a 27-42 record. He has tossed 507.2 innings and struck out 298. Batters have hit .254 against him, his lifetime ERA stands at 2.38 and he has 96 saves.
