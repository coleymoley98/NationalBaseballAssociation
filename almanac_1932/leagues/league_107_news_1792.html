Spokane Second Baseman Picked WBL Player of the Week
Monday, July 20th, 1931
15-year-old Phillip Minney made a few Spokane headlines during this past week as the rookie second baseman proved he could compete at the plate with his high-school idols. He captured the attention of fans, journalists and Western Baseball League officials last week and today took home Player of the Week honors for his effort.

He cracked 14 hits in 28 at-bats to put up a .500 average. Minney collected 3 home runs, 13 RBIs and scored 12 runs.

This season Minney is batting at a .331 clip with 5 home runs and 24 RBIs.
