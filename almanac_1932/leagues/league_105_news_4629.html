Cincinnati, Veteran Bass Sign Deal
Friday, November 9th, 1923
Happy times in Cincinnati as the Reds and star first baseman Jomphon Bass put the finishing touches on a $6,000 contract extension that will keep the 29-year-old in town 1 year longer.

The signing is great news for Cincinnati because players of this caliber are hard to come by.

For his career Bass has batted .303 and hit 31 home runs.
