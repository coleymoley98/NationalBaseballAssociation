Jr. Cardinaux Not Competitive in 9-0 Loss to Winnipeg
Thursday, June 18th, 1925
Lamarcus Guillemin of the Winnipeg Jr. Thunder spun his magic on the St. Louis Jr. Cardinaux today. He blanked them 9-0 on 1 hit, registering 10 strikeouts and 2 walks.

"The bottom line was I just kept the ball down," Guillemin said. "My breaking stuff was down. Everything was down."

Talking to reporters in the postgame interview, Winnipeg skipper Rafaël Bérubé said the team "got solid pitching, good defense and took great team at-bats in a lot of different situations."

"Lamarcus pitched really well but this was a solid performance by everyone," he said.

This year Guillemin has gone 3-1 with an ERA of 1.08 and has 39 strikeouts in 33.1 innings.
