Gosselin Spectacular in 3-0 Win
Friday, June 1st, 1928
Narongsak Gosselin subdued the Pittsburgh Liberty, 3-0, pitching a 1-hit gem for the London Indians at Progressive Field.

"He was kind of teasing them with his off-speed stuff, and his fastball was moving everywhere," London skipper Bunnawat Gresh said afterward.

Gosselin registered 9 strikeouts and issued no walks in his superb performance.

"We just didn't swing the bats very well. Or else he just pitched good, whichever way you want to look at it," said Pittsburgh manager Virgil McLain after the defeat.
