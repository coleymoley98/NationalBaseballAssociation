Another Setback For Toronto's Cooper Yuschak
Thursday, October 2nd, 1930
Blue Sox starting pitcher Cooper Yuschak has suffered a setback in his rehabilitation for a herniated disc. He was expected to finish treatment soon and rejoin the team slightly ahead of schedule. Doctors reevaluated him after he felt severe pain in a recent workout. The preliminary examination revealed more damage and is worse than the first injury. Anonymous sources in Toronto told BNN Cooper Yuschak would be absent from the roster quite a bit longer than originally diagnosed. He would now have to miss at least 2 weeks.

To date this year in 15 starts, Yuschak has crafted a 11-5 mark with a 3.08 ERA.
