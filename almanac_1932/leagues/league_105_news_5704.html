Lions Dominate, Sweep League Championship Series
Monday, September 22nd, 1924
With a 5-2 victory at West Side Grounds, the Detroit Lions have completed a sweep of the Chicagou Frontenacs in the League Championship Series.

"On to the Riel Cup and feeling good," said Benjamin Bélanger. "I don't think our guys expected a sweep, but we'll certainly take it and rest up a bit."

For the series, Bélanger hit .667 and won series MVP honors. He had 2 home runs, drove in 5 RBI and scored 6 runs.

The Detroit Lions don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Boston Redcoats - New York Rebels series. New York leads the series 3 games to 2. The Riel Cup schedule will be announced after the League Championship Series finishes.
