It's Official: SP Phomee Signs with Rebels
Thursday, December 15th, 1932
Bunnawat Phomee and New York put the finishing touches on a $22,433-a-year deal that will keep the 26-year-old in a New York uniform for 6 years.

Team officials reportedly held their breath at the last moment, since Phomee had second thoughts mulling over other offers.

Over his career Phomee has 34 victories and 28 defeats with a 3.39 ERA. The 26-year-old hurler also has struck out 219 batters in 570.2 innings and surrendered 556 hits.
