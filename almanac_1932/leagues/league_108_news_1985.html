Razor-Sharp Eston Blanks Seattle
Saturday, August 8th, 1931
Magic was in the air and on the mound at Ballpark of Seattle today as Greg Eston held the Seattle Rainiers spellbound in his 3-0 shutout victory. The Saskatoon Coyotes pitcher went the distance, rang up 8 strikeouts, walked 2 and held the Rainiers to 4 hits to garner the win.

"He just had us in his thrall," Seattle manager Sebastian Woodhall said later. "We couldn't put anything together against him."

Eston has made 14 starts, tossed 119.2 innings, struck out 67 batters and walked 36. His record is 10-4 with a 3.23 ERA and he has limited other teams to a .225 batting average.
