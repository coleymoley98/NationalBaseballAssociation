EBL Pitcher of the Year Goes to Adeleye
Saturday, August 18th, 1923
The Eastern Baseball League has heralded Prayad Adeleye of the Canton Legends as the #1 man on the mound for the 1923 campaign.

This year's EBL Pitcher of the Year winner stood out by running up an impressive 7-2 record, holding opposing hitters to a .200 batting average and posting a 1.98 ERA. The 16-year-old hurler tossed 122.2 innings, gave up 90 hits, fanned 106 and issued 17 walks.

He received 10 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Chanchai Bourgault of the Morgantown Mountaineers finished second in voting, while Sakunchai Landis of the Springfield Falcons finished third.
