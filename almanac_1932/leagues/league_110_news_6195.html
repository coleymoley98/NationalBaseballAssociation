Hopson Has 20-Game Hitting Streak
Wednesday, June 17th, 1925
Apisit Hopson of the Adams Mounties extended his hitting streak to 20 straight games with 2 hits in today's 10-9 triumph over the Omaha Jr. Falcons at Ballpark of Adams.

"Apisit has been making it easy for me to pencil his name in the lineup every day," Mounties manager Joseph Jones joked after the game.
Apisit Hopson struck out in the 1st, flied out in the 3rd, singled in the 6th, hit a two-run home run off Philip Lightford in the 7th and walked in the 9th.

Hitting .429 to date, Hopson has 6 home runs and 18 RBIs. He has played in 16 games.
