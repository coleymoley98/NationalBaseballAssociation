Wolfeburg Celtics Ship Lacroixille to Baltimore
Monday, July 13th, 1925
Fans in Wolfeburg woke up this morning to learn that their team had traded 24-year-old RHP Jiramet Lacroixille to the Baltimore Robins. In return, the Celtics will receive 19-year-old minor league SS Danny DeWitt, 24-year-old minor league RHP Kazuhiro Aoki, 22-year-old minor league C Alex Massey and 23-year-old minor league RHP Daniel Porte.


This year Lacroixille has registered 5 wins and 7 losses in 13 starts with a 4.11 ERA while registering 24 strikeouts and 37 walks in 100.2 innings.

Since joining the league, Aoki has compiled an ERA of 4.45 with a career record of 8-9. He has 52 strikeouts in 161.2 innings and has held opponents to a .300 batting average.
