Injury Derails Season for Blanchette
Saturday, May 2nd, 1931
Team officials for the Minneapolis Jr. Saints confirmed that a torn flexor tendon in his elbow will force Carl Blanchette to miss the rest of the season. Blanchette sustained the injury on 04/29/1931. It remains to be seen who will replace Blanchette on the active roster.
