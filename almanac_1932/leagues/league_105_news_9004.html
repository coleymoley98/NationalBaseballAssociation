Pelletier Stretches Hit String to 20
Friday, August 26th, 1927
Jason Pelletier, right fielder for the Boston Redcoats, ran his consecutive-game hitting streak to 20 games with 4 hits in his club's 8-5 victory today over the Wolfeburg Celtics at Fenway Park 1912 today.

"Not the outcome we wanted," Wolfeburg manager A.J. Desjardins said. "There's never a good time to lose, and it's never fun to be on the wrong end of somebody else's accomplishment."
Jason Pelletier singled in the 1st, doubled in the 3rd, hit an RBI single in the 4th, flied out in the 6th and doubled in the 8th.

This year Pelletier is batting .351 with 6 home runs and 60 RBIs.
