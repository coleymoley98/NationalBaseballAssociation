Garza at His Best, Blanks Calgary
Monday, July 11th, 1932
Magic was in the air and on the mound at Eagles Ballpark today as Manuel Garza held the Calgary Wranglers spellbound in his 7-0 shutout victory. The Spokane Eagles pitcher went the distance, rang up 1 strikeout, walked 2 and held the Wranglers to 2 hits to garner the win.

"He just had us in his thrall," Calgary manager Hunter Jones said later. "We couldn't put anything together against him."
