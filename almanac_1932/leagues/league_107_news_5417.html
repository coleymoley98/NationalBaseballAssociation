Boulay Wins WBL Batter of the Month
Friday, August 1st, 1924
Taradin Boulay made his presence known as one of the best young stars in the Western Baseball League this past month.

The Boise Broncos first baseman shined through the month of July and has won the Batter of the Month award.

The 18-year-old hit .430 with 6 home runs, 19 RBIs and 17 runs scored.

So far on the season Boulay is hitting .382 with 8 home runs, 31 RBIs and 35 runs scored. He has 71 hits and played in 50 games.
