Côté Blanks Voyageurs, 3-0
Thursday, July 23rd, 1931
In an impressive mound performance, D.J. Côté of the Quebec City Jr. Nordiques checked the Coude Voyageurs on a 4-hitter and held them scoreless.

"He was getting ahead of us and keeping the ball off the plate," said Coude manager Pat Sharpe. "Côté kept us confused the whole game."

The Quebec City hurler rang up 4 strikeouts and issued no walks in his 3-0 shutout victory.

This year Côté has a 4-5 mark in 11 starts with a 2.57 ERA. He has given up 63 hits in 77 innings, struck out 56 and issued 16 walks. Opposing clubs have compiled a .221 batting average against him.
