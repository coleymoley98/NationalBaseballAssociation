National League Announces #1 Starter
Wednesday, July 1st, 1931
D.J. Pham's been around the league long enough to appreciate the honor he received today. After going 5-0 in June, the 28-year-old Detroit hurler has been named National League Pitcher of the Month.

He fanned 15 in 44 innings, logged an ERA of 1.64 and allowed opponents to hit .186 against him.

Pham has fashioned a 8-2 mark for the season, 2.63 ERA and thrown 78.2 innings in 11 starts. Opposing clubs are batting .231 against him.
