Rehab Setback For Wilfred Fredericks
Friday, July 8th, 1932
Wilfred Fredericks, center fielder for the Philadelphia Mets, was making great progress in healing a strained abdominal muscle according to a team spokesman, but suffered a setback yesterday. "Wilfred was doing great in his rehab until he overdid it in a recent workout. Now doctors say it will be at least one week before he will be able to rejoin the club."

So far this season Fredericks is batting .283 with no home runs, 3 RBIs and 6 runs scored. He has 13 hits in 46 at-bats, 5 walks and a .353 OBP. Fredericks has played in 13 games.
