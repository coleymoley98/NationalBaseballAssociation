Ethan Irish Hurt, Will Miss Rest of Year
Saturday, August 25th, 1928
The doctors have spoken and Ethan Irish of the Toronto Blue Sox didn't like what he heard. The 26-year-old second baseman is almost certainly gone for the rest of the year and depending on his progress could miss some of next season as well. Irish sustained his season-ending injury during the Chicagou White Sox game yesterday at Comiskey Park 1910. He had to leave the game because he hurt himself while throwing the ball. Today the Toronto medical staff reported Irish had a sprained ankle and would be absent from the lineup for at least 6 weeks.

So far this year in 92 games Irish is hitting .281 with 7 home runs, 57 RBIs and 64 runs scored.
