Chicagou: 3B Thompson Coming to Town
Tuesday, November 20th, 1923
The Chicagou front office announced the signing of Sheehan Thompson today as the team's new third baseman. The 27-year-old came to terms with the White Sox and will be hauling in $11,950 a year over 8 years.

In his 708-game career Thompson is batting .295 with 825 hits, 18 home runs, 337 runs scored and 354 RBIs.
