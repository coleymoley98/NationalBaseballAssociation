Joly Electric in 1-0 Win
Friday, July 23rd, 1926
Renato Joly led the Davenport Rivermen to a 1-0 shutout victory over the St. Louis Jr. Cardinaux at Ballpark of Davenport today.

"My pitching coach told me before the game just to bear down and focus," Joly recalled later. "He said no one was going to hit me if I'm throwing a good breaking ball with good location."

Joly yielded 3 hits and had 14 strikeouts and 2 walks in the standout performance.

"I thought he put the ball where he wanted to," St. Louis manager Khalil Norrell said. "And that's just about the whole secret to pitching."

To date Joly is 9-2 in 13 starts with a 1.88 ERA and has 134 strikeouts in 100.1 innings.
