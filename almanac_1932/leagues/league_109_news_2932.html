EL's Top Pitcher in June Is Wiles
Friday, July 1st, 1932
Kittisak Wiles of Albany had an award-winning June as he took home the Eastern League Pitcher of the Month award.

"He told me once people used to make fun of the way he pitches," Wiles's pitching coach told reporters. "There wasn't anybody laughing in June except for Kittisak."

Wiles was all business when he took the mound though, collecting a 4-0 record in 5 starts. He struck out 23 batters in 44 innings and kept opponents to a .241 batting average.
