Morgantown's Legault Goes Ballistic with 5 Hits
Saturday, June 28th, 1924
It was high-fives all around for Oliver Legault at Juniors Ballpark today.

He got them from his teammates and he got them at the plate during an outstanding hitting performance against the Springfield Falcons. Legault smashed 5 hits in 5 at-bats on his monster day, leading his club to a 11-7 win.

"Oliver was hitting everything," said Mountaineers manager Logan Hevey. "It really was a special game for him."

In the opposing dugout, Falcons manager Cal Mentink told BNN beat writers that Legault "ambushed" the club.

"Obviously we didn't have much success against him, but we'll have another chance," he said. "Hopefully, Legault oversleeps next time."

Oliver Legault singled in the 1st, hit a two-run double in the 3rd, hit a solo-shot off Anthony Pringle in the 4th, doubled in the 6th and hit an RBI double in the 8th.

This year Legault is hitting .360 with 36 hits, 3 home runs, 16 RBIs and has scored 17 runs.
