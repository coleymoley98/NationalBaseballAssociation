Western Baseball League Picks Best Player
Monday, June 24th, 1929
Richard Weets drew on his youthful energy and pitching skill to win Western Baseball League Player of the Week honors last week.

The Everett Silvertips pitcher registered 3 wins and no losses, 0.48 ERA and 21 strikeouts in 18.2 innings to sew up the award. The 16-year-old also held opposing hitters to 12 hits and a .185 batting average.

On the season Weets has compiled a 4-1 mark with a 2.02 ERA in 7 starts. In pitching 40 innings he has 40 strikeouts and 10 walks while limiting the opposition to a .227 batting average.
