Wolfeburg Celebrates Wild Card Win
Monday, September 14th, 1931
It may have been a wild card win, but the Wolfeburg Celtics plan to "celebrate like it's a division title," according to center fielder Elvis Sylvia.

"The playoffs is the playoffs no matter how you got there. This means a lot to the guys in the clubhouse as we have a chance to win a Riel Cup," he said.

With a 57-54 record, Wolfeburg will be seeking its 2nd National Baseball Association championship.
