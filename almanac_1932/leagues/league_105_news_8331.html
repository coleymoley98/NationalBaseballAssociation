Indians Paholpat Claims 100th Victory
Wednesday, May 25th, 1927
Ottawa ace Chutipol Paholpat solidified his place in league history with his 100th career victory today at Baker Bowl 1910. Paholpat and the Indians bested Philadelphia 1-0. He checked the Liberty on 5 hits in 8.1 innings and finished the day with 3 strikeouts and 3 walks.

"I've worked hard for this day," Paholpat said. "Sometimes I didn't think it would happen. Most of the time it seemed so far off. Now it's here. It's like a dream!"

So far in his career, Paholpat has collected 100 wins and 116 losses and has recorded an ERA of 3.88 and struck out 579 batters while pitching 1968.2 innings.
