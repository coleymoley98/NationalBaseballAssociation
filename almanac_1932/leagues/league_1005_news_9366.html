Diehl New All-Time Hits Leader
Saturday, March 10th, 1928
Baseball has a new Hit King.

"Team New York veteran Lucien Diehl surpassed the old mark in a contest at Los Angeles Grounds today, logging hit number 98.

"I never expected to have the wonderful career that I have been so blessed with," Diehl told the press after the game in which he set the new league career record. "I just hoped to be around a decade or so and be a good ballplayer. Well, it's been amazing."

With 98 hits, the Team star surpasses the record previously held by the great Ted Jones.

Lucien Diehl singled in the 1st, flied out in the 3rd and singled in the 4th.

This year Diehl is batting .389 with no home runs and 5 RBIs.
