Welcome Back Gaillard
Tuesday, November 11th, 1924
Today marks the realization of a dream that has been years in the making, finally coming to fruition: the chance for Luke Gaillard to finally play for his hometown team, the Boston Redcoats.

"It's funny", Gaillard observed to reporters during the press conference announcing the acquisition. "You spend your whole childhood with posters from your hometown team on the bedroom wall, telling your friends that you'll be a member of the Redcoats when you grow up. But even when you finally make "the Show", it still doesn't usually work out that way. There are lots of other teams out there, and you still have a family to feed, you know?"

After putting on the colors of his new team, he was asked how he felt. He broke into a big grin.

"I feel like a kid again."
