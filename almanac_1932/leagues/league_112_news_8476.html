Gomes and Siteti Fight, Get Suspended
Saturday, June 18th, 1927
After an intense confrontation, both the Voltigeurs Mahama Gomes and the Yankees B.J. Siteti were handed their punishments today. The league commissioner's office issued suspensions for an on-the-field conflict that occurred at a recent Drummondville-New Britain game. Mahama Gomes will miss 3 games, while B.J. Siteti was suspended for 6 games.

"Sometimes we go too far and have to pay the consequences," Mahama Gomes commented later. "Hopefully cooler heads will prevail the next time we play."

Gomes is batting .172 this year with 3 home runs, 9 RBIs and 6 runs scored. He has 10 hits in 58 at-bats.

In 5 starts this year Siteti has recorded a 1-0 record and a 5.40 ERA.
