Juneau RF J.R. Beauséjour Injured
Thursday, June 30th, 1927
The Juneau Brasseurs will be minus J.R. Beauséjour for about 5 weeks after the right fielder suffered a strained hamstring while running the bases in a recent game against the St. Louis Cardinaux. Beauséjour and Brasseurs skipper Jake Downing had been hoping the post-game examination would show less damage than originally thought.

So far this season Beauséjour has hit .376 with 3 home runs, 34 RBIs and scored 24 times.
