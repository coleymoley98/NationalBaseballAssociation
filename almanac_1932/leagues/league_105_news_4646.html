Toronto Blue Sox Ship Paholpat to Philadelphia
Monday, November 19th, 1923
The Blue Sox and Liberty announced a deal that was completed today. Toronto sent 28-year-old LHP Chutipol Paholpat to Philadelphia for 27-year-old 2B Blake Qualey. Both teams appear happy with the outcome of the trade. The general managers told the Baseball News Network they felt the swap was beneficial for all parties involved.


Paholpat has gone 78-96 with an ERA of 3.78 in his career, piling up 477 strikeouts in 1548 innings pitched.

During his career Qualey has played in 519 games, batted .297 with 11 home runs and 587 base hits.
