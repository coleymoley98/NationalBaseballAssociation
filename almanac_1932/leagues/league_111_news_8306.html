Paul Keeton to Retire
Friday, May 20th, 1927
All good things must end. So it does for the career of Springfield reliever Paul Keeton. This morning, Keeton announced his retirement from baseball. The decision does not come as a complete surprise. Keeton suffered a torn rotator cuff earlier this season. Treatment of the injury has not resulted in much progress. And Keeton, cognizant of the odds, had already called his return doubtful. The team will continue to explore its options for replacing Keeton.
