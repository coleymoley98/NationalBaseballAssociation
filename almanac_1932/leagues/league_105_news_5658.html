Detroit Lions Clinch First in National League
Friday, September 12th, 1924
Detroit Lions fans are walking a little prouder today after their team officially clinched the National League.

"There's no reason to believe we can't win it all," shortstop Noël Cordier said bluntly. "Whether or not we're favorites, we can definitely do what it takes to bring the Riel Cup home to Detroit."

The division crown is the 5th for the Lions. The club will be looking for its 5th Riel Cup victory.

Team manager Brian Ahn said "preparation and focus" will be key to bring home the championship.
