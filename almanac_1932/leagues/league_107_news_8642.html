Tuesday, July 5th, 1927
Even as a kid, Burt O'Neill said his coaches told him the key to hitting was making contact, and letting everything else flow from there. At Ballpark of Ranier today, O'Neill showed how well he learned that lesson.

The 15-year-old center fielder had 5 hits in 6 at-bats for the Vancouver Giants, helping his team knock off the Ranier Seahawks by a score of 12-11.

"They always said, just keep your eye on the ball, don't overswing, make contact and you never know what will happen," he said. "That's all I did today."

Burt O'Neill singled in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, grounded out in the 4th, hit an RBI single in the 6th, singled in the 8th and hit a two-run single in the 9th.

This year O'Neill has 2 home runs and a .325 batting average.
