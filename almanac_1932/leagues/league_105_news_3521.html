Frontenacs Heading to League Chamionship Series
Tuesday, September 20th, 1932
The Chicagou Frontenacs are riding high after winning the Division Series and eliminating the St. Louis Cardinaux by a series score of 3-1.

The final blow came tonight at St. Louis Grounds, when Chicagou secured its spot in the League Chamionship Series with a 14-0 victory.

"It feels good to put them away because we really didn't want to let them crawl back into this series," said center fielder Gautam Mijangos, who was named series MVP. "A team like that, you need to bury them when you get the chance."

Mijangos certainly helped his team's cause, as he hit .600 with a .667 on-base percentage in the series. He also chipped in 0 home runs, drove in 4 RBI and scored 4 runs.

The Chicagou Frontenacs don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Quebec Nordiques - Montreal Royales series. That series is tied at 2 games apiece. The League Chamionship Series schedule will be announced after the Division Series finishes.
