Pittsburgh-Montreal Trade: Dinsmore for Narcisse
Saturday, March 5th, 1932
The Quakers and Royales pulled the trigger on a deal that will see 27-year-old minor league RF Beckham Dinsmore head to Montreal in exchange for 24-year-old minor league RHP Jame Narcisse. A source close to Pittsburgh officials said "The teams spent long hours coming to the agreement and in the end, both agreed the deal should help both clubs."


Dinsmore has batted .327 for his career with 393 hits, 79 doubles, 7 triples, 36 home runs, and 201 RBIs.
