Star LF Hita Signs for 2 years
Wednesday, September 30th, 1931
The Reds inked a deal with 26-year-old Diogenes Hita today signaling that it not only wanted him on the field but in the clubhouse.

Publicly, Diogenes Hita has spoken highly of Cincinnati, so the extension wasn't a total surprise.

Hita will draw $11,600 over the next 2 years.

Hita is hitting .309 this year with 3 home runs, 19 RBIs and 23 runs scored. He has 50 hits in 162 at-bats, 14 walks and a .362 on-base percentage. Hita has played in 58 games.
