Cunningham Crowned Top ABL Hitter in July
Thursday, August 1st, 1929
Sol Cunningham put together a good July to earn the Atlantic Baseball League Batter of the Month prize. A virtual unknown, he made the most of his chance to prove to Drummondville officials that he belongs on the big league roster.

The Voltigeurs second baseman posted a .368 batting average with 9 home runs, 21 RBIs and 12 runs scored.

So far in 1929 Cunningham is batting .342 with 13 home runs, 37 RBIs and 24 runs scored. He has 51 hits in 149 at-bats and a .363 OBP.
