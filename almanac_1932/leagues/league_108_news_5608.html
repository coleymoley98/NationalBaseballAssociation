Wong Wins Best Bat Award in August
Monday, September 1st, 1924
Even though he is a newcomer to Western League, Nathan Wong put up some good numbers in August and earned the WL Batter of the Month award.

He was one of Omaha's offensive leaders with a .337 average in 27 games. He also racked up 6 home runs, 28 RBIs, and 24 runs scored.

In his stats this season, Wong has posted a batting average of .313 with 98 hits in 313 at-bats, 14 home runs and 64 RBIs. He has also scored 60 runs, walked 49 times and compiled a .402 OBP.
