St-Onge Snares EBL Top Batter Award in July
Wednesday, August 1st, 1928
When Wyatt St-Onge stepped up to the plate in July he did it with confidence, and today he was honored with the Eastern Baseball League Batter of the Month award.

The young Hamilton second baseman posted a .494 batting average with 2 home runs, 24 RBIs and scored 18 runs. St-Onge also worked pitchers for 7 walks to post a .527 on-base percentage.

Thus far in 1928 St-Onge is hitting .419 with 4 home runs, 37 RBIs, 36 runs scored and has compiled a .471 OBP.
