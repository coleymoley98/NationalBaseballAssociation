League Suspends Fournier and Henton for Fisticuffs
Tuesday, June 21st, 1932
Moses Fournier of Winnipeg and Zach Henton of Omaha were the main participants in a recent bench-clearing brawl and today the league handed out its punishment. Fournier will have to miss 5 games for his part in the brawl, while Henton was given 6 games. League officials announced their suspensions today.

To date this season Fournier is hitting .125 with 2 home runs.

Henton has posted a 0-2 record for the year with a 3.53 ERA. Teams are hitting .205 against him and he has rung up 27 strikeouts in 35.2 innings.
