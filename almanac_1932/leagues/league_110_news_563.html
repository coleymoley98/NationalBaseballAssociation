Week's Best Player Honored by CBL
Monday, June 30th, 1930
16-year-old Giordano Thompson made a few St. Louis headlines during this past week as the rookie right fielder proved he could compete at the plate with his high-school idols. He captured the attention of fans, journalists and Central Baseball League officials last week and today took home Player of the Week honors for his effort.

He cracked 10 hits in 19 at-bats to put up a .526 average. Thompson collected 2 home runs, 10 RBIs and scored 5 runs.

So far this season Thompson is batting .413 with 8 home runs, 32 RBIs and 23 runs scored. He has 43 hits in 104 at-bats, 4 walks and a .441 OBP. Thompson has played in 26 games.
