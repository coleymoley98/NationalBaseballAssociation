1929 Top Player Honored By EBL
Friday, August 16th, 1929
The manager of the Canton Legends said he thinks Marc Fortin is only going to get better as he gets older.

He may only be 17, but it will be hard to top the right fielder's 1929 campaign, as he has been named the winner of the Eastern Baseball League EBL Player of the Year.

His winning campaign featured a .322 batting average and a .406 on-base percentage this year. In 45 games he piled up 49 hits, 10 doubles, no triples, 13 home runs, 31 RBIs and 27 runs scored.

He received 5 first place votes out of a possible 10. Alex Trudel of the Gallant Black Sox finished second in voting with 3 first place votes, while Dylan Gagné of the Buffalo Rapids finished third with 2 first place votes.
