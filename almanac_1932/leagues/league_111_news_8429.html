Cheek Stands Out in North Bay Shutout
Sunday, June 12th, 1927
Kriangkrai Cheek of the North Bay Battalion was on fire against the Hamilton Jr. Steelers at Battalion Ballpark today. He shut them out 1-0 on 5 hits, collecting 8 strikeouts and allowing 2 walks.

"The first two pitches I'm looking for an out, a ground ball or a fly ball," Cheek said later. "I'm trying to get quick outs. I've never been a big believer in trying to strike a guy out from the very first pitch. It's too easy to end up behind in the count doing that."
