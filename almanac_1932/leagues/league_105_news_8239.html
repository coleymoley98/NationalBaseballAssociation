Cliche Quiet on Chicagou Discussions
Monday, April 11th, 1927
There's excitement amongst Chicagou fans, as rumors of a possible deal between the Frontenacs and Calvin Cliche spread like wildfire, causing each camp to issue a statement denying the existence of talks.

Still, BNN's sources tell us that a deal is in the pipeline and could involve $78,400 to bring the All Star-caliber player to town.

In his lifetime, the 28-year-old Cliche has won 63 and lost 62, piling up 400 strikeouts and 335 walks in 1119 innings with a 4.03 ERA. Opponents have hit .285 against him.
