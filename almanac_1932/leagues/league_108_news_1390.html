Great Day for Welter -- 5 Hits
Sunday, June 7th, 1931
Victoria third baseman Cole Welter had a day to remember today, 5-for-6 against the Spokane Eagles at Eagles Ballpark as the Admirals picked up a 24-8 victory.

"He was patient up there today," Victoria skipper Michel Arquette told the press after the game. "I like the way he waited for his pitch and wasn't overly aggressive. Welter has a tendency to chase bad balls sometimes."

Cole Welter flied out in the 1st, hit a solo-shot off Manuel Valcourt in the 2nd, doubled in the 4th, singled in the 6th, walked in the 6th, singled in the 8th and hit a three-run home run off Zachary Morneau in the 9th.

This season in 7 games Welter is batting .389 with 3 home runs, 10 RBIs and has scored 10 times.
