1500 Hits for Morales
Sunday, May 13th, 1928
"It would have been a lot nicer if my 1500th hit had won the game," said Carmen Morales after the Boston Redcoats had been tripped up by the Ottawa Indians, 5-4, spoiling his big day. The Redcoats shortstop gained access to the exclusive 1500-hit club by going 1-for-5 in the game played at Fenway Park 1912.

"The standing ovation by the fans is not something I will ever forget," Morales said to the press after the game. "I think even the Indians fans were cheering me."

In his all-time stats Morales is hitting .357 with 1500 hits, 28 home runs, 682 runs scored and 673 RBIs.
