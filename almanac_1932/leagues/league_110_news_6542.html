Adams Tops St. Louis
Saturday, August 1st, 1925
It's party time in Adams. The confetti is flying and residents are smiling.

The Adams Mounties have reached the mountaintop, capturing the 1925 Dumont Cup today. They defeated the St. Louis Jr. Cardinaux 4-3 at Ballpark of St. Louis to officially wrap up the series 4-1.

Asked how it feels to be champion, left fielder Lou Lepore was to the point.

"It feels great, like a million bucks. I'm so happy right now, not just for me but for all the guys," he said. "We put a lot of work into this and deserve to be champions."
