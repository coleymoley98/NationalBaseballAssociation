Thanh and McLoughlin Fight, Get Suspended
Friday, August 24th, 1923
Jackie Thanh of the Chicagou Frontenacs and Colin McLoughlin of the Juneau Brasseurs will be on the sidelines for a few days. They have been suspended for 5 and 6 games respectively by the league office for causing the bench-emptying free-for-all at the recent Frontenacs-Brasseurs game at West Side Grounds.

Neither player had any comment about the suspensions. Both clubs told BNN Thanh and McLoughlin will begin serving them soon.

In the 1923 season Thanh is hitting .328 with 1 home run.

In pitching stats this season McLoughlin has rung up 6 wins and 10 losses in 19 games. He has an ERA of 3.33 and has fanned 40 batters in 140.2 innings.
