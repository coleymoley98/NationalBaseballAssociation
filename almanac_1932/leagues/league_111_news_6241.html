Legends Not Competitive in 8-0 Loss to Gallant
Tuesday, June 23rd, 1925
In an outstanding mound performance, Gallant Black Sox starter Nathan Grimes fired a complete-game shutout against the Canton Legends at Juniors Ballpark today.

Grimes was the key to the Black Sox's 8-0 win. The 16-year-old right-hander was in high spirits on the mount for much of the game, his mood largely undented by the 6 hits and 2 walks he allowed.

"6 hits?" he said with a laugh following the game. "Listen, I'd be happy with a 30-hitter as long as it was a shutout."

This season Grimes has fanned 52 in 53.2 innings and collected a 2-3 record with a 3.52 ERA.
