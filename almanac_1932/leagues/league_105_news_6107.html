Chicagou-Ottawa Fight Results in Suspensions
Wednesday, June 3rd, 1925
Cedric Broussard of the Chicagou White Sox and Isaac Coulter of the Ottawa Indians will be on the sidelines for a few days. They have been suspended for 4 and 6 games respectively by the league office for causing the bench-emptying free-for-all at the recent White Sox-Indians game at Comiskey Park 1910.

Neither player had any comment about the suspensions. Both clubs told BNN Broussard and Coulter will begin serving them soon.

For the year Broussard has hit .288 with no home runs, 9 RBIs and scored 6 runs.

To date this season Coulter is 1-2 with 5 starts and an ERA of 5.71 with 6 strikeouts in 41 innings.
