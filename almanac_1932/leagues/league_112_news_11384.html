Atlantic Baseball League Top Hurler Announced
Sunday, August 25th, 1929
This past season Daoyod Sampahungsith put on quite a show in the Atlantic Baseball League. In 9 starts he posted a 7-0 record for Champlain to capture the 1929 ABL Pitcher of the Year.

In 9 starts and 56.2 innings, Sampahungsith yielded 31 hits, holding the opposition to a .150 average. He chalked up 85 strikeouts and posted a 0.64 ERA.

He received 11 first place votes out of a possible 14. Blake Grashorn of the Halifax Highlanders finished second in voting, while Azwan Philips of the Providence Bruins finished third with 2 first place votes.
