Lafon Tags Redcoats for 5 Hits
Thursday, September 18th, 1930
It was high-fives all around for Élie Lafon at League Park today.

He got them from his teammates and he got them at the plate during an outstanding hitting performance against the Boston Redcoats. Lafon smashed 5 hits in 6 at-bats on his monster day, leading his club to a 12-11 win.

"Élie was hitting everything," said Celtics manager Chaichan Phonsa. "It really was a special game for him."

In the opposing dugout, Redcoats manager Brian Ahn told BNN beat writers that Lafon "ambushed" the club.

"Obviously we didn't have much success against him, but we'll have another chance," he said. "Hopefully, Lafon oversleeps next time."

Élie Lafon singled in the 1st, singled in the 4th, singled in the 5th, doubled in the 7th, grounded out in the 8th and singled in the 10th.

So far this season Lafon is hitting .373 with 6 home runs, 86 RBIs and 79 runs scored. He has 175 hits in 469 at-bats and a .417 OBP.
