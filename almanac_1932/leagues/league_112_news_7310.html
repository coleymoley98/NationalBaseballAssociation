Hirst Collects Player of the Week Award
Monday, June 21st, 1926
Ali Hirst burst onto the Atlantic Baseball League stage in a big way last week. This promising rookie second baseman for Pointe-Sainte-Anne garnered everyone's respect by putting up some remarkable numbers and picking up the Player of the Week trophy.

The 15-year-old haunted opposing pitchers hitting .464 (13-for-28) with 3 home runs and 12 RBIs last week.

This year Hirst has 4 home runs and a .410 batting average.
