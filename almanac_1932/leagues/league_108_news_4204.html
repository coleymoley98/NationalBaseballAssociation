Calgary Bats Silenced by Lunsford
Monday, July 23rd, 1923
Magic was in the air and on the mound at Thorns Ballpark today as Seven Lunsford held the Calgary Wranglers spellbound in his 6-0 shutout victory. The Portland Thorns pitcher went the distance, rang up 7 strikeouts, walked 3 and held the Wranglers to 3 hits to garner the win.

"He just had us in his thrall," Calgary manager Malik Hill said later. "We couldn't put anything together against him."

Presently Lunsford has a 6-4 mark in 11 starts with a 4.70 ERA. He has given up 94 hits in 82.1 innings, struck out 56 and issued 41 walks. Opposing clubs have compiled a .275 batting average against him.
