Kingston Hurler Tops in Pitcher of the Year Award Voting
Thursday, September 18th, 1930
Mathis Séverine of the Kingston Indians had a fine season and has been rewarded with the Eastern League Pitcher of the Year Award for 1930.

The Indians ace fashioned an ERA of 2.47 as he won 13 games, dropped 2, fanned 100 and gave up 61 walks in 138.1 innings of work.

He received 10 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Sebastian Tidwell of the Kingston Indians finished second in voting, while Tom Teixeira of the Trois-Rivieres Royales finished third.
