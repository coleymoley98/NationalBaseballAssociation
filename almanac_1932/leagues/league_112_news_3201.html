July's ABL Top Pitcher is Whalers Bordieu
Monday, August 1st, 1932
Andrew Bordieu hasn't been around long enough to know that youngsters shouldn't be winning Atlantic Baseball League Pitcher of the Month. But when you put up numbers like his, you're going to get some notice.

In July the Hartford starter earned a 4-0 mark in 6 starts. He threw 51.1 innings, struck out 54 and compiled a solid 0.88 ERA.

For the year Bordieu has compiled a 10-0 record with a 1.75 ERA.
