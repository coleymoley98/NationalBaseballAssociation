NBA News: First-Year Player Draft
Thursday, November 5th, 1931
The National Baseball Association moguls will be maneuvering, conniving and cajoling today to get the pick of the litter in this year's amateur draft. They will be drooling and salivating over the new pups available in the rookie player draft. Toronto, Philadelphia, Hamilton, Minnesota and Cincinnati have plenty to bark and yelp about, since they will have the first five picks. In the opinion of most baseball experts, these are the most highly prized players in the 1931 draft:


1. Yoann Christiani, 18 year-old SS out of college.
2. Steve Lowenstein, 18 year-old RHP out of college.
3. Woranet Cooper, 18 year-old LF out of college.
4. Kevin Dumas, 18 year-old 2B out of college.
5. Earnest Ettrick, 18 year-old 1B out of college.
6. Vincent Beaulieu, 18 year-old 2B out of college.
7. Jenson Medcalf, 18 year-old 3B out of college.
8. Krisya Bardin, 18 year-old 2B out of college.
9. Rattanapon Dostie, 18 year-old CF out of college.
10. Noppadol Houdini, 18 year-old RHP out of college.
