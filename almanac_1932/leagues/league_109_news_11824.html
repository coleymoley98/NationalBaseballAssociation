Redcoats Say Adios to Manager Fini
Saturday, October 4th, 1930
"I'm just a scapegoat," said Phi Fini to reporters in the stadium parking lot after the Providence Redcoats announced his firing as manager early today. "You think I did a bad job? It's the players who couldn't hit, couldn't pitch, couldn't catch the damn ball. And you can print that," he added, as he stamped out a cigarette and stomped off.

Fini's replacement wasn't immediately named, but a Providence official said an announcement could come as soon as tomorrow.
