5-Hit Barrage by Balderas
Thursday, May 23rd, 1929
Dennis Balderas told the press after his 5-hit day, "Every batter wants to have a quality at-bat every time he steps up to the plate. He wants to hit the ball hard."

Well, that's just what Balderas of the Quebec Nordiques did against the Juneau Brasseurs. He went to the plate 6 times and 5 times he delivered base hits, but his club still suffered a 10-9 defeat.

"Not much I can say," offered Balderas in the postgame press conference. "I got a bunch of hits, but it would have been much better to get a win. Can I trade 5 hits for the win?"

Dennis Balderas singled in the 1st, hit a solo-shot off Keith Saibara in the 3rd, singled in the 4th, hit an RBI double in the 6th, flied out in the 7th and hit a solo-shot off Stephen Côté in the 9th.

This year Balderas has hit at a .371 clip with 9 RBIs, 2 home runs and 15 runs scored.
