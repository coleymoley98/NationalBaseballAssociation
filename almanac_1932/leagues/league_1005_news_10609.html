The Team Oregon stopped the Team Pennsylvania by a score of 9-8 at Blue Sox Ballpark today, with the lion's share of the credit going to second baseman Raymond Wilhelm. The 21-year-old had a career day, collecting 5 hits.

"Every batter wants to have a quality at-bat every time he steps up to the plate," Wilhelm said later. "He wants to hit the ball hard."
