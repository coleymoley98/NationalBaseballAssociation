Chicagou Narrowly Wins Canadian League
Monday, September 12th, 1927
Winning a division is always special, but winning it in the final days of the season is especially so, according to members of the Chicagou White Sox.

The Canadian League club has secured top spot late in the season and third baseman Rodney McNeil said it's certainly a relief.

"We would have liked to have won it sooner, but as long as you get it done, that's all that matters," he said.

The club has a 66-46 and its their 4th division crown. The White Sox will be looking for their 2nd National Baseball Association championship.
