Kitti Lei Wins NL Batting Title
Tuesday, September 14th, 1926
To hit .338 in National Baseball Association is an extraordinary feat.

It becomes even more outstanding when you consider it was done by a 22-year-old.

Kitti Lei of the Montreal Royales is your National League batting champion.

On the year, Lei collected 135 hits, 14 doubles, 7 triples and 6 home runs for the year. In 110 games Lei totaled 53 RBIs and scored 65 runs.
