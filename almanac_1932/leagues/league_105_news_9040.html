Serre Wins CL Prize, Named Best Hitter in August
Thursday, September 1st, 1927
When Emmanuel Serre stepped up to the plate in August he did it with confidence, and today he was honored with the Canadian League Batter of the Month award.

The young Baltimore center fielder posted a .448 batting average with 5 home runs, 19 RBIs and scored 23 runs. Serre also worked pitchers for 7 walks to post a .500 on-base percentage.

This year Serre has hit at a .335 pace and produced 42 RBIs, 10 home runs and scored 65 runs.
