Eskimos Whitewash Thunder, Edwards Winner
Wednesday, August 1st, 1928
A favor to his bullpen. That's what Dirk Edwards was doing at Thunder Stadium today as he blanked the Winnipeg Thunder in a 9-inning complete game shutout.

"I went out there to try to eat up innings and help out our bullpen," the Edmonton Eskimos left-hander said later. "I felt I had my good stuff, and it turned out my command was solid."

Edwards notched 4 strikeouts, surrendered 4 walks, allowed 4 hits, and kept the bullpen phone quiet all day long.

On the season Edwards has compiled a 5-5 mark with a 4.47 ERA in 13 starts. In pitching 110.2 innings he has 51 strikeouts and 62 walks while limiting the opposition to a .281 batting average.
