McGowan Shuts Out Sherbrooke, 6-0
Saturday, August 1st, 1925
Jacob McGowan of the Pittsburgh Liberty was masterful today, pitching a 5-hit shutout, 6-0, against the Sherbrooke Nordiques.

By going the distance, he was at the same time able to give his bullpen a rest, a fact appreciated by Pittsburgh manager Virgil McLain.

"It's nice to have someone who can do that from time to time," he said. "He had command of every ball and every pitch. Great location, too."

McGowan had 5 strikeouts and 1 walk in the outing.

In 13 starts this season McGowan has recorded a 8-2 record and a 3.17 ERA.
