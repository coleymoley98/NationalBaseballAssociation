Dupont Takes WL #1 Pitcher Trophy
Tuesday, September 1st, 1931
There's something about a young rookie winning the Western League Pitcher of the Month trophy that sends shock waves through the league. Such is the case with Isaac Dupont, a 24-year-old starter for the Portland Thorns.

In August, he topped off the month with a 6-0 mark and 1.33 ERA in 6 starts, tossed 54 innings, fanned 16 and held opposing teams to a .255 average.

In 17 starts this season Dupont has crafted a 15-2 record with a 1.87 ERA.
