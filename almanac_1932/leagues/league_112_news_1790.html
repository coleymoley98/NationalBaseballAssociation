Boudreau's Strong Week Earns ABL Trophy
Monday, July 20th, 1931
Lance Boudreau burst onto the Atlantic Baseball League stage in a big way last week. This promising rookie first baseman for Drummondville garnered everyone's respect by putting up some remarkable numbers and picking up the Player of the Week trophy.

The 16-year-old haunted opposing pitchers hitting .520 (13-for-25) with 4 home runs and 4 RBIs last week.
