Ottawa Punchless in 8-0 Loss to Rebels
Monday, July 14th, 1924
Louis Mongeau was throwing strikes early and getting ahead in the count, his manager said after the New York Rebels hurler tossed a 6-hit, complete game shutout against the Ottawa Indians.

"I went right after each hitter, trying to get ahead in the count," said Mongeau. "I wanted them to swing the bats and keep my pitch count down. It worked. I felt strong the whole game, even in the last two innings."

Mongeau walked 2 and struck out 2 in the 8-0 victory.

This season Mongeau has crafted a 9-3 mark with a 2.83 ERA in 114.2 innings. Opposing clubs are batting .227 against him and he has 41 strikeouts.
