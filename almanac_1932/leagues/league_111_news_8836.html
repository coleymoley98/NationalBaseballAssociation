Gallant Sweeps into the Adams Cup
Saturday, July 30th, 1927
With a 4-0 victory at Juniors Ballpark, the Gallant Black Sox have completed a sweep of the Fort Wayne Pioneers in the Division Finals.

"On to the Adams Cup and feeling good," said Bruno Leblanc. "I don't think our guys expected a sweep, but we'll certainly take it and rest up a bit."

For the series, Leblanc hit .500 and won series MVP honors. He had 0 home runs, drove in 0 RBI and scored 1 runs.

The Gallant Black Sox will play against the North Bay Battalion in the next round. North Bay won their series against the Buffalo Rapids 3 games to 0. The Adams Cup schedule will be announced after the Division Finals finishes.
