Goulet Shuts Out Saskatoon
Wednesday, August 8th, 1928
François Goulet hurled a fine game for the Edmonton Eskimos at Coyotes Ballpark today, holding the Saskatoon Coyotes to 6 hits in a shutout win.

"When he started out, his command wasn't that good, but he's a tough guy," Edmonton manager Nattaporn Lamontagne said. "The more pitches he throws, the better his command is."

The right-hander had 6 strikeouts and 4 walks in the 5-0 victory.

"An outstanding job," Saskatoon manager Maurice Wigglesworth said. "We couldn't get anything going. Every time we got a hit, he got out of the jam. He pitched a terrific game."

For this year Goulet has chalked up 10 wins and 2 losses in 11 starts with 54 strikeouts in 99.1 innings and a 3.35 ERA.
