Acadiens Win Tight Saint Lawrence Race
Sunday, July 22nd, 1928
Grown men turned into little boys, some even breaking into tears, in the Pointe-Sainte-Anne Acadiens clubhouse, after the team clinched the Saint Lawrence.

"We can relax a bit, for now anyway," said manager Rudy Seguro. "I'm proud of every guy in the room for what we've done this season."

The club has a 31-17 record as they head towards the playoffs in search of their 3rd Laurier Cup championship. Starting Pitcher Cédric Bruneau said the team is happy to clinch what is the club's 2nd division title, but "wouldn't be happy until we're lifting that trophy."
