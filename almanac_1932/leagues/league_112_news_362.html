Big Day with Bat: Concepcion 5-for-5
Tuesday, June 3rd, 1930
Sean Concepcion put on a hitting clinic today as the Pointe-Sainte-Anne Acadiens dumped the Champlain Coureurs 18-14 at Field of the Acadiens.

The Pointe-Sainte-Anne first baseman made Champlain defenders earn their pay, lashing Champlain pitching for 5 hits in 5 at-bats in the game. He told BNN reporters after the game that his strategy was "just hit it where they pitched it."

"Too often I try too hard to pull the ball," Concepcion said in his postgame press conference. "Today, I just went with the pitch."

Sean Concepcion doubled in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 2nd, singled in the 3rd, hit a solo-shot off Ethan Boudreau in the 4th and singled in the 6th.
