Laksamee 5-for-5 Against Blue Sox
Sunday, June 20th, 1926
It was high-fives all around for Anisong Laksamee at Harrisburg Grounds today.

He got them from his teammates and he got them at the plate during an outstanding hitting performance against the Hamilton Blue Sox. Laksamee smashed 5 hits in 5 at-bats on his monster day, leading his club to a 12-4 win.

"Anisong was hitting everything," said Robins manager Cedric Chéron. "It really was a special game for him."

In the opposing dugout, Blue Sox manager Newin Prariwanta told BNN beat writers that Laksamee "ambushed" the club.

"Obviously we didn't have much success against him, but we'll have another chance," he said. "Hopefully, Laksamee oversleeps next time."

Anisong Laksamee singled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, doubled in the 7th and hit a three-run home run off Albie Edmunds in the 7th.
