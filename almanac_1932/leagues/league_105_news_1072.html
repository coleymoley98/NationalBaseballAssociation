Philadelphia Liberty Ship Hoy to Baltimore
Saturday, February 14th, 1931
It likely won't turn either team into an instant contender or provide a huge franchise shakeup, but the Philadelphia Liberty and Baltimore Robins finalized a trade today. Philadelphia will send 27-year-old minor league RF Brian Hoy to the Robins, while the Liberty will receive 23-year-old minor league RHP Don McNair and 20-year-old minor league LHP Olivier Tardif.

"We think this is a deal that will help out both teams and we're glad to get it done," Liberty general manager Bob Roux said.

The trade is expected to be officially completed later today with the players joining their new ballclubs by the end of the day.


Looking at his all-time stats, Hoy has a career .300 average with 96 hits, 13 doubles, 2 triples, 5 home runs and 31 RBIs to date. He has scored 46 runs, walked 25 times and stolen 0 bases.
