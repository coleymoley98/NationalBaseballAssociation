Eastern Baseball League Names League's Best
Sunday, August 19th, 1923
Gautam Mijangos took Eastern Baseball League by storm this year, and he did it all at the age of 18.

For his spectacular campaign, the North Bay Battalion star has been named the 1923 Eastern Baseball League EBL Player of the Year recipient.

"To be considered amongst the best in the league is overwhelming," said the center fielder.

On the season he compiled a .333 batting average to go with a .411 on-base percentage. The native of Maltais, The United Provinces also added 7 doubles, 3 triples, 13 home runs, 41 RBIs and 33 runs scored.

He received 10 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Arlo Cunningham of the Springfield Falcons finished second in voting, while Apichai Gendron of the Toronto Jr. Blue Sox finished third.
