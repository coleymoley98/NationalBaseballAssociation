Prescher Connects for 3 Homers for Lions
Wednesday, July 6th, 1927
Detroit first baseman Zeshon Prescher blasted three homers today at Great American Ball Park in the 7-1 Lions victory over the Cincinnati Reds.

For the game, Zeshon Prescher scored 5 times and knocked in 4 runs. When he hit his last home run, his teammates, hearing the crack of the bat, leaped off the bench and rushed on the field to greet the jubilant Prescher when he reached home plate.

"It was just one game, but it sure was a lot of fun -- 3-for-3, three roundtrippers and a win," a gleeful Prescher told BNN reporters in a postgame interview. "What a day."

Zeshon Prescher hit a two-run home run off Zachary Beaulieu in the 1st, hit a solo-shot off Zachary Beaulieu in the 3rd, walked in the 5th, walked in the 7th and hit a solo-shot off Ignace Patry in the 9th.

This year Prescher has collected a .353 batting average to date with 18 hits in 51 at-bats, 13 RBIs, 4 home runs and scored 13 times. He has played in 14 games.
