6-Hit Blitz by Barrera
Saturday, July 28th, 1923
Ron Barrera had an answer for everything the Vancouver Giants threw at him today at Oracle Park. The Calgary left fielder went 6-for-6 in the game to put his name in the record book and put the Wranglers on top, 15-7.

Summing up his batting philosophy to BNN writers after the game, Barrera said, "I just see the ball and I hit it."

Ron Barrera hit an RBI single in the 1st, singled in the 4th, singled in the 5th, hit a three-run home run off Zach Guglielmo in the 6th, singled in the 8th and doubled in the 9th.

Thus far this season Barrera has hit .275 with 4 home runs, 23 RBIs and scored 36 times.
