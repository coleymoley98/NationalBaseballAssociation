Kunmee 5-for-5 for Canton
Friday, July 20th, 1923
It was a parade of hits for Kittipat Kunmee of the Canton Legends at Juniors Ballpark today.

Connecting almost every time he went to the plate, Kunmee finished the day with a total of 5 hits in 5 at-bats. His strong individual effort propelled the Legends to a 16-4 victory.

"Kunmee was really good and we were really bad," groaned Pioneers manager Ethan Bax. "Next time he's that hot, we'll just walk him."

Kittipat Kunmee singled in the 2nd, hit an RBI single in the 3rd, hit a solo-shot off William Reid in the 4th, singled in the 5th and hit a solo-shot off James Pittman in the 7th.

Kunmee is swinging at a .214 pace on the year with 2 home runs, 5 RBIs and 10 runs scored. He has played in 23 games.
