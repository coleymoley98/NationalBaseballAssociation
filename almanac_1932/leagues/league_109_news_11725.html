Bullpen Ace Wins Best EL Pitcher
Monday, September 1st, 1930
Any time a rookie makes a splash on the scene, it's got to be great for his career potential. Just ask Winford Bethea, who put together a stellar month out of the pen. The Harrisburg Robins reliever, 35, kept the opposition off balance all month as he held them to a .210 average and walked away with the Eastern League Pitcher of the Month award for August.

Bethea posted a 5-1 mark and a 3.22 ERA in 16 relief appearances. In 44.2 innings, he had 5 saves, 61 strikeouts, and 17 walks.

This year Bethea has registered 7 saves in 26 relief appearances. He has also compiled a 5-1 won-lost mark with a 2.42 ERA. In 67 innings he has fanned 84.
