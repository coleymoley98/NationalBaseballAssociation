Raj Lunn Gets 100th Victory
Tuesday, September 3rd, 1929
Royales ace Raj Lunn reeled in his 100th career victory by beating the Quebec Nordiques 6-5 at Montreal Grounds. In his historic win, he notched 3 strikeouts in 9 innings, yielded 5 runs and 8 hits, while surrendering no walks.

"I tried to focus on each batter," said Lunn after the game, "but as the game goes by, I couldn't help but think about the possibility. 100 is a big number. It's an exclusive club and I'm honored to be a member."

During his career Lunn has a 100-83 won-lost record with a 3.33 ERA.
