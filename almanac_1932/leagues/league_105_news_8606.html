Boston Trades Ito for Toronto's Millette
Saturday, July 2nd, 1927
Boston and Toronto have agreed to a trade today. 27-year-old 3B Harukazu Ito will go to the Blue Sox. In exchange the Redcoats will receive 27-year-old RHP Larry Millette and 19-year-old minor league RHP Bryant Allman.


In 1927 Ito has hit at a .326 clip with 14 hits, 2 home runs, 13 RBIs and has scored 7 times.
