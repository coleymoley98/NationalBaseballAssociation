Will Von Moore be elected to the National Baseball Association Hall of Fame?
Wednesday, December 30th, 1931
The 1932 National Baseball Association Hall of Fame class could be headed up by former first round draft pick Von Moore.

Moore is expected to be the leading vote-getter with the best opportunity to get in. His 13-year career has drawn the attention of not only Toronto Blue Sox fans, but also baseball observers and those who cast votes. He retired in 1925 at the age of 34.

Voters are reminded that ballots may be cast up until the votes are announced on January 6th, 1932.

Moore has a .329 career batting average and has hit a total of 14 home runs.
