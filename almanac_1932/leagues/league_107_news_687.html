O'Neill Swings for the Cycle
Saturday, July 12th, 1930
Burt O'Neill saw a spectacular performance go to waste in his club's 7-6 loss to the Kelowna Apple Sox at Ballpark of Kelowna today.

The Vancouver Giants center fielder had 3 RBI and went 4-for-6, including hitting for the cycle, but his club went down to defeat anyway.

"Everything they threw him, he was swatting around the field," said the Vancouver skipper Danupong Chan-inkaew. "It really was a special performance for Burt. It's a shame we couldn't win the game, too."

Burt O'Neill flied out in the 1st, tripled in the 4th, flied out in the 6th, hit a two-run home run off Matteo Dupont in the 9th, hit an RBI single in the 10th and doubled in the 12th.

So far this year O'Neill has 2 home runs and a .329 batting average.
