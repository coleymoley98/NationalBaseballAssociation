Morillon Wins Award for CL Dominance
Wednesday, October 12th, 1927
Weerasak Morillon said he thinks hitters often underestimate him, as he's only 22 years old.

But he'll certainly be getting a lot more respect now, as the Chicagou White Sox rising star has won the Canadian League Reliever of the Year Award for 1927.

He captured the award on the strength of his 2.89 ERA over 112 innings. He also had 6 saves in 31 relief appearances, 33 strikeouts and only 32 walks.

He received 16 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Niall O'Reilly of the Wolfeburg Celtics finished second in voting, while Andrew Patterson of the Boston Redcoats finished third.
