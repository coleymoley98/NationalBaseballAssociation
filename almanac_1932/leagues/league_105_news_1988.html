Hit Streak Over, Saints Stop Blanchard
Sunday, August 9th, 1931
It just wasn't Amran Blanchard's day.

The St. Louis Cardinaux took the 5-3 win today over the Minnesota Saints, but the Saints mound staff did what nobody else could -- they stopped the 25-game hitting streak of Cardinaux first baseman Amran Blanchard.

"We might have focused a bit too much on stopping Blanchard," Minnesota manager Datsakorn Bergeron commented afterward. "He had a nice hitting streak going, but the Cardinaux have other hitters and they did their job today."

To date, Blanchard has collected 51 hits for a .429 average. He has posted 5 home runs, scored 18 runs and driven in 29 runs.
