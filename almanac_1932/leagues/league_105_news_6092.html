Setback For White Sox and Stanislaus Gorostiza
Monday, June 1st, 1925
"It's worse than we thought." That was the news from the White Sox clubhouse today as it was learned that second baseman Stanislaus Gorostiza will be out for an added 5 days as a result of a recent injury. His treatment for a strained lat will have to be extended. Initially it didn't seem the injury was a major concern, but the Chicagou second baseman told team trainers of some discomfort yesterday morning, and upon examination it was determined to be a more serious injury. "We'd rather take this thing slowly and make sure Stanislaus gets back onto the field healthy," said the team's head trainer.

In 12 games this season Gorostiza has batted at a .130 pace with 6 hits and 1 home run.
