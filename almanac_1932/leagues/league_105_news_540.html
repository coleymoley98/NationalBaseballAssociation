Celtics End Gunning's Streak
Saturday, June 28th, 1930
The 24-game hitting streak of New York Rebels left fielder Russ Gunning came to an end today at League Park. The Wolfeburg Celtics held him hitless in 4 at-bats and also sent the Rebels down to defeat, 5-1.

Later, Gunning acknowledged to BNN reporters that the hit streak meant something to him.

"It was special," he said, "but I think other people make a bigger deal out of it than I do, or the players do. It was what we're all supposed to do; go out and hit the ball and try to score runs. It was a cool deal, but it's over now. Let's try to start a new one."

So far this season Gunning is hitting .418 with 4 home runs, 32 RBIs and 48 runs scored. He has 71 hits in 170 at-bats and a .482 OBP.
