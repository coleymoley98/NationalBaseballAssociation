Quebec Wins Lottery for RP Montano
Monday, December 3rd, 1928
Word of a huge deal between Lashawn Montano and Quebec broke on BNN only minutes before it was officially announced.

The reliever will join the Nordiques for an estimated $6,500 per year over 1 year. Montano has shown he is capable of putting together big money campaigns, which is exactly what Nordiques ownership was looking for.

Montano has 86 wins and 64 losses with 95 saves in his career. His ERA stands at 3.09, and he has 308 strikeouts in 807 innings.
