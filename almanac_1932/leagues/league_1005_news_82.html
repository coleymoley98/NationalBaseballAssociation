Lim 5-for-5 Against Manitoba
Thursday, March 6th, 1930
Team shortstop Simon Lim could do no wrong in today's 10-4 win over the Manitoba as he collected 5 hits.

"Things were in his favor in every one of his at-bats," Manitoba skipper Jake Brisset said to the press corps after the game. "He got hitters' counts."

Lim agreed, saying that getting ahead early in the counts was "the key" to his game.

"Once you get ahead, it makes it a lot easier because you get a better pitch to hit," he said. "You know the pitcher has to come in with it. And you're ready."

Simon Lim doubled in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, doubled in the 4th, hit a two-run single in the 5th and singled in the 7th.
