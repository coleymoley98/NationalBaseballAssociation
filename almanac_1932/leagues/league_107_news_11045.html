Hossain Superb Against Ranier
Sunday, July 7th, 1929
Victoria hurler Anamul Hossain put on quite a show today as he blitzed the Ranier Seahawks with a 5-0 shutout. He baffled them with fastballs and changeups, allowing 2 hits with 5 strikeouts and 1 walk to pick up the win.

"I think every time we were looking for a fastball, he threw a changeup and every time we were looking for a changeup, he threw a fastball," Ranier manager Suthep Aiton told reporters after the game.

This year Hossain has compiled a 3-3 mark with a 3.13 ERA.
