Star CF Babin Signs for 4 years
Friday, October 9th, 1931
Fans hailed the signing of Jordan Babin today as the Juneau Brasseurs center fielder came to terms with the club.

Set to make $56,000 over the upcoming 4 years, Babin signed primarily out of loyalty to the organization, thrilling the most diehard supporters who think there isn't much loyalty in a market driven by free agency.

Thus far this season in 107 games Babin is batting .306 with 8 home runs, 55 RBIs and 53 runs scored. He has 117 hits in 382 at-bats.
