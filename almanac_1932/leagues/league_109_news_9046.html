Mathews Gets EL Batter of the Month Trophy
Thursday, September 1st, 1927
It's perhaps an award he'll win many more times through his career, as at age 20 Josh Mathews has a lot of baseball ahead of him.

But for now the young Sherbrooke Nordiques star can enjoy the numbers he put up in August, which have garnered him the nod as the Eastern League Batter of the Month.

His breakout month featured a .393 average with 7 home runs, 36 RBIs and 27 runs scored.

This year Mathews is hitting at a .354 pace with 10 home runs and 52 RBIs. He has played in 51 games and scored 41 times.
