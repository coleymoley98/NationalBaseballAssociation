Saints Richardson Earns 150th Win
Monday, August 1st, 1927
History was made today at Field of the Saints -- Kitsada Richardson won game #150 to seal his name in the league record books. The Minnesota ace downed the Cincinnati Reds 9-2 to gain entrance into the elite 150 Club.

"I was wound up at first. I think I threw too hard," remarked Richardson, "I had to calm down and go out there and pitch... not just throw. I think I got better as the game wore on. I started to think less about 150 wins and more about doing what I had to do."

Richardson's won-lost record is 150 wins and 130 losses with a 2.76 ERA and opposing hitters have compiled a .253 average against him.
