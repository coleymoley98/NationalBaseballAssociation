Injury Brings Season to End for Hébert
Friday, July 11th, 1924
Obviously disappointed, Ranyawan Hébert confirmed today that his season is officially over. The Springfield closer offered mostly monosyllabic responses to reporters. "Nope," he said, when asked if he might possibly return this year. The 18-year-old Hébert suffered a partially torn ulnar collateral ligament on 07/08/1924 and has been out of action since.

This season Hébert has worked 8 innings in 5 relief appearances and picked up 1 save. He has dished out 10 strikeouts and walked 2 with a 1-1 mark and a 5.63 ERA.
