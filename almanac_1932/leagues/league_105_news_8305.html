Trottier Sizzles with 20-Game Batting Streak
Wednesday, May 18th, 1927
Wolfeburg left fielder Charles Trottier has extended his hitting streak to 20 straight games with a 4-for-5 day. However, the Ottawa Indians took the 12-4 victory over the Celtics at Lincoln Park.

"Disappointing," Trottier said of the outcome.
