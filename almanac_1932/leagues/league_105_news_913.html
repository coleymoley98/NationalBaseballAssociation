Braves Listless in 6-0 Loss to Brasseurs
Wednesday, August 6th, 1930
Magic was in the air and on the mound at Brasseurs Ballpark today as Arthur Jenkins held the Toronto Braves spellbound in his 6-0 shutout victory. The Juneau Brasseurs pitcher went the distance, rang up 5 strikeouts, walked 2 and held the Braves to 3 hits to garner the win.

"He just had us in his thrall," Toronto manager Peerapong Owens said later. "We couldn't put anything together against him."

So far this season, Jenkins has logged a 8-3 record with a 3.24 ERA. He has started 10 games, hurled 94.1 innings and registered 34 strikeouts and 27 walks.
