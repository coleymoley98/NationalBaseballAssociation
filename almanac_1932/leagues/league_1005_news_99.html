Big Day for Bostock: Swats 5 Hits
Tuesday, March 11th, 1930
It seemed that Team third baseman Bruno Bostock could do no wrong as the Dakota KO'd the Columbia, 15-5 today. Bostock rapped out 5 hits in 6 at-bats and seemed to know which pitches Columbia hurlers were throwing.

"I think the Dakota were stealing signs," growled one unhappy Team fan after the game.

"I just felt good today. I saw the ball well and just wanted to make good contact and put the ball in play," said the jubilant Bostock in the postgame press interview.

Bruno Bostock singled in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, flied out in the 5th, doubled in the 7th and doubled in the 8th.
