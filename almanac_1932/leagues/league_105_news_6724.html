Redcoats Win Canadian League Crown
Tuesday, September 1st, 1925
The Boston Redcoats are Canadian League champions, and they made it look easy.

The club's 65-35 record has allowed them to cruise into the postseason as division champions, where they will no doubt be considered front runners to win a 12th Riel Cup.

"It's always special to win the division," said first baseman Suphanan Duwell. "But we want to win the Riel Cup. That's all that matters."
