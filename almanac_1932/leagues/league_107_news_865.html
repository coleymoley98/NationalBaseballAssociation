WBL's #1 Batter Chosen
Friday, August 1st, 1930
When Mike Cordaro stepped up to the plate in July he did it with confidence, and today he was honored with the Western Baseball League Batter of the Month award.

The young Vancouver right fielder posted a .447 batting average with 7 home runs, 28 RBIs and scored 27 runs. Cordaro also worked pitchers for 7 walks to post a .480 on-base percentage.

Cordaro has a .406 average and a .438 on-base percentage for the year. He has picked up 80 hits, 8 home runs and driven in 42 runs.
