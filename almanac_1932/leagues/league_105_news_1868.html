Boston's Lovato Goes Ballistic with 5 Hits
Sunday, July 26th, 1931
Marc Lovato of the Redcoats was unstoppable in today's 25-4 victory over the Pittsburgh Quakers at Ballpark of Pittsburgh. He battered them for 5 hits in 6 at-bats.

"Boy, I was on the mark today," said the Boston right fielder after the game. "I could see the ball real well. It's a great feeling to do this. And besides, 5-for-6 won't hurt your batting average like 0-for-5 will."

Marc Lovato hit a two-run triple in the 1st, walked in the 2nd, singled in the 3rd, hit an RBI triple in the 5th, hit a three-run home run off Salton Sharpe in the 6th, struck out in the 7th and hit a two-run double in the 9th.

This season Lovato is batting at a .337 clip with 4 home runs and 35 RBIs.
