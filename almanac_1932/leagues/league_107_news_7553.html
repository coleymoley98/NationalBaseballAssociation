O'Dell Nabs WBL Weekly Award
Monday, July 19th, 1926
18-year-old Bobby O'Dell made a few Victoria headlines during this past week as the rookie left fielder proved he could compete at the plate with his high-school idols. He captured the attention of fans, journalists and Western Baseball League officials last week and today took home Player of the Week honors for his effort.

He cracked 14 hits in 23 at-bats to put up a .609 average. O'Dell collected no home runs, 2 RBIs and scored 5 runs.

This year O'Dell has hit at a .311 clip with 15 RBIs, 3 home runs and 22 runs scored.
