Mounties Ace, Pitcher of the Year Award Winner
Monday, August 18th, 1924
Logan Fortier of the Adams Mounties is in the spotlight today. He was acclaimed the best pitcher in the Central Baseball League and will be honored with the 1924 Pitcher of the Year Award.

He started 13 games and compiled a 8-2 record with an ERA of 1.78 in 91 innings with 108 strikeouts.

He received 8 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Jerome Whitaker of the Adams Mounties finished second in voting, while Mason Hough of the Minneapolis Jr. Saints finished third.
