White Sox Chill Sexauer's Hot Streak
Friday, June 15th, 1928
Seamus Sexauer, left fielder for the Boston Redcoats, gave it a good try, but his time chasing history came to an end when he went hitless in 3 tries against the Chicagou White Sox today. Not only did the White Sox break his 20-game hitting streak, they also beat Boston by the score of 9-3.

"Now that the streak has ended, I can admit I'll breathe a bit easier the next time I step up to bat," Sexauer said, speaking with the media after the game. "It was tough because I put a little pressure on myself. Now that it's over, I will definitely be a little bit looser at the plate."

Sexauer is hitting .382 in 1928 with 33 RBIs and 9 home runs.
