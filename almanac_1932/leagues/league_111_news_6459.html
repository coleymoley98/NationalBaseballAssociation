Division Remains Up for Grabs, but Fort Wayne in Postseason
Wednesday, July 22nd, 1925
The Fort Wayne Pioneers celebration was a bit downplayed today, as the team officially secured a playoff spot.

Starting Pitcher Colt Munro said his club expected to have got to the playoffs and thus, it's not as exciting as it will be when the team wins the West or advances to the Adams Cup.

The Pioneers record currently sits at 26-22 and they will be vying for the 8th Eastern Baseball League championship in franchise history.
