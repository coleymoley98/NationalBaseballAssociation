Wolfeburg Trades di Matteo for Baltimore's Massey
Tuesday, June 29th, 1926
Everybody is overjoyed with a trade consummated today by the Wolfeburg Celtics and the Baltimore Robins. In the deal Wolfeburg got 19-year-old minor league RHP Donte Green and 23-year-old minor league C Alex Massey from Baltimore and dealt 31-year-old LHP Franck di Matteo to the Robins. The general managers that made the swap are happy and the players are happy, too. One of the GMs commented, "This was a good swap for all concerned."


di Matteo has a 7-3 season record so far. In 12 starts, he has fanned 27 batters in 99.1 innings with a 3.53 ERA.
