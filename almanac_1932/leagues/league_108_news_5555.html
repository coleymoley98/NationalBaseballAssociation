WL Star of the Week Chosen
Monday, August 25th, 1924
23-year-old Choem Gross made a few Saskatoon headlines during this past week as the rookie center fielder proved he could compete at the plate with his high-school idols. He captured the attention of fans, journalists and Western League officials last week and today took home Player of the Week honors for his effort.

He cracked 14 hits in 29 at-bats to put up a .483 average. Gross collected 1 home run, 7 RBIs and scored 8 runs.

For the season Gross has hit .314 with 2 home runs and 22 RBIs. He has played in 46 games and scored 40 times.
