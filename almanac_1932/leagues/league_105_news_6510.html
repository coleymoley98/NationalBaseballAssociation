Ottawa-Wolfeburg Trade: Lemon for Beck
Tuesday, July 28th, 1925
Several Indians and Celtics players soon will have to start shopping for a new home. It was announced today that the two teams came to agreement on a trade. Ottawa will get 24-year-old minor league 1B Matthew Beck and Wolfeburg will pick up 31-year-old LF Yanrit Lemon in return. "I'm not surprised by the news," one of the players told reporters, "It's part of the business and I understand that. I am looking forward to playing for the Indians."


Lemon is currently batting .340 with 2 home runs, 8 RBIs and 8 runs scored. He has 18 hits in 53 at-bats.
