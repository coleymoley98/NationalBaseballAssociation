"He was kind of teasing them with his off-speed stuff, and his fastball was moving everywhere," Seattle skipper Sebastian Woodhall said afterward.

Dukes registered 3 strikeouts and issued 1 walk in pitching a gem.

"We just didn't swing the bats very well. Or else he just pitched good, whichever way you want to look at it," said Spokane manager Laurent Fontaine after the defeat.
