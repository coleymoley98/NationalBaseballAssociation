Boston Re-Signs McInnis to Extension
Saturday, May 21st, 1932
Team officials triumphantly announced that the Redcoats and superstar first baseman Phuwanart McInnis have closed a deal that keeps the 26-year-old in a Boston uniform for another 4 years.

McInnis will earn $86,400 as part of the settlement.

Presently McInnis is batting .212 with 1 home run and 4 RBIs. He has played in 9 games and scored 5 times.
