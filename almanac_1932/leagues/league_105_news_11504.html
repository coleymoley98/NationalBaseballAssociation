Wolfeburg Takes League Championship Series in Deciding Game
Thursday, September 19th, 1929
Wolfeburg Celtics won a nail-biting series against the Baltimore Robins 4-3, wrapping up the League Championship Series finale 6-3 at League Park.

Celtics center fielder Logan Maisonneuve was named the series MVP.

He batted .375, drove in 9 runs and scored 5 to lead Wolfeburg into the next round of play.

"Logan was real solid for us, real solid," said Celtics manager Surabordee Yodyingyong. "I normally say I'd like to have nine of him out there, but thankfully we got by with just one this time."

The Wolfeburg Celtics will play against the Montreal Royales in the next round. Montreal won their series against the Detroit Lions 4 games to 3. The Riel Cup schedule will be announced after the League Championship Series finishes..
