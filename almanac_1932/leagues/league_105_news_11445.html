CL Star of the Week Named
Monday, September 2nd, 1929
It can be difficult for a young player to step out of the shadows and assert himself in the Canadian League, but last week Jonathan Steward of the Baltimore Robins reminded everyone just why he's here as he threw up numbers that made him an easy selection for Player of the Week. Steward rocked opposing pitching for a .417 average (10-for-24), 2 home runs and 8 RBIs to come away with the award.

Steward is batting at a .371 pace this season, picking up 117 hits in 315 at-bats with 21 home runs, 66 RBIs and 91 runs scored.
