Coyotes Suffer 10-0 Drubbing from Falcons
Saturday, June 7th, 1924
Today was Kritsana Ta-ngam's turn in the rotation for the Omaha Falcons and he made the most of it, shutting out the Saskatoon Coyotes on 6 hits.

The 19-year-old right-hander led the Falcons to an easy 10-0 victory, allowing 2 walks and tallying 3 strikeouts.

"I hope we miss him next time," Saskatoon manager James Sangapong said. "Ta-ngam certainly pitched a strong game against us today. He was in a groove, throwing everything for strikes. Throwing hard. Changing speeds."
