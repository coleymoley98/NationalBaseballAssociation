Boulay Snares NL Top Batter Award in June
Sunday, July 1st, 1928
Minnesota Saints fans may just have witnessed a star player blossoming before their eyes in June.

Taradin Boulay put up a remarkable month at the age of 22 and has been named the National League Batter of the Month.

For the month, Boulay hit .446 with 2 home runs, 12 RBIs and 21 runs scored.

This season Boulay is hitting .411 with 79 hits, 5 home runs, 32 RBIs, scored 36 runs and played in 50 games.
