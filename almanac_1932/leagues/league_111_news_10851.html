Jr. Brasseurs Spank Legends, 9-0
Wednesday, June 12th, 1929
Nathan Lin led the Juneau Jr. Brasseurs to a 9-0 shutout victory over the Canton Legends at Juniors Ballpark today.

"My pitching coach told me before the game just to bear down and focus," Lin recalled later. "He said no one was going to hit me if I'm throwing a good breaking ball with good location."

Lin yielded 3 hits and had 13 strikeouts and 2 walks in the standout performance.

"I thought he put the ball where he wanted to," Canton manager Von Rivera said. "And that's just about the whole secret to pitching."
