Playoff Bound: Quebec City Secures Wild Card
Thursday, July 22nd, 1926
They have their eye on the Saint Lawrence crown, but for now the Quebec City Jr. Nordiques are happy to have guaranteed themselves a playoff berth.

"We'd like to go in as division champions, that's for sure," said left fielder Thaiwat Jinta.

The Jr. Nordiques currently have a 30-18 record and when playoffs begin will be vying for the 2nd Atlantic Baseball League championship in franchise history.
