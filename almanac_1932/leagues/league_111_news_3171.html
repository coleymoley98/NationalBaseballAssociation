Battalion Dominate, Sweep Division Finals
Friday, July 29th, 1932
It was a sweet sweep for the North Bay Battalion as they eased past the Springfield Falcons to advance to the Adams Cup.

North Bay won the final game by a 18-6 count and the series 3-0.

"We're just fortunate to get through it quickly and be able to rest up for the next series," said René Laflamme, who was named series MVP.

For the series, Laflamme hit .600 with a .636 on-base percentage. He had 2 home runs, drove in 8 RBI and scored 4 runs.

The North Bay Battalion don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Buffalo Rapids - Morgantown Mountaineers series. Buffalo leads the series 2 games to 1. The Adams Cup schedule will be announced after the Division Finals finishes.
