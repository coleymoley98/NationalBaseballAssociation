CL Selects Chollet July's Best Pitcher
Saturday, August 1st, 1931
Nicholas Chollet took a much-deserved turn in the spotlight earlier today as he accepted the Canadian League Pitcher of the Month award for July.

The 26-year-old Blue Sox starter is a relative newcomer to the league, but nonetheless racked up an impressive 4-0 won-lost record in 6 starts. This month he fanned 18 batters in 53 innings and compiled a 1.53 ERA.

For the year Chollet has a 10-4 record with a 2.76 ERA in 17 starts.
