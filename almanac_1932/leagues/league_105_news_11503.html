Royales Prevail, as League Championship Series Goes to the Wire
Wednesday, September 18th, 1929
In an exciting League Championship Series that had its fair share of drama and intrigue, perhaps it is National Baseball Association fans who have come out the winner.

But on the field, the winners were the Montreal Royales, who edged the Detroit Lions 4-3 to advance to the Riel Cup.

Montreal won the final game by a 4-1 count to wrap up the series. Kitti Lei earned series MVP honors, by hitting .345 with a .345 on-base percentage. He had 0 home runs, drove in 4 RBI and scored 5 runs.

The Montreal Royales don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Baltimore Robins - Wolfeburg Celtics series. That series is tied at 3 games apiece. The Riel Cup schedule will be announced after the League Championship Series finishes.
