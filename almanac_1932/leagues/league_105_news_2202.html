Hunt Shuts Out Cardinaux, 7-0
Monday, September 14th, 1931
Verne Hunt shut 'em down and shut 'em out, hurling the Montreal Royales past the St. Louis Cardinaux 7-0 in a complete game victory.

"This is a game of adjustments and I made some good ones in this game," he told BNN sportswriters in the postgame interview.

Hunt held the St. Louis Cardinaux without a run, tossing a 2-hitter while walking 3 and fanning 2.

In 8 starts this season Hunt has registered a 4-6 record and a 4.03 ERA.
