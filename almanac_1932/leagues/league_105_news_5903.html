Ottawa-Chicagou Trade: Bédard for Bonds
Thursday, February 12th, 1925
BNN has learned the Ottawa Indians have reportedly worked out a deal with the Chicagou White Sox. Sources close to the Indians indicated that a trade has been reached in principle where 19-year-old minor league 3B Dez Bonds, 22-year-old minor league LHP Jon Bachtiar and 21-year-old minor league C Genzo Fujiyama will be swapped to Ottawa with 27-year-old LHP Zackary Bédard heading to Chicagou. Neither organization would comment on the report, saying it was club policy not to speak about a trade until it was finalized.


Bédard has posted 32 victories and 32 defeats in his career with a 4.33 ERA.
