Blue Sox Harukazu Ito Wins EL Award
Monday, July 20th, 1931
At the age of 31, Harukazu Ito might have some potential he hasn't realized yet -- if his success at the plate last week is any indication. The Brampton third baseman put up some exceptional numbers the past seven days with a .440 batting average. For his efforts Ito collected the Eastern League Player of the Week Award.

He racked up 11 hits in 25 at-bats, 3 home runs and 6 RBIs last week in 7 games.

Ito is swinging at a .297 pace on the year with 5 home runs, 15 RBIs and 26 runs scored. He has played in 45 games.
