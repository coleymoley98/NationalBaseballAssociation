Rebels Blank Chicagou, Mongeau Winner
Tuesday, July 10th, 1923
In an excellent mound performance, Louis Mongeau of the New York Rebels whitewashed the Chicagou White Sox 4-0 on a 2-hitter.

"I had my best stuff and did a good job of mixing up my pitches," he said after the game.

In all, the New York hurler rang up 3 strikeouts and 2 walks in the shutout win.

"We just didn't swing the bats well today," said White Sox manager Phi Fini. "Mongeau did a good job keeping us guessing."

In 14 starts this season Mongeau has registered a 10-4 record and a 2.92 ERA.
