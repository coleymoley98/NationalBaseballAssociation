Bruins Come up Short of Playoffs
Friday, July 22nd, 1932
When it comes to a late-season playoff disappointment, the old axiom "wait until next year" isn't overly comforting.

"We really thought we would get into the playoffs this year," said Providence Bruins left fielder Nat Falardeau. "It's really disappointing to come up short."

Before this season, the Bruins last missed the playoffs in 1929. In the 38 years they have been a Atlantic Baseball League franchise, they have missed the playoffs 33 times. In the last 10 years, they have missed the playoffs 5 times. Providence last won the Laurier Cup in 1930.
