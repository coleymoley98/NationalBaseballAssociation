CL's Best Rookie Honored
Tuesday, October 9th, 1923
What a season it was for Ottawa's Ambler Stahl in this new star's first year in the Canadian League. He put together a year that earned him the 1923 Rookie of the Year Award.

The 20-year-old first baseman crunched some good numbers with a .349 batting average and a .405 on-base percentage to go with his 10 home runs, 64 RBIs and 63 runs scored.

He received 16 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Trent Norrell of the New York Rebels finished second in voting, while Nantapol Etling of the Ottawa Indians finished third.
