Montreal Wins Top Spot in National League East
Wednesday, August 31st, 1932
A 1932 National League East champions banner will soon be adorning the home field of the Montreal Royales.

The club has easily captured the 14th division crown in franchise history today and will now be looking to win their 10th Riel Cup.

Starting Pitcher Larry Millette said these are the types of memories you take with you long after you retire from professional baseball.

"It's been a memorable season so far and we hope we can keep it going and make some even better memories," he said.
