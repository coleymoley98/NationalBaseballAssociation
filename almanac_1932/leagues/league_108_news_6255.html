League Punishes Pair for Fighting
Sunday, June 28th, 1925
Yesterday at Edmonton Grounds Francisco Macia and Thomas Guérin got into each other's space and then into each other's face. They began to grapple and fell to the ground. That's when it boiled over into a full-fledged bench-clearing tussle between the Edmonton Eskimos and Calgary Wranglers. Reason finally prevailed as the umpires restored order. After much discussion, only the two major combatants were ejected from the game.

Today the league office notified the ballclubs that Macia will have to sit out 3 while Guérin would be given a 6-game suspension for their roles in the brawl.

This season Macia is hitting .262 with 11 hits, no home runs, 3 RBIs, scored 6 runs and played in 14 games.

In 6 starts this season Guérin has fashioned a 2-3 record and a 7.07 ERA.
