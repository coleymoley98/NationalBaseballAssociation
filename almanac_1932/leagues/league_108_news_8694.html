5-Hit Game for McFarland
Tuesday, July 12th, 1927
Troy McFarland got the glory and the headlines today, but the Portland Thorns left with the win. The Vancouver right fielder easily handled the Thorns pitching, going 5-for-5, but his Vancouver Giants came out on the short end of the 12-10 score.

McFarland told BNN reporters in the postgame interview he would happily trade "three or four" of his hits for a win.

"Heck, maybe even all of them," he said with a laugh. "Right now, it just seems like one loss in a long season but who knows what it could mean at the end."

Troy McFarland doubled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 4th, singled in the 6th, hit an RBI single in the 7th and walked in the 9th.

So far this year McFarland is batting .444 with 6 home runs.
