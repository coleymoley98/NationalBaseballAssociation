Minnesota-Juneau Trade: Frantz for Voisin
Friday, March 7th, 1924
After prolonged negotiations Minnesota agreed to ship 27-year-old SS Matt Frantz to Juneau for 31-year-old LHP Nathan Voisin and 21-year-old minor league 3B Runrot Savitree. The deal is awaiting confirmation from the league office but neither team expects any hang-ups.

"It took longer than expected," said one GM, "but we knew that the deal was going to happen."


Since breaking into the league, Voisin has compiled a 3-3 mark, has pitched in 18 games and has been credited with no saves and a 2.37 ERA.
