Semple Speaks Out About Clubhouse
Wednesday, September 7th, 1932
Hakim Semple said things aren't so peachy in the Minnesota Saints clubhouse these days.

The outspoken player told reporters today that there are a lot of issues and conflict between members of the team.

"There's fighting, there's arguing, a lot of personality clashes, things aren't so pretty right now," he said. "I don't know what we can do to turn things around, but it would be nice if someone did something. It's hard to concentrate on baseball like this."
