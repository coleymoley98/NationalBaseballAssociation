Wilder Hits in 33 Consecutive Games
Saturday, July 3rd, 1926
With 3 hits at Ballpark of Hamilton today, Harry Wilder added another game to his hitting streak -- the Albany center fielder has now hit safely for 33 games in a row. But the hitting feat wasn't enough for the Rebels today, as they fell, 8-3, to the Hamilton Blue Sox.

"Frankly, I don't care about hits," Hamilton manager Newin Prariwanta said later. "I'll let him get 4 hits in 4 at-bats if it means we get a win."
Harry Wilder struck out in the 1st, singled in the 4th, singled in the 6th and hit a solo-shot off Vihaan Nuregesan in the 8th.

Wilder is batting .380 this season in 30 games with 10 home runs, 23 RBIs and 34 runs scored. He has 46 hits in 121 at-bats.
