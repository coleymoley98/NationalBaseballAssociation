EBL Names EBL Pitcher of the Year Winner
Thursday, August 21st, 1924
What does it take to win the EBL Pitcher of the Year?

Just ask Vihaan Nuregesan of the North Bay Battalion -- the latest Eastern Baseball League EBL Pitcher of the Year winner.

"My dad always told me to dream big and dream always," he said. "It's not like I shoot for the EBL Pitcher of the Year," he said. "I have big aspirations for what I want to do in this game and what I want to achieve in a long career."

The Battalion pitching ace made some of his dreams come true this year, winning 9 games and losing only 3. In 17 starts, Nuregesan registered a 1.89 ERA and limited other clubs to a .204 batting average. He fanned 122 in 119 innings and issued 31 walks.

He received 7 first place votes out of a possible 10. Chanchai Bourgault of the Morgantown Mountaineers finished second in voting with 2 first place votes, while Tanat Boonyai of the Buffalo Rapids finished third with 1 first place vote.
