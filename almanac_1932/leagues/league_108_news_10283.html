Top WL Reliever Honored
Friday, September 21st, 1928
At the age of 19, Ewen Vidal says he still has a lot to learn about life in Western League.

But he certainly seems to be a quick study, as the Winnipeg Thunder reliever was better than all others this year and has captured the 1928 Western League Reliever of the Year Award.

"I hope to win this a few more times before all is said and done in my career," the McMinnville, The United Provinces native said.

For the season, Vidal had no saves in 17 relief appearances, which totaled 67.2. He fanned 47 betters, while walking 49 and put up a record of 2-3 to go with his 9.18 ERA.

He received 8 first place votes, as a unanimous winner.
