Gagnon on Fire: 7-for-8 for Team
Wednesday, March 9th, 1932
A jubilant Etienne Gagnon joined the postgame press conference at Villanueva Fields today to offer some unusual words of encouragement -- for the Team Saskatchewan pitching staff he had just vanquished.

"It may not have looked like it," said the Team Pallister center fielder, "but I saw some awful good pitches today. If luck hadn't been on my side it might have turned out a lot different."

That would not have been the conclusion of anyone who saw his dominant 7-for-8 hitting performance, which led the Pallister to a 29-10 win.

"He made it look easy," said Morris McGhee, manager of the Saskatchewan. "We never figured out what to do with him. Obviously."
