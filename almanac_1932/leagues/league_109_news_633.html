Robins Chill Becker's Hot Streak
Saturday, July 5th, 1930
Reilly Becker, second baseman for the Providence Redcoats, gave it a good try, but his time chasing history came to an end when he went hitless in 4 tries against the Harrisburg Robins today. Not only did the Robins break his 22-game hitting streak, they also beat Providence by the score of 6-0.

"Now that the streak has ended, I can admit I'll breathe a bit easier the next time I step up to bat," Becker said, speaking with the media after the game. "It was tough because I put a little pressure on myself. Now that it's over, I will definitely be a little bit looser at the plate."
