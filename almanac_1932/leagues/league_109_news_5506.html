Godin Hits for the Cycle
Wednesday, August 13th, 1924
Pittsburgh's René Godin accomplished what few others have done -- he hit for the cycle, collecting a single, double, triple and home run in the same game.

For the day, Godin finished 5-for-5 with 3 RBI and 6 runs scored and helped the Liberty knock off the Harrisburg Robins 18-1 at Liberty Ballpark.

"I didn't even know I was about to hit for the cycle until after," Godin told reporters following the game. "One of the guys mentioned it when I got back to the dugout."

René Godin singled in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 2nd, tripled in the 4th, hit a two-run home run off Alexis Chiasson in the 5th, doubled in the 7th and walked in the 8th.

This season Godin is swinging the bat at a .299 pace. He has hit 9 home runs and scored 30 times, while driving in 26. His on-base percentage is .418 and he has played in 37 games.
