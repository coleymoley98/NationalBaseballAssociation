Big Day for Rufolo: Swats 5 Hits
Saturday, March 7th, 1925
Even as a kid, Edge Rufolo said his coaches told him the key to hitting was making contact, and letting everything else flow from there. At Yokohama Memorial Grounds today, Rufolo showed how well he learned that lesson.

The 18-year-old center fielder had 5 hits in 6 at-bats for the Team Pennsylvania, helping his team knock off the Team Ontario by a score of 19-14.

"They always said, just keep your eye on the ball, don't overswing, make contact and you never know what will happen," he said. "That's all I did today."

Edge Rufolo singled in the 1st, hit a two-run double in the 2nd, hit a two-run single in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, walked in a run in the 6th, grounded out in the 7th and doubled in the 9th.

Thus far in 1925 Rufolo is hitting .421 with no home runs, 6 RBIs, 8 runs scored and has compiled a .538 OBP.
