Hit Parade: Peña Gets Cycle
Monday, July 2nd, 1928
Saskatoon Coyotes left fielder Ivan Peña had one of the best days of his career today when he hit for the cycle and paved the way for the Coyotes 24-11 win over the Winnipeg Thunder at Coyotes Ballpark.

"Everything was just clicking today," he said.

Peña shelled the Thunder for 4 hits in 5 at-bats, collected 4 runs batted in and scored 4 runs.

Ivan Peña singled in the 1st, tripled in the 3rd, hit a three-run double in the 4th, flied out in the 5th, hit a solo-shot off Timo Cobarrubias in the 6th and walked in the 8th.

Peña is batting .356 with 5 home runs and 27 RBIs this season.
