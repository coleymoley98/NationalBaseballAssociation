Super Swinger -- Fontaine Slams 5 Hits
Monday, June 23rd, 1930
Nathan Fontaine had an answer for everything the Baltimore Robins threw at him today at Sportsmans Park 1911. The Pittsburgh left fielder went 5-for-5 in the game to put his name in the record book and put the Quakers on top, 10-9.

Summing up his batting philosophy to BNN writers after the game, Fontaine said, "I just see the ball and I hit it."

Nathan Fontaine singled in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, singled in the 5th, hit an RBI single in the 6th and doubled in the 7th.

Fontaine has collected 48 hits for the Quakers this year, 1 of them home runs. He has also netted 17 RBIs and scored 37 runs while batting .287.
