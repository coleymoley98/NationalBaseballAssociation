Juneau Trades Saibara for New York's Wood
Tuesday, July 15th, 1930
Signed ... sealed ... and delivered.

Juneau has worked out terms of a deal to send 28-year-old RHP Keith Saibara to New York for 20-year-old minor league 1B William Wood, 21-year-old minor league LHP Rocky Goodine, 24-year-old minor league RHP J.D. Moliere and 24-year-old minor league RF Curtis Sumpter. Both teams expect to introduce their new players at press conferences later in the week.


Thus far this season, Saibara has worked 47.1 innings with 20 strikeouts en route to a 1-4 record and a 5.13 ERA.
