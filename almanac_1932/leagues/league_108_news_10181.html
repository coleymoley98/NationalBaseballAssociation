Garnier Has 25-Game Hitting Streak
Sunday, August 26th, 1928
The Edmonton Eskimos stopped the Portland Thorns today 9-4 and Jordane Garnier collected a base hit yet again. The Edmonton third baseman finished with 2 hits to run his consecutive-game hitting streak to 25.

"I want to get a hit every time up," a visibly excited Garnier told reporters after the contest. "I want to get a hit every game I play. Right now, I feel like I could take this streak to 100 games."
Jordane Garnier hit an RBI single in the 1st, grounded out in the 3rd, singled in the 4th, hit a sac fly in the 5th and grounded out in the 8th.

For the year Garnier is batting .360 with 107 hits and 11 home runs. He has scored 63 times and driven in 74.
