Liberty Celebrate Wild Card Win
Monday, August 30th, 1926
While you set out to win a division each year, members of the Pittsburgh Liberty seemed plenty satisfied with their place as Eastern League wild card winners.

"The crown would have been great, but we're still in a better spot than all the teams that are going home after the regular season is over," said starting pitcher Jacob McGowan. "You can't complain."

There was certainly no complaining going on as the Liberty clubhouse was filed with loud music and even louder celebrations following the postseason clinch.
