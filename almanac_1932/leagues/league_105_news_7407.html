Canadian League Announces #1 Starter
Thursday, July 1st, 1926
As Justin Moragne hoisted up the Canadian League Pitcher of the Month trophy for June, rumors have already begun to swirl about whether he will leave Wolfeburg once his contract is up at the end of the season.

Moragne put pressure on the Celtics to keep him around by posting a 4-1 record and an ERA of 3.20 over 56.1 innings last month. He also rolled up 21 strikeouts, increasing his value both to Wolfeburg and to every other team in the league.

This year Moragne has compiled a 5-3 record with a 4.24 ERA.
