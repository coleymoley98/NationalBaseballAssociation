Provost Mows Down Quebec, 1-0
Thursday, July 9th, 1925
The St. Louis Cardinaux did little to support starter Elliot Provost against the Quebec Nordiques, but it turned out he didn't need the help.

The right-hander spun a complete game shutout against the Nordiques, striking out 3 and surrendering 6 hits. The performance from Provost allowed St. Louis to escape with the 1-0 win despite an anemic offense.

"Nobody came to me and said 'Elliot, we're gonna need you to win this one for us today,' but I could feel it," Provost told BNN reporters following the win. "I'm the guy that will step up for you when you need it."

This year in 9 starts, Provost has recorded 5 wins and absorbed 2 losses. He has pitched 82.1 innings, allowed 66 hits, struck out 25 and has a 1.64 ERA.
