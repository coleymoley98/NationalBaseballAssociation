WBL's Star Player Named
Monday, July 22nd, 1929
17-year-old Burt O'Neill made a few Vancouver headlines during this past week as the rookie center fielder proved he could compete at the plate with his high-school idols. He captured the attention of fans, journalists and Western Baseball League officials last week and today took home Player of the Week honors for his effort.

He cracked 13 hits in 29 at-bats to put up a .448 average. O'Neill collected no home runs, 2 RBIs and scored 6 runs.

So far this season O'Neill is batting .314 with no home runs, 22 RBIs and 28 runs scored. He has 59 hits in 188 at-bats, 14 walks and a .365 OBP. O'Neill has played in 45 games.
