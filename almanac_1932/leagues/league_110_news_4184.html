Mantaux Picked Best Player
Monday, July 23rd, 1923
Central Baseball League pitchers tried and tried all week long to stop Denis Mantaux, but to no avail.

His eyes would light up when he came to the plate and he hit everything in sight, batting .563 for the week to win the CBL Player of the Week Award. The Winnipeg center fielder cracked 9 hits in 16 at-bats, 3 home runs, drove in 7 runs and scored 6 times.

To date Mantaux has chalked up a .317 average with 10 home runs, driven in 29 runs and scored 33 times.
