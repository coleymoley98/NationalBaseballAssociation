Ratté Garners CL #1 Pitcher Award
Wednesday, July 1st, 1925
Sommai Ratté's been around the league long enough to appreciate the honor he received today. After going 5-1 in June, the 30-year-old Boston hurler has been named Canadian League Pitcher of the Month.

He fanned 5 in 52.2 innings, logged an ERA of 2.73 and allowed opponents to hit .258 against him.

This season-to-date, Ratté has fashioned a 9-1 mark and 2.44 ERA in 85 innings. Opposing clubs are batting .269 against him and he has logged 10 strikeouts.
