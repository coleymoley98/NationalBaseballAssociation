Red-Hot Pitcher Chills Out on Award
Sunday, July 1st, 1923
Steely Hennessy might not have fully digested the impact of winning June's National League Rookie of the Month award when he got the news earlier today, but the 23-year-old quickly learned that he was no longer your typical rookie.

Posting a mark of 3-1 won-lost record will do that. In 6 starts he tossed 54.2 innings with 22 strikeouts and a 2.14 ERA. Opposing teams hit .234 against him during the month.

Not bad for a first-year man.

This season Hennessy has compiled a 4-4 mark with a 3.21 ERA in 10 starts.
