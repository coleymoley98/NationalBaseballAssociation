Toronto Stops Vignal's Hit Streak
Saturday, July 12th, 1930
The Toronto Jr. Blue Sox clamped down on Ethan Vignal and put a stop to his hit streak. He went without a hit, ending the run at 21 straight games, but it didn't keep the Rapids from winning. Buffalo downed the Jr. Blue Sox 4-2.

"It was bound to happen," Vignal said with a shrug after the game.
