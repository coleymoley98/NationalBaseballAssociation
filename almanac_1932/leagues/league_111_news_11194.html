Jr. Steelers Win Tight East Race
Thursday, July 25th, 1929
Prognosticators had a difficult time handicapping the East this year. And thus, it should come as no surprise that the division title hung in the balance late into the season.

In the end, the Hamilton Jr. Steelers have reigned supreme and captured the franchise's 2nd division crown.

"Hopefully there are more celebrations to come," said starting pitcher Nicolas Pineault. "We could get used to this."

The club currently has a 30-20 record and will be seeking the 1st championship in franchise history.

"I believe in this group and I think our best is yet to come," said manager Fabrice Hardy.
