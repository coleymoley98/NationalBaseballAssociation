Coyotes Celebrate Wild Card Win
Wednesday, August 29th, 1928
The entertaining brand of baseball played by the Saskatoon Coyotes this year has culminated in a late-season wild card berth.

The team battled down to the wire and now with a 45-35 record, they will be heading to the playoffs.

"I think we have that big-game experience now after winning this thing late in the year. That's only going to benefit us in the playoffs," said starting pitcher Alberto Badeau.
