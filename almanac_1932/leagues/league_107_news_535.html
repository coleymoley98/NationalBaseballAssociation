No Comeback This Season for Hossain
Wednesday, June 25th, 1930
The news was not unexpected, but that didn't make it easier to accept for the Victoria Admirals: starting pitcher Anamul Hossain is out for the year. Hossain suffered a ruptured ulnar collateral ligament on 06/23/1930. The team knew that the chances of seeing him return were slim to none. But today it was made official.

Hossain has made 5 starts so far this year and put up a 1-1 won-lost record with a 1.54 ERA. In 23.1 innings he has allowed 13 hits, walked 2 and struck out 18.
