Joly Untouchable, Tosses No-Hitter
Tuesday, August 11th, 1931
Is it better to be lucky or good?

After leading the St. Louis Cardinaux to victory, 13-0, and tossing a no-hitter over the Minnesota Saints, Renato Joly didn't have the answer.

"I don't know," Joly said. "To do what I did today, I'm pretty sure it takes both."

On his big day at Field of the Saints, Joly fanned 4 and had 3 walks. And it seemed that when the Saints managed to hit the ball hard, it went right at a defender.

"That's how you get a no-hitter," Joly said with a smile. "A lot of skill mixed with a little luck."

To date this season Joly is 7-6 with 19 starts and an ERA of 4.04 with 81 strikeouts in 133.2 innings.
