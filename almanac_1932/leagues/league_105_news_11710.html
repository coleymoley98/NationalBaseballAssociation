Norrell Sets Hit Record
Saturday, August 30th, 1930
At Lincoln Park today Trent Norrell put his name atop of the single-season hit list with hit number 174. The New York Rebels star replaced Carmen Morales as the all-time leader. Teasing with the press after the game Norrell said, "Hey, guys. I'm nothing special as a hitter. I only have two arms, two legs and 174 hits."

Trent Norrell flied out in the 1st, grounded out in the 2nd, struck out in the 4th, grounded out in the 6th and singled in the 8th.

Norrell has collected 174 hits for the Rebels this year, 21 of them home runs. He has also netted 123 RBIs and scored 90 runs while batting .414.
