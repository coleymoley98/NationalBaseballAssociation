Playoff Bound: Pointe-Sainte-Anne Secures Wild Card
Wednesday, July 20th, 1932
Celebrating a playoff clinch never gets old, according to Aiden Nadeau of the Pointe-Sainte-Anne Acadiens.

"I could do this each and every year," the starting pitcher said as he celebrated his team officially having clinched a playoff spot. "It's why we play the game - to get to the playoffs and have a chance to become Laurier Cup champions."

The Saint Lawrence club has a record of 30-16 and will be seeking the franchise's 4th Atlantic Baseball League championship.
