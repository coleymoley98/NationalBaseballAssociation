Quakers End Cheng's Streak
Thursday, May 29th, 1930
Toronto Blue Sox right fielder Dustin Cheng and his hot bat finally met their match today at Ballpark of Pittsburgh.

The Pittsburgh Quakers snapped his 24-game hitting string, holding Cheng hitless in 4 at-bats and also whipping Toronto for good measure.

"We're real pleased with this one," Quakers manager Pierre Boutin said. "Nobody wants to be on the wrong end of a streak like that, and clobbering Toronto makes it even sweeter."

On the year Cheng has hit .455 with 6 home runs, 19 RBIs and scored 23 runs.
