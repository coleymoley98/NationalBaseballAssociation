Polk on Fire: 5-for-5 for Ottawa
Wednesday, May 6th, 1931
It wasn't just another day at the ballpark for Mozes Polk today. Polk went on a tear and cracked hit after hit off New York pitchers. When the dust settled on the diamond at Yankee Stadium 1923, he had a 5-for-5 line and a day he will long remember. And the Ottawa Indians had a 22-20 victory over the Rebels.

"Yeah, it'll be something nice to tell the grandkids, but it won't get me a hit tomorrow," he joked in the postgame interview.

Mozes Polk singled in the 1st, hit a three-run home run off Zachary Beaulieu in the 2nd, hit an RBI single in the 3rd, hit a three-run home run off Keith Saibara in the 5th, hit a solo-shot off Ernest Robertson in the 7th, walked in the 8th and walked in the 9th.
