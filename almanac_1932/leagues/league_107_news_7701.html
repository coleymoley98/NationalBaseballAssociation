Tessier Holds Vancouver Hitless
Monday, August 2nd, 1926
Jonny Tessier was untouchable today at Broncos Ballpark, displaying a good fastball and wicked breaking balls in his 3-0 win against the Vancouver Giants. The Boise hurler did not allow a single hit, fanning 13 while walking 1.

"This is great," Tessier told reporters after the game. "Every pitcher wants a no-hitter. I just wanted the win, but the no-hitter is a little extra icing on the victory cake."

This year Tessier has compiled a 6-4 record with a 2.07 ERA in 13 starts.
