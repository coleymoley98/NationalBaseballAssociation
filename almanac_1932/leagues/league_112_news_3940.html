Red-Hot Rampage Reaps ABL Reward
Monday, June 25th, 1923
16-year-old Leroy Smyth made a few Pawtucket headlines during this past week as the rookie left fielder proved he could compete at the plate with his high-school idols. He captured the attention of fans, journalists and Atlantic Baseball League officials last week and today took home Player of the Week honors for his effort.

He cracked 12 hits in 21 at-bats to put up a .571 average. Smyth collected 4 home runs, 11 RBIs and scored 8 runs.

This year Smyth is batting .422 with 10 home runs.
