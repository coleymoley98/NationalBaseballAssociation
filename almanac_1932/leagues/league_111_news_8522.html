Strasburg Tosses 6-0 Shutout Against Canton
Wednesday, June 22nd, 1927
In a very sharp performance at Juniors Ballpark, Jimmy Strasburg of the Morgantown Mountaineers blanked the Canton Legends, 6-0. He permitted 8 hits and finished with 10 strikeouts and 1 walk in the Mountaineers victory.
