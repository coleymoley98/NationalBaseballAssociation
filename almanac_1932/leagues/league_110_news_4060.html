Laksamee Collects Player of the Week Award
Monday, July 9th, 1923
The hottest bat of the week belonged to St. Louis third baseman Anisong Laksamee.

He went on a rampage last week against CBL hurlers and rapped out 19 hits in 30 at-bats for a .633 average while hitting 1 home run, driving in 7 runs and scoring 6 runs -- numbers quite worthy of the Central Baseball League Player of the Week trophy.

Laksamee was honored today at a press conference.

This season Laksamee is hitting at a .397 pace with 4 home runs, 28 RBIs and has scored 22 times.
