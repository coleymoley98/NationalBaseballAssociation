Lookjan Sends Toronto Bats to Deep Freeze
Wednesday, July 22nd, 1931
Chairat Lookjan pitched a strong game at Ballpark of Quebec today, blanking the Toronto Braves 4-0. Lookjan allowed no runs on 2 hits, totaled 7 strikeouts and gave up no walks.

Quebec manager Donatien Auger said he could tell immediately it was going to be a good game for Lookjan.

"Right from the start, you could see how economical he was," Auger said. "He threw a lot of strikes."

Thus far this season, Lookjan has worked 65.2 innings with 27 strikeouts en route to a 3-4 record and a 3.43 ERA.
