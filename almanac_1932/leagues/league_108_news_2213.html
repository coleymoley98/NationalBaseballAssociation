Thorns Star Receives Pitcher of the Year Award
Thursday, September 17th, 1931
Isaac Dupont had a marvelous 1931 season. The Portland mound mainstay garnered the most votes and has been named the WL Pitcher of the Year Award honoree.

The Thorns star dominated his opposition, finishing with 15 wins, 2 losses and a 1.87 ERA. In 17 starts he worked a total of 149 innings, striking out 51, while walking 39. Other clubs managed to hit only .248 against him.

He received 10 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Greg Eston of the Saskatoon Coyotes finished second in voting, while Ashley Matcham of the Victoria Admirals finished third.
