New York Trades Zavala for Toronto's Jones
Tuesday, June 17th, 1930
Several Rebels and Braves players soon will have to start shopping for a new home. It was announced today that the two teams came to agreement on a trade. New York will get 26-year-old SS Ted Jones and Toronto will pick up 31-year-old minor league CF Esteban de la Rosa, 21-year-old minor league RHP Ajax Pitts and 23-year-old minor league 1B Virgil Zavala in return. "I'm not surprised by the news," one of the players told reporters, "It's part of the business and I understand that. I am looking forward to playing for the Rebels."


Jones on the season is batting .308 with no home runs. His totals include 3 RBIs and 11 runs scored. He has played in 24 games.
