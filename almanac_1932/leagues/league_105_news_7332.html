Irish Day-to-Day with Injury
Monday, June 21st, 1926
The topic du jour around Toronto is whether Ethan Irish of the Blue Sox will suit up for today's contest. He was injured yesterday against the Robins while colliding with a player at a base. The standout 24-year-old second baseman has a strained oblique and didn't rule out playing, but did say that if pain persisted, he would not risk further injury.

"Ethan will play if he can," said the team doctor. "He hasn't got a major injury, but at the same time, we need to monitor the situation and protect him as much as possible."

This season Irish is hitting .382 with 1 home run.
