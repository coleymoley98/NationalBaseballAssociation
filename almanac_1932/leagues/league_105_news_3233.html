St. Louis's Amran Blanchard Suffers Setback
Friday, August 5th, 1932
Yesterday St. Louis first baseman Amran Blanchard sustained a setback in his recovery from a strained oblique. The Cardinaux medical staff told him it would be at least 5 days before he will be able to return to action. Blanchard told reporters, "The healing process has gone extremely well. This is just a little hiccup and a little setback." Apparently Blanchard worked a little too hard in a recent workout and caused more damage. His doctors have advised him to do only light exercise and give the injury time to heal.

Thus far this year Blanchard is hitting .329 with 3 home runs, 30 RBIs and 30 runs scored.
