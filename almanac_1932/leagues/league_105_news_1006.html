National League East Won: Montreal Crowned
Saturday, August 23rd, 1930
Those who doubted them are eating crow and those who believed in them are reminding everyone of their faith. The Montreal Royales have captured the crown as National League East champions.

"It's nice to wrap this up a little bit early so we don't have to sweat it out down the stretch," said starting pitcher Larry Millette. "A lot of people didn't know how we would make out this year, so it's nice to show people what our team is all about."

The club currently has a 71-29 record which is good for their 12th division title.

The team will be vying for its 8th Riel Cup.
