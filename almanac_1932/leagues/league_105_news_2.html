Star RP Gagné Settles on Chicagou
Monday, January 6th, 1930
Surprising some observers, the Chicagou Frontenacs announced the signing of 35-year-old Mathieu Gagné today. The contract runs for 2 years and is valued at $11,200.

Gagné was described as an "elder statesman" by club officials and former teammates, who apparently expect him to contribute as much in the clubhouse as he does on the field.

During his career Gagné has 56 saves with a 39-37 record and a 4.35 ERA.
