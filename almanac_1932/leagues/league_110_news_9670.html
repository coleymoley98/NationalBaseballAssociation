Devin Gallegos Wins CBL Trophy
Monday, June 18th, 1928
His hard work and hustle paid off this week. The hard-working and hard-playing Devin Gallegos was rewarded with the Central Baseball League Player of the Week honors.

The scrappy Winnipeg Jr. Thunder right fielder hit .389 while collecting 7 hits in 18 at-bats, 4 home runs and 5 RBIs.

For the season Gallegos has hit .389 with 6 home runs and 15 RBIs. He has played in 16 games and scored 17 times.
