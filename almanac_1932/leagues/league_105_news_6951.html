Hall of Fame Selection announcement coming next week
Wednesday, December 30th, 1925
The annual Hall of Fame voting results will soon be revealed, and it's not yet known what shape the 1926 class will take. Baseball News Network predicts that the leading vote-getter is likely to be 1910 Pitcher of the Year Award winner Dermot Pilkington, but it's not believed he will have enough votes for enshrinement.

He played for 12 years in National Baseball Association, retiring in 1919 at the age of 36.

Voters are reminded that ballots may be cast up until the votes are announced on January 6th, 1926.

His career totals show Pilkington has 117 victories and 94 defeats with a 2.91 ERA.
