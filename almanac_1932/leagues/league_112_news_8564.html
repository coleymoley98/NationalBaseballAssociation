Monday, June 27th, 1927
Donovan Cho kept the Hartford Whalers hitters from doing any damage at Hartford Grounds today.

"I don't know if I was on or if they were off today," Cho told reporters after the shutout win. "But whatever it was, I hope it happens again next time."
