Injury Brings Season to End for Saneevong
Sunday, August 23rd, 1925
"Not what I wanted to hear," said Cincinnati's Quan Saneevong to reporters this afternoon, as he revealed that the injury he sustained on 08/20/1925 would force him to miss the rest of the season. "The doctor made it clear," Saneevong said. "There's no chance for me -- none at all -- to come back this year. That's the last thing I wanted to hear." Saneevong has been out of action since he suffered a torn flexor tendon in his elbow.

On his stats sheet for the year Saneevong shows a 9-9 record, 21 starts, 163.1 innings, 49 strikeouts and a 3.25 ERA.
