Castleberry Shuts Out Detroit, 5-0
Saturday, June 23rd, 1928
Jeremy Castleberry bent a little, but he didn't break against the Detroit Lions. The Quebec Nordiques pitcher hurled a 7-hit 5-0 shutout in a fine performance, surrendering 1 walk and tallying 5 strikeouts.

"Jeremy is good at getting out of jams," Nordiques manager Donatien Auger told reporters after the game. "He's almost more effective when he gets guys on base or gets behind in the count, because he uses your aggressiveness against you."

This year Castleberry has posted a record of 4 wins and 4 losses with a 4.29 ERA. He has appeared in 10 games and struck out 35 in 84 innings.
