Injury Brings Season to End for Wichitchan
Monday, June 21st, 1926
Clearly dejected, Theeratorn Wichitchan left the Victoria clubhouse nearly in tears this afternoon. He acknowledged that his season is over after sustaining a torn labrum in a ballgame recently. It brings an early end to the Victoria reliever's season.

Wichitchan has nailed down no saves for the year. He has also totaled 5 strikeouts and 4 walks in 9.2 innings while registering a 6.52 ERA and a 0-2 won-lost record.
