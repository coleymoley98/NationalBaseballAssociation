Bender Slams 5 Hits for Champlain
Monday, June 21st, 1926
No matter what the Sherbrooke Canadiens threw at him today, Drew Bender hit it. He easily handled Canadiens pitching, going 5 for 5 and helping the Coureurs to a 18-3 triumph at Field of the Juniors.

"The way I was swinging today, I think I could have closed my eyes and hit the ball," he told BNN reporters in the postgame interview.

Told of Bender's quip, Canadiens skipper C.J. Chambers said he "would take him up on that next time."

Drew Bender hit an RBI double in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, doubled in the 6th, singled in the 7th and singled in the 8th.

So far this season Bender is batting .351 with no home runs.
