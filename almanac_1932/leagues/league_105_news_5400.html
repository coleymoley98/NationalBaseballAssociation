More Woes For Reds D.J. Ruth
Wednesday, July 30th, 1924
Cincinnati received another blow today when doctors revealed that Reds reliever D.J. Ruth had suffered a setback in his recovery and will miss additional time because of a herniated disc. Ruth has been nearing his projected return date, but now that will be pushed back 6 days.

This year Ruth has pitched 42.2 innings over 22 relief appearances, picking up no saves and held opponents to a .358 average. Ruth has a 0-0 record with a 6.33 ERA and 21 strikeouts.
