García Extends Hit Streak to 20 Games
Thursday, August 11th, 1932
The streak is alive and well -- at least for today.

Aleixo García did it again, bashing 3 hits in the contest at Ballpark of Victoria today. For the last 20 games in a row, the 28-year-old left fielder has collected at least one hit. His efforts helped the Seattle Rainiers beat the Victoria Admirals 7-0 today.

"I'm happy I kept the streak going," García told a standing-room-only crowd of reporters gathered in the Seattle clubhouse. "Honestly though, I'm looking forward to going home and not thinking about this for the rest of the day."
