Wednesday, June 15th, 1927
Jeremy Castleberry dominated the St. Louis Cardinaux with a brilliant 2-hit shutout in a Quebec Nordiques 3-0 triumph at Ballpark of Quebec. The Quebec hurler fanned 2 and walked 1 in the game and was singled out for praise afterward by the opposing manager.
