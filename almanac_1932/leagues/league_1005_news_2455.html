Lapierre Keeps Hit Streak Alive
Thursday, March 17th, 1932
The Team New York fell to the Team Pennsylvania in Villanueva Fields today, even as 21-year-old third baseman José Lapierre upped his consecutive-game hitting streak to 1 games. Lapierre finished with 3 hits in 6 at-bats, but could not stop the New York from dropping the 13-11 decision.

"Yeah, I kept my streak going, but it would have been better to come away with a win," said Lapierre.

This year Lapierre is hitting .526 with 3 home runs and 11 RBIs in 8 games played. He also boasts an OBP of .591 with 5 walks and 16 runs scored.
