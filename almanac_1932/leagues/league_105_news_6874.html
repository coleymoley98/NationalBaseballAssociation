Veteran Richardson Signs with Saints
Saturday, November 7th, 1925
Happy times in Minnesota as the Saints and star starting pitcher Kitsada Richardson put the finishing touches on a $123,400 contract extension that will keep the 30-year-old in town 6 years longer.

The signing is great news for Minnesota because players of this caliber are hard to come by.

Thus far in his career, Richardson has worked 2375.2 innings with 1070 strikeouts en route to a 131-112 record. The 30-year-old also has a 2.74 ERA.
