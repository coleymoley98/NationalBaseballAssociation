Boston Announces Deal for Veteran SS
Saturday, November 17th, 1928
Team insiders were tight-lipped prior to today's deal that brought 31-year-old shortstop Carmen Morales to Boston.

Morales is considered one of the league's A-list players and certainly will enhance any chances the Redcoats have to win a playoff spot this year.

In his career Morales has hit .355 with 1613 hits, 277 doubles, 98 triples and 30 home runs. His totals also include 714 RBIs and 740 runs scored.
