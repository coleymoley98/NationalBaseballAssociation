Lions Show No Mercy in 11-0 Thrashing of Brasseurs
Thursday, June 11th, 1931
D.J. Pham had an impressive outing at Brasseurs Ballpark today, hurling the Detroit Lions to a 11-0 shutout victory over the Juneau Brasseurs.

"He threw strikes and he was always around the plate," Juneau skipper Joshua Barry said. "The ball was doing everything he wanted it to do. We hit some balls hard, but they were all right at the fielders."

On the day, the left-handed Pham yielded 5 hits and tallied 2 strikeouts with no walks.

To date this season Pham has compiled a 5-2 record with a 2.79 ERA in 7 starts.
