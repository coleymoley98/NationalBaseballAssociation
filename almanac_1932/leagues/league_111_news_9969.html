Black Sox Celebrate Wild Card Win
Thursday, July 26th, 1928
The Gallant Black Sox clubhouse was filled with loud music and revelry late into the night, as the team celebrated a Eastern Baseball League wild card position.

The team, whose record stands at 28-23, battled throughout the year, but ultimately saw the quest for the playoffs come down to the season's final games.

"I'd be lying if I said we knew we were going to make it," said catcher Dylan Bernini. "We did have confidence, but in the back of your mind you always wonder what's going to happen. There were some good teams pushing us."

Now the Black Sox don't have to worry, as they are in position to potentially win the 3rd Eastern Baseball League championship in franchise history.
