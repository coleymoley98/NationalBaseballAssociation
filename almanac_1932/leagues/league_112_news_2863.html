Michaud 5-for-5 for Acadiens
Friday, June 24th, 1932
Pointe-Sainte-Anne left fielder Anthony Michaud had a big day at Field of the Acadiens today against the St. John's Mariners. The 18-year-old, who is not typically considered a big threat with the bat, went 5-for-5 and led the Acadiens to a 19-3 triumph over St. John's.

"Hey, I'm as surprised as you guys are," Michaud said in his postgame comments, smiling as he scolded the press corps for underestimating him. "Maybe I'm not so bad at the plate after all."

Anthony Michaud hit a two-run home run off Chanin Branche in the 1st, hit an RBI double in the 2nd, hit a two-run double in the 3rd, singled in the 5th and hit a three-run home run off René Francoeur in the 6th.

This year Michaud has hit at a .318 pace and produced 19 RBIs, 8 home runs and scored 21 runs.
