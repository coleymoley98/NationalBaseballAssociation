Lyons Smacks 5 Hits for White Sox
Friday, July 25th, 1930
Dylan Lyons told the press after his 5-hit day, "Every batter wants to have a quality at-bat every time he steps up to the plate. He wants to hit the ball hard."

Well, that's just what Lyons of the Chicagou White Sox did against the Boston Redcoats. He went to the plate 5 times and 5 times he delivered base hits, but his club still suffered a 10-9 defeat.

"Not much I can say," offered Lyons in the postgame press conference. "I got a bunch of hits, but it would have been much better to get a win. Can I trade 5 hits for the win?"

Dylan Lyons singled in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, singled in the 7th, hit a two-run double in the 8th and singled in the 9th.

Lyons is hitting .375 this season with 1 home run, 6 RBIs and 4 runs scored. He has 6 hits in 16 at-bats.
