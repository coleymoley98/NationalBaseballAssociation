Monday, July 13th, 1931
Minneapolis Jr. Saints pitcher Elvis Guégan blanked the St. Louis Jr. Cardinaux 10-0 at Juniors Ballpark today, tossing a 2-hitter while issuing no walks and collecting 10 strikeouts.

After his sharp shutout performance, he shared credit with the rest of the club.
