Brasseurs End Silawarn's Streak
Sunday, May 27th, 1923
St. Louis left fielder Phakhawat Silawarn came into today's game against the Juneau Brasseurs on a hot streak. He left on a cold one.

The Brasseurs did their jobs well, beating the St. Louis Cardinaux 7-3 and holding Silawarn hitless to snap his 21-game hitting streak. The 21-year-old left fielder had a few words to say after finishing 0-for-4, telling reporters only that he "came up short."

"What Phakhawat did was exciting and quite an achievement," said Cardinaux manager Surabordee Yodyingyong. "It's tough enough to get one hit in this league, much less go 21 games in a row. I'm even more proud of the way he handled the press coverage. He never let the frenzy get to him or affect his play."

So far this year Silawarn has no home runs and a .362 batting average.
