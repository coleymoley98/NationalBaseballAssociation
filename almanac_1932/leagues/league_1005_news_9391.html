Ellingson Raps Team for 5 Hits
Sunday, March 18th, 1928
5 times is the charm -- at least for Panuwat Ellingson.

The Team Cordillera first baseman tore up the Team Dakota to the tune of 5 hits in 6 at-bats at Los Angeles Grounds today. The offensive onslaught from Ellingson powered the Cordillera to a 22-11 win.

"Ellingson was a tough out today," Team manager Allan Shaw said to the press in the postgame interview. "He hit some very good pitches with that quick bat of his. Not an easy out, that's for sure."

Panuwat Ellingson hit an RBI single in the 1st, flied out in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, hit a two-run single in the 6th, hit an RBI single in the 7th, walked in the 9th and singled in the 9th.

This season Ellingson is batting .333 with 15 hits, 1 home run, 11 RBIs, .388 OBP and has scored 8 runs.
