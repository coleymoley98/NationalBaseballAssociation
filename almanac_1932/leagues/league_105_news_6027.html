Chicagou Over Juneau 5-0, 100th Win for Boisvert
Saturday, May 16th, 1925
Chicagou's Caleb Boisvert took the hill at West Side Grounds today in search of his 100th career win. The 28-year-old ace found it against the Juneau Brasseurs.

After the historic victory, his manager told reporters, "I saw a tremendous sigh of relief when this thing was over. He was just looking to hug everyone in sight."

He worked 9 innings with 6 strikeouts and 2 walks in the 5-0 victory, checking Juneau on 4 hits.

"It a big milestone in my career," admitted Boisvert. "When you get close to it, you worry about it a lot. It's done now... and I can relax and enjoy it."

In all, Boisvert has compiled a 100-71 mark during his career and a 2.94 ERA.
