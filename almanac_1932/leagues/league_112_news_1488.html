Champlain Shuts Out New Britain, 4-0
Wednesday, June 17th, 1931
Roger Opperman of the Champlain Coureurs toyed with the New Britain Yankees today, blanking them 4-0 at Gauthier Grounds. He was almost untouchable, baffling them on a 5-hit shutout with 3 strikeouts and 1 walk.

"I'm sweating now because it's hot in here," Opperman said in the clubhouse after the game. "But it didn't even feel like I was sweating out there."
