Falcons No Match for Badeau, Coyotes in 15-0 Thumping
Tuesday, June 11th, 1929
The Omaha Falcons had no answer today for Saskatoon Coyotes pitcher Alberto Badeau. They failed to score a single run against him, losing 15-0. Omaha managed only 1 hit off Badeau, who had 7 strikeouts.

The extraordinarily popular Badeau got a standing ovation from the hometown crowd as he left the field, the applause continuing until he returned from the dugout to tip his cap to the crowd.

"Coyotes fans are the best fans, in my opinion," he said. "It's a great feeling to throw a shutout under any circumstances but to do it with the crowd behind you like that is just incredible."
