Record "W" for Richardson
Friday, March 6th, 1931
Kitsada Richardson of the Team Michigan notched another win, this one breaking the all-time record for pitchers. The previous owner of the record was Thomas Guérin. The win puts Richardson at 8 for his career.

"I've been fortunate," said Richardson. "I've played on a lot of good teams and with quality teammates. I could never have done it without them."
