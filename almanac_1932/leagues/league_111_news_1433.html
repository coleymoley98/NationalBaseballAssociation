Beaulieu Bangs Out 5 Base Hits
Thursday, June 11th, 1931
The Hamilton Jr. Steelers stopped the Buffalo Rapids by a score of 11-0 at Ballpark of Buffalo today, with the lion's share of the credit going to second baseman Vincent Beaulieu. The 18-year-old had a career day, collecting 5 hits.

"Every batter wants to have a quality at-bat every time he steps up to the plate," Beaulieu said later. "He wants to hit the ball hard."

Beaulieu did just that in his 5 at-bats, much to the chagrin of the Rapids.

"Not much that I can say," offered Buffalo manager Thawatchai Doors. "He just hammered the heck out of us."

Vincent Beaulieu singled in the 2nd, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, singled in the 6th and singled in the 8th.

Currently Beaulieu is batting .286 with 2 home runs, 6 RBIs and 5 runs scored.
