Playoff Bound: Wranglers Win Wild Card
Sunday, August 31st, 1924
The Calgary Wranglers certainly made things interesting.

The club have battled to the bitter end and have come up with a wild card berth.

"It's satisfying to clinch the playoffs so late in the season," said second baseman Calvin Boisvert. "You start to doubt yourself and wonder if you can even make it."

But the Wranglers have in fact made it, with their 44-38 record holding up to now give them a chance at the franchise's 1st Western League championship.
