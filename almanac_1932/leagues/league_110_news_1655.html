Hits Blitz for Jr. Cardinaux Thompson
Saturday, July 4th, 1931
Giordano Thompson had a day to remember at Jr. Coyotes Ballpark today, but the memory will be bittersweet.

Even though Thompson hit for the cycle, his St. Louis Jr. Cardinaux still fell to the Saskatoon Jr. Coyotes, 13-12. The 17-year-old right fielder went 4-for-6 with 6 RBI and 3 runs scored in the loss.

"It's a heartbreaker for Giordano," said St. Louis manager Jasper Widow. "He deserved to get a win after a day like that."

Giordano Thompson hit a solo-shot off Rémy Landry in the 1st, hit an RBI triple in the 3rd, flied out in the 5th, hit a three-run double in the 7th, hit an RBI single in the 9th and struck out in the 10th.

In this current campaign Thompson is hitting at a .293 clip with 3 home runs and 18 RBIs.
