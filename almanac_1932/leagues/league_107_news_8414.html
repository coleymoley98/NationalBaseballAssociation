Fortin Tags Silvertips for 5 Hits
Thursday, June 9th, 1927
The Abbotsford Alpines stopped the Everett Silvertips by a score of 10-1 at Alpines Ballpark today, with the lion's share of the credit going to shortstop Rodney Fortin. The 16-year-old had a career day, collecting 5 hits.

"Every batter wants to have a quality at-bat every time he steps up to the plate," Fortin said later. "He wants to hit the ball hard."

Fortin did just that in his 5 at-bats, much to the chagrin of the Silvertips.

"Not much that I can say," offered Everett manager Joseph Jones. "He just hammered the heck out of us."

Rodney Fortin singled in the 2nd, hit a two-run single in the 3rd, hit an RBI single in the 5th, doubled in the 7th and singled in the 8th.

In the current season, Fortin has put up a .419 batting average with 13 hits, no home runs, 9 RBI and scored 7 runs.
