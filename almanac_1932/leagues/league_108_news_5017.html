One Run Enough for Winnipeg
Wednesday, June 11th, 1924
It wasn't easy but the Winnipeg Thunder made it look that way.

Starter Joel Cottle shut out the Edmonton Eskimos despite having what he later called "sub-par" stuff, and the Winnipeg Thunder cruised to victory, 1-0. Cottle allowed only 4 hits and rang up 1 strikeout but insisted later that he did not deserve primary credit for the win.

"It didn't feel quite right today, but I got a lot of help to get through," he said. "I trusted my catcher and the guys behind me bailed me out more than once when I screwed up."
