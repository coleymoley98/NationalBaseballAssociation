Thorns Get One to Win
Friday, July 1st, 1932
Antonio Castañeda held the Edmonton Eskimos to 4 hits and fashioned a fine 1-0 shutout at Edmonton Grounds today.

Speaking after the game, Portland Thorns manager Charlie Fleury said "Castañeda had everything going for him today."

He changed speeds effectively, stayed down, had a lot of movement," he said. "He was masterful."

Castañeda rung up 2 strikeouts and surrendered 4 walks in the game.

"We couldn't get anything going. He certainly had our number," Edmonton manager Nattaporn Lamontagne told reporters.

Checking out his stats for the season, Castañeda has racked up 5 wins and no losses in 7 starts, 3.94 ERA and struck out 11 batters in 48 innings.
