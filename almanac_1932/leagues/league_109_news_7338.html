Recovery Slow For Royales Arlo Cunningham
Wednesday, June 23rd, 1926
All is not well with Arlo Cunningham in his recovery from a hand contusion.

The 20-year-old Royales shortstop has already reached some significant steps in his rehabilitation, but his manager said that the team will continue to be cautious with Cunningham and take his progression one day at a time, especially after the setback yesterday.

"Yesterday he was feeling good but he didn't do a lot on the field," he said. "Today he was supposed to be on the field doing light stuff and then gradually start working right back up. I think he overdid it."

A post-workout examination revealed that Cunningham had re-injured himself and would now miss at least 6 days because of it.

This year Cunningham is batting .243 with 17 hits, 2 home runs, 10 RBIs, 5 walks, .293 OBP and has scored 9 runs.
