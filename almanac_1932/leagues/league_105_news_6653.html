Burns Tosses 3-0 Shutout
Friday, August 21st, 1925
Lomana Burns of the Wolfeburg Celtics was lights-out in a 8-hit whitewash of the Ottawa Indians.

"I had my good stuff and it went where I wanted it to go," he told BNN reporters after the contest.

Burns registered 1 strikeout and 4 walks in shutting out Ottawa, 3-0.

This year Burns has fashioned a 8-3 mark, 4.19 ERA and pitched 124.2 innings. Opposing clubs are batting .310 against him and he has logged 24 strikeouts.
