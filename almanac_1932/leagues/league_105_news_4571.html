Reliever of the Year Award Goes to Nattakul
Sunday, October 7th, 1923
The Boston Redcoats got more than they expected out of Jackie Nattakul in 1923.

At the age of 35, there were questions about whether or not Nattakul could still compete at a high level, but those questions were answered emphatically as he has won the Canadian League Reliever of the Year Award.

On the season, the Bluffton, The United Provinces native recorded 8 saves in 47 relief appearances and threw 86.1 innings. He recorded 38 strikeouts and 26 walks, while posting an opponent's batting average of .275.

He received 9 first place votes out of a possible 16. Prentice Swain of the Ottawa Indians finished second in voting with 7 first place votes, while Noah Asselin of the Boston Redcoats finished third.
