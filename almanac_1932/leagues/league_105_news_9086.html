Indians Come up Short of Playoffs
Saturday, September 10th, 1927
The Ottawa Indians will have to focus on playing for pride for the remainder of the season. Today, the team was officially eliminated from playoff contention.

Ottawa last appeared in the postseason in 1920. This is their 7th consecutive year missing the playoffs, and in total they have not advanced past the regular season 20 times in their 23-year history. In the last 10 seasons, the Indians have missed the playoffs 8 times.
