National League Defeats Canadian League 8-6 in Tight All-Star Game
Tuesday, July 19th, 1927
National Baseball Association put its best players on the field today, and the game certainly showed that.

In the annual Midseason Classic, the National League came out over the Canadian League by a final score of 8-6. Leading the way for the National League All-Stars was left fielder Geoff Sarault.

"I go out there and do my best every game, All-Star game or not," the star said after his great show, going 1-for-1 in the contest.

During his 441-game career Sarault has batted .285 and collected 476 hits, 51 home runs and 254 RBIs.
