Jr. Royales Dominate, Sweep Division Finals
Wednesday, July 30th, 1924
After their 9-3 win today over the Quebec City Jr. Nordiques, the Montreal Jr. Royales will advance to the Laurier Cup.

Despite coming out on the losing side, the series MVP award went to Quebec City left fielder Thaiwat Jinta. Jinta told a reporter from the Quebec City News that he was disappointed in the loss, and hopes to be back next year competing for the Laurier Cup

Montreal won the series 4-0. The Montreal Jr. Royales don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Drummondville Voltigeurs - Providence Bruins series. Providence leads the series 3 games to 0. The Laurier Cup schedule will be announced after the Division Finals finishes.
