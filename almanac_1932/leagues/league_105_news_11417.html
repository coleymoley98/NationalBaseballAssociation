National League Winner is Montreal Royales
Friday, August 30th, 1929
Grown men turned into little boys, some even breaking into tears, in the Montreal Royales clubhouse, after the team clinched the National League.

"We can relax a bit, for now anyway," said manager Hervé Morel. "I'm proud of every guy in the room for what we've done this season."

The club has a 65-38 record as they head towards the playoffs in search of their 7th Riel Cup championship. Starting Pitcher Ray O'Gorman said the team is happy to clinch what is the club's 11th division title, but "wouldn't be happy until we're lifting that trophy."
