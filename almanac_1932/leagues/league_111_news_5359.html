Legends Win Tight West Race
Friday, July 25th, 1924
The Canton Legends are the class of the West.

With their record sitting at 27-23, the club officially captured the division title today for the 2nd time.

"Every guy in the room has worked towards this goal all year long," said manager Sean Wilkinson. "I'm proud to call this my team."

The club will now go in search of their 1st Eastern Baseball League championship.

"It's not going to be easy, but winning the Adams Cup is our ultimate goal," said starting pitcher Prayad Adeleye.
