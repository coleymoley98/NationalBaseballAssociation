Veteran RP Tonnessen Stays in Chicagou
Wednesday, November 5th, 1930
Happy times in Chicagou as the Frontenacs and star reliever Alan Tonnessen put the finishing touches on a $22,200 contract extension that will keep the 32-year-old in town 3 years longer.

The signing is great news for Chicagou because players of this caliber are hard to come by.

In his career Tonnessen has appeared in 379 games and earned 84 saves with 272 Ks in 502 innings. He has a record of 59 wins and 43 losses and a 2.90 ERA.
