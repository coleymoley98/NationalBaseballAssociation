Playoff Bound: Legends Win Wild Card
Saturday, July 24th, 1926
It may have been a wild card win, but the Canton Legends plan to "celebrate like it's a division title," according to starting pitcher Brishen Hefti.

"The playoffs is the playoffs no matter how you got there. This means a lot to the guys in the clubhouse as we have a chance to win a Adams Cup," he said.

With a 25-24 record, Canton will be seeking its 1st Eastern Baseball League championship.
