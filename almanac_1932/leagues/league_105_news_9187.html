NBA's annual arbitration period will again be in the books after today.

After today, you'll be able to find out all of the nitty gritty details on who won and who lost. Of course, the wins and losses this time are not occurring on the field against a competitor, but in an office surrounded by a player's bosses. Arbitration hearings are known for making National Baseball Association's players and their bosses uncomfortable, year after year.
