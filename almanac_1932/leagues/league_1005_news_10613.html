Team's Mays Elbows His Way to CJBT Award
Saturday, April 6th, 1929
The Canadian Junior Baseball Championship announced today that Jayden Mays has been chosen the Best Pitcher winner for 1929. The Team hurler had everything working for him as he cruised to an outstanding 2-1 record.

Mays put up an impressive 1.23 ERA in his award-winning season. He started 4 times, worked 22 innings, yielded 17 hits, walked 4, fanned 9 and held opposing hitters to a .210 average.

He received 10 first place votes out of a possible 18. Max Humprey of the Team New England finished second in voting with 8 first place votes, while Trent Busakorn of the Team Columbia finished third.
