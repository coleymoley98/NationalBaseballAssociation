Thorns Say Adios to Manager Greene
Tuesday, October 5th, 1926
Portland Thorns general manager Joe Unknown today fired his manager, Girvan Greene, citing "failure to utilize all elements of his roster properly."

Speaking to reporters while cleaning out his office, Greene was asked to comment on that assessment.

"Part of a team's success comes from the talent that is put on the field every day. It's common knowledge that the Thorns are missing a few key components," he said. "I got the best out of the talent I was given. Maybe the wrong person was fired?"
