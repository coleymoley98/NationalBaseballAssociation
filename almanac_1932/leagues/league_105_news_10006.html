Van Buren Shines, Shuts Out St. Louis, 6-0
Monday, July 30th, 1928
In an outstanding mound performance, Detroit Lions starter Elijah Van Buren fired a complete-game shutout against the St. Louis Cardinaux at St. Louis Grounds today.

Van Buren was the key to the Lions's 6-0 win. The 28-year-old right-hander was in high spirits on the mount for much of the game, his mood largely undented by the 9 hits and 3 walks he allowed.

"9 hits?" he said with a laugh following the game. "Listen, I'd be happy with a 30-hitter as long as it was a shutout."

In 9 starts this season Van Buren has fashioned a 7-4 record and a 4.45 ERA.
