Washburn of Ranier Player of the Week
Monday, July 18th, 1932
16-year-old Jason Washburn made a few Ranier headlines during this past week as the rookie left fielder proved he could compete at the plate with his high-school idols. He captured the attention of fans, journalists and Western Baseball League officials last week and today took home Player of the Week honors for his effort.

He cracked 11 hits in 29 at-bats to put up a .379 average. Washburn collected 3 home runs, 10 RBIs and scored 7 runs.

In 41 games this season Washburn has compiled a .269 batting average with 36 hits and 8 home runs. He has also scored 26 times and knocked in 22 runs.
