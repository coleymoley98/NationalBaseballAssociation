National League #1 Rookie Named
Wednesday, October 15th, 1930
This year's best newcomer has been chosen and the 1930 National League Rookie of the Year Award trophy went to Detroit left fielder Ivan Peña.

It was a season to remember for the 23-year-old Lions rookie. He wrapped up his first year with a .372 batting average, 14 doubles, 14 triples and 5 home runs. Peña also had 51 RBIs and scored 54 times.

He received 8 first place votes out of a possible 20. Sid Moth of the Juneau Brasseurs finished second in voting with 9 first place votes, while Bryant Patten of the Quebec Nordiques finished third with 2 first place votes.
