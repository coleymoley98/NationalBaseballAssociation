Gutierrez Holds Edmonton Scoreless
Wednesday, June 26th, 1929
The Omaha Falcons did little to support starter Martino Gutierrez against the Edmonton Eskimos, but it turned out he didn't need the help.

The left-hander spun a complete game shutout against the Eskimos, striking out 3 and surrendering 7 hits. The performance from Gutierrez allowed Omaha to escape with the 2-0 win despite an anemic offense.

"Nobody came to me and said 'Martino, we're gonna need you to win this one for us today,' but I could feel it," Gutierrez told BNN reporters following the win. "I'm the guy that will step up for you when you need it."

In 5 starts this year Gutierrez has posted 1 win while suffering 4 losses. He has pitched 33.2 innings, allowed 47 hits, struck out 19 and has a 7.22 ERA.
