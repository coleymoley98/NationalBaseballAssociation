NL's Best Rookie Named
Tuesday, October 9th, 1923
All the votes are in for the National League Rookie of the Year Award. The honor for 1923 has been presented to Adam Lachance, right fielder for the Royales.

He fashioned a .329 batting average, piling up 103 hits in 313 at-bats, 8 home runs, 26 walks, 56 RBIs and a .382 OBP.

He received 13 first place votes out of a possible 16. Jackson Vine of the Quebec Nordiques finished second in voting with 3 first place votes, while Valéry Jetté of the Montreal Royales finished third.
