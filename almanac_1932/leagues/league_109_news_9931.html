Rebels McMahon Snags EL Player of the Week
Monday, July 23rd, 1928
Peter McMahon demonstrated his potential to be a star in the game last week and today he took home the Eastern League Player of the Week Award.

The Albany third baseman wreaked havoc on opposing pitchers last week, pounding them for a .391 average (9-for-23) with 5 home runs and 8 RBIs on his way to claiming the honor.

So far this season McMahon has hit .281 with 13 home runs, 31 RBIs and scored 38 times.
