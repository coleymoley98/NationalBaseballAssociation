Strasburg Hurls Morgantown to Shutout Win
Monday, June 10th, 1929
Jimmy Strasburg really had his stuff working at Juniors Ballpark today. The Morgantown Mountaineers hurler mystified the Springfield Falcons and shut them out 3-0. Strasburg allowed a total of 5 hits in the ballgame and struck out 8.

"The shutout is nice but I'm just happy we won," Strasburg said with a smile after the game. "Coach can be a real bear when we lose."
