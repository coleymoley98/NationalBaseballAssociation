4-Game Suspension for Jr. Falcons Salinas
Wednesday, July 16th, 1930
Jr. Falcons catcher Gerard Salinas was incensed after being suspended for 4 games by the commissioner's office following a heated argument the day before in the Omaha-Adams game at Ballpark of Adams.

He was thrown out of the game for arguing a strike call. The league office cited Salinas for "inappropriate and aggressive conduct," which included throwing his hat at the umpire several times.

So far this season Salinas is batting .240 with 4 home runs.
