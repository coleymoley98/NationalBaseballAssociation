Saints Get No Resistance from Reds in 10-0 Beat-Down
Friday, July 13th, 1928
Kitsada Richardson was in a mood to talk following the Minnesota Saints contest against the Cincinnati Reds, expounding to BNN reporters on his pitching philosophy. He had good reason to be excitable, having tossed a complete game shutout of the Reds, 10-0.

"There are three important elements in pitching success: location, location, and location," Richardson said. "I was hitting my spots time and time again."

The left-hander blanked Cincinnati on a 6-hitter, allowing 3 walks and racking up 4 strikeouts.

This season Richardson has registered 7 wins and 5 losses in 15 starts with a 2.05 ERA.
