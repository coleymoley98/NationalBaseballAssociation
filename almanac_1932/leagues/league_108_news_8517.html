St-Louis Batters Edmonton Big Time
Tuesday, June 21st, 1927
All game long, Seattle's Sujarit St-Louis was rattling the walls at Edmonton Grounds and banging out base hits against the Edmonton Eskimos. When the dust finally settled on the diamond, he had a 5-hit day and the Rainiers had a 10-3 victory.

Speaking with reporters after his standout performance, the 23-year-old left fielder said, "Get 5 hits, win a ballgame, it's nice. I could get used to this."

Sujarit St-Louis hit an RBI single in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, singled in the 6th and hit a solo-shot off Kan Lheureux in the 8th.

St-Louis has a .434 average and a .538 on-base percentage for the year. He has picked up 33 hits, 6 home runs and driven in 18 runs.
