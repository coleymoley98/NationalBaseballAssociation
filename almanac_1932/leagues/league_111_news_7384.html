Wohlers's Hit Streak Soars to 20 Games
Monday, June 28th, 1926
Santipab Wohlers's consecutive-game hitting streak has now climbed to 20 games. The Fort Wayne third baseman smacked 1 hit against the Morgantown Mountaineers in today's game at Juniors Ballpark, but he expressed mixed emotions about the streak after watching the Pioneers drop the game, 21-9.

"I feel good about the streak, but don't feel good when we lose," he told reporters. "I'd love to get a hit as well as a win next time so I can celebrate properly."
