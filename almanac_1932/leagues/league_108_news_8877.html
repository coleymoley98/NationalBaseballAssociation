Rowdy Players Suspended by League
Tuesday, August 2nd, 1927
Yesterday at Coyotes Ballpark Glenn Hurd and Tida Tremblay got into each other's space and then into each other's face. They began to grapple and fell to the ground. That's when it boiled over into a full-fledged bench-clearing tussle between the Saskatoon Coyotes and Winnipeg Thunder. Reason finally prevailed as the umpires restored order. After much discussion, only the two major combatants were ejected from the game.

Today the league office notified the ballclubs that Hurd will have to sit out 4 while Tremblay would be given a 6-game suspension for their roles in the brawl.

Thus far in 1927 Hurd is hitting .194 with 5 home runs, 25 RBIs, 33 runs scored and has compiled a .364 OBP.

To date this year Tremblay has compiled a 4-1 mark with a 4.33 ERA in 7 starts.
