Kingston Wins Top Spot in
Wednesday, August 24th, 1932
Fans of the Kingston Indians have a lot to get excited about, as the club has captured the crown.

It's the 2nd time in franchise history that the club has finished atop the standings and they now have an opportunity to capture the franchise's 4th Eastern League championship.

Manager Bunnawat Gresh said there's "a lot of excitement" in Kingston over the club right now and they hope to live up to expectations.

For his part, first baseman Evan Pichette said the division championship is "just a start" for his team.

"We want to go all the way, win the World Series," he said. "Winning the division - we could get used to this."
