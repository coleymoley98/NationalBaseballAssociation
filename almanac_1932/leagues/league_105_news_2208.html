Lobdell of Ottawa Earns CL Batting Crown
Tuesday, September 15th, 1931
At an age where many of his peers are still trying to establish themselves in National Baseball Association, Beau Lobdell of the Ottawa Indians stepped up to win a Canadian League batting title, with a .397 mark.

On the season, he compiled 141 hits, 34 doubles, 6 triples and 7 home runs. In 87 games Lobdell totaled 74 RBIs and scored 74 runs.
