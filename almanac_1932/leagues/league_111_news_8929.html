Gallant's Edwards Elbows His Way to EBL Award
Sunday, August 14th, 1927
Dirk Edwards of the Gallant Black Sox is in the spotlight today. He was acclaimed the best pitcher in the Eastern Baseball League and will be honored with the 1927 EBL Pitcher of the Year.

He started 12 games and compiled a 11-1 record with an ERA of 1.22 in 95.2 innings with 123 strikeouts.

He received 9 first place votes out of a possible 10. Roy Broehl of the North Bay Battalion finished second in voting with 1 first place vote, while Kriangkrai Cheek of the North Bay Battalion finished third.
