Nordiques Out of Time, Out of Playoffs
Tuesday, August 21st, 1928
There might have been some flickers of hopes of a playoff berth at some point during the season, but those flickers have been snuffed out. The Quebec Nordiques were officially eliminated from playoff contention today.

Quebec last appeared in the postseason in 1926. This is their 2nd consecutive year missing the playoffs, and in total they have not advanced past the regular season 16 times in their 34-year history. In the last 10 seasons, the Nordiques have missed the playoffs 8 times. Quebec last won the Riel Cup in 1923.
