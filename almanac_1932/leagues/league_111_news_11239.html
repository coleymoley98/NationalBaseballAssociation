Jr. Steelers Dominate, Sweep Division Finals
Tuesday, July 30th, 1929
The Hamilton Jr. Steelers outclassed the Fort Wayne Pioneers in all facets of the game throughout the EBL Division Finals.

The end result was a Hamilton series sweep to advance to the Adams Cup, capped off by a 4-3 win in the final game.

"We're a bit surprised it's over in a sweep, to be honest with you," said right fielder Lloyd Holtby, who hit .286 in the series.

Holtby's standout effort garnered him MVP honors. He also put up a .286 on-base percentage and had 3 home runs, drove in 5 RBI and scored 3 runs.

The Hamilton Jr. Steelers don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Toronto Jr. Blue Sox - Juneau Jr. Brasseurs series. Toronto leads the series 2 games to 1. The Adams Cup schedule will be announced after the Division Finals finishes.
