Manager James Sangapong Fired by Rebels
Tuesday, October 1st, 1929
When news broke over the firing of James Sangapong, members of the New York Rebels did not seem disappointed.

One player who asked not to be named said, "It's been heading down this road for a while now. He lost the players, lost the coaches, and lost the fans."

When pressed for an opinion on which direction the team should head in looking for a new manager, the same player suggested, "Someone players will listen to."

Management could not be reached for comment.
