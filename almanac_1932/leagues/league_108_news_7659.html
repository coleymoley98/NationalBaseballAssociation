Thunder No Match for Adler, Giants in 15-0 Thumping
Friday, July 30th, 1926
Goose eggs were the order of the day for the Winnipeg Thunder as starter Nigel Adler shut out the Winnipeg Thunder 15-0 at Oracle Park. The Vancouver pitcher struck out 4 and walked 4 while allowing 9 hits.

"Adler was good but he wasn't flawless," Winnipeg manager Jeremy Anglin said later. "We had a few opportunities to make something happen and we didn't do it. Not to take anything away from Vancouver, but this is on us."

His record for the year is 5-4 in 11 starts and Adler has a 3.49 ERA.
