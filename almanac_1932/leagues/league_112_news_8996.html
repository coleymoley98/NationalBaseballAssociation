Jones Wins Atlantic Baseball League Pitching Crown
Wednesday, August 24th, 1927
Crawford Jones of Montreal excelled on the mound in 1927 with a 10-3 record and was named the Atlantic Baseball League ABL Pitcher of the Year winner.

The Jr. Royales star put on quite a show with a 1.27 ERA in 15 starts and 155 strikeouts in 120 innings.

He received 14 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Arthur Jenkins of the Halifax Highlanders finished second in voting, while Ashley Matcham of the Montreal Jr. Royales finished third.
