Rapids Win Tight East Race
Thursday, July 22nd, 1926
Fans of the Buffalo Rapids are certainly optimistic regarding their team's chances to become Adams Cup champions, after it became official that the club has won the East.

"I think our fans think we're the best team in the league and you know what? Our guys think the same thing," said second baseman Weston Nester. "We've got a real good chance to take this title."

It would be the 3rd league championship for the club, which has won the East for the 7th time.

Manager Thawatchai Doors said he's spent many years in pro ball, but there's "just something special" about his current group of players.

"This team has got what it takes to be a champion," he said.
