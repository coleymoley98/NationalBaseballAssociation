Galliano Reaches 1500
Tuesday, August 21st, 1923
"It would have been a lot nicer if my 1500th hit had won the game," said Maxwell Galliano after the Toronto Blue Sox had been tripped up by the Ottawa Indians, 8-2, spoiling his big day. The Blue Sox third baseman gained access to the exclusive 1500-hit club by going 2-for-5 in the game played at Griffith Stadium 1911.

"The standing ovation by the fans is not something I will ever forget," Galliano said to the press after the game. "I think even the Indians fans were cheering me."

In his career Galliano has played in 1481 games, totaling 1500 hits, 211 doubles, 84 triples and 18 homers. He has hit .268 with 492 RBIs and scored 849 times.
