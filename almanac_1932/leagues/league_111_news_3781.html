Jr. Brasseurs Chill Bonds's Hot Streak
Sunday, June 3rd, 1923
The Juneau Jr. Brasseurs pitching staff did its job today, holding hot-hitting Dez Bonds hitless in 1 at-bat during the club's 12-11 victory over the Canton Legends at Juniors Ballpark. The disappointing performance ended a 26-game hitting streak for the third baseman.

"I'm a little down right now," Bonds said after the game. "I couldn't get anything going when the team needed me, and I hated to see the streak end, too."
