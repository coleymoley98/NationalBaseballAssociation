Best EL Reliever Named
Sunday, September 20th, 1925
1925 was a year to remember for Mathieu Gagné.

The Albany Rebels reliever was the owner of a 2.86 ERA this season over 56.2 innings of work. He made 32 relief appearances and had 9 saves, 69 strikeouts, 16 walks and a record of 3 wins and 4 losses.

He's also now got some hardware, as the 31-year-old has been named the Eastern League Reliever of the Year Award winner.

"It's something I can tell my grandchildren some day, pretty special," he said.

He received 7 first place votes out of a possible 8. Andy Carper of the Providence Redcoats finished second in voting, while Watcharin Reske of the Pittsburgh Liberty finished third with 1 first place vote.
