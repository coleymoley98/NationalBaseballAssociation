McLaren Blisters Drummondville
Friday, June 28th, 1929
It's not something you read about in the sports pages every day -- a 5-for-5 day. For many, it might come around only once in a career -- and for Riley McLaren, it came around today.

The 18-year-old center fielder scattered hits all over Juniors Ballpark to pace the Halifax Highlanders to a 6-5 victory over the Drummondville Voltigeurs.

Drummondville skipper Tai Grigsby told reporters in the press room that "McLaren deserves a hat-tip."

"He hit some good pitches against us," he said. "In fact, according to our scouting reports, McLaren hit several that he is not supposed to be able to hit."

Riley McLaren singled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, doubled in the 5th, singled in the 7th and doubled in the 9th.

In 12 games this season McLaren has batted at a .364 pace with 12 hits and no home runs.
