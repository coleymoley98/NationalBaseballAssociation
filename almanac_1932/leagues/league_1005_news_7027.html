Diehl Batters Team Big Time
Monday, March 8th, 1926
Lucien Diehl was at the head of a Team hit parade today at Toronto Memorial Fields. The right fielder went to the plate 6 times and came away with 5 hits, sparking the New York to a 14-1 victory over the Team New England.

"I told the guys in the dugout, just be thinking about hitting the ball hard somewhere," the sweet-swinging Diehl told reporters after the game. "Seems like it worked out well for us today."

New England skipper Tony Green said later that the club gave up "too much plate."

"Not the way we planned it," he said. "Every hitter in the league will rip you when you do that."

Lucien Diehl singled in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 2nd, grounded out in the 4th, doubled in the 6th, hit a three-run double in the 6th and hit an RBI double in the 8th.
