Pioneers Torpedoed by Bonnet in 1-0 Loss to Toronto
Wednesday, July 29th, 1931
Val Bonnet of the Toronto Jr. Blue Sox was lights-out in a 6-hit whitewash of the Fort Wayne Pioneers.

"I had my good stuff and it went where I wanted it to go," he told BNN reporters after the contest.

Bonnet registered 12 strikeouts and 2 walks in shutting out Fort Wayne, 1-0.

Thus far this season, Bonnet has worked 87.1 innings with 94 strikeouts en route to a 7-2 record and a 1.96 ERA.
