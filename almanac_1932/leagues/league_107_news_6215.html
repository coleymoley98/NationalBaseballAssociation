Hamel Tosses Shutout Against Seahawks
Sunday, June 21st, 1925
Alain Hamel blitzed the Ranier Seahawks at Alpines Ballpark today, pitching the Abbotsford Alpines to victory, 3-0. Hamel needed 9 innings to shut out the Seahawks, striking out 7.

"I knew he had it in him, but it's still nice to see him put it all together," said Abbotsford manager Erick Sethna.

Hamel surrendered 6 hits and no walks in the outing.

This year Hamel has worked 30 innings with 25 strikeouts en route to a 3-2 record and 2.70 ERA.
