Sherbrooke Southpaw Tosses 5-0 Shutout
Monday, July 21st, 1924
It wasn't easy but the Sherbrooke Nordiques made it look that way.

Starter Lance Woodson shut out the Trois-Rivieres Royales despite having what he later called "sub-par" stuff, and the Sherbrooke Nordiques cruised to victory, 5-0. Woodson allowed only 10 hits and rang up 3 strikeouts but insisted later that he did not deserve primary credit for the win.

"It didn't feel quite right today, but I got a lot of help to get through," he said. "I trusted my catcher and the guys behind me bailed me out more than once when I screwed up."

To date this season, Woodson has registered 7 starts, 3-2 won-lost mark, 5.30 ERA, 56 IP, 34 strikeouts and 49 walks. Opposing teams have hit .266 against him.
