Going, Going, Gone! Labrie Hits 3 Homers
Thursday, August 4th, 1927
Omaha Falcons left fielder Gaël Labrie sensed he was going to have a good day at the plate, but he still might have underestimated it a little.

Prior to the start of today's contest with the Seattle Rainiers, Labrie told teammates he was sure he would hit a home run. He did -- three times.

"During batting practice I really had my swing down pat," he recalled. "I felt some thunder in the lumber."

The big day from Labrie pushed the Falcons past the Rainiers, 43-4. To go along with the homers, he collected 11 RBIs and scored 5 runs.

"I was seeing good, feeling good and swinging good," Labrie told reporters after the game.

Gaël Labrie singled in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, hit a three-run home run off Vince Berger in the 4th, hit a three-run home run off Greg Eston in the 5th, walked in the 6th, walked in the 6th, walked in the 7th, walked in a run in the 8th, hit a GRAND SLAM off Alfredo Conn in the 9th and walked in the 9th.

So far this year, Labrie is batting .325 with 8 home runs and 50 RBIs.
