Ratanaveroj Garners NL #1 Pitcher Award
Tuesday, September 1st, 1931
As Giovanni Ratanaveroj hoisted up the National League Pitcher of the Month trophy for August, rumors have already begun to swirl about whether he will leave Quebec once his contract is up at the end of the season.

Ratanaveroj put pressure on the Nordiques to keep him around by posting a 5-0 record and an ERA of 2.15 over 50.1 innings last month. He also rolled up 24 strikeouts, increasing his value both to Quebec and to every other team in the league.

Checking out his stats for the season, Ratanaveroj has notched 8 wins and 10 losses in 21 starts, 3.09 ERA and struck out 60 batters in 171.2 innings.
