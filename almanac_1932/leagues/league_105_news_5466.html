Juneau Loses Staples for Season
Friday, August 8th, 1924
The news was not unexpected, but that didn't make it easier to accept for the Juneau Brasseurs: shortstop Phat Staples is out for the year. Staples suffered a torn back muscle on 08/05/1924. The team knew that the chances of seeing him return were slim to none. But today it was made official.

In his 1924 season totals, Staples has 2 home runs, .240 BA, .274 OBP, 15 RBIs and 14 runs scored.
