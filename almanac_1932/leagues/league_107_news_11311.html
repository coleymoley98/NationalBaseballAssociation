King of the Hill Announced by Western Baseball League
Sunday, August 11th, 1929
The Western Baseball League has heralded Azman Eason of the Vancouver Giants as the #1 man on the mound for the 1929 campaign.

This year's WBL Pitcher of the Year winner stood out by running up an impressive 7-6 record, holding opposing hitters to a .203 batting average and posting a 2.00 ERA. The 18-year-old hurler tossed 121.1 innings, gave up 89 hits, fanned 157 and issued 33 walks.

He received 6 first place votes out of a possible 8. Gustav Lafrenière of the Abbotsford Alpines finished second in voting with 1 first place vote, while Theo Giguère of the Vancouver Giants finished third.
