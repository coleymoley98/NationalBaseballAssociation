Royales on to League Chamionship Series
Sunday, September 20th, 1931
The Montreal Royales said they felt the Division Series with the Juneau Brasseurs would go the distance.

But it didn't end up being that difficult, as Montreal advanced to the League Chamionship Series with a 5-4 score, to clinch the series 3-1 at Brasseurs Ballpark.

"Whether it goes to the final game or not, we faced a good team and got the job done. It's a big momentum boost for us," said Stan Bowers, who was named series MVP. "We're all feeling good about our chances to win the Riel Cup."

For the series, Bowers hit .353 with a .353 on-base percentage. He had 0 home runs, drove in 2 RBI and scored 2 runs.

The Montreal Royales don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Detroit Lions - Chicagou Frontenacs series. That series is tied at 2 games apiece. The League Chamionship Series schedule will be announced after the Division Series finishes.
