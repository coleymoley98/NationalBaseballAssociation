Falcons Show No Mercy in 9-0 Thrashing of Jr. Steelers
Monday, July 7th, 1930
The Springfield Falcons rolled to an easy 9-0 win over the Hamilton Jr. Steelers at Juniors Ballpark today. Leading the way to victory was starter Brodie Stevens, who shackled the Hamilton Jr. Steelers and held them scoreless on a 3-hitter.

"This is what every pitcher wants to do when they go out there," Stevens said in his postgame press conference.

To date this season Stevens is 8-1 with 10 starts and an ERA of 1.62 with 110 strikeouts in 77.2 innings.
