Erie Star Receives Best Pitcher
Saturday, April 3rd, 1926
The Canadian Junior Baseball Championship has heralded Watcharapong Hanprab of the Team Erie as the #1 man on the mound for the 1926 campaign.

This year's Best Pitcher winner stood out by running up an impressive 4-0 record, holding opposing hitters to a .245 batting average and posting a 1.21 ERA. The 20-year-old hurler tossed 29.2 innings, gave up 27 hits, fanned 8 and issued 5 walks.

He received 15 first place votes out of a possible 16. Randy Kau of the Team Columbia finished second in voting with 1 first place vote, while Kittichai Kejmanee of the Team Erie finished third.
