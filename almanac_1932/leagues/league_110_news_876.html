Tin Earns CBL Top Pitcher Trophy
Friday, August 1st, 1930
Chayapol Tin really strutted his stuff in July with 6 wins and 1 loss and walked off with the Pitcher of the Month Award in the Central Baseball League. The Omaha starter was good news for his club, but bad news for the opposition.

In 46.1 innings of work, Tin limited opposing batters to 27 base hits and a .172 batting average. He totaled 44 strikeouts, issued 19 walks and had a 1.36 ERA.

This season Tin has worked 83.1 innings with 86 strikeouts en route to a 8-3 record and a 3.35 ERA.
