Richardson Named NL Pitcher of the Year Award Winner
Friday, October 17th, 1924
This year the National League spotlight fell on Kitsada Richardson of the Minnesota Saints. What a 1924 season he had!

A fine 13-7 won-lost record, 2.10 ERA, 25 starts, 197.1 innings and 78 strikeouts -- a season good enough to earn him the NL Pitcher of the Year Award.

He received 10 first place votes out of a possible 16. Caleb Boisvert of the Chicagou Frontenacs finished second in voting with 6 first place votes, while Gautier Beaupré of the Montreal Royales finished third.
