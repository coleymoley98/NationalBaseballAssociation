Injury Derails Season for Beattie
Wednesday, June 23rd, 1926
In a news release, the Minneapolis Jr. Saints announced that catcher Dennis Beattie would miss the rest of the season due to a torn calf muscle. Beattie sustained the injury on 06/21/1926. He "will be sorely missed," said his manager.

Beattie is hitting at a .283 mark this season. His stats show 2 home runs, 11 RBIs and 9 runs scored. He has played 17 games for the Jr. Saints.
