Trouble in New York, Dumoulin Suffers Setback
Wednesday, August 29th, 1923
Things have gotten worse for Eliott Dumoulin of the New York Rebels. The 25-year-old right fielder will not be back as soon as originally expected. Dumoulin is undergoing treatment for knee tendinitis, but his doctors have expanded his recovery time to at least 2 weeks. An unidentified team official told BNN, "Eliott tried to rush his rehab and suffered a setback."

Dumoulin is hitting .318 for the season with 2 home runs, 51 RBIs and 36 runs scored. He has 87 hits in 274 at-bats and played in 70 games.
