Royales Get Zilch in 1-0 Loss to Saints, Semple
Wednesday, August 6th, 1930
The Minnesota Saints shut out the Montreal Royales 1-0 today behind the strong pitching of Hakim Semple. Semple surrendered only 4 hits in recording the complete-game victory, and struck out 3.

Following the last out, Semple appeared to celebrate a little too boisterously for Montreal manager Hervé Morel, who could be seen scolding the famously outspoken left-hander as the clubs left the field. Semple's own manager then intervened with some words for his counterpart.

The Royales skipper would not comment on the exchange, but at the Saints press conference, Semple confirmed that it was regarding the celebration.

"He basically told me I was being a lousy winner," Semple said.

"And I told him he was being a lousy loser," Minnesota manager A.J. Provost added.

This season Semple has compiled a 10-5 mark with a 3.12 ERA in 17 starts.
