#1 Pitcher Trophy Won by Bruins Tauzin
Saturday, August 25th, 1923
Christian Tauzin of the Providence Bruins has been selected the premier pitcher in the Atlantic Baseball League and will receive the ABL Pitcher of the Year for 1923.

The top vote-getter completed the year with a 8-2 record. He started 15 times, threw 88 innings, chalked up 119 strikeouts and 19 walks, limited hitters to a .175 batting average and posted an impressive 1.02 ERA.

He received 11 first place votes out of a possible 14. Donte Green of the Drummondville Voltigeurs finished second in voting with 3 first place votes, while Joe Eckes of the St. John's Mariners finished third.
