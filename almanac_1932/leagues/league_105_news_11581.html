NBA BREAKING NEWS: Expansion Teams Announced
Thursday, October 24th, 1929
The National Baseball Association has announced the teams that have won the bid for the current expansion process! The new teams will be the Hamilton Steelers, the Pittsburgh Quakers, the Toronto Braves, and the Philadelphia Mets.

Existing teams have to submit the protection lists for the upcoming expansion draft until November 6th, 1929.The draft itself is held one day later.

League officials are excited to see these new cities compete!
