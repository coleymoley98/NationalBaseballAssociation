Chen Belts 3 HR Against Calgary
Wednesday, June 20th, 1923
Loik Chen went yard three times in a Saskatoon Coyotes victory over the Calgary Wranglers at Coyotes Ballpark today.

"I was in a groove," he said. "Every pitch they tossed up there looked like a grapefruit."

For the day, the Saskatoon catcher went 4-for-5 against Calgary with 6 RBIs and 3 runs scored in a brilliant display of power hitting that led the Coyotes to the 13-12 win.

"I tell you what. I'm shocked because I didn't think that last ball had enough to get out," he told BNN reporters in the clubhouse following the game. "I thought it was just going to be a long out."

Loik Chen flied out in the 1st, hit a two-run home run off Sakutaro Kaneko in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, hit a three-run home run off Joseph Horn in the 6th and hit a solo-shot off Seamus Viloria in the 8th.

Thus far this season in 18 games Chen is batting .349 with 4 home runs, 15 RBIs and 10 runs scored. He has 22 hits in 63 at-bats.
