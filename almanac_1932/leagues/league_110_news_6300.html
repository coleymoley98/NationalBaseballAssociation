CBL Announces Outstanding Batter Award
Wednesday, July 1st, 1925
Emilson Monteon put together a good June to earn the Central Baseball League Batter of the Month prize. A virtual unknown, he made the most of his chance to prove to Lincoln officials that he belongs on the big league roster.

The Pioneers first baseman posted a .422 batting average with 7 home runs, 17 RBIs and 31 runs scored.
