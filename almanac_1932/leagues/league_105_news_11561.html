1929 CL Pitcher of the Year Award Announced
Sunday, October 13th, 1929
Wolfeburg ace Stephen Gwynn put up some sensational stats for the 1929 season and has been voted the Canadian League Pitcher of the Year Award winner.

The 34-year-old Celtics star made 22 starts and racked up a 14-4 record. He pitched 185 innings, collected 48 strikeouts, gave up 75 walks and 175 hits in holding the opposition to a .249 batting average.

He received 16 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Jeremy Castleberry of the Wolfeburg Celtics finished second in voting, while Davian White of the Chicagou White Sox finished third.
