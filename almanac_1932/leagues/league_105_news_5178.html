Baluch Hurls Frontenacs to 3-0 Shutout Win
Wednesday, July 2nd, 1924
In a splendid 3-0 shutout performance, Liam Baluch of the Chicagou Frontenacs subdued the Juneau Brasseurs on a 5-hitter.

"He kept hitting his spots and putting up zeroes, so I kept sending him out there," Chicagou manager Jetsadaporn Sheerin said in postgame comments.

Baluch registered 5 strikeouts and 5 walks.

So far this year Baluch has made 11 starts, tossed 82.2 innings, struck out 36 batters and walked 39. His record is 6-2 with a 3.38 ERA and he has limited opposing teams to a .270 batting average.
