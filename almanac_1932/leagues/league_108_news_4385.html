Dinsmore Powers 3 HR Against Seattle
Sunday, August 19th, 1923
Beckham Dinsmore was sending souvenirs to the sun gods all game long in the 9-3 Winnipeg Thunder triumph at Thunder Stadium today.

The 19-year-old put on a show for fans, launching not one, not two, but three home runs into the stands and powering the Thunder past the Seattle Rainiers.

"One homer's good, two's great, three's out of sight," Dinsmore told BNN reporters after the game. "I can't wait until next game. I really feel good at the plate."

The right fielder went 3-for-5, rung up 5 RBIs and scored 3 runs on his record-setting day.

Beckham Dinsmore flied out in the 1st, hit a solo-shot off Liam Simard in the 2nd, hit a solo-shot off Liam Simard in the 5th, flied out in the 6th and hit a three-run home run off Lance Aguayo in the 8th.

So far in 1923 Dinsmore is batting .357 with 19 home runs, 61 RBIs and 71 runs scored. He has 100 hits in 280 at-bats and a .448 OBP.
