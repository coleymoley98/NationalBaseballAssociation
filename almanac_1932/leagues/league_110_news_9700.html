One Run Enough for St. Louis
Thursday, June 21st, 1928
Stefano Garza handcuffed the Lincoln Pioneers at Ballpark of St. Louis today, coming away with a 4-hit, complete game shutout.

"I had everything working for me out there," the St. Louis Jr. Cardinaux right-hander said later. "I used every one of my pitches, hit both sides of the plate really well and felt like I could do whatever I wanted."

With his stuff working, Garza had little trouble with the Pioneers and led the Jr. Cardinaux to victory, 1-0. He recorded 6 strikeouts and no walks in an impressive performance.

This season Garza has worked 54.1 innings with 37 strikeouts en route to a 3-3 record and a 2.98 ERA.
