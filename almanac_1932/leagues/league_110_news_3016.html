Blanchard Pounds CBL Pitchers This Week
Monday, July 11th, 1932
It was announced today by the Central Baseball League that Minneapolis right fielder Worrawut Blanchard had received the Player of the Week Trophy for his superlative play last week.

During his week-long batting binge he reeled off a .545 batting average, 12 hits and 2 home runs. Blanchard also racked up 6 RBIs and scored 4 runs.

So far this season Blanchard has 3 home runs and a .331 batting average.
