Cincinnati's Gustin Injured, Out of Lineup
Sunday, July 11th, 1926
Cincinnati fans will be disappointed to learn that Landon Gustin suffered a torn abdominal muscle while running the bases in today's game. After the doctor informed the Cincinnati Reds manager that Gustin was expected to miss 6 weeks, the manager told the press that the rest of the team would have to step up their play until the center fielder could return. "You can't make excuses in baseball, because there's always a game tomorrow," said the team's skipper.

On the season, Gustin is batting .394 with no home runs and 12 RBIs. He has played in 18 games.
