Pittsburgh Quakers Ship Silawarn to Minnesota
Saturday, August 16th, 1930
The Pittsburgh Quakers and Minnesota Saints have put the finishing touches on a deal that was weeks in the making. Pittsburgh will part with 28-year-old CF Phakhawat Silawarn, while the Saints have dealt 20-year-old minor league LF Aaron Almanza, 22-year-old minor league 2B Atchawut Ulger and 18-year-old minor league RHP Kyle Nedd.

"This one took a while to figure out," said Pittsburgh general manager Senquez Hopkins. "We must have gone back to the drawing board five or six times over the past few weeks. I'm glad we finally got the deal done. I think it is a good one for both clubs."


This year Silawarn has collected a .296 batting average to date with 91 hits in 307 at-bats, 36 RBIs, no home runs and scored 37 times. He has played in 83 games.
