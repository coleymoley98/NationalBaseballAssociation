Playoffs Won't Happen for Jr. Brasseurs
Saturday, July 26th, 1924
There won't be a Eastern Baseball League championship banner being raised in Juneau this year. The Juneau Jr. Brasseurs came close to qualifying for the postseason, but ultimately have failed to make it in. They were officially eliminated today.

This is the club's 3rd playoff miss in a row. They last appeared in the postseason in 1921. In the last 10 seasons, the Jr. Brasseurs have missed the playoffs 9 times. In total, the Jr. Brasseurs have missed the playoffs 29 times in their 30-year history. Juneau has won the Adams Cup 3 times in their history.
