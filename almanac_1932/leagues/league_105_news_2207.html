NBA News: Playoffs begin

Another regular season has played out, and the stage is set for the next level. The National Baseball Association playoffs are here. It has been a long, hard grind for the contending clubs to make the Division Series. All the playoff-bound teams want to cap off the year in glorious style by capturing the prestigious 1931 Riel Cup trophy. Each team will begin that journey today with these playoff games:
Wolfeburg Celtics versus Boston Redcoats, Ottawa Indians versus Toronto Blue Sox, Juneau Brasseurs versus Montreal Royales and Detroit Lions versus Chicagou Frontenacs.
