CL Star of the Week Named
Monday, July 9th, 1923
Carmen Morales demonstrated his potential to be a star in the game last week and today he took home the Canadian League Player of the Week Award.

The Boston shortstop wreaked havoc on opposing pitchers last week, pounding them for a .655 average (19-for-29) with no home runs and 7 RBIs on his way to claiming the honor.

For the season Morales has produced 86 hits in 232 at-bats for a .371 average with 3 home runs and gotten on base at a .414 clip.
