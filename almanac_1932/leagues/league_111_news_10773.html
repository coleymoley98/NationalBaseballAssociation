Pichette Hittin' on All Cylinders
Saturday, June 1st, 1929
Evan Pichette of the Springfield Falcons had an amazing day at the plate today in a game against the Morgantown Mountaineers. The Springfield first baseman hit for the cycle as the Falcons downed the Mountaineers 5-3.

"I would love to say it was all skill, but I have to admit it was some luck, too," Pichette said. "Everything went where it needed to go."

Pichette finished the day with 4 hits in 4 tries and raised his batting average a few points. He also had 2 runs batted in and 2 runs scored.

Evan Pichette hit a two-run home run off Jimmy Strasburg in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, doubled in the 5th and tripled in the 8th.
