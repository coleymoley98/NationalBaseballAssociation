Cowan on Fire: 5-for-5 for Team
Monday, March 24th, 1924
It doesn't happen very often, but it did for Simon Cowan today -- a 5-hit day. He went on a rampage and sparkled on the diamond at San Francisco Memorial Fields, but the performance lost a little luster when the Columbia were downed 8-6 by the Team Pennsylvania.

After his glittering performance, the Team second baseman told the press that he "just tried to get myself in a good count and have a good at-bat."

His batting theory paid dividends today as he slammed 5 hits in 5 tries and put his name in the record book.

"I just wish we had added a win in the record book today, too," Cowan said.

Simon Cowan singled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, doubled in the 6th and singled in the 8th.

This season Cowan has hit at a .619 clip with 2 RBIs, 0 home runs and 6 runs scored. He has played in 7 games.
