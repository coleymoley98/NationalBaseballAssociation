Juneau Brasseurs Ship Hennessy to Ottawa
Tuesday, October 23rd, 1928
It's being reported in the Juneau press that the Brasseurs have traded 29-year-old RHP Steely Hennessy to the Ottawa Indians for 25-year-old LF Conner Sramek and 22-year-old minor league RHP Logan Fortier. However, there is no confirmation by the two ball clubs.


Hennessy has crafted a 72-60 won-lost record in his career with a 3.73 ERA.

In 114 career games Sramek is hitting at a .302 pace with 98 hits with 22 doubles, 5 triples and 3 homers while scoring 50 runs and racking up 48 RBIs. He logged 41 walks and owns a .380 on-base percentage.
