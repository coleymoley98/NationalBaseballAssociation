Mostrom Fires 3-0 Shutout in Battalion Win
Tuesday, July 5th, 1932
Stirling Mostrom of North Bay easily handled the Springfield Falcons, stopping them on 5 hits and chucking a complete-game 3-0 shutout.

"He really did a job on us," said Springfield manager Cal Mentink. "He had good command of his pitches. He changed speeds well and confused us. We never could zero in on him, but he certainly zeroed in on us."

He finished the game with 9 strikeouts and no walks in a solid outing.

In 9 starts this season Mostrom has recorded a 7-1 record and a 1.98 ERA.
