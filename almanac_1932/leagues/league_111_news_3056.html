Postseason Bid Ends for Toronto
Sunday, July 17th, 1932
The dream of lifting a championship banner above the home field of the Toronto Jr. Blue Sox has been extinguished, at least for 1932. The club was formally eliminated from playoff contention today.

Asked to comment on how he felt about the news, Jr. Blue Sox manager Pongsak Strong issued a one-word statement, which cannot be printed by BNN.

Before this season, the Jr. Blue Sox last missed the playoffs in 1928. In the 38 years they have been a Eastern Baseball League franchise, they have missed the playoffs 35 times. In the last 10 years, they have missed the playoffs 7 times. Toronto has won the Adams Cup 4 times in their history.
