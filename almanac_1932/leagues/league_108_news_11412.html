Beauchemin Hit String Snapped at 20
Wednesday, August 28th, 1929
For 20 straight games, Zack Beauchemin tried to ignore his hitting streak. It wasn't easy.

Well, the 20-year-old Calgary Wranglers center fielder can forget about it now -- it's over, courtesy of the Seattle Rainiers. The Rainiers held him hitless in 3 at-bats at Calgary Grounds today and snapped his consecutive-game hitting string at 20.

There was at least one good note to the end of the streak, however, as the Wranglers took the win by the score of 6-5.

"I tried to ignore it for weeks, but you guys in the media wouldn't let me," he joked with reporters after the game. "I'm looking forward to some peace and quiet now."

In 78 games this year Beauchemin has posted a .326 average and put up these numbers: 6 home runs, 40 RBIs and 63 runs scored.
