Bad Behavior Costs Jennings and Green
Thursday, July 19th, 1923
Joshua Jennings of the Quincy Gunners and Donte Green of the Drummondville Voltigeurs will be on the sidelines for a few days. They have been suspended for 4 and 6 games respectively by the league office for causing the bench-emptying free-for-all at the recent Gunners-Voltigeurs game at Juniors Ballpark.

Neither player had any comment about the suspensions. Both clubs told BNN Jennings and Green will begin serving them soon.

A look at his season totals shows Jennings with 9 home runs, .254 BA, .344 OBP, 27 RBIs and 27 runs scored.

This season to date Green has a record of 10 wins and no losses with a 1.73 ERA, has appeared in 15 games and struck out 111 in 104.1 innings.
