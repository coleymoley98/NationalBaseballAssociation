Boston's McInnis Snaps Up Player of the Week
Monday, August 16th, 1926
It's always a thrill for fans of the team and students of the game alike when a superstar in the making shows what he's got. Phuwanart McInnis, the Boston Redcoats 21-year-old first baseman, put himself in the spotlight last week making him the clear-cut choice for Canadian League Player of the Week honors.

He ripped 16 hits in 23 at-bats, compiling a .696 average with no home runs and 5 RBIs.

McInnis is swinging at a .315 pace on the year with 1 home run, 39 RBIs and 40 runs scored. He has played in 68 games.
