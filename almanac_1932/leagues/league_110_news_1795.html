Central Baseball League Pitcher Honored
Monday, July 20th, 1931
Orlando Pineda drew on his youthful energy and pitching skill to win Central Baseball League Player of the Week honors last week.

The St. Louis Jr. Cardinaux pitcher registered 2 wins and no losses, 0.00 ERA and 16 strikeouts in 18 innings to sew up the award. The 15-year-old also held opposing hitters to 7 hits and a .125 batting average.

This season Pineda has worked 106.1 innings with 106 strikeouts en route to a 6-4 record and a 1.52 ERA.
