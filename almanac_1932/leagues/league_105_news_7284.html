Reds Suffer 10-0 Drubbing from Frontenacs
Thursday, June 17th, 1926
Israel Hunt of the Chicagou Frontenacs overwhelmed the Cincinnati Reds and walked off the mound with a complete-game shutout. He blanked them 10-0 on 7 hits with a total of 1 strikeout and 3 walks.

"Israel was on quite a roll," Chicagou manager Jetsadaporn Sheerin said. "He gets great late movement and he doesn't give in to the hitters."

This year Hunt has recorded a 3-4 mark. In 8 starts he has fanned 17 batters in 67 innings with a 2.82 ERA.
