CBL Reliever of the Year Award Winner Named
Monday, August 12th, 1929
Jacob Perreault was a calming influence out of the Saskatoon Jr. Coyotes bullpen this season.

His teammates and manager said he had a poised demeanor about him that was typical of someone much older and more experienced. His poise has paid off, as the 18-year-old turned in a sparkling season and has been named the winner of the Central Baseball League Reliever of the Year Award for 1929.

The Saskatoon, The United Provinces native won the award with 3 saves in 22 relief appearances, 69 strikeouts in 48 innings and a record of 6-2 to go with his 2.06 ERA.

He ended up tied with Cris Rogers with 32 total votes and 32 first place votes.
