Patcharapa Thwarts Halifax, 3-0
Saturday, July 23rd, 1927
Techonan Patcharapa of the Quincy Gunners whitewashed the Halifax Highlanders 3-0 in a complete-game win, limiting them to 4 hits while chalking up 7 strikeouts and giving up no walks.

"He's got the makeup. He's not afraid to challenge guys," Quincy manager Graham Cross said to BNN reporters after the game. "Techonan is poised out there. He knows how to pitch and how to use his stuff."
