Jr. Thunder Clinch Wild Card
Thursday, July 25th, 1929
The Winnipeg Jr. Thunder will be seeking a 4th Dumont Cup victory as a wild card team this season.

The club has secured a postseason berth and first baseman Woody Choe said "it would be a long night" as the team planned to celebrate.

"We'll get back to business tomorrow, but for now it's time to relax and enjoy," he said.
