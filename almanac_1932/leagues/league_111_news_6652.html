1925 Best Player Selected In EBL
Friday, August 21st, 1925
Ben Moncassin took Eastern Baseball League by storm this year, and he did it all at the age of 17.

For his spectacular campaign, the Buffalo Rapids star has been named the 1925 Eastern Baseball League EBL Player of the Year recipient.

"To be considered amongst the best in the league is overwhelming," said the third baseman.

On the season he compiled a .354 batting average to go with a .392 on-base percentage. The native of Detroit, The United Provinces also added 22 doubles, no triples, 8 home runs, 28 RBIs and 32 runs scored.

He received 10 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Danny Sellers of the Hamilton Jr. Steelers finished second in voting, while Kyle Nadeau of the Morgantown Mountaineers finished third.
