Beaulieu Records Shutout Against Lions
Friday, August 22nd, 1930
Guillaume Beaulieu kept the Detroit Lions hitters from doing any damage at Great American Ball Park today.

Detroit could only muster 6 hits and take 2 walks against the right-handed Beaulieu, who controlled them throughout the game as Cincinnati took the contest, 4-0. Beaulieu added 1 strikeout in his complete-game effort.

"I don't know if I was on or if they were off today," Beaulieu told reporters after the shutout win. "But whatever it was, I hope it happens again next time."

This year Beaulieu has a 7-12 record and a 4.36 ERA.
