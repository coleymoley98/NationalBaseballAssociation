Wolfeburg Trades Keating for Quebec's Chantacharoen
Thursday, October 25th, 1923
It came down to the last hour of a self-imposed trade deadline, but the Wolfeburg Celtics and Quebec Nordiques finally came to an agreement on a trade that had been rumored for weeks. The trade sends Wolfeburg's 26-year-old minor league SS Liam Keating and 20-year-old minor league RHP D.J. Rhoden to Quebec in a swap for 19-year-old minor league 3B Ekkaphan Chantacharoen. Both GMs seemed relieved that the deal had been completed with one stating, "We had to set a deadline to get it done." The players are expected to report to their new teams by tomorrow.
