Coude Takes Division Finals
Saturday, August 1st, 1931
The Coude Voyageurs have advanced to the Laurier Cup, with a 3-2 triumph at Coude Grounds over the Drummondville Voltigeurs.

The win sealed a 4-1 series win for Coude.

The series MVP honor went to Voyageurs starting pitcher Tyler Nimmo.

"It takes every guy in that dugout to win games, not just me," Nimmo said afterward. "I'm proud to be moving on."

The Coude Voyageurs don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Pointe-Sainte-Anne Acadiens - Providence Bruins series. Pointe-Sainte-Anne leads the series 3 games to 1. The Laurier Cup schedule will be announced after the Division Finals finishes.
