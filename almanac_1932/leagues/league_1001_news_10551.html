Godbout Touches All the Bases, Hits for Cycle
Friday, March 15th, 1929
Samuel Godbout roughed up Team Dakota pitching in a big way at Blue Sox Ballpark today, leading the Team to a 8-5 win.

With his parents and family watching from the stands, the 22-year-old right fielder put on a magnificent show, hitting for the cycle -- collecting a single, double, triple and home run in the same game.

"It was a good day for that to happen," a beaming Godbout said after the game. "I really got some good looks and delivered. Sometimes you're just on."

He finished his historic day with 4 hits in 5 at-bats, 1 run batted in and 2 runs scored.

Samuel Godbout grounded out in the 1st, hit a solo-shot off Zed Tremblay in the 3rd, tripled in the 5th, doubled in the 7th and singled in the 8th.
