Dumont Batters Team for 5 Hits
Tuesday, March 17th, 1931
Team Saskatchewan left fielder Julian Dumont had a day to remember at New York Ballpark today, ripping apart the Team Minnesota and sparking the Saskatchewan to a 17-2 victory.

Dumont wrote his name in the record books with a 5-hit barrage, finishing his special day 5-for-6.

"I'm usually a first ball hitter," Dumont told BNN reporters in the postgame press conference. "I think pitchers think batters are going to let the first one go by. I never do. I've always found that it's better to pull the trigger early in the count. Pitchers get real nasty when they get ahead of you, so I try to avoid it."

Julian Dumont hit a two-run single in the 1st, flied out in the 3rd, singled in the 4th, singled in the 6th, hit a two-run triple in the 7th and hit an RBI single in the 8th.
