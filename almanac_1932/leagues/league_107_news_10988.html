Western Baseball League Announces June's Best Batter
Monday, July 1st, 1929
At only 17 years of age, Glenn Bloom could very well be having many more months like the last this in his career.

That would be great news for fans of the Everett Silvertips, as June was one to remember for the left fielder and he has captured the Western Baseball League Batter of the Month award.

On the month he hit .429 with 9 home runs, 37 RBIs and 25 runs scored.
