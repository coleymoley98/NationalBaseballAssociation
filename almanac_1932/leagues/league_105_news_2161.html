Frontenacs Crowned Kings of the National League West
Monday, September 7th, 1931
The Chicagou Frontenacs clubhouse more closely resembled a college party tonight as the team officially captured the National League West.

Laughter abounded and loud music blared as players and coaches alike celebrated the accomplishment.

"It's not often you get to kick back and let loose with your teammates," said left fielder Justin Lapierre. "We're earned the right to party a little bit. And then we can focus on getting it done in the playoffs."

Team manager Archie Cooper said even the coaching staff can enjoy occasions like this.

"I'm proud of my guys, they deserve this," he said.

It's the 4th division championship for the team and they will be seeking their 5th National Baseball Association championship.
