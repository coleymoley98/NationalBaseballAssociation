Minnesota Takes League Championship Series
Tuesday, September 22nd, 1925
The Minnesota Saints have beaten the Juneau Brasseurs and will advance to the Riel Cup.

Minnesota won the final game by a 10-7 count to take the series 4-2. The Saints triumph was no doubt in large part because of the performance of first baseman Taradin Boulay, who was named MVP of the series.

"The only thing that matters is that we're off to the next round and one step closer to a Riel Cup," he said.

The Minnesota Saints don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Philadelphia Liberty - Boston Redcoats series. Boston leads the series 3 games to 2. The Riel Cup schedule will be announced after the League Championship Series finishes.
