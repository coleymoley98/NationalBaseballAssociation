Cincinnati Trades Gagnon for Montreal's Vandiver
Saturday, November 15th, 1930
Reports have surfaced that Cincinnati has agreed to send 32-year-old RHP Dwight Lynch and 22-year-old minor league 2B Sornnarai Gagnon to the Montreal Royales in exchange for 20-year-old minor league 2B Wyatt Vandiver and 20-year-old minor league 3B Jordan Diomede. "It's a done deal," said one executive speaking on the condition of anonymity. "I'm surprised word got around so quickly. We just signed off on this an hour ago." The deal apparently is awaiting physicals before an official announcement is made by either team.
