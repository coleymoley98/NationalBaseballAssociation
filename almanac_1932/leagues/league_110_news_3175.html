Great Day for Skinner, Hits for Cycle
Saturday, July 30th, 1932
Lincoln Pioneers center fielder Anusak Skinner hit for the cycle today, but his club still went home with their heads hung low.

The Pioneers were upended 12-11 by the St. Louis Jr. Cardinaux, despite the big 4-for-4 day from Skinner

"It was a good game for me," Skinner said later. "I wish it could have been a good game for all of us."

The Pioneers center fielder sparked the offense with a truly special performance, hitting 1 single, 1 double, 1 triple and 1 home run to add his name to the record books. Skinner also finished with 2 runs scored and 7 RBI.

Anusak Skinner walked in the 1st, hit an RBI double in the 3rd, hit an RBI single in the 5th, hit a two-run triple in the 6th and hit a three-run home run off Samir Magana in the 8th.

For the year Skinner is batting .216 with 9 home runs, 26 RBIs and 33 runs scored.
