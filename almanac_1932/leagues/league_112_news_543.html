Coureurs Chill Bartlett's Hot Streak
Saturday, June 28th, 1930
Sherbrooke Canadiens right fielder Danny Bartlett and his hot bat finally met their match today at Field of the Juniors.

The Champlain Coureurs snapped his 21-game hitting string, holding Bartlett hitless in 3 at-bats and also whipping Sherbrooke for good measure.

"We're real pleased with this one," Coureurs manager Porawat Jewett said. "Nobody wants to be on the wrong end of a streak like that, and clobbering Sherbrooke makes it even sweeter."

This season Bartlett has delivered 45 hits in 105 at-bats for a .429 average with 3 home runs. He is getting on base at a .459 clip and his totals also include 13 RBIs and 22 runs scored. This year Bartlett has played in 26 games.
