Quebec Clinches Wild Card
Tuesday, September 2nd, 1930
Winning a Riel Cup is no easy task. Winning it as a wild card is even more challenging.

But the Quebec Nordiques are up for the challenge.

"If we get hot at the right time, it will be tough to stop us," said starting pitcher Martin Daves as he celebrated his team's wild card berth. "I think we've got the players and coaches to make it happen."

With their record sitting at 63-46, the Nordiques will indeed have a challenge ahead if they hope to win the 12th Riel Cup in team history.
