Eastern League Selects Manager of the Year
Sunday, September 23rd, 1928
Malik Hill, skipper of the Sherbrooke Nordiques, was voted 1928 Eastern League Manager of the Year and accepted the award earlier today.

He guided Sherbrooke to first place in the with a 54-28 record.

"I'm very happy about this," he said at the press conference. "But I'm more happy about what my boys did on the field. They battled their butts off for me all year. They should get the credit."
