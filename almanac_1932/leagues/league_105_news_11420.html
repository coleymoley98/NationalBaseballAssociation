Postseason Bid Ends for Juneau
Saturday, August 31st, 1929
They put up a valiant effort, but the Juneau Brasseurs didn't have what it takes to make the National Baseball Association playoffs in 1929. The club was officially eliminated from postseason qualification today.

"Sucks," said Brasseurs reliever Gautier Beaupré.

The Brasseurs have missed the playoffs 17 times in their 26 years as a National Baseball Association franchise. This playoff miss is the club's 4th in a row, with their last postseason appearance coming in 1924. In the last 10 seasons, the Brasseurs have missed the playoffs 6 times. Juneau has won the Riel Cup 2 times in their history.
