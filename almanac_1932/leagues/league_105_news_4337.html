Silva Rocks Royales for 3 HRs
Saturday, August 11th, 1923
Marc Silva brutalized the Montreal Royales pitching staff, launching three home runs in today's 10-7 victory at Montreal Grounds. The Detroit Lions third baseman was red-hot, the ball just exploding off his bat all day.

"I don't try to hit homers," he said later. "I try to go out there and put a good swing on the ball, and sometimes it is going to go out of the park. I didn't try to hit the first one or the second one or the third one."

For the game, he went 3-for-6 with 6 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

"Too much Marc Silva today," Royales manager Mustafa Campeau said. "That's my assessment of the game. That's what our problems boiled down to."

Marc Silva grounded out in the 1st, flied out in the 3rd, hit a two-run home run off Gautier Beaupré in the 5th, grounded out in the 7th, hit a solo-shot off Panai Guidry in the 9th and hit a three-run home run off Panai Guidry in the 10th.

This season Silva has hit at a .312 clip with 52 RBIs, 8 home runs and 41 runs scored. He has played in 84 games.
