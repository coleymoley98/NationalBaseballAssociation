Gunning Ups Hit Streak to 20 Consecutive Games
Thursday, July 24th, 1930
Russ Gunning of the New York Rebels extended his hitting streak to 20 straight games with 1 hit in today's 6-0 triumph over the Boston Redcoats at Yankee Stadium 1923.

"Russ has been making it easy for me to pencil his name in the lineup every day," Rebels manager William Davison joked after the game.
Russ Gunning singled in the 1st, struck out in the 2nd, grounded out in the 5th and flied out in the 7th.

This season Gunning has hit at a .387 clip and produced 48 runs batted in, 6 home runs and 71 runs.
