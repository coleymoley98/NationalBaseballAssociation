Sharp Belts 5 Hits
Monday, June 2nd, 1930
Pawtucket Red Sox center fielder Khusmana Sharp had a day to remember at Juniors Ballpark today, ripping apart the Quincy Gunners and sparking the Red Sox to a 23-5 victory.

Sharp wrote his name in the record books with a 5-hit barrage, finishing his special day 5-for-6.

"I'm usually a first ball hitter," Sharp told BNN reporters in the postgame press conference. "I think pitchers think batters are going to let the first one go by. I never do. I've always found that it's better to pull the trigger early in the count. Pitchers get real nasty when they get ahead of you, so I try to avoid it."

Khusmana Sharp flied out in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 3rd, singled in the 6th, hit an RBI single in the 6th, hit a two-run home run off Dural Loftis in the 7th and hit a three-run double in the 8th.
