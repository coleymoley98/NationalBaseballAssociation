Randell McClinton Named Best EL Batter in August
Wednesday, September 1st, 1926
When Randell McClinton stepped up to the plate in August he did it with confidence, and today he was honored with the Eastern League Batter of the Month award.

The young Providence right fielder posted a .453 batting average with 10 home runs, 32 RBIs and scored 29 runs. McClinton also worked pitchers for 16 walks to post a .532 on-base percentage.

For the year McClinton is hitting .367 with 18 home runs. His stats include 53 RBI and 51 runs scored.
