Moore Tops CL with .384 Average
Monday, September 14th, 1925
He may only be 23 years old, but Methanon Moore has already established himself as an elite National Baseball Association player.

The Toronto Blue Sox star posted a .384 batting average this year, which won him the Canadian League batting crown.

"I can't even believe it, to be honest," he told Baseball News Network.

His fantastic campaign consisted of 165 hits, 29 doubles, 4 triples and 19 home runs for the year. In 108 games Moore added 97 RBIs and scored 81 runs.
