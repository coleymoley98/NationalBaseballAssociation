Dinsmore Touches All the Bases, Hits for Cycle
Friday, June 22nd, 1923
Winnipeg's Beckham Dinsmore accomplished what few others have done -- he hit for the cycle, collecting a single, double, triple and home run in the same game.

For the day, Dinsmore finished 4-for-5 with 1 run batted in and 4 runs scored and helped the Thunder knock off the Edmonton Eskimos 10-5 at Thunder Stadium.

"I didn't even know I was about to hit for the cycle until after," Dinsmore told reporters following the game. "One of the guys mentioned it when I got back to the dugout."

Beckham Dinsmore struck out in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, doubled in the 5th, tripled in the 6th and hit a solo-shot off Aimery McKinney in the 7th.

This season Dinsmore has delivered 33 hits in 83 at-bats for a .398 average with 2 home runs. He is getting on base at a .485 clip and his totals also include 15 RBIs and 16 runs scored. This year Dinsmore has played in 21 games.
