Week's Top Player: Baltimore's Ginsburg
Monday, June 27th, 1927
One of the league's most exciting young players, Malden Ginsburg of the Baltimore Robins earned himself Canadian League Player of the Week honors with a standout performance in last week's games.

For the week the 23-year-old right fielder had a lofty .517 average (15-for-29), collecting no home runs, 9 RBIs and 10 runs scored.

So far this season Ginsburg is batting .387 with no home runs, 34 RBIs and 39 runs scored. He has 67 hits in 173 at-bats, 20 walks and a .448 OBP. Ginsburg has played in 42 games.
