Fanchaiwang Misbehaves, Suspended 2 Games
Thursday, July 26th, 1923
The agitation was hot and heavy at Providence Grounds yesterday. After a called third strike Indians right fielder Somjit Fanchaiwang protested the call in no uncertain terms. While he and the home plate umpire were jawing face-to-face, a few bumps took place and Fanchaiwang was ejected from the game.

"It was verbal, nothing more," according to Fanchaiwang. However, the league frowned on what transpired and suspended him for 2 games.

"The league will never tolerate a player touching an umpire in anger," the commissioner said in the suspension notification.

Fanchaiwang is hitting .236 this year with 5 home runs, 23 RBIs and 12 runs scored. He has 30 hits in 127 at-bats, 6 walks and a .271 on-base percentage. Fanchaiwang has played in 36 games.
