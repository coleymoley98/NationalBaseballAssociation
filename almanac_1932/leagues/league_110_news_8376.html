Jr. Thunder End Dollar's Streak
Saturday, June 4th, 1927
Edwin Dollar of the Lincoln Pioneers came up empty at the plate today and went hitless in 5 at-bats for the first time in 21 games. Making matters even worse for the catcher, Lincoln dropped the game to the Winnipeg Jr. Thunder 13-9.

"It was not a good day today," Dollar told the press in postgame comments. "We lost the game and the streak is over."
