Robins End Sparger's Streak
Monday, June 15th, 1931
Punnarat Sparger, third baseman for the Ottawa Indians, gave it a good try, but his time chasing history came to an end when he went hitless in 4 tries against the Baltimore Robins today. Not only did the Robins break his 20-game hitting streak, they also beat Ottawa by the score of 7-6.

"Now that the streak has ended, I can admit I'll breathe a bit easier the next time I step up to bat," Sparger said, speaking with the media after the game. "It was tough because I put a little pressure on myself. Now that it's over, I will definitely be a little bit looser at the plate."

For the year Sparger is batting .342 with 1 home run.
