Lions End Green's Streak
Thursday, July 17th, 1930
Leon Green, left fielder for the Minnesota Saints, gave it a good try, but his time chasing history came to an end when he went hitless in 4 tries against the Detroit Lions today. Not only did the Lions break his 20-game hitting streak, they also beat Minnesota by the score of 4-2.

"Now that the streak has ended, I can admit I'll breathe a bit easier the next time I step up to bat," Green said, speaking with the media after the game. "It was tough because I put a little pressure on myself. Now that it's over, I will definitely be a little bit looser at the plate."

On the year Green has hit .384 with 1 home run, 12 RBIs and scored 20 runs.
