Hamilton Steelers Ship Busakorn to Juneau
Sunday, April 19th, 1931
Hamilton and Juneau have agreed to a trade today. 25-year-old 3B Worakorn Busakorn and 23-year-old LF Bertrand Costigan will go to the Brasseurs. In exchange the Steelers will receive 28-year-old 1B Ethan Brunel.


Brunel has put up a career batting average of .282 with 849 hits, 36 homers and 285 RBIs.
