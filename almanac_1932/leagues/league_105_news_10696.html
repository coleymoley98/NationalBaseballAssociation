Paquette Bangs Out 5 Base Hits
Wednesday, May 15th, 1929
5 times is the charm -- at least for Sripatana Paquette.

The Ottawa Indians second baseman tore up the Baltimore Robins to the tune of 5 hits in 5 at-bats at Sportsmans Park 1911 today. The offensive onslaught from Paquette powered the Indians to a 9-5 win.

"Paquette was a tough out today," Baltimore manager Eldin Gabriel said to the press in the postgame interview. "He hit some very good pitches with that quick bat of his. Not an easy out, that's for sure."

Sripatana Paquette tripled in the 1st, hit a two-run home run off Weerasak Massey in the 2nd, hit an RBI single in the 4th, doubled in the 6th and singled in the 9th.

So far this season Paquette has 2 home runs and a .343 batting average.
