WL Presents Player of the Week Trophy
Monday, August 19th, 1929
One of the league's most exciting young players, Kyle Nadeau of the Vancouver Giants earned himself Western League Player of the Week honors with a standout performance in last week's games.

For the week the 22-year-old right fielder had a lofty .448 average (13-for-29), collecting 4 home runs, 10 RBIs and 9 runs scored.

This season Nadeau is batting .392 with 38 hits and 7 home runs. He has scored 26 times and has 26 runs batted in.
