Perras Slaps 5 Hits
Friday, September 5th, 1924
Christophe Perras had an answer for everything the Ottawa Indians threw at him today at Lincoln Park. The Philadelphia first baseman went 5-for-6 in the game to put his name in the record book and put the Liberty on top, 21-3.

Summing up his batting philosophy to BNN writers after the game, Perras said, "I just see the ball and I hit it."

Christophe Perras singled in the 2nd, grounded out in the 3rd, singled in the 6th, hit a two-run home run off Grandy Rucker in the 7th, singled in the 9th and hit a two-run single in the 9th.

This season Perras is batting .301 with 9 home runs. He has scored 62 runs and driven in 65.
