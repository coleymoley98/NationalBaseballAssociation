June's Outstanding NL Rookie Announced
Sunday, July 1st, 1928
Few players his age have the consistency to put a solid month together, but Montreal's Bunnawat Phomee carved up the opposition all June long and hauled off the National League Rookie of the Month award today as a result.

He sizzled last month with a 2-2 mark, fanning 10 in 36.1 innings with an ERA of 2.48 in 4 starts.

To this point in the season, Phomee shows a 2-2 record, 4 starts, 38.1 innings, 11 strikeouts and a 2.35 ERA.
