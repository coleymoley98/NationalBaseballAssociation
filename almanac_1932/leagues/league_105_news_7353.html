Another Setback For Baltimore's Hubert Green
Saturday, June 26th, 1926
The Baltimore Robins will have to do without second baseman Hubert Green even longer than expected. Team doctors say that Green's recovery from a sore elbow has not gone well and more time will be needed to treat it successfully. The Robins trainer indicated it would be at least 2 weeks before they would consider activating Green.

Green currently has a .248 batting average with 25 hits, 3 homers, 11 RBIs, 11 runs scored and a .309 OBP.
