Gallant Devastated by Playoff Elimination
Sunday, July 22nd, 1923
Gallant Black Sox first baseman Jacob McGowan said it is a "bitter disappointment" for he and his teammates to miss the playoffs. The remarks came hours after the club was mathematically eliminated from 1923 postseason contention.

The Black Sox have missed the playoffs 29 times in their 29 years as a Eastern Baseball League franchise. This playoff miss is the club's 30th in a row, with their last postseason appearance coming in 1892. Gallant has won the Adams Cup 2 times in their history.
