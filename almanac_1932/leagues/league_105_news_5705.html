New York Win League Championship Series
Wednesday, September 24th, 1924
Graham Kadyrov sparked his New York Rebels past the pesky Boston Redcoats in a closely-contested League Championship Series.

New York topped Boston 10-2 at Polo Grounds to decide the series, 4-2

For his efforts, Kadyrov was rewarded with the series MVP trophy for his .480 batting average, 3 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

"You never want to leave anything on the field," Kadyrov said. "You never want to miss a play of defense you could have made, or give up an extra base you could have gotten, because in a series this tight you never know what you'll need to win."

The New York Rebels will play against the Detroit Lions in the next round. Detroit won their series against the Chicagou Frontenacs 4 games to 0. The Riel Cup schedule will be announced after the League Championship Series finishes.
