Toronto Starter Wins July's Pitcher Award
Friday, August 1st, 1930
Cooper Yuschak's been around the league long enough to appreciate the honor he received today. After going 4-0 in July, the 30-year-old Toronto hurler has been named Canadian League Pitcher of the Month.

He fanned 12 in 36 innings, logged an ERA of 1.50 and allowed opponents to hit .219 against him.

To this point in the season, Yuschak shows a 7-2 record, 8 starts, 81.2 innings, 28 strikeouts and a 3.20 ERA.
