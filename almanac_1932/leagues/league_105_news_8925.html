Ng Thwarts Chicagou, 5-0
Friday, August 12th, 1927
It was a dismal game for the Chicagou White Sox with Jude Ng on the hill. The Boston Redcoats starter was in top form and made it a long day for Chicagou with a 5-0 shutout. He surrendered a total of 8 hits and piled up 1 strikeout to pick up the win in a rout.

"No offense to the White Sox but that was a cakewalk today," Ng said to BNN reporters following the win.

This year Ng has a 5-11 record with a 4.79 ERA.
