Great Week for Morgantown's Adam Klein
Monday, July 11th, 1927
Adam Klein is respected by most Morgantown Mountaineers fans for his no-frills style of play. The 19-year-old shortstop went about his business with vigor last week to grab the Eastern Baseball League Player of the Week honors.

He compiled a .500 average (11-for-22) with 1 home run, 7 RBIs and 4 runs scored.

Klein is batting .246 this season in 31 games with 5 home runs, 16 RBIs and 15 runs scored. He has 28 hits in 114 at-bats.
