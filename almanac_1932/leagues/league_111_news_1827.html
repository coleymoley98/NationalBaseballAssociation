No Late-Season Magic Coming: Juneau Out of Playoffs
Wednesday, July 22nd, 1931
While some Eastern Baseball League pundits expected the Juneau Jr. Brasseurs to be in this year's playoffs, that won't be the case. The Jr. Brasseurs have been mathematically eliminated from postseason contention.

"Today was not a fun day," said starting pitcher Kuekkong Matu.

Juneau last appeared in the postseason in 1929. This is their 2nd consecutive year missing the playoffs, and in total they have not advanced past the regular season 34 times in their 37-year history. In the last 10 seasons, the Jr. Brasseurs have missed the playoffs 7 times. Juneau has won the Adams Cup 3 times in their history.
