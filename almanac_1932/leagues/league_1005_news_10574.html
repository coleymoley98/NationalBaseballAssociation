Cordillera Pitching Ravaged by McEwan
Wednesday, March 20th, 1929
It wasn't just another day at the ballpark for Bill McEwan today. McEwan went on a tear and cracked hit after hit off Team pitchers. When the dust settled on the diamond at Blue Sox Ballpark, he had a 5-for-6 line and a day he will long remember. And the Team New York had a 17-4 victory over the Cordillera.

"Yeah, it'll be something nice to tell the grandkids, but it won't get me a hit tomorrow," he joked in the postgame interview.

Bill McEwan singled in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, flied out in the 5th, hit an RBI double in the 7th, hit an RBI double in the 8th and hit an RBI single in the 9th.

This season McEwan has hit at a .333 clip with 12 RBIs, 1 home runs and 11 runs scored.
