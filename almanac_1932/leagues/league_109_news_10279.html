EL Honors Great Glove Award Winners
Thursday, September 20th, 1928
Great glovemen can range far and wide to make a backhand stop or an over-the-shoulder catch -- and the best usually make it look easy. Today the winners of the Eastern League Great Glove Award for 1928 were announced. These awards for defensive excellence go to:

Pitcher
Jon Bachtiar (London Indians)

Catcher
Calvy Gaddis (Providence Redcoats)

First Baseman
Sidney Boyer (Providence Redcoats)

Second Baseman
Supakit Swett (Hamilton Blue Sox)

Third Baseman
Peter McMahon (Albany Rebels)

Shortstop
Nattawut Branagan (London Indians)

Left Fielder
Arthit Kurata (London Indians)

Center Fielder
Dilbertino Moran (Providence Redcoats)

Right Fielder
Ting Mingchai (Sherbrooke Nordiques)
