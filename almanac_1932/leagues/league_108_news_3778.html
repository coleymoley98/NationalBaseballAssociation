Wranglers Not Competitive in 20-0 Loss to Omaha
Saturday, June 2nd, 1923
Martino Gutierrez of the Omaha Falcons spun his magic on the Calgary Wranglers today. He blanked them 20-0 on 2 hits, registering 8 strikeouts and 4 walks.

"The bottom line was I just kept the ball down," Gutierrez said. "My breaking stuff was down. Everything was down."

Talking to reporters in the postgame interview, Omaha skipper Emeril Martineau said the team "got solid pitching, good defense and took great team at-bats in a lot of different situations."

"Martino pitched really well but this was a solid performance by everyone," he said.
