Top Pitcher Trophy Won by Admirals Brushett
Saturday, August 14th, 1926
The Western Baseball League announced today that Albus Brushett has been chosen the WBL Pitcher of the Year winner for 1926. The Victoria starter had everything working for him this past season as he soared to a 10-4 record.

In his exceptional season Brushett chalked up a 2.05 ERA in 127.1 innings with 127 strikeouts and 29 walks. Enemy teams hit .206 against him in his 16 starts.

He received 6 first place votes out of a possible 8. Jonny Tessier of the Boise Broncos finished second in voting with 2 first place votes, while Patrick Riewerts of the Vancouver Giants finished third.
