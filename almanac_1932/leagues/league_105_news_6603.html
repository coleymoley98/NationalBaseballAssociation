Saints Richardson Dominates Nordiques
Wednesday, August 12th, 1925
The Minnesota Saints whipped the Quebec Nordiques 1-0 at Ballpark of Quebec today behind a masterful outing from starter Kitsada Richardson.

Richardson shut out the Nordiques on 5 hits and no walks. He registered 6 strikeouts over 9 innings.

"Velocity felt good, everything felt good today," a smiling Richardson said later. "Command felt good, conditions felt good, glove felt good, hat felt good. Seriously everything."

To date this season, Richardson has a record of 9 wins and 5 losses and a 2.88 ERA. He has appeared in 19 games and struck out 67 in 162.1 innings.
