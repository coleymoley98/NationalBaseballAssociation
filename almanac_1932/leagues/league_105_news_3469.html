Bergeron Addresses Clubhouse Issues
Friday, September 9th, 1932
The manager of the Minnesota Saints said he believes clubhouse moral should soon start to improve, after Hakim Semple spoke out about how things were going.

"We had a closed-door meeting recently and talked things out. We aired out a lot of our issues and I think things will be smoother from here on in," said Datsakorn Bergeron. "The guys know they can come to me if they have any issues."

For his part, Semple said he'll wait and see how things progress.

"I feel better about things for sure, but we'll take it day by day," he said.
