Battalion Torpedoed by Rung in 4-0 Loss to Morgantown
Sunday, July 10th, 1932
The bats were ice cold today for the North Bay Battalion at Battalion Ballpark. Morgantown Mountaineers starter Krita Rung kept the Battalion cold all day, shutting them out in a 4-0 victory. In a fine outing, the 16-year-old hurler allowed 4 hits while striking out 10 and walking 1.

"I'm not quite sure what was different today," Rung admitted later. "I didn't feel like I was doing anything differently, but the results speak for themselves."

On his stats sheet for the year Rung shows a 4-4 record, 10 starts, 77.1 innings, 69 strikeouts and a 4.89 ERA.
