5-for-6 Day for Cossette
Sunday, March 17th, 1929
It was high-fives all around for William Cossette at Blue Sox Ballpark today.

He got them from his teammates and he got them at the plate during an outstanding hitting performance against the Team Saskatchewan. Cossette smashed 5 hits in 6 at-bats on his monster day, leading his club to a 13-8 win.

"William was hitting everything," said Montana manager Guillaume Grenier. "It really was a special game for him."

In the opposing dugout, Saskatchewan manager Korraphat Trujillo told BNN beat writers that Cossette "ambushed" the club.

"Obviously we didn't have much success against him, but we'll have another chance," he said. "Hopefully, Cossette oversleeps next time."

William Cossette hit an RBI double in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, flied out in the 7th and singled in the 9th.
