Lions End Alvarez's Streak
Sunday, August 30th, 1925
It was a doubly miserable day today for Teeratat Alvarez of the Cincinnati Reds, as he saw his 24-game hitting streak end and his team lose 6-3 to the Detroit Lions at Bennett Park. The 25-year-old shortstop was held hitless in 5 at-bats by the Lions.

"Anyone on a 24-game hitting streak is going to be a tough out," Detroit manager Brian Ahn said later. "But we had a plan and we were careful about it, and got it done."

This season Alvarez is hitting .350 with 105 hits, 1 home run, 21 RBIs, scored 47 runs and played in 72 games.
