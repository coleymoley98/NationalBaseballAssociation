Great June Gets Prescher NL Best Hitter Award
Tuesday, July 1st, 1930
When Zeshon Prescher stepped up to the plate in June he did it with confidence, and today he was honored with the National League Batter of the Month award.

The young Detroit first baseman posted a .451 batting average with 4 home runs, 25 RBIs and scored 20 runs. Prescher also worked pitchers for 4 walks to post a .459 on-base percentage.

Prescher is hitting .407 this season with 7 home runs, 48 RBIs and 35 runs scored. He has 79 hits in 194 at-bats.
