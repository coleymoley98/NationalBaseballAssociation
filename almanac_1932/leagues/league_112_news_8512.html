Coureurs No Match for Eastman, Highlanders in 9-0 Thumping
Monday, June 20th, 1927
Ellis Eastman dominated the Champlain Coureurs with a brilliant 4-hit shutout in a Halifax Highlanders 9-0 triumph at Juniors Ballpark. The Halifax hurler fanned 9 and walked 1 in the game and was singled out for praise afterward by the opposing manager.

"He really kept us in check today," Coureurs skipper Porawat Jewett said in his postgame comments. "Everything looked to be moving. Seemed like the pitch was right down the middle and it went over down and away. We took some pathetic swings."
