Lheureux Named Top Reliever for WL
Saturday, September 21st, 1929
At the young age of only 25, Kan Lheureux has been named the Western League Reliever of the Year Award winner for 1929.

The future appears bright for the Vancouver Giants relief ace, who captured the award with 5 saves in 27 relief appearances, 22 strikeouts in 37 innings and a record of 5-5 to go with his 4.62 ERA.

He received 8 first place votes, as a unanimous winner.
