Matte Collects Cycle
Tuesday, June 5th, 1928
Andrew Matte truly did it all at Jr. Thunder Ballpark today.

The Winnipeg Jr. Thunder second baseman hit for the cycle -- collecting 1 single, 1 double, 1 triple and 1 home run -- as he paced his club to victory, 8-7 over the Davenport Rivermen.

"You know that sweet sound you hear when you hit the ball just right? I heard that every time today," Matte said later. "It was a heck of a feeling."

It was a game good enough for him to make a little history. He bombed Davenport pitching for 4 hits in 6 at-bats and his name will now go into the record books. Matte also racked up 4 runs batted in and 2 runs scored in the game.

Andrew Matte hit a two-run home run off Raphael Hétu in the 1st, hit an RBI triple in the 3rd, hit an RBI double in the 4th, flied out in the 6th, struck out in the 9th and singled in the 11th.

Matte is hitting .300 this season with 1 home run, 5 RBIs and 3 runs scored. He has 6 hits in 20 at-bats.
