Legends Win Tight West Race
Sunday, July 26th, 1931
The Legends kicked off a raucous celebration in Canton tonight, as they edged the competition to win the West.

"We all knew what was at stake and how important it was to finish strong. We're proud to be able to call ourselves champions of the West," said closer Blake Helberg.

Manager Von Rivera said with his club's 30-21 record, they may not be considered odds-on favorites to win the Adams Cup, but nobody should look past them or count them out.

"We are division champions for a reason," he said.

This was the 3rd division title in franchise history.
