Juneau's Coker Collects June Batting Honor
Thursday, July 1st, 1926
Bryan Coker made his presence known as one of the best young stars in the National League this past month.

The Juneau Brasseurs second baseman shined through the month of June and has won the Batter of the Month award.

The 22-year-old hit .316 with 2 home runs, 10 RBIs and 20 runs scored.

This season Coker has banged out 42 hits in 147 at-bats for a .286 average with 2 home runs. He is getting on base at a .438 pace and his totals also include 17 RBIs and 27 runs scored.
