Frontenacs Not Competitive in 6-0 Loss to Detroit
Wednesday, September 1st, 1926
Detroit hurler Steve Roca put on quite a show today as he blitzed the Chicagou Frontenacs with a 6-0 shutout. He baffled them with fastballs and changeups, allowing 7 hits with 4 strikeouts and 1 walk to pick up the win.

"I think every time we were looking for a fastball, he threw a changeup and every time we were looking for a changeup, he threw a fastball," Chicagou manager Jetsadaporn Sheerin told reporters after the game.

This season Roca has compiled a 12-8 mark, 2.99 ERA and made 23 starts.
