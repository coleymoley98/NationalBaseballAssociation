McIntyre Tosses 5-0 Shutout
Wednesday, July 20th, 1932
The Vancouver Giants shut out the Spokane Eagles 5-0 today behind the strong pitching of Dermot McIntyre. McIntyre surrendered only 4 hits in recording the complete-game victory. He registered 8 strikeouts and 1 walk.
