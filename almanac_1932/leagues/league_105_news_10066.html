NBA News: Draft Pool Revealed
Tuesday, August 7th, 1928
National Baseball Association today released the final list of all players eligible for next month's rookie draft. Now that the list has been made official, close scrutiny of these players can be expected as scouts begin to review this year's up and coming talent.

BNN has added to its Transactions page a section dedicated to the draft pool, which will be available 30 days prior to draft day.

The early favorites to be high picks are the following:
1. Weston Nester, 18 year-old 2B out of college.
2. Simon Lim, 18 year-old SS out of college.
3. Russ Gunning, 18 year-old LF out of college.
4. Derek Kurtz, 18 year-old 3B out of college.
5. Arthur Jenkins, 18 year-old RHP out of college.
6. Fingers Ordoñez, 18 year-old RHP out of college.
7. Langley Hubbard, 18 year-old SS out of college.
8. Andrew Shaw, 18 year-old 3B out of college.
9. Tawil O'Brien, 18 year-old 1B out of college.
10. Chi Broadus, 18 year-old RHP out of college.

The official OSA Mock Draft will be released on the day of the draft, or you can review the draft pool on the draft page.
