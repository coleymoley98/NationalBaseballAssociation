Best Pitcher Winner Announced
Sunday, April 3rd, 1927
It was a history-making year for Team reliever Surasak Pona. He put up some impressive numbers, including no saves in no save opportunities on his way to earning the Canadian Junior Baseball Championship Best Pitcher for 1927.

The 19-year-old relief star went 2-0 in 4 games with a 0.48 ERA.

He received 11 first place votes out of a possible 16. William Shelley of the Team Erie finished second in voting with 5 first place votes, while Chaiya Junjira of the Team New England finished third.
