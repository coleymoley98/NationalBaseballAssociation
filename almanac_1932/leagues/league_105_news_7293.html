Frontenacs Blank Cincinnati, Drouin Winner
Friday, June 18th, 1926
Dominic Drouin of the Chicagou Frontenacs was on fire against the Cincinnati Reds at Great American Ball Park today. He shut them out 2-0 on 6 hits, collecting 1 strikeout and allowing 3 walks.

"The first two pitches I'm looking for an out, a ground ball or a fly ball," Drouin said later. "I'm trying to get quick outs. I've never been a big believer in trying to strike a guy out from the very first pitch. It's too easy to end up behind in the count doing that."

This year Drouin has a 3-2 record with a 3.37 ERA.
