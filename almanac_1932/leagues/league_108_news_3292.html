5-Hit Blitz by Deutsch
Friday, August 12th, 1932
It's not something you read about in the sports pages every day -- a 5-for-6 day. For many, it might come around only once in a career -- and for Kent Deutsch, it came around today.

The 19-year-old right fielder scattered hits all over Ballpark of Seattle to pace the Calgary Wranglers to a 16-8 victory over the Seattle Rainiers.

Seattle skipper Romie Huynh told reporters in the press room that "Deutsch deserves a hat-tip."

"He hit some good pitches against us," he said. "In fact, according to our scouting reports, Deutsch hit several that he is not supposed to be able to hit."

Kent Deutsch hit an RBI single in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, singled in the 6th, grounded out in the 8th and doubled in the 9th.

Thus far this year Deutsch is hitting .319 with no home runs, 12 RBIs and 16 runs scored.
