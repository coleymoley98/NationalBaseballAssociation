Watitapun Dominates Jr. Coyotes in 4-0 Shutout
Tuesday, June 22nd, 1926
It was a dismal game for the Saskatoon Jr. Coyotes with Phat Watitapun on the hill. The Winnipeg Jr. Thunder starter was in top form and made it a long day for Saskatoon with a 4-0 shutout. He surrendered a total of 5 hits and piled up 3 strikeouts to pick up the win in a rout.

"No offense to the Jr. Coyotes but that was a cakewalk today," Watitapun said to BNN reporters following the win.

Over the season Watitapun has thrown 37.2 innings and dished out 30 strikeouts with a 2-2 mark and a 3.82 ERA.
