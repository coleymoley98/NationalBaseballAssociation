Baltimore Robins Ship O'Gorman to New York
Tuesday, July 13th, 1926
The Robins and Rebels confirmed this afternoon that a trade had been agreed upon. In the deal, Baltimore will get 22-year-old minor league 1B Boon-Mee Maiwong, 21-year-old minor league C Winston Aguilera and 19-year-old minor league RF Sam Siemann, while New York will receive 31-year-old RHP Ray O'Gorman.


Up to now O'Gorman has recorded a record of 8-6. In 15 starts he has fanned 46 batters in 124.1 innings with a 5.43 ERA.
