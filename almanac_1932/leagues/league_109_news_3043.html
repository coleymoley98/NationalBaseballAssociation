Berman Electric in 1-0 Kingston Win
Wednesday, July 13th, 1932
It wasn't easy but the Kingston Indians made it look that way.

Starter Mel Berman shut out the Harrisburg Robins despite having what he later called "sub-par" stuff, and the Kingston Indians cruised to victory, 1-0. Berman allowed only 9 hits and rang up 10 strikeouts but insisted later that he did not deserve primary credit for the win.

"It didn't feel quite right today, but I got a lot of help to get through," he said. "I trusted my catcher and the guys behind me bailed me out more than once when I screwed up."

Up to now Berman has recorded a record of 6-1. In 9 starts he has fanned 34 batters in 75.2 innings with a 3.93 ERA.
