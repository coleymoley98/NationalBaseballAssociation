Ottawa-Chicagou Trade: Auger for Gendron
Monday, November 5th, 1928
The Indians have reached a deal with Chicagou, sending 31-year-old RF Noah Auger to the Frontenacs in exchange for 23-year-old minor league 3B Apichai Gendron, 19-year-old minor league RHP Glen Boyer and 20-year-old minor league RHP Andrew Swalve. Reports had been hinting at a deal in recent days, but weren't confirmed until this afternoon.


In his 1082-game career Auger is hitting .321 with 1312 hits, 222 doubles, 79 triples and 86 home runs. He has scored 690 runs and driven in 625.
