Red-Hot Woodmore Cools Off Juneau
Monday, August 20th, 1923
Eric Woodmore of the Minnesota Saints was lights-out in a 4-hit whitewash of the Juneau Brasseurs.

"I had my good stuff and it went where I wanted it to go," he told BNN reporters after the contest.

Woodmore registered 4 strikeouts and 4 walks in shutting out Juneau, 4-0.

This season-to-date, Woodmore has fashioned a 12-6 mark and 1.93 ERA in 186.2 innings. Opposing clubs are batting .232 against him and he has logged 54 strikeouts.
