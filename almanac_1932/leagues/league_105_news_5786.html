Ottawa Trades Jones for New York's Demers
Sunday, November 16th, 1924
Reports have surfaced that Ottawa has agreed to send 25-year-old 2B Aaron Jones to the New York Rebels in exchange for 24-year-old minor league RF Parth Subramanian and 21-year-old minor league 1B Jeter Demers. "It's a done deal," said one executive speaking on the condition of anonymity. "I'm surprised word got around so quickly. We just signed off on this an hour ago." The deal apparently is awaiting physicals before an official announcement is made by either team.


In his career Jones has hit .336 with 300 hits, 59 doubles, 22 triples and 8 home runs. His totals also include 133 RBIs and 133 runs scored.
