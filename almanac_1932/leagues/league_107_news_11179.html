Apple Sox No Match for Colman, Seahawks in 9-0 Thumping
Monday, July 22nd, 1929
Sujin Colman of the Ranier Seahawks shut out the Kelowna Apple Sox 9-0 at Ballpark of Ranier today, holding them to 4 hits with 1 walk and 13 strikeouts.

Pressed for comments on his club's defeat, Kelowna skipper Jesse Daniels called the outing from Colman "a jewel."

"The way Colman was pitching, it would have been tough for anyone to beat him," he said.

This year Colman has fanned 65 in 67.2 innings and collected a 5-3 record with a 2.39 ERA.
