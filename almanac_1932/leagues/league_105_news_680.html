Toronto Blue Sox Ship Wise to Ottawa
Friday, July 11th, 1930
It came down to the last hour of a self-imposed trade deadline, but the Toronto Blue Sox and Ottawa Indians finally came to an agreement on a trade that had been rumored for weeks. The trade sends Toronto's 26-year-old RHP Jamison Wise to Ottawa in a swap for 27-year-old CF Sirayut Pedneault and 23-year-old RHP Sakunchai Landis. Both GMs seemed relieved that the deal had been completed with one stating, "We had to set a deadline to get it done." The players are expected to report to their new teams by tomorrow.


In season stats Wise shows a 8-6 record in 14 games, 108.1 innings, 42 strikeouts and a 5.15 ERA.

In 13 starts this season Landis has recorded a 9-2 record and a 4.35 ERA.
