Prioleau Hits for the Cycle
Thursday, June 16th, 1932
Yutta Prioleau accomplished one of the rarer feats in baseball today when he hit for the cycle against the Providence Redcoats in the 15-3 Sherbrooke Nordiques win at Nordiques Ballpark.

Afterward, Prioleau acknowledged that his accomplishment was special, but said he was more interested in helping the Nordiques win.

"Maybe one day I'll look back on this with more meaning, but right now I just want to go out tomorrow and put some good swings on the ball and help my club win another game," Prioleau said.

On his historic day, he went 4-for-5 with 3 RBI and 4 runs scored -- and also got a spot in the history books.

Yutta Prioleau grounded out in the 1st, doubled in the 3rd, hit an RBI triple in the 4th, singled in the 7th and hit a two-run home run off Danny LeBrun in the 8th.

Thus far this year Prioleau has hit .371 with 1 home run and 10 RBIs.
