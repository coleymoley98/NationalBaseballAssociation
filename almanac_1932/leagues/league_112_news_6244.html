Highlanders Show No Mercy in 10-0 Thrashing of Mariners
Wednesday, June 24th, 1925
Arthur Jenkins of the Halifax Highlanders spun his magic on the St. John's Mariners today. He blanked them 10-0 on 5 hits, registering 9 strikeouts and 2 walks.

"The bottom line was I just kept the ball down," Jenkins said. "My breaking stuff was down. Everything was down."

Talking to reporters in the postgame interview, Halifax skipper Kip Lofton said the team "got solid pitching, good defense and took great team at-bats in a lot of different situations."

"Arthur pitched really well but this was a solid performance by everyone," he said.

On the season Jenkins has compiled a 5-0 mark with a 0.80 ERA in 5 starts. In pitching 45 innings he has 31 strikeouts and 9 walks while limiting the opposition to a .190 batting average.
