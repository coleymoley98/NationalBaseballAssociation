Adeleye Struts Stuff in Win Over Fort Wayne
Monday, June 11th, 1923
Prayad Adeleye of the Canton Legends was one tough customer on the hill today, blanking the Fort Wayne Pioneers 4-0 on 1 hit.

"When he's on, he's tough to hit," said Fort Wayne manager Ethan Bax after the game. "He's just a bulldog out there."

The 16-year-old Legends right-hander registered 7 strikeouts and allowed 1 walk and 1 hit.
