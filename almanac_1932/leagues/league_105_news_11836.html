McKinzie Finalizes Contract Extension with Detroit
Saturday, October 11th, 1930
Fans hailed the signing of Punkorn McKinzie today as the Detroit Lions starting pitcher came to terms with the club.

Set to make $105,000 over the upcoming 5 years, McKinzie signed primarily out of loyalty to the organization, thrilling the most diehard supporters who think there isn't much loyalty in a market driven by free agency.

Over his entire career McKinzie has worked 1931.2 innings with 698 strikeouts en route to a 117-93 record. The 33-year-old also has a 3.36 ERA.
