News Goes From Bad to Worse for Rabbitt
Wednesday, April 30th, 1930
T.J. Rabbitt of the Calgary Wranglers has made it official: he will retire from baseball, effective immediately. Rabbitt had sought a second opinion about a torn rotator cuff. But it only confirmed the earlier diagnosis that put his career in jeopardy. "It made my mind up for me," Rabbitt said. The 28-year-old expressed his gratitude to the organization for their patience during this ordeal. He also thanked the fans for their continuing support.
