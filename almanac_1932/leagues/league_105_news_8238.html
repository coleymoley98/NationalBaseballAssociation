Baltimore Robins Ship Howarth to Boston
Saturday, April 9th, 1927
It came down to the last hour of a self-imposed trade deadline, but the Baltimore Robins and Boston Redcoats finally came to an agreement on a trade that had been rumored for weeks. The trade sends Baltimore's 34-year-old RHP Dwight Howarth and 19-year-old minor league RHP Zayden Lange to Boston in a swap for 25-year-old 2B Sylvain Barbe. Both GMs seemed relieved that the deal had been completed with one stating, "We had to set a deadline to get it done." The players are expected to report to their new teams by tomorrow.

Baltimore will retain a portion of Howarth's remaining contract.

The 34-year-old Howarth has a career record of 67-88 and has fanned 585 batters in 1429 innings with a 3.55 ERA.
