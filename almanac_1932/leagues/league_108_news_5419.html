Woznicki Takes Best Bat Award in July
Friday, August 1st, 1924
At only 20 years of age, Grant Woznicki could very well be having many more months like the last this in his career.

That would be great news for fans of the Vancouver Giants, as July was one to remember for the first baseman and he has captured the Western League Batter of the Month award.

On the month he hit .398 with 8 home runs, 31 RBIs and 27 runs scored.

This season Woznicki has hit 9 home runs and posted a .382 batting average.
