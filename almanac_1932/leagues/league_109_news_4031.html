Robins Get No Resistance from Blue Sox in 10-0 Beat-Down
Wednesday, July 4th, 1923
Kazuhiro Aoki of the Harrisburg Robins spun his magic on the Hamilton Blue Sox today. He blanked them 10-0 on 3 hits, registering 8 strikeouts and no walks.

"The bottom line was I just kept the ball down," Aoki said. "My breaking stuff was down. Everything was down."

Talking to reporters in the postgame interview, Harrisburg skipper Cedric Chéron said the team "got solid pitching, good defense and took great team at-bats in a lot of different situations."

"Kazuhiro pitched really well but this was a solid performance by everyone," he said.

For the year Aoki has a 3-2 record with a 3.34 ERA in 8 starts.
