Big Bat Pays Off, Green Player of the Week
Monday, July 20th, 1925
Omaha's Carl Green would not be denied the Central Baseball League Player of the Week Award for this past week. His consistent stroke, which produced 11 hits in 27 at-bats and a .407 average, made him the clear favorite to win the honor.

"You just go out there and do what you have to do," said Green, "When I'm up, I try to focus and block everything else out of my mind and have a good at-bat. I'm up there to get a hit or advance a runner, not to get caught up in the emotion of the moment. I hope I can win this award every week."

He also tallied 2 home runs, 3 RBIs, and scored 6 runs.

Green is currently batting .320 with 6 home runs, 21 RBIs and 23 runs scored. He has 55 hits in 172 at-bats.
