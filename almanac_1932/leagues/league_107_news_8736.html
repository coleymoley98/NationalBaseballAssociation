Vancouver Giants Clinch First in
Monday, July 18th, 1927
Chi Broadus said it takes "a lot of dedication and sacrifice" to win a division title. That's why he and his Vancouver Giants teammates were so excited to celebrate their championship today.

"It's been a team effort all year long," he said. "You don't get to this point without the guys in the room believing in what we're doing."

That belief has paced the club to a 32-10 record on the season. It is the club's 2nd championship and puts them in position for their 1st Western Baseball League championship.

"It's a talented bunch in that room," manager Danupong Chan-inkaew said pointing to his celebrating players. "I wouldn't bet against us."
