Sams Selected Top Performer in EBL
Monday, July 9th, 1928
It can be difficult for a young player to step out of the shadows and assert himself in the Eastern Baseball League, but last week Lek Sams of the Springfield Falcons reminded everyone just why he's here as he threw up numbers that made him an easy selection for Player of the Week. Sams rocked opposing pitching for a .474 average (9-for-19), 2 home runs and 9 RBIs to come away with the award.

This season Sams is swinging the bat at a .432 rate with 38 hits in 88 at-bats. To date he has hit 8 home runs, scored 22 runs and driven in 28.
