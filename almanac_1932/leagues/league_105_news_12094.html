NBA News: Free Agents File
Monday, October 31st, 1932
Today is the first day of the free agency period.

With the arrival of free agency comes the opportunity for each club across National Baseball Association to propel themselves into the playoffs simply by making a few shrewd, well-calculated signings. General managers have to choose wisely where to spend their dollars. An expensive signing could be the key to a playoff spot or it could handcuff a franchise for years to come.

This year's key free agents include:


Howard Barraza, 26 year-old SS
Randell McClinton, 25 year-old RF
Hubert Green, 28 year-old 2B
Archie Campbell, 28 year-old SS
Clark McGregor, 25 year-old RF
Dustin Cheng, 28 year-old RF
Steve Shupe, 33 year-old C
Samuel Gilliard, 26 year-old CF
Renato Joly, 24 year-old RH SP
Cameron MacAlister, 28 year-old C

Each GM has a different approach to free agency. Many fans enjoy this offseason maneuvering just as much as the regular season as they watch to see how the face of their team changes over the next several months.
