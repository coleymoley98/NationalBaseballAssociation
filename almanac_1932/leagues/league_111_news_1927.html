LeForce Brings Home EBL Batter of Month Honor
Saturday, August 1st, 1931
Ken LeForce is not used to being associated with Batter of the Month-caliber players, but today the Fort Wayne first baseman joined that select company when the Eastern Baseball League announced the results of July's voting.

LeForce totaled 35 hits, 18 walks, 6 home runs, 20 RBIs and 25 runs scored last month to put together a .385 batting average and .482 on-base percentage.

For the year LeForce is batting .348 with 65 hits and 10 home runs. He has scored 39 times and driven in 31.
