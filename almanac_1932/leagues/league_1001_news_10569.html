Hits Blitz for Montana Gustin
Tuesday, March 19th, 1929
Landon Gustin had a special day at Blue Sox Ballpark today, contributing to the Team Montana' 10-2 win and hitting for the cycle along the way.

The 23-year-old center fielder went 5-for-5, raking a single, double, triple and home run against the Team Manitoba.

"Boy, Landon had himself a ballgame, didn't he?" Team manager Tommy Dupuis said later. "You don't see that very often."

For the day, Gustin finished with 2 runs batted in and 3 runs scored to go with his 5 hits.

Landon Gustin hit a two-run home run off Kevin Devlin in the 2nd, doubled in the 5th, singled in the 6th, tripled in the 8th and singled in the 9th.

In 10 games this season Gustin is batting .405 with 15 hits and 2 home runs. He has scored 10 runs and driven in 6.
