Montreal Southpaw Tosses 3-0 Shutout
Tuesday, June 19th, 1928
Daniel Ibarra of the Montreal Royales humiliated the Minnesota Saints at Montreal Grounds today, spinning a complete game shutout and holding the visitors to 7 hits.

"He stayed aggressive and his ball was really moving," said Minnesota manager A.J. Provost, after the game.

Ibarra whiffed 2 and walked 1 in the 3-0 win.

"If I make good pitches, I'm going to get some outs," Ibarra said. "I was making good pitches the entire game."

Ibarra has made 9 starts, tossed 80 innings, struck out 26 batters and walked 25. His record is 7-2 with a 1.46 ERA and he has limited other teams to a .244 batting average.
