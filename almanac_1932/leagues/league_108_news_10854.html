Suspensions Handed Out for Bench-Clearing Brawl
Thursday, June 13th, 1929
Oliver Legault of the Omaha Falcons and Jerome Cullum of the Saskatoon Coyotes will be on the sidelines for a few days. They have been suspended for 4 and 8 games respectively by the league office for causing the bench-emptying free-for-all at the recent Falcons-Coyotes game at Falcons Ballpark.

Neither player had any comment about the suspensions. Both clubs told BNN Legault and Cullum will begin serving them soon.

Legault is currently batting .286 with 2 home runs and 5 RBIs.
