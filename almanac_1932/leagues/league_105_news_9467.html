NBA News: Opening Day
Monday, May 7th, 1928
How sweet it is! National Baseball Association starts play today. It is a new season and a new beginning for all the boys of baseball. Opening Day is here! It is a day of great excitement for everyone in the world of baseball -- players, fans, sportswriters, broadcasters, managers, coaches, scouts, groundkeepers, batboys, ballpark vendors, agents and the owners. Everybody is eagerly awaiting the first pitch of the game and the first home run. Old records will be broken and new marks set. It all gets under way today with this lineup of games:

Philadelphia Liberty @ Ottawa Indians
Toronto Blue Sox @ Wolfeburg Celtics
Boston Redcoats @ Baltimore Robins
Detroit Lions @ Juneau Brasseurs
St. Louis Cardinaux @ Minnesota Saints
Cincinnati Reds @ Quebec Nordiques
