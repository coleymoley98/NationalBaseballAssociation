Mountaineers, Assayas Silence Springfield, 1-0
Friday, July 19th, 1929
A masterful pitching performance carried the Morgantown Mountaineers to a 1-0 victory over Springfield. Fang Assayas held the Falcons scoreless in a complete-game effort.

He permitted 8 hits, had 2 strikeouts and allowed 5 walks.

"Assayas was sharp," Falcons manager Cal Mentink said later. "We never did figure him out."

On the season Assayas has compiled a 1-3 mark with a 5.68 ERA in 5 starts. In pitching 31.2 innings he has 17 strikeouts and 19 walks while limiting the opposition to a .292 batting average.
