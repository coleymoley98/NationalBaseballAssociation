Steelers End Campbell's Streak
Friday, June 19th, 1931
Nontawat Campbell came rolling into today's game against the Hamilton Steelers with a 24-game hitting string, but he left it on the field at Steelers Ballpark.

The 25-year-old Chicagou White Sox center fielder went 0-for-4 against the Steelers as the White Sox fell, 2-1.

"I swung at a couple pitches I probably shouldn't have and I didn't swing at a couple I probably should have," he said, grimacing. "But that's what makes a hit streak so impressive in the first place -- it only takes a couple mistakes to end it all."

Batting .363 to date, Campbell has 2 home runs and 26 RBIs.
