"All we've got to do is get hot and the next thing you know we'll be in the Laurier Cup," said starting pitcher Tarik Fabre. "Everyone in the league knows we're capable of going on a run and can be dangerous."

The club's 28-22 record has guaranteed them a shot in the postseason, where they will be looking for the 1st Atlantic Baseball League championship in franchise history.
