Cazares Batters Baltimore Big Time
Wednesday, May 23rd, 1923
White Sox left fielder Jimmie Cazares had his way with Baltimore pitching today at Comiskey Park 1910 and mauled them for 5 hits in 6 at-bats. His efforts were all in vain, though, as the Robins ruined his great day by toppling the White Sox, 11-9.

"I had a great day, but you've got to give Baltimore credit, they did what they had to do to win," he told reporters after the game. "They weren't very impressed with my 5 hits."

"Oh, we noticed what Cazares did today," Robins skipper Eldin Gabriel said. "He had quite a game. I can tell you this, we will pitch him a little differently the next time we face him."

Jimmie Cazares singled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, singled in the 7th, singled in the 9th and flied out in the 11th.

For the year Cazares has hit at a .368 clip with 5 RBIs, 1 home run and 13 runs scored.
