Juneau Southpaw Tosses 2-0 Shutout
Wednesday, June 11th, 1930
The Juneau Brasseurs shut out the Quebec Nordiques 2-0 today behind the strong pitching of Kevin Haas. Haas surrendered only 4 hits in recording the complete-game victory, and struck out 5.

Following the last out, Haas appeared to celebrate a little too boisterously for Quebec manager Donatien Auger, who could be seen scolding the famously outspoken left-hander as the clubs left the field. Haas's own manager then intervened with some words for his counterpart.

The Nordiques skipper would not comment on the exchange, but at the Brasseurs press conference, Haas confirmed that it was regarding the celebration.

"He basically told me I was being a lousy winner," Haas said.

"And I told him he was being a lousy loser," Juneau manager Joshua Barry added.

This season Haas has worked 67.1 innings with 26 strikeouts en route to a 2-5 record and a 3.07 ERA.
