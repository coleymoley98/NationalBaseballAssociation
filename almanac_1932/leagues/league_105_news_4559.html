More Injury Problems For Detroit's Punkorn McKinzie
Monday, October 1st, 1923
The Detroit Lions announced today that Punkorn McKinzie would be missing for longer than expected as the 26-year-old starting pitcher recovers from a forearm strain. "Punkorn had been working out with the team recently, but felt some discomfort yesterday," the Lions GM explained to the press. "After re-examining the injury, the team's medical staff decided that McKinzie would need extra time to heal. Their estimate is 6 days more recovery time."

The 26-year-old McKinzie has sat down 229 batters on strikes, walked 219 and put up a 3.46 ERA. His career won-lost record is 34-37.
