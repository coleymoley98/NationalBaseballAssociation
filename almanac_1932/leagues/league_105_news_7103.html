Cincinnati Inks Star RP to $14,700 Deal
Wednesday, May 5th, 1926
The Reds and Wachirawit Therrien reached an accord earlier today which brings the reliever to town for 2 years.

Therrien has made 120 games with 12 saves in his career. His record includes 11 wins and 10 losses with a 4.25 ERA and 143 strikeouts in 201.1 innings.
