Weaver Fires 5-0 Shutout in Braves Win
Monday, August 10th, 1931
John Weaver subdued the Detroit Lions, 5-0, pitching a 5-hit gem for the Toronto Braves at Bennett Park.

"He was kind of teasing them with his off-speed stuff, and his fastball was moving everywhere," Toronto skipper Aubrey Nagle said afterward.

Weaver registered 2 strikeouts and issued 2 walks in his superb performance.

"We just didn't swing the bats very well. Or else he just pitched good, whichever way you want to look at it," said Detroit manager Jake Downing after the defeat.

Checking out his stats for the season, Weaver has registered 2 wins and 7 losses in 9 starts, 4.38 ERA and struck out 19 batters in 72 innings.
