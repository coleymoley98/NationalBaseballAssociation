Indians, Markham Stonewall Baltimore, 5-0
Friday, May 22nd, 1925
It wasn't easy but the Ottawa Indians made it look that way.

Starter Shawn Markham shut out the Baltimore Robins despite having what he later called "sub-par" stuff, and the Ottawa Indians cruised to victory, 5-0. Markham allowed only 7 hits and rang up 2 strikeouts but insisted later that he did not deserve primary credit for the win.

"It didn't feel quite right today, but I got a lot of help to get through," he said. "I trusted my catcher and the guys behind me bailed me out more than once when I screwed up."
