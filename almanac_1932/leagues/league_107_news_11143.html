Playoff Bound: Vancouver Secures Wild Card
Saturday, July 20th, 1929
Vancouver Giants players hooted and hollered with excitement today when the club made it official - they've secured a playoff spot.

The title is not yet secured, but the club will be going to the postseason regardless, on the strength of their current 29-15 record.

"Proud of the guys, proud of our effort," said first baseman Theo Giguère. "These guys are hungry to bring the 2nd Bennett Cup to our franchise."
