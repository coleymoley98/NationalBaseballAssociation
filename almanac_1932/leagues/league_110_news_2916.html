Jr. Falcons End Mackinnon's Streak
Thursday, June 30th, 1932
It was the best of both worlds for the Omaha Jr. Falcons today at Omaha Grounds, as they defeated the St. Louis Jr. Cardinaux 5-4 and also snapped Ed Mackinnon's 22-game hitting streak. The 17-year-old St. Louis first baseman failed to get a hit in 4 at-bats.

"Nothing personal against Mackinnon, we just wanted to win the game," said Jr. Falcons skipper Sean Wilkinson. "We did a great job keeping him off-balance and guessing."

When asked by reporters about his streak coming to an end, Mackinnon frowned and said, "I won't lie and say that I'm not disappointed about it, but I'd feel much better if we had won."

To date, Mackinnon has collected 34 hits for a .330 average. He has posted 2 home runs, scored 16 runs and driven in 21 runs.
