Mountaineers Show No Mercy in 9-0 Thrashing of Battalion
Tuesday, July 24th, 1928
Jimmy Strasburg of the Morgantown Mountaineers shut out the North Bay Battalion 9-0, stopped them on 4 hits and had 8 strikeouts and no walks in a strong outing at Juniors Ballpark today.

"He just came out and was on the attack from the first pitch he threw," Morgantown manager James Ferro said later. "When Jimmy stays ahead in the count, he does well. That's the key."

This year Strasburg has 5 wins and 5 losses and a 3.14 ERA.
