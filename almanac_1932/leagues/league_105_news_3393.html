Injury Shuts Down Steward for Season
Sunday, August 28th, 1932
The news was not unexpected, but that didn't make it easier to accept for the Baltimore Robins: right fielder Jonathan Steward is out for the year. Steward suffered a torn labrum on 08/27/1932. The team knew that the chances of seeing him return were slim to none. But today it was made official.

So far this year in 95 games Steward is hitting .298 with 11 home runs, 73 RBIs and 66 runs scored.
