Woznicki Bangs Out 5 Base Hits
Saturday, June 2nd, 1928
Grant Woznicki knows as well as anyone that in baseball, the biggest contracts go to the biggest hitters. At Thorns Ballpark today, he made his case to be considered among that group.

The 24-year-old first baseman hammered 5 hits in 5 chances, though the remarkable effort came even as his Vancouver Giants fell, 12-4, to the Portland Thorns.

"We made some bad pitches and he took advantage of it," Thorns manager Romie Huynh told BNN sportswriters after the game. "Thankfully, we were able to do enough elsewhere that Woznicki wasn't the difference."

Grant Woznicki hit an RBI single in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 5th, singled in the 7th and singled in the 9th.
