1925 ABL Pitcher of the Year Winner Announced
Tuesday, August 25th, 1925
In 1925, the premier pitcher in the Atlantic Baseball League was Mason Honeywell. The Quebec City starter is this year's recipient of the ABL Pitcher of the Year.

He went 8-2 in 13 starts with 116 strikeouts in 101.2 innings and an impressive 1.77 ERA.

He received 7 first place votes out of a possible 14. Crawford Jones of the Montreal Jr. Royales finished second in voting with 7 first place votes, while Stephen McCord of the Montreal Jr. Royales finished third.
