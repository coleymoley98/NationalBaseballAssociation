Celtics End Moore's Streak
Thursday, July 16th, 1925
The 22-game hitting streak of Toronto Blue Sox first baseman Methanon Moore came to an end today at Griffith Stadium 1911. The Wolfeburg Celtics held him hitless in 4 at-bats and also sent the Blue Sox down to defeat, 2-1.

Later, Moore acknowledged to BNN reporters that the hit streak meant something to him.

"It was special," he said, "but I think other people make a bigger deal out of it than I do, or the players do. It was what we're all supposed to do; go out and hit the ball and try to score runs. It was a cool deal, but it's over now. Let's try to start a new one."

Moore is batting .395 with 9 home runs and 49 RBIs this season.
