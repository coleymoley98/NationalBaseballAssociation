Reds No Match for Woodmore, Saints in 12-0 Thumping
Friday, August 10th, 1923
Eric Woodmore of the Minnesota Saints shut out the Cincinnati Reds 12-0, stopped them on 3 hits and had 4 strikeouts and 1 walk in a strong outing at Great American Ball Park today.

"He just came out and was on the attack from the first pitch he threw," Minnesota manager A.J. Provost said later. "When Eric stays ahead in the count, he does well. That's the key."

This year Woodmore has a 11-6 record and a 1.91 ERA.
