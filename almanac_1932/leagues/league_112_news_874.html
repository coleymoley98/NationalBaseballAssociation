ABL Selects Carrier July's Best Starter
Friday, August 1st, 1930
In July, William Carrier tore through the Atlantic Baseball League like a seasoned veteran -- quite impressive for a player only 17 years old.

The young Pointe-Sainte-Anne starter posted a 5-1 mark and an ERA of 1.77 to earn the Pitcher of the Month award. In 45.2 innings he collected 61 strikeouts while limiting the opposition to a .205 average.

To date this year Carrier has compiled a 7-1 mark with a 1.48 ERA in 10 starts.
