WBL Presents Outstanding Pitcher Award
Sunday, August 14th, 1927
The WBL Pitcher of the Year for 1927 has been announced and the Western Baseball League winner is Chi Broadus of the Vancouver Giants.

With a record of 13-1, Broadus was certainly a worthy winner. He made 15 starts and rang up 108.2 innings, 131 strikeouts and a 1.49 ERA.

He received 8 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Ryder Bureau of the Vancouver Giants finished second in voting, while Azman Eason of the Vancouver Giants finished third.
