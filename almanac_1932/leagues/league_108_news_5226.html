Silven Rips Seattle for 5 Hits
Tuesday, July 8th, 1924
5 times is the charm -- at least for Ned Silven.

The Portland Thorns shortstop tore up the Seattle Rainiers to the tune of 5 hits in 6 at-bats at Ballpark of Seattle today. The offensive onslaught from Silven powered the Thorns to a 17-10 win.

"Silven was a tough out today," Seattle manager Sebastian Woodhall said to the press in the postgame interview. "He hit some very good pitches with that quick bat of his. Not an easy out, that's for sure."

Ned Silven hit an RBI double in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, doubled in the 5th, grounded out in the 6th, hit an RBI single in the 7th and singled in the 9th.

Currently Silven is batting .361 with 1 home run and 14 RBIs.
