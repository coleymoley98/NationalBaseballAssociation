McNeely Ups Hit Streak to 3 Consecutive Games
Saturday, March 19th, 1932
Jakkapan McNeely has been on a tear for several weeks, and his hitting streak just keeps getting longer. The 21-year-old Team New York third baseman extended his streak today to 3 games, going 4-for-5 as Team got the best of the Team New England at Ecatepec Memorial Grounds, 13-4.

"Jakkapan has been playing real well lately and we're proud of him," New York manager Sulaiman Merchant said.
Jakkapan McNeely hit an RBI single in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, hit a two-run double in the 4th, singled in the 6th and flied out in the 7th.

For the year McNeely has hit .455 with 1 home run, 12 RBIs and scored 15 runs.
