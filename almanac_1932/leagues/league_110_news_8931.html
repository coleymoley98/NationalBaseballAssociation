Best CBL Reliever Named
Sunday, August 14th, 1927
At the young age of only 16, Cris Rogers has been named the Central Baseball League Reliever of the Year Award winner for 1927.

The future appears bright for the Winnipeg Jr. Thunder relief ace, who captured the award with 5 saves in 14 relief appearances, 53 strikeouts in 47.2 innings and a record of 4-2 to go with his 2.83 ERA.

He received 8 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Edward Leung of the Lincoln Pioneers finished second in voting, while Todd Brafford of the Adams Mounties finished third.
