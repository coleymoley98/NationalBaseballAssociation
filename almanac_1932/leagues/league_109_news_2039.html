Peters Nabs EL Weekly Award
Monday, August 17th, 1931
It's always a thrill for fans of the team and students of the game alike when a superstar in the making shows what he's got. Haden Peters, the Providence Redcoats 23-year-old third baseman, put himself in the spotlight last week making him the clear-cut choice for Eastern League Player of the Week honors.

He ripped 12 hits in 25 at-bats, compiling a .480 average with 3 home runs and 8 RBIs.

Presently Peters is batting .288 and has collected 8 home runs, 37 RBIs, .362 OBP and scored 40 runs.
