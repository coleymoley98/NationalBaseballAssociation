Minnesota Saints Ship Ramirez to Chicagou
Sunday, September 4th, 1932
The Minnesota Saints have made a deal sending 26-year-old RHP Pierce Ramirez to the Chicagou White Sox for 20-year-old minor league 1B Leandro González and 20-year-old minor league 2B Ross Picou. Both sides are said to be pleased with the way the trade worked out.


This year Ramirez has 5 saves, totaled 85.2 innings, allowed 117 base hits and fashioned a 6.09 ERA while racking up 39 strikeouts and a record of 2 wins and 13 losses.
