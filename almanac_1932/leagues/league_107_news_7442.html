Younger Wins WBL Honor
Monday, July 5th, 1926
Josiah Younger burst onto the Western Baseball League stage in a big way last week. This promising rookie third baseman for Vancouver garnered everyone's respect by putting up some remarkable numbers and picking up the Player of the Week trophy.

The 19-year-old haunted opposing pitchers hitting .481 (13-for-27) with 2 home runs and 8 RBIs last week.

Younger is hitting .257 for the season with 4 home runs, 17 RBIs and 11 runs scored. He has 28 hits in 109 at-bats and played in 30 games.
