New Britain Star, ABL Pitcher of the Year Winner
Friday, August 20th, 1926
Tom Teixeira joined an elite group of pitchers today by picking up the Atlantic Baseball League ABL Pitcher of the Year for 1926.

The 16-year-old hurler finished the year 12-3 for the New Britain Yankees, piling up 122.1 innings, registering 134 strikeouts and walking 26 on his way to a solid 2.06 ERA.

He received 12 first place votes out of a possible 14. Giorgi Lallier of the St. John's Mariners finished second in voting with 2 first place votes, while Ashley Matcham of the Montreal Jr. Royales finished third.
