Auger Passes Polk, Is Home Run King
Thursday, May 28th, 1925
With home run number 56, Noah Auger of the Ottawa Indians has set a new all-time record. The previous record was held by Somchai Polk.

The record-setting home run puts an exclamation point on an exciting pursuit that has captivated fans everywhere. None were more interested in the chase than those in Ottawa, and the hometown crowd was rewarded for their devotion by getting to host the historic blast.

"What a wild ride it's been," said Auger after the game. "I hope now we can get away from the will-he/won't-he stuff and get back to playing baseball."

Noah Auger hit an RBI double in the 1st and homered off Thurman Richardson in the 3rd.

Auger is batting .397 this season in 17 games with 1 home run, 10 RBIs and 12 runs scored. He has 23 hits in 58 at-bats.
