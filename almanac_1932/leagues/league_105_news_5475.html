Morales's Hit Streak Over
Sunday, August 10th, 1924
Maybe it really is taboo to talk about a hitting streak before it's over. Hours after speaking to the press about his 29-game hitting streak, Boston shortstop Carmen Morales went hitless in 4 at-bats today at Lincoln Park.

The sudden hitting drought came as the Redcoats took the 9-0 win over the Ottawa Indians.

"Nope, it's not magic," Morales said in his postgame press conference. "Nobody jinxed me. Streaks don't last forever. I've heard about it every day for weeks now. My roomie told me to get a hit before every game and wished me luck. I just ran out of hits."

Morales is swinging at a .379 pace on the year with 3 home runs, 46 RBIs and 55 runs scored. He has played in 77 games.
