Failai Batters Team Big Time
Tuesday, March 3rd, 1925
A jubilant Issara Failai joined the postgame press conference at Yokohama Memorial Grounds today to offer some unusual words of encouragement -- for the Team Pallister pitching staff he had just vanquished.

"It may not have looked like it," said the Team Columbia shortstop, "but I saw some awful good pitches today. If luck hadn't been on my side it might have turned out a lot different."

That would not have been the conclusion of anyone who saw his dominant 5-for-6 hitting performance, which led the Columbia to a 23-5 win.

"He made it look easy," said Ryan Vézina, manager of the Pallister. "We never figured out what to do with him. Obviously."

Issara Failai hit an RBI single in the 1st, hit an RBI double in the 2nd, hit a two-run double in the 3rd, grounded out in the 5th, walked in the 6th, singled in the 7th and singled in the 8th.
