20-Game Hitting Streak for Bélanger
Thursday, May 24th, 1923
The number just keeps getting higher.

Boston shortstop Nicolas Bélanger has now extended his hitting string to 20 games. He did it today with 3 hits in 5 at-bats as the Boston took the 10-2 win over the Ottawa Indians at Lincoln Park.

"Who knows how long it will go on?" Ottawa manager Owen Turcotte said. "He's certainly tough to pitch to right now."
Nicolas Bélanger singled in the 2nd, singled in the 3rd, singled in the 4th, flied out in the 7th and grounded out in the 8th.

So far this year Bélanger has hit .460 with 4 home runs, 10 RBIs and 15 runs scored.
