Duchesneau Puts Spell on Team
Saturday, March 8th, 1924
In an outstanding mound performance, Team Manitoba starter Cédric Duchesneau fired a complete-game shutout against the Team Quebec at San Francisco Memorial Fields today.

Duchesneau was the key to the Manitoba's 2-0 win. The 27-year-old right-hander was in high spirits on the mount for much of the game, his mood largely undented by the 8 hits and no walks he allowed.

"8 hits?" he said with a laugh following the game. "Listen, I'd be happy with a 30-hitter as long as it was a shutout."
