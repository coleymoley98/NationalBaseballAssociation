It's Over: Jr. Royales Stop Dostie
Sunday, July 1st, 1923
The 20-game hitting streak is over.

Drummondville Voltigeurs first baseman Derek Dostie failed to get a hit today against the Montreal Jr. Royales and had his consecutive-game hitting streak broken. The 16-year-old went 0-for-4 in his club's 7-4 victory over the Jr. Royales at Ballpark of Drummondville.

"I think I only made it to 20 games because I got lucky once or twice," he said. "So I guess I can't complain too much that I got unlucky and broke the streak."

Dostie is batting .356 this season in 29 games with 10 home runs, 29 RBIs and 24 runs scored. He has 42 hits in 118 at-bats.
