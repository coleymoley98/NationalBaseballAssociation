Red-Hot Rachal Cools Off Sherbrooke
Sunday, August 21st, 1932
There was no spell, no black magic -- just good pitching.

Dee Dee Rachal of the Harrisburg Robins blanked the Sherbrooke Nordiques, 8-0, at Nordiques Ballpark today, and he did it the old-fashioned way. After finishing the 3-hit, complete-game shutout, he was all smiles.

"I threw a lot of my pitches for strikes in this game," Rachal said. "I mixed it up and tried keeping these guys off balance."

Rachal walked 3 and struck out 6 in the victory.

"He was throwing his breaking ball for strikes and we never capitalized on any of his mistakes," Nordiques manager Michael Halliday said later.

This season Rachal has crafted a 6-7 mark with a 5.33 ERA in 125 innings. Opposing clubs are batting .292 against him and he has 44 strikeouts.
