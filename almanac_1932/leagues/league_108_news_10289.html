Lamontagne of Edmonton Voted Top Manager in WL
Sunday, September 23rd, 1928
Nattaporn Lamontagne skippered the Edmonton Eskimos to a very successful year and has been acclaimed as the 1928 Western League Manager of the Year.

He kept it short and sweet in the press conference as he accepted the award. "Thanks, but it wasn't just me out there this season."

The Eskimos finished the regular season in first place in the with a 53-29 record.
