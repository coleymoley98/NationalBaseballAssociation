Cherry Fires 2-0 Shutout for Buffalo
Sunday, July 3rd, 1927
Karter Cherry had the Springfield Falcons dancing to his tune today with a sparkling 2-0 shutout performance for the Buffalo Rapids. He gave up 3 hits, issued 2 walks and had 5 strikeouts.

Springfield hitters were bewildered throughout the game by his delivery.

"When you face a guy who hides the ball like he does, it takes a couple of at-bats to try to find his release point," opposing manager Cal Mentink said. "We just never figured him out."

This year Cherry is hitting .233 with no home runs.
