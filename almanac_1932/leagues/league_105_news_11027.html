Chicagou Wins Lottery for CL MacGinnis
Friday, July 5th, 1929
The Frontenacs today announced that they have come to terms with Dom MacGinnis, a 37-year-old closer they've been scouting for a while.

MacGinnis will earn $7,300 a year over 1 year and is expected to play a key role with the club.

In the 1929 season MacGinnis has worked 13.1 innings in 8 relief appearances and earned 4 saves. He has dished out 5 strikeouts and walked 4 with a 1-1 mark and a 3.38 ERA.
