Wolfeburg Celtics Ship Servais to Philadelphia
Friday, August 17th, 1928
Both sides appear content with the trade finalized today that will send 24-year-old CF Warawut Servais to the Philadelphia Liberty, while the Wolfeburg Celtics will receive 20-year-old minor league 1B Raphaël Fortin, 20-year-old minor league 1B Marcel Lebel, 24-year-old minor league C Will Rines and 20-year-old minor league C Ward Boehmer.

"This is a move that filled a few gaps in our roster," said Wittawat Kraisil, Celtics general manager.

For his part, the Liberty GM said the trade was made "in the best interest of our organization's future." BNN analysts reported that on paper the trade seemed to be a plus for both ball clubs.


Lifetime Servais has played in 206 games, batted .292 with 10 home runs and 200 base hits.
