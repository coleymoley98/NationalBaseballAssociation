Polk Gets Number 1500
Tuesday, August 4th, 1925
"It would have been a lot nicer if my 1500th hit had won the game," said Somchai Polk after the Chicagou White Sox had been tripped up by the Toronto Blue Sox, 9-5, spoiling his big day. The White Sox left fielder gained access to the exclusive 1500-hit club by going 2-for-4 in the game played at Griffith Stadium 1911.

"The standing ovation by the fans is not something I will ever forget," Polk said to the press after the game. "I think even the Blue Sox fans were cheering me."

Lifetime Polk is batting .334 with 251 doubles, 203 triples, 58 home runs and 658 RBIs in 1186 games.
