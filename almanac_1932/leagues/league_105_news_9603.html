Royales Get One to Win
Friday, June 8th, 1928
Daniel Ibarra kept the Cincinnati Reds hitters off balance all game long as the Montreal Royales pitcher blanked the Reds, 1-0.

Ibarra gave up 7 hits, struck out 2 and walked 5. In the postgame press conference, the left-hander was reluctant to talk about the outing at length.

"It was a nice win in a long season," he said. "I don't want to dwell too much on it."

So far this season Ibarra has a 5-2 record, 7 starts, 62 innings, 17 strikeouts and a 1.74 ERA.
