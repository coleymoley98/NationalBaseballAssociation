Injury Setback for Frontenacs St-Onge
Tuesday, April 23rd, 1929
Things have gotten worse for Surapong St-Onge of the Chicagou Frontenacs. The 22-year-old first baseman will not be back as soon as originally expected. St-Onge is undergoing treatment for an elbow strain, but his doctors have expanded his recovery time to at least 1-2 weeks. An unidentified team official told BNN, "Surapong tried to rush his rehab and suffered a setback."
