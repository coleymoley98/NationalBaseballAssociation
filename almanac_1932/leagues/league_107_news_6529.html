Broadus Picked as July's Pitcher of the Month
Saturday, August 1st, 1925
There's something about a young rookie winning the Western Baseball League Pitcher of the Month trophy that sends shock waves through the league. Such is the case with Chi Broadus, a 15-year-old starter for the Vancouver Giants.

In July, he topped off the month with a 5-1 mark and 1.60 ERA in 9 starts, tossed 56.1 innings, fanned 56 and held opposing teams to a .225 average.

Thus far this season Broadus has posted a 10-2 record, started 17 games, struck out 106 batters in 109 innings and compiled a 1.82 ERA.
