Dawkins Fires Shutout in Indians Victory
Tuesday, July 31st, 1923
Ottawa Indians fans were pleased to see so many goose eggs up on the scoreboard as Dylan Dawkins blanked the Toronto Blue Sox, 2-0 at Lincoln Park today. Dawkins stopped them on 4 hits while walking 2 and striking out 2.

"Once he gets in a groove, he can be dynamite," Indians skipper Owen Turcotte said. "He was on top of his game in this one."

This season Dawkins has crafted a 5-10 mark with a 4.45 ERA in 117.1 innings. Opposing clubs are batting .315 against him and he has 24 strikeouts.
