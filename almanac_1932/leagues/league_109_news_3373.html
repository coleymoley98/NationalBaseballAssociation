Lapierre Hit String Snapped at 29
Wednesday, August 24th, 1932
All good things must come to an end -- and so it was today for José Lapierre as he saw his 29-game hitting streak end. He went hitless in 3 at-bats at the hands of the Tecumseh Braves.

It wasn't all bad for the 21-year-old third baseman and the Columbus Mets however, as they defeated Tecumseh 3-1 at Rampage Ballpark.

When asked about his hitting streak, a smiling Lapierre laughed and said, "I guess my luck ran out today."

This year Lapierre is batting .321 with 18 home runs and 72 RBIs. In 77 games he has scored 80 times.
