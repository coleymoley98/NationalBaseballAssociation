Reliever Narcisse August's Pitcher of the Month
Tuesday, September 1st, 1931
Any time a rookie makes a splash on the scene, it's got to be great for his career potential. Just ask Jame Narcisse, who put together a stellar month out of the pen. The Trois-Rivieres Royales reliever, 24, kept the opposition off balance all month as he held them to a .153 average and walked away with the Eastern League Pitcher of the Month award for August.

Narcisse posted a 4-0 mark and a 1.83 ERA in 12 relief appearances. In 19.2 innings, he had 5 saves, 29 strikeouts, and 5 walks.

During this season Narcisse has 37 relief appearances and registered 15 saves. Batters are hitting him at a .211 rate and his season record is 11-2 with a 3.39 ERA. He has logged 61 innings and struck out 94.
