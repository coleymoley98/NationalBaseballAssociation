Suspensions Handed Out for Bench-Clearing Brawl
Thursday, May 15th, 1930
Santo Vivas of the Toronto Blue Sox and Andrew Patterson of the Boston Redcoats received notification from the league commissioner's office that they had been suspended for 4 and 8 games respectively for their part in the on-field ruckus in a recent game at Griffith Stadium 1911.
