Injury Shuts Down Hairston for Season
Monday, August 18th, 1930
The news was not unexpected, but that didn't make it easier to accept for the Philadelphia Mets: starting pitcher Khai Hairston is out for the year. Hairston suffered radial nerve compression on 08/15/1930. The team knew that the chances of seeing him return were slim to none. But today it was made official.

In season stats Hairston shows a 0-4 record in 6 games, 23.1 innings, 7 strikeouts and a 10.41 ERA.
