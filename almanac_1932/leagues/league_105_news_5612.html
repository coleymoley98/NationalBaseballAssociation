Nagai's Treatment Not Going Well, Suffers Setback
Monday, September 1st, 1924
Tsuneto Nagai was taking it slow and easy nursing biceps tendinitis back to good health, when he suffered a setback. According to a Montreal team official, "Tsuneto was making excellent progress and it appeared he would return to the squad ahead of schedule. But then he reinjured it in a light workout." Club doctors now say the 32-year-old Royales right fielder will be out at least another 3 weeks.

This year Nagai is hitting .266 with 7 home runs and 33 RBIs to his credit. He has gotten on base at a .345 clip and scored 40 runs.
