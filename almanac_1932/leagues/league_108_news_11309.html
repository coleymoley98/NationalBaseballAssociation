Falcons Take It on Chin, Lose, 14-0, to Winnipeg
Friday, August 9th, 1929
Magic was in the air and on the mound at Thunder Stadium today as Mason Hough held the Omaha Falcons spellbound in his 14-0 shutout victory. The Winnipeg Thunder pitcher went the distance, rang up 9 strikeouts, walked 2 and held the Falcons to 2 hits to garner the win.

"He just had us in his thrall," Omaha manager Kiatisuk Sanders said later. "We couldn't put anything together against him."

On his stats sheet for the year Hough shows a 7-6 record, 13 starts, 106.1 innings, 66 strikeouts and a 3.39 ERA.
