Pittsburgh Quakers Ship Ashcroft to St. Louis
Sunday, July 13th, 1930
Pittsburgh and St. Louis have agreed to a trade today. 26-year-old SS D.J. Ashcroft will go to the Cardinaux. In exchange the Quakers will receive 26-year-old 2B Sonny Dagbo and 20-year-old minor league C Anuman Chamberlain.


Ashcroft is swinging at a .340 pace on the year with 3 home runs, 30 RBIs and 38 runs scored. He has played in 56 games.

So far in 1930 Dagbo is batting .301 with no home runs, 10 RBIs and 5 runs scored. He has 22 hits in 73 at-bats and a .362 OBP.
