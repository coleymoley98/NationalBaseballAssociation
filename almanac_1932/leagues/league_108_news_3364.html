Simpson Misses Team Bus
Monday, August 22nd, 1932
Word out of Thorns camp today is that Thomas Simpson might be fined by the team or benched for the next game as punishment for missing the team bus. "It's unacceptable," said manager Charlie Fleury. "He should know better and should take responsibility for his actions. Something needs to be done to curb this behavior before it becomes a real problem." Pressed for a comment, Simpson simply said, "I overslept. It happens."
