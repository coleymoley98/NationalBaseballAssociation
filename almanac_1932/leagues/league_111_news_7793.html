Eli Sinclair Captures EBL EBL Player of the Year
Friday, August 20th, 1926
The future looks extremely bright for Eli Sinclair. At the age of 17, many players are still trying to find their way in Eastern Baseball League.

Sinclair has not only found his way, he's already established himself as one of the game's brightest stars, today being named the winner of the Eastern Baseball League EBL Player of the Year.

"I didn't expect to hit .369 this year, that's for sure," the third baseman said.

The North Bay Battalion star also put up a .416 on-base percentage this year, with 66 hits, 14 doubles, 2 triples, 10 home runs, 35 RBIs and 38 runs scored.

He received 4 first place votes out of a possible 10. Weston Nester of the Buffalo Rapids finished second in voting with 3 first place votes, while Santipab Wohlers of the Fort Wayne Pioneers finished third with 2 first place votes.
