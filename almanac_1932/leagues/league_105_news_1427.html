Cincinnati Third Baseman Hurt
Wednesday, June 10th, 1931
Cincinnati fans will be disappointed to learn that Eli Sinclair suffered back spasms while running the bases in today's game. After the doctor informed the Cincinnati Reds manager that Sinclair was expected to miss 5-6 weeks, the manager told the press that the rest of the team would have to step up their play until the third baseman could return. "You can't make excuses in baseball, because there's always a game tomorrow," said the team's skipper.
