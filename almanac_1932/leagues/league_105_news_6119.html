Beauséjour Wins NL Player of the Week Prize
Monday, June 8th, 1925
One of the league's most exciting young players, J.R. Beauséjour of the Juneau Brasseurs earned himself National League Player of the Week honors with a standout performance in last week's games.

For the week the 21-year-old right fielder had a lofty .438 average (7-for-16), collecting 2 home runs, 5 RBIs and 3 runs scored.

In 24 games this year Beauséjour has posted a .291 average and put up these numbers: 7 home runs, 16 RBIs and 13 runs scored.
