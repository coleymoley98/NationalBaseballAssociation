Cazares Collects Player of the Week Award
Monday, May 28th, 1923
It can be difficult for a young player to step out of the shadows and assert himself in the Canadian League, but last week Jimmie Cazares of the Chicagou White Sox reminded everyone just why he's here as he threw up numbers that made him an easy selection for Player of the Week. Cazares rocked opposing pitching for a .481 average (13-for-27), 2 home runs and 8 RBIs to come away with the award.

In his stats this season, Cazares has posted a batting average of .333 with 23 hits in 69 at-bats, 2 home runs and 9 RBIs. He has also scored 16 runs, walked 3 times and compiled a .361 OBP.
