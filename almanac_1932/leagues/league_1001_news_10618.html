Best Pitcher Presented to Team Star
Tuesday, April 9th, 1929
The Team Montana have a rising superstar or maybe even a budding Hall-of-Famer in the making.
It's Panuwat Sumonrat, this year's Canadian Senior Baseball Tournament Best Pitcher selection. This blossoming hurler wowed fans, peers and sportswriters with a fabulous performance.

Sumonrat compiled a 3-0 record in 4 starts, tallied 20 strikeouts in 36 innings and recorded a 0.50 ERA. Other clubs managed only a .194 batting average against him.

He received 17 first place votes out of a possible 18. Jeremy Self of the Team Minnesota finished second in voting with 1 first place vote, while Thomas Guérin of the Team Oregon finished third.
