Detroit Clinches Wild Card
Monday, September 14th, 1931
As the clock struck midnight in the Detroit Lions clubhouse, the celebration showed no signs of slowing down. The Lions were certainly ready to celebrate their National League wild card berth.

"It's time to let off a little steam after a grind of a season," said starting pitcher Panuwat Sumonrat. "We may act a bit like teenagers tonight celebrating, but we'll be back to being big leaguers tomorrow."

With a 60-51 record, Detroit will be seeking its 5th National Baseball Association championship.
