Injury Worsens, Return Delayed For Rebels Dostie
Thursday, August 15th, 1929
"It's worse than we thought." That was the news from the Rebels clubhouse today as it was learned that first baseman Derek Dostie will be out for an added 2-3 months as a result of a recent injury. His treatment for a torn quad will have to be extended. Initially it didn't seem the injury was a major concern, but the Albany first baseman told team trainers of some discomfort yesterday morning, and upon examination it was determined to be a more serious injury. "We'd rather take this thing slowly and make sure Derek gets back onto the field healthy," said the team's head trainer.

Derek Dostie is expected to undergo surgery in Albany(New York) tomorrow.

So far this year Dostie is batting .355 with 15 home runs, 45 RBIs and 36 runs scored.
