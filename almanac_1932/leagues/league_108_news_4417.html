The Omaha Falcons are champions of the , but don't plan the parade quite yet.

"It's a nice accomplishment, but we haven't done anything until we win the World Series," said starting pitcher Marcello Thébault. "We're going to celebrate this for sure, but the real celebration is still to come for fans in Omaha."
