Veteran Jenkins Agrees to 1-Year Deal
Thursday, January 18th, 1923
Jay Jenkins stepped before the podium this afternoon with New York officials flanking him on both sides as he doffed the Rebels cap and announced he'd reached an agreement to play in New York for 1 year.

The deal, an estimated $5,900 in total, is widely seen as a positive move for an organization and an upgrade.

Jenkins has appeared in 294 games in his career, picked up 54 saves, tossed 543.2 innings and posted a 36-33 record. He has a lifetime ERA of 2.73.
