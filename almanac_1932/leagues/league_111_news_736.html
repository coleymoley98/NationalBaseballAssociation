Falcons Sit Atop West
Wednesday, July 16th, 1930
Fans of the Springfield Falcons are certainly optimistic regarding their team's chances to become Adams Cup champions, after it became official that the club has won the West.

"I think our fans think we're the best team in the league and you know what? Our guys think the same thing," said starting pitcher Brodie Stevens. "We've got a real good chance to take this title."

It would be the 1st league championship for the club, which has won the West for the 6th time.

Manager Cal Mentink said he's spent many years in pro ball, but there's "just something special" about his current group of players.

"This team has got what it takes to be a champion," he said.
