Guérin Baffles Juneau Batters
Friday, August 24th, 1928
Goose eggs were the order of the day for the Juneau Brasseurs as starter Thomas Guérin shut out the Juneau Brasseurs 1-0 at Wrigley Field 1928. The Chicagou pitcher struck out 7 and walked 4 while allowing 6 hits.

"Guérin was good but he wasn't flawless," Juneau manager Jake Downing said later. "We had a few opportunities to make something happen and we didn't do it. Not to take anything away from Chicagou, but this is on us."

Guérin has started 22 times this year, pitched 182 innings, fanned 100 batters and walked 78. His current record is 14 wins and 6 losses with a 3.31 ERA.
