Pioneers Win Division Finals
Friday, July 31st, 1925
Peter Rougely sparked his Fort Wayne Pioneers past the pesky North Bay Battalion in a closely-contested Division Finals.

Fort Wayne topped North Bay 8-1 at Battalion Ballpark to decide the series, 3-1

For his efforts, Rougely was rewarded with the series MVP trophy for his .529 batting average, 6 RBIs and 2 runs scored.

"You never want to leave anything on the field," Rougely said. "You never want to miss a play of defense you could have made, or give up an extra base you could have gotten, because in a series this tight you never know what you'll need to win."

The Fort Wayne Pioneers don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Canton Legends - Morgantown Mountaineers series. That series is tied at 2 games apiece. The Adams Cup schedule will be announced after the Division Finals finishes.
