Tonkanyaa 5-for-6 Against Thorns
Thursday, July 25th, 1929
It was high-fives all around for Atchawut Tonkanyaa at Thorns Ballpark today.

He got them from his teammates and he got them at the plate during an outstanding hitting performance against the Portland Thorns. Tonkanyaa smashed 5 hits in 6 at-bats on his monster day, leading his club to a 10-5 win.

"Atchawut was hitting everything," said Coyotes manager Maurice Wigglesworth. "It really was a special game for him."

In the opposing dugout, Thorns manager Romie Huynh told BNN beat writers that Tonkanyaa "ambushed" the club.

"Obviously we didn't have much success against him, but we'll have another chance," he said. "Hopefully, Tonkanyaa oversleeps next time."

Atchawut Tonkanyaa singled in the 1st, singled in the 4th, singled in the 4th, singled in the 6th, singled in the 8th and flied out in the 9th.

On the year Tonkanyaa has hit .500 with no home runs, 4 RBIs and scored 8 runs.
