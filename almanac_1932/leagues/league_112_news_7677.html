Hartford's Simoneau Snags July Batting Title
Sunday, August 1st, 1926
When Jay Simoneau stepped up to the plate in July he did it with confidence, and today he was honored with the Atlantic Baseball League Batter of the Month award.

The young Hartford first baseman posted a .391 batting average with 10 home runs, 33 RBIs and scored 29 runs. Simoneau also worked pitchers for 15 walks to post a .485 on-base percentage.

For the year Simoneau is batting .359 with 61 hits and 11 home runs. He has scored 48 times and driven in 48.
