Minnesota First Baseman NL's Best Batter in July
Friday, August 1st, 1930
Minnesota Saints fans believe they've got a talented young star in Taradin Boulay.

He did nothing to dissuade that notion in July, as he hit .385 with 4 home runs, 25 RBIs and 22 runs scored.

The end result was the first baseman being named the National League Batter of the Month.

This season Boulay has 8 home runs and a .397 batting average.
