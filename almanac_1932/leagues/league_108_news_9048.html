Western League Picks August's Best Batter
Thursday, September 1st, 1927
For a rookie just breaking in, Sam Chabot certainly put together a successful month. It was good enough to win the WL Batter of the Month award for August.

The 27-year-old left fielder for the Vancouver Giants hit .422 with 9 home runs, 31 RBIs and 23 runs scored.

In his season totals, Chabot has 17 home runs, .338 BA, .455 OBP, 56 RBIs and 57 runs scored. He has played in 52 games.
