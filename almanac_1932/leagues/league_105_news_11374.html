Cincinnati Draws Blanks Against Richardson
Tuesday, August 20th, 1929
Minnesota Saints starter Kitsada Richardson really did a number on the Cincinnati Reds today, shutting them out 6-0 on 5 hits in a complete game victory. He issued 1 walk and had 5 strikeouts.

"We didn't have good at-bats," Cincinnati manager Hoot Sinkus. "I don't make excuses, but when we were playing the game, our energy didn't show. That comes from either extremely good pitching or extremely lousy hitting and seeing as our team has a bunch of good hitters, I have to tip my cap to Richardson."

Up to now Richardson has recorded a record of 9-9. In 21 starts he has fanned 77 batters in 178 innings with a 2.98 ERA.
