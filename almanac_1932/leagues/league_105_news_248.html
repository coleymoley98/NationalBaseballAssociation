Rehab Road Tough For Frontenacs Warawut Servais
Tuesday, May 13th, 1930
Chicagou Frontenacs center fielder Warawut Servais informed reporters that he will be missing additional time because of a setback in his recovery from a tight hamstring. "It's a bit frustrating, sure," Servais admitted. "I want to be out on the field. At the same time, I don't want to make things worse and miss even more time because I'm pushing it too hard." Frontenacs doctors told BNN Servais would not return for 4 days.

So far this year Servais is hitting .233 with 1 home run.
