Coyotes Get No Resistance from Eskimos in 11-0 Beat-Down
Saturday, July 5th, 1930
The Edmonton Eskimos were almost helpless today in their 11-0 loss to the Saskatoon Coyotes and hurler Chitpanya Korkorat. Korkorat kept the Eskimos confused all game, spinning a 5-hit shutout.

"Tip your hat to Korkorat," Edmonton manager Nattaporn Lamontagne said in his postgame interview. "He kept us off balance all night."

The 23-year-old left-hander had 2 walks and 4 strikeouts in a strong performance.

"It just happened to be one of those games where even when I made mistakes, they either fouled them off or someone would make a fine defensive play," Korkorat said. "It was just one of those games that things went my way."

Korkorat has made 7 starts so far this year and put up a 2-5 won-lost record with a 3.70 ERA. In 65.2 innings he has allowed 70 hits, walked 37 and struck out 40.
