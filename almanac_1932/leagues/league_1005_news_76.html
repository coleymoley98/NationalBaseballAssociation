Vogelzang Torches Dakota for 5 Hits
Monday, March 3rd, 1930
Scott Vogelzang had an answer for everything the Team Dakota threw at him today at Tokyo Memorial Coliseum. The Team right fielder went 5-for-6 in the game to put his name in the record book and put the Ontario on top, 15-5.

Summing up his batting philosophy to BNN writers after the game, Vogelzang said, "I just see the ball and I hit it."

Scott Vogelzang hit an RBI triple in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, singled in the 3rd, doubled in the 5th, doubled in the 6th and flied out in the 8th.
