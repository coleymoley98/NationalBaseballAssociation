Broncos Take It on Chin, Lose, 11-0, to Ranier
Friday, July 13th, 1923
It wasn't easy but Bentley Lundeen made it look that way.

Lundeen silenced the Boise Broncos, 11-0, in a Ranier Seahawks triumph at Ballpark of Ranier today. Despite the appearance of dominance, Lundeen said later that he felt like he was struggling all game. He gave up no walks and 4 hits.

"It didn't feel quite right today, but I made the decision to bear down and work through it," he said. "I made a few mistakes but it goes to show what you can do with grit and not much else."

This season Lundeen has worked 55.1 innings with 65 strikeouts en route to a 6-0 record and a 3.09 ERA.
