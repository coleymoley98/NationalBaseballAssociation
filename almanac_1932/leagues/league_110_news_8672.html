Winnipeg Left Fielder Nets CBL Award
Monday, July 11th, 1927
The Central Baseball League presented its Player of the Week Award to Winnipeg Jr. Thunder left fielder Laurent Ruel.

"His numbers speak for themselves," a club publicist said. "He's been really seeing the ball well."

Indeed, Ruel has. Last week he went 14 for 23, batted .609 with 3 home runs and 10 RBIs.

This season Ruel is hitting .364 with 44 hits, 8 home runs, 27 RBIs, scored 36 runs and played in 36 games.
