Juneau Takes Division Finals in Deciding Game
Sunday, August 1st, 1926
The sweet swing of Hunter Wogoman has led his team through the Division Finals.

The Juneau Jr. Brasseurs star earned series MVP honors as his club downed the Canton Legends to advance to the Adams Cup.

The Jr. Brasseurs won the final game at Juniors Ballpark by a score of 5-3 and the series 3-2.

"I'm seeing the ball well and happy to contribute to a win," said Wogoman. "I hope it continues for the rest of the playoffs."

For the series, Wogoman hit .444 with a .444 on-base percentage. He had 1 home runs, drove in 2 RBI and scored 3 runs.

The Juneau Jr. Brasseurs will play against the Buffalo Rapids in the next round. Buffalo won their series against the Fort Wayne Pioneers 3 games to 0. The Adams Cup schedule will be announced after the Division Finals finishes.
