National League Announces July's Best Batter
Friday, August 1st, 1924
When Joshua Comtois stepped up to the plate in July he did it with confidence, and today he was honored with the National League Batter of the Month award.

The young Detroit shortstop posted a .372 batting average with 4 home runs, 29 RBIs and scored 19 runs. Comtois also worked pitchers for 5 walks to post a .396 on-base percentage.

This season Comtois has hit at a .376 clip and produced 50 runs batted in, 5 home runs and 46 runs.
