Playoff Bound: St. John's Secures Wild Card
Friday, July 20th, 1928
St. John's Mariners fans had reason to rejoice tonight, as the team is heading to the Atlantic Baseball League playoffs.

With a record of 29-17, St. John's is guaranteed a spot in the postseason, although it remains to be officially decided whether or not that's as Atlantic champions or in a wild card position.

"We have fantastic fans and it feels good to give them something to cheer about," said closer Rafaël Paquin. "Hopefully there's a lot more celebrating to come."
