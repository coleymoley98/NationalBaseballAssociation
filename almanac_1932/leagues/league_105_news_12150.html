Philadelphia-Toronto Trade: Straughter for Walizer
Saturday, December 10th, 1932
Philadelphia and Toronto have agreed to a trade today. 30-year-old 2B Kenzie Straughter will go to the Blue Sox. In exchange the Mets will receive 23-year-old minor league RHP Sergio Halloway, 18-year-old minor league SS Francesco McCosh and 22-year-old minor league RF Zane Walizer.


Straughter has a lifetime .325 average and .368 on-base percentage in his career with 320 hits, 51 doubles, 16 triples, 9 home runs, 132 RBIs and 128 runs scored.
