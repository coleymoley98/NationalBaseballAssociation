Acadiens End Blanchette's Streak
Sunday, July 14th, 1929
Ruell Blanchette's 25-game hitting streak has now come and gone. It was snapped today as the Halifax Highlanders suffered a 8-3 setback against the Pointe-Sainte-Anne Acadiens.

Blanchette went without a hit in 2 trips to the plate. He was visibly upset during a postgame press conference, but it appeared that the streak had little to do with it.

"The hitting streak was one of those things where it was fun while it lasted, but I'm not going to lose any sleep about it tonight," he said. "On the other hand, I am going to lose sleep about dropping this game today."

Currently Blanchette is batting .422 with 5 home runs, 40 RBIs and 38 runs scored.
