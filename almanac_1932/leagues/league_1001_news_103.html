McInnis Claims #1 Spot in Record Book
Saturday, March 15th, 1930
Phuwanart McInnis has had the all-time home run record held by Pum Beasley in his sights for quite a while now.

As of today, it belongs to him. At Blue Sox Ballpark, he launched home run number 7 to surpass Beasley in the record book and complete his long-watched pursuit.

"It doesn't take away our focus on winning the championship," said the Team star, "but I'll admit I'm excited about this accomplishment."

Phuwanart McInnis grounded out in the 1st, flied out in the 2nd, flied out in the 5th, flied out in the 7th and homered off Kittichai Kejmanee in the 9th.

On the season, McInnis is batting .233 with 2 home runs and 7 RBIs. He has played in 7 games.
