In an outstanding outing, Omaha Falcons starter Martino Gutierrez tamed the Winnipeg Thunder with a 2-hit shutout at Falcons Ballpark today. The Omaha hurler went the distance, finishing with 7 strikeouts and 4 walks in the 4-0 win.
