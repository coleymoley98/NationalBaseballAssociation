Patterson Beats White Sox for 100th Win
Saturday, July 6th, 1929
History was made today at Sportsmans Park 1911 -- Nicola Patterson won game #100 to seal his name in the league record books. The Baltimore ace downed the Chicagou White Sox 10-6 to gain entrance into the elite 100 Club.

"I was wound up at first. I think I threw too hard," remarked Patterson, "I had to calm down and go out there and pitch... not just throw. I think I got better as the game wore on. I started to think less about 100 wins and more about doing what I had to do."

Patterson has fashioned a 100-105 won-lost record in his career with a 4.15 ERA.
