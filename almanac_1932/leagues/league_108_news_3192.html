Portland's Rodríguez Snags July Batting Title
Monday, August 1st, 1932
Cookie Rodríguez may be much younger than other hitters in the Western League, but he performed like a seasoned veteran in July to win the Batter of the Month award.

The 23-year-old Portland left fielder hit .482 with 7 home runs, 32 RBIs and 32 runs scored.

So far this season, Rodríguez has recorded a .407 batting average with 94 hits, 15 home runs, 57 RBIs, 60 runs scored and a .448 OBP.
