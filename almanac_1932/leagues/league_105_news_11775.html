Redcoats Prevail, as Division Series Goes to the Wire
Friday, September 12th, 1930
The sweet swing of Takeaki Morikawa has led his team through the Division Series.

The Boston Redcoats star earned series MVP honors as his club downed the Ottawa Indians to advance to the League Chamionship Series.

The Redcoats won the final game at Fenway Park 1912 by a score of 3-1 and the series 3-2.

"I'm seeing the ball well and happy to contribute to a win," said Morikawa. "I hope it continues for the rest of the playoffs."

For the series, Morikawa hit .478 with a .500 on-base percentage. He had 0 home runs, drove in 3 RBI and scored 5 runs.

The Boston Redcoats will play against the Wolfeburg Celtics in the next round. Wolfeburg won their series against the New York Rebels 3 games to 1. The League Chamionship Series schedule will be announced after the Division Series finishes.
