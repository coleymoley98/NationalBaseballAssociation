Setback For Redcoats and Arnon Roberge
Saturday, July 14th, 1928
The Boston Redcoats will have to do without center fielder Arnon Roberge even longer than expected. Team doctors say that Roberge's recovery from a strained rib cage muscle has not gone well and more time will be needed to treat it successfully. The Redcoats trainer indicated it would be at least 3 weeks before they would consider activating Roberge.

Roberge is presently hitting at a .295 pace with no home runs, 24 RBIs and has scored 16 times.
