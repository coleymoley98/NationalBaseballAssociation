Aquino Collects Cycle
Sunday, August 3rd, 1930
Finishing with 4 hits is a great day for any player, but Nat Aquino made it even more special by hitting for the cycle at Ballpark of Victoria today.

Aquino had 1 single, 1 double, 1 triple and 1 home run for the Vancouver Giants and led them to a 18-4 decision over the Victoria Admirals. The left fielder also finished with 4 runs scored and 6 runs batted in.

"Awesome," Aquino said when asked how the accomplishment felt.

Nat Aquino walked in the 1st, hit a two-run double in the 3rd, hit a three-run triple in the 3rd, hit a solo-shot off Russ Del Rico in the 5th, singled in the 7th and flied out in the 8th.

Aquino is currently batting .330 with 3 home runs and 54 RBIs.
