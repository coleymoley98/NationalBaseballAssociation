NL Reliever of the Year Award Winner Named
Tuesday, October 18th, 1932
At the young age of only 25, Jerome Whitaker has been named the National League Reliever of the Year Award winner for 1932.

The future appears bright for the Chicagou Frontenacs relief ace, who captured the award with 18 saves in 37 relief appearances, 28 strikeouts in 45.2 innings and a record of 1-3 to go with his 1.38 ERA.

He received 20 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Dwight Lynch of the Montreal Royales finished second in voting, while Zachary Ricard of the Quebec Nordiques finished third.
