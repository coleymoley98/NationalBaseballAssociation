Point Hurls Pallister to 2-0 Shutout Win
Friday, March 16th, 1928
It wasn't easy but the Team Pallister made it look that way.

Starter Antonio Point shut out the Team Columbia despite having what he later called "sub-par" stuff, and the Team Pallister cruised to victory, 2-0. Point allowed only 9 hits and rang up 2 strikeouts but insisted later that he did not deserve primary credit for the win.

"It didn't feel quite right today, but I got a lot of help to get through," he said. "I trusted my catcher and the guys behind me bailed me out more than once when I screwed up."
