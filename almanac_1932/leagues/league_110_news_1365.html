Jr. Cardinaux Shut Out by Mimbs, 7-0
Tuesday, June 2nd, 1931
Mo Mimbs hurled a fine game for the Winnipeg Jr. Thunder at Ballpark of St. Louis today, holding the St. Louis Jr. Cardinaux to 5 hits in a shutout win.

"When he started out, his command wasn't that good, but he's a tough guy," Winnipeg manager Rusty Dang said. "The more pitches he throws, the better his command is."

The right-hander had 8 strikeouts and 2 walks in the 7-0 victory.

"An outstanding job," St. Louis manager Jasper Widow said. "We couldn't get anything going. Every time we got a hit, he got out of the jam. He pitched a terrific game."
