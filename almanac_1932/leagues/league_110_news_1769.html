Pineda 'Lights Out' in Shutout Win
Thursday, July 16th, 1931
Orlando Pineda really had his stuff working at Ballpark of Davenport today. The St. Louis Jr. Cardinaux hurler mystified the Davenport Rivermen and shut them out 3-0. Pineda allowed a total of 3 hits in the ballgame and struck out 7.

"The shutout is nice but I'm just happy we won," Pineda said with a smile after the game. "Coach can be a real bear when we lose."

For this year Pineda has chalked up 5 wins and 4 losses in 11 starts with 97 strikeouts in 97.1 innings and a 1.66 ERA.
