Manager Robbins History; Celtics Start Search
Friday, September 28th, 1923
Few players in the Wolfeburg Celtics organization seemed upset to learn about D'marcus Robbins's dismissal as manager, a move announced today in a team press release.

While no player would speak on the record, one disgruntled reliever complained about Robbins's confrontational style, saying "He's the kind of guy who thinks the best leader is the guy who yells the most."

Another added, "D'marcus wanted to win, but he just didn't have the respect of the clubhouse."

Robbins's replacement has not yet been named, though an official announcement is expected soon.
