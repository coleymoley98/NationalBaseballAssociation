Another Setback For Montreal's Dwight Lynch
Sunday, September 4th, 1932
The Montreal Royales announced today that Dwight Lynch would be missing for longer than expected as the 34-year-old closer recovers from a tender elbow. "Dwight had been working out with the team recently, but felt some discomfort yesterday," the Royales GM explained to the press. "After re-examining the injury, the team's medical staff decided that Lynch would need extra time to heal. Their estimate is 6 days more recovery time."

To date Lynch has 13 saves with a 4-4 mark and 2.39 ERA. Opposing teams are hitting .290 against him.
