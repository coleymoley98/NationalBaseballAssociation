EL Star of the Week Announced
Monday, August 15th, 1932
J.F. Monquet demonstrated his potential to be a star in the game last week and today he took home the Eastern League Player of the Week Award.

The Brampton second baseman wreaked havoc on opposing pitchers last week, pounding them for a .500 average (14-for-28) with 3 home runs and 6 RBIs on his way to claiming the honor.

Monquet is hitting .433 for the season with 10 home runs, 39 RBIs and 33 runs scored. He has 61 hits in 141 at-bats and played in 41 games.
