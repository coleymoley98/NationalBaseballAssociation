Despite Today's Loss, Frontenacs Make Playoffs
Thursday, September 3rd, 1931
Some people may say the Chicagou Frontenacs backed into the playoffs after clinching a spot despite today's loss. But none of those people are in the team's clubhouse.

"I'm so proud of this team," said Frontenacs manager Archie Cooper. "Despite today's loss, this is something we've worked toward all year long. We're not gonna let a single game's outcome tarnish that, not after a whole season of winning."

Winning, indeed. The Chicagou Frontenacs are currently sitting at a record of 62-38 and in first place in the National League West.
