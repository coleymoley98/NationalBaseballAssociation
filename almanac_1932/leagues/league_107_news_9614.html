Victoria-Spokane Brawl, Imamura and Bélanger Suspended
Sunday, June 10th, 1928
After an intense confrontation, both the Admirals Nahathai Imamura and the Eagles Tripob Bélanger were handed their punishments today. The league commissioner's office issued suspensions for an on-the-field conflict that occurred at a recent Victoria-Spokane game. Nahathai Imamura will miss 4 games, while Tripob Bélanger was suspended for 8 games.

"Sometimes we go too far and have to pay the consequences," Nahathai Imamura commented later. "Hopefully cooler heads will prevail the next time we play."

In this current campaign Imamura is hitting at a .341 clip with no home runs and 2 RBIs.

Bélanger has taken the mound for 6 relief appearances this season, nailing down no saves while notching a 0-0 record with a 9.28 ERA. The Spokane reliever has limited opposing batters to a .262 batting average.
