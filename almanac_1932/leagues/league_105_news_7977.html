It's Official -- St. Louis Fires Yodyingyong as Manager
Tuesday, October 5th, 1926
St. Louis has fired manager Surabordee Yodyingyong.

"We were not doing the things you've got to do every day," Cardinaux general manager Joe Unknown told reporters. "We saw that things were not going to change. We had huge expectations coming into the season and we did not live up to them."

The current coaching staff will be assigned to run the ballclub in the interim until a new manager can be hired. Yodyingyong was told of his dismissal yesterday morning and was not available for comment.

The St. Louis spokesman called it a "sensitive and emotional" meeting with Yodyingyong -- and would not discuss whether Yodyingyong would be offered another job in the Cardinaux system.
