Burch's 23-Game Hit Streak Ends
Saturday, August 31st, 1929
The hitting streak that Ottawa third baseman Stuart Burch had kept going for 23 games was ended today by the Toronto Blue Sox. Burch was stymied over 1 at-bat today, but the rest of his team picked him up, taking the 5-4 victory at Griffith Stadium 1911.

"Hitting streaks come and go," Burch said. "It did put a little pressure on me. It's hard to be natural at the plate with something like that always in the back of your mind. If it had to end, I'm glad it was in a win."

For the season, Burch is hitting at a .318 clip with 2 home runs, 46 RBIs and has scored 35 runs. He has 82 hits, 8 walks and a .351 on-base percentage.
