No Playoff Berth Coming for Detroit
Sunday, September 4th, 1932
Fans in Detroit may been holding onto a glimmer of hope, but that has now been officially snuffed out -- the Lions were mathematically eliminated from playoff contention today.

"It's a tough day for everyone in the Detroit Lions organization," owner Zachary Croteau told reporters. "We will just have to come back stronger."
