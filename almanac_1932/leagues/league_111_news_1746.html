5-for-5 Game for Windley
Monday, July 13th, 1931
The 5th time was not the charm for Harvey Windley today.

The shortstop for the Gallant Black Sox hit the milestone today at Juniors Ballpark, but it was the Canton Legends that salvaged a 8-6 victory. Windley finished with a 5-for-5 line at the plate, and it still wasn't enough.

"He was not an easy out, that's for sure," Canton manager Von Rivera said to the press in the postgame interview. "I'm just glad we held everybody else in check and got the win."

Windley declined to speak to reporters at length following the game, saying only that "no loss is a good one."

Harvey Windley singled in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, singled in the 7th, hit an RBI single in the 8th and singled in the 9th.

For the year Windley is batting .292 with no home runs.
