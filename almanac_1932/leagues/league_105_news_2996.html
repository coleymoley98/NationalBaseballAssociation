Philadelphia Trades Esteve for Toronto's Nalley
Friday, July 8th, 1932
Done deal! The Philadelphia Liberty have agreed to send 37-year-old LHP Joseph Esteve to the Toronto Braves for 22-year-old minor league 1B Gunner Nalley, 20-year-old minor league CF Khusmana Sharp and 24-year-old minor league 2B Mathis Fortin. The trade, rumored for days, was completed in the wee hours this morning.


This year Esteve has compiled a 9-4 record with a 3.32 ERA in 13 starts.
