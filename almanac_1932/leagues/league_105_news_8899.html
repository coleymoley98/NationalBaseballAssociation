NBA News: Draft Pool Revealed
Sunday, August 7th, 1927
Earlier today, the NBA released its amateur draft pool, sending organizations into a flurry of action. For the next 90 days, scouts will be trying to gauge as many players as they can, hoping to get a true sense of the character, ability and passion of these young men with professional baseball aspirations.

The league has created a special Draft Area on the transactions page, which will be available for 90 days prior to draft day.

The early favorites to be high picks are the following:
