CL's Top Rookie Crowned
Saturday, October 16th, 1926
Santo Vivas turned a lot of heads in Canadian League circles this season, putting together a solid rookie campaign. Today he claimed the CL Rookie of the Year Award.

The 26-year-old Toronto left fielder played in 87 games, hitting .395 with 13 home runs, 67 RBIs and scoring 66 runs.

He received 16 first place votes, as a unanimous winner. Noël Nadeau of the Chicagou White Sox finished second in voting, while Zak Salo of the Philadelphia Liberty finished third.
