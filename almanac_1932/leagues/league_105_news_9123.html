Chicagou Moves on to Riel Cup
Monday, September 19th, 1927
Fans at Fenway Park 1912 saw the League Championship Series come to a close today, as the Chicagou White Sox defeated the Boston Redcoats by a score of 13-11, and advanced to the Riel Cup.

Chicagou won the series 4-1 and will now continue their march toward the Riel Cup.

"It feels good to be moving on, I can't deny it," said right fielder Félix Courtemanche, who was named series MVP. "Not everyone thought we'd make it this far."

The Chicagou White Sox don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Chicagou Frontenacs - Minnesota Saints series. Minnesota leads the series 3 games to 2. The Riel Cup schedule will be announced after the League Championship Series finishes.
