Brushett Sizzles, Vancouver Fizzles
Wednesday, June 10th, 1925
Albus Brushett led the Victoria Admirals to a 5-0 shutout victory over the Vancouver Giants at Oracle Park today.

"My pitching coach told me before the game just to bear down and focus," Brushett recalled later. "He said no one was going to hit me if I'm throwing a good breaking ball with good location."

Brushett yielded 3 hits and had 7 strikeouts and no walks in the standout performance.

"I thought he put the ball where he wanted to," Vancouver manager Danupong Chan-inkaew said. "And that's just about the whole secret to pitching."
