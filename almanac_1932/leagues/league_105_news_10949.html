Big Bang! 3 HR for Ottawa's Wintringham
Tuesday, June 25th, 1929
Anyone wearing a glove and sitting in the bleachers for today's Indians-Robins game had a good chance at snagging a home run ball.

Ottawa left fielder David Wintringham thrice sent eager fans scurrying to catch a souvenir, helping lead the Ottawa to a 13-5 win over Baltimore.

"Hey, the three homers were great, but I'm just trying to help my ballclub," Wintringham said later. "I'm not going out there thinking I need another home run."

Wintringham was 4-for-4 on the day with 6 runs batted in.

David Wintringham hit a two-run home run off Bentley Lundeen in the 1st, hit a three-run home run off Bentley Lundeen in the 2nd, walked in the 4th, hit a solo-shot off Nicola Patterson in the 6th and singled in the 8th.

Wintringham has a .390 average and a .430 on-base percentage for the year. He has picked up 73 hits, 16 home runs and driven in 49 runs.
