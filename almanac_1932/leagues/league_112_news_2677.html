5-Hit Barrage by Arcand
Thursday, June 2nd, 1932
Getting 5 hits in a game has always been memorable feat in baseball. In the Pointe-Sainte-Anne's 8-6 win over Drummondville today, second baseman Raymond Arcand was red-hot, scorching them for hit after hit at Field of the Acadiens.

"To hit the ball consistently well, you have to relax," Arcand said afterward. "I was relaxed for the first four hits, but I have to admit, I got a little tight when I realized I had a chance for five. On the last at-bat, I was ready to swing at anything close."

Raymond Arcand doubled in the 1st, singled in the 3rd, doubled in the 5th, hit a solo-shot off Terapat Swoope in the 8th and hit a GRAND SLAM off Pratum Killingsworth in the 9th.
