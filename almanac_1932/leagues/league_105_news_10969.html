Woodmore Holds Nordiques Scoreless
Sunday, June 30th, 1929
A favor to his bullpen. That's what Eric Woodmore was doing at Ballpark of Quebec today as he blanked the Quebec Nordiques in a 9-inning complete game shutout.

"I went out there to try to eat up innings and help out our bullpen," the Chicagou Frontenacs right-hander said later. "I felt I had my good stuff, and it turned out my command was solid."

Woodmore notched 4 strikeouts, surrendered no walks, allowed 4 hits, and kept the bullpen phone quiet all day long.

In 12 starts this season, Woodmore has recorded 4 wins and absorbed 6 losses. He has pitched 93 innings, allowed 103 hits, struck out 33 and has a 4.55 ERA.
