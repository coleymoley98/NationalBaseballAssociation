Jr. Royales Come up Short of Playoffs
Saturday, July 24th, 1926
It's a "gut-wrenching feeling," Montreal Jr. Royales starting pitcher Kevin Gibb said, for the team to have gotten so close to a playoff berth, only to be eliminated in the waning days of the season. The club was officially eliminated from the postseason chase today.

Before this season, the Jr. Royales last missed the playoffs in 1923. In the 32 years they have been a Atlantic Baseball League franchise, they have missed the playoffs 29 times. In the last 10 years, they have missed the playoffs 7 times. Montreal last won the Laurier Cup in 1924.
