Rapids Torpedoed by Pineault in 2-0 Loss to Hamilton
Friday, July 19th, 1929
Hamilton Jr. Steelers pitcher Nicolas Pineault blanked the Buffalo Rapids 2-0 at Jr. Steelers Ballpark today, tossing a 2-hitter while issuing no walks and collecting 15 strikeouts.

After his sharp shutout performance, he shared credit with the rest of the club.

"I was just locked in out there and some of that is due to having a catcher who has such a great feel for hitters," Pineault said. "I couldn't do what I did without all the guys out there today."

Up to now Pineault has compiled an ERA of 2.56 with a 7-2 mark over 14 starts. He has 118 strikeouts in 105.2 innings and has held opponents to a .214 batting average.
