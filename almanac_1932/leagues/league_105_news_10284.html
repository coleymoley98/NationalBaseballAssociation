Cunningham Socks it to Redcoats, Cracks 5 Hits
Friday, September 21st, 1928
Arlo Cunningham was at the head of a Montreal hit parade today at Fenway Park 1912. The shortstop went to the plate 6 times and came away with 5 hits, sparking the Royales to a 12-9 victory over the Boston Redcoats.

"I told the guys in the dugout, just be thinking about hitting the ball hard somewhere," the sweet-swinging Cunningham told reporters after the game. "Seems like it worked out well for us today."

Redcoats skipper Brian Ahn said later that the club gave up "too much plate."

"Not the way we planned it," he said. "Every hitter in the league will rip you when you do that."

Arlo Cunningham singled in the 1st, hit an RBI single in the 2nd, hit an RBI double in the 4th, hit an RBI single in the 5th, doubled in the 7th and grounded out in the 9th.

In his season totals Cunningham has 2 home runs and a .299 batting average.
