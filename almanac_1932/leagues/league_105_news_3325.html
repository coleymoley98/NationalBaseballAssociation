Another Setback For Toronto's Sutep Pickett
Wednesday, August 17th, 1932
The Toronto Braves announced today that Sutep Pickett will miss an additional one week after the starting pitcher suffered a setback in his rehabilitation from elbow inflammation. While the setback is believed to be minor, the Braves want Pickett to take his time and fully recover before coming back.

This season Pickett has crafted a 10-8 mark with a 3.24 ERA in 144.2 innings. Opposing clubs are batting .243 against him and he has 77 strikeouts.
