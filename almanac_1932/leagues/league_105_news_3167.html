Palmisano Hits for Cycle Against Detroit
Friday, July 29th, 1932
Most fans have never seen a player hit for the cycle, and most players have never done it -- but it happened today at Wrigley Field 1928. Jerold Palmisano of the Chicagou Frontenacs accomplished the feat against Detroit Lions.

Palmisano went 5-for-5 with 2 singles, 1 double, 1 triple and 1 home run in the 7-0 Chicagou victory.

"That was one for the grandkids, am I right?" Palmisano said after the game.

Palmisano also scored 3 runs and had 4 runs batted in in a day to remember for both Palmisano and the crowd at Wrigley Field 1928.

Jerold Palmisano doubled in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, hit a two-run triple in the 4th, hit an RBI single in the 6th and hit a solo-shot off Hafit Duangsawang in the 8th.

So far this season Palmisano has 1 home run and a .359 batting average.
