Wednesday, June 27th, 1923
Israel Hunt put a hex on the Minnesota Saints in a sparkling 3-0 shutout performance, mesmerizing them on a 7-hit gem.

"I felt good physically," he said after the game. "Everything's comfortable. You should pitch well when that's the case."

The Saints were anything but comfortable against the 24-year-old Chicagou Frontenacs hurler, who fanned 2 and gave up 2 walks.

"Hunt used his changeup very well, very effectively," said opposing manager A.J. Provost. "He threw it in hitter's counts."

To date Hunt is 5-1 in 9 starts with a 2.27 ERA and has 24 strikeouts in 83.1 innings.
