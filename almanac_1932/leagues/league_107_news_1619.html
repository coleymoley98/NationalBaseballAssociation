Williams Awarded June's WBL Top Bat Prize
Wednesday, July 1st, 1931
For a rookie just breaking in, Joseph Williams certainly put together a successful month. It was good enough to win the WBL Batter of the Month award for June.

The 15-year-old first baseman for the Ranier Seahawks hit .409 with 7 home runs, 28 RBIs and 28 runs scored.
