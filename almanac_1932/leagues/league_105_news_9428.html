Ottawa-Quebec Trade: Gaines for Leclerc
Sunday, April 8th, 1928
25-year-old 3B Warren Gaines, 20-year-old minor league 3B Brian Cloutier and 20-year-old minor league RF Alex Tassé will now be playing for the Quebec Nordiques and 25-year-old CF Logan Leclerc will be suiting up for the Ottawa Indians. The trade was announced today at a press conference by club officials.


Leclerc is hitting .273 lifetime with 115 hits, 11 doubles, 8 triples, 1 home run and 31 RBIs to date. He has scored 72 runs, walked 41 times and has a .345 OBP.
