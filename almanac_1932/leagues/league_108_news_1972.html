5 Hits for Giants Schilcher
Thursday, August 6th, 1931
Hudson Schilcher put on a hitting clinic today as the Vancouver Giants dumped the Omaha Falcons 10-7 at Falcons Ballpark.

The Vancouver second baseman made Omaha defenders earn their pay, lashing Omaha pitching for 5 hits in 8 at-bats in the game. He told BNN reporters after the game that his strategy was "just hit it where they pitched it."

"Too often I try too hard to pull the ball," Schilcher said in his postgame press conference. "Today, I just went with the pitch."

Hudson Schilcher flied out in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, singled in the 4th, flied out in the 5th, singled in the 7th, grounded out in the 8th, doubled in the 10th and singled in the 11th.

This year Schilcher is hitting .350 with 9 home runs and 54 RBIs to his credit. He has gotten on base at a .413 clip and scored 58 runs.
