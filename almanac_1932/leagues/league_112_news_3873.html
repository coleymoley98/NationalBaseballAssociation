Consecutive-Game Hit Streak Continues for Lacasse
Saturday, June 16th, 1923
Bradley Lacasse of the Drummondville Voltigeurs extended his hitting streak to 20 straight games with 2 hits in today's 6-2 triumph over the New Britain Yankees at Ballpark of Drummondville.

"Bradley has been making it easy for me to pencil his name in the lineup every day," Voltigeurs manager Tai Grigsby joked after the game.
Bradley Lacasse singled in the 1st, doubled in the 3rd and was hit by a pitch in the 5th.

Lacasse is hitting .400 for the season with 1 home run, 5 RBIs and 6 runs scored. He has 22 hits in 55 at-bats and played in 13 games.
