Royales Show No Mercy in 10-0 Thrashing of Cardinaux
Saturday, May 25th, 1929
Fans at St. Louis Grounds witnessed the Montreal Royales blank the St. Louis Cardinaux, 10-0 behind a superb effort by starter Bunnawat Phomee. The Montreal right-hander allowed 4 hits in recording the shutout.

"Bunnawat was good today but I don't want to focus too much on a single game," Montreal manager Hervé Morel said later. "If you're capable of playing at this level, you're capable of having a good game. The real question is if you can learn from that game and repeat it."
