White Sox Win League Chamionship Series
Friday, September 30th, 1932
The sweet swing of Nat Aquino has led his team through the League Chamionship Series.

The Chicagou White Sox star earned series MVP honors as his club downed the Ottawa Indians to advance to the Riel Cup.

The White Sox won the final game at Lincoln Park by a score of 11-7 and the series 4-2.

"I'm seeing the ball well and happy to contribute to a win," said Aquino. "I hope it continues for the rest of the playoffs."

For the series, Aquino hit .370 with a .419 on-base percentage. He had 0 home runs, drove in 8 RBI and scored 5 runs.

The Chicagou White Sox don't know who they will face in the next round. They will play the winner of the Montreal Royales - Chicagou Frontenacs series. Chicagou leads the series 3 games to 2. The Riel Cup schedule will be announced after the League Chamionship Series finishes.
