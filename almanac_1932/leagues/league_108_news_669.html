Admirals Lead with Chin, Get KO'd, 12-0
Tuesday, July 8th, 1930
Jacob McGowan kept the opposition off the scoreboard and the Omaha Falcons were all smiles after winning 12-0 against the Victoria Admirals at Ballpark of Victoria today. The right-handed McGowan gave up no runs in his start, permitted 5 hits and had 5 strikeouts.

"If there was a stat for 'team shutout,' this would be it," McGowan said later. "My catcher called a great game, guys delivered at the plate and when I messed something up the guys behind me were where they needed to be."

Season-to-date McGowan has rung up 7 wins and 1 loss in 8 appearances. He has an ERA of 2.32 and has fanned 37 batters in 73.2 innings.
