Vidal Twirls 1-0 Shutout
Sunday, August 26th, 1928
Ewen Vidal hurled a fine game for the Winnipeg Thunder at Thunder Stadium today, holding the Calgary Wranglers to 3 hits in a shutout win.

"When he started out, his command wasn't that good, but he's a tough guy," Winnipeg manager Charlie Fleury said. "The more pitches he throws, the better his command is."

The right-hander had 3 strikeouts and 7 walks in the 1-0 victory.

"An outstanding job," Calgary manager Eric Fitzsimmons said. "We couldn't get anything going. Every time we got a hit, he got out of the jam. He pitched a terrific game."

This year Vidal has a 2-3 record with a 9.18 ERA.
