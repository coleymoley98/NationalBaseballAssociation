Wolfeburg-Toronto Trade: Williams for Gulley
Thursday, November 1st, 1923
Wittawat Kraisil, general manager of the Wolfeburg Celtics, has signed off on a trade for Toronto's 22-year-old C Carson Gulley, 19-year-old minor league RF Michael Lawson and 20-year-old minor league C Mason Poole. To complete the deal, the Celtics will send 27-year-old SS Craig Williams to the Blue Sox.


In his career Williams has hit .304 with 430 hits, 59 doubles, 32 triples and 4 home runs. His totals also include 145 RBIs and 214 runs scored.
