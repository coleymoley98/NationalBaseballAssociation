Lamontagne Collects Cycle
Thursday, July 7th, 1932
Davenport third baseman Hayden Lamontagne had a field day at Ballpark of Davenport, battering Winnipeg pitchers and hitting for the cycle.

Lamontagne sparked the Rivermen to a 8-7 triumph over the Jr. Thunder by going 4-for-5 with 3 RBI and scoring 3 runs.

"I was seeing the ball great today," Lamontagne said. "I'm glad it translated into results on the field."

Hayden Lamontagne grounded out in the 2nd, tripled in the 5th, singled in the 7th, hit an RBI double in the 8th and hit a two-run home run off Gery Després in the 9th.

To date this season Lamontagne is hitting .341 with 8 home runs.
