Star RP Montano Settles on New York
Monday, December 23rd, 1929
In what local scribes are hailing as a huge move for the team's future, the Rebels inked a $12,600 deal with reliever Lashawn Montano.

Team officials expect Montano to fill an immediate void on the roster and felt the deal would solidify them as a playoff contender.

Over his career Montano has a 87-66 won-lost record with a 3.15 ERA. In 504 games he has recorded 100 saves, and the 35-year-old has fanned 317 batters in 837.1 innings.
