Montreal Celebrates Wild Card Win
Sunday, July 26th, 1925
The Montreal Jr. Royales are celebrating a trip to the postseason and say they plan to make the most of the opportunity.

The team officially clinched a wild card berth today and starting pitcher Stephen McCord said they are "looking forward to making some noise" in the playoffs.

"There's nothing better than postseason baseball," he said. "We hope to go on a deep run and give our fans in Montreal something to be excited about."

For now, excitement is running high as the team's 29-22 record has clinched the playoff position and the team has a chance to win its 5th Atlantic Baseball League championship.
