Red-Hot Bat: Coutu Collects Cycle
Tuesday, August 26th, 1930
Finishing with 4 hits is a great day for any player, but Blake Coutu made it even more special by hitting for the cycle at Ballpark of Victoria today.

Coutu had 1 single, 1 double, 1 triple and 1 home run for the Spokane Eagles and led them to a 9-0 decision over the Victoria Admirals. The second baseman also finished with 3 runs scored and 4 RBI.

"Awesome," Coutu said when asked how the accomplishment felt.

Blake Coutu doubled in the 1st, singled in the 2nd, flied out in the 4th, hit a three-run triple in the 5th, grounded out in the 7th and hit a solo-shot off Gordon Mercier in the 9th.

For the season Coutu has hit .304 with 5 home runs and 36 RBIs. He has played in 62 games and scored 45 times.
